---
title: "Tattvartha Sutra - Chapter 1"
scripture: "tattvartha-sutra"
chapter: 1
---

**1** Right faith, right knowledge, and right conduct together constitute the path of liberation.

**2** Right faith is the belief in the substances ascertained as they are.

**3** Right knowledge is the detailed knowledge of the substances, without doubt, error, or uncertainty.

**4** Right conduct is the abstinence from what is harmful and the practice of what is beneficial to the soul.

**5** The substances are: soul, matter, the principle of motion, the principle of rest, space, and time. These six constitute the whole of reality.

**6** The soul is characterized by consciousness. Its function is to know and to perceive. This is its essential nature.

**7** The non-soul is that which is devoid of consciousness. Matter, the principles of motion and rest, space, and time are the five non-soul substances.

**8** Knowledge is of five kinds: sensory knowledge, scriptural knowledge, clairvoyance, telepathy, and omniscience. The first two may be erroneous; the last three are always correct.

**9** Sensory knowledge is that which is obtained through the senses and the mind. Scriptural knowledge is that which is obtained from the study of the sacred texts.

**10** Clairvoyance is the direct knowledge of material things without the use of the senses. Telepathy is the direct knowledge of the thoughts of others. Omniscience is the complete and perfect knowledge of all things.
