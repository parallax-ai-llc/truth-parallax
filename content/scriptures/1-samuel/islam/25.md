## Wisdom Prevents Bloodshed

Islamic engagement with 1 Samuel 25 celebrates Abigail's intervention as hikmah -- wisdom the Quran identifies as God's greatest gift: "Whoever has been given wisdom has been given much good" (Surah 2:269).

## Key Themes and Islamic Parallels

### Hikmah in Action

Abigail's counsel prevents David from shedding blood in anger. Her peacemaking aligns with the principle that the best jihad is speaking truth to power.

### Restraint from Sin

David accepts wise correction -- a mark of true spiritual maturity.

## Theological Themes

- **Hikmah**: Wisdom as God's greatest gift
- **Peacemaking**: The obligation to prevent bloodshed
- **Restraint from Sin**: The community's role in preventing moral failure
- **Women as Agents of Peace**: Islamic recognition of women's contributions

## Reflection

Abigail demonstrates hikmah -- practical wisdom that sees clearly, speaks courageously, and acts decisively to prevent evil.
