## Forbearance Deepened

Islamic engagement with 1 Samuel 26 reads David's second refusal as settled hilm -- forbearance formed through repeated practice.

## Key Themes and Islamic Parallels

### Character Formation

"Whoever is patient, Allah will bestow patience upon him" (Sahih al-Bukhari). Patience practiced becomes patience perfected.

### Tawakkul Renewed

David's trust deepens with each test. Virtue is built through sustained practice.

## Theological Themes

- **Hilm as Habit**: Forbearance deepened through practice
- **Tawakkul**: Renewed trust in God's timing
- **Character Formation**: Virtue built through sustained choices
- **Divine Testing**: God tests the same virtue to establish it

## Reflection

Every act of patience strengthens the soul, every refusal to retaliate deepens the virtue.
