## Return to God and Complete Restoration

Islamic engagement with 1 Samuel 30 reads David's response to Ziklag as spiritual recovery through tawbah and renewed tawakkul. "Whoever fears Allah -- He will make for him a way out" (Surah 65:2-3).

## Key Themes and Islamic Parallels

### Tawbah and Recovery

David's "strengthening himself in the LORD" represents decisive return to God. The equal spoils division resonates with the Islamic ethic behind zakat.

### Complete Restoration

God restores everything -- a sign of mercy for those who return.

## Theological Themes

- **Tawbah**: Decisive return to God
- **Complete Restoration**: God restores what the enemy has stolen
- **Equitable Distribution**: Blessings must be shared fairly
- **Tawakkul Renewed**: Restoration of trust

## Reflection

"Turn to Allah in repentance, all of you, O believers, that you might succeed" (Surah 24:31).
