## Seeking Divine Guidance

Islamic engagement with 1 Samuel 23 reads David's repeated consultation of God as a model of istikharah -- seeking divine guidance before important decisions.

## Key Themes and Islamic Parallels

### Istikharah

David's inquiry through the ephod models the Islamic practice of seeking divine direction through prayer.

### Mutual Encouragement

Jonathan's visit -- strengthening David in God -- models the hadith: "The believers in their mutual kindness are just like one body" (Sahih al-Bukhari).

## Theological Themes

- **Istikharah**: Seeking God's guidance through established means
- **Mutual Encouragement**: Believers strengthening one another
- **Tawakkul in Uncertainty**: Trusting God when the path is unclear
- **The Wilderness as Testing Ground**: Difficulty proves faith

## Reflection

The faithful seek divine guidance through prayer. The Istikharah prayer echoes David's practice of consulting God before every major decision.
