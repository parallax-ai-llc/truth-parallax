---
title: "Sunan Ibn Majah - Chapter 4"
scripture: "hadith-ibn-majah"
chapter: 4
---

**1** Narrated Abu Hurairah: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "The Hour will not be established until the sun rises from the west. When it rises from the west, all people will believe, but that will be a time when faith will not benefit a soul that did not believe before or did not earn good through its faith."

**2** Narrated Hudhaifah: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, stood among us and did not leave out anything that would happen until the Hour without mentioning it. Some of us remembered it and some of us forgot it. These companions of mine know it, and there are things which I may have forgotten but when I see them I remember them, just as a man remembers the face of someone who has been absent, and when he sees him, he recognizes him.

**3** Narrated Abu Hurairah: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "Hasten to do good deeds before six events: the rising of the sun from the west, the smoke, the Dajjal, the beast, the affair that particularly concerns one of you, and the general affair."

**4** Narrated Anas: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "The Hour will not come until time passes swiftly, so a year will be like a month, a month will be like a week, a week will be like a day, a day will be like an hour, and an hour will be like the flicker of a flame."

**5** Narrated Abu Hurairah: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "The Hour will not come until knowledge is taken away, earthquakes increase, time passes quickly, tribulations appear, and killing increases."

**6** Narrated Anas: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "The Hour will not come until people compete with one another in building tall buildings."

**7** Narrated Abu Hurairah: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "By Him in Whose hand is my soul, the world will not come to an end until a day comes when the killer will not know why he killed and the victim will not know why he was killed."

**8** Narrated Abu Hurairah: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "There will come a time when holding on to your faith will be like holding on to hot coals."
