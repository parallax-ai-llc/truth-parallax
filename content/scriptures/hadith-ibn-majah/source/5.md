---
title: "Sunan Ibn Majah - Chapter 5"
scripture: "hadith-ibn-majah"
chapter: 5
---

**1** Narrated Abu Hurairah: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "Whoever believes in Allah and the Last Day, let him honor his guest. Whoever believes in Allah and the Last Day, let him maintain the ties of kinship. Whoever believes in Allah and the Last Day, let him speak good or remain silent."

**2** Narrated Abu Hurairah: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "The most perfect of believers in faith are those who are best in character. The best of you are those who are best to their wives."

**3** Narrated Mu'adh ibn Jabal: The last advice the Messenger of Allah gave me when I put my foot in the stirrup was: "Make your character good for the people, O Mu'adh."

**4** Narrated Abu Darda: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "Nothing will be heavier on the Day of Resurrection in the scale of the believer than good character. Allah hates the one who is shameless and vulgar."

**5** Narrated Abu Hurairah: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "A person may speak a word that is pleasing to Allah, not giving it much importance, for which Allah will raise him in rank. And a person may speak a word that displeases Allah, not giving it much importance, for which he will be cast into the Hellfire."

**6** Narrated Anas: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "Do not hate one another, do not envy one another, do not turn your backs on one another, but be servants of Allah, brothers. It is not lawful for a Muslim to shun his brother for more than three days."

**7** Narrated Abu Hurairah: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "Beware of suspicion, for suspicion is the most untrue of speech. Do not spy, do not compete, do not envy, do not hate, do not turn away. Be servants of Allah, brothers."

**8** Narrated Abu Hurairah: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "It is not lawful for a Muslim to forsake his brother for more than three days. Whoever forsakes his brother for more than three days and dies will enter the Fire."
