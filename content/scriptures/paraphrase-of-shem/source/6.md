---
title: "Paraphrase of Shem — Section 6"
scripture: "paraphrase-of-shem"
chapter: 6
---

**1** "Then the Darkness sent forth the flood upon the earth, to destroy the seed of the Light. But the power of the Light preserved those who knew the truth."

**2** "After the flood, the fire was sent, and after the fire, the wind. Each time, the Darkness sought to destroy the Light, and each time the Light was preserved."

**3** "I appeared again and again in the world, in various forms, to teach the souls of the Light."

**4** "I appeared in the form of a beast, in the form of a man, in the form of fire. Each time I came to separate the Light from the Darkness."

**5** "And those who received my teaching were saved. And those who rejected it remained in the power of Darkness."
