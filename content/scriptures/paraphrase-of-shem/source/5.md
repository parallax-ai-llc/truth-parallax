---
title: "Paraphrase of Shem — Section 5"
scripture: "paraphrase-of-shem"
chapter: 5
---

**1** "Then I, Derdekeas, appeared upon the earth in the form of a beast, that I might rescue the Light that was trapped in Nature."

**2** "I came to the womb of Darkness and found the power of the Spirit dwelling there in suffering. And I raised it up."

**3** "And I taught the human form about the three roots: that Light is above, that Darkness is below, and that the Spirit moves between them."

**4** "I said to the souls of the Light: Do not be afraid of the Darkness. For the power of Light is greater than the power of Darkness."

**5** "Remember your origin in the Light. Purify yourselves from the mixture of Darkness, and ascend to the place of rest."
