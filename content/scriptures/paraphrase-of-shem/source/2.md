---
title: "Paraphrase of Shem — Section 2"
scripture: "paraphrase-of-shem"
chapter: 2
---

**1** "The Nature of Darkness was agitated. It was troubled. It desired the Light and wished to mingle with it."

**2** "The Darkness rose up from its abyss and desired to seize the Light. But the Light withdrew and did not mingle with it."

**3** "Then the Darkness became angry and brought forth from itself a great womb. And the womb was filled with fire and darkness."

**4** "And the Spirit saw the agitation of the Darkness and was filled with compassion. But the Spirit did not pour itself into the Darkness."

**5** "Instead, it brought forth from itself the Mind, which is the power of the Spirit. And the Mind looked upon the Darkness and took pity on the Light that was trapped within it."
