---
title: "Paraphrase of Shem — Section 4"
scripture: "paraphrase-of-shem"
chapter: 4
---

**1** "Then the womb of Darkness brought forth every form of beast and demon. And Nature was disturbed by the descent of the Mind."

**2** "And Nature brought forth the clouds and the winds and the mountains and the seas."

**3** "The Mind looked upon the forms that Nature had brought forth, and it purified them. It separated the good from the evil."

**4** "And the Mind said, I shall place my power in the forms of Nature, that they may become vessels of Light."

**5** "Then the human form was fashioned, in the likeness of the Mind. And the spark of Light was placed within it, that humanity might remember its origin."
