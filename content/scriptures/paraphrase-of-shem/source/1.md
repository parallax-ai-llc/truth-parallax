---
title: "Paraphrase of Shem — Section 1"
scripture: "paraphrase-of-shem"
chapter: 1
---

**1** The paraphrase which was about the unbegotten Spirit. What Derdekeas revealed to me, Shem, according to the will of the Majesty.

**2** My thought which was in my body snatched me away from my race. It took me up to the top of creation, which is close to the light that shone upon the whole area there.

**3** I saw no earthly likeness, but there was light. And my thought separated from the body of darkness as though in sleep.

**4** I heard a voice saying to me, "Shem, since you are from an unmixed power and you are the first being upon the earth, hear and understand what I shall say to you first concerning the great powers who were in the beginning."

**5** "In the beginning there were three roots: Light, Darkness, and Spirit. The Spirit was between them as a gentle power."

**6** "These three roots existed individually and they were powers by themselves. And Darkness was wind in waters. He had a mind wrapped in a chaotic fire."

**7** "And the Light was gentle and clear. The Spirit between them was a gentle, humble light."
