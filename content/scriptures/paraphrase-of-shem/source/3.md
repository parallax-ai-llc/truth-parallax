---
title: "Paraphrase of Shem — Section 3"
scripture: "paraphrase-of-shem"
chapter: 3
---

**1** "Then the Mind descended into the boundary between Light and Darkness. And it fashioned a covering for itself from the wind and the water."

**2** "And the Mind began to separate the Light from the Darkness. It drew out the sparks of Light that were scattered throughout the chaotic water."

**3** "The Darkness raged against the Mind, for it knew that its power was being diminished. And it cast forth demons and beasts to oppose the Mind."

**4** "But the Mind was stronger than the Darkness, for it had the power of the Light within it."

**5** "And the Mind established the firmament between the upper waters and the lower waters, separating the realm of Light from the realm of Darkness."
