---
title: "Paraphrase of Shem Section 7"
scripture: "paraphrase-of-shem"
chapter: 7
---

**1** And Derdekeas said to me: Shem, at the consummation of the age, the three roots shall be fully separated. The Light shall return to the Light, the Darkness to the Darkness, and the Spirit shall be freed from both.

**2** And the womb of Nature shall be dissolved, and the forms of the Darkness shall be no more. And the mind of the Darkness shall sink into the abyss, and its chaotic fire shall be extinguished.

**3** And the Spirit which was bound shall rise to the heights of the Light. And it shall be united with the great Light, and it shall rest in the silence of the unbegotten.

**4** And those who knew the truth shall put off the garments of Nature and shall clothe themselves in garments of Light. And they shall pass through the regions without hindrance, for the powers shall have no authority over them.

**5** But those who served the Darkness, who did not awaken from their sleep, they shall descend with the Darkness into the abyss. And they shall be dissolved with the forms of Nature.

**6** And the Light shall be pure, as it was in the beginning. And the silence of the unbegotten shall be restored. And the three roots shall be as they were before the mixture.

**7** Then Derdekeas said to me: Shem, you shall preserve these words and reveal them to those who are worthy. Do not give them to the unworthy, for they will not understand.

**8** And you shall be a witness to the truth, that the Light is above the Darkness, and the Spirit is between them, and the consummation shall come when all things return to their roots.

**9** And I, Shem, received these words and kept them in my heart. And I wrote them down so that the race of the Spirit might know the truth and be saved from the mixture.

**10** This is the paraphrase of Shem, the revelation of Derdekeas, the word of the unbegotten Light, given to the first-born of the Spirit for the salvation of those who dwell in the mixture.

**11** The Paraphrase of Shem.
