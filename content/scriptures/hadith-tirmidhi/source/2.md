---
title: "Jami at-Tirmidhi - Chapter 2"
scripture: "hadith-tirmidhi"
chapter: 2
---

**1** Narrated Abu Hurairah: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "Allah does not look at your outward appearance or your wealth. Rather, He looks at your hearts and your deeds."

**2** Narrated Abu Hurairah: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "Whoever treads a path seeking knowledge, Allah will make easy for him a path to Paradise."

**3** Narrated Muawiyah: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "When Allah wishes good for someone, He gives him understanding of the religion."

**4** Narrated Abu Umamah: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "The superiority of the scholar over the worshipper is like the superiority of the moon on a full-moon night over the rest of the stars."

**5** Narrated Abu Hurairah: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "When the son of Adam dies, all his deeds come to an end except three: ongoing charity, beneficial knowledge, or a righteous child who prays for him."

**6** Narrated Abu Hurairah: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "The best of people are those who are most beneficial to people."

**7** Narrated Mu'adh: The Prophet, peace be upon him, said: "Fear Allah wherever you are, follow a bad deed with a good deed and it will erase it, and treat people with good character."

**8** Narrated Aisha: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "The most beloved of deeds to Allah are those done consistently, even if they are small."
