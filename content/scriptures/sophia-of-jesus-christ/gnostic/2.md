## The Nature of the Father

The Savior's teaching about the ineffable Father reveals the highest reality to disciples prepared to receive it. The Gnostic reader understands that this knowledge — the nature of the transcendent God — is the foundation of all salvation. Without knowing the Father's true nature (as distinct from the demiurge), liberation from the material world is impossible.
