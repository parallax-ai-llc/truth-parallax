## The Risen Savior Appears

The appearance of the risen Savior to the twelve disciples and seven women on a mountain establishes the revelatory authority of what follows. For Gnostic readers, this scene signifies that the deepest cosmic knowledge comes only from the post-resurrection Christ — the revealer who has passed through death and transcended the material realm. The mountain setting echoes biblical theophanies while marking this revelation as surpassing all previous ones.
