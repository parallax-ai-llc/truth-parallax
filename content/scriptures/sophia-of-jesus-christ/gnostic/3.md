## The Emanation of the Aeons

The emanation of the aeons from the divine source recapitulates Eugnostos's cosmology but with the authority of Christ's own voice. The Gnostic reader receives this not as abstract speculation but as revealed truth about the architecture of the divine realm — the realm to which the enlightened soul will return.
