## The Fall and the Material World

The narrative of Sophia's fall — her unauthorized desire that produced the material world — explains the origin of the cosmos as a tragic accident rather than a divine plan. The Gnostic reader sees in this story both the cause of present suffering and the promise of redemption: what fell through ignorance can be restored through knowledge.
