## Salvation and Return

The Savior's teaching on salvation describes the means by which the spiritual seed — the divine sparks trapped in matter — can return to the Pleroma. The Gnostic reader understands this as the practical application of the cosmological knowledge revealed in the preceding sections: knowing the architecture of the divine realm enables the soul to navigate its return.
