## The Risen Savior Appears

The opening scene has been analyzed for its genre conventions. The post-resurrection appearance on a mountain follows patterns attested in both canonical (Matthew 28:16-20) and non-canonical literature. Scholars debate whether the Sophia of Jesus Christ depends on a known Gospel tradition or draws on an independent pool of resurrection-appearance conventions.
