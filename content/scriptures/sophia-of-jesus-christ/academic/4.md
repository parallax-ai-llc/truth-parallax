## The Fall and the Material World

The section on Sophia's fall and the material world contains material not found in Eugnostos, representing the Christianizer's most significant theological addition. This section brings the text into closer alignment with Valentinian and Sethian cosmogonic narratives that explain the origin of the material world as the result of a divine error.
