## Salvation and Return

The salvation teaching explicitly Christianizes the soteriology by making Christ the agent of salvation rather than gnosis alone. Scholars debate whether this represents a genuine theological development or a superficial addition of Christian terminology to an essentially non-Christian soteriology.
