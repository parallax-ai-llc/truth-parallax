## The Emanation of the Aeons

The aeon emanation section demonstrates the closest correspondence with Eugnostos, suggesting that the Christianizer found this cosmological material most suitable for direct reuse. The addition of disciple questions at key points transforms the monologue into a pedagogical dialogue, a common device in ancient instructional literature.
