---
title: "Sophia of Jesus Christ - Chapter 5"
scripture: "sophia-of-jesus-christ"
chapter: 5
---

**1** Thomas said to him: "Lord, Savior, how many are the aeons of those who surpass the heavens?"

**2** The Savior said: "I praise you because you ask about the great aeons, for your roots are in the infinities. Now when those whom I have discussed appeared, Self-begetter Father first appeared.

**3** "He created for himself a great aeon on account of his greatness. He gave him great authority, and he ruled over all creation.

**4** "He created for himself gods and archangels and angels, myriads without number for retinue.

**5** "Now that whole multitude of those aeons has one will, one mind, one thought, one consent, one word. And they see each other equally well.

**6** "Whoever knows the Father in pure knowledge will depart to the Father and repose in the Unbegotten Father. But whoever knows him deficiently will depart to deficiency.

**7** "The Son of Man agreed with Sophia, his consort, and revealed a great androgynous light. Its male nature is called the Savior, the begetter of all things. Its female nature is called All-begettress Sophia.

**8** "Whoever knows this, let him keep these things within himself. And the silence which contains the thought, let him who understands keep silence.

**9** "I have given you authority over all things as sons of light, that you might tread upon their power with your feet."

**10** These are the things the blessed Savior said, and he disappeared from them. Then all the disciples were in great, ineffable joy in the spirit from that day on. And his disciples began to preach the Gospel of God, the eternal, imperishable Spirit. Amen.
