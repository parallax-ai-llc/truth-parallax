---
title: "Sophia of Jesus Christ - Chapter 4"
scripture: "sophia-of-jesus-christ"
chapter: 4
---

**1** The Savior continued: "All those who came from the immortal one, from the First One who has no image — they came in the power of the Immortal Man, whom they call 'the First Begetter' and 'the Sophia,' the mother of the universe.

**2** "Some call her 'Love.' But the First One, since he has authority from his Father, created for himself an immeasurable multitude of angels to serve and praise.

**3** "These possess the ineffable and immeasurable joy and jubilation. They rejoice over his incorruptible glory, which has never been heard of and is not manifest.

**4** "I came from the Boundless One. I am with you until the end of the days of the impious. And the companion of the divine Mother-Father is you, the people of the great Seth.

**5** "The drops from the light of the Holy Spirit will descend on you. The five seals which possess the five garments shall be stamped upon you, and they shall overcome death.

**6** "Then the great Seth rejoiced in what was given to him by the incorruptible Child. He knew that he was protected by the great invisible Spirit and the five seals.

**7** "All those who have known this Way exist deathless in the midst of dying mankind. That sown seed will not perish, but will endure, since it is sown in the good earth.

**8** "Whoever has known the Father in pure knowledge will depart to the Father, and will repose in the Unbegotten Father.

**9** "But whoever has known him deficiently will depart to the deficiency and to what belongs to him.

**10** "Whoever does not know the root of all things — they are strangers to all things. This is the revelation of the Sophia of Jesus Christ."
