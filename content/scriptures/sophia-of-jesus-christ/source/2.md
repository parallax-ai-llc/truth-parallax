---
title: "Sophia of Jesus Christ - Chapter 2"
scripture: "sophia-of-jesus-christ"
chapter: 2
---

**1** "He who is able to, let him arise to hear about the one who exists before all of these, the Boundless One.

**2** "I am the one who is with you always. I am the Father, I am the Mother, I am the Son. I am the undefiled and incorruptible one.

**3** "Now I have come to teach you what is, and what was, and what will come to be, so that you may know the things which are not revealed and those which are revealed, and to teach you about the unwavering race of the perfect Man.

**4** "Now lift up your thought, for I wish to reveal to you that which the understanding cannot comprehend.

**5** "The Father of the Universe, the one who first existed, is called the First Man. He revealed his own nature within himself, and he is the one whom the thought contemplated.

**6** "His thinking became a thing. She appeared before him, in the radiance of his light. She is the power which exists before the All. She is the thought of his thought, the knowledge of his knowledge, the decree of his decree, the power of his power.

**7** "She is the first power which was before all of them, and which came forth from his mind. She is the forethought of the All.

**8** "Her light resembles his light. She is the perfect power which is the image of the invisible, virginal Spirit who is perfect. She is the first power, the glory, Barbelo, the perfect glory in the aeons, the glory of the revelation.

**9** "She glorified the virginal Spirit and praised him, because thanks to him she had come forth. She is the first thought, his image. She became the womb of everything, for she is prior to them all.

**10** "The Father looked into her with the pure light which surrounds the invisible Spirit and his radiance. And she conceived from him, and he produced a spark of light resembling the blessed Light, and it was not equal to his greatness.
