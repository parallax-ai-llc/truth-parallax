## The Risen Savior Appears

The appearance scene challenges Christian readers to consider the boundaries of legitimate post-resurrection tradition. The canonical Gospels present the risen Christ giving brief commands; the Sophia of Jesus Christ presents extended cosmological teaching. The difference reflects fundamentally different understandings of what the resurrection accomplishes: vindication and commissioning versus the unveiling of hidden cosmic knowledge.
