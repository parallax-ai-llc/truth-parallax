## Overview

For Christian readers, the Sophia of Jesus Christ raises important questions about the genre of post-resurrection teaching. The canonical Gospels describe brief resurrection appearances focused on commissioning the disciples (Matthew 28, Luke 24, John 20-21), while Gnostic texts like this one present extended theological instruction by the risen Christ. The question of which traditions about the risen Christ are authentic — and who has the authority to determine this — was central to the struggle between proto-orthodox and Gnostic Christianity.
