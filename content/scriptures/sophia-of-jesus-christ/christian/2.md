## The Nature of the Father

The teaching about the Father engages with Christian theology at a deep level. The Sophia of Jesus Christ's portrait of God as transcending all categories resonates with Christian apophatic theology but pushes it in a direction that undermines the incarnational principle — the claim that God has made himself fully known in the person of Jesus Christ.
