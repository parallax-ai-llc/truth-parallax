## The Emanation of the Aeons

The aeon emanation scheme challenges the Christian doctrine of creation ex nihilo. Where orthodox theology affirms that God freely created the world from nothing, the Gnostic model describes a necessary emanation from the divine source — a difference with profound theological consequences for understanding God's freedom and the world's contingency.
