## The Fall and the Material World

Sophia's fall presents the creation of the material world as a cosmic accident — a view directly contradicting the Genesis affirmation that creation is 'very good' (Genesis 1:31). Christian readers must grapple with the implications of a theology that sees the material world as the product of divine error rather than divine intention.
