## Salvation and Return

The salvation teaching's emphasis on knowledge as the means of redemption contrasts with the Christian emphasis on faith, grace, and the atoning work of Christ. The question of whether salvation comes through knowing or through believing — through gnosis or through pistis — remains one of the deepest theological differences between Gnostic and orthodox Christianity.
