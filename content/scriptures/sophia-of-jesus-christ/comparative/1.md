## The Risen Savior Appears

The post-resurrection appearance of a teacher to assembled disciples parallels the Buddhist tradition of the Buddha's dharma talks, the Bhagavad Gita's battlefield instruction, and the Hermetic dialogues. In each case, cosmic knowledge is transmitted through a privileged teacher-student encounter framed as a decisive revelatory moment.
