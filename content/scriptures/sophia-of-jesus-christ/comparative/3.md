## The Emanation of the Aeons

The emanation of the aeons parallels the Hindu concept of cosmic manifestation (the gunas proceeding from prakriti), the Neoplatonic procession (prohodos), and the Kabbalistic unfolding of the sefirot. Each tradition maps the process by which unity gives rise to multiplicity.
