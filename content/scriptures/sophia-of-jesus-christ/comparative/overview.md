## Overview

The Sophia of Jesus Christ participates in a widespread cross-cultural pattern: a divine teacher reveals cosmic knowledge to prepared disciples through dialogue. The format parallels the Hindu Upanishads (guru-shishya dialogue), the Buddhist sutras (the Buddha instructing disciples), and the Hermetic Corpus (Hermes Trismegistus teaching Asclepius and Tat). The content — the nature of the highest God, the structure of the divine realm, the origin of the material world through a fall — resonates with emanationist and cosmogonic traditions worldwide.
