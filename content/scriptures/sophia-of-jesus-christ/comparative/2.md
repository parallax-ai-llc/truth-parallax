## The Nature of the Father

The teaching about the ineffable Father addresses the universal theological question of how the highest reality can be described without being diminished. The Upanishadic approach (neti neti), the Buddhist via negativa (sunyata), and the Taoist paradox ('the Tao that can be told is not the eternal Tao') all wrestle with the same fundamental challenge.
