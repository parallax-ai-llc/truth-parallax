## The Fall and the Material World

Sophia's fall — divine wisdom overreaching and producing an imperfect world — parallels the Hindu concept of maya (cosmic illusion), the Buddhist analysis of ignorance (avidya) as the root of samsara, and the Kabbalistic account of the breaking of the vessels (shevirat ha-kelim). Each tradition explains the imperfection of the manifest world through some form of cosmic error or excess.
