## Salvation and Return

The promise of return and restoration resonates with the universal longing for home — the intuition that our deepest identity lies beyond the limitations of ordinary existence. Whether expressed as moksha, nirvana, teshuvah, fana, theosis, or return to the Pleroma, the hope for final restoration is one of humanity's most persistent and powerful aspirations.
