## The Risen Savior Appears

The gathering of disciples to receive teaching from a divine master is a scene enacted in every contemplative tradition: the sangha gathered around the Buddha, the students seated at the guru's feet, the Sufi circle around the sheikh. The specifics differ but the structure is universal: divine knowledge flows through a qualified teacher to prepared students.
