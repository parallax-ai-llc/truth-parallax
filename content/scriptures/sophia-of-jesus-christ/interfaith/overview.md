## Overview

The Sophia of Jesus Christ speaks to universal religious concerns: the desire for direct access to divine knowledge, the need to understand why the world is imperfect, and the hope for restoration to an original state of wholeness. The figure of divine Wisdom — known as Sophia in Greek, Hokhmah in Hebrew, Saraswati in Sanskrit, and Prajna in Buddhist traditions — appears across cultures as the personification of the divine intelligence that pervades creation.
