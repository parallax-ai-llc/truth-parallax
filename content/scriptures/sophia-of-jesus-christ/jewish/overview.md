## Overview

Jewish readers encounter in the Sophia of Jesus Christ a radical transformation of the Jewish Wisdom tradition. The figure of Sophia (Hokhmah) — celebrated in Proverbs 8, Wisdom of Solomon, and Sirach as God's companion in creation — is recast as a tragic figure whose error produces the flawed material world. This inversion of the Jewish Wisdom tradition represents one of the most dramatic examples of the Gnostic appropriation and transformation of Jewish theological concepts.
