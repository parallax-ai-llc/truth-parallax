## The Nature of the Father

The teaching about the ineffable Father intensifies the Jewish emphasis on divine transcendence (Ein Sof in later Kabbalah) but severs the connection between transcendence and covenant that is central to rabbinic theology. In Judaism, God is both utterly transcendent and intimately involved with Israel; in this text, the highest God is transcendent only.
