## Salvation and Return

The salvation teaching's focus on return to the Pleroma resonates with the Jewish concept of teshuvah (return) but in a radically different key. Jewish repentance involves returning to God within the framework of covenant and Torah; Gnostic salvation involves escaping the material world entirely and returning to a purely spiritual realm.
