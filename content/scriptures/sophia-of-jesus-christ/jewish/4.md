## The Fall and the Material World

The Sophia fall narrative directly inverts the Jewish celebration of Wisdom's role in creation. In Proverbs 8:22-31, Wisdom is 'beside [God] as a master worker' in creating the world; in this text, Sophia's unauthorized act produces the flawed material cosmos. This inversion represents a fundamental rejection of the Jewish affirmation of creation's goodness.
