## The Risen Savior Appears

The post-resurrection revelation scene has no parallel in Jewish tradition, though the genre of heavenly revelation (1 Enoch, 4 Ezra, 2 Baruch) provides a formal analogue. The Jewish revelation apocalypse typically features an angel or divine voice; the use of the risen Jesus as revealer reflects the Christianized layer of the text.
