## The Emanation of the Aeons

The emanation of aeons transforms Jewish intermediary theology. Where Second Temple Judaism developed the figure of Wisdom as a divine attribute or hypostasis mediating between God and creation (Proverbs 8, Wisdom of Solomon 7), the Sophia of Jesus Christ multiplies intermediaries into an elaborate hierarchy that distances God from the world.
