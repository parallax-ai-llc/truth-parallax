---
title: "Brahma Purana - Chapter 3"
scripture: "brahma-purana"
chapter: 3
---

**1** Brahma said: I shall now describe the lineage of the great kings and sages who governed the world according to dharma.

**2** In the beginning, from Brahma were born the mind-born sons: Marichi, Atri, Angiras, Pulastya, Pulaha, Kratu, and Vasishtha. These seven are the great sages.

**3** From Marichi was born Kashyapa, the father of the gods, the demons, and all living creatures. From Atri was born Soma, the moon god.

**4** From Kashyapa and Aditi were born the twelve Adityas, the solar deities who illuminate the worlds and uphold the cosmic order.

**5** From Kashyapa and Diti were born the Daityas, the mighty demons who waged war against the gods. From Kashyapa and Danu were born the Danavas.

**6** Manu, the son of the Sun, was the first king of men. He established the laws that govern human society and the moral order.

**7** From Manu descended the great solar dynasty, the Suryavamsha, in which were born Ikshvaku, Harishchandra, Sagara, Dilipa, Raghu, Dasharatha, and Rama.

**8** The lunar dynasty, the Chandravamsha, descended from Soma through Budha and Pururavas. In this line were born Nahusha, Yayati, Puru, Bharata, Kuru, Shantanu, and the Pandavas.

**9** These dynasties upheld dharma through countless generations. Their kings performed great sacrifices, protected their subjects, and ruled with justice and mercy.

**10** He who hears the genealogies of these great kings with faith and devotion, who remembers their deeds of valour and righteousness, is freed from all sins and attains long life and prosperity.
