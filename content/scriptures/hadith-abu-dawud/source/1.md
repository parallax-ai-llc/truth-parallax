---
title: "Sunan Abu Dawud - Chapter 1"
scripture: "hadith-abu-dawud"
chapter: 1
---

**1** Narrated Abu Hurairah: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "Were it not that I would place a hardship on my people, I would have commanded them to use the tooth-stick at every prayer."

**2** Narrated Aisha: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "Ten acts are of the natural disposition: trimming the mustache, letting the beard grow, using the tooth-stick, sniffing water into the nose, cutting the nails, washing the finger joints, plucking the armpit hair, shaving the pubic hair, and conserving water." The narrator said: "I have forgotten the tenth, unless it was rinsing the mouth."

**3** Narrated Laqit ibn Sabirah: I said: "O Messenger of Allah, tell me about ablution." He said: "Perform ablution well, wash between your fingers, and when sniffing water into your nose do so deeply unless you are fasting."

**4** Narrated Abu Hurairah: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "When any one of you wakes from sleep, let him not dip his hand into the vessel until he has washed it three times, for he does not know where his hand has spent the night."

**5** Narrated Ali: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "The key to prayer is purification, its opening is the takbir, and its closing is the taslim."

**6** Narrated Ibn Umar: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "Prayer without purification is not accepted, nor is charity from misappropriated wealth."

**7** Narrated Abu Hurairah: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "The prayer of one who breaks his ablution is not accepted until he performs ablution again."

**8** Narrated Abdullah ibn Amr: The Prophet, peace be upon him, passed by Sa'd while he was performing ablution. He said: "What is this extravagance, O Sa'd?" Sa'd replied: "Is there extravagance even in ablution?" He said: "Yes, even if you are by a flowing river."
