---
title: "Sunan Abu Dawud - Chapter 3"
scripture: "hadith-abu-dawud"
chapter: 3
---

**1** Narrated Abu Hurairah: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "When any one of you eats food, let him mention the name of Allah at the beginning. If he forgets to mention the name of Allah at the beginning, let him say: 'In the name of Allah at the beginning and at the end of it.'"

**2** Narrated Umar ibn Abu Salamah: I was a boy under the care of the Messenger of Allah, and my hand used to wander about in the dish. The Messenger of Allah said to me: "O boy, mention the name of Allah, eat with your right hand, and eat from what is near you."

**3** Narrated Jabir: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "Do not eat with the left hand, for Satan eats with his left hand."

**4** Narrated Abu Hurairah: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, never found fault with food. If he desired it, he ate it; if he disliked it, he left it.

**5** Narrated Aisha: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "When any one of you eats, let him mention the name of Allah the Exalted. If he forgets to mention the name of Allah the Exalted at the beginning, let him say: 'In the name of Allah at the beginning and at the end.'"

**6** Narrated Anas: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, never ate at a high table, nor from small plates, nor was flatbread ever baked for him. He was asked: "What did you eat on?" He replied: "On leather mats."

**7** Narrated Abu Hurairah: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "The food of one person is enough for two, the food of two persons is enough for four, and the food of four persons is enough for eight."

**8** Narrated Abu Hurairah: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "It is sufficient sin for a person to waste what he eats."
