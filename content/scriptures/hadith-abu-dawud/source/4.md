---
title: "Sunan Abu Dawud - Chapter 4"
scripture: "hadith-abu-dawud"
chapter: 4
---

**1** Narrated Abu Hurairah: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "The most perfect of believers in faith are those who are best in character, and the best of you are those who are best to their women."

**2** Narrated Abu Darda: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "Nothing is heavier in the balance of the believer on the Day of Resurrection than good character. And Allah dislikes the obscene and vulgar person."

**3** Narrated Aisha: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "Gentleness is not found in anything except that it beautifies it, and it is not removed from anything except that it disgraces it."

**4** Narrated Abu Hurairah: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "A man follows the religion of his friend, so let each one of you look at whom he befriends."

**5** Narrated Abu Hurairah: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "Do not envy one another, do not compete with one another in raising prices, do not hate one another, do not turn away from one another, and do not undercut one another in trade. Be servants of Allah, brothers."

**6** Narrated Mu'adh ibn Jabal: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, took my hand and said: "O Mu'adh, by Allah, I love you. I advise you: do not fail, at the end of every prayer, to say: 'O Allah, help me to remember You, to thank You, and to worship You well.'"

**7** Narrated Abu Hurairah: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "Whoever is asked about knowledge he has and conceals it, will be bridled on the Day of Resurrection with a bridle of fire."

**8** Narrated Abu Hurairah: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "When a person dies, his deeds come to an end except for three: ongoing charity, beneficial knowledge, or a righteous child who prays for him."
