## Overview

An interfaith reading of the Ziji seeks to build bridges of understanding between Bon and other religious communities, fostering mutual respect and appreciation through engagement with sacred texts. Presents a founder-figure with universal compassion and inclusive spiritual vision. This approach emphasizes shared values, complementary insights, and the potential for enriching dialogue.

Interfaith engagement with the Ziji addresses common misconceptions, highlights the depth and sophistication of Bon theological thought, and identifies points of convergence with other traditions' scriptures and teachings. Such readings contribute to a more inclusive and informed understanding of humanity's diverse spiritual heritage.
