## Overview

A comparative approach to the Ziji situates this text within the broader context of world religious literature, identifying parallels, contrasts, and mutual influences across traditions. Invites comparison with Buddha biographies, Christian gospels, and other sacred lives. This comparative perspective enriches understanding both of the Ziji itself and of the religious traditions with which it is brought into dialogue.

Key areas of comparative inquiry include cosmological narratives, theological concepts, ethical frameworks, ritual practices, and soteriological visions. The comparative study of the Ziji contributes to a deeper appreciation of both the universal themes in human religious experience and the distinctive contributions of Bon to the world's spiritual heritage.
