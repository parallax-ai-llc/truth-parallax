---
title: "Ziji Section 12: The Fruition and the Rainbow Body"
scripture: "ziji"
chapter: 12
---

The ultimate fruition of the Bon path is the complete realization of the primordial state, in which the practitioner recognizes beyond any doubt that their own nature has always been identical with the unconditioned base from which all phenomena arise. This recognition is not a temporary experience but a permanent transformation that pervades every moment of the practitioner's remaining life.

The signs of realization manifest progressively as the practitioner's practice deepens. In the initial stages, moments of recognition alternate with periods of distraction, like the sun appearing and disappearing behind clouds. As practice matures, the recognition becomes more stable, and the periods of distraction grow shorter. Eventually, the recognition becomes continuous, and the distinction between meditation and daily life dissolves completely.

The physical body itself is transformed by the depth of realization. Masters who have attained the highest levels of practice may display extraordinary signs such as leaving handprints and footprints in stone, remaining in meditation posture for weeks after the cessation of breath, or emitting rainbows and fragrant scents. These signs are not supernatural miracles but natural manifestations of the profound transformation that occurs when awareness fully recognizes its own nature.

The rainbow body, the jalü, represents the pinnacle of realization. At the time of death, the physical body of a fully realized master dissolves into light over a period of days, leaving behind only the hair and nails. The body, which was always a manifestation of the primordial state, returns to its luminous essence when the last traces of obscuration are purified. This phenomenon has been witnessed and documented many times throughout the history of the Bon tradition.

The teachings of the Ziji conclude with the aspiration that all beings, without exception, may realize the primordial state and achieve the rainbow body. The compassion of the enlightened ones is limitless and impartial, extending to every being in every realm of existence. The path has been laid out, the teachings have been given, the lineage continues unbroken. What remains is for each practitioner to take up the practice with diligence and devotion, trusting in the truth of the teachings and the blessings of the lineage, and walking the path to its ultimate fruition.
