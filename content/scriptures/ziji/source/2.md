---
title: "Ziji Section 2: The Nine Ways of Bon"
scripture: "ziji"
chapter: 2
---

The Nine Ways of Bon encompass the complete range of teachings and practices through which beings can progress from ignorance to enlightenment. These nine ways are arranged in ascending order of subtlety, from the most external and ritualistic practices to the most internal and contemplative.

The first four ways are called the Causal Ways and deal primarily with the relationship between beings and the natural world. The Way of the Shen of Prediction teaches divination, astrology, and the reading of omens. Through these practices, the practitioner learns to perceive the interconnected patterns of cause and effect that govern worldly affairs. The Way of the Shen of the Visual World teaches the rituals for communicating with the various classes of beings that inhabit the visible and invisible worlds.

The Way of the Shen of Illusion teaches the methods for dispelling harmful influences, healing diseases caused by spirit interference, and performing the rituals that maintain harmony between the human world and the realms of spirits. The Way of the Shen of Existence teaches the funerary rites and practices related to the journey of consciousness after death, guiding the deceased through the intermediate states and toward favorable rebirth.

The fifth through eighth ways are called the Ways of the Fruit. These include the Way of the Virtuous Lay Practitioners, who follow ethical guidelines similar to those of monastic codes. The Way of the Monks introduces full monastic discipline and the study of philosophical texts. The Way of Primordial Sound teaches the use of mantra, visualization, and subtle body practices. The Way of the Primordial Shen teaches the higher tantric practices involving deity yoga and the manipulation of subtle energies.

The ninth and highest way is Dzogchen, the Great Perfection, which transcends all graduated approaches and directly introduces the practitioner to the primordial state described in the first chapter of this text.
