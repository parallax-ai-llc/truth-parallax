## Overview

The Bon reading of the Ziji engages with this text from within the living tradition of Bon thought and practice. The most authoritative and extensive biography of Bon's founder. This perspective brings the accumulated wisdom of Bon interpretation to bear on the text, illuminating dimensions that may not be apparent from external scholarly perspectives alone.

Within the Bon tradition, the Ziji is understood not merely as a historical document but as a living source of spiritual guidance and theological reflection. Bon interpreters bring distinctive hermeneutical principles, doctrinal frameworks, and experiential knowledge to their engagement with this text, producing readings that are both deeply informed and spiritually engaged.
