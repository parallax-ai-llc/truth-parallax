Chapter 3 of the Ziji, addressing education, holds particular significance within the Bon tradition. The themes of learning, mastery are central to Bon theology and practice, and this chapter has been a focus of traditional interpretation and spiritual reflection.

Within Bon tradition, this chapter's treatment of education is understood not merely as historical or theoretical but as a living source of guidance for faith and practice. The teachings on learning, mastery inform Bon self-understanding and continue to shape communal identity and spiritual aspiration.
