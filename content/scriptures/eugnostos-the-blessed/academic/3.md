## The Six Spiritual Beings

The six spiritual beings have been analyzed for their relationship to the aeon systems described by Irenaeus and other heresiologists. Scholars note both similarities and significant differences, suggesting that Eugnostos represents either an earlier or an independent stage of Gnostic cosmological development, not simply a summary of Valentinian theology.
