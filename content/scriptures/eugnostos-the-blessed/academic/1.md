## The Ineffable One and the First Father

The opening cosmological framework — an Ineffable God generating a First Father through emanation — has been compared to Middle Platonic theology (Numenius, Alcinous) and to Jewish speculation about divine intermediaries. Scholars debate whether the text's cosmology is fundamentally Platonic, Jewish, or represents an independent Gnostic development. The systematic quality of the presentation suggests a sophisticated philosophical milieu.
