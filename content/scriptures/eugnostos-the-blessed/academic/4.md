## The Aeons and Their Realms

The structure of the aeons has been compared to Valentinian Pleroma theology, Sethian cosmology, and Middle Platonic emanation schemes. The precise relationship between Eugnostos's system and those described in other Gnostic texts remains debated, with some scholars seeing a common source and others positing independent development.
