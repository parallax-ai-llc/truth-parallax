## The Androgynous Being

The concept of divine androgyny has attracted extensive scholarly discussion. Parrott connects it to Platonic traditions about the original androgynous human (Aristophanes' speech in the Symposium), while others see Jewish influences (the rabbinic tradition that Adam was originally androgynous, Genesis Rabbah 8:1). The concept functions differently in Eugnostos than in either tradition.
