## The Ineffable One and the First Father

The concept of an ineffable divine source speaks to a universal religious intuition: the deepest reality exceeds all human categories. The Taoist sage, the Jewish mystic, the Sufi poet, and the Buddhist philosopher all recognize that the highest truth cannot be captured in words. This shared recognition creates a meeting point for interfaith dialogue at the deepest level.
