## The Six Spiritual Beings

The emanation of multiple spiritual beings from a single source addresses the mystery of the one and the many — how unity gives rise to diversity without being diminished. This question is central to Hindu philosophy (Brahman and the multiplicity of forms), Buddhist thought (emptiness and dependent origination), and Neoplatonism (the One and its emanations).
