## Overview

Eugnostos the Blessed addresses questions that arise in every religious tradition: What is the ultimate nature of reality? How does the multiplicity of the world arise from the unity of the divine? What is the place of humanity in the cosmic order? The text's answers — an ineffable God beyond all categories, a divine realm unfolding through emanation, and humanity as a reflection of the divine — resonate with contemplative and philosophical traditions worldwide, from Neoplatonism to Vedanta to Kabbalah.
