## The Androgynous Being

The androgynous divine being challenges the tendency in many traditions to identify the divine with one gender. The insight that ultimate reality transcends gender — encompassing and surpassing both masculine and feminine — is shared by Hindu Shakta traditions, Taoist yin-yang philosophy, and the via negativa of Christian mysticism.
