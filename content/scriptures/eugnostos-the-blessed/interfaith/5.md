## Humanity and the Divine Order

The vision of humanity as a reflection of the divine order affirms human dignity in a way that resonates across traditions. Whether expressed as the Jewish tzelem Elohim, the Islamic khalifah (vicegerent), the Hindu divine spark, or the Buddhist Buddha-nature, the conviction that humanity bears a relationship to the ultimate ground of reality is one of the most widespread and consequential ideas in human thought.
