## The Aeons and Their Realms

The structured divine realm provides a map of reality that, while specific to the Gnostic tradition, parallels the cosmological maps of other traditions: the Buddhist cosmology of multiple worlds, the Hindu lokas, the Kabbalistic Tree of Life, and the Islamic seven heavens. Each tradition maps the layers of reality through which the seeker must pass.
