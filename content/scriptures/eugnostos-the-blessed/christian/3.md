## The Six Spiritual Beings

The emanation of spiritual beings raises questions about the Christian doctrine of creation. The emanationist model — in which beings proceed necessarily from the divine source — differs from the orthodox Christian teaching that God freely chose to create from nothing (ex nihilo). The distinction between emanation and creation has been a major point of contention between Christian theology and Gnostic/Neoplatonic thought.
