## The Androgynous Being

The androgynous divine being challenges Christian trinitarian theology, which describes the divine as three persons rather than as a masculine-feminine dyad. The question of gender in the divine — whether God is beyond gender, whether feminine imagery is appropriate for God — has been a recurring issue in Christian theology.
