## The Aeons and Their Realms

The structured divine realm of the aeons stands in contrast to the relative simplicity of orthodox Christian cosmology. Christian theology typically posits a single God creating a single world, with angels as created servants. Eugnostos presents a far more complex divine hierarchy that challenges the Christian emphasis on divine simplicity.
