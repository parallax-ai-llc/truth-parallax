## The Aeons and Their Realms

The architecture of the aeons mirrors the Kabbalistic Tree of Life with its ten sefirot, the Buddhist Pure Lands, and the Neoplatonic hierarchy of hypostases. Each tradition maps the divine realm as a structured, multi-leveled reality through which the practitioner can ascend toward the source.
