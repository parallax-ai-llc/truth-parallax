## The Androgynous Being

The androgynous divine being resonates with Hindu conceptions of Ardhanarishvara (the half-male, half-female form of Shiva), the Tao's unity of yin and yang, and the Kabbalistic ideal of the restored union of Tiferet and Shekhinah. The recurring appearance of divine androgyny across cultures suggests a widespread intuition that ultimate reality transcends binary gender distinctions.
