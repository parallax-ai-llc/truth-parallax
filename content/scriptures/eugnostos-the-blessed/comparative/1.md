## The Ineffable One and the First Father

The concept of an Ineffable God beyond all categories parallels the Upanishadic 'neti neti' (not this, not this), the Buddhist rejection of conceptual elaboration regarding ultimate reality, and the Taoist recognition that 'the Tao that can be told is not the eternal Tao.' Across cultures, the highest reality exceeds the capacity of language and thought.
