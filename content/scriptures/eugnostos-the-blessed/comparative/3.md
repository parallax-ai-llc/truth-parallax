## The Six Spiritual Beings

The proliferation of spiritual beings from the divine source parallels the Hindu concept of the devas emanating from Brahman, the Buddhist cosmology of celestial bodhisattvas, and the Neoplatonic hierarchy of intelligences. The pattern of unity-in-diversity — many beings sharing one divine nature — appears to be a structural feature of emanationist cosmologies worldwide.
