**1.** Eugnostos, the Blessed, to those who are his. Rejoice in this, that you know. Greetings. I want you to know that all people born from the foundation of the world until now are dust. They have inquired about God—who he is and what he is like. They have not found him.

The wisest among them have speculated from the ordering of the world and its movement. But their speculation has not reached the truth. For the ordering is spoken of in three opinions by all the philosophers, and hence they do not agree with one another. Some of them say that the world is directed by itself. Others, that it is providence that directs it. Others, that it is fate. But it is none of these. Again, of the three voices I have just described, none is close to the truth, and they are from human speculation.

But I, who have come from the Infinite Light—I am here, for I know the truth. For the truth that is from the Immeasurable is not known through human speculation, but through the revelation of the Unbegotten Father.
