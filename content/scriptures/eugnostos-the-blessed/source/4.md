**4.** The First Begotten Son, the Immortal Man, brought forth from himself a great androgynous being. His masculine name is called "First-Begetter Son of God"; his feminine name is called "First-Begettress Sophia, Mother of the Universe." Some call her "Love."

Now, the First-Begotten called "Christ" has his own authority, and he created a multitude of angels without number to serve and to minister. If you wish to know the nature of these authorities—it is revealed in the administration of the Immortal Man.

From the Immortal Man came forth a first knowledge. Those who received knowledge from the power of the light became luminaries. They established themselves in the imperishable Aeons. They were not counted among any ruler, for they had gone forth from the One who is above every ruler.

And they knew that the One who brought them forth was incomprehensible. They worshipped him in truth and in the silence of their minds. For the Unbegotten Father revealed himself to them in his light, and they beheld him. And in beholding him, they became imperishable like him.
