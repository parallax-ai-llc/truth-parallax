**5.** Thus, all the Aeons and their orders are established. The twelve Aeons belong to the Son of the Immortal Man. The will of the Father brought forth everything. The All was revealed.

The Aeons that came into being from the Unbegotten Father have life eternal, for they came forth from the Imperishable. The Aeons that came into being from the Perishable have death, for they came forth from the Perishable.

Now, whoever knows the Father in pure knowledge will depart to the Father and repose in the Unbegotten Father. But whoever does not know him will depart to the deficiency and the ignorance. For what does the ignorant person have? The answer is: ignorance.

Whoever knows the truth and the Aeons and the powers will pass through them all and ascend to the Unbegotten Father, and the Unbegotten Father will receive that person.

This is the knowledge of the Blessed Eugnostos. The things that have been hidden from the ages have been revealed. The truth has been proclaimed. Let the one who has ears hear. And let the one who knows himself rejoice.

Eugnostos the Blessed.
