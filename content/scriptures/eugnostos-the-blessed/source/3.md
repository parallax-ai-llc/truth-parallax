**3.** Before anything is visible among those that are visible, the majesty and the authority are in him, since he embraces the whole of the totalities, while nothing embraces him. For he is all mind, thought, reflecting, considering, rationality, and power.

They are all equal powers. They are the sources of the totalities. And their whole race, from first to last, is in the Foreknowledge of the Unbegotten Father, for they had not yet come to visibility.

Now a difference existed among the imperishable Aeons. Let us speak of it in this way. Everything that came from the Perishable will perish, since it came from the Perishable. Whatever came from the Imperishable does not perish but becomes imperishable, since it came from the Imperishable.

So then, many people went astray because they did not know this difference. That is, they did not know that the imperishable Aeons come from the Unbegotten Father, and that the perishable things come from the one who was fashioned—the ruler of the material world.

The Unbegotten Father brought forth from himself the First Begotten—Immortal Man, whom they call "First Adam." He gave him a power, and he ruled over the first creation.
