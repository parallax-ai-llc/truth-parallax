## The Six Spiritual Beings

The proliferation of spiritual beings from a divine source has parallels in the Jewish angelological traditions of 1 Enoch, 2 Enoch, and the later Hekhalot literature. The question of whether Gnostic aeon-speculation derived from Jewish angel-speculation or developed independently remains debated in scholarship.
