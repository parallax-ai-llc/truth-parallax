## The Aeons and Their Realms

The architecture of the divine realm parallels later Kabbalistic descriptions of the sefirot and the divine world. Some scholars (Gershom Scholem, Moshe Idel) have argued for a genetic connection between Gnostic cosmology and Jewish mystical speculation, while others see the parallels as the result of shared Platonic influences.
