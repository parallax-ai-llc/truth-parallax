## The Androgynous Being

The androgynous divine being has a significant Jewish background. Genesis Rabbah 8:1 reports the tradition that the first Adam was created androgynous, and Philo discusses the ideal human as an androgynous Platonic form. Eugnostos may draw on such traditions while developing them within a cosmological framework foreign to rabbinic Judaism.
