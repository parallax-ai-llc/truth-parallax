## The Aeons and Their Realms

The aeons that emanate from the spiritual beings constitute the broader architecture of the divine realm. Each aeon is a sphere of divine activity and knowledge, populated by angelic beings and pervaded by divine light. The Gnostic reader maps the structure of the Pleroma as a guide to spiritual ascent: to know the aeons is to know the path by which the fallen soul can return to its source.
