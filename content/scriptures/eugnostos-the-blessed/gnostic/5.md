## Humanity and the Divine Order

The final section situates humanity within the divine order. The Gnostic reader understands that humanity is not a random product of material creation but a reflection — however distorted — of the divine androgynous beings above. The human soul contains a spark of the divine light, and the purpose of gnosis is to recognize this spark and trace it back to its source in the Pleroma.
