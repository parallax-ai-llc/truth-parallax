## The Androgynous Being

The androgynous being who proceeds from the First Father embodies the Gnostic ideal of original unity. Before the fall into matter, the divine reality is both masculine and feminine in perfect harmony. This androgyny is not a mixing of genders but a transcendence of the gender division that characterizes material existence. The Gnostic reader sees in this figure a model of the spiritual wholeness to which the enlightened soul aspires to return.
