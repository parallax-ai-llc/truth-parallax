## The Six Spiritual Beings

The six spiritual beings populate the divine realm with differentiated but harmonious entities. Each being is itself androgynous, generating further emanations. The Gnostic reader understands this proliferation not as fragmentation but as the divine plenitude expressing itself in ever-greater richness. The Pleroma is full precisely because it contains infinite differentiation within absolute unity.
