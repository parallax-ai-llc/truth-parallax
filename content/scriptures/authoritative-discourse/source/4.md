**4.** Therefore, let us be vigilant. Let us put on the armor of the Logos. Let us prepare for the struggle. For great is the contest, and the stakes are eternal.

We must become merchants of the soul, exchanging the things that perish for the things that endure. Let us trade ignorance for knowledge, desire for self-control, fear for courage, pride for humility.

The spiritual nourishment is the word of truth. Those who eat it shall not hunger. Those who drink from the spring of the Logos shall not thirst. For the food of every soul is the Logos of God, and the drink is the knowledge of the Father.

This is the Authoritative Discourse. The soul who receives it shall live. She shall put off the body of ignorance and put on the garment of light. She shall enter the bridal chamber and receive the kiss of the Bridegroom.

And the Bridegroom shall say to her, "Come, blessed one. Enter into the rest that has been prepared for you from the foundation of the world." And the soul shall rejoice, and she shall dwell in the light forever, and no darkness shall touch her again.

The Authoritative Discourse is finished.
