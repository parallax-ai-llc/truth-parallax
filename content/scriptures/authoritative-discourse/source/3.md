**3.** But those souls who do not receive the word of truth—they are led astray by the adversaries. They are dragged down into the abyss. They are like sheep without a shepherd, wandering in the desert. They are like a boat without a helmsman, tossed upon the waves.

These are the ones who follow the pleasures of the body. They love the things that perish. They heap up treasures that the moth consumes and the rust destroys. They seek honor among men, not knowing that the honor of men is shame before God.

The material soul feeds on every lawlessness and every evil. She enjoys the things that are transient. She does not know that within her she has a helper—the rational soul—who has been given to her by the Father.

The adversaries rejoice over such a soul. They say, "We have caught her! She is ours!" They bind her with chains of forgetfulness. They clothe her in ignorance. They make her drunk with the wine of deception, so that she does not remember the place from which she came.

But the Father does not cease to call her. He sends his Logos again and again, until the soul turns and hears and remembers.
