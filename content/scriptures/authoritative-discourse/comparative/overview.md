## Overview

The Authoritative Discourse's metaphor of the soul navigating a marketplace of deceptions resonates with traditions worldwide that describe the spiritual life as a journey through a world of illusions. The Buddhist concept of samsara as a realm of attachment and craving, the Hindu concept of maya (cosmic illusion), the Taoist distinction between the true Way and false paths, and the Sufi concept of the nafs (lower self) deceived by worldly attachments all describe the same fundamental challenge: how to distinguish what is genuinely valuable from what merely appears to be so.
