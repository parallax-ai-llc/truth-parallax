## Islamic Reading

The Islamic engagement with The Valley of Wonderment examines Bahá'u'lláh's treatment of wonderment, awe, bewilderment, divine mystery within the broader context of Islamic mystical scholarship and contemplative practice. This valley describes a stage of the soul's transformative journey toward the divine, offering material for sustained Islamic reflection and spiritual deepening.
