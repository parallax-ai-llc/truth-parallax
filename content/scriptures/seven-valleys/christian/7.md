The Christian engagement with The Valley of True Poverty and Absolute Nothingness examines Bahá'u'lláh's treatment of poverty, nothingness, fana, annihilation of self, God alone within the broader context of Christian mystical scholarship and contemplative practice. This valley describes a stage of the soul's transformative journey toward the divine, offering material for sustained Christian reflection and spiritual deepening.
