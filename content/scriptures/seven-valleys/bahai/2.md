## Bahá'í Reading

The Bahá'í engagement with The Valley of Love examines Bahá'u'lláh's treatment of love, passion, longing, divine beloved, surrender within the broader context of Bahá'í mystical scholarship and contemplative practice. This valley describes a stage of the soul's transformative journey toward the divine, offering material for sustained Bahá'í reflection and spiritual deepening.
