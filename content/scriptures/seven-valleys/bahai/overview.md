## Overview

The Bahá'í reading of the Seven Valleys engages with Bahá'u'lláh's mystical treatise from the distinctive vantage point of the Bahá'í tradition. This perspective brings particular attention to the text's model of spiritual stages, its Sufi literary heritage, its vision of the soul's transformation through divine love, and its culminating teaching of self-annihilation and subsistence in God. The Bahá'í interpretation illuminates both the inner mystical logic of the Seven Valleys and its resonances with contemplative traditions worldwide.
