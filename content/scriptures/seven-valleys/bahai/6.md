## Bahá'í Reading

The Bahá'í engagement with The Valley of Wonderment examines Bahá'u'lláh's treatment of wonderment, awe, bewilderment, divine mystery within the broader context of Bahá'í mystical scholarship and contemplative practice. This valley describes a stage of the soul's transformative journey toward the divine, offering material for sustained Bahá'í reflection and spiritual deepening.
