## Interfaith Reading

The Interfaith engagement with The Valley of Knowledge examines Bahá'u'lláh's treatment of knowledge, inner meaning, wisdom, discernment within the broader context of Interfaith mystical scholarship and contemplative practice. This valley describes a stage of the soul's transformative journey toward the divine, offering material for sustained Interfaith reflection and spiritual deepening.
