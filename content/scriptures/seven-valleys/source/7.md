---
title: "Seven Valleys - Chapter 7"
scripture: "seven-valleys"
chapter: 7
---

**1** These are the attributes of the exalted, and constitute the stations of the holy. Should all the servants read and meditate upon these words, there shall be kindled in their hearts an eager flame and a mighty fire.

**2** In this journey the traveler abideth in every land and dwelleth in every region. In every face, he seeketh the beauty of the Friend; in every country he looketh for the Beloved.

**3** He joineth every company of lovers, and he seeketh every assembly of insight. He looketh for the secret with the keenest sight, and searcheth for the Beloved with the utmost effort.

**4** And if by the help of the Creator, he findeth on this journey a trace of the traceless Friend, he will step into the Valley of Love and be dissolved in the fire of love.

**5** In this city, even the veils of light are split asunder and pass away. "His beauty hath no veiling save light, His face no covering save revelation."

**6** How strange that while the Beloved is visible as the sun, yet the heedless still hunt after tinsel and base metal. Yea, the intensity of His revelation hath covered Him, and the fullness of His shining forth hath hidden Him.

**7** Peace be upon him who followeth the Right Path! And praise be to God, the Lord of the worlds!

**8** The end of these Seven Valleys is here attained. From the exalted source, in the land of the immortal, floweth the stream of the divine decree.

**9** May the travellers reach the Desired One; may the seekers attain the object of their quest; may the lovers arrive at the abode of the Beloved.

**10** Praise be to God, the Lord of all worlds! May the peace and blessings of God rest upon the holy ones, from first to last.
