---
title: "Seven Valleys - Chapter 2"
scripture: "seven-valleys"
chapter: 2
---

**1** And if, confirmed by the Creator, the lover escapes from the claws of the eagle of love, he will enter the Valley of Knowledge and come out of doubt into certitude, and turn from the darkness of idle fancy to the guiding light of the fear of God.

**2** In this Valley, the wayfarer leaveth behind him the stages of the oneness of being and of reaching to God, and reacheth a dwelling-place that hath no beginning and no end.

**3** After passing through the Valley of Knowledge, which is the last plane of limitation, the wayfarer cometh to the Valley of Unity and drinketh from the cup of the Absolute, and gazeth on the Manifestations of Oneness.

**4** In this Valley, he pierceth the veils of plurality, fleeth from the worlds of the flesh, and ascendeth to the heaven of singleness. With inward and outward eyes he witnesseth the mysteries of return.

**5** He looketh on all things with the eye of oneness, and seeth the brilliant rays of the divine sun shining from the dawning-point of Essence alike on all created things, and the lights of singleness reflected over all creation.

**6** He perceiveth that all things are of one essence. All these differences are in appearance, not in reality.

**7** They who soar in the heaven of singleness and reach to the sea of the Absolute, count this city — the dwelling of the human condition — as nothing.

**8** In this station he is content with the decree of God, and seeth war as peace, and findeth in death the secrets of everlasting life.

**9** With inward and outward eyes he witnesseth the mysteries of return in the realms of God, and with a pure heart apprehendeth the divine wisdom in the endless Manifestations of God.

**10** He passeth beyond this station and cometh to the Valley of Contentment, where from the breezes of true contentment blowing from the spirit, he perceiveth the scent of God.
