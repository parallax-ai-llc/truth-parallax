---
title: "Seven Valleys - Chapter 6"
scripture: "seven-valleys"
chapter: 6
---

**1** These journeys have no visible ending in the world of time, but the severed wayfarer — if invisible confirmation descend upon him and the Guardian of the Cause assist him — may cross these seven stages in seven breaths, nay rather in seven instants.

**2** And this is dependent on the grace of God. They who soar to the heaven of singleness and reach to the sea of the Absolute, count this city — which is the dwelling of the human condition — as nothing.

**3** "There is no knowledge for him whose heart hath not been severed," and the condition of severance from all things is expressed thus: "If ye be not severed, go!"

**4** From this station the wayfarer entereth the Valley of Knowledge. In this Valley he seeth in the fire the fire-worshipper, and in the fire he findeth the mystery of the Love.

**5** O My Brother! Until thou enter the Egypt of love, thou shalt never come to the Joseph of the Beauty of the Friend; and until, like Jacob, thou forsake thine outward eyes, thou shalt never open the eye of thine inward being.

**6** Until thou burnest with the fire of love, thou shalt never attain the communion of the Beloved. Until thou yield thy life, thou shalt never be granted eternal life.

**7** Many a head hath been raised on the gallows of love, and many a heart consumed in the fire of longing. Yet none who is a true lover would flee from the Beloved.

**8** A lover feareth nothing and no harm can come nigh him: thou seest him chill in the fire and dry in the sea.

**9** O friend, give up thy self, that thou mayest find the Peerless One. Pass by this mortal earth, that thou mayest seek a home in the nest of heaven.

**10** Be as naught, if thou wouldst kindle the fire of being and be fit for the pathway of love.
