## Overview

Interfaith engagement with the Shahnameh brings distinctive interpretive methods and concerns to this foundational text. This approach illuminates dimensions of the text that may be less visible from other perspectives.

## Key Interpretive Approaches

### Dialogue and Understanding
Interfaith interpreters approach the Shahnameh as a text that can foster mutual understanding between religious traditions, identifying shared values and concerns that transcend confessional boundaries.

### Universal Themes
The Shahnameh addresses fundamental human questions — meaning, suffering, justice, transcendence — that resonate across religious and cultural boundaries.

### Respectful Engagement
Interfaith reading emphasizes approaching the Shahnameh on its own terms, seeking to understand how it functions within its native tradition before drawing broader connections.
