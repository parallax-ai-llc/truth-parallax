---
title: "Shahnameh - Chapter 1"
scripture: "shahnameh"
chapter: 1
---

**1** In the Name of the Lord of Life and Wisdom, beyond whose power thought cannot soar.

**2** The Lord of all things named and nameless, the Lord of life and intellect, the Lord of all the provinces of heaven, who sustains the rolling firmament.

**3** He first created mind; and from mind shone forth the adornment of the world. He raised the sky, and spread the waters, and the earth He fixed in its appointed place.

**4** Then the rising and the setting of the sun began. The head of Aries was exalted, and the earth put on fresh garments of green.

**5** Three bright beings appeared upon the earth: the sun, moon, and fire. Wood sprouted from the ground, and growing things covered the world.

**6** First of the world-kings was Kayumars, who dwelt in the mountains. He wore a leopard skin and from his throne of power he spread justice among all creatures.

**7** From him the arts of living were learned by all. Men gathered beneath his rule and knew the difference between right and wrong.

**8** But Ahriman, the evil one, watched with envious eyes. He sent his son, the Black Demon, to make war upon the good.

**9** Siyamak, the son of Kayumars, went forth to battle. The demons met him in combat, and the young prince fell.

**10** Kayumars wept for his son, and all the creatures of the world mourned with him. For a year he grieved, and then he raised an army and struck down the Black Demon, avenging the blood of Siyamak.
