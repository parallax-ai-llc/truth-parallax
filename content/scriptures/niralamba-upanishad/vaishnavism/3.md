Section 3 presents teachings on the self-dependent truth that Vaishnava commentators read through the lens of devotion to Vishnu-Narayana. The Upanishad's philosophical insights are understood as revealing aspects of the Lord's nature and His relationship to the individual soul and the created world.

Vaishnava interpreters emphasize that the Niralamba Upanishad's teachings in this section ultimately point to the necessity of divine grace for liberation. The philosophical knowledge presented here purifies the mind and prepares the devotee for the direct experience of God's presence.
