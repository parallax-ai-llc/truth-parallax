## The Reality of the Resurrection

The opening section's address to a student about whether life persists beyond death touches the most fundamental human concern. Comparative analysis reveals parallels in the Egyptian Book of the Dead, the Tibetan Bardo Thodol, and the Upanishadic teaching on the immortality of the atman. The Valentinian framework reframes the question: not 'Will I survive death?' but 'Am I already beyond death?' — paralleling Zen Buddhism's insistence on present-moment awakening.
