## The Nature of the Spiritual Body

The discussion of the spiritual body invites comparison with the Vedantic distinction between gross body and subtle body, the Buddhist analysis of form and consciousness, and the Neoplatonic concept of the soul's vehicle (*ochema*). The ray-of-light metaphor finds parallels in Sufi poetry (Rumi), Hindu philosophy (atman-Brahman), and Neoplatonism (the One's emanation). The claim that 'the world is an illusion' directly parallels Shankara's Advaita Vedanta.
