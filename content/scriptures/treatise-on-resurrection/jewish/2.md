## The Nature of the Spiritual Body

The use of Platonic imagery reflects the deep interpenetration of Jewish and Greek thought in the Hellenistic period. Philo of Alexandria represents the most sophisticated Jewish engagement with Platonism, and his allegorical interpretation shares some features with the Treatise's approach. The tension between body-affirming and body-denying tendencies in Jewish thought was a live issue in the first and second centuries.
