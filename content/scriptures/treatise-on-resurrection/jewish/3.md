## Exhortation and Practice

The emphasis on restricting knowledge to the worthy has parallels in rabbinic restrictions on teaching ma'aseh bereshit and ma'aseh merkavah (Mishnah Hagigah 2:1). However, the Valentinian esotericism differs fundamentally: the rabbis restricted specific mystical traditions while maintaining that Torah was given publicly to all Israel. The Valentinian restriction involves the entire soteriological message.
