## The Nature of the Spiritual Body

The interpretation of Pauline categories represents a challenge because it demonstrates the genuine ambiguity of Paul's language. When Paul writes of the 'spiritual body,' does he mean transformed matter or non-material reality? The proto-orthodox and Valentinian traditions answered differently. Eastern Orthodox theology's understanding of theosis (deification) shares the Treatise's emphasis on present participation in divine life, while still affirming future bodily resurrection.
