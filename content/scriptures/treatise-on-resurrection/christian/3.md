## Exhortation and Practice

The final section raises questions about the relationship between faith and knowledge. The Treatise identifies certainty with gnosis rather than pistis (faith) as understood in the Pauline tradition. The instruction to restrict the teaching challenges the Christian commitment to universal proclamation, though the canonical tradition also recognizes degrees of readiness (1 Corinthians 3:1-2, Hebrews 5:12-14).
