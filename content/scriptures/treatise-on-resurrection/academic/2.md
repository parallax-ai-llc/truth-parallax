## The Nature of the Spiritual Body

The second section's engagement with Pauline categories provides important evidence for the reception history of 1 Corinthians 15. Paul's distinction between psychikon and pneumatikon soma was interpreted differently by different communities: the proto-orthodox emphasized continuity between present and resurrection body, while Valentinians emphasized discontinuity. The ray-of-light metaphor has parallels in both Valentinian literature and Middle Platonic discussions of emanation.
