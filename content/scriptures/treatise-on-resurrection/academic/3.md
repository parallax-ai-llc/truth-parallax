## Exhortation and Practice

The final section's shift to parenesis follows standard epistolary conventions. The instruction to restrict the teaching to the worthy reflects the esoteric character of Valentinian Christianity. Scholars debate the date (consensus: second half of second century), provenance (possibly Alexandria or Syria), and whether the Coptic version preserves the Greek original faithfully.
