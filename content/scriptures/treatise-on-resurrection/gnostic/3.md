## Exhortation and Practice

The concluding exhortation urges Rheginos to live in the certainty of resurrection already accomplished. The Gnostic reading emphasizes the transformative power of gnosis: once one truly knows that resurrection is a present reality, fear of death dissolves and attachment to the material world loosens. The instruction to share the teaching selectively reflects the esoteric character of Gnostic knowledge.
