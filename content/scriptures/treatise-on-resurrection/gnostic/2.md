## The Nature of the Spiritual Body

The Valentinian understanding of the spiritual body draws on Paul's language in 1 Corinthians 15 but interprets it distinctly: the 'spiritual body' is not a future transformation of flesh but the already-existing spiritual reality concealed within material existence. The metaphor of the ray of light — distinct from yet sharing the nature of the sun — describes the relationship between the individual and the Pleroma. The teacher declares: 'the world is an illusion, but the resurrection is truth.'
