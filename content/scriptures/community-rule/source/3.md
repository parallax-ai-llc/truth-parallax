---
title: "Community Rule Section 3"
scripture: "community-rule"
chapter: 3
---

**3:1** No man who refuses to enter the Covenant of God and walks in the stubbornness of his heart shall be admitted to the Community of His truth. For his soul has loathed the disciplines of knowledge and righteous judgment.

**3:4** He shall not be reckoned among the upright. He cannot be purified by atonement, nor cleansed by purifying waters, nor sanctified by seas and rivers, nor washed clean with any ablution.

**3:6** Unclean, unclean shall he be. For as long as he despises the precepts of God he shall receive no instruction in the Community of His counsel.

**3:9** For it is through the spirit of God's true counsel concerning the ways of man that all his sins are atoned, so that he may gaze upon the light of life. It is through the Holy Spirit of the Community, in its truth, that he is cleansed of all his sins.

**3:13** The Instructor shall instruct and teach all the sons of light concerning the nature of all the children of men, with regard to all the varieties of their spirits according to their signs, concerning their deeds in their generations, and concerning the visitation of their afflictions together with their periods of peace.

**3:15** From the God of knowledge comes all that is and shall be. Before ever they existed He established their whole design, and when, as ordained for them, they come into being, it is in accord with His glorious design that they accomplish their task without change.

**3:17** He has created man to govern the world, and has appointed for him two spirits in which to walk until the time of His visitation: the spirits of truth and injustice.

**3:19** Those born of truth spring from a fountain of light, but those born of injustice spring from a source of darkness. All the children of righteousness are ruled by the Prince of Light and walk in the ways of light, but all the children of injustice are ruled by the Angel of Darkness and walk in the ways of darkness.

**3:24** The Angel of Darkness leads all the children of righteousness astray, and until his end, all their sin, iniquities, wickedness, and all their unlawful deeds are caused by his dominion.
