---
title: "Community Rule Section 1"
scripture: "community-rule"
chapter: 1
---

**1:1** For the Instructor, the Book of the Rule of the Community. He shall seek to do the will of God in accordance with all that has been revealed for each period of time.

**1:2** He shall learn all the wisdom found according to the times, and the statute of the time.

**1:3** He shall separate and weigh the sons of righteousness according to their spirits. He shall hold firmly to the chosen of the time according to His will, as He has commanded.

**1:4** He shall judge every man according to his spirit. He shall admit him according to the cleanness of his hands and advance him according to his understanding.

**1:5** And thus shall his love and his hatred be. He shall not reprove or argue with the men of the pit, but shall conceal the counsel of the Law among the men of injustice.

**1:7** He shall transmit true knowledge and righteous judgment to those who choose the Way, each according to his spirit, according to the rule of time.

**1:8** He shall guide them with knowledge and instruct them in the mysteries of wonder and truth among the members of the Community, so that they may walk blamelessly with one another in all that has been revealed to them.

**1:9** That is the time for clearing the way to the wilderness. He shall instruct them in all that is found to be done at this time, and to be separate from every man who has not turned his way from all injustice.

**1:11** These are the rules of the Way for the Instructor in these times, concerning his loving and his hating: everlasting hatred for the men of the pit in a spirit of concealment.

**1:13** He shall leave to them wealth and wages, like a servant to his master, and like the oppressed before his ruler.

**1:16** He shall be a man zealous for the statute and its time, for the Day of Vengeance. He shall do the will of God in all his undertakings, and in all that comes under his power he shall delight in the will of God.
