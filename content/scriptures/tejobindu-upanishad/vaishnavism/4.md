Section 4 systematically negates the reality of the phenomenal world — a teaching that Vaishnava commentators interpret carefully. The negation is not nihilistic but devotional: the world is negated not as absolutely non-existent but as having no reality independent of the Lord. When compared to God's infinite radiance, the world is as nothing. This devotional negation focuses the mind entirely on God.

The Vaishnava reading preserves the reality of the world as God's creation while acknowledging its complete dependence on and subordination to the divine. The world is not false — it is real as God's energy — but it is not ultimate.
