Section 5 describes the *jivanmukta* — the one liberated while still alive — as seen through the Vaishnava lens. The liberated soul dwells in constant awareness of Vishnu's presence, acting in the world without attachment because every action is an offering to God. The jivanmukta is not someone who has transcended the world but someone who sees God in everything.

The Vaishnava jivanmukta retains individuality and continues to serve God and His creation with love, free from the ego's claims but not from the heart's devotion.
