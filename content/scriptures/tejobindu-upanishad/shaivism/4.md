Section 4's systematic negation of phenomenal reality aligns with the Shaiva understanding that all apparent limitation is consciousness's own self-limitation. The world is not denied as absolutely non-existent but recognized as having no independent reality apart from Shiva's consciousness. What appears as world, body, and mind is Shiva's creative play (*lila*) — real as expression but not as independent existence.

The negation practiced here is the Shaiva discipline of dissolving thought-constructs (*vikalpas*) — the mental formations that create the appearance of a world separate from consciousness.
