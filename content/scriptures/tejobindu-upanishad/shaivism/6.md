Section 6 declares the supreme truth: only the radiant consciousness exists. For Shaiva interpreters, this is the final word of recognition (*pratyabhijna*) — the self recognizes itself as unlimited consciousness, and the appearance of limitation dissolves forever. The teaching is Shiva's own self-recognition expressed through the Upanishadic tradition.

The radiant point (*tejobindu*) is revealed as not a point at all but the infinite — consciousness concentrated appears as a point, but recognized appears as everything. This paradox of the infinitesimal and the infinite being one is the supreme teaching of Shaiva non-dualism.
