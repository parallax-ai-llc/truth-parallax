## Overview

Shaiva interpreters find in the Tejobindu Upanishad a direct description of Shiva's nature as the luminous point of consciousness at the center of all experience. The *tejobindu* — the radiant drop — is the bindu (point) from which all manifestation arises and into which it dissolves, central to Shaiva tantric cosmology.

## Theological Emphases

The fifteen-limbed yoga extends the eight-limbed path to include the subtlest levels of consciousness that Shaiva meditation aims to access. The systematic negation of all phenomenal categories mirrors the Shaiva path of dissolution (*laya*) through which the practitioner transcends successive layers of limitation until only the luminous core of consciousness remains — Shiva in His absolute nature (*Paramashiva*).
