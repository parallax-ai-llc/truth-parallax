Section 3 presents the *tejobindu* — the luminous point at the center of consciousness — which Shaiva interpreters identify with the *bindu* of tantric cosmology. The bindu is the point of pure consciousness from which all manifestation arises and into which it dissolves. It is Shiva's essence — the concentrated drop of infinite awareness that contains the seed of all creation.

Meditation on the tejobindu is the Shaiva practice of concentrating awareness at its source-point, allowing the expansion of consciousness from the point outward to infinity. The radiant point is not a spatial location but the very center of awareness itself — the "place" where consciousness recognizes its own luminous nature.
