Section 4 represents the *apavada* (negation) stage of Advaita teaching — the systematic denial of all that was provisionally attributed to Brahman through *adhyaropa* (superimposition). The world, the body, the mind, the ego — all are systematically shown to be unreal when measured against the standard of the self-luminous Brahman.

This radical negation is the Advaita method at its most intensive. By denying reality to everything that is not Brahman, the text forces the seeker to confront the question: What remains? The answer is the radiant point — the self-luminous consciousness that was never born, never changes, and never dies.
