**Chapter XI: The Chapter of Going Into the Hall of the Two Truths**

**1** The Osiris Ani says: "I have come to thee, O great God, Lord of the Two Truths. I know thee and I know the names of the two and forty gods who dwell with thee in this Hall of the Two Truths.

**2** I have not committed sin. I have not committed robbery with violence. I have not stolen. I have not slain men and women. I have not stolen grain.

**3** I have not purloined offerings. I have not stolen the property of God. I have not uttered lies. I have not carried away food.

**4** I have not uttered curses. I have not committed adultery. I have not made any man to cry.

**5** I have not trespassed. I have not acted with violence. I have not judged hastily. I have not multiplied my words upon speaking.

**6** I have not committed fraud. I have not looked upon evil. I have not reviled the King."
