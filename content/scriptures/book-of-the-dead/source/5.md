**Chapter V: The Chapter of Not Allowing the Deceased to Do Work in Khert-Neter**

**1** The Osiris Ani says: "Hail Khepera in his boat, the self-created! I am Yesterday, I am Today, I am Tomorrow.

**2** I am the divine soul who dwells among the gods. I am not driven back. I am not repulsed.

**3** I come to do the work which the god has decreed for me. Let not the work be done for me in Khert-Neter, but let me have peace in the beautiful Amentet."
