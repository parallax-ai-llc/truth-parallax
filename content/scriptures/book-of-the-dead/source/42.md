**Chapter XLII: The Chapter of Avoiding Slaughter in Hensu**

**1** The Osiris Ani says: "Hail, land of the Sceptre! I am the babe. I am the babe. I am the babe.

**2** I am come into thee, O land of the two halves. I have come. I am glorious. I am mighty. I am a soul. I am a powerful one.

**3** I bring unto thee the two jaws of Ruti. The two halves of the sky have been given to me.

**4** My head is the head of Ra. I am assembled, I am made whole. My face is the face of the Disk. My eyes are the eyes of Hathor. My ears are the ears of Ap-uat.

**5** My nose is the nose of Khenti-khas. My lips are the lips of Anpu. My teeth are the teeth of Serqet. My neck is the neck of the divine goddess Isis.

**6** My hands are the hands of Ba-neb-Tattu. My forearms are the forearms of Neith, the Lady of Sais. My backbone is the backbone of Suti. My phallus is the phallus of Osiris.

**7** My flesh is the flesh of the Lords of Kheraha. My chest is the chest of Aa-shefit. My belly and my back are the belly and back of Sekhmet. My buttocks are the buttocks of the Eye of Horus.

**8** My hips and my legs are the hips and legs of Nut. My feet are the feet of Ptah. My fingers and my leg-bones are the fingers and leg-bones of the Living Gods.

**9** There is no member of my body which is not the member of a god. Thoth shields my body altogether, and I am Ra day by day.

**10** I shall not be dragged back by my arms, and no one shall lay violent hands upon me. Not men, not gods, not the glorified dead, not the damned, not the great one of terror shall do me harm.

**11** I am he who comes forth and goes forward. His name is not known. I am Yesterday. I see millions of years.

**12** I pass along, I pass along the paths of the divine celestial judges. I am the Lord of Eternity. I decree and I judge like Khepera. I am the lord of the crown.

**13** I am he who dwells in the Utchat and in the Egg. It is granted to me to live with them. I am he who dwells in the Utchat when it closes, and I exist by their strength.

**14** I come forth and I shine; I go in and I come to life. I am in the Utchat. My seat is upon my throne. I sit in the pupil of the Eye in its time.

**15** I am Horus who traverses millions of years. I have established my seat. I rule it. My mouth is equipped with words of power. I am he who is in his father.

**16** I am given the beautiful Amentet. Let all the Osiris Ani say: O all ye gods of the underworld, hear my voice!"
