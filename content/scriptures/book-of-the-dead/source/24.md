**Chapter XXIV: The Chapter of Bringing Charms to the Deceased**

**1** The Osiris Ani says: "I am Tem-Khepera, who produced himself on the thigh of his divine mother. I am brought hither, and I snuff the air.

**2** The great goddess Tefnut brings me the charms. She establishes them. She places them on my breast.

**3** I am equipped. I am the divine hawk. My word of power is my protection."
