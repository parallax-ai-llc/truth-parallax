**Chapter XXIII: The Chapter of Opening the Mouth of the Deceased**

**1** The Osiris Ani says: "My mouth is opened by Ptah. My mouth is unclosed by my city-god. Thoth has come fully equipped with charms.

**2** He unfastens the swathings, the swathings of Set which have sealed my mouth. Atum has hurled back those who would restrain him with their arms.

**3** My mouth is firmly established. My mouth is opened. My mouth is unclosed by Shu with his iron knife with which he opened the mouths of the gods.

**4** I am the goddess Sekhet. I sit upon a place by the side of Amsu, the Great Wind of heaven. I am the great star-goddess Sahit, who dwells among the souls of Heliopolis."
