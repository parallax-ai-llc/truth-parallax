**Chapter XXXVIII: The Chapter of Living by Air in Khert-Neter**

**1** The Osiris Ani says: "I am the jackal of jackals. I am Shu. I draw air from the presence of the god of Light to the bounds of heaven, to the bounds of earth, to the bounds of the uttermost limits of the flight of the Nebeh bird.

**2** May air be given unto this young divine being."
