**Chapter IV: Another Chapter of Passing Along the Way Over the Earth**

**1** The Osiris Ani says: "The chapter of passing along the way over the earth.

**2** I am the Bull, the son of the ancestress of Osiris. Grant ye that his father, the Lord of his divine companions, may bear witness on his behalf.

**3** I have weighed the mightiest of them. I have given the decree for him that is in Unnu. I have opened the way in heaven, on earth, in the six estates, in the seven Arit.

**4** I stand up and I make myself ready. I am the Bull which is in the Lion-house."
