**Chapter XXVI: The Chapter of Giving a Heart to the Deceased**

**1** The Osiris Ani says: "My heart is with me, and it shall never come to pass that it shall be carried away. I am the lord of hearts, the slayer of the heart.

**2** I live in right and truth, and I have my being therein. I am Horus, the dweller in hearts, the dweller in the body.

**3** I live by my word, and my heart has being. Let not my heart be taken from me. Let it not be wounded.

**4** Let not wounds and cuts be dealt to me on account of it. My heart is with me, and it shall rest with me. I am the lord thereof."
