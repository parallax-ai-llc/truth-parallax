**Chapter XXXII: The Chapter of Repulsing the Crocodile That Comes to Carry Away the Charm**

**1** The Osiris Ani says: "I am the one who comes forth from the flood. He has given me power. I have come to save my charms from him who would carry them off.

**2** Be destroyed, O crocodile of the South! Be destroyed, O crocodile of the North! The lance is in my hand."
