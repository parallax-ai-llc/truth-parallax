**Chapter XXXIV: The Chapter of Not Being Bitten by a Serpent**

**1** The Osiris Ani says: "O serpent! I am the flame which shines on the Opener of hundreds of thousands of years.

**2** Get thee gone from me, for I am the divine lynx. Get thee gone from me, for I am the divine lion. I am the god who shines in the darkness."
