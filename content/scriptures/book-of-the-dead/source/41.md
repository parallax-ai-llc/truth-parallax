**Chapter XLI: The Chapter of Avoiding the Slaughter in Khert-Neter**

**1** The Osiris Ani says: "O Land of the Sceptre! O Crown of the Uraeus! I am the child. I am the child. I am the child.

**2** O Knife that cuts, do not seize me! O Fingers, do not clutch me! I am the Lord of the Crown. I have come to see my father Osiris."
