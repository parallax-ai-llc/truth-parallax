**Chapter XIV: The Chapter of Driving Away Shame From the Heart of the Deceased**

**1** The Osiris Ani says: "Hail to thee, thou whose heart is still! Hail to thee, who holdest together all hearts!

**2** I am made clean by the Eye of Horus; I am adorned by the Goddess of the Two Eyes. My shame is driven away.

**3** I sit in the great hall. I come forth in peace. The great gods drive away my shame."
