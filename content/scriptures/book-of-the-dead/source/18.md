**Chapter XVIII: The Chapter of the Festivals of the Gods**

**1** Thoth says: "I am Thoth, the advocate of Ra, the lord of divine words. I come to make the word of Osiris true against his enemies.

**2** Hail, Thoth! What has happened to the divine children of Nut? They have stirred up war, they have created tumult. They have done evil. They have created fiends.

**3** They have made slaughter. They have created trouble. In all their doings the mighty have worked against the weak. Grant, O might of Thoth, that that which the god Atum has decreed may be done.

**4** And thou regardest not evil, nor art thou provoked to anger when they bring their years to confusion, and press in and disturb their months."
