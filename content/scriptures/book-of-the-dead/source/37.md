**Chapter XXXVII: The Chapter of Repulsing the Two Merti Goddesses**

**1** The Osiris Ani says: "The two Merti goddesses, the two Eyes of the Lord, have been repulsed. The watchers have been repulsed.

**2** The serpent on its belly has been made to retreat. I am the lord of the crown. I am the Uraeus of fire."
