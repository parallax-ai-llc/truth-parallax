**Chapter XXXIII: The Chapter of Repulsing Serpents**

**1** The Osiris Ani says: "O serpent Rerek! Advance not! Behold, the gods Seb and Shu stand against thee.

**2** Thou hast eaten the rat, the abomination of Ra, and thou hast crunched the bones of the putrid cat. Stand back!"
