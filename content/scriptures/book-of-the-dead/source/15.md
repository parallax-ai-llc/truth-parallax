**Chapter XV: A Hymn of Praise to Ra When He Riseth in the Horizon**

**1** Homage to thee, O Ra, when thou risest, thou Temu-Heru-Khuti! The company of the gods praise thee.

**2** The gods of the earth praise thee. The goddess Maat embraces thee at the two seasons. Thou passest over heaven, thy heart is glad.

**3** The sektet boat advances, it prospers. Thy mother Nut stretches her arms to receive thee. Thou art crowned as the king of the gods.

**4** Thy mother Nut does an act of worship before thee. The mountain of the West receives thee in peace. Maat embraces thee at morn and at eve.

**5** Hail, all ye gods of the temple of the Soul who weigh heaven and earth in a balance! Hail, Temu, lord of the two lands of Anu!

**6** Homage to thee, Ra, Lord of Maat! Thou art hidden in thy shrine. Thou causest the gods of the two lands to come into being.

**7** The doors of heaven are opened to thee, the gates of the sky are thrown open for thee. O thou beautiful being, the gods and their ka's come to thee.

**8** Thou shinest in the eastern horizon, and the earth is illumined by thy beams. All creatures bow before thee, O thou lord of heaven, lord of earth."
