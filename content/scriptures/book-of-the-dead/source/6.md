**Chapter VI: The Chapter of Making the Shabti Figure Do Work for a Man in Khert-Neter**

**1** The Osiris Ani says: "O thou shabti figure of the Osiris Ani! If I be called, or if I be adjudged to do any work whatsoever of the labours which are to be done in Khert-Neter—

**2** Let the judgment fall upon thee instead of upon me always, in the matter of sowing the fields, of filling the canals with water, and of bringing the sands from the East to the West.

**3** 'Here am I,' thou shalt say."
