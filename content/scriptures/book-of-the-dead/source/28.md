**Chapter XXVIII: The Chapter of Not Allowing the Heart to Be Carried Away**

**1** The Osiris Ani says: "Hail, Lion-God! I am Unb. That which I hate is the block of slaughter.

**2** Let not my heart be carried away from me by those who stab. Let it not be placed far from me.

**3** I am the lord of my heart. I am the lord of my body. My heart obeys me. I have power over it. I have made it to dwell in its place."
