**Chapter XII: The Chapter of Going Into and Coming Out of the Tuat**

**1** The Osiris Ani says: "Hail, thou who art great, thou who art shining! Behold, I have come to thee. I am glorious, I am pure.

**2** My two arms are about thee. Thy portion is of those who have gone before thee. Give me my mouth that I may speak with it. I guide my heart at its season of fire."
