**Chapter XXXIX: The Chapter of Repulsing the Serpent in Khert-Neter**

**1** The Osiris Ani says: "Get thee back, fiend! Get thee back, serpent Apep! Retreat from the boat of Ra!

**2** The divine company of the gods is against thee. Thy head is cut off. Thy memory is no more. Thou art overthrown. Get thee back, Apep!"
