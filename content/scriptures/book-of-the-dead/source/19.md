**Chapter XIX: The Chapter of the Crown of Triumph**

**1** The Osiris Ani says: "Thy father Atum has made for thee this beautiful crown of triumph. That living uraeus upon thy head has established it.

**2** Thou livest, thou art sound, thou art renewed, thou art young again, thou art true of voice. The Eye of Horus gives thee thy strength.

**3** The gods come to thee bowing. Thoth has adorned thee in triumph. The crown of triumph upon thy head is the proof of thy righteousness."
