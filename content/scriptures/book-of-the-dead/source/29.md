**Chapter XXIX: The Chapter of Not Allowing the Heart to Be Carried Away**

**1** The Osiris Ani says: "Get thee back, O messenger of all the gods! Art thou come to carry away this my heart which lives?

**2** My heart which lives shall not be given to thee. The gods who dwell on high shall obey my words. My heart shall not be given to thee."
