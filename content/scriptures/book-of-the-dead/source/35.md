**Chapter XXXV: The Chapter of Not Being Eaten by Serpents**

**1** The Osiris Ani says: "O thou serpent Shu, I am the divine fire, the lord of millions, the mighty one of the company of the gods.

**2** Thou shalt not devour me. I am the lord of thousands. I stand up and I am in my place. I sit down and I am in my place."
