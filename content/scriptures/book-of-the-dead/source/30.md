**Chapter XXX: The Chapter of Not Letting the Heart of the Deceased Be Driven Away in Khert-Neter**

**1** The Osiris Ani says: "My heart, my mother! My heart, my mother! My heart of my existence! Let there be nothing to resist me at the judgment.

**2** Let there be no opposition to me from the Tchatcha. Let there be no parting of thee from me in the presence of him who keeps the Balance!

**3** Thou art my ka within my body, the god Khnemu who makes sound my limbs. When thou goest forth to the Felicity, let not my name be made to stink by the Shenit officials, who make men to stand fast.

**4** Let it be satisfactory to us, and let the hearing be satisfactory, and let there be joy of heart at the weighing of words.

**5** Let not that which is false be uttered against me before the Great God, the Lord of Amentet! Verily how great shalt thou be when thou risest in triumph!"
