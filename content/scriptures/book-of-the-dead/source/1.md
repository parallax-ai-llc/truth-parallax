**Chapter I: The Chapter of Coming Forth by Day**

**1** Here begin the chapters of coming forth by day, and of the songs of praise and of glorifying, and of coming forth from and of going into the glorious Khert-Neter, in the beautiful Amentet.

**2** They shall be said on the day of the burial; going in after coming forth.

**3** The Osiris, the scribe Ani, triumphant, says: "Homage to thee, O Bull of Amentet! Thus says Thoth, the king of eternity: I am the great god in the boat of the Sun; I have fought for thee.

**4** I am one of those gods, those divine chiefs, who made the word of Osiris true against his enemies on that day of the weighing of words.

**5** I am thy kinsman, O Osiris. I am one of those gods to whom Nut gave birth, who slay the foes of Osiris, and who hold for him in bonds the fiend Sebau.

**6** I am thy kinsman, O Horus. I have fought for thee. I have come to thee for thy name's sake.

**7** I am Thoth, who made the word of Osiris true against his enemies on that day of the weighing of words in the great House of the Prince, which is in Heliopolis.

**8** I am Teti, the son of Teti. I was conceived in Tattu, and I was born in Tattu.

**9** I am with those who weep and with the women who bewail Osiris in the two lands of Rekhti; and I make the word of Osiris true against his enemies.

**10** Ra commanded that Thoth should make the word of Osiris true against his enemies; and that which was bidden for me, Thoth did.

**11** I am with Horus on the day of the clothing of Teshtesh, and of the opening of the storehouses, and of the purifying of the Silent God, and of the guarding of the key of the door of Ma'at.

**12** I am with Horus as the protector of the left shoulder of Osiris, which is in Sekhem; I go in and I come out from the divine flames on the day of the destruction of the Fiends in Sekhem.

**13** I am with Horus on the day of the festivals of Osiris, making offerings on the sixth-day festival and on the Tenat festival in Heliopolis.

**14** Hail, ye who make perfected souls to enter into the house of Osiris, make ye the perfected soul of the Osiris, the scribe Ani, triumphant, to enter with you into the house of Osiris.

**15** Let him hear even as ye hear; let him see even as ye see; let him stand even as ye stand; let him sit even as ye sit."
