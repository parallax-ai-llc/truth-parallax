**Chapter XIII: The Chapter of Going Into and Coming Forth From Amentet**

**1** The Osiris Ani says: "I am the god Atum, I who am alone. I came into being from Nu. I am Ra who rose in the beginning, the ruler of what he has made.

**2** I am the great god who came into being by himself, Nu, who created his names, the lord of the company of the gods. Who is he? It is Ra, who created the names of the parts of his body.

**3** These came into being in the form of the gods who are in the train of Ra."
