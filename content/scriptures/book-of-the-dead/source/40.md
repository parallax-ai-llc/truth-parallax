**Chapter XL: The Chapter of Repulsing the Eater of the Ass**

**1** The Osiris Ani says: "O thou Eater of the Ass! I am the Cat. I sat at the Lake of the Persea Tree in Heliopolis on that night of battle.

**2** I am the Cat which fought near the Persea Tree in Heliopolis on that night when the foes of Neb-er-tcher were destroyed.

**3** I am Ra. I am the light which overcomes darkness."
