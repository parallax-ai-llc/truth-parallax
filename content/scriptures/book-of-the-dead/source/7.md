**Chapter VII: The Chapter of Passing Over the Back of Apep, the Evil One**

**1** The Osiris Ani says: "Hail, thou serpent Rerek! Get thee back, advance not! The flames of the Seven Spirits are against thee.

**2** Thy bones shall be broken. Thy venom shall be on thine own body. Ra has overthrown thee. The company of the gods has turned thee back.

**3** Thy head is cut off. Thy memory shall be no more. Get thee hence from the Osiris Ani!"
