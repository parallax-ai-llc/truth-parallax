**Chapter IX: The Chapter of Coming Forth by Day, Having Passed Through the Tomb**

**1** The Osiris Ani says: "Hail, Soul, thou mighty one of terror! Behold, I have come to thee. I see thee.

**2** I have forced a way through the Duat. I see my father Osiris. I drive away the darkness. I love him.

**3** I have come. I see my father Osiris. He has destroyed Set. I have opened every way in heaven, on earth, and in the deep."
