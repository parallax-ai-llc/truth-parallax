**Chapter XVI: [Vignette Chapter - The Scene of Worship]**

[This chapter consists primarily of the vignette (illustration) showing the deceased worshipping the rising sun. The accompanying text is minimal.]

**1** Praise to Ra when he rises on the horizon. The Osiris Ani worships the Lord of Eternity and says:

**2** "Homage to thee, Heru-Khuti-Temu-Heru-Khuti! Thou risest, thou shinest, thou makest bright the sky. Thou art the king of the gods. The goddess Nut receives thee."

**3** "Thy mother Meri stretches out her hands to thee. Thou art united with the Evening Star. Thy heart is glad. The sektet boat rejoices."
