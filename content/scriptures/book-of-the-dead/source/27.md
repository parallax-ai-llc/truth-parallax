**Chapter XXVII: The Chapter of Not Allowing the Heart to Be Taken From the Deceased**

**1** The Osiris Ani says: "Hail, ye who carry away hearts! Hail, ye who steal hearts! Ye shall not carry away my heart. Ye shall not steal my heart.

**2** Those who steal hearts, who carry away hearts, they shall not carry away my heart. For I am the lord of my heart. I am the lord of my breast-bone.

**3** I sit in the middle of all the great ones. I shall have my being. I shall be master of my heart. My mouth shall speak. My feet shall walk. My arms shall move."
