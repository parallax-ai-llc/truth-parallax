**Chapter XXII: The Chapter of Restoring the Mouth**

**1** The Osiris Ani says: "I am the god Shu. I have seated myself in the place of the Mighty One who is in Khemenu.

**2** My mouth has been given to me. My mouth has been opened by Ptah with those iron instruments with which he opened the mouths of the gods.

**3** I am Sekhet. I sit upon the great western side of heaven. I am the great goddess Sahit among the souls of Heliopolis.

**4** Now, as concerning every charm and all the words which may be spoken against me, the whole company of the gods shall set themselves to defend me."
