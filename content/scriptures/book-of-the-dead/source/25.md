**Chapter XXV: The Chapter of Causing the Deceased to Remember His Name**

**1** The Osiris Ani says: "My name is given to me in the Great House. My name is remembered in the House of Fire, on the night of the counting of the years.

**2** I am the dweller in Sekhem. I am seated at the right side of the god. My name shall not perish in this land for ever.

**3** I have come into the city. I know the doors thereof. My name is established. I remember my name. I shall not be destroyed."
