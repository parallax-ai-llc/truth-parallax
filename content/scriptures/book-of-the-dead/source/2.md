**Chapter II: The Chapter of Coming Forth by Day and of Living After Death**

**1** The Osiris Ani says: "Hail, Only One, shining from the Moon! Hail, Only One, shining from the Moon! Grant that this Osiris Ani may come forth among thy multitudes at the gate.

**2** Let him be with the Light-God. Let the Duat be opened for him. Behold, the Osiris Ani shall come forth by day to do his will upon earth among the living."
