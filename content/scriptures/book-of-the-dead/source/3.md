**Chapter III: Another Chapter Like Unto It**

**1** The Osiris Ani says: "O Atum, who comes forth from the Great Deep, who shines gloriously beneath the stars of Nut!

**2** Grant that I may become a being honoured before Osiris, and that I may come forth to his ka. Let the Duat be opened for me."
