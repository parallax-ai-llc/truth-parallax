**Chapter X: Another Chapter of Coming Forth by Day**

**1** The Osiris Ani says: "Hail, thou door! I have made my way through thee. I know thee and I know thy name.

**2** I am the mighty one who creates his own light. I have come to thee. Open thou for me.

**3** I am he who makes firm the backbone and gives stability to the ribs and limbs. I am he who puts an end to destruction."
