**Chapter VIII: The Chapter of Passing Through Amentit by Night**

**1** The Osiris Ani says: "I am Atum, the creator of the gods. I have come forth from the earth. I have made my word true.

**2** I am the Lord of Light. I am the maker of darkness, which comes from the light. I know the hidden things in the holy places. I am the Great One.

**3** I am the Bennu Bird, which is in Heliopolis. I am the keeper of the book of things which are and of things which shall be."
