**Chapter XXXVI: The Chapter of Repulsing the Tortoise**

**1** The Osiris Ani says: "Get thee back, thou tortoise Apshait! Get thee back! Stand still! Retreat!

**2** For I am Ra. I am the flame. I am the lord of the gods. My magical words are with me. Be destroyed!"
