**Chapter XXI: The Chapter of Giving a Mouth to the Deceased**

**1** The Osiris Ani says: "I rise out of the Egg in the hidden land. May my mouth be given to me that I may speak with it before the great god, the lord of the Duat.

**2** Let not my hand and my arm be repulsed by the divine chiefs of any god. I am Osiris, the lord of the mouth of the tomb.

**3** The Osiris Ani has a portion with him who is upon the top of the steps. I have come at the wish of my heart from the Lake of Fire, and I have quenched it.

**4** Homage to thee, lord of brightness, who art at the head of the Great House, and who dwellest in the darkness and in the thick darkness! I have come unto thee.

**5** I am glorious, I am pure. My two arms are about thee. Thy portion is of those who have gone before thee. Give me my mouth that I may speak with it."
