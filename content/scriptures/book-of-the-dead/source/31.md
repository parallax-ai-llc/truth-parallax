**Chapter XXXI: The Chapter of Repulsing the Crocodile**

**1** The Osiris Ani says: "Get thee back! Retreat! Get thee back, thou crocodile Sui! Come not against me! My magical words are with me.

**2** I am Amu. I come forth like Atum. The waterfloods of the gods are against thee. Be destroyed! Thou shalt not come near me. I live in my truth.

**3** I am Ra. I am the Light. I am the Wall. Retreat before my spear. I am he whose name is hidden."
