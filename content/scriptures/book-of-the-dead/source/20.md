**Chapter XX: Another Chapter of the Crown of Triumph**

**1** The Osiris Ani says: "Atum has established for me my crown of triumph. My father has given me the crown.

**2** My father Atum has built for me my house. My twin sister has established it. The fields of grain give me their offerings.

**3** I travel over the lands. I come into being among the gods. I am strong to protect them."
