## Setting Out: The Courage to Seek

The Valley of the Quest speaks to the universal need for courage at the beginning of the spiritual journey. Every tradition acknowledges that the path is difficult and that many who begin will not continue. The Quran's "narrow is the gate" finds its parallel in Matthew's "narrow is the way" and in the Buddha's observation that the path to liberation is "against the stream" of ordinary human desire.

The interfaith insight is that the Valley of the Quest is not unique to any tradition — it is the common starting point of every genuine spiritual life. Whether one seeks God, enlightenment, liberation, or truth, the first step requires the same courage: the willingness to leave the known behind and step into uncertainty, trusting that the longing that propels the journey will also sustain it.
