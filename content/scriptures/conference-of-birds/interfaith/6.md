## One World, One God, One Heart: The Vision of Unity

The Valley of Unity addresses the deepest intuition of the mystical life: that beneath the surface diversity of the world — its many religions, many cultures, many languages — there lies a single reality. This intuition is not the erasure of difference but the perception of a depth in which all differences are held and honored.

The Sufi *wahdat al-wujud*, the Hindu *Tat tvam asi*, the Buddhist *sunyata*, the Christian mystic's *unio*, the Jewish *Ein Sof* — these concepts point, from different directions, toward the same horizon: the recognition that reality is ultimately one, and that the spiritual journey is the progressive awakening to this unity.
