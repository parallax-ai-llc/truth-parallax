## The Valley of Unity: The Light of Muhammad

For Shia readers, the Valley of Unity resonates with the concept of the *Nur Muhammadi* — the pre-existent Light of Muhammad that pervades all creation. If all being is unified in the divine light, then the Light of Muhammad (and the imams who carry it) is the medium through which this unity is perceived and experienced.

The valley's teaching that multiplicity is the surface of an underlying unity aligns with the Shia metaphysical tradition, particularly as developed by Mulla Sadra (1571-1636), whose *al-Hikma al-Muta'aliya* (Transcendent Philosophy) presents an elaborate ontology of being in which all existents participate in a single reality differentiated only by degrees of intensity.
