## The Valley of Love: Divine Love in Sunni Tradition

The Valley of Love resonates with the Sunni devotional tradition's celebration of love for God (*hubb Allah*) and love for the Prophet (*hubb al-Rasul*). The Quran declares that God "loves them and they love Him" (5:54), and the prophetic tradition affirms that the sweetness of faith is found in loving God and His Messenger above all else.

For Sunni readers, the Valley of Love does not override the shari'a but deepens it. The fire of love that burns away rational calculation does not destroy the structure of Islamic practice but reveals its inner meaning. The lover who is consumed by love for God performs the five daily prayers not as an obligation but as a meeting with the Beloved; fasts not as a duty but as a purification of desire; gives charity not as a tax but as an expression of love's generosity.
