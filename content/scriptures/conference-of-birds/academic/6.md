## Tawhid and Wahdat al-Wujud: Theological Implications

The Valley of Unity has been the most theologically contested of the seven valleys, raising the question of whether Attar espouses *wahdat al-wujud* (the unity of being), associated with Ibn Arabi and later Sufism, or a more orthodox formulation of *tawhid* (divine unity) compatible with Sunni theology. Scholars have generally concluded that Attar's position predates and is more ambiguous than the systematic formulations of Ibn Arabi, occupying a space between experiential mysticism and philosophical monism.
