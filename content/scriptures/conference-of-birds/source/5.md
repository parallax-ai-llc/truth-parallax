## The Valley of Love

**1.** The hoopoe said: "The second valley is the Valley of Love. Here the fire of love burns without ceasing."

**2.** "When you enter this valley, you will be consumed by flames. Love is fire—and the lover must burn."

**3.** "One who has not been burned by love cannot know the Way. Love alone opens the door that reason cannot find."

**4.** "Reason is a fine thing for the marketplace, but in the Valley of Love, reason is a beggar at the gate."

**5.** "The lover must plunge in headfirst, without thought for safety. A hundred worlds may perish—the lover does not care."

**6.** "When Majnun was asked why he poured sand through his fingers all day, he replied: I am searching for Layla. They said: But Layla cannot be found in sand. He answered: I look for her everywhere, in the hope of finding her somewhere."

**7.** "This is the madness of love. The lover seeks the Beloved in every atom of creation."

**8.** "Love is not a thing of prudence—it is a flame that consumes all prudence."

**9.** "If you possess a single hair of true love, you will discover beneath it the entire universe."

**10.** "A moth circles the flame and does not retreat. It gives its body to the fire. That is love."

**11.** "The moth does not say: The fire will burn me, therefore I will stay away. It says: I am the fire. And then it is consumed."

**12.** "There was once a man who burned his harvest, his house, and all his possessions, and then danced in the ashes. They said he was mad. He said he was free."

**13.** "Love turns bitterness to sweetness, copper to gold, sickness to health, the prison to the garden."

**14.** "Without love, the world is a dead thing. With love, every dead thing comes alive."

**15.** "In this valley, blood rains down from the clouds and the earth trembles beneath your feet. Yet the true lover presses on, heedless of all danger."

**16.** "When love arrives, fear departs. When love fills the heart, there is no room for anything else."
