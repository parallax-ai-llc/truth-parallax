## The Valley of the Quest

**1.** When the birds at last resolved to set forth, the hoopoe said: "The first valley you must cross is the Valley of the Quest."

**2.** "In this valley you must relinquish desire and renounce the world. You must cast away everything you have ever possessed."

**3.** "When you enter this valley, a hundred trials will assail you at every turn. You will meet obstacles at every step."

**4.** "Here the traveler must wander for years, for the valley is vast, and the way is not revealed to those who are faint of heart."

**5.** "You must purify your heart of all that is not God. Every attachment must be burned away."

**6.** "A king once fell in love with the face of a poor boy and gave up his throne for a single glance. This is the way of the Quest."

**7.** "When you have given up everything, you will be given everything. But first, you must be emptied."

**8.** "A dervish once knocked at the door of a house. The owner called out: Who is there? The dervish replied: I. The door remained shut."

**9.** "He knocked again and again, year after year. Each time the answer was the same: Who is there? And each time he said I, the door stayed closed."

**10.** "At last the dervish understood. He knocked once more. Who is there? came the voice. It is you, said the dervish. And the door opened."

**11.** "This is the secret of the Quest: as long as you exist, the door will remain closed."

**12.** "You must search as a man searches for his only treasure, lost in a vast desert."

**13.** "Do not sit idle on the path. Each moment wasted is a lifetime lost."

**14.** "A man once searched for fire while carrying a lit candle. The wise man said: You already carry what you seek."

**15.** "The Quest begins when you realize that what you are looking for has been with you all along—but you must make the journey to discover it."

**16.** "If the Quest costs you everything, it is still a bargain, for what you gain is beyond all reckoning."
