## The Valley of Detachment and the Valley of Unity

**1.** The hoopoe said: "The fourth valley is the Valley of Detachment. Here the traveler casts off all desire and all attachment."

**2.** "In this valley neither the past nor the future holds any meaning. The traveler stands in the eternal present, free of all bonds."

**3.** "A hundred thousand worlds may be offered to the traveler here—he turns from them all, for they are nothing."

**4.** "Everything perishes. The beauty of the world fades like a flower in autumn. He who clings to it clings to a corpse."

**5.** "A mystic was asked: What is detachment? He replied: It is to be in the world but not of it—to hold the world in your hand, but never in your heart."

**6.** "When a wind blows through an empty place, it passes freely. But where there are obstructions, the wind howls and beats against them."

**7.** "Be like the empty place. Let the wind of the spirit pass through you without resistance."

**8.** "All that you possess possesses you in return. Every treasure you hold is a chain upon your foot."

**9.** "The truly detached man walks through fire and is not burned, through water and is not drowned."

**10.** The hoopoe continued: "Beyond the Valley of Detachment lies the Valley of Unity."

**11.** "In this valley, the many become one. All the separate things of the world are seen as reflections of a single Light."

**12.** "Here there is no longer I and you. The lover, the Beloved, and love itself are one."

**13.** "When you look in a mirror, you see your own face. But you are not the mirror, and you are not the reflection. Yet without you, neither would exist."

**14.** "All beings are the reflections of one Being. All faces are the face of the One."

**15.** "The sea and the wave are not two things. The wave rises from the sea and returns to the sea. For a moment it has a name—then it is again the sea."

**16.** "In this valley, the traveler understands at last that the Simorgh he seeks and the bird he is are not separate—they are one and the same."
