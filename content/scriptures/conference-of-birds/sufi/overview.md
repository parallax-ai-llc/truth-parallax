## Overview

For the Sufi tradition, the Conference of the Birds is the definitive allegorical map of the spiritual path (*tariqah*) — a poem that charts the soul's journey from the first stirring of desire for God through the progressive stages of purification, illumination, and annihilation to the final realization that the seeker and the Sought are one. The poem has been used as a teaching text in Sufi orders for eight centuries, its stories deployed by masters to diagnose and treat the spiritual conditions of their disciples.
