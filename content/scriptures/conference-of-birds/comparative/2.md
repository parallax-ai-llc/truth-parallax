## The Quest: Pilgrimage and Sacred Journey

The Valley of the Quest connects to the cross-cultural tradition of pilgrimage and sacred journey — the conviction that spiritual transformation requires physical or metaphorical movement through space. The Hajj in Islam, the pilgrimages to Varanasi and Kashi in Hinduism, the Camino de Santiago in Christianity, the circumambulation of Mount Kailash in Buddhism — all express the intuition that the soul grows through journeying.

The Valley of the Quest's demand for renunciation parallels the stripping-away that characterizes pilgrimage across traditions: the pilgrim leaves behind the familiar, wears simple clothing, and enters a liminal space where ordinary social structures are suspended.
