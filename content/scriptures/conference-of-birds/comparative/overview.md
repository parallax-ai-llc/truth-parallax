## Overview

The Conference of the Birds invites comparison with quest narratives and spiritual journey literature from traditions around the world. The structure — a group of seekers embarks on a difficult journey, most fall away, and the few who persevere discover that what they sought was within them all along — is one of the most widespread narrative patterns in human culture, appearing in forms as diverse as the Odyssey, the Pilgrim's Progress, the Wizard of Oz, and the Hero's Journey archetype identified by Joseph Campbell.
