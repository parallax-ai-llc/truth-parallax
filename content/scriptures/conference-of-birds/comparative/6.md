## The Experience of Oneness: Mystical Union

The Valley of Unity resonates with reports of unitive experience across mystical traditions: the Hindu *moksha* (liberation through realization of Brahman-Atman identity), the Christian *unio mystica* (mystical union with God), the Buddhist experience of *sunyata* (emptiness/interdependence), and the Jewish concept of *devekut* (cleaving to God). Each tradition describes an experience in which the ordinary boundary between self and other, subject and object, human and divine becomes transparent or dissolves entirely.
