## Academic Reading

Academic analysis of the seventh astpadi's critique of ritualism situates it within the broader anti-formalist discourse of the Sant-Bhakti movement. Scholars examine how this critique functioned socially — challenging Brahmanical authority and creating space for non-Brahman religious leadership — while also noting the paradox that Sikhism subsequently developed its own rich liturgical and ritual traditions.
