## Academic Reading

Scholars analyze the Sukhmani's concluding astpadi as a literary and theological summation, examining how Guru Arjan achieves closure through the return to the opening theme of *Nam Simran*. The circular structure is compared with similar devices in other devotional compositions, and the text's overall architecture is assessed as a carefully constructed theological argument in poetic form.
