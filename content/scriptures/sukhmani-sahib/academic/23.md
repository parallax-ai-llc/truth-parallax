## Academic Reading

Academic study of the twenty-third astpadi's concept of *sahaj* traces this term through its complex genealogy in Nath-Yogi, Tantric, and Sant-Bhakti traditions. Scholars note how Guru Arjan transforms the Tantric concept of *sahaja* (spontaneous awakening) into a distinctively Sikh ideal of natural, everyday divine awareness that does not require withdrawal from worldly life.
