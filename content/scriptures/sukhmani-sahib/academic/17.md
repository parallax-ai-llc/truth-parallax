## Academic Reading

Scholars analyze the seventeenth astpadi's teaching on the preciousness of human birth in relation to the Indian doctrine of transmigration (*samsara*) and the distinctively Sikh understanding of the purpose of human life. The urgency of Guru Arjan's appeal — this life is your chance for liberation — is situated within the broader soteriological framework of Indian religious thought.
