## Academic Reading

The twelfth astpadi's analysis of pride and humility is studied in relation to the extensive South Asian literature on the spiritual dangers of *ahamkara* (ego). Scholars trace how Guru Arjan's treatment draws on and transforms earlier discussions in the Yoga Sutras, the Bhagavad Gita, and the writings of the Sant-Bhakti poets.
