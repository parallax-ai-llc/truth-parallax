## Academic Reading

The sixteenth astpadi's teaching on speech ethics is examined in relation to both Indian and Islamic traditions of ethical discourse. Scholars note how Guru Arjan's injunctions against slander draw on the extensive Qur'anic and hadith literature on the ethics of speech while also reflecting distinctively Indian concerns about the karmic consequences of harmful words.
