## Academic Reading

The eighth astpadi's theology of the *Shabad* (divine Word) is analyzed in relation to the broader Indian concept of *shabda* (word/sound as cosmic principle) and the Islamic concept of *kalam Allah* (divine speech). Scholars examine how the Sikh elevation of *Gurbani* to the status of living Guru reflects both Indian and Islamic models of scriptural authority.
