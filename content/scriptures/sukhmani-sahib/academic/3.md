## Academic Reading

The third astpadi's theology of divine immanence is analyzed in relation to the *nirguna-saguna* dialectic that characterizes Sant-Bhakti theology. Scholars note how Guru Arjan navigates between the formless (*nirguna*) and personal (*saguna*) dimensions of the divine, creating a theology that affirms both transcendence and immanence without fully identifying with either the Advaita or the Vaishnava position.
