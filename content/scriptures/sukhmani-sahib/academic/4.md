## Academic Reading

Academic study of the fourth astpadi's teaching on *sat sangat* examines the social dimensions of Sikh spiritual practice. Scholars analyze how the concept of holy fellowship functioned as an alternative social formation in early modern Punjab — creating bonds of spiritual kinship that crossed caste, class, and religious boundaries. The *sangat* is studied as both a theological concept and a social institution.
