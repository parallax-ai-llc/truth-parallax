## Academic Reading

The twenty-second astpadi's teaching on the Guru's role is examined in relation to the broader South Asian *guru* tradition and the distinctive Sikh development of this concept. Scholars analyze how Guru Arjan's teaching anticipates the eventual transfer of guruship from human Gurus to the Guru Granth Sahib — the scripture as living Guru — that would occur under Guru Gobind Singh.
