## Academic Reading

Academic analysis of the nineteenth astpadi's soteriology examines how Guru Arjan's teaching on liberation through *Nam* relates to other Indian salvation models: the Advaita path of knowledge (*jnana*), the Yoga path of practice, and the Bhakti path of devotion. Scholars note how Guru Arjan affirms the Bhakti path while incorporating elements of the others into a comprehensive spiritual vision.
