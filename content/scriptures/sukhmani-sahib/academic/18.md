## Academic Reading

The eighteenth astpadi's concept of contentment (*santokh*) is examined in relation to both Indian and Islamic ethical traditions. Scholars trace the concept through its Sanskrit (*santosha*), Pali, and Persian genealogies, examining how Guru Arjan synthesizes these diverse influences into a distinctively Sikh ethic of joyful acceptance.
