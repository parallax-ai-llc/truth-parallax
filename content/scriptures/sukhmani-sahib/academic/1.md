## Academic Reading

Academic analysis of the opening astpadi examines Guru Arjan's concept of *Nam Simran* in relation to earlier devotional practices in the Sant-Bhakti and Nath-Yogi traditions. Scholars trace the genealogy of divine-name meditation through Kabir, Namdev, and Guru Nanak, noting how Guru Arjan synthesizes these influences into a systematic spiritual theology. Prosodic analysis reveals the meditative effects of the composition's rhythmic structure.
