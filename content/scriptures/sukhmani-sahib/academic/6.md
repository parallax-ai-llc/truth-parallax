## Academic Reading

The sixth astpadi's treatment of *maya* is examined in relation to the complex history of this concept in Indian philosophical and devotional traditions. Scholars note how Guru Arjan's concept differs from the Advaita Vedanta understanding of *maya* as cosmic illusion, instead treating it as the psychological condition of attachment and forgetfulness of God — a position closer to some Vaishnava and Sant-Bhakti interpretations.
