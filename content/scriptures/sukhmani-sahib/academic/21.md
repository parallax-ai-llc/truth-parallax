## Academic Reading

Scholars examine the twenty-first astpadi's concept of *Hukam* (divine will/order) as one of the most distinctive and important concepts in Sikh theology. The relationship between *Hukam* and human free will is analyzed in comparison with Islamic discussions of *qadar* and Indian philosophical treatments of *karma* and *daiva*.
