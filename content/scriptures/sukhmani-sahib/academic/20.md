## Academic Reading

The twentieth astpadi's proclamation of divine oneness is studied as a key text for understanding Sikh monotheism and its relationship to Islamic *tawhid*, Hindu *advaita*, and the *nirguna* theology of the Sant tradition. Scholars analyze how Guru Arjan's concept of divine unity accommodates religious diversity — affirming one God while acknowledging multiple valid approaches to the Divine.
