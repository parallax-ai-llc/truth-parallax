## Academic Reading

The fourteenth astpadi's celebration of cosmic wonder is analyzed as a contribution to the Indian tradition of cosmological speculation and to the broader genre of religious praise poetry. Scholars examine how Guru Arjan combines the celebratory mode of Vedic and Puranic cosmogony with the devotional intimacy of the Bhakti tradition, creating a distinctive Sikh approach to natural theology.
