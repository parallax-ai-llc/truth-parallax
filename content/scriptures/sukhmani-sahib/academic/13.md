## Academic Reading

Scholars analyze the thirteenth astpadi's concept of *haumai* as a distinctive Sikh contribution to the Indian discourse on selfhood and its transcendence. The *Gurmukh-Manmukh* distinction is examined as a binomial that organizes Sikh ethical and spiritual thought, providing a framework for moral self-examination that is both psychologically acute and theologically grounded.
