---
title: "Sukhmani Sahib - Chapter 1"
scripture: "sukhmani-sahib"
chapter: 1
---

**1** Salok: I bow to the Primal Guru. I bow to the Guru of the ages. I bow to the True Guru. I bow to the Great, Divine Guru.

**2** Ashtpadi: Meditate, meditate, meditate in remembrance of Him, and find peace. Worry and anguish shall be dispelled from your mind.

**3** Remember, remember God in meditation, and find the company of the Holy.

**4** By the Grace of the Saints, all diseases are healed. By the Grace of the Saints, all pains depart.

**5** Meditate on the Lord, the Destroyer of fear and dread. Meditate on the Lord, and suffering shall depart.

**6** The Lord is the support of the Saints. The Naam, the Name of the Lord, is the support of His humble devotees.

**7** He Himself is the Creator, and He Himself is the Enjoyer. He Himself is the Giver, and He Himself is the Receiver.

**8** He Himself is the Being, and He Himself is the Knower. He Himself pervades all, and is contained in all.

**9** He is the treasure of the Naam. He is the support of the supportless. He is the honour of the dishonoured.

**10** Nanak seeks the Sanctuary of God, who is the Support of all. Meditate, meditate in remembrance of Him, and find peace.
