---
title: "Sukhmani Sahib - Chapter 21"
scripture: "sukhmani-sahib"
chapter: 21
---

**1** Salok: The Lord is the Creator of all. He is the Sustainer of all. He is the Destroyer of all. He alone knows His own ways.

**2** Ashtpadi: This section speaks of the bliss of meditation. Hear, O mind, the teaching of the Guru.

**3** He who serves the True Guru is blessed. He who follows the Guru's Word is liberated.

**4** In the company of the Saints, the Naam is obtained. In the company of the Saints, the mind is purified.

**5** He who forgets the Naam wanders in the cycle of birth and death. He who remembers the Naam is freed from all bondage.

**6** The world is like a dream. It appears for a moment and then vanishes. Only the Naam is real. Only the Naam endures.

**7** He who grasps the Naam as his anchor crosses the ocean of suffering. He who lets go of the Naam is drowned.

**8** The Lord is merciful to all. He forgives the sinner who repents. He embraces the one who returns to Him.

**9** There is no limit to His mercy. There is no end to His grace. He is the refuge of the refugeless, the support of the supportless.

**10** Nanak says: Meditate on the Naam, O my mind. The Naam is the essence of all joy. The Naam is the path to liberation. Waheguru, Waheguru, Waheguru.
