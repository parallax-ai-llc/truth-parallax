---
title: "Sukhmani Sahib - Chapter 2"
scripture: "sukhmani-sahib"
chapter: 2
---

**1** Salok: He Himself is the tree, and He Himself is the fruit. He Himself is the Enjoyer, and He Himself is the Giver of enjoyment.

**2** Ashtpadi: Many are the forms of worship and many the ways of devotion. But without the Naam, none shall find liberation.

**3** He who has the Naam enshrined in his heart — he alone is the true devotee of God.

**4** Through the Naam, all treasures are obtained. Through the Naam, all desires are fulfilled.

**5** Through the Naam, one crosses the ocean of suffering. Through the Naam, one finds the door of liberation.

**6** The Naam is the wealth of the poor. The Naam is the strength of the weak. The Naam is the light of the blind.

**7** He who dwells upon the Naam never suffers sorrow. He who dwells upon the Naam is honoured in the Court of the Lord.

**8** The Naam is the medicine for all diseases. The Naam is the cure for all ills. The Naam is the destroyer of all sins.

**9** Blessed is the tongue that chants the Naam. Blessed are the ears that hear the Naam. Blessed is the heart that enshrines the Naam.

**10** Nanak says: He who meditates on the Naam finds peace in this world and the next. The Naam is the essence of all prayers.
