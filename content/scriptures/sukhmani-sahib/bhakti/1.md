## Bhakti Reading

The Bhakti reader recognizes in Guru Arjan's opening celebration of *Nam Simran* the central practice of the Sant-Bhakti tradition: *nam japa* — the devoted repetition of God's name as the supreme path to liberation. Kabir, Ravidas, Namdev, and the other Bhakti poets whose compositions are included in the Guru Granth Sahib share Guru Arjan's conviction that the divine Name is accessible to all, regardless of caste, learning, or ritual purity.
