## Bhakti Reading

The Bhakti tradition's reverence for sacred utterance — the belief that the words of the realized saints carry transformative spiritual power — underlies Guru Arjan's celebration of the divine Word. The Bhakti poets understood their compositions not as mere literature but as vehicles of divine grace, and the Sikh tradition's elevation of these compositions to scriptural status in the Guru Granth Sahib confirms this understanding.
