## Bhakti Reading

The Bhakti reader arrives at the Sukhmani's conclusion with the same recognition: in the end, there is only *Nam* — the divine Name that encompasses all teaching, all practice, all experience. Guru Arjan's final celebration echoes the Bhakti poets' unanimous testimony that the Name is the beginning and end of the spiritual journey, the alpha and omega of devotion, the pearl of great price for which all else is gladly surrendered.
