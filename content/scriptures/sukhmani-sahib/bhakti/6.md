## Bhakti Reading

The Bhakti poets shared Guru Arjan's warning against the seductions of *maya*. Kabir's fierce denunciations of worldly attachment, Ravidas's celebrations of divine love over material wealth, and Namdev's stories of God's intervention in the devotee's life all echo the Sukhmani's teaching that the soul's true treasure is not found in the fleeting pleasures of the world but in the eternal joy of divine communion.
