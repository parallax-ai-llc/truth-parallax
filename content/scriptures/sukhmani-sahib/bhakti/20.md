## Bhakti Reading

Guru Arjan's proclamation of the one God of all echoes the Sant-Bhakti poets' insistence on the unity of God beyond all sectarian labels. Kabir famously declared that 'Ram and Rahim are one,' and Guru Nanak's *Mool Mantar* — the foundational Sikh statement of divine unity — continues this tradition. The Sukhmani's twentieth astpadi is a powerful reaffirmation of this nonsectarian monotheism.
