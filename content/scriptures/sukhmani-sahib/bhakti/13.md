## Bhakti Reading

The Bhakti critique of ego (*ahamkara*) as the source of spiritual suffering aligns with Guru Arjan's teaching on *haumai*. The Sant-Bhakti poets identified ego — manifesting as pride of caste, wealth, learning, or religious practice — as the primary obstacle on the path of devotion. Guru Arjan's distinction between *Gurmukh* and *Manmukh* corresponds to the Bhakti distinction between the devotee who has surrendered the ego and the worldly person still enslaved by it.
