## Bhakti Reading

The Bhakti tradition's rejection of empty ritual finds powerful expression in Guru Arjan's seventh astpadi. The Sant-Bhakti poets consistently challenged the Brahmanical monopoly on religious authority, declaring that God cares nothing for ritual purity, pilgrimage, or caste status but responds only to sincere love. Guru Arjan's teaching continues this tradition, affirming that inner devotion far outweighs any external religious performance.
