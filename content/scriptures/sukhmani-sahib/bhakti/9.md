## Bhakti Reading

The Bhakti reader finds in Guru Arjan's portrait of the *Brahmgiani* an echo of the Bhakti ideal of the *sant* — the realized devotee who has transcended ego and caste to live in constant awareness of God. The Bhakti saints — Kabir the weaver, Ravidas the cobbler, Namdev the calico-printer — demonstrated by their lives that spiritual perfection has nothing to do with social status and everything to do with the depth of one's love for God.
