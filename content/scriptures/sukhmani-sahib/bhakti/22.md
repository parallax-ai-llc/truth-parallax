## Bhakti Reading

The Bhakti tradition's reverence for the *guru* as the essential spiritual guide finds expression in Guru Arjan's teaching on the Guru's grace. The Sant-Bhakti poets consistently testified that they owed their spiritual awakening to the guru's compassionate intervention — that without the guru's light, the path would remain forever hidden in darkness.
