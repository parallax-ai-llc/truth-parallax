## Bhakti Reading

The Bhakti tradition's ideal of the *sahaj* state — natural, effortless devotion that permeates every moment of daily life — finds its most systematic expression in Guru Arjan's twenty-third astpadi. The Bhakti poets described this state in terms of the *sahaja* experience: a spontaneous, unforced awareness of divine presence that transforms the mundane into the sacred.
