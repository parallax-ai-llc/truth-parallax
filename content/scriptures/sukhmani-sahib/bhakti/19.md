## Bhakti Reading

The Bhakti tradition's conviction that liberation is attainable through devotion alone — without the need for Vedic learning, priestly mediation, or ascetic practice — finds its Sikh expression in Guru Arjan's teaching that *Nam* is the key to *mukti*. This radical democratization of salvation is perhaps the most revolutionary contribution of the Sant-Bhakti movement to Indian religious thought.
