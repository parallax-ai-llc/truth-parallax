## Bhakti Reading

The Bhakti tradition's teaching on surrender to divine will — the recognition that the devotee's role is not to control but to love, not to direct but to trust — resonates through Guru Arjan's teaching on *Hukam*. The Bhakti poets who surrendered everything to God — Mirabai her royal status, Kabir his social respectability, Ravidas his caste stigma — model the total surrender that Guru Arjan commends.
