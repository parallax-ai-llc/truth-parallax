## Bhakti Reading

Guru Arjan's theology of the Supreme Being reflects the Bhakti movement's distinctive emphasis on a personal, loving God — *saguna Brahman* who responds to devotion and enters into relationship with the devotee. While Guru Arjan also affirms God's transcendence (*nirguna*), the dominant register of the Sukhmani is relational and devotional, in keeping with the Bhakti tradition's insistence that God is not merely an abstract principle but a loving presence.
