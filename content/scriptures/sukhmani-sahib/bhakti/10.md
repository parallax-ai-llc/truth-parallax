## Bhakti Reading

The Brahmgiani's qualities of compassion and equanimity described in this astpadi resonate with the Bhakti ideal of *samadarshi* — seeing all beings with equal vision. The Sant-Bhakti poets, like Guru Arjan, taught that the realized devotee transcends all distinctions of high and low, pure and impure, friend and enemy, recognizing the same divine light in every heart.
