## Bhakti Reading

The Bhakti movement's radical universalism — its insistence that God's grace is available to all regardless of caste, gender, or religious affiliation — finds its most powerful Sikh expression in this astpadi. The Bhakti poets' rejection of spiritual exclusivism and their affirmation that God hears the cry of every sincere heart resonate through Guru Arjan's declaration that divine grace knows no boundaries.
