## Bhakti Reading

The Bhakti poets shared Guru Arjan's condemnation of slander and judgment as spiritual poisons. Kabir warned that 'the one who speaks ill of others fills their own mouth with filth,' and Ravidas taught that the devotee who sees only God in all beings has no occasion for criticism. The Sukhmani's teaching reinforces this Bhakti ethic of radical non-judgment.
