## Bhakti Reading

The Bhakti tradition's emphasis on the preciousness and urgency of human life — the teaching that human birth is the rare opportunity for liberation that must not be squandered — pervades this astpadi. Kabir's famous image of the swan that must cross the ocean of existence before the body fails captures the same urgency that Guru Arjan expresses in his call to devote this precious human life to *Nam Simran*.
