## Bhakti Reading

The Bhakti tradition's teaching on contentment resonates with Guru Arjan's celebration of *santokh*. The Sant-Bhakti poets, living often in material poverty, testified that the wealth of divine love far surpasses any worldly possession. Their contentment was not resignation but the overflowing joy of those who have found the treasure that no thief can steal and no king can confiscate.
