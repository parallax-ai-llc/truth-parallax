## Bhakti Reading

The Bhakti reading of Guru Arjan's celebration of holy fellowship recalls the tradition of the *satsang* — the gathering of devotees for communal singing and spiritual discourse. The Bhakti poets treasured the company of fellow devotees as essential nourishment for the spiritual life, teaching that even a brief encounter with a realized saint could transform one's entire existence.
