## Bhakti Reading

Guru Arjan's celebration of creation's wonder echoes the Bhakti tradition's practice of seeing God's handwork in every natural phenomenon. The Bhakti poets found God in the monsoon rain, the flowing river, the blooming lotus, and the rising sun — an approach to nature as divine self-expression that the Sukhmani shares and amplifies. This natural theology is one of the Bhakti tradition's most distinctive contributions to Indian religious thought.
