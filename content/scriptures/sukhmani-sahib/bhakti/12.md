## Bhakti Reading

The Bhakti movement's emphasis on humility as the prerequisite for divine grace finds powerful confirmation in Guru Arjan's teaching. Kabir declared that 'the seed of devotion grows only in the soil of humility,' and Guru Arjan's analysis of pride as the fundamental obstacle to spiritual realization echoes this teaching. The Bhakti tradition, like the Sukhmani, teaches that God fills only the empty vessel.
