## Bhakti Reading

The Bhakti tradition's emphasis on the tangible, experiential fruits of devotion finds expression in Guru Arjan's second astpadi. The Bhakti poets consistently taught that devotion to God yields not merely future salvation but present transformation — inner peace, freedom from fear, and the joy of divine companionship. Guru Arjan's promises resonate with Mirabai's ecstatic declarations and Kabir's confident assertions of the Name's power.
