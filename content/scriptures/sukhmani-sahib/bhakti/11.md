## Bhakti Reading

The Bhakti tradition's trust in divine protection echoes through Guru Arjan's eleventh astpadi. The Bhakti poets consistently testified that God protects those who take refuge in the divine Name — Prahlada before Hiranyakashipu, Draupadi in the court of the Kauravas, and countless unnamed devotees whose stories of divine rescue fill the Bhakti hagiographic tradition.
