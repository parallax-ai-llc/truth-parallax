## Interfaith Reading

Guru Arjan's proclamation of the one God of all provides the theological foundation for interfaith respect without requiring theological uniformity. The teaching that the same divine reality is approached through different names, forms, and practices allows for genuine religious diversity within a framework of mutual recognition and shared devotion to the sacred.
