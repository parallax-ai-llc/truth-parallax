## Interfaith Reading

The Sukhmani's teaching on *maya* opens interfaith conversation about materialism, consumerism, and the spiritual cost of worldly attachment. Every tradition warns against the spiritual dangers of excessive attachment to material things, and this shared concern provides a basis for interfaith collaboration on issues of economic justice and sustainable living.
