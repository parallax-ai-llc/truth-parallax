## Interfaith Reading

The Sukhmani's celebration of spiritual community invites interfaith reflection on the role of fellowship in the spiritual life. Interfaith groups themselves represent a new form of 'holy fellowship' — communities of practitioners from different traditions who support each other's spiritual growth while maintaining their distinctive identities.
