## Interfaith Reading

The Sukhmani's teaching on the ethics of speech has direct practical application in interfaith relations: the commitment not to slander or misrepresent other traditions, to speak of others as we would wish to be spoken of ourselves, and to recognize the divine image in every person regardless of their religious identity.
