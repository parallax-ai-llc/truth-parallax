## Interfaith Reading

Guru Arjan's teaching on humility provides a foundation for interfaith encounter itself — for genuine dialogue requires the humility to acknowledge that one's own tradition does not possess all truth and that the other may have insights of genuine spiritual value. The Sukhmani's destruction of pride is the precondition for authentic interfaith listening.
