## Interfaith Reading

Guru Arjan's portrait of the God-realized person opens interfaith dialogue about what spiritual perfection looks like across traditions. The shared recognition that the fully realized human being is characterized by compassion, equanimity, and service to others — regardless of the theological framework — provides powerful common ground for interfaith ethics.
