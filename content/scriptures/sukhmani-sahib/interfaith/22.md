## Interfaith Reading

Guru Arjan's teaching on the grace of the Guru opens interfaith dialogue about the role of teachers, mentors, and spiritual guides in the life of faith. Every tradition recognizes the need for guidance on the spiritual path, and sharing stories of the teachers who have shaped our spiritual lives is one of the most personal and connecting forms of interfaith conversation.
