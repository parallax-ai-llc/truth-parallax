## Interfaith Reading

The Sukhmani's promises of peace, protection, and spiritual well-being through devotion resonate with the shared experience of practitioners across traditions. Interfaith dialogue discovers that the fruits of sincere spiritual practice — inner peace, reduced anxiety, deepened compassion, strengthened resilience — are remarkably consistent across religious boundaries, suggesting a common human capacity for spiritual transformation.
