## Interfaith Reading

The Sukhmani's teaching on divine protection and providence opens interfaith dialogue about trust and the problem of suffering. Every tradition must account for the presence of suffering in a world governed by a loving God, and the Sukhmani's combination of trust in divine care with honest acknowledgment of life's difficulties provides a mature starting point for this conversation.
