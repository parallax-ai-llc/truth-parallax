## Interfaith Reading

The Brahmgiani's equanimity and non-judgment provide a model for interfaith practitioners: the capacity to encounter difference without anxiety, to appreciate the other's tradition without abandoning one's own, and to find the shared divine light that illumines all authentic spiritual paths.
