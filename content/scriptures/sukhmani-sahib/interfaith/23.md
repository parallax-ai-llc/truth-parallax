## Interfaith Reading

The Sukhmani's description of *sahaj* — natural, effortless divine awareness — opens interfaith dialogue about the goal of the spiritual life. This state of integration, in which the sacred and the ordinary are no longer experienced as separate, represents the aspiration of contemplatives across traditions and provides a shared vision of what spiritual maturity looks like.
