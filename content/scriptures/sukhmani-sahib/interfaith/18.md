## Interfaith Reading

The Sukhmani's celebration of contentment invites interfaith reflection on the meaning of the good life. In a consumer culture that equates well-being with material accumulation, the shared wisdom of contentment traditions across religions offers a powerful countercultural message that interfaith groups are uniquely positioned to articulate.
