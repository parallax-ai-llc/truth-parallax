## Interfaith Reading

Guru Arjan's invitation to cosmic wonder creates a shared contemplative space for interfaith encounter. The practice of gazing at the night sky, walking in a forest, or listening to running water can become an interfaith contemplative practice in which participants from different traditions share the same experience of awe before the mystery of creation.
