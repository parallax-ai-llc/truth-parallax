## Interfaith Reading

The Sukhmani's teaching on liberation through devotion opens interfaith dialogue about the ultimate purpose of human existence. While traditions differ on the specifics of salvation, liberation, or enlightenment, all share the conviction that human life aims at a fulfillment that transcends ordinary worldly achievement — and this shared conviction provides the deepest common ground for interfaith encounter.
