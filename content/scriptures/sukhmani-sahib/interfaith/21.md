## Interfaith Reading

The Sukhmani's teaching on surrender to divine will opens interfaith dialogue about the spiritual practice of letting go — releasing the need to control outcomes and trusting in a wisdom greater than our own. This teaching resonates across traditions and provides a shared basis for spiritual practice in times of uncertainty and crisis.
