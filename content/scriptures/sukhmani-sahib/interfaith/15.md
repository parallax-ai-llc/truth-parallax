## Interfaith Reading

The Sukhmani's affirmation of universal divine grace provides the theological foundation for interfaith openness: if God's compassion extends to all beings without exception, then no tradition can claim an exclusive monopoly on divine truth or favor. This teaching is the Sikh contribution to the interfaith principle of mutual recognition and respect.
