## Interfaith Reading

Guru Arjan's teaching on the preciousness of human life opens interfaith dialogue about human dignity, rights, and the sacred value of every person. This shared conviction — that human life is a gift of transcendent worth — provides the basis for interfaith collaboration on issues of social justice, human rights, and the protection of vulnerable populations.
