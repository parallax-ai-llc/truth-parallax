## Interfaith Reading

Guru Arjan's theology of the Supreme Being opens interfaith dialogue about how different traditions name and understand the Ultimate Reality. This conversation need not aim at theological agreement but can deepen each participant's understanding of their own tradition through encounter with others. The Sukhmani's simultaneous affirmation of divine transcendence and immanence provides a balanced starting point.
