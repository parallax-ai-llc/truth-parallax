## Interfaith Reading

The Sukhmani's joyous conclusion invites interfaith readers to celebrate together the gift of shared spiritual seeking. The journey through 24 astpadis — encountering themes of meditation, humility, wonder, grace, and peace — creates a bond among readers that transcends doctrinal differences. Guru Arjan's final celebration of the divine Name is an invitation to all seekers, of every tradition, to taste the peace that is the birthright of every human soul.
