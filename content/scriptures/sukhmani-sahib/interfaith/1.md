## Interfaith Reading

Guru Arjan's opening teaching on divine remembrance provides a powerful starting point for interfaith dialogue about meditation and contemplative practice. Practitioners of all faiths — and contemplative traditions within secular contexts — share the experience that sustained attention to the sacred transforms consciousness. Interfaith meditation groups find in the Sukhmani's opening a universal invitation to the practice of presence.
