## Interfaith Reading

Guru Arjan's teaching on God's presence in all creation opens interfaith dialogue about the relationship between the sacred and the natural world. In an era of ecological crisis, this shared teaching — that nature is not merely a resource to be exploited but a manifestation of the divine — provides a theological foundation for interfaith environmental action.
