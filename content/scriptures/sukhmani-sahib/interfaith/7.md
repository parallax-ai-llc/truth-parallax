## Interfaith Reading

Guru Arjan's critique of empty ritualism opens interfaith conversation about the relationship between form and spirit in religious practice. Every tradition wrestles with the danger that its rituals may become mechanical performances devoid of inner meaning. This shared challenge provides a basis for mutual understanding and constructive self-examination across religious boundaries.
