## Interfaith Reading

The Sukhmani's teaching on the transformative power of sacred scripture opens interfaith dialogue about the role of texts in spiritual life. Interfaith scripture study — the practice of reading each other's sacred texts with respect and openness — is one of the most fruitful forms of interfaith encounter, and the Sukhmani itself is an ideal text for such shared reading.
