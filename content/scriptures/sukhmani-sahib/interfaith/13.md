## Interfaith Reading

The Sukhmani's analysis of ego as the fundamental obstacle to spiritual realization resonates across traditions, opening interfaith dialogue about the nature of the self and the possibility of its transformation. This shared anthropological concern — what is the self, and how can it be liberated from its own limitations? — lies at the heart of all serious interfaith theological conversation.
