## Sikh Reading

Guru Arjan teaches that slander, criticism, and judgment of others are among the most destructive spiritual vices — poisoning the soul of the speaker far more than the one spoken about. The Sikh reads this teaching as a practical discipline for daily life, learning to guard the tongue and to see the divine light in every person, however flawed they may appear. This astpadi reinforces the Sikh ethical commitment to seeing the best in others.
