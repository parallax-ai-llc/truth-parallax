## Sikh Reading

This astpadi deepens the analysis of *haumai* (ego/self-will) as the root cause of spiritual suffering. Guru Arjan distinguishes between the *Gurmukh* (one oriented toward the Guru) and the *Manmukh* (one oriented toward the self), showing how self-will leads to separation from God, while surrender to the Guru's wisdom leads to liberation. The Sikh reads this teaching as a daily challenge to examine whether their choices flow from ego or from divine guidance.
