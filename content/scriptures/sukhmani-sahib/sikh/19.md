## Sikh Reading

This astpadi teaches that true liberation (*mukti*) is found not through elaborate spiritual techniques but through the simple, devoted practice of *Nam Simran*. Guru Arjan declares that the Name is the key that unlocks the door of liberation, freeing the soul from the cycle of birth and death. The Sikh reads this teaching as a reaffirmation that the path to God is accessible to all — requiring not scholarly learning or ascetic prowess but loving devotion.
