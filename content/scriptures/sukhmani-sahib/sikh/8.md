## Sikh Reading

The eighth astpadi celebrates the *Shabad* — the divine Word as revealed through the Guru — as the supreme instrument of spiritual transformation. For the Sikh, the Guru Granth Sahib is the living Guru, and its *Gurbani* (the Guru's utterance) has the power to transform consciousness, dissolve ego, and unite the soul with God. This astpadi reinforces the centrality of scripture in Sikh spiritual practice.
