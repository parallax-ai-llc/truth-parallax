## Sikh Reading

Guru Arjan proclaims the absolute oneness of God — the One Creator who fashioned all worlds, all beings, all faiths. This astpadi is a powerful statement of Sikh monotheism and universalism, affirming that the same divine light illumines every tradition, every culture, every human heart. The Sikh reads this teaching as the theological basis for interfaith respect and the rejection of religious exclusivism.
