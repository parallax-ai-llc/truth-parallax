## Sikh Reading

This astpadi deepens the teaching on surrender to *Hukam* — the divine will that governs all creation. Guru Arjan teaches that true spiritual maturity consists in joyful acceptance of whatever comes — pleasure or pain, success or failure, life or death — as the expression of Waheguru's loving purpose. The Sikh devotee who has internalized this teaching lives with an unshakeable serenity that the world's turbulence cannot disturb.
