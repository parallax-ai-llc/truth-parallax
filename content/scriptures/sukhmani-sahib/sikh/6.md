## Sikh Reading

Guru Arjan addresses the power of *maya* — the illusory attachment to worldly things that keeps the soul trapped in the cycle of suffering. The Sikh understanding of maya is distinctive: the world itself is not illusory (as in some interpretations of Advaita Vedanta) but the attachment to worldly things in forgetfulness of God is the source of suffering. This astpadi teaches the devotee to live in the world while remaining inwardly oriented toward Waheguru.
