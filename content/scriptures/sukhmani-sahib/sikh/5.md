## Sikh Reading

This astpadi is a sustained meditation on the nature of the Supreme Being — *Akal Purakh* (the Timeless One) — who is beyond all description yet intimately present in all creation. Guru Arjan employs the *neti-neti* (not this, not that) approach alongside positive descriptions, acknowledging that God transcends all categories while affirming the divine qualities of love, justice, compassion, and grace that the devotee encounters in practice.
