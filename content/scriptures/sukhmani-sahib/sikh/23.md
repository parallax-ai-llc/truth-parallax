## Sikh Reading

This astpadi describes the state of *sahaj* — the natural equipoise of the soul that has realized its unity with God. *Sahaj* is not an extraordinary trance state but the ordinary consciousness of one who lives in constant divine awareness — performing daily duties, engaging with the world, yet never losing the inner connection with Waheguru. The Sikh reads this teaching as the goal of all spiritual practice: not to escape the world but to live in it with divine grace.
