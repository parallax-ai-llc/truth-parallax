## Sikh Reading

Guru Arjan invites the devotee to wonder at the vastness and intricacy of God's creation — the countless worlds, the endless variety of life, the unfathomable divine play (*lila*) that sustains all existence. This astpadi cultivates the Sikh virtue of *vismad* (wonder) — the capacity to be astonished by the beauty and mystery of creation as a direct perception of God's nature. The Sikh who recites these verses is drawn into an experience of cosmic awe.
