## Sikh Reading

The second astpadi reveals the abundant fruits of *Nam Simran*: inner peace, protection from suffering, freedom from fear, and the blessing of divine companionship. Guru Arjan teaches that one who remembers God is never alone, never destitute, never without purpose. The Sikh reader experiences this astpadi as both promise and invitation — an assurance that the practice of divine remembrance yields tangible spiritual benefits in this life, not merely in some distant afterlife.
