## Sikh Reading

The fourth astpadi celebrates the transformative power of *sat sangat* — the company of the holy. Guru Arjan teaches that spiritual growth flourishes in community, that the seeker finds strength, inspiration, and correction in the fellowship of those who love God. For the Sikh, this teaching is lived in the daily practice of *sangat* at the Gurdwara, where the community gathers for *kirtan* (devotional singing), *langar* (communal meal), and *sewa* (selfless service).
