## Sikh Reading

Guru Arjan begins a sustained portrait of the *Brahmgiani* — the God-realized person who has attained the highest spiritual state while remaining active in the world. This ideal figure is not a withdrawn ascetic but a fully engaged human being whose every action flows from divine awareness. The Sikh reads this description as both an aspiration and a recognition of the qualities embodied by the Sikh Gurus themselves.
