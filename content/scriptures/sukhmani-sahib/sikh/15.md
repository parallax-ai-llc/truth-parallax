## Sikh Reading

This astpadi affirms the universality of God's grace — that divine compassion extends to all beings regardless of merit, status, or religious affiliation. Guru Arjan's teaching here is the theological foundation of the Sikh commitment to *sarbat da bhala* (the welfare of all humanity). The Sikh reads this astpadi as a call to embody divine grace in their own lives through *sewa* (selfless service) and compassion for all beings.
