## Sikh Reading

Guru Arjan celebrates the grace of the True Guru — the divine teacher who awakens the sleeping soul and guides it home to God. For the Sikh, the True Guru is ultimately Waheguru, speaking through the historical Gurus and now through the Guru Granth Sahib. This astpadi teaches that the seeker cannot find God through self-effort alone but needs the light of the Guru's wisdom to dispel the darkness of ignorance.
