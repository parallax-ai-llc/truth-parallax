## Sikh Reading

The Sikh devotee begins the Sukhmani journey with Guru Arjan's foundational teaching: *Simrau Simar Simar Sukh Paavau* — 'Meditate, meditate, meditate and find peace.' This opening astpadi establishes *Nam Simran* as the supreme spiritual practice, the means by which the mind is stilled, the heart is purified, and the soul is reunited with its divine source. For the Sikh, Nam is not merely a word or mantra but the living presence of Waheguru — the Wondrous Lord — vibrating in every atom of creation.
