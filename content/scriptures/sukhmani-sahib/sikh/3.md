## Sikh Reading

Guru Arjan expands the devotee's vision to recognize God's presence in every particle of creation — in the vast cosmos and in the smallest insect, in the saint and in the sinner, in life and in death. This astpadi cultivates the Sikh awareness of *Ik Onkar* — the One Creator pervading all existence. The Sikh reads this teaching as the theological foundation of the Sikh commitment to equality: because God dwells in all beings, no person may be considered high or low by birth, caste, or status.
