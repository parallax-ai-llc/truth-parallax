## Sikh Reading

Guru Arjan celebrates *santokh* (contentment) and *razaa* (acceptance of divine will) as essential qualities of the spiritual life. The Sikh understanding of contentment is not passivity but active acceptance — living fully in the present moment while trusting that Waheguru's *Hukam* (divine order) governs all things for the ultimate good. This astpadi teaches the devotee to release the burden of anxiety about outcomes and to rest in divine providence.
