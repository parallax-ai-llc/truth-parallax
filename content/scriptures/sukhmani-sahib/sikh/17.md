## Sikh Reading

This astpadi celebrates the preciousness of human birth as a rare opportunity for spiritual realization. Guru Arjan teaches that human life, with its capacity for conscious devotion and moral choice, is the gateway to liberation — and that to waste this opportunity in pursuit of worldly pleasure is a tragedy of cosmic proportions. The Sikh devotee receives this teaching as an urgent call to dedicate their life to *Nam Simran* and *sewa*.
