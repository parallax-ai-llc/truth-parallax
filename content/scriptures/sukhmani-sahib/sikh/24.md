## Sikh Reading

The final astpadi is a joyous celebration of the divine Name and a summation of the Sukhmani's entire spiritual journey. Guru Arjan returns to the theme of *Nam Simran* with which the composition began, now enriched by all the teachings that have intervened. The Sikh devotee who completes the recitation of Sukhmani Sahib experiences a sense of spiritual completion and renewal — the peace (*sukh*) that is the jewel (*mani*) promised in the composition's title.
