## Sikh Reading

This astpadi celebrates God as the protector and sustainer of all creation — the One who provides for every being, who hears every prayer, and whose grace is the foundation of all existence. The Sikh devotee receives this teaching as an invitation to *bharosa* (trust) — the confident reliance on divine providence that frees the heart from anxiety and fear. Guru Arjan's poetry here reaches heights of tender devotion that have made these verses among the most beloved in the Sikh tradition.
