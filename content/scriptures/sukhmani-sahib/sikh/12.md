## Sikh Reading

Guru Arjan addresses the spiritual disease of pride (*ahankar*) and the healing power of humility. The Sikh understanding of humility is not self-deprecation but the accurate recognition that all gifts come from Waheguru and that the individual self has no independent existence apart from the divine. This astpadi teaches the practice of *nimrata* (humility) as the essential complement to *Nam Simran* — for the Name can only take root in a heart freed from the thorns of ego.
