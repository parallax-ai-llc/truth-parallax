## Comparative Reading

The Sukhmani's celebration of holy fellowship connects with the universal recognition that spiritual growth requires community: the Christian *ecclesia*, the Buddhist *sangha*, the Muslim *ummah*, the Jewish *kehillah*, and the Hindu *satsang*. Comparative study examines how different traditions structure their communities of practice and what role communal worship plays in individual spiritual development.
