## Comparative Reading

The affirmation of divine oneness connects with monotheistic and monistic traditions worldwide: Islamic *tawhid*, Jewish *Shema*, Christian Trinitarianism, Hindu *advaita*, Sikh *Ik Onkar*, and the Bahá'í concept of progressive revelation. Comparative study examines how different traditions understand divine unity and what implications their concepts of oneness have for religious diversity.
