## Comparative Reading

The analysis of ego as the source of spiritual suffering is remarkably consistent across traditions: the Buddhist concept of *atta/anatta*, the Christian teaching on original sin, the Sufi analysis of *nafs*, the Hindu concept of *ahamkara*, and the Daoist critique of self-assertion. Guru Arjan's *haumai* concept adds a distinctive voice to this global conversation about the self and its transcendence.
