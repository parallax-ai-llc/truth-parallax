## Comparative Reading

The celebration of cosmic wonder as a spiritual practice connects with similar traditions: the Jewish *berakhot* (blessings) that sanctify every experience, the Christian *Canticle of the Sun*, the Daoist appreciation of nature's spontaneous order, and the Buddhist practice of mindful awareness. Each tradition finds in the contemplation of creation a pathway to the sacred.
