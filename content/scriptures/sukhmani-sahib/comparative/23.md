## Comparative Reading

The ideal of natural, effortless spiritual awareness finds expression across traditions: Sikh *sahaj*, Daoist *ziran* (naturalness), Buddhist *sahaja* in Tantric traditions, Christian *infused contemplation*, and the Zen concept of *mushin* (no-mind). Each tradition describes a state beyond effort in which spiritual awareness becomes as natural as breathing.
