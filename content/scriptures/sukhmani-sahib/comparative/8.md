## Comparative Reading

The concept of a transformative sacred text finds parallels across traditions: the Qur'an as *kalam Allah*, the Torah as divine instruction, the Buddhist *sutras* as 'fingers pointing at the moon,' and the Vedas as *shruti* (that which is heard). Comparative study examines how different traditions understand the relationship between the written word and the living spiritual reality it conveys.
