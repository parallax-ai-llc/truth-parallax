## Comparative Reading

Guru Arjan's critique of ritualism connects with a broad cross-cultural tradition of prophetic protest against empty formalism: the Hebrew prophets' declaration that God desires mercy rather than sacrifice, the Buddhist critique of Brahmanical ritual, Jesus's cleansing of the Temple, and the Confucian emphasis on sincerity (*cheng*) over ceremony. Each tradition contains within itself a self-critical voice that challenges institutional religion in the name of authentic spirituality.
