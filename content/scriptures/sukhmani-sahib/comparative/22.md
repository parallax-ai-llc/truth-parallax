## Comparative Reading

The role of the spiritual teacher is acknowledged across traditions: the Sikh *Guru*, the Buddhist *kalyana-mitta*, the Christian spiritual director, the Sufi *murshid*, the Hindu *guru*, and the Jewish *rebbe*. Comparative study examines the different models of spiritual authority and transmission that these figures represent.
