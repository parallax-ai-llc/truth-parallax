## Comparative Reading

The teaching on the preciousness of human life connects with similar doctrines across traditions: the Buddhist concept of the 'precious human rebirth,' the Jewish teaching that each life is an entire world, the Islamic concept of humanity as God's *khalifa* (vicegerent), and the Christian doctrine of the *imago Dei*. Each tradition provides a distinctive rationale for valuing human existence as a unique spiritual opportunity.
