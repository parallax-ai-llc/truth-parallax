## Comparative Reading

The theme of divine protection and providence connects with similar teachings across traditions: the Jewish concept of *hashgacha pratit* (divine providence), the Christian teaching on God's care for the lilies and sparrows, the Islamic *tawakkul*, and the Hindu concept of *ishvara pranidhana* (surrender to God). Each tradition affirms that trust in divine care is both spiritually transformative and practically liberating.
