## Comparative Reading

Comparative study places Guru Arjan's teaching on *Nam Simran* alongside other traditions' practices of sacred invocation: the Christian *Jesus Prayer* ('Lord Jesus Christ, Son of God, have mercy on me'), the Islamic *dhikr*, the Hindu *japa*, the Buddhist *nembutsu*, and the Jewish practice of repeating the *Shema*. Each tradition affirms that the repetition of a sacred name or formula transforms consciousness and opens the soul to divine grace.
