## Comparative Reading

The concept of surrender to divine will finds expression across traditions: Islamic *islam* (submission), Christian prayers of abandonment, Hindu *prapatti* (self-surrender), Buddhist refuge, and Jewish *bittul* (self-nullification). Guru Arjan's *Hukam* adds a distinctive emphasis on divine will as an all-encompassing order that includes both nature and moral law.
