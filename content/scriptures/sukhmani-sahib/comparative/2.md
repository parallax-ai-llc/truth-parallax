## Comparative Reading

The spiritual fruits of divine remembrance described in this astpadi find parallels across traditions: the Christian concept of the 'peace that passes understanding,' the Buddhist *sukha* (well-being) arising from meditation, the Stoic *ataraxia*, and the Jewish *menucha* (rest of the soul). Comparative analysis reveals a near-universal recognition that sustained spiritual practice yields tangible benefits for inner life.
