## Comparative Reading

The virtue of contentment is celebrated across traditions: Stoic *autarkeia*, Buddhist *santutthi*, Epicurean simplicity, Jewish *histapkut*, and Islamic *qana'a*. Guru Arjan's *santokh* adds the distinctively Sikh dimension of contentment as acceptance of *Hukam* — finding peace not in the absence of difficulty but in the trust that divine order governs all things.
