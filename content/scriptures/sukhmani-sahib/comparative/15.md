## Comparative Reading

The affirmation of universal divine grace connects with similar teachings across traditions: the Christian doctrine of prevenient grace, the Buddhist teaching that all beings possess buddha-nature, the Islamic affirmation that God's mercy encompasses all things, and the Jewish concept of divine *chesed* (lovingkindness). Guru Arjan's universalism participates in a broad cross-traditional affirmation of divine generosity.
