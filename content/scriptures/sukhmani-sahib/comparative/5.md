## Comparative Reading

Comparative theology examines how different traditions describe the nature of Ultimate Reality: the personal God of Abrahamic monotheism, the *nirguna Brahman* of Advaita Vedanta, the *Dharmakaya* of Mahayana Buddhism, the *Dao* of Chinese philosophy. Guru Arjan's synthesis of personal and impersonal divine attributes provides a distinctive perspective in this global theological conversation.
