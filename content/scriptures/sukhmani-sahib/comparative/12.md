## Comparative Reading

Humility as a universal spiritual virtue is examined across traditions: Christian *humilitas*, Islamic *tawadu*, Buddhist recognition of *anatta*, Confucian deference, and Daoist praise of the 'valley spirit.' Guru Arjan's teaching on humility as the destruction of ego participates in this global consensus while adding the distinctive Sikh emphasis on *nimrata* as active service rather than passive self-deprecation.
