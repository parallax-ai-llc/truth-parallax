## Comparative Reading

The concept of *maya* or worldly illusion finds parallels across traditions: the Buddhist teaching of *avidya* (ignorance), the Platonic distinction between appearance and reality, the Christian concept of the 'world' as opposed to the 'Kingdom,' and the Daoist critique of conventional values. Comparative analysis reveals both shared insights and important differences in how traditions understand the relationship between spiritual reality and the material world.
