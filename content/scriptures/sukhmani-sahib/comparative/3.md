## Comparative Reading

Guru Arjan's teaching on divine immanence invites comparison with similar teachings across traditions: the Christian concept of *omnipresence*, the Sufi *wahdat al-wujud*, the Buddhist teaching of *buddha-nature* pervading all beings, and the Stoic *logos* permeating all reality. Each tradition affirms, in its own terminology, that the sacred is not confined to temples and scriptures but pervades all existence.
