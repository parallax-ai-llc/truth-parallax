## Comparative Reading

The theme of liberation or salvation takes different forms across traditions: Sikh *mukti*, Hindu *moksha*, Buddhist *nirvana*, Christian salvation, and Islamic *najat*. Comparative study examines how these concepts differ — in their understanding of what one is liberated from and what one is liberated into — while noting the shared conviction that human existence aims at a fulfillment that transcends ordinary worldly life.
