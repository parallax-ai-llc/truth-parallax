## Sufi Reading

The Sukhmani's celebration of the divine Word (*Shabad*) as a transformative force parallels the Sufi understanding of the Qur'an as *kalam Allah* (the speech of God) — a living divine utterance that has the power to transform the soul of the receptive listener. Both traditions teach that sacred scripture is not merely information about God but a direct encounter with divine presence.
