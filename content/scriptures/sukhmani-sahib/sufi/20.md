## Sufi Reading

Guru Arjan's proclamation of divine oneness resonates with the Sufi concept of *tawhid* — the absolute unity of God that is the foundation of Islamic theology and the ultimate realization of the mystic. The Sufi reads this astpadi as an expression of the same *tawhid* that Sufi masters have proclaimed through the centuries — the recognition that beneath the multiplicity of creation, there is only One.
