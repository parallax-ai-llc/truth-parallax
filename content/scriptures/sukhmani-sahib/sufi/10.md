## Sufi Reading

The Brahmgiani's equanimity and freedom from judgment described in this astpadi parallel the Sufi station of *rida* (contentment with divine decree). The Sufi who has reached this station, like the Brahmgiani, remains undisturbed by praise or blame, seeing all events as expressions of divine will. Both traditions teach that the highest spiritual attainment is not ecstatic experience but steady, unshakeable peace.
