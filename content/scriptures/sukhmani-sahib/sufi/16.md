## Sufi Reading

Guru Arjan's warning against slander and judgment parallels the Sufi teaching on *ghiba* (backbiting) as a spiritual poison. The Sufi masters, like Guru Arjan, teach that speaking ill of others harms the speaker far more than the spoken-about, darkening the mirror of the heart and obstructing the flow of divine light.
