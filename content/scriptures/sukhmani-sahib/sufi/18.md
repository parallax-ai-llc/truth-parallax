## Sufi Reading

The Sukhmani's teaching on contentment and divine will corresponds directly to the Sufi station of *rida* (contentment) and the practice of *tawakkul* (trust). The Sufi who has reached the station of *rida* lives, like Guru Arjan's ideal devotee, in joyful acceptance of whatever the divine will brings, finding peace not in favorable circumstances but in the unshakeable certainty of divine love.
