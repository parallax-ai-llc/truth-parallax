## Sufi Reading

The state of *sahaj* described in this astpadi corresponds to the Sufi station of *baqa* — the state of subsistence in God that follows the annihilation (*fana*) of the ego. Both *sahaj* and *baqa* describe a condition of natural, effortless divine awareness in which the distinction between sacred and secular, worship and daily life, dissolves in the continuous experience of God's presence.
