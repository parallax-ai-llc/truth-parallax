## Sufi Reading

The Sukhmani's teaching on *maya* resonates with the Sufi concept of *dunya* (the worldly) as the veil that obscures divine reality. The Sufi, like Guru Arjan, does not reject the material world as inherently evil but warns against *ghafla* (heedlessness) — the spiritual sleep that comes from absorption in worldly concerns to the exclusion of divine awareness.
