## Sufi Reading

The spiritual fruits of divine remembrance described in this astpadi correspond to the Sufi teaching on the transformative effects of *dhikr*: inner peace (*sakina*), protection from spiritual harm, and the gradual purification of the heart (*tasfiya al-qalb*). The Sufi recognizes in Guru Arjan's poetry the same experiential knowledge that Sufi masters have transmitted through centuries of practice and teaching.
