## Sufi Reading

The Sukhmani's teaching on liberation through the Name parallels the Sufi teaching that *dhikr* is the key to *fana* (annihilation of the ego) and *baqa* (subsistence in God). Both traditions affirm that the repetition of the divine Name is the most direct and accessible path to spiritual liberation — requiring no special qualifications beyond sincere devotion and persistent practice.
