## Sufi Reading

The Sufi reads Guru Arjan's analysis of ego (*haumai*) with recognition, finding in it a precise counterpart to the Sufi analysis of *nafs* (the ego-self). Both traditions identify self-will as the source of spiritual blindness and both prescribe the same remedy: surrender to the divine will through the guidance of the spiritual master.
