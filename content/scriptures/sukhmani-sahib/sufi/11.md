## Sufi Reading

The Sukhmani's teaching on divine protection resonates with the Sufi concept of *tawakkul* (trust in God) — the complete reliance on divine providence that frees the heart from fear and anxiety. The Sufi tradition's stories of saints who trusted entirely in God's care and were never disappointed find a parallel in Guru Arjan's poetic assurances of divine solicitude.
