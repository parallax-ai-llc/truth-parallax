## Sufi Reading

The Sukhmani's affirmation of universal divine grace resonates with the Sufi teaching of *rahma* (divine mercy) as the fundamental attribute of God. The Sufi tradition affirms, as does Guru Arjan, that God's compassion extends to all beings without exception, and that the recognition of this universal mercy is the foundation of authentic spiritual life.
