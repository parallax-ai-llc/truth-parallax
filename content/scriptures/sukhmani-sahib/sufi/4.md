## Sufi Reading

The Sukhmani's celebration of holy fellowship parallels the Sufi emphasis on the *suhba* (companionship) of the spiritual master and the *majlis* (gathering) of the seekers. Both traditions teach that spiritual growth is nourished by the company of those who have tasted divine love, and that the *baraka* (spiritual blessing) of the realized master is transmitted through physical proximity.
