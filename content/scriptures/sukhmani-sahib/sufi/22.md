## Sufi Reading

The Sukhmani's celebration of the Guru's grace parallels the Sufi emphasis on the *murshid* (spiritual guide) as the essential intermediary between the seeker and God. Both traditions teach that the path cannot be walked alone — that divine grace operates through the agency of the realized teacher who has traversed the path and can guide others along it.
