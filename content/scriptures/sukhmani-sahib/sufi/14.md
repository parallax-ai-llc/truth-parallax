## Sufi Reading

Guru Arjan's invitation to wonder at creation resonates with the Sufi practice of *tafakkur* (contemplation of God's creation) as a form of worship. The Sufi tradition teaches that the contemplation of nature's beauty and intricacy is a direct path to the awareness of divine majesty — a teaching beautifully exemplified in this astpadi's celebration of cosmic wonder.
