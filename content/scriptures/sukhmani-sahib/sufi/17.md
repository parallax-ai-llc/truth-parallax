## Sufi Reading

The Sukhmani's celebration of human life as a precious spiritual opportunity echoes the Sufi teaching that the human state is the unique locus of divine self-knowledge. The Sufi hadith qudsi — 'I was a hidden treasure and I loved to be known, so I created the creation' — suggests that human consciousness is the mirror through which God contemplates the divine beauty.
