## Sufi Reading

The Sufi reader recognizes in Guru Arjan's theology of divine attributes a kindred approach to the Islamic theological tradition of the *asma al-husna* (beautiful names of God). Both traditions affirm that God is beyond all description yet knowable through the divine attributes that manifest in creation and in the experience of the devoted heart.
