## Sufi Reading

The Sufi recognizes in Guru Arjan's portrait of the *Brahmgiani* many qualities of the Sufi *wali* (friend of God): constant awareness of the divine presence, detachment from worldly concerns, compassion for all beings, and the capacity to transmit spiritual blessing. The *Brahmgiani* and the *wali* both represent the fully realized human being who has passed through *fana* to live in perpetual *baqa* (subsistence in God).
