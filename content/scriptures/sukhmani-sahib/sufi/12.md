## Sufi Reading

The Sukhmani's teaching on humility and the destruction of pride parallels the Sufi emphasis on *faqr* (spiritual poverty) as the essential precondition for receiving divine grace. The Sufi masters teach, as does Guru Arjan, that the ego is the fundamental obstacle to spiritual realization and that its dissolution — through humility, service, and devotion — is the central task of the spiritual life.
