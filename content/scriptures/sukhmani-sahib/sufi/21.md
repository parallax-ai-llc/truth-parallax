## Sufi Reading

The Sukhmani's teaching on surrender to *Hukam* corresponds to the Sufi practice of *tafwid* (entrusting all affairs to God) and the station of *rida*. Both traditions teach that the deepest peace comes not from controlling circumstances but from surrendering control to the divine will, trusting that God's purpose, though often hidden, is always loving.
