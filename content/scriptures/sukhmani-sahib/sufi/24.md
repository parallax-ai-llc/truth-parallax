## Sufi Reading

The Sufi reads Guru Arjan's final celebration of the divine Name as a culmination that echoes the Sufi teaching: in the end, as in the beginning, there is only God. The Name that was invoked at the Sukhmani's opening is revealed at its close as the alpha and omega of spiritual life — the beginning, the path, and the destination. The Sufi recognizes in this circular structure the mystic's journey from God, through creation, and back to God.
