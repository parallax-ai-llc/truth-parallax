## Sufi Reading

Guru Arjan's teaching on God's immanence in all creation resonates with the Sufi concept of *wahdat al-wujud* (unity of being) associated with Ibn Arabi — the mystical insight that all existence is a manifestation of the One divine reality. The Sufi reads this astpadi as an expression of the *tajalli* (self-disclosure) of God through the mirror of creation.
