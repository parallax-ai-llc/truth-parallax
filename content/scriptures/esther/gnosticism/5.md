## Strategy Within the Material Drama

Gnostic interpretation reads Esther's banquet strategy as worldly wisdom operating within the archontic system. Her use of beauty, food, wine, and social manipulation — however effective — employs the Demiurge's own tools. Haman's gallows, meanwhile, illustrate how the material system's violence turns on itself.

## Theological Significance

- **Worldly wisdom**: Material tools serve material purposes
- **Self-destructive evil**: The archontic system contains its own contradictions
- **Beauty as instrument**: Physical charm serves political ends
- **Material drama**: The banquet operates entirely within the Demiurge's realm
