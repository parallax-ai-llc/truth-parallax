## Reversal Within the Material System

Gnostic interpretation acknowledges the narrative's stunning reversal while noting its limitations. The reversal operates entirely within the material realm: a human king's insomnia, a bureaucratic record, a social humiliation. Nothing transcends the archontic sphere.

## Theological Significance

- **Material justice**: The reversal is real but limited to the material plane
- **Archontic irony**: Even the archontic system contains reversals
- **No transcendence**: The narrative never breaks through to the Pleroma
- **Limited vindication**: Physical honor does not equate to spiritual liberation
