## Salvation Within the Material Realm

Gnostic interpretation acknowledges the joy of the Jews' deliverance while noting that it is salvation within the material world, not from it. The transformation from mourning to joy is genuine but does not address the fundamental condition: the divine spark remains trapped in matter.

## Theological Significance

- **Material salvation**: Physical deliverance is real but limited
- **Joy within prison**: Celebration without liberation from the material realm
- **Conversion question**: Those who "became Jews" enter a different religion, not a higher reality
- **Partial good**: Survival is better than genocide but is not ultimate liberation
