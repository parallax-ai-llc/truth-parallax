## Justice Within the Prison

Gnostic interpretation reads Haman's downfall as justice within the material system — genuine but insufficient for spiritual liberation. The Demiurge's system, while capable of internal corrections, cannot transcend its own nature. Haman is punished, but the prison itself remains intact.

## Theological Significance

- **Internal justice**: The material system can correct its own excesses
- **Unchanged prison**: Even after justice, the material realm persists
- **Systemic limits**: Political liberation is not spiritual liberation
- **Gallows irony**: The instruments of oppression serve a limited justice
