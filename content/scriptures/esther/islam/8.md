## Deliverance and Celebration

Islamic interpretation reads the Jews' authorization to defend themselves as consistent with the Quranic permission of self-defense: "Permission has been given to those who are being fought, because they were wronged" (22:39). The transformation from mourning to joy reflects God's power to reverse the fortunes of the oppressed.

## Theological Significance

- **Self-defense permitted**: God authorizes the oppressed to defend themselves
- **Reversal**: Mourning becomes joy through divine action
- **Divine protection**: God shields the vulnerable from destruction
- **Celebration**: Joy is the proper response to divine deliverance
