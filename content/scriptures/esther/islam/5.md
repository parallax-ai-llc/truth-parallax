## Patience and Divine Timing

Islamic interpretation reads Esther's strategic patience through the lens of *sabr* (patience). The Quran promises that "Allah is with the patient" (2:153). Esther's willingness to wait for the right moment reflects the Islamic understanding that rushing ahead of God's timing undermines His purposes.

## Theological Significance

- **Sabr**: Patience is rewarded by divine support
- **Divine timing**: God's schedule supersedes human urgency
- **Strategic wisdom**: Planning serves divine purposes
- **Pride's blindness**: The arrogant cannot see their approaching downfall
