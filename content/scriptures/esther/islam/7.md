## Divine Justice Fulfilled

Islamic interpretation reads Haman's downfall as an instance of divine justice (*adl*). The Quran declares: "And never think that Allah is unaware of what the wrongdoers do. He only delays them for a Day when eyes will stare in horror" (14:42). Haman's execution on his own gallows perfectly illustrates this principle.

## Theological Significance

- **Divine justice**: God does not forget the deeds of oppressors
- **Measure for measure**: The wicked receive what they planned for others
- **Intercession**: One person's courage can save an entire community
- **Divine timing**: Justice arrives at the appointed moment
