## Divine Reversal

Islamic interpretation of the king's sleepless night reads it as an instance of divine intervention through natural means. The Quran teaches that God's actions in the world often operate through ordinary causation, and the reversal of Haman's fortunes illustrates this principle.

## Theological Significance

- **Divine intervention**: God works through ordinary events
- **Reversal of fortune**: The proud are humiliated
- **Delayed justice**: God's vindication comes in His timing
- **Divine irony**: The oppressor's plans become instruments of justice
