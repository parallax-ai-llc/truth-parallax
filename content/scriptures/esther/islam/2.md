## Divine Preparation

Islamic interpretation reads Esther's elevation through the lens of divine *qadr* (decree/destiny). God places individuals in positions of influence according to His plan. The Quran teaches that God "creates what He wills and chooses" (28:68), and Esther's appointment reflects this divine selection.

## Theological Significance

- **Qadr**: God's decree governs the placement of individuals
- **Hidden purpose**: God's plan unfolds through ordinary events
- **Divine favor**: God grants favor to whom He wills
- **Preparation**: God positions His servants before the crisis

## References

- Quran 28:68 on divine choice.
