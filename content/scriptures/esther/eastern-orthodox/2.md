## Esther's Hidden Holiness

Orthodox interpretation reads Esther's concealed identity as a model of hidden holiness. Many Orthodox saints lived in concealment — their true spiritual identity hidden from the world. Esther's inner life of prayer (revealed in the deuterocanonical additions) sustains her through the trial of living in a pagan court.

## Theological Significance

- **Hidden holiness**: Spiritual identity may be concealed from the world
- **Divine favor**: God's grace elevates the humble
- **Ascetical endurance**: Living faithfully in hostile environments
- **Providential preparation**: God positions His servants before the crisis
