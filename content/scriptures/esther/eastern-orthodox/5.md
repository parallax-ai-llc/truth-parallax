## Patience and Divine Timing

Orthodox interpretation reads Esther's delayed revelation as guided by the Holy Spirit's sense of *kairos* (right timing). The chapter illustrates the Orthodox virtue of patience — waiting for God's moment rather than forcing human timing.

## Theological Significance

- **Kairos**: The Spirit guides the right moment for action
- **Patience**: Waiting is itself a spiritual discipline
- **Pride's blindness**: Haman cannot see his own destruction approaching
- **Divine irony**: The proud prepare the instruments of their own judgment
