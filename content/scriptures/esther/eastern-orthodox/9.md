## Victory and Liturgical Memorial

Orthodox interpretation reads the institution of Purim as a model of liturgical memorial. The annual commemoration makes the saving event present to each generation, just as the Orthodox liturgical calendar makes Christ's saving acts present in worship.

## Theological Significance

- **Liturgical memorial**: Remembrance makes the saving event present
- **Reversal**: The central theological theme reaches its climax
- **Communal celebration**: Deliverance is celebrated together
- **Generosity**: Genuine celebration overflows into charity
