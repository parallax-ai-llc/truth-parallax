## Divine Justice

Orthodox interpretation reads Haman's downfall as the vindication of divine justice operating through human events. Esther's identification with her people models the Orthodox understanding of the communion of saints — solidarity with the suffering Body.

## Theological Significance

- **Divine justice**: God's judgment operates through human agents
- **Solidarity**: The intercessor identifies completely with her people
- **Measure for measure**: The wicked are caught in their own devices
- **Vindication**: God does not abandon His faithful
