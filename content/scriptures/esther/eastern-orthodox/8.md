## Deliverance and Paschal Joy

Orthodox interpretation reads the transformation from mourning to joy as a prefiguration of the Paschal mystery. The passage from death to life, from despair to celebration, anticipates the Church's movement from Great Friday to Pascha.

## Theological Significance

- **Paschal prefiguration**: From death sentence to joyful life
- **Transformation**: Mourning becomes celebration through divine action
- **Universal witness**: Deliverance attracts others to faith
- **Paschal joy**: The joy of salvation anticipates the Resurrection
