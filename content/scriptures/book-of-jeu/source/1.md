---
title: "Book of Jeu Section 1"
scripture: "book-of-jeu"
chapter: 1
---

1. This is the book of the gnoses of the Invisible God, by means of the hidden mysteries that show the way to the elect race, leading them in advancement and ascent to the silent order.

2. Jesus, the living one, spoke to his apostles: "Blessed is the one who has known these mysteries, for that one shall inherit the Kingdom of Light."

3. "I will tell you the mystery of the Invisible God, whose name no tongue can utter, whose form no eye has seen."

4. "The Invisible God brought forth from himself a great power, and that power is Jeu, the True God, the overseer of all emanations."

5. "Jeu is the first emanation, and from Jeu came forth all the treasuries of Light, all the orders of the invisible world."

6. "Jeu established sixty treasuries in the realm of Light, and in each treasury he placed a head, and each head rules over a multitude of receivers and servants."

7. "These sixty treasuries are the stations through which the ascending soul must pass on its journey to the Invisible God."

8. "At each treasury, the soul must speak the name of the head of that treasury, and show the seal, and give the cipher, and then the doors will open."

9. "Without the names, the seals, and the ciphers, the soul cannot pass. It will be turned back and cast into the outer darkness."

10. "This is why I give you these mysteries now, so that you may possess the keys to every door in the realm of Light."

11. "The first treasury is guarded by the head whose true name is Zarazaz, and his seal is this: a circle within a circle, with three lines descending."

12. "When you approach the first treasury, speak the name and trace the seal and say: 'Withdraw your gates, for I am a child of the Light, and I carry the seal of Jeu.'"

13. "The guards will test you, and if you speak truly, the gates will open, and the receivers of that treasury will clothe you in a white garment and lead you forward."

14. "And so it continues through each treasury, ascending higher, receiving greater garments of Light, until you reach the place of the Invisible God."

15. Jesus said: "Write these things down and guard them. Do not give them to the unworthy, for these are the mysteries of the Kingdom."

16. "Only those who have renounced the world, who have kept themselves pure, who have given up all falsehood—only these are worthy to receive the seals and the names."

17. "For the seals are not mere words. They are powers. When spoken by a pure mouth, they compel the Archons to open their gates. When spoken by an impure mouth, they have no effect."

18. "Therefore, purify yourselves first, and then receive the mysteries, and the path to the Invisible God shall be opened before you."
