---
title: "Book of Jeu Section 2"
scripture: "book-of-jeu"
chapter: 2
---

1. Jesus continued to instruct his disciples concerning the mysteries of the baptisms and the rituals of ascent.

2. "There are three baptisms that you must receive before you can enter the treasuries of Light: the baptism of water, the baptism of fire, and the baptism of the Holy Spirit."

3. "The baptism of water purifies the body and washes away the stain of matter. It is the first cleansing, the outer purification."

4. "For this baptism, take branches of the vine and the fig and other sacred plants, and prepare the water with frankincense and myrrh."

5. "Place the offering toward the four directions—east, west, north, and south—and pray: 'Hear me, Father of all Fatherhood, Infinite Light, make the water pure for this baptism.'"

6. "When the disciple enters the water, the receivers of Light descend and seal the disciple with the seal of the first mystery, and the stain of the Archons is washed away."

7. "The baptism of fire purifies the soul and burns away the residue of Darkness that the water could not reach."

8. "For this baptism, place vine branches upon the offering and set them alight, and the smoke shall carry the impurities upward."

9. "Pray: 'Zorokothora Melchizedek, bring the fire of purification upon this disciple, that the Darkness within may be consumed.'"

10. "When the fire of the mystery touches the soul, the bonds of the Archons are loosened, and the Light within the disciple grows brighter."

11. "The baptism of the Holy Spirit is the highest baptism, the inner transformation that prepares the soul for the ascent through the treasuries."

12. "For this baptism, there is no outward element, for the Spirit comes from above, from the First Mystery itself."

13. "Pray: 'Send your Holy Spirit upon this disciple, O Ineffable One, that they may receive the fullness of the mystery and be sealed for the treasuries of Light.'"

14. "When the Holy Spirit descends, the Light Mind is fully awakened within the disciple, and they see with the eyes of the spirit, and they know with the knowledge of the Light."

15. "After these three baptisms, the disciple receives the mystery of the Ineffable—the highest mystery, which cannot be spoken aloud but is communicated in silence."

16. "The one who receives the mystery of the Ineffable is higher than all the Archons, higher than all the powers, higher than all the invisible ones."

17. "Such a one passes through every treasury without hindrance, for the seal of the Ineffable opens every gate and commands every guardian."

18. "This is the completion of the mysteries. The one who possesses them has conquered death and the Archons and the chaos, and shall dwell in the Light forever."

19. Jesus said: "I have given you everything. I have held nothing back. Go forth and practice these mysteries, and teach them only to the worthy."

20. "For the time is short, and the gathering of Light hastens toward its completion. Blessed are those who receive these mysteries and keep them, for they shall inherit the Kingdom of Light."

21. The disciples prostrated themselves and said: "We give thanks to you, Lord, for you have revealed to us the hidden things. We will guard these mysteries and practice them faithfully."

22. And Jesus blessed them and said: "Peace be upon you. The Light of the Father shines upon you. Go forth as children of the Light."
