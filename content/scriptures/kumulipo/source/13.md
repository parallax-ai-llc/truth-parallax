---
title: "Thirteenth Era - The Sacred Knowledge"
scripture: "kumulipo"
chapter: 13
---

**1** The priests preserved the sacred knowledge,
The chants and the genealogies, the rituals and the prayers,
The knowledge of the stars and the tides,
The knowledge of healing and of prophecy.

**2** In the heiau, the temple on the mountain,
The priests performed the ceremonies,
They called upon Kane and Ku and Lono,
They offered sacrifice and prayer.

**3** The knowledge was passed from priest to priest,
From generation to generation, word for word,
Not a syllable changed, not a name omitted,
For the chant must be perfect or it has no power.

**4** This is why we chant the Kumulipo,
This is why we remember every name,
For in the names is the power of the ancestors,
And in the ancestors is the power of the gods.
