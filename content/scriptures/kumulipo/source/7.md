---
title: "Seventh Era - The Dog and the Pig"
scripture: "kumulipo"
chapter: 7
---

**1** Born was the dog, companion of man,
Born was the pig, the wild boar of the forest,
Born was the four-legged creature,
The creature that walks upon the ground.

**2** The dog that guards, the pig that roots,
The animals that would serve mankind,
Born before mankind, prepared for mankind,
Waiting in the darkness for mankind.

**3** Man for the dog, woman for the pig,
The male born first, the female following,
In proper order, in sacred sequence,
The creation unfolding as ordained.

**4** And now the seventh night was passed,
Seven eras of darkness, seven births of darkness,
The creatures of the night were all born,
And the world waited for the light.
