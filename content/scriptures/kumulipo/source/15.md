---
title: "Fifteenth Era - The Prophecies"
scripture: "kumulipo"
chapter: 15
---

**1** The priests foretold the coming of strangers,
Men with white skin who would come in great ships,
Bringing new gods and new ways,
Changing the world of the islands forever.

**2** They said: A time will come when the old ways are challenged,
When the kapu is broken and the temples fall,
When the old gods are forgotten and new gods are worshipped,
When the children no longer know the chants.

**3** But they also said: The knowledge will not die,
The chants will be preserved by the faithful,
The genealogies will be remembered by those who care,
And a day will come when the old wisdom is valued again.

**4** For the Kumulipo is more than a chant,
It is the identity of the people,
It is the record of creation from the very beginning,
It is the proof that we belong to this land and this land belongs to us.
