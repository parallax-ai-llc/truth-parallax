---
title: "Tenth Era - The Genealogy Begins"
scripture: "kumulipo"
chapter: 10
---

**1** From La'ila'i and Ki'i came the first generation,
And from them came the second, and the third,
Generation upon generation, name upon name,
The unbroken chain of ancestry stretching forward.

**2** Each generation remembered, each name preserved,
For the genealogy is the backbone of the people,
The record of who we are and where we came from,
The proof of our connection to the gods and to the land.

**3** In the tenth era, the human race spread across the islands,
They learned to fish and to farm, to build and to sail,
They learned the ways of the sea and the ways of the land,
They became the people of the Pacific.

**4** And the ancestors watched over them from the spirit world,
And the gods guided them in their voyages,
And the land provided for them as a mother provides,
And the people were grateful and gave thanks.
