---
title: "Sixth Era - The Lizards and Rodents"
scripture: "kumulipo"
chapter: 6
---

**1** Born was the lizard, the gecko on the wall,
Born was the rat, the mouse in the field,
Born was the bat that flies by night,
Born was the small creatures of the twilight.

**2** Born was the turtle that carries its house,
Born was the salamander, child of water and land,
The creatures that crawl between the worlds,
Not quite of the sea, not quite of the land.

**3** In the darkness they multiplied,
In the night they spread across the land,
From shore to mountain, from valley to ridge,
The small ones filled every hollow.

**4** The world was teeming with life,
The world was thick with creatures,
But still there was no light,
Still the darkness covered all.
