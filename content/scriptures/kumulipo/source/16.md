---
title: "Sixteenth Era - The Royal Lineage"
scripture: "kumulipo"
chapter: 16
---

**1** This is the genealogy of the great chiefs,
From the gods to the present day,
Name upon name, generation upon generation,
The unbroken chain of sacred descent.

**2** From Kane and Ki'i and La'ila'i,
Through the generations of the ancestors,
Through the voyagers and the settlers,
Through the warrior chiefs and the peacemakers.

**3** Down to Lonoikamakahiki, the great chief,
And to Keawe, and to Kalani'opu'u,
And to Kamehameha, who united all the islands,
The fulfillment of the ancient prophecy.

**4** The Kumulipo was chanted at the birth of chiefs,
For three nights the chanters recited without pause,
Every name, every era, every creature,
From the coral in the dark sea to the chief in his palace.

**5** This is the Kumulipo, the chant of creation,
The source of darkness, the beginning of all things,
The song that connects us to the beginning of time,
The song that shall endure as long as the islands stand.
