---
title: "Fourteenth Era - The Wars"
scripture: "kumulipo"
chapter: 14
---

**1** In this era the chiefs made war upon each other,
Island against island, district against district,
The strong conquered the weak, the cunning defeated the brave,
And the land was troubled with warfare.

**2** But even in war the sacred laws were observed,
The kapu was maintained, the temples were respected,
The priests continued their ceremonies,
And the genealogy continued unbroken.

**3** From the wars arose the great chiefs,
Those who unified the islands under one rule,
Those who brought peace after long conflict,
Those whose names live forever in the chant.

**4** And the wars were not without purpose,
For from them came the great kingdom,
The unified rule of the islands,
The fulfillment of the ancient prophecies.
