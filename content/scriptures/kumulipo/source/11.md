---
title: "Eleventh Era - The Voyagers"
scripture: "kumulipo"
chapter: 11
---

**1** Born was the voyager, the navigator,
The one who reads the stars and the currents,
Who follows the flight of birds across the ocean,
Who sails beyond the horizon to find new lands.

**2** The great canoes were built, double-hulled and strong,
Provisioned for the long journey across the open sea.
The navigators knew the star paths, the wind patterns,
The signs of land beyond the visible horizon.

**3** From Kahiki they came, the homeland far away,
Following the stars, following the currents,
Until they saw the clouds that gather over mountains,
Until they saw the birds that nest on islands.

**4** They found Hawaii, the great island,
They found Maui and Oahu and Kauai,
They settled the islands and made them their home,
And the genealogy continued, name upon name.
