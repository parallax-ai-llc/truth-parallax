---
title: "Twelfth Era - The Chiefs"
scripture: "kumulipo"
chapter: 12
---

**1** From the ancestors came the chiefs, the ali'i,
Those of sacred blood, those of divine descent,
The rulers of the people, the guardians of the land,
The living connection between gods and men.

**2** The chief is the father of the people,
His mana flows through the land,
His kapu protects the sacred,
His wisdom guides the nation.

**3** Born was the line of chiefs, generation upon generation,
Each chief more powerful than the last,
Each chief adding to the glory of the lineage,
Until the great chiefs of Hawaii arose.

**4** And the chant records them all,
Every name, every birth, every marriage,
For to forget a name is to break the chain,
And to break the chain is to lose the people.
