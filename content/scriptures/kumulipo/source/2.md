---
title: "Second Era - The Fish"
scripture: "kumulipo"
chapter: 2
---

**1** Born was the fish in the sea,
In the deep blue sea where they dwell.
Born was the eel, striped, long and slender,
Born was the shark, born was the ray,
Born was the cuttlefish, born was the octopus.

**2** The deep sea brought forth its children,
The broad sea gave birth in darkness.
Born was the porpoise, born was the whale,
Born was the flying fish that leaps above the wave.

**3** There was spawning in the depths,
There was spawning on the reef,
Spawning in the channels, spawning in the pools,
Fish of many kinds multiplying.

**4** The sea was black with fish,
The reef was covered with shellfish,
The tide pools teemed with life,
The ocean floor was alive with crawling things.

**5** Man for the narrow stream, woman for the broad sea,
Born was the fish, born was the creature of the water,
It was day in the world of the sea,
But still darkness on the land.
