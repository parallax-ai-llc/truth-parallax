---
title: "Fourth Era - The Insects"
scripture: "kumulipo"
chapter: 4
---

**1** Born was the crawling creature,
The caterpillar in the leaf, the beetle in the bark,
Born was the moth, born was the butterfly,
Born was the dragonfly that hovers over the stream.

**2** Born was the ant that builds its mound,
Born was the spider that weaves its web,
Born was the centipede, born was the scorpion,
Born was the cricket that sings in the night.

**3** The buzzing things filled the air,
The crawling things covered the ground,
The burrowing things dug beneath the earth,
The flying things circled among the trees.

**4** In the darkness they stirred,
In the night they sang,
In the blackness they moved,
For they were the children of the night.

**5** Each creature born in its proper order,
Each creature placed in its proper home,
The pattern unfolding, the design emerging,
As the chant of creation continued.
