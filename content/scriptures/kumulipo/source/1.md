---
title: "First Era - The Coral"
scripture: "kumulipo"
chapter: 1
---

**1** At the time when the earth became hot,
At the time when the heavens turned about,
At the time when the sun was darkened,
To cause the moon to shine,
The time of the rise of the Pleiades.

**2** The slime, this was the source of the earth,
The source of the darkness that made darkness,
The source of the night that made night,
The intense darkness, the deep darkness,
Darkness of the sun, darkness of the night.

**3** Nothing but night.
The night gave birth.
Born was Kumulipo in the night, a male.
Born was Po'ele in the night, a female.

**4** Born was the coral, born was the reef,
Born was the small starfish, born was the large starfish,
Born was the sea cucumber, born was the sea urchin,
Born was the barnacle, born was the mother-of-pearl,
Born was the shellfish, born was the conch.

**5** Born was the seaweed, born was the moss,
Born was the grub in the coral,
Born was the worm in the rock,
Born was the egg in the wave,
Born was the creature that clung to the reef.
