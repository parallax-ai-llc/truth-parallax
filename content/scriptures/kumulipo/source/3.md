---
title: "Third Era - The Plants"
scripture: "kumulipo"
chapter: 3
---

**1** Born was the plant on the land,
The fern that unfurls in the forest,
Born was the moss that clings to the stone,
Born was the grass that covers the plain.

**2** Born was the taro, food of the people,
Born was the breadfruit, born was the banana,
Born was the coconut palm, reaching to the sky,
Born was the sugarcane, sweet to the taste.

**3** The forest grew thick, the jungle spread wide,
The vines climbed the trees, the roots dug deep.
The earth was clothed in green,
The valleys were filled with growing things.

**4** In the darkness the plants grew,
Without sun they reached upward,
Without rain they found water,
For the earth was their mother and the night their nurse.

**5** The land that was bare was now covered,
The rock that was naked was now dressed,
The earth that was silent now whispered with leaves,
And still the world waited in darkness.
