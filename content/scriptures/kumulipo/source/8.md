---
title: "Eighth Era - The Dawn of Light"
scripture: "kumulipo"
chapter: 8
---

**1** Now the night has passed, now the dawn approaches,
The darkness lifts, the light comes creeping,
The stars fade, the sky brightens,
The long darkness is ending.

**2** Born was the light, born was the day,
The sun rose for the first time,
The moon took its place in the sky,
The stars arranged themselves in their courses.

**3** The world that had been in darkness now stood revealed,
The mountains and valleys, the forests and seas,
All that had been born in darkness now saw the light,
And the light was good, and the world was beautiful.

**4** With the light came a new kind of being,
A being that walked upright, that spoke, that thought,
The dawn of humankind approached,
The final creation was at hand.
