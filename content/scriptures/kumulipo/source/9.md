---
title: "Ninth Era - The First Humans"
scripture: "kumulipo"
chapter: 9
---

**1** Born was La'ila'i, a woman,
Born was Ki'i, a man,
Born was Kane, a god,
Born was Kanaloa, the companion of Kane.

**2** La'ila'i was the woman of the daylight world,
She was the mother of the race of humankind,
From her came the chiefs and the commoners,
From her came all the people of the islands.

**3** Ki'i was the first man, the ancestor,
He who stood upright in the new light,
He who looked upon the world with wonder,
He who named the creatures and the places.

**4** Kane was the creator, the giver of life,
He who breathed the breath of life into man,
He who set the sun and moon in the sky,
He who made the fresh water spring from the rock.

**5** Kanaloa was the companion, the second god,
He who ruled the deep ocean,
He who knew the currents and the tides,
The dark counterpart to Kane's light.
