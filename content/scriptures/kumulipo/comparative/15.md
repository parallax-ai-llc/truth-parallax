The density of genealogical information increases as the chant nears its culmination, with each connection carefully documented to establish the unbroken chain of descent from the cosmic origins through the divine ancestors to the present ruling line.

Comparative analysis of The Lineage of Chiefs (Part 5) reveals both parallels with and distinctive departures from similar themes in other world traditions.
