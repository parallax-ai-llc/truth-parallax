## Overview

Protestant engagement with the Rule of Blessings focuses on its illumination of the Jewish background of the New Testament and early Christianity. The text provides crucial context for understanding the world in which Jesus and the apostles lived and the theological categories they inherited from Second Temple Judaism.

## Interpretive Approach

Protestant scholars have been particularly attentive to themes of covenant, grace, law, and eschatology in the Dead Sea Scrolls, exploring how these concepts inform the New Testament's theological vocabulary and the early church's self-understanding.
