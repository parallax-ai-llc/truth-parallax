---
title: "Rule of Blessings Section 5"
scripture: "rule-of-blessings"
chapter: 5
---

**5:1** For the Instructor, concerning the blessings for the time of war. May God be gracious to you and may He illumine your understanding with the wisdom of life.

**5:3** May He open for you the door of eternal knowledge. May He lift up His gracious countenance to give you peace and everlasting love.

**5:5** And may He bless all the work of your hands in truth and establish your counsel among the Holy Ones forever. May He raise you to everlasting heights.

**5:7** May He open every door for you and may He shut every gate of violence before you. May the Lord bless you and keep you from all evil.

**5:10** May He illuminate your path and open before you the way of peace. May He set your foot upon the rock and your steps upon the path of righteousness.

**5:12** May He choose you with His righteous ones and number you among the Holy Ones who stand before Him forever.

**5:15** May He give you a renewed spirit and establish you in His holy presence. May He draw you near with His lovingkindness and enfold you in the arms of His mercy.

**5:17** May He sanctify you with His truth and clothe you with the garment of His righteousness, and may He place upon your head the crown of His glory, forever and ever.

**5:20** May you serve Him all the days of your life, and may He bless you, granting you peace among all the members of His Covenant.
