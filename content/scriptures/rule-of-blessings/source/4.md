---
title: "Rule of Blessings Section 4"
scripture: "rule-of-blessings"
chapter: 4
---

**4:1** For the Instructor, to bless the men of the Community who walk blamelessly in all the ways prescribed by God, to uphold the Covenant of His faithfulness and to be united in the assembly of God.

**4:3** May He bless all the righteous who come to sanctify His Name, confessing all their former sins and iniquities.

**4:5** May the Lord bless you and keep you. May He illuminate your heart with the discernment of life, and favor you with eternal knowledge.

**4:7** May He lift His merciful countenance towards you for everlasting peace. May He make you a holy instrument in His treasury.

**4:9** May He bless all your ways, and may all your actions be righteous before Him. May He choose you with His favored ones and establish your lot among the Holy Ones.

**4:11** And may He keep you from all the snares of the pit and from the traps of the men of corruption. And may He keep far from you the devisers of evil.

**4:14** May He open for you the eternal spring, and may He not withhold the living waters from those who thirst. May He make you drink from the well of holiness and from the fountain of life.
