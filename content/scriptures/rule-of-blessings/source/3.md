---
title: "Rule of Blessings Section 3"
scripture: "rule-of-blessings"
chapter: 3
---

**3:1** For the Instructor, to bless the Prince of the Congregation, whom God has chosen to stand at the head of the Congregation.

**3:3** He shall renew for him the Covenant of the Community that he may establish the kingdom of His people forever, that he may judge the poor with righteousness and dispense justice with equity to the oppressed of the land.

**3:5** And that he may walk blamelessly in all the ways of God, and that he may establish His holy Covenant when distress comes upon those who seek God.

**3:7** May the Lord raise you up to everlasting heights, and as a fortified tower upon a high wall. May you smite the peoples with the might of your hand and ravage the earth with your scepter.

**3:9** May you bring death to the ungodly with the breath of your lips. May He shed upon you the spirit of counsel and eternal might, the spirit of knowledge and of the fear of God.

**3:11** May righteousness be the girdle of your loins and may your reins be girded with faithfulness.

**3:14** May He make your horns of iron and your hooves of bronze; may you toss like a young bull and trample the peoples like the mire of the streets.

**3:16** For God has established you as the scepter. The rulers shall come before you and all the nations shall serve you. He shall strengthen you with His holy Name and you shall be as a lion.
