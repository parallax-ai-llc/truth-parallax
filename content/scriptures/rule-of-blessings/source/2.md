---
title: "Rule of Blessings Section 2"
scripture: "rule-of-blessings"
chapter: 2
---

**2:1** For the Instructor, to bless the sons of Zadok, the Priests, whom God has chosen to confirm His Covenant forever, to inquire into all His precepts in the midst of His people, and to instruct them as He commanded.

**2:3** They who have established the Covenant of truth, and in righteousness have overseen all His precepts, and have walked according to the way that He has chosen.

**2:5** May the Lord bless you from His holy abode. May He set you as a splendid jewel in the midst of the congregation of the saints.

**2:7** May He renew for you the Covenant of the everlasting priesthood. May He grant you a place in the holy abode.

**2:9** May He judge all the nobles by your works, and all the princes of the peoples by the words of your mouth. May He grant you as your inheritance the firstfruits of all delights.

**2:11** May He bless every mortal counsel by your hand. May you walk before Him in perfection in all the ways of truth, and may He choose you for all that pleases Him.

**2:14** May He make you holy among His people, and an eternal light to illumine the world with knowledge, and to enlighten the face of the Congregation with wisdom.

**2:16** May He consecrate you to the Holy of Holies, for you are made holy for Him and you shall glorify His name and His Holy Ones.
