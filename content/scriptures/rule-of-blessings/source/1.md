---
title: "Rule of Blessings Section 1"
scripture: "rule-of-blessings"
chapter: 1
---

**1:1** Words of blessing. For the Instructor, to bless those who fear God, who do His will, who keep His commandments, and hold fast to His holy Covenant, and walk perfectly in all the ways of His truth; whom He has chosen for an eternal Covenant which shall stand forever.

**1:3** May the Lord bless you from His holy abode; may He open for you the eternal spring that shall not fail. May He favor you with every heavenly blessing.

**1:5** May He teach you with the knowledge of the Holy Ones. May He open for you an eternal spring that shall not run dry.

**1:7** May He favor you with every blessing of heaven. May He bless you with the knowledge of understanding and with the wisdom of the sons of heaven.

**1:9** May He grant you an eternal name among those who bless, and may you be accounted righteous when He judges the world, dealing out chastisement to all the wicked.

**1:12** May He lift His gracious countenance towards you in eternal lovingkindness. May He inscribe you in the Book of Life together with the Holy Ones.

**1:15** May He grant you the heritage of all earthly pleasures and bless all the works of your hands, and favor you with the knowledge of eternal things.
