## Islamic Reading

Book 8's preservation of lost Avestan knowledge resonates with the Islamic concept of the 'People of the Book' (*Ahl al-Kitab*) and the recognition that communities with revealed scriptures occupy a special status. The Islamic reader finds in the Denkard's monumental effort to preserve Zoroastrian scripture a parallel to the Islamic tradition's own deep reverence for scriptural preservation — the collection and standardization of the Qur'an under the early caliphs.
