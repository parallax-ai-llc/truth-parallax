## Islamic Reading

Book 4's apologetic defense against Islam provides the Islamic reader with a unique perspective on the Zoroastrian-Islamic encounter — the view from the other side. The Denkard's arguments against Islamic claims challenge the Muslim reader to engage seriously with a tradition that existed in close proximity to early Islam and that contributed significantly to Islamic civilization. The Islamic reader may find in these arguments both unfair characterizations and genuine intellectual challenges that merit thoughtful response.
