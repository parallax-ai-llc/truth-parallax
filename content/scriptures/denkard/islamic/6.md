## Islamic Reading

The Islamic reader of Book 6's wisdom sayings discovers ethical teachings that resonate with the Islamic emphasis on practical morality, moderation, and the integration of faith and daily life. The Zoroastrian ethic of *humata, hukhta, hvarshta* (good thoughts, good words, good deeds) parallels the Islamic emphasis on *niyyah* (intention), truthful speech, and righteous action (*'amal salih*). These convergences reflect both shared Near Eastern moral heritage and possible direct influence.
