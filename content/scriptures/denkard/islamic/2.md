## Islamic Reading

Book 2's cosmological fragments are of interest to the Islamic reader as evidence of the pre-Islamic Persian cosmological tradition that influenced Islamic descriptions of the heavens, the earth, and the cosmic order. The seven-layered heaven, the cosmic mountain, and the bridge of judgment are concepts shared between Zoroastrian and Islamic cosmography, with the direction of influence generally understood to run from the Zoroastrian to the Islamic tradition.
