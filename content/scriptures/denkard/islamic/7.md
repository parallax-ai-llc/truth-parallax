## Islamic Reading

Book 7's biography of Zoroaster is read by the Islamic tradition through the lens of the Qur'anic teaching that God sent prophets to every nation. Some Islamic scholars have identified Zoroaster as one of these prophets, while others have been more cautious. The Islamic reader finds in the Denkard's account of Zoroaster's prophetic mission — his divine call, his period of testing, his revelation of God's will — patterns consistent with the Qur'anic description of prophethood.
