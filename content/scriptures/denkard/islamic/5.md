## Islamic Reading

Book 5's eschatological teachings are of particular interest to the Islamic reader, as numerous scholars have identified Zoroastrian influence on Islamic eschatology. The concepts of the *Sirat* (bridge of judgment, from the Zoroastrian *Chinvat*), the *Mizan* (scales of judgment), the physical descriptions of paradise and hell, and the concept of a savior figure (the Mahdi, perhaps influenced by the *Saoshyant*) all bear the marks of Zoroastrian influence. The Islamic reader encounters in the Denkard the pre-Islamic roots of concepts that became central to Islamic faith.
