## Islamic Reading

Book 9's interpretive commentary on Avestan texts invites Islamic comparison with the tradition of Qur'anic *tafsir* (commentary). Both traditions developed sophisticated hermeneutical methods for interpreting sacred scripture — methods that include linguistic analysis, citation of authoritative interpretive traditions, and the derivation of legal and ethical guidance from prophetic texts. The Islamic reader recognizes in the Zoroastrian commentarial tradition a kindred hermeneutical enterprise.
