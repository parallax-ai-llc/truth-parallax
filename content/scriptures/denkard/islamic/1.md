## Islamic Reading

The Islamic reader of Book 1's creation fragments encounters a pre-Islamic creation theology that influenced Islamic cosmological thinking. The Zoroastrian concept of creation as a deliberate act of a wise and good deity resonates with the Qur'anic teaching that God created the heavens and the earth with purpose and truth (*bi'l-haqq*). Scholars have traced Zoroastrian influence on Islamic creation narratives, particularly in the hadith literature and in Persian Islamic cosmological texts.
