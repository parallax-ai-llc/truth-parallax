## Academic Reading

Scholars study Book 6's wisdom literature in relation to the broader Near Eastern tradition of wisdom collections, from the Babylonian *Counsels of Wisdom* to the biblical Proverbs and the Islamic *hadith* collections. The text's ethical maxims are analyzed for their philosophical content, their relationship to Avestan ethical teachings, and their reflection of social conditions in early Islamic Iran.
