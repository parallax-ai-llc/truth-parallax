## Academic Reading

Scholars analyze Book 9's Avestan commentaries as evidence of Sasanian hermeneutical methods and theological interpretation. The relationship between the Avestan source texts and their Pahlavi interpretations reveals the interpretive strategies of the Zoroastrian priestly tradition — strategies that are compared with hermeneutical methods in other scriptural traditions (Jewish midrash, Islamic tafsir, Christian patristic exegesis).
