## Academic Reading

Academic analysis of Book 5's eschatology examines its relationship to the development of eschatological concepts across Near Eastern religions. Scholars trace the influence of Zoroastrian eschatological ideas — the *Saoshyant*, bodily resurrection, final judgment, the renovation of the world — on Jewish, Christian, and Islamic eschatology. The text is also analyzed for evidence of reverse influence — the possible impact of Islamic eschatological concepts on the Denkard's formulations.
