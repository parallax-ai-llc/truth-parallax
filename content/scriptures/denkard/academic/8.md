## Academic Reading

Book 8's *nask* summaries are studied by scholars as the primary source for reconstructing the contents of the lost Sasanian Avesta. Each summary is analyzed for its information about lost Avestan texts — their titles, subjects, and probable contents — and compared with surviving Avestan fragments to assess the accuracy of the Pahlavi compiler's representations. This analysis is fundamental to the academic reconstruction of Zoroastrian intellectual history.
