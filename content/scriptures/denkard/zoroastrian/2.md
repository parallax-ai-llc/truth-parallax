## Zoroastrian Reading

The surviving fragments of Book 2, dealing with cosmology, preserve Zoroastrian teachings about the structure of the universe and the nature of the cosmic struggle between *Asha* (Truth/Righteousness) and *Druj* (Falsehood/Deception). These cosmological teachings provide the framework within which all Zoroastrian ritual, ethics, and eschatology operate. The Zoroastrian reader finds in these fragments confirmation that the Good Religion possesses a comprehensive account of the cosmos and humanity's place within it.
