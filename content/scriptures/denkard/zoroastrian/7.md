## Zoroastrian Reading

Book 7's biography of Zoroaster is treasured by the Zoroastrian community as the most comprehensive account of their prophet's life, from his miraculous birth through his divine revelation to his establishment of the Good Religion. The Zoroastrian reader approaches this narrative not merely as history but as sacred biography — the story of the supreme prophet through whom Ahura Mazda revealed the truth to humanity. Zoroaster's trials and ultimate triumph serve as a model for the community's own struggle to maintain the faith.
