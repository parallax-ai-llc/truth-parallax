## Zoroastrian Reading

The fragments surviving from Book 1 are treasured by the Zoroastrian community as precious remnants of a lost section that addressed the foundational themes of creation and divine revelation. These fragments describe Ahura Mazda's creation of the spiritual (*menog*) and material (*getig*) worlds and the revelation of the Good Religion to Zoroaster. The Zoroastrian reader approaches these fragments with awareness of how much has been lost — and with gratitude for what the Denkard's compilers managed to preserve from the destruction of the Arab conquest.
