## Zoroastrian Reading

Book 6's wisdom sayings and ethical counsels provide the Zoroastrian reader with practical guidance for living according to the threefold path of *humata* (good thoughts), *hukhta* (good words), and *hvarshta* (good deeds). These maxims cover every aspect of daily life — from business dealings to family relationships to personal hygiene — reflecting the Zoroastrian conviction that religion is not separate from daily life but infuses every action with spiritual significance.
