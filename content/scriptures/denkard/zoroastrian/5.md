## Zoroastrian Reading

Book 5's eschatological teachings provide the Zoroastrian community with hope in the face of present suffering. The teaching of the *Saoshyant* (Savior) who will come at the end of time to lead the final battle against evil and inaugurate the *frashokereti* (renovation) assures the faithful that the present triumph of evil is temporary. The Zoroastrian reader finds in Book 5's eschatology a cosmic narrative that makes sense of the community's suffering and promises ultimate vindication.
