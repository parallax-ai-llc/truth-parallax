## Zoroastrian Reading

Book 8's summaries of the 21 *nasks* (volumes) of the Sasanian Avesta are of incalculable value to the Zoroastrian community, as they preserve the only detailed account of scriptures that are now almost entirely lost. Of the original Avesta — said to have been inscribed on 12,000 cowhides — only a fraction survives. The Zoroastrian reader approaches Book 8 with a mixture of gratitude for what was preserved and grief for what was lost — a grief that intensifies the community's commitment to protecting the scriptures that remain.
