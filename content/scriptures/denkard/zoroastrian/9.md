## Zoroastrian Reading

Book 9's interpretive commentary on surviving Avestan texts provides the Zoroastrian reader with authorized Pahlavi interpretations of often difficult and archaic Avestan passages. These interpretations reflect centuries of priestly learning and theological reflection, providing a window into how the Sasanian clergy understood and taught the sacred texts. The Zoroastrian reader values Book 9 as a guide to the deeper meanings of the Avesta — meanings that require the interpretive tradition to unlock.
