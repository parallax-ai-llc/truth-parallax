## Jewish Reading

Book 8's effort to preserve the contents of the lost Avesta resonates deeply with the Jewish experience of textual preservation and transmission. The Jewish community's determination to preserve the Torah — from the scribes of Ezra through the Masoretes — parallels the Zoroastrian effort to save their scriptures from the destruction of the Arab conquest. Both communities understood that the survival of their faith depended on the survival of their texts.
