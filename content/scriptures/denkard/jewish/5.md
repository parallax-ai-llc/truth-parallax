## Jewish Reading

Book 5's eschatological teachings are of profound interest to the Jewish reader, as the Zoroastrian concepts of a coming savior (*Saoshyant*), bodily resurrection, and cosmic renovation (*frashokereti*) bear striking resemblance to Jewish messianic and eschatological expectations that developed during the Persian period. The question of whether the Jewish concept of the Messiah, the resurrection of the dead, and *olam ha-ba* (the world to come) were influenced by Zoroastrian eschatology is one of the most debated issues in the comparative study of Judaism and Zoroastrianism.
