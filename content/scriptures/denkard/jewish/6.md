## Jewish Reading

The Jewish reader of Book 6's wisdom sayings discovers ethical teachings that resonate with the biblical wisdom tradition — Proverbs, Ecclesiastes, and Ben Sira. The emphasis on truth-telling, justice, moderation, and productive labor reflects a shared moral vocabulary between the Zoroastrian and Jewish traditions. The convergence of ethical teaching across these traditions is all the more striking given the historical proximity of Jews and Zoroastrians during the Persian and Parthian periods.
