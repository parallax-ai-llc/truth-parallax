## Jewish Reading

Book 2's cosmological fragments raise for the Jewish reader questions about Persian influences on Jewish cosmological ideas. The concept of multiple heavens, angelic guardians, and a structured cosmos that reflects divine order finds parallels in Jewish apocalyptic literature (1 Enoch, 2 Enoch) that emerged during and after the Persian period. The Jewish reader considers the extent to which these cosmological developments reflect Persian influence.
