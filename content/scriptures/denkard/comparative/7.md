## Comparative Reading

Comparative study of Book 7's Zoroaster biography places it alongside other prophetic biographies: the Gospel accounts of Jesus, the Islamic *sira* of Muhammad, the Buddhist accounts of Siddhartha Gautama, and the Jain life of Mahavira. Common motifs — miraculous birth, period of testing, moment of revelation, mission and opposition — suggest shared patterns in how religious communities construct the narratives of their founders.
