## Comparative Reading

Book 8's preservation of lost Avestan knowledge invites comparison with other traditions' efforts to preserve endangered scriptures: the compilation of the Hebrew Bible after the Babylonian exile, the Buddhist councils that preserved the Pali canon, and the Islamic collection of the Qur'an under Uthman. Each tradition faced a crisis of textual preservation and responded with systematic efforts to collect, organize, and authorize a scriptural canon.
