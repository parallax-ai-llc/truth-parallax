## Comparative Reading

Comparative study of Book 1's creation theology places Zoroastrian cosmogony alongside other ancient Near Eastern creation accounts: the Babylonian Enuma Elish, the Genesis creation narratives, the Egyptian Memphite theology, and the Hindu Nasadiya Sukta. The Zoroastrian distinctive — creation as an act of divine will in the context of a cosmic struggle between good and evil — provides a unique perspective in this comparative conversation.
