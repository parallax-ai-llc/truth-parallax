## Comparative Reading

Book 6's wisdom sayings invite comparison with wisdom traditions across cultures: the biblical Proverbs, the Confucian Analects, the Egyptian Instruction of Ptahhotep, and the Greek gnomic tradition. Comparative analysis reveals both universal themes (honesty, moderation, care for others) and culturally specific emphases (the Zoroastrian stress on truth-telling, the rejection of asceticism, and the positive valuation of agriculture and productive labor).
