## Comparative Reading

Comparative study of Book 3's philosophical theology reveals the Denkard's engagement with fundamental questions shared across philosophical and religious traditions: the nature of ultimate reality, the origin of evil, the relationship between faith and reason, and the basis of moral obligation. The Denkard's arguments are compared with Platonic, Aristotelian, Islamic, Jewish, and Christian approaches to these perennial questions.
