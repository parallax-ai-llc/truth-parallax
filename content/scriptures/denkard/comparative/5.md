## Comparative Reading

Comparative study of Book 5's eschatology is among the most productive areas of cross-traditional analysis, as Zoroastrian eschatological concepts have demonstrably influenced Jewish, Christian, and Islamic end-time expectations. The *Saoshyant* is compared with the Jewish Messiah and the Islamic Mahdi, the *frashokereti* with the Christian *apokatastasis* and the Jewish *tikkun olam*, and the Chinvat Bridge with the Islamic *Sirat*.
