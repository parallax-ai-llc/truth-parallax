## Protestant Reading

Book 2's cosmological fragments raise for the Protestant reader questions about the relationship between biblical and Zoroastrian worldviews. The concept of a multi-layered cosmos with heavens above and underworld below is shared by both traditions, and the Protestant reader considers whether these parallels reflect common ancient Near Eastern cosmological assumptions, direct influence, or independent apprehensions of cosmic truth.
