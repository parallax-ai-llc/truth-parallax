## Protestant Reading

Book 5's eschatological teachings are of particular interest to the Protestant reader, as the concepts of a coming savior, bodily resurrection, final judgment, and cosmic renovation are central to Christian eschatology and may have historical roots in Zoroastrian theology. The *Saoshyant*'s role in the final triumph of good over evil bears striking resemblance to Christ's eschatological role, raising questions about the relationship between these two eschatological visions.
