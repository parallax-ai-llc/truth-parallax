## Protestant Reading

Book 9's interpretive commentary on Avestan texts invites Protestant reflection on the hermeneutical tradition — the ways in which communities of faith interpret their scriptures across changing historical circumstances. The Zoroastrian commentarial tradition's combination of linguistic analysis, theological interpretation, and practical application parallels the Protestant tradition of biblical commentary from Luther and Calvin to the present.
