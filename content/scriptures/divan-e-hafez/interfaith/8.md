## Interfaith Reading

Hafez's garden poetry creates a shared aesthetic space for interfaith encounter. The beauty of gardens is universally accessible across religious boundaries, and the symbolic identification of the garden with paradise is shared by the Abrahamic traditions. Interfaith gatherings in gardens — and interfaith readings of garden poetry — provide occasions for the kind of relaxed, beauty-centered encounter that can build trust across religious lines.
