## Interfaith Reading

Hafez's critique of religious hypocrisy offers perhaps the most fertile ground for interfaith encounter, as every tradition recognizes the danger of form without substance, ritual without intention, and religion without genuine spirituality. Interfaith dialogue around these poems allows participants to acknowledge the failures of their own traditions while affirming the shared aspiration for authenticity in spiritual life.
