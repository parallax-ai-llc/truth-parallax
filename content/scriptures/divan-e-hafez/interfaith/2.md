## Interfaith Reading

Hafez's wine imagery opens interfaith conversation about the role of ecstatic experience in the spiritual life. Traditions that use wine sacramentally (Christianity, Judaism) and traditions that prohibit it (Islam) both recognize states of spiritual intoxication that transcend ordinary consciousness. Hafez's poetic treatment provides common ground for discussing how different traditions understand and cultivate encounters with the numinous.
