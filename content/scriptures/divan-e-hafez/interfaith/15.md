## Interfaith Reading

Hafez's love for Shiraz opens interfaith conversation about the role of place, homeland, and particular belonging in the spiritual life. The universal tension between the particular and the universal — loving one's own tradition deeply while remaining open to others — is one of the central challenges of interfaith dialogue, and Hafez's example of passionate local attachment combined with universal spiritual vision provides a model for navigating this tension.
