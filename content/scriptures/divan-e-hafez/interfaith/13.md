## Interfaith Reading

Hafez's symbolic imagery of the beloved's features opens interfaith conversation about the relationship between the material and the spiritual — how physical forms can serve as windows onto transcendent realities. This conversation connects with broader interfaith discussions about iconography, sacred art, the incarnation, and the sacramental potential of the created world.
