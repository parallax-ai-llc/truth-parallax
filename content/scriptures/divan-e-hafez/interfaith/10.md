## Interfaith Reading

Hafez's nocturnal ghazals open interfaith conversation about the role of silence, solitude, and darkness in the spiritual life. Contemplative practices across traditions — from Christian centering prayer to Buddhist sitting meditation to Jewish *hitbodedut* — share the recognition that silence and withdrawal from the world's noise are conditions for the deepest encounter with the sacred.
