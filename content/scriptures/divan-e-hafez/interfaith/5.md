## Interfaith Reading

Interfaith readers find in Hafez's reflections on fate a shared human grappling with the mystery of providence, suffering, and the meaning of events beyond our control. Every tradition offers resources for navigating uncertainty — trust in God, acceptance of karma, submission to divine will, finding meaning in suffering — and Hafez's poetic wisdom provides a meeting point for these diverse yet resonant responses.
