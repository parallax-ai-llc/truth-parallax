## Interfaith Reading

Hafez's love ghazals provide a powerful starting point for interfaith dialogue about the role of love in the spiritual life. The universal experience of longing — for beauty, for connection, for meaning, for the transcendent — cuts across religious boundaries. Interfaith readers discover in Hafez a voice that speaks from deep within the Islamic mystical tradition yet addresses the common human experience of desire and its potential for spiritual transformation.
