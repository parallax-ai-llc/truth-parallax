## Interfaith Reading

Interfaith readers find in Hafez's celebration of beauty a shared recognition that the experience of beauty can serve as a pathway to the sacred. Whether understood as divine self-disclosure (*tajalli*), as natural theology, as *sacramental* encounter, or as *darshan*, the contemplation of beauty is a spiritual practice recognized across traditions. Hafez's poetry creates a space for interfaith appreciation of beauty as a universal language of the sacred.
