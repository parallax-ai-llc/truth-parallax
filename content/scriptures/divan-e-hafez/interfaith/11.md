## Interfaith Reading

Hafez's celebration of spiritual poverty provides common ground for interfaith reflection on the relationship between material simplicity and spiritual depth. In an age of ecological crisis driven by overconsumption, the shared wisdom of renunciation traditions across religions offers a powerful interfaith resource for addressing the moral and spiritual dimensions of sustainability.
