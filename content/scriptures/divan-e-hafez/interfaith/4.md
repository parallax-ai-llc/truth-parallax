## Interfaith Reading

Hafez's ghazals on separation resonate with the universal human experience of loss and longing, creating a space for interfaith dialogue about suffering, absence, and hope. Every tradition has its own vocabulary for the anguish of distance from the sacred — the 'dark night of the soul,' the 'hiding of God's face,' the 'winter of the heart' — and Hafez's poetry provides a common text for exploring these shared experiences.
