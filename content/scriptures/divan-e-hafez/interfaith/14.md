## Interfaith Reading

Hafez's meditations on death and remembrance provide a starting point for interfaith dialogue about mortality, legacy, and the hope for continuity beyond death. Every tradition offers its own response to the challenge of mortality — resurrection, reincarnation, nirvana, the endurance of good works — and Hafez's poetic confidence in love's triumph over death provides a common text for exploring these diverse yet resonant responses.
