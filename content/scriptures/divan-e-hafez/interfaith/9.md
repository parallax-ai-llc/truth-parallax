## Interfaith Reading

Hafez's teaching that love is both the path and the goal resonates with mystical voices across traditions who affirm that the spiritual journey is not primarily about correct doctrine but about the transformation of the heart. Interfaith dialogue around these poems allows participants to explore the relationship between love and knowledge, practice and belief, in their own traditions.
