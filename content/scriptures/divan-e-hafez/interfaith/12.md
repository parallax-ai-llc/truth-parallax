## Interfaith Reading

The *saqi* figure in Hafez's poetry opens interfaith dialogue about the role of mediation and grace in the spiritual life. Every tradition has its own understanding of how divine gifts are transmitted — through sacraments, through the teacher, through scripture, through direct experience — and Hafez's poetic treatment of the cupbearer provides a common image for exploring these different models.
