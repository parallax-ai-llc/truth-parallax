## Interfaith Reading

Hafez's poems of mystical union open interfaith dialogue about peak spiritual experiences and their role in the life of faith. Contemplatives across traditions report experiences that share remarkable phenomenological features — the dissolution of boundaries, the overwhelming sense of presence, the subsequent transformation of perspective — and Hafez's poetry provides a common text for discussing these experiences without requiring theological agreement about their interpretation.
