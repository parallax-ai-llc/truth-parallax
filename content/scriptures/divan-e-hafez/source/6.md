## Ghazals on the Garden of Mysticism

**1.** In the garden of the heart, a rose has bloomed that no autumn can wither and no frost can kill.

**2.** This rose was planted by the hand of the Beloved, and it is watered by the tears of the lover.

**3.** The nightingale that sings in this garden sings not for the world—it sings for the One who planted the garden.

**4.** O heart, abandon the garden of the world, for its roses are painted and its fragrance is borrowed. Seek the garden that has no winter.

**5.** I saw a gardener who watered his garden with his own blood. They said: "This is madness." He replied: "Only blood can bring forth true roses."

**6.** The world-worshipper and the mystic cannot travel together, for the one sees the garden and the other sees the Gardener.

**7.** Break the idol of self and enter the temple of the heart, for therein dwells a beauty before which all idols are as dust.

**8.** The Magian elder said to me at dawn: "O Hafez, do you know why the mirror of the heart shows nothing? It is covered with the dust of vanity. Polish it with love."

**9.** When I polished the mirror, I saw neither myself nor the world—I saw only the face of the Beloved, and that face was everywhere.

**10.** The ascetic locks himself in his cell and thinks he has found God. The mystic walks in the marketplace and finds God in every face.

**11.** Do not seek Him in the mosque alone, for He is in the tavern too. Do not seek Him in the prayer-rug alone, for He is in the wine-cup too.

**12.** He is in the laugh of the child and the weeping of the old man. He is in the bud and in the thorn. He is in the light and in the shadow.

**13.** The sufi who claims to have found Him has not found Him. The drunkard who claims to have lost Him has not lost Him. He is beyond all finding and losing.

**14.** I knocked at every door and asked: "Where is the Beloved?" At last a voice replied: "Stop knocking. You are the door."

**15.** Hafez, the garden of the mystics is not on any map. You can find it only by losing your way.
