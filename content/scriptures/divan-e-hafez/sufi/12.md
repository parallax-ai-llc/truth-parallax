## Sufi Reading

The *saqi* (cupbearer) in Hafez's poetry is the spiritual master or divine grace itself — the agency through which the wine of mystical knowledge is poured into the prepared heart. The Sufi reads these ghazals as celebrations of the relationship between *murshid* and *murid* (master and disciple), in which the master serves as the channel of divine intoxication. The cup offered by the saqi represents the *bai'a* (oath of allegiance) through which the seeker receives the transmission of spiritual knowledge.
