## Sufi Reading

Hafez's praise of renunciation and poverty reflects the Sufi station of *faqr* (spiritual poverty) — the recognition that the soul possesses nothing of its own and that true wealth lies in absolute dependence on God. The *rend* who has abandoned respectability, reputation, and worldly security is the authentic Sufi who has passed through *fana* and discovered that the poverty of the self is the doorway to the infinite riches of the Divine. Hafez's celebration of the tavern over the mosque is the Sufi's preference for lived experience over mere doctrine.
