## Sufi Reading

Hafez's ghazals on union and ecstasy describe the supreme goal of the Sufi path: *wisal* (union) with the Beloved and the experience of *fana* (annihilation of the self in God). These moments of mystical communion are by nature fleeting in this world — 'flashes' (*lamahat*) of the eternal that break through the veil of ordinary consciousness — and Hafez's poetry captures their luminous intensity with unparalleled artistry. The Sufi reads these poems as testimonies of authentic mystical experience, the poet serving as a witness to realities that transcend ordinary language.
