## Sufi Reading

The path of love (*tariq-e ishq*) in Hafez's poetry is the Sufi path itself — the way of passionate devotion that leads through the stages of longing, purification, and surrender to the ultimate goal of union with the Divine. Hafez insists that this path cannot be walked through intellectual effort alone but requires the total engagement of the heart. The Sufi reads these ghazals as descriptions of the *suluk* (spiritual journey) that every genuine seeker must undertake, with all its dangers, ecstasies, and transformations.
