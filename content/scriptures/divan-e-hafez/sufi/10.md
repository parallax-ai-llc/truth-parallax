## Sufi Reading

Night in Hafez's poetry is the privileged time of mystical encounter — the darkness in which the soul's inner light shines brightest. The Sufi reads these nocturnal ghazals through the lens of *khalwa* (spiritual retreat) and *tahajjud* (night prayer), practices in which the seeker withdraws from the world's distractions to meet the Beloved in solitude. The darkness that terrifies the worldly person is, for the lover, the intimate chamber where the most secret communion takes place.
