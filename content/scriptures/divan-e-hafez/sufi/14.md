## Sufi Reading

Hafez's meditations on death and eternity are read by the Sufi as invitations to practice *fana* — the death of the ego — before the death of the body. The Sufi who has died to self while still alive has nothing to fear from physical death, which becomes merely the final veil between the lover and the Beloved. Hafez's confidence that his poetry will endure after his death reflects the Sufi teaching that works created from a place of genuine spiritual realization participate in the eternity of the divine source from which they spring.
