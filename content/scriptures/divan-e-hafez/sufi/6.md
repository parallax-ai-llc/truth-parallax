## Sufi Reading

Hafez's searing critique of religious hypocrisy is central to the Sufi reading, which sees in the *zahid* (outward ascetic) and the *muhtasib* (morality enforcer) the perennial enemies of authentic spirituality. The Sufi tradition distinguishes between *zahir* (outward form) and *batin* (inner reality), and Hafez's poetry exposes those who possess the form of religion without its spirit. The true worshipper, in Hafez's vision, is the *rend* — the spiritual rogue who scorns conventional piety in favor of direct encounter with the Beloved in the tavern of love.
