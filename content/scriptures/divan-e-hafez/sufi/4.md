## Sufi Reading

Separation (*firaq*) from the Beloved is the fundamental condition of the Sufi path — the soul's exile in the material world that gives rise to the longing that drives the spiritual journey. Hafez's ghazals on sorrow and separation are read as authentic expressions of this existential condition, in which the pain itself is treasured because it testifies to the reality of the love that causes it. The Sufi tradition teaches that the fire of separation purifies the lover, burning away everything that is not love until only the essential relationship with the Beloved remains.
