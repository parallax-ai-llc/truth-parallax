## Shia Reading

The Shia reading of Hafez's fate ghazals is colored by the distinctive Shia theology of divine justice (*'adl*), which insists that God does not decree injustice. The reversals of fortune Hafez describes are understood as tests that refine the faithful, echoing the Ahl al-Bayt's experience of worldly suffering combined with spiritual triumph. Hafez's equanimity in the face of fate's cruelty models the *rida* (acceptance) of the Imams.
