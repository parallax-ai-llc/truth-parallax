## Shia Reading

Night in the Shia reading is the time of *munajat* — intimate supplicatory prayer in the tradition of Imam Zayn al-Abidin's Sahifa Sajjadiyya. The darkness and solitude of Hafez's nocturnal poems create the conditions for the deepest encounter with the Divine, recalling the nights of prayer that the Imams spent in weeping and supplication. The Shia devotional tradition's emphasis on night prayer gives these poems a liturgical resonance.
