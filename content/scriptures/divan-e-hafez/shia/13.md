## Shia Reading

The Shia *irfan* tradition has developed elaborate symbolic interpretations of Hafez's imagery of hair, mole, and beauty. The beloved's hair represents the veils of divine mystery, the mole is the point of divine unity, and the beauty of the face is the *tajalli* (self-disclosure) of divine light through the countenance of the Imam. These readings transform Hafez's love poetry into a spiritual manual for contemplation of divine beauty.
