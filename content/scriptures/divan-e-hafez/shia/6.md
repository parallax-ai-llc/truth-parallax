## Shia Reading

Hafez's critique of religious hypocrisy resonates deeply with the Shia historical experience of persecution by outwardly pious rulers. The *zahid* whom Hafez mocks represents the false caliph or unjust ruler who cloaks tyranny in religious garb, while the *rend* represents the authentic believer — often a Shia in the hostile Sunni political environment — who maintains inner fidelity to truth despite outward appearances. The practice of *taqiyya* (prudent concealment) adds another layer of meaning to Hafez's poetry of hidden truth.
