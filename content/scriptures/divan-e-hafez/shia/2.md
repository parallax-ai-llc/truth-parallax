## Shia Reading

In the Shia *irfan* tradition, wine represents the esoteric knowledge (*batin*) transmitted through the Imamic line — a spiritual intoxication that the worldly-minded cannot comprehend. Hafez's tavern is the inner circle of the Imam's teaching, where the initiated receive the wine of divine gnosis. The Shia reading finds in these poems an affirmation that authentic spiritual knowledge cannot be obtained through external learning alone but requires the guidance of the divinely appointed teacher.
