## Shia Reading

Separation in the Shia reading acquires particular poignancy through association with the Greater Occultation of the Twelfth Imam. Hafez's grief at the beloved's absence mirrors the community's longing for the Mahdi, and the hope of reunion that sustains the lover corresponds to the eschatological hope of the Imam's return. These poems are often recited in Shia devotional gatherings, where their emotional power reinforces the community's spiritual bond with the Hidden Imam.
