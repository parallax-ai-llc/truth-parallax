## Shia Reading

The Shia reading of Hafez's poems of union celebrates the moments of spiritual communion with the divine presence that are the fruit of devotion to the Ahl al-Bayt. The ecstasy Hafez describes corresponds to the heightened spiritual states experienced during *rawda* (mourning assemblies for Imam Husayn) and the recitation of *du'a* (supplicatory prayers) attributed to the Imams. These are moments when the veil between the visible and invisible worlds grows thin.
