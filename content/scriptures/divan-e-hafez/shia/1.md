## Shia Reading

The Shia reading of Hafez's love ghazals draws on the rich *irfan* tradition in which love for the Divine is expressed through and alongside love for the Ahl al-Bayt. The longing that pervades these poems is understood as the believer's longing for the Hidden Imam and for the eschatological fulfillment of divine justice. Hafez's lover, separated from the Beloved by the cruelty of fate, mirrors the Shia community's historical experience of separation from their rightful spiritual leaders.
