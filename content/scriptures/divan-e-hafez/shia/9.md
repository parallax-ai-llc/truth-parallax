## Shia Reading

The path of love in the Shia reading is the path of *walaya* — devotion to and spiritual guidance from the Imams. Hafez's insistence that love cannot be learned from books but must be experienced directly resonates with the Shia teaching that authentic knowledge comes through the living chain of Imamic transmission. The lover's willingness to sacrifice everything on the path mirrors Imam Husayn's supreme sacrifice at Karbala.
