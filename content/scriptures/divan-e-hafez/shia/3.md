## Shia Reading

The Shia reading of Hafez's descriptions of the beloved's beauty interprets them through the concept of the divine light (*nur*) that is transmitted through the Prophet to the Imams. The beloved's face reflects the *nur-e Muhammadi* — the Muhammadan light that is the first creation and the source of all spiritual illumination. The beauty Hafez celebrates is ultimately the beauty of the divine presence as it manifests through the lineage of the Ahl al-Bayt.
