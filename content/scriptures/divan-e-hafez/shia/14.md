## Shia Reading

The Shia reading of Hafez's poems on death and remembrance is colored by the intense awareness of martyrdom that pervades Shia spirituality. Hafez's confidence that love endures beyond death resonates with the Shia conviction that the martyrs of Karbala are alive in God's presence, and that the bond between the community and the Ahl al-Bayt transcends the boundary of death. Hafez's own tomb in Shiraz has become a site of pilgrimage that embodies this continuity of love beyond the grave.
