## Shia Reading

The garden in the Shia reading evokes both the earthly paradise of Shiraz — the cultural heartland of Shia Iran — and the heavenly paradise promised to the faithful followers of the Ahl al-Bayt. Hafez's celebration of spring's renewal carries eschatological overtones: the coming of spring mirrors the anticipated coming of the Mahdi, who will renew the earth with justice as spring renews the garden with beauty.
