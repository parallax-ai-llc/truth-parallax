## Shia Reading

The *saqi* in the Shia reading may be understood as the Imam — the divinely appointed dispenser of spiritual sustenance. The cup poured by the saqi contains the wine of *ma'rifa* (gnosis) that flows through the Imamic lineage from the Prophet. The relationship between saqi and drinker mirrors the relationship between Imam and follower, characterized by love, trust, and the transmission of transformative knowledge.
