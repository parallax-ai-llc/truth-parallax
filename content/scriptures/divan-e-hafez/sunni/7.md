## Sunni Reading

Sunni readers appreciate Hafez's poems of union and ecstasy as literary expressions of the spiritual joy that Islam promises to the faithful. The experience of closeness to God through worship — particularly in the *salat* (prayer) and the recitation of the Qur'an — provides the experiential basis for understanding Hafez's rapturous verses. The Sunni tradition values these poems for their capacity to inspire devotional fervor while maintaining that the ultimate union with God belongs to the afterlife.
