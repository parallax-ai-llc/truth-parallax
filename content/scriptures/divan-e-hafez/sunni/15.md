## Sunni Reading

The Sunni appreciation of Hafez's Shiraz poems honors the Islamic teaching that love of homeland (*hubb al-watan*) is a natural and praiseworthy human sentiment. Hafez's celebration of Shiraz's beauty and his refusal to exchange it for other cities models the rootedness in place and community that the Sunni tradition values as a foundation for moral and spiritual life.
