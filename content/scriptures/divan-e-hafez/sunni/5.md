## Sunni Reading

Sunni readers find in Hafez's reflections on fate a poetic elaboration of the Islamic concept of *qadar* (divine decree). Hafez's acceptance of fortune's turns, combined with his insistence on moral effort, reflects the mainstream Sunni position that acknowledges divine predestination while affirming human moral responsibility — a balance that Hafez navigates with characteristic subtlety.
