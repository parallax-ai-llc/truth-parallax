## Sunni Reading

The Sunni literary tradition appreciates Hafez's elaborate descriptions of the beloved's features as supreme examples of the *wasf* (descriptive) conventions of Persian poetry. The detailed physical imagery is understood as the poet's vehicle for expressing spiritual realities that transcend literal description — the beauty of the beloved pointing always beyond itself toward the divine source of all beauty.
