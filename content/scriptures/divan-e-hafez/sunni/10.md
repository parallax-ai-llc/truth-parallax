## Sunni Reading

Sunni appreciation of Hafez's nocturnal poems draws on the Islamic tradition of night prayer (*tahajjud*), which the Qur'an commends as a time of special closeness to God. The quiet hours of the night, when the distractions of daily life recede, provide the setting for the intimate divine encounter that Hafez describes with such beauty and tenderness.
