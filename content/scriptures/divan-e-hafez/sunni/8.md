## Sunni Reading

Hafez's garden imagery resonates with the Sunni tradition's rich appreciation of nature as a sign (*ayah*) of divine creativity and providence. The Qur'anic descriptions of paradise as gardens beneath which rivers flow provide the theological context for Hafez's celebration of earthly gardens as reflections of heavenly beauty. The Sunni reader finds in these poems an invitation to *tafakkur* — contemplation of God's creation as a path to deeper faith.
