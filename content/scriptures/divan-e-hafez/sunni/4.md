## Sunni Reading

The Sunni reading of Hafez's ghazals on separation understands them as expressions of the universal human experience of longing for divine proximity — the spiritual yearning described in the Qur'anic verse 'Return to your Lord, well-pleased and well-pleasing' (89:28). Hafez's sorrow resonates with the Prophetic teaching that this world is a prison for the believer, whose true home is with God.
