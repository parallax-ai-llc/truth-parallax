## Sunni Reading

The Sunni reading of Hafez's poems on renunciation connects them with the Islamic virtue of *zuhd* (world-renunciation) praised in hadith literature and practiced by the early Muslim ascetics. Hafez's celebration of spiritual poverty resonates with the Prophetic teaching that 'poverty is my pride' — though Sunni scholars debate whether this hadith is authentic — and with the broader Islamic teaching that attachment to worldly things is an obstacle to spiritual growth.
