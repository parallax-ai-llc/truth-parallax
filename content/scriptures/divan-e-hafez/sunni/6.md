## Sunni Reading

The Sunni tradition has a complex relationship with Hafez's anti-hypocrisy poems. On one hand, the critique of performative piety resonates with the Prophetic warning against *riya'* (ostentation in worship) and the Qur'anic condemnation of hypocrites. On the other hand, some Sunni scholars worry that Hafez's mockery of the *zahid* and *muhtasib* could undermine respect for religious law and institutional authority. The mainstream Sunni reading resolves this tension by understanding Hafez as criticizing false piety, not genuine religion.
