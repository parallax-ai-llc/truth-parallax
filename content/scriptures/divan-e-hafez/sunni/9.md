## Sunni Reading

The Sunni reading of Hafez's path-of-love ghazals interprets the path (*tariq*) as the Islamic way of life guided by Qur'an and Sunnah. Love of God and the Prophet is the motivating force of the believer's journey, and Hafez's poetry captures the passion and commitment that this journey demands. The Sunni reader values the emotional intensity of these poems as a corrective to merely formal observance.
