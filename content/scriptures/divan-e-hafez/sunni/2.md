## Sunni Reading

Sunni scholars have long debated the proper interpretation of Hafez's wine imagery, with some reading it as purely metaphorical and others acknowledging its deliberate provocativeness. The mainstream Sunni literary tradition treats the wine poems as masterful deployments of a conventional Persian poetic vocabulary that signifies spiritual joy, divine knowledge, and the intoxication of worship. Hafez's wine is the wine of divine love described in the Qur'anic image of the sealed nectar of paradise.
