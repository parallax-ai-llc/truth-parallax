## Sunni Reading

Sunni readers interpret the *saqi* of Hafez's poetry as a poetic figure for divine grace — the unmerited gift of God that makes spiritual progress possible. The cup of wine becomes the cup of divine knowledge or the Qur'an itself, which nourishes the soul of the faithful. This interpretation maintains the Islamic theological framework while honoring the literary power of Hafez's imagery.
