## Sunni Reading

Sunni appreciation of Hafez's descriptions of beauty draws on the Islamic teaching that beauty (*jamal*) is a divine attribute and that the contemplation of beauty in creation can lead the heart toward its Creator. The Sunni reading maintains that Hafez's beloved, however vividly described in human terms, ultimately points toward the divine source of all beauty — an interpretation supported by Hafez's own frequent references to the Qur'an and Islamic theology.
