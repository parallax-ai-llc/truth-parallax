## Sunni Reading

The Sunni tradition reads Hafez's love ghazals within the established framework of Islamic love theory (*nazariyyat al-'ishq*), which distinguishes between love of God, love of the Prophet, and human love. Hafez's language of longing echoes Qur'anic descriptions of the believer's yearning for divine closeness and the Prophetic hadith in which God says 'I was a hidden treasure and I loved to be known.' The Sunni reader appreciates the literary artistry while attending to the theological content embedded in Hafez's imagery.
