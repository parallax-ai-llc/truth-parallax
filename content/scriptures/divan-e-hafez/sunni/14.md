## Sunni Reading

Sunni readers find in Hafez's poems on death and remembrance a literary expression of the Islamic emphasis on *dhikr al-mawt* (remembrance of death) as a spur to righteous living. Hafez's confidence in the endurance of his poetry is read through the Islamic teaching that good deeds — including the creation of beautiful and instructive literature — constitute *sadaqa jariya* (ongoing charity) that benefits the author after death.
