## Academic Reading

Academic study of Hafez's path-of-love ghazals examines how the poet constructs a distinctive ethic of love that challenges both conventional morality and institutional religion. Scholars analyze the figure of the *rend* as a literary-philosophical type — the antinomian lover who claims moral authority not from religious law but from the authenticity of his love — and trace its genealogy in earlier Persian and Arabic literature.
