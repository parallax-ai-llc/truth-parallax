## Academic Reading

Academic analysis of Hafez's poems of union examines how the poet constructs the experience of mystical ecstasy through linguistic and prosodic means. Scholars note Hafez's characteristic technique of placing the most intense moment of the poem in a single couplet — the *bait al-ghazal* or signature verse — surrounded by more restrained lines, creating a structural analogue for the fleeting, overwhelming nature of the mystical experience described.
