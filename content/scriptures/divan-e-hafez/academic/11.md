## Academic Reading

Academic analysis of Hafez's renunciation poems examines the tension between the *ghazal's* aristocratic literary context — these poems were composed for court patrons — and their celebration of poverty and anti-materialism. Scholars analyze this tension as productive rather than contradictory, noting how Hafez uses the literary conventions of his social milieu to critique the values of that milieu, creating a poetry that is simultaneously courtly and subversive.
