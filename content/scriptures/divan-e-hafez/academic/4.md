## Academic Reading

Scholars study Hafez's ghazals of separation within the broader Persian literary tradition of *hijran* (separation) poetry, examining how the experience of loss and longing is constructed through specific rhetorical and prosodic techniques. The emotional intensity of these poems is analyzed not as spontaneous expression but as carefully crafted literary performance, with attention to how meter, rhyme, and imagery create effects of authentic anguish. Comparative studies trace the migration of separation motifs between Arabic, Persian, and Turkish literary traditions.
