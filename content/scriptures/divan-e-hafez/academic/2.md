## Academic Reading

Scholars analyze Hafez's wine poems in relation to the long tradition of *khamriyyat* (wine poetry) in Arabic and Persian literature, from Abu Nuwas to Omar Khayyam. Academic study examines how Hafez deploys wine imagery within the conventions of the *ghazal* form while subverting expectations through ironic self-awareness and theological sophistication. The relationship between literal and figurative wine in these poems remains one of the most contested issues in Hafez studies.
