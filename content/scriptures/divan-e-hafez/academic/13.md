## Academic Reading

Academic study of Hafez's descriptive imagery examines the symbolic lexicon (*rumuz*) that medieval Persian poets and their audiences shared. Scholars reconstruct the interpretive conventions that allowed specific physical features — hair, mole, eyebrow, lip — to carry specific metaphysical meanings, while also analyzing how Hafez plays with and against these conventions, creating poems that are simultaneously conventional and innovative.
