## Academic Reading

Scholars analyze the *saqi* figure in Hafez's poetry as a complex literary persona with roots in both the Persian wine-poetry tradition and Sufi symbolic discourse. The saqi's functions — pouring wine, initiating the drinker into intoxication, mediating between the divine source and the human recipient — are examined in relation to broader patterns of mediation and transmission in Islamic religious and literary culture.
