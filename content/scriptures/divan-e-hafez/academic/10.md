## Academic Reading

Scholars study Hafez's nocturnal ghazals in relation to the literary convention of the *shab* (night) poem in Persian and Arabic poetry. The night functions as a chronotope — a literary time-space — that enables specific kinds of discourse: intimate address, confessional speech, and the exploration of liminal psychological and spiritual states. Academic analysis examines how Hafez exploits the night setting to create poems of extraordinary emotional intensity and philosophical depth.
