## Comparative Reading

Hafez's wine poetry invites comparison with Bacchic traditions across cultures: the Greek symposium, the Chinese wine poets (Li Bai, Du Fu), Khayyam's *rubaiyat*, and the Vedic *soma* hymns. Comparative analysis examines how different cultures have used the imagery of intoxication to explore altered states of consciousness, the dissolution of ego boundaries, and the relationship between pleasure and spiritual insight.
