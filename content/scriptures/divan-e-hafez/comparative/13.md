## Comparative Reading

Comparative study of Hafez's symbolic imagery of hair, mole, and beauty alongside similar symbolic systems in other mystical traditions — the Kabbalistic interpretation of the Song of Songs, Hindu temple sculpture's spiritual iconography, and Byzantine icon theology — reveals a cross-cultural tendency to read physical beauty as a window onto metaphysical reality.
