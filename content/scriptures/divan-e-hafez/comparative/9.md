## Comparative Reading

Comparative analysis of Hafez's path-of-love ghazals alongside other traditions' descriptions of the spiritual path — the Buddhist Eightfold Path, the Christian *via mystica*, the Hindu *yoga marga*, and the Jewish Kabbalistic *sefirot* — reveals different structures for understanding the spiritual journey. Hafez's distinctive contribution is his insistence that love itself is both the path and the destination, collapsing the distance between the way and the goal.
