## Comparative Reading

Comparative study of Hafez's ecstasy poems alongside mystical poetry from other traditions — John of the Cross's *Dark Night of the Soul*, Mirabai's *bhajans*, Rumi's *Masnavi*, Kabir's *dohas*, and Teresa of Avila's *Interior Castle* — reveals both shared features of mystical experience and culturally specific modes of expression. The fleeting, overwhelming nature of mystical union is described across traditions in remarkably similar terms.
