## Comparative Reading

The *saqi* figure in Hafez's poetry parallels mediating figures across religious traditions: the Christian priest administering the Eucharistic wine, the Hindu *guru* transmitting *shakti*, the Buddhist teacher pouring the dharma, and the Kabbalistic *tzaddik* channeling divine abundance. Each tradition recognizes the need for a human intermediary who makes the divine accessible to the seeker.
