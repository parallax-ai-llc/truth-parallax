## Comparative Reading

Comparative study of Hafez's beauty descriptions alongside the Song of Songs' *wasf*, classical Greek and Roman love poetry, Indian *nayika* descriptions, and Japanese *bijin* aesthetics reveals both shared conventions and culturally specific features. The cross-cultural tendency to describe the beloved's body in terms drawn from nature and precious materials suggests deep structural parallels in how human cultures process and express the experience of beauty.
