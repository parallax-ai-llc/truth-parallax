## Comparative Reading

Hafez's garden imagery participates in a cross-cultural tradition of the sacred garden that includes the Garden of Eden, the Persian *pairidaeza* (from which 'paradise' derives), the Chinese scholar's garden, the Japanese Zen garden, and the Indian pleasure garden. Each tradition understands the garden as a space where nature and culture, the earthly and the divine, meet in harmonious beauty.
