## Comparative Reading

Hafez's ghazals of separation connect with a universal literary theme: the lover's lament in the absence of the beloved. Comparative analysis traces this motif through the Sumerian laments of Inanna, Ovid's *Heroides*, the Arabic *nasib*, medieval European *alba* (dawn song), and contemporary love poetry, revealing how different cultures construct the experience of absence and longing through specific literary conventions.
