## Comparative Reading

Hafez's fate ghazals invite comparison with other traditions' reflections on destiny, fortune, and the human condition: Greek tragedy's exploration of fate (*moira*), the Chinese concept of *ming* (mandate), the Hindu doctrine of *karma*, Ecclesiastes' meditations on time and chance, and the Stoic acceptance of *amor fati*. Each tradition offers a distinctive framework for understanding the relationship between human agency and cosmic order.
