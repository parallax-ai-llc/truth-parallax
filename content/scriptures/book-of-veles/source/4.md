---
title: "The Migration Westward"
scripture: "book-of-veles"
chapter: 4
---

**1** When the great drought came upon the eastern lands, our forefathers looked to the west, where rivers were broad and forests were deep.

**2** They gathered their cattle and their goods, their sacred images and their fire, and they set out on the long journey.

**3** For many years they traveled, crossing rivers and mountains, fighting with the peoples who stood in their way.

**4** The god Veles went before them in the form of a great bear, and he showed them the way through the dark forests. The god Stribog sent winds to clear the path before them.

**5** At last they came to the land between the great rivers, and there they settled. They cleared the forests and plowed the fields, built their homes and their temples.

**6** And the gods were pleased, and they blessed the land, and the crops grew and the cattle multiplied, and our people prospered in their new home.
