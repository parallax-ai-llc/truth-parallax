---
title: "Origins of the Slavs"
scripture: "book-of-veles"
chapter: 1
---

**1** This is the Book of Veles, the book of the glory of the Slavic people, from the most ancient times to the present.

**2** The mighty Svarog is the god of heaven, and from him descend all the gods: Perun of thunder, Dazhbog of the sun, Stribog of the winds, Veles of cattle and the underworld.

**3** In the beginning, Svarog separated light from darkness, truth from falsehood, and the world of the living from the world of the dead.

**4** Our forefathers came from the land of Seven Rivers, from the steppes beyond the mountains, driven by war and by the will of the gods.

**5** They traveled for many generations, through forests and across rivers, carrying their gods and their sacred fire, until they came to the lands where we now dwell.

**6** And Dazhbog looked upon them and said: You are my grandchildren. I give you this land. Tend it well, and honor the gods, and you shall prosper.
