---
title: "Wars with the Greeks"
scripture: "book-of-veles"
chapter: 3
---

**1** In those days our forefathers fought against the Greeks, who came from the south with their armies. The Greeks were cunning and treacherous, and they sought to enslave our people.

**2** But our warriors were brave and strong. They called upon Perun to strengthen their arms, and Perun heard them and sent his thunder against the enemy.

**3** Many battles were fought, and much blood was spilled. Our people were driven back, but they did not break. They retreated to the forests and the marshes, where the Greeks could not follow.

**4** And the elders said: We must unite. If we fight as separate tribes, we shall be destroyed. But if we fight as one people, no enemy can defeat us.

**5** And so the tribes came together under one leader, and they drove the Greeks back across the rivers. And there was peace for a time.
