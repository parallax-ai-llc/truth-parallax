---
title: "Lament for the Lost Glory"
scripture: "book-of-veles"
chapter: 8
---

**1** O Slavic people, remember your glory! Remember the days when our warriors were feared from sea to sea, when our princes ruled with justice, when our priests served the true gods.

**2** We have been driven from our lands, scattered by enemies, enslaved by foreigners. Our temples have been destroyed, our sacred groves cut down, our gods forgotten.

**3** But the blood of the ancestors runs in our veins. The memory of the old ways lives in our songs. The gods have not abandoned us, though we have turned from them.

**4** The oak still stands, though its branches are bowed. The sacred fire still burns, though it is hidden. The gods still watch, though we no longer see them.

**5** A time will come when the old ways are remembered, when the sacred fire is rekindled, when the gods are honored once more. Until that time, guard the memory. Pass the stories from father to son.
