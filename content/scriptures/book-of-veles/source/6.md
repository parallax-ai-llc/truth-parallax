---
title: "Teachings of the Ancestors"
scripture: "book-of-veles"
chapter: 6
---

**1** The ancestors teach us: The world is sustained by three pillars: Prav, the world of truth and the gods; Yav, the world of the living; and Nav, the world of the dead.

**2** These three worlds are connected by the great tree, the oak that reaches from the depths of Nav to the heights of Prav. Upon this tree sits the falcon of Perun, and at its roots lies the serpent of Veles.

**3** The battle between Perun and Veles is the battle that sustains the world. It is the battle between sky and earth, between summer and winter, between life and death.

**4** But it is not a battle of good against evil, for both are necessary. Without Perun there would be no rain, and without Veles there would be no harvest. Without death there would be no new life.

**5** Honor both the gods of the sky and the gods of the earth. Pray to Perun for victory in battle, and to Veles for wealth in cattle. Both are needed for the people to prosper.
