---
title: "The Prophecy"
scripture: "book-of-veles"
chapter: 9
---

**1** Veles spoke through the mouths of the seers: A time of darkness comes upon the Slavic people. Foreign gods will be set up in our temples. Our own gods will be cast down and forgotten.

**2** But this darkness will not last forever. After many generations, the people will remember. They will seek the old gods in the sacred groves and by the rivers.

**3** And the gods will answer, for they have not departed. They wait in the unseen world, in Prav, watching over their children.

**4** When the people return to the old ways, the land will be healed. The forests will grow again, the rivers will run clean, and the cattle will multiply.

**5** This is the prophecy of Veles: The world turns in a great circle. What was shall be again. The gods are eternal, and the bond between gods and people cannot be broken, though it be stretched thin.

**6** Guard this book. Keep its words alive. For in these words is the soul of the Slavic people, and as long as the words endure, the people endure.
