---
title: "The Law of the Gods"
scripture: "book-of-veles"
chapter: 2
---

**1** The gods have given us their law: Honor Svarog the heavenly father. Honor Perun the thunderer. Honor Veles who guards the cattle and guides the souls of the dead.

**2** Make offerings at the proper times: at the solstices and equinoxes, at the sowing and the harvest, at births and deaths and marriages.

**3** Keep the sacred fire burning. Let it not go out, for the fire is the presence of Svarog among us. Through the fire we speak to the gods, and through the fire the gods answer.

**4** Do not shed kindred blood. Do not steal from your neighbor. Do not lie before the gods. Do not dishonor your father and mother.

**5** The man who follows the law of the gods shall dwell in the bright meadows of Iriy after death. The man who breaks the law shall wander in the dark places.

**6** And Veles said: I guard the boundary between the living and the dead. Those who honor me shall pass safely. Those who forget me shall be lost in darkness.
