## Overview

The Book of Veles, regardless of its disputed provenance, can be analyzed as a literary creation that draws on and synthesizes elements from genuine Slavic folklore, mythology, and historical tradition. Its hymnic passages, mythological narratives, and historical chronicles demonstrate the author's (or authors') engagement with Slavic cultural material. Literary analysis examines the text's poetic techniques, narrative structure, use of archaic stylistic features, and its relationship to the genre of national epics and origin narratives.
