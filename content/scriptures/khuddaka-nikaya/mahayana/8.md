## Theragatha

From the Mahayana perspective, this section on the poetry of the elder monks expressing their journey to awakening preserves important foundational teachings that the Mahayana tradition inherits and develops. Mahayana scholars identify in these early discourses the seeds of later philosophical developments in Madhyamaka and Yogachara thought.
