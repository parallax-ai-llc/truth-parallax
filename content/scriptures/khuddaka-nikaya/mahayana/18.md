## Milindapanha

From the Mahayana perspective, this section on the philosophical dialogue between King Milinda and the monk Nagasena preserves important foundational teachings that the Mahayana tradition inherits and develops. Mahayana scholars identify in these early discourses the seeds of later philosophical developments in Madhyamaka and Yogachara thought.
