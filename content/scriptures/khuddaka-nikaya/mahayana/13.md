## Apadana

Mahayana Buddhism engages with this teaching on biographical accounts of the lives and deeds of Buddhist saints through its parallel Agama traditions and recognizes it as foundational material upon which later Mahayana developments build. The emphasis on compassion, wisdom, and the welfare of all beings connects these early teachings to the bodhisattva ideal.
