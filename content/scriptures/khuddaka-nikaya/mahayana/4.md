## Itivuttaka

From the Mahayana perspective, this section on short discourses introduced with 'thus it was said by the Blessed One' preserves important foundational teachings that the Mahayana tradition inherits and develops. Mahayana scholars identify in these early discourses the seeds of later philosophical developments in Madhyamaka and Yogachara thought.
