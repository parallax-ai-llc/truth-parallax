## Vimanavatthu

From the Mahayana perspective, this section on stories of those reborn in heavenly mansions through meritorious deeds preserves important foundational teachings that the Mahayana tradition inherits and develops. Mahayana scholars identify in these early discourses the seeds of later philosophical developments in Madhyamaka and Yogachara thought.
