## Patisambhidamagga

From the Mahayana perspective, this section on the Path of Discrimination's analytical philosophical treatise on wisdom preserves important foundational teachings that the Mahayana tradition inherits and develops. Mahayana scholars identify in these early discourses the seeds of later philosophical developments in Madhyamaka and Yogachara thought.
