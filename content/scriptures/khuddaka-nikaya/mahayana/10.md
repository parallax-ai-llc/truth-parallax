## Jataka

From the Mahayana perspective, this section on the Jataka birth stories of the Buddha's previous lives as a bodhisattva preserves important foundational teachings that the Mahayana tradition inherits and develops. Mahayana scholars identify in these early discourses the seeds of later philosophical developments in Madhyamaka and Yogachara thought.
