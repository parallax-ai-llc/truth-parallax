## Vimanavatthu

Zen practitioners appreciate this section on stories of those reborn in heavenly mansions through meritorious deeds for its emphasis on direct experience and practical application. The teaching's focus on immediate awareness and the transformation of consciousness aligns with the Zen aim of awakening to one's true nature in this very moment.
