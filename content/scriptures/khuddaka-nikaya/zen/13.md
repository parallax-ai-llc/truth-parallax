## Apadana

Zen Buddhism finds in this teaching on biographical accounts of the lives and deeds of Buddhist saints a directness and practicality that resonates with Zen values. While Zen emphasizes experience beyond textual study, these teachings provide foundations for the mindful awareness and direct insight that are central to Zen practice.
