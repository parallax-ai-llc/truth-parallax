## Therigatha

Zen Buddhism finds in this teaching on the poetry of the elder nuns recording women's path to liberation a directness and practicality that resonates with Zen values. While Zen emphasizes experience beyond textual study, these teachings provide foundations for the mindful awareness and direct insight that are central to Zen practice.
