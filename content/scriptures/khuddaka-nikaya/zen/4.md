## Itivuttaka

Zen practitioners appreciate this section on short discourses introduced with 'thus it was said by the Blessed One' for its emphasis on direct experience and practical application. The teaching's focus on immediate awareness and the transformation of consciousness aligns with the Zen aim of awakening to one's true nature in this very moment.
