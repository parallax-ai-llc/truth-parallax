## Petavatthu

Zen Buddhism finds in this teaching on stories of hungry ghosts suffering the consequences of unwholesome karma a directness and practicality that resonates with Zen values. While Zen emphasizes experience beyond textual study, these teachings provide foundations for the mindful awareness and direct insight that are central to Zen practice.
