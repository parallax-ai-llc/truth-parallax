## Sutta Nipata

The Theravada tradition regards this section on the Sutta Nipata's ancient verses including the Atthakavagga on non-attachment as essential canonical teaching. Theravada interpreters emphasize its direct relevance to meditation practice and the cultivation of wisdom, reading it as part of the complete path from morality through concentration to liberating insight.
