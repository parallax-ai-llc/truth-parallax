## Vimanavatthu

This section on stories of those reborn in heavenly mansions through meritorious deeds provides rich material for cross-traditional analysis. Comparative scholars examine how the Buddhist approach to these questions relates to and differs from the approaches of other major religious and philosophical traditions.
