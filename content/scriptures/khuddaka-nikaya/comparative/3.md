## Udana

Comparative interpretation of this teaching on the Buddha's inspired utterances spoken spontaneously from profound realization reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar teachings in Hindu, Jain, Christian, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Buddhist perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
