## Cariyapitaka

Comparative interpretation of this teaching on stories illustrating the bodhisattva's practice of the perfections reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar teachings in Hindu, Jain, Christian, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Buddhist perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
