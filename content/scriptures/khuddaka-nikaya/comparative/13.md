## Apadana

Comparative interpretation of this teaching on biographical accounts of the lives and deeds of Buddhist saints reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar teachings in Hindu, Jain, Christian, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Buddhist perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
