## Niddesa

Comparative interpretation of this teaching on the Niddesa commentary on portions of the Sutta Nipata reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar teachings in Hindu, Jain, Christian, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Buddhist perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
