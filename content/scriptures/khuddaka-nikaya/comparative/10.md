## Jataka

This section on the Jataka birth stories of the Buddha's previous lives as a bodhisattva provides rich material for cross-traditional analysis. Comparative scholars examine how the Buddhist approach to these questions relates to and differs from the approaches of other major religious and philosophical traditions.
