## Petavatthu

Comparative interpretation of this teaching on stories of hungry ghosts suffering the consequences of unwholesome karma reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar teachings in Hindu, Jain, Christian, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Buddhist perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
