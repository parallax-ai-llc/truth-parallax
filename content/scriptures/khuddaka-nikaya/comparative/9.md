## Therigatha

Comparative interpretation of this teaching on the poetry of the elder nuns recording women's path to liberation reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar teachings in Hindu, Jain, Christian, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Buddhist perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
