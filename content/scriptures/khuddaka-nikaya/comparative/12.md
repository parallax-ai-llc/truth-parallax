## Patisambhidamagga

This section on the Path of Discrimination's analytical philosophical treatise on wisdom provides rich material for cross-traditional analysis. Comparative scholars examine how the Buddhist approach to these questions relates to and differs from the approaches of other major religious and philosophical traditions.
