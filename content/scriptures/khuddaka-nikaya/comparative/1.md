## Khuddakapatha

Comparative interpretation of this teaching on short readings for novice training and daily recitation reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar teachings in Hindu, Jain, Christian, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Buddhist perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
