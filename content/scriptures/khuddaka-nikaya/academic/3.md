## Udana

Academic scholars analyze this section on the Buddha's inspired utterances spoken spontaneously from profound realization using the methods of textual criticism, historical contextualization, and comparative study. The text provides evidence for understanding the development of early Buddhist thought and its relationship to the broader intellectual culture of ancient India.
