## Buddhavamsa

From an academic perspective, this teaching on the chronicle of past Buddhas and the lineage of awakened ones is examined for its compositional history, its relationship to parallel versions in other Buddhist canons, and its significance for reconstructing the teachings of the historical Buddha.
