## Vimanavatthu

From an academic perspective, this teaching on stories of those reborn in heavenly mansions through meritorious deeds is examined for its compositional history, its relationship to parallel versions in other Buddhist canons, and its significance for reconstructing the teachings of the historical Buddha.
