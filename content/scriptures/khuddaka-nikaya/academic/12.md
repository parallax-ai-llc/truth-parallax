## Patisambhidamagga

From an academic perspective, this teaching on the Path of Discrimination's analytical philosophical treatise on wisdom is examined for its compositional history, its relationship to parallel versions in other Buddhist canons, and its significance for reconstructing the teachings of the historical Buddha.
