## Jataka

From an academic perspective, this teaching on the Jataka birth stories of the Buddha's previous lives as a bodhisattva is examined for its compositional history, its relationship to parallel versions in other Buddhist canons, and its significance for reconstructing the teachings of the historical Buddha.
