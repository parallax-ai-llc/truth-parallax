## Petavatthu

Academic scholars analyze this section on stories of hungry ghosts suffering the consequences of unwholesome karma using the methods of textual criticism, historical contextualization, and comparative study. The text provides evidence for understanding the development of early Buddhist thought and its relationship to the broader intellectual culture of ancient India.
