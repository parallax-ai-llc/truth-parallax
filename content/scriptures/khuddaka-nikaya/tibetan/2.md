## Dhammapada

In the Tibetan scholastic tradition, this section on the Dhammapada's verses of truth on ethics, mind, and the path provides essential doctrinal foundations studied in the monastic curriculum. Tibetan scholars connect these teachings to the broader framework of Buddhist philosophy, from Abhidharma analysis through Madhyamaka and Yogachara to tantric practice.
