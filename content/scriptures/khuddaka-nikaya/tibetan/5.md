## Sutta Nipata

Tibetan Buddhism engages with this teaching on the Sutta Nipata's ancient verses including the Atthakavagga on non-attachment as part of the foundational sutra vehicle upon which the complete Tibetan Buddhist path is constructed. These teachings inform the analytical meditation practices and the stages-of-the-path (lam-rim) literature that structures Tibetan Buddhist education.
