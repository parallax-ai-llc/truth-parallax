## Jataka

In the Tibetan scholastic tradition, this section on the Jataka birth stories of the Buddha's previous lives as a bodhisattva provides essential doctrinal foundations studied in the monastic curriculum. Tibetan scholars connect these teachings to the broader framework of Buddhist philosophy, from Abhidharma analysis through Madhyamaka and Yogachara to tantric practice.
