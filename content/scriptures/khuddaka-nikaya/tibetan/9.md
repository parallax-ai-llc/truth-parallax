## Therigatha

Tibetan Buddhism engages with this teaching on the poetry of the elder nuns recording women's path to liberation as part of the foundational sutra vehicle upon which the complete Tibetan Buddhist path is constructed. These teachings inform the analytical meditation practices and the stages-of-the-path (lam-rim) literature that structures Tibetan Buddhist education.
