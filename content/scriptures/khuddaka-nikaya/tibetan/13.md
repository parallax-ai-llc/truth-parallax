## Apadana

Tibetan Buddhism engages with this teaching on biographical accounts of the lives and deeds of Buddhist saints as part of the foundational sutra vehicle upon which the complete Tibetan Buddhist path is constructed. These teachings inform the analytical meditation practices and the stages-of-the-path (lam-rim) literature that structures Tibetan Buddhist education.
