## Petavatthu

Tibetan Buddhism engages with this teaching on stories of hungry ghosts suffering the consequences of unwholesome karma as part of the foundational sutra vehicle upon which the complete Tibetan Buddhist path is constructed. These teachings inform the analytical meditation practices and the stages-of-the-path (lam-rim) literature that structures Tibetan Buddhist education.
