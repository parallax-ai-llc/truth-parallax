## Theragatha

In the Tibetan scholastic tradition, this section on the poetry of the elder monks expressing their journey to awakening provides essential doctrinal foundations studied in the monastic curriculum. Tibetan scholars connect these teachings to the broader framework of Buddhist philosophy, from Abhidharma analysis through Madhyamaka and Yogachara to tantric practice.
