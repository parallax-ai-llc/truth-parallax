---
title: "Kalevala Canto 20"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 20
---

The bride departed from her homeland,
From the cold and dreary Northland,
To the warm and sunny Southland,
To the home of Ilmarinen.

She wept upon the journey southward,
Wept to leave her mother's dwelling,
Wept to leave the hills of childhood,
Wept to leave the friends of girlhood.

And her mother counseled her:
Weep not, daughter, on thy journey.
Thou shalt find a fairer dwelling,
Thou shalt find a kinder husband.

In the home of Ilmarinen
She learned the ways of her new household,
Tended cattle, baked the bread,
Kept the fire and swept the hearthstone.
