---
title: "Kalevala Canto 6"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 6
---

Joukahainen, youthful minstrel,
Journeyed onward, drove his reindeer,
On the highway of the Northland,
On the road to Vainamoinen.

Then the two sleds met together,
Shaft was tangled fast with shaft,
Reins with reins were interwoven,
Trace with trace entwined together.

Joukahainen spoke and challenged:
Art thou Vainamoinen, minstrel,
Of the ancient race of singers?
Let us match our songs together,
Each his wisdom demonstrating.

Vainamoinen, old and steadfast,
Answered thus the youthful singer:
What dost thou know, youthful fellow?
Come and share thy store of wisdom.
