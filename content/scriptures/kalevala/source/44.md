---
title: "Kalevala Canto 44"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 44
---

This is the story of the Sampo,
How it was forged and how it was stolen,
How it shattered on the ocean,
How its fragments brought abundance.

The Sampo that ground out riches,
Ground out grain and salt and money,
Lost forever in the ocean,
But its blessings still remain.

For wherever fragments landed,
There the earth produced its bounty.
Finland's shores were ever blessed
By the pieces of the Sampo.

And the memory of the Sampo
Lives in song and lives in story,
Passed from singer unto singer,
Through the generations evermore.
