---
title: "Kalevala Canto 45"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 45
---

Hear the tale of Kullervo,
Saddest story ever told,
Of the orphan boy whose hatred
Brought destruction on them all.

Let the mothers learn from Kullervo,
Let the fathers heed the warning:
Raise your children well and wisely,
Do not let them grow in bitterness.

For the child who grows in darkness,
In the shadow of injustice,
Carries darkness in his spirit,
Spreads destruction all around him.

But the child who grows in sunlight,
Nurtured well with love and wisdom,
Brings the light to all around him,
Blesses all who know his presence.
