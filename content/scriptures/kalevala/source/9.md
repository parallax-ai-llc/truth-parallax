---
title: "Kalevala Canto 9"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 9
---

Vainamoinen built a vessel,
Built a boat of magic timber,
Built it from the words of wisdom,
From the songs of ancient knowing.

Three words failed him in the building,
Three enchanted words were wanting,
Words to finish bow and quarter,
Words to fasten keel and rudder.

Then the aged Vainamoinen
Sought these words in every quarter,
From the mouths of swallows sought them,
From the tongues of summer wild-geese,
Underneath the tongues of reindeer.

But the words he sought were hidden,
Hidden deep in Tuonela,
In the kingdom of the dead ones,
In the realm of Tuoni's people.
