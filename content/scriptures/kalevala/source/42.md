---
title: "Kalevala Canto 42"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 42
---

Vainamoinen built his last vessel,
Built a boat of gleaming copper.
He gathered all his songs together,
All the wisdom of the ages.

He said farewell to his people:
I am going to the far lands,
To the upper world ascending,
To the lower world descending.

But my kantele I leave you,
And my songs I leave behind me,
For the children of the future,
For the people yet unborn.
