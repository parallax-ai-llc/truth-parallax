---
title: "Kalevala Canto 18"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 18
---

In the hall of Pohja gathered
All the heroes of the Northland.
Vainamoinen sang his greatest,
Sang the hall to breathless silence.

Every heart was moved to wonder,
Every eye was filled with tears,
At the beauty of his singing,
At the sweetness of his music.

He sang of the world beginning,
Of the air and water parting,
Of the islands rising upward,
Of the trees and mountains forming.

Then the maiden of Pohjola
Chose the smith, not the singer.
Ilmarinen won the maiden,
He who forged the magic Sampo.
