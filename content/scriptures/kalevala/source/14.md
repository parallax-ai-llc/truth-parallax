---
title: "Kalevala Canto 14"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 14
---

Lemminkainen, reckless hero,
Island-dweller, handsome suitor,
Heard about the maid of Pohja,
Set his heart upon the maiden.

His mother warned him, saying:
Do not go unto Pohjola,
To the dark and dreary Northland,
There the sorcerers will slay thee,
There they will sing thee into ruin.

But Lemminkainen heeded not,
Brushed his hair and donned his finest,
Set upon the road to Pohja,
Singing as he went his journey.

With his songs of power he conquered
Every peril on the pathway,
Every snare and every ambush,
Till he reached the gates of Pohja.
