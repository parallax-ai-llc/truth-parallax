---
title: "Kalevala Canto 47"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 47
---

Hear the tale of Ilmarinen,
He the smith, the great creator.
From his forge came wondrous workings,
From his anvil, marvels endless.

He who forged the Sampo treasure,
He who forged the sky and heavens,
He who hammered out the firmament,
So that not a mark was showing.

Yet for all his skill and cunning,
He could not forge love or comfort.
Golden wife gave him no warmth,
Silver bride gave him no solace.

Let the craftsmen learn from this:
Art cannot replace the human,
Skill cannot create a soul,
And no forge can fashion love.
