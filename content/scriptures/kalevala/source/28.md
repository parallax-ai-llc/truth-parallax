---
title: "Kalevala Canto 28"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 28
---

Lemminkainen, reckless hero,
Wished to sing upon the journey.
Vainamoinen warned him: Silence!
Do not wake the sleeping Northland.

But the hero would not listen,
Raised his voice in joyful singing.
And a crane upon a hillock
Heard the song and screamed in terror.

The crane flew to dark Pohjola,
Woke the sleeping mistress Louhi.
Louhi saw the Sampo stolen,
Raised a tempest on the waters.

Wind and wave and fog and darkness
Fell upon the fleeing vessel.
Vainamoinen lost his kantele,
Lost it in the heaving ocean.
