---
title: "Kalevala Canto 10"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 10
---

Vainamoinen, old and steadfast,
Journeyed down to Tuonela,
To the kingdom of the dead ones,
To the realm beneath the waters.

Tuonetar, the hostess aged,
Brought a tankard filled with beer,
With the frogs in foam disporting,
With the worms beneath the surface.

She spoke words of false persuasion:
Drink, thou ancient Vainamoinen,
Drink the beer of Tuonela.

Vainamoinen, old and wisely:
I have not come here to drink,
Not to taste the beer of darkness.
Those who drink in Tuonela,
There forever they must tarry.

From the land of death he struggled,
Burst through nets of steel and copper,
Homeward fled the aged singer,
Warning all the world hereafter:
Never seek the dead men's kingdom.
