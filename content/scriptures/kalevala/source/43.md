---
title: "Kalevala Canto 43"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 43
---

Vainamoinen sailed away,
In his boat of gleaming copper,
Between the heavens and the waters,
To the boundary of the world.

And the people watched him going,
Watched the copper vessel gleaming,
Growing smaller on the horizon,
Till it vanished from their sight.

But he left a word behind him,
Left his songs to guide the people:
Never shall there be a shortage
Of good singers in this country,
Not while moonlight shines on rooftops,
Not while sunlight warms the meadows.
