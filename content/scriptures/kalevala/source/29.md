---
title: "Kalevala Canto 29"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 29
---

Louhi sent a ship of warriors,
Sent them after the escaping heroes.
On the open sea they battled,
Louhi's forces and the heroes.

Vainamoinen raised from water
A great reef of hidden stone.
Louhi's ship was dashed upon it,
Shattered into thousand pieces.

But the mistress of Pohjola,
Changed herself into a great eagle,
Talons grasping, wings extended,
Swooped upon the heroes' vessel.

On the gunwale perched the eagle,
Seized the Sampo in her talons.
In the struggle, Sampo shattered,
Fell in pieces to the ocean.
