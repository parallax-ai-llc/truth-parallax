---
title: "Kalevala Canto 19"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 19
---

Ilmarinen, smith eternal,
Wed the maiden of Pohjola,
Fairest daughter of the Northland.
Great the feast and great the feasting.

Vainamoinen sang the wedding,
Sang the ancient marriage blessings,
Counseled bride and counseled bridegroom,
In the customs of their fathers.

To the bride he sang his counsel:
Be a light within your household,
Be the mistress of your dwelling,
First to rise and last to slumber.

To the groom he sang his counsel:
Be a pillar of your household,
Shield your wife from cold and hunger,
Let no harsh word wound her spirit.
