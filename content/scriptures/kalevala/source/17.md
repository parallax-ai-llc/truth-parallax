---
title: "Kalevala Canto 17"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 17
---

Vainamoinen, old and steadfast,
Built himself a boat of magic,
From the fragments of the spindle,
From the splinters of the distaff.

In the boat he set out sailing,
To the cold and dreary Northland,
Where the maiden waited, fairest,
Daughter of the North, most lovely.

But the sea-god raised a tempest,
And the vessel spun and shuddered,
In the grip of wind and water.

Vainamoinen calmed the waters,
Spoke the words of ancient power,
Stilled the storm and smoothed the billows.
Then he sailed in peace to Pohja.
