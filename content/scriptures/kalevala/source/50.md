---
title: "Kalevala Canto 50"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 50
---

Thus I end my song, my singing,
Thus I close the book of legends.
I have sung the songs of Suomi,
I have told the ancient stories.

Even so shall end the singer,
Even so the best must finish.
Not a waterfall runs ever,
And no singer sings forever.

What if I have sung but poorly,
What if I have told but badly?
I am but a humble singer,
I have done the best I could.

I have set the course for others,
I have blazed a path through forest.
Let the young and strong come after,
Let them sing a better singing.

The road is open, the path is cleared,
The threshold waits for younger feet.
Longer songs will yet be needed,
Longer tales remain for telling.
