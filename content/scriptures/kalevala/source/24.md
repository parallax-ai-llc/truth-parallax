---
title: "Kalevala Canto 24"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 24
---

Kullervo went forth to battle,
Went to war against Untamo,
He who slew his family.

With his sword he struck down Untamo,
Burnt his holdings to the ground,
Left not one stone upon another.

But in victory no peace,
For a greater sorrow waited.
In a forest, on a journey,
He had met a maiden fair.

Too late he learned the maiden
Was his sister, long since lost.
She, on learning, cast herself
Into the rushing river.

Kullervo, consumed by grief,
Cast himself upon his sword.
Thus the wretched hero perished,
Thus the hapless Kullervo ended.
Vainamoinen warned all people:
Raise your children well and wisely.
