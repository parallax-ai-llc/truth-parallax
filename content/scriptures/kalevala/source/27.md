---
title: "Kalevala Canto 27"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 27
---

Ilmarinen broke the fastenings,
Nine great locks of hardened copper.
With an ox he pulled the Sampo
From its resting place of stone.

The Sampo, lid of many colors,
Ground out riches for Pohjola:
Ground out grain upon one surface,
Ground out salt upon another,
Ground out money on the third side.

In the vessel placed the Sampo,
Set it deep within the hold,
Then they sailed for home in haste,
Over waters dark and gleaming.
