---
title: "Kalevala Canto 16"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 16
---

Lemminkainen at the river
Of the dead was struck and slaughtered
By a cowherd, blind and ancient,
With a water-serpent thrust him,
Cast him in the stream of Tuoni.

There the dead man drifted downward,
Down the dark and rushing torrent,
To the whirlpool of the death-stream.

But his mother, far in homeland,
Saw the blood upon her hairbrush,
Knew her son had met destruction.
With a rake of copper, forging,
Raked the river of the dead ones,
Found her son and drew him homeward.

With her ointments, salves, and magic,
She restored his life and breathing.
Lemminkainen lived once more.
