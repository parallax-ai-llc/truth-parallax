---
title: "Kalevala Canto 3"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 3
---

Vainamoinen, old and steadfast,
Lived his days within the waters,
On the open sea he floated.

Then the aged Vainamoinen,
Raised his head above the waters,
And began the work of creation.

Where his hand he turned to landward,
There appeared a promontory;
Where his foot he pressed beneath him,
There he hollowed out a fish-pond;
Where he dived beneath the water,
Deeper hollows yet he fashioned.

Thus he shaped the lands and shallows,
Called to being shores and waters,
Formed the contour of the mainland,
Traced the courses of the rivers.
