---
title: "Kalevala Canto 15"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 15
---

Lemminkainen came to Pohja,
To the feasting hall of Louhi.
Louhi set him tasks to conquer
Ere he could obtain the maiden.

First he must pursue the elk,
The great elk of Hiisi's forest,
On the plains of Hiisi running.
This he did with magic snowshoes.

Then he must bridle Hiisi's stallion,
Fire-breathing horse of evil,
On the green and open meadow.
This he did with words of power.

Last he must shoot the swan of Tuoni,
On the dark and dismal river,
With a single shaft and arrow.
To the death-stream Lemminkainen ventured.
