---
title: "Kalevala Canto 7"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 7
---

Vainamoinen, old and steadfast,
Sang the ancient songs of power,
Sang the youthful Joukahainen
Deep into the marshy places.

To his chin he sang him downward,
To his waist in mud and marshland.
Joukahainen cried in terror,
Begged for mercy from the singer.

Then he offered Vainamoinen
All his goods and all his treasures,
Offered lands and golden riches,
But the ancient singer heeded
None of these, and still continued.

Then in desperation crying,
Joukahainen pledged his sister,
Aino, fairest maid of Northland.
Vainamoinen ceased his singing,
Freed the youth from marsh and mire.
