---
title: "Kalevala Canto 37"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 37
---

Louhi gathered all her forces,
All the warriors of the Northland,
Sent them south to wage destruction,
Sent them forth against the heroes.

She who once possessed the Sampo,
She whose wealth had all been stolen,
Raised an army vast and fearsome,
Armed with swords and spears of iron.

Vainamoinen saw them coming,
Standing on the shore he watched them,
Ship on ship, the fleet approaching,
Dark as thunderclouds on water.

He prepared the land's defenses,
Sang the ancient songs of warding,
Called upon the gods of sky and water
To protect the land of heroes.
