---
title: "Kalevala Canto 23"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 23
---

Kullervo journeyed through the forest,
Seeking his lost family members.
An old woman told him truly
That his mother still was living.

In a distant hut he found her,
Found his mother, aged and weeping.
Mother and son embraced each other,
Tears of joy upon their faces.

Then his mother told him sadly:
All our family has perished,
All destroyed by cruel Untamo.
Only I remain, and thou,
And thy sister, who was lost
Long ago, and none can find her.

Kullervo swore a mighty oath:
He would avenge his family,
Destroy Untamo and his people,
Repay wrong with righteous anger.
