---
title: "Kalevala Canto 35"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 35
---

Ilmarinen forged a new sun,
Forged a moon of gleaming silver.
But the golden sun gave no warmth,
And the silver moon no radiance.

Vainamoinen cast the lots,
Read the future in the wood-chips.
The true sun and moon, they told him,
Lay within the hill of Pohja.

Armed with sword and wearing armor,
Vainamoinen went to Pohja,
Slew the warriors of the Northland,
Broke the doors of copper mountain.

But the sun and moon he found not,
For the mistress Louhi hid them
In a place beyond his finding.
Then at last old Louhi yielded,
Fearing worse, released the captives.
Sun and moon shone forth once more.
