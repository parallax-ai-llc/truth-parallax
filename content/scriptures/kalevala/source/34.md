---
title: "Kalevala Canto 34"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 34
---

The fire fell from heaven downward,
Through the clouds and through the heavens,
Through nine vaults of sky descending.

Vainamoinen and the smith
Sought the fire where it had fallen.
Through the forest, through the marshland,
By the shore of lake and river.

A fish had swallowed up the fire,
A whitefish in a blue lake's waters.
They drew it from the fish's belly,
But the fire leaped and burned them.

Forest burned and field was blazing,
Half the Northland was consumed.
At last they caught the flame and tamed it,
Set it in the birch-bark tinder,
Brought the fire back to the hearthstone.
