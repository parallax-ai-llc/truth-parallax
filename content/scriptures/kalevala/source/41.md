---
title: "Kalevala Canto 41"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 41
---

The child spoke from out the manger,
Spoke and rebuked Vainamoinen:
Thou hast sinned as greatly, old one,
Yet no one condemned thee to drowning.

The child was baptized and blessed,
Named the King of all Karelia.
Vainamoinen, old and angered,
Felt his time had come to ending.

The old singer, deeply wounded,
Knew the child would rule hereafter,
Knew a new age was beginning,
Knew his own time had departed.
