---
title: "Kalevala Canto 4"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 4
---

Vainamoinen, old and steadfast,
In the new-formed land abiding,
On the treeless plains and barren,
On the surface bare and grassless.

Sampsa Pellervoinen, small one,
Was the youth who sowed the country,
Sowed the lands with trees and bushes,
Sowed the hills with pine and fir-trees,
And the valleys filled with birches.

In the swamps he sowed the alders,
On the sandy heaths the willows,
Rowan-trees in holy places,
On the banks he sowed the willows,
On the marshlands sowed the junipers.

Then the trees grew up and flourished,
And the saplings sprouted bravely.
With their bloom the firs were loaded,
And the pines their boughs extended.
