---
title: "Kalevala Canto 30"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 30
---

Fragments of the Sampo scattered,
Scattered widely on the waters.
Some sank deep beneath the billows,
Some were carried to the shoreline.

Vainamoinen gathered pieces,
Gathered fragments from the beaches.
These he carried to his homeland,
Planted them upon the headlands.

From these fragments grew abundance:
Fields of grain and barley growing,
Wealth and plenty for his people,
Fortune for the land of Suomi.

But the lid of many colors
Sank beneath the waves forever.
Louhi cursed and Louhi threatened,
Swore revenge upon the heroes.
