---
title: "Kalevala Canto 49"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 49
---

The kantele still sounds its music
In the evenings of the Northland.
The old songs have not been silenced,
Though the singers are departed.

Young men, listen to these verses,
Young women, hear these songs.
They are the heritage of ages,
Born from joy and born from sorrow.

In the song is all our history,
In the song is all our wisdom,
In the song is all our longing,
For the beauty that has been.

Let the kantele keep sounding,
Let the old songs keep resounding,
Till the last day of the ages,
Till the sun no longer rises.
