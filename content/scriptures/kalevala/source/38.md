---
title: "Kalevala Canto 38"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 38
---

Louhi sent a bear of evil,
A great beast against the herds,
To destroy the cattle of the Southland,
To devour the sheep and oxen.

Vainamoinen, old and steadfast,
Heard the cries of his people.
He called upon the ancient bear-songs,
Sang the origin of the bear.

The bear was born on heaven's shoulders,
By the moon among the stars,
Lowered down on golden chains
To the forest floor of Northland.

Vainamoinen hunted well,
Brought the bear to ground with honor,
Then prepared the feast of bear-meat,
Sang the songs of celebration.
