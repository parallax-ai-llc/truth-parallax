---
title: "Kalevala Canto 36"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 36
---

Lemminkainen heard of feasting
In the halls of dark Pohjola.
He had not been invited,
Louhi had not sent him summons.

Angry, reckless Lemminkainen
Journeyed north without an asking.
On the road he met great dangers:
Serpents, ditches, wolves, and bears.

With his songs he conquered all,
With his magic passed each peril.
To the gates of Pohja coming,
He demanded food and drink.

Louhi served him at the table,
But with malice in her heart.
Lemminkainen sensed the danger,
Left before the trap was sprung.
