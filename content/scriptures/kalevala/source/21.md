---
title: "Kalevala Canto 21"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 21
---

Kullervo, the hapless orphan,
Child of sorrow, born in darkness,
Sold as servant to the smith,
To Ilmarinen, harsh taskmaster.

His father slain by Untamo,
His mother taken as a slave,
Young Kullervo, strong but reckless,
Broke whatever task was set him.

Sent to rock the baby, rocking,
He destroyed the baby's cradle.
Sent to clear the trees and forest,
He destroyed the woods entirely.

Burning hatred filled his spirit,
Hatred for his master's household,
For the wrong done to his people,
For the slavery of his family.
