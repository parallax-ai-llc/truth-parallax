---
title: "Kalevala Canto 22"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 22
---

Kullervo was sent to herd
The cattle of the smith's young wife.
She gave him bread with stone inside,
That broke his father's ancient knife.

This knife was all his heritage,
The sole remembrance of his father.
Rage consumed the wretched Kullervo.
He drove the cattle to the marshes.

In their place he herded wolves
And bears with magic transformed,
Sent them home to Ilmarinen.
When the wife went forth to milk them,
Wolves and bears they fell upon her.

Thus was the wife of the smith destroyed,
And Ilmarinen wept in sorrow
For the maiden of Pohjola.
