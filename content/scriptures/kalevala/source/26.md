---
title: "Kalevala Canto 26"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 26
---

Vainamoinen, old and steadfast,
With the smith and Lemminkainen,
Sailed to steal the Sampo treasure,
From the vaults of dark Pohjola.

Three heroes in one vessel sailing,
Over waves of blue and silver,
To the cold and dreary Northland,
Where the Sampo ground its riches.

Vainamoinen sang the guardians
Into deep enchanted slumber.
Louhi and her people sleeping,
Locked in bonds of magic music.
