---
title: "Kalevala Canto 8"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 8
---

Aino wept upon the meadow,
Wept beside the deep blue ocean,
Wept for her unhappy fortune,
That she must wed aged singer.

To the ocean shore she wandered,
To the rocks beside the water,
Cast aside her robes and garments,
Cast herself into the billows.

Never more was Aino seen,
Never more the maiden wandered
On the meadows of her homeland,
On the shores of her own country.

Vainamoinen, old and steadfast,
Cast his nets upon the water,
Drew them through the deeps of ocean,
Seeking Aino, seeking ever.
