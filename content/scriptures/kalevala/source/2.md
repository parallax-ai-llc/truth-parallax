---
title: "Kalevala Canto 2"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 2
---

Vainamoinen, old and steadfast,
He the great primeval minstrel,
Went upon his way in sadness,
On the blue sea's open surface,
On the far extending waters.

He stood upon the open ocean,
On the vast and boundless water.
There he pondered and reflected,
In his mind the thought arising:
How to live, and in what manner,
On the water all unshaded,
On the vast and open ocean.

Then a bird, a beauteous teal-duck,
Hovered round and flew about him,
Seeking for a place to rest in,
Seeking for a home to dwell in.
