---
title: "Kalevala Canto 48"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 48
---

These are the ancient songs of Suomi,
Gathered from the lips of singers,
From the memories of the aged,
From the tales of generations.

I have gathered them together,
Bound them in a single bundle,
Like a ball of thread for spinning,
Like a spool upon the spindle.

From the cold homes of the Northland,
From the warm homes of the Southland,
From the meadows of Karelia,
From the shores of lake and river.

These songs I give to you, my people,
Guard them well and pass them onward,
That the wisdom of the ancients
May not perish from the earth.
