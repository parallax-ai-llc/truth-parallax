---
title: "Kalevala Canto 32"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 32
---

Louhi, mistress of Pohjola,
Sent diseases on the Southland,
Sent them riding on the night-wind,
Plagues and pestilence and fever.

Vainamoinen, old and steadfast,
Healed the people with his magic.
He brewed salves from nine ingredients,
Spoke the origin of ailments.

He who knows the word of origin,
He who speaks the primal cause,
Has the power over sickness,
Has the mastery of healing.

Fire was born from heaven's lightning,
Iron from the milk of maidens,
Water from the ancient fountain:
Know the origin, know the cure.
