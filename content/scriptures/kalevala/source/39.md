---
title: "Kalevala Canto 39"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 39
---

Vainamoinen played his kantele,
Played it at the feast of bear-meat.
All the people came to listen,
All the creatures of the forest.

He played until the kantele
Fell from his aged fingers.
He wept, and from his weeping
Tears like pearls fell on the ground.

The tears rolled down to the ocean,
Down beneath the waves they tumbled.
There they hardened into pearls,
Blue pearls resting on the sea-floor.

A duck dove down to gather them,
But could not lift them from the bottom.
Vainamoinen said: These tears
Are too heavy even for the ocean.
