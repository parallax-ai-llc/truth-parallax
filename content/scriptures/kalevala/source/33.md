---
title: "Kalevala Canto 33"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 33
---

Louhi stole the sun and moon,
Hid them deep within the mountain,
In the copper-covered cavern,
In the iron-bolted fortress.

Darkness fell upon the Northland,
Darkness fell upon the Southland.
Neither sun nor moon gave brightness,
And the world was wrapped in shadow.

Vainamoinen, old and steadfast,
Sought to bring the light returning.
Ukko, god above the heavens,
Struck new fire from his sword-blade,
Gave it to the air-born maiden
To be rocked into a new sun.
