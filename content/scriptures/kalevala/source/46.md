---
title: "Kalevala Canto 46"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 46
---

Hear the tale of Lemminkainen,
Reckless hero, handsome suitor.
From his story learn this lesson:
Heed the counsel of your mother.

She who bore you, she who nursed you,
She who knows the ways of danger,
When she warns you, stop and listen,
For her wisdom comes from love.

Lemminkainen heeded never,
Rushed to danger without thinking,
Died upon the stream of Tuoni,
Only mother's love could save him.

Let the young men learn from this:
Courage is not recklessness,
Bravery is not blindness,
And a mother's word is sacred.
