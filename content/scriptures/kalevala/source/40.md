---
title: "Kalevala Canto 40"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 40
---

Mariatta, lovely maiden,
Virgin of the air and beauty,
Swallowed up a lingonberry
That she found upon the hillside.

From this berry she conceived,
Bore a son of wondrous power.
But no one would give her shelter,
No one aid the shamed maiden.

In a stable, in a manger,
There she bore her infant son.
The child was lost and then was found
Beneath a bush of juniper.

They brought the child to Vainamoinen,
Asked him to pronounce the judgment:
Should the boy be raised or drowned?
Shall the fatherless be spared?
