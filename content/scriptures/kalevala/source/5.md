---
title: "Kalevala Canto 5"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 5
---

Vainamoinen, old and steadfast,
He the great primeval sorcerer,
Gave himself to deeper thinking,
Long reflected, deeply pondered:
Who shall sow this barren region,
Sow the seed of future harvest?

Pellervoinen, son of meadow,
Sampsa, boy of smallest stature,
He shall sow this barren region,
He shall scatter seed around him.

Seeds he gathered from the marshes,
Seeds from every hill and valley,
Then upon the land he scattered
Seeds of every tree and flower.

On the hills he sowed the pine-trees,
On the mounds he sowed the fir-trees,
Heather on the sandy stretches,
Saplings in the valleys planted.
