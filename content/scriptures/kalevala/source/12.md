---
title: "Kalevala Canto 12"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 12
---

Vainamoinen, old and steadfast,
Having gained the words of power,
Sailed unto the misty island,
Sought the maiden of Pohjola.

Louhi, mistress of the Northland,
Spoke to him and asked his errand.
Vainamoinen told his mission:
Forge a Sampo, magic treasure,
And the maiden shall be given.

Only Ilmarinen, master,
He the eternal hammerer,
He alone can forge the Sampo,
Forge the lid of many colors.

Louhi said: Who forges Sampo
Gets my daughter as his bride,
Gets the maiden of Pohjola,
Fairest daughter of the Northland.
