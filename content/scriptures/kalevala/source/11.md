---
title: "Kalevala Canto 11"
scripture: "kalevala"
chapter: 11
---

Vainamoinen, old and steadfast,
Found the words of power hidden,
On the tongue of dead Antero,
Giant sage beneath the ground.

Antero Vipunen slumbered,
Great in body, great in wisdom,
Trees upon his shoulders growing,
Forests on his mighty forehead.

Vainamoinen sought entrance boldly,
Down into the giant's body,
Through the mouth of Antero,
To the cavernous interior.

There he built a forge and smithy,
Hammered on the giant's vitals,
Till Antero Vipunen woke
And yielded up the words of magic.
