Gnostic readers interpret Mattathias's revolt as the awakening of the pneumatic consciousness that refuses to submit to the counterfeit authority of the Demiurge. His zeal for the law is transposed from external Torah observance to the inner law of gnosis — the direct knowledge of the divine that transcends all written codes and institutional structures. The killing of the apostate Jew at the altar symbolizes the annihilation of the psychic (soul-bound) self that collaborates with archontic power.

Mattathias's deathbed exhortation to remember the ancestors is read as a call to remember the pneumatic lineage — the chain of awakened beings who have transmitted gnosis across generations. This spiritual genealogy, rather than biological descent, constitutes the true Israel in Gnostic understanding.
