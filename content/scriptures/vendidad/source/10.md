---
title: "Vendidad Chapter 10"
scripture: "vendidad"
chapter: 10
---

**1** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! What are the words that shall be spoken to bring help to a man who is sick?

**2** Ahura Mazda answered: Thou shalt recite the Ahuna Vairya five times, and the Ashem Vohu three times.

**3** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! Shall I urge upon the godly man to give help to the faithful who is sick and needs treatment?

**4** Ahura Mazda answered: Thou shalt urge, O Zarathushtra! He who does not give help to the sick when he can shall be punished.

**5** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! Who is the physician that heals best?

**6** Ahura Mazda answered: He who heals with the Holy Word. He is the best of all healers, O Spitama Zarathushtra! He who heals with the Mathra Spenta is the most beneficial of all healers.

**7** For the Holy Word is the most powerful of all remedies. The next best healer is he who heals with the knife. The next best healer is he who heals with herbs and plants.

**8** He shall first heal the priest, then the warrior, then the husbandman. He shall heal the lord of the province for the value of a fine horse; the lord of the town for the value of a fine ox; the lord of the village for the value of a fine bull.

**9** He shall heal the wife of the master of a house for the value of a she-ass; and a child for the value of an ox of low value.

**10** If the healer, who heals with the Holy Word, shall heal the master of the house, or the lord of the village, or the lord of the town, or the lord of the province, he shall be paid according to the worth of the man he has healed.
