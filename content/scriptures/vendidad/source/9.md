---
title: "Vendidad Chapter 9"
scripture: "vendidad"
chapter: 9
---

**1** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! If worshippers of Mazda, walking, or running, or riding, or driving, come upon a corpse-burning fire, what shall they do?

**2** Ahura Mazda answered: They shall kill the man that burns the corpse. Surely they shall kill him. They shall take off the corpse from the fire. They shall quench the fire.

**3** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! If worshippers of Mazda come upon a house where a fire is burning, a pot boiling, and meat cooking with the flesh of a dead man, what shall they do?

**4** Ahura Mazda answered: They shall remove the pot, they shall remove the fire, they shall remove the dead matter, and the faithful shall make the fire blaze again in the proper place.

**5** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! Can the fire on which a corpse has been cooked be purified?

**6** Ahura Mazda answered: It cannot, O holy Zarathushtra. The fire that has cooked dead matter shall be quenched and shall not be kindled again.

**7** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! What is the penalty for the burning of a corpse?

**8** Ahura Mazda answered: It is a sin that admits of no atonement, O Spitama Zarathushtra! The man who burns a corpse shall be punished with a thousand stripes.

**9** The Fire of Ahura Mazda shall not be defiled with dead matter. The Fire is holy, the son of Ahura Mazda, and it is to be worshipped with reverence and kept in purity.
