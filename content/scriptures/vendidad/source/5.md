---
title: "Vendidad Chapter 5"
scripture: "vendidad"
chapter: 5
---

**1** How long shall the ground lie fallow where a man has been buried?

**2** Ahura Mazda answered: A year long shall the ground lie fallow where a man has been buried, O holy Zarathushtra!

**3** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! How long shall the ground lie fallow where a dog has been buried?

**4** Ahura Mazda answered: Half a year shall the ground lie fallow where a dog has been buried.

**5** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! Can the ground be tilled again where a man has been buried?

**6** Ahura Mazda answered: It can, O holy Zarathushtra! after the body has been there a year. The worshippers of Mazda shall not till it before that time, unless they desire to bring upon themselves the displeasure of Ahura Mazda.

**7** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! Is the Drug-Nasu from the dead carried to water, to fire, to the faithful?

**8** Ahura Mazda answered: She is; the Drug-Nasu falls upon them. From the dead she rushes to the living; from the living she rushes to the dead. She corrupts them all.

**9** Therefore the faithful shall expose the dead upon the mountain-tops, that the corpse-devouring creatures may dispose of them. He who buries a dead body shall be punished with stripes.

**10** Purity is for man, next to life, the greatest good, that purity which is procured by the law of Mazda to him who cleanses his own self with good thoughts, words, and deeds.
