---
title: "Vendidad Chapter 17"
scripture: "vendidad"
chapter: 17
---

**1** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! If a man cut off his hair or pare his nails, what shall he do with the hair and the nails?

**2** Ahura Mazda answered: He shall dig a hole in the earth, ten fingers deep, at a distance from the faithful, from the Fire of Ahura Mazda, from the water, from the plants, from the Baresman.

**3** He shall take the hair or the nails to that hole, and he shall say: "O Spenishta Mainyu! These are the hairs and these are the nails which I offer to thee. May they become as trees and grow in the paradise of Ahura Mazda."

**4** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! What happens to the hair and nails that are not consecrated with the proper prayers?

**5** Ahura Mazda answered: Every time the hair or nails are cut off, if they are not consecrated with the proper prayers, they become weapons in the hands of the Daevas.

**6** The Daevas shall make arrows of them, and spears, and swords, and bows. Therefore shall the faithful consecrate their hair and nails with the proper prayers.

**7** The righteous man shall say the Sraosho-Charana prayer, and the Ahuna Vairya, and the Ashem Vohu, when he cuts his hair or pares his nails.
