---
title: "Vendidad Chapter 14"
scripture: "vendidad"
chapter: 14
---

**1** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! If the faithful give bad food to a dog, what penalty shall they pay?

**2** Ahura Mazda answered: The penalty is the same as for giving bad food to a righteous man of high standing.

**3** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! If the faithful hit a dog with a blow that injures him, what penalty shall they pay?

**4** Ahura Mazda answered: The penalty is the same as for hitting a righteous man with a blow that injures him.

**5** He who kills a dog with young, or when she is pregnant, shall pay the penalty for it, and his soul shall suffer therefor at the Bridge of the Accountant.

**6** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! If there be in the house of a righteous man a mad dog, what shall the righteous man do?

**7** Ahura Mazda answered: He shall put a wooden shoe on its foot. If a mad dog bite a sheep or wound a man, the dog shall be held responsible.

**8** If the dog bites without provocation, the owner shall pay the damage. On the first offence, the dog shall be given a warning. On the second offence, the dog shall be confined.

**9** Let no one ill-treat or neglect a dog. He who neglects a dog is as guilty as one who neglects a child.
