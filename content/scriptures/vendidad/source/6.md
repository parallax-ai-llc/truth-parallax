---
title: "Vendidad Chapter 6"
scripture: "vendidad"
chapter: 6
---

**1** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! How long after the death of a man is the Drug-Nasu upon him?

**2** Ahura Mazda answered: Immediately upon death, O Spitama Zarathushtra! As soon as the soul has left the body, the Drug-Nasu comes and settles upon the dead, from the head to the very extremities.

**3** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! If it be summer, how long after death does the Drug-Nasu come?

**4** Ahura Mazda answered: As long as it takes to recite the prayer Ashem Vohu three times — so long after death the Drug-Nasu comes.

**5** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! If it be winter, how long after death does the Drug-Nasu come?

**6** Ahura Mazda answered: As long as it takes to recite the prayer Ashem Vohu three times — so long after death the Drug-Nasu comes.

**7** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! Can the Drug-Nasu be driven away?

**8** Ahura Mazda answered: She can, O Spitama Zarathushtra! When the Nasush-salars have come, they shall wash their hands with gomez, and then the Drug shall be driven off.

**9** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! What part of a man's body does the Drug-Nasu first attack?

**10** Ahura Mazda answered: She first seizes the nails, then proceeds to the extremities, then to the torso, and at last to the skull.

**11** Therefore shall the righteous man be purified with the Bareshnum, the greatest of all purifications.
