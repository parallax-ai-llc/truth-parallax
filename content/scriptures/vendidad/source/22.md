---
title: "Vendidad Chapter 22"
scripture: "vendidad"
chapter: 22
---

**1** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! To what place shall the faithful bring the dead body for purification?

**2** Ahura Mazda answered: To the highest summits, O Spitama Zarathushtra! where they know there are always corpse-eating dogs and corpse-eating birds.

**3** The faithful shall fasten the dead body to the Dakhma, by the feet and by the hair, that the corpse-eating dogs and the corpse-eating birds may not bring the bones to the water nor to the plants.

**4** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! If the bones of the dead have been exposed to the sun and the rain for a year, what shall be done with them?

**5** Ahura Mazda answered: They shall be gathered together and placed in an ossuary, O Spitama Zarathushtra!

**6** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! What is the word that is most powerful for the purification of the dead?

**7** Ahura Mazda answered: It is the Ahuna Vairya, O Spitama Zarathushtra! The Ahuna Vairya is the most powerful word. The Ashem Vohu is next in power. The Yenghe Hatam is the third in power.

**8** Through these sacred words the Drug-Nasu is driven from the dead body. Through these sacred words the faithful shall be purified, and the dead shall find rest.

**9** We worship Ahura Mazda. We worship the Amesha Spentas. We worship the entire righteous creation of Ahura Mazda. Ashem Vohu.
