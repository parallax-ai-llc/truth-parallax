---
title: "Vendidad Chapter 21"
scripture: "vendidad"
chapter: 21
---

**1** The Ahuna Vairya prayer: He who recites the Ahuna Vairya prayer, he who studies and meditates upon it — he is the best of men in Asha.

**2** O Zarathushtra! The Ahuna Vairya was spoken before the creation of the sky, before the creation of water, before the creation of earth, before the creation of cattle, before the creation of the plants, before the creation of Fire, the son of Ahura Mazda.

**3** It was spoken before the creation of the righteous man, before the creation of the Daevas, before the whole creation of Ahura Mazda.

**4** He who in this material world, O Spitama Zarathushtra! recites the Ahuna Vairya, he who studies it and meditates upon it, and chants it — as a part of the Yasna — four times:

**5** I will protect him, as much as a hundred horses, a thousand oxen, ten thousand lambs, against the Drug.

**6** The Ahuna Vairya is the most victorious of all words. The Ahuna Vairya is the most fiend-smiting of all words. The Ahuna Vairya is the most healing of all words.

**7** The Ahuna Vairya is the most victorious of all the Mathra Spenta. The first recital of the Ahuna Vairya gives the reciter as much merit as if he had chanted a hundred hymns.

**8** This Mathra, the Ahuna Vairya, is the foremost among all that is good. And the Religion of Zarathushtra, the Mazdayasnian, is the foremost of all Religions.
