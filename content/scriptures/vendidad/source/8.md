---
title: "Vendidad Chapter 8"
scripture: "vendidad"
chapter: 8
---

**1** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! If a man shall come upon a corpse in a stream of running water, what shall he do?

**2** Ahura Mazda answered: He shall take the dead body out of the water, O Spitama Zarathushtra! He shall lay it down on the dry ground; and he shall do so regardless of whether the corpse be that of a righteous man or of a wicked one.

**3** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! If he cannot take the dead body out of the water, what shall he do?

**4** Ahura Mazda answered: He shall go into the water and strive to take it out with both hands, as a man of Asha, a righteous man.

**5** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! If a man who touches a corpse shall afterwards touch the water, does the water become unclean?

**6** Ahura Mazda answered: It does, O Spitama Zarathushtra! All water that touches the dead body becomes unclean.

**7** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! How shall these waters be made clean?

**8** Ahura Mazda answered: They shall be purified with the Bareshnum. They shall stand still until the three following summers have passed, and then they shall be clean.

**9** The faithful shall dig a channel, and the waters shall flow again, purified and clean. Purity is for man the greatest good, that purity which the law of Mazda gives to those who cleanse themselves with good thoughts, good words, and good deeds.
