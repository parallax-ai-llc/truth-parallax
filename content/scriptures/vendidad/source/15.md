---
title: "Vendidad Chapter 15"
scripture: "vendidad"
chapter: 15
---

**1** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! What is the penalty when a man forces a righteous woman to commit an unrighteous act?

**2** Ahura Mazda answered: The penalty is a thousand stripes with the Aspahe-astra.

**3** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! What is the penalty when a man keeps a woman unlawfully?

**4** Ahura Mazda answered: The penalty is a thousand stripes with the Aspahe-astra, a thousand stripes with the Sraosho-charana.

**5** If a man has intercourse with a woman unlawfully and she bears a child, and if he denies the child, his sin is as great as murder.

**6** He who abandons a pregnant woman commits a Tanafuhr sin. He shall provide for her and for the child.

**7** She who brings forth a child in secret and destroys it commits a Tanafuhr sin. There is no atonement for this crime; it is a sin that leads to the House of the Lie.

**8** The faithful man shall marry a righteous woman and raise righteous children. He who raises a righteous family furthers the creation of Ahura Mazda.
