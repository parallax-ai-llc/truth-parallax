---
title: "Vendidad Chapter 20"
scripture: "vendidad"
chapter: 20
---

**1** Thrita was the first of healers, the first of the wise, the first of the happy, who drove back sickness to sickness, who drove back death to death, who first withstood the flooding of fever and the cutting of the knife.

**2** Thrita sought medicine against sickness, against death, against pain, against fever, against disease, against rottenness, against infection.

**3** And he found it, the most powerful of all medicines — the Airyaman prayer, the mighty, the holy. That is the most powerful of all remedies, that is the most healing of all healings.

**4** He who recites the Airyaman Ishyo prayer heals the body of the sick. He drives away the sickness, he drives away the fever, he drives away the death.

**5** The creation of the plants, too, is a healing. Ahura Mazda gave ten thousand healing plants, and among them the Gaokerena plant, the tree of all seeds.

**6** And Ahura Mazda said: I have given these plants as remedy against the ninety-nine thousand nine hundred and ninety-nine diseases that Angra Mainyu has created.

**7** And the healers shall use these plants to heal the faithful, and they shall drive away sickness from the righteous.
