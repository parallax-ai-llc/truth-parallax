---
title: "Vendidad Chapter 4"
scripture: "vendidad"
chapter: 4
---

**1** He who does not restore a thing lent to him when it is asked for back, steals the thing; he robs the man. So he does every day, every night, as long as he keeps in his house his neighbour's property, as though it were his own.

**2** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! How many are the contracts, O holy Ahura Mazda?

**3** Ahura Mazda answered: There are six sorts, O holy Zarathushtra! The first is the word-contract; the second is the hand-contract; the third is the contract to the amount of a sheep; the fourth is the contract to the amount of an ox; the fifth is the contract to the amount of a man; the sixth is the contract to the amount of a field.

**4** The breach of the word-contract is redeemed by three hundred stripes with the Aspahe-astra, three hundred stripes with the Sraosho-charana.

**5** The breach of the hand-contract is redeemed by six hundred stripes with the Aspahe-astra, six hundred stripes with the Sraosho-charana.

**6** The breach of the contract to the amount of a sheep is redeemed by seven hundred stripes with the Aspahe-astra, seven hundred stripes with the Sraosho-charana.

**7** The breach of the contract to the amount of an ox is redeemed by eight hundred stripes with the Aspahe-astra, eight hundred stripes with the Sraosho-charana.

**8** The breach of the contract to the amount of a man is redeemed by nine hundred stripes with the Aspahe-astra, nine hundred stripes with the Sraosho-charana.

**9** The breach of the contract to the amount of a field is redeemed by a thousand stripes with the Aspahe-astra, a thousand stripes with the Sraosho-charana.

**10** If a man rise up with a weapon in hand, it is an Agereptem. If he come upon a man with the weapon, it is an Avaoirishtem. If he smite a man with the weapon, it is an Aredush. Upon the fifth Aredush he becomes a Peshotanu.

**11** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! If a man smite another man with a weapon so that the blood comes, what is the atonement? For it I cleanse the faithful man, for it I heal the faithful man. The penalty is ninety stripes with the Aspahe-astra.
