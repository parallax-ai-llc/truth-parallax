---
title: "Vendidad Chapter 11"
scripture: "vendidad"
chapter: 11
---

**1** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! Can a woman who has given birth be purified?

**2** Ahura Mazda answered: She can, O Spitama Zarathushtra.

**3** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! How shall she be purified?

**4** Ahura Mazda answered: She shall wash her body three times with gomez, three times with water, and her clothes shall be washed and purified.

**5** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! How long shall she remain in the place of purification?

**6** Ahura Mazda answered: She shall remain there for twelve days, if she has given birth to a living child; she shall remain there for nine days, if the child be stillborn.

**7** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! What food shall be given to her?

**8** Ahura Mazda answered: She shall eat bread with meat and drink water mixed with ashes, for three days and three nights.

**9** After the purification, she shall wash her body with water and with gomez. Then she shall be clean and may return to her household duties.

**10** The woman is the joy of the house. Where the woman is righteous, there the house prospers. Where the woman is wise, there the children grow strong.
