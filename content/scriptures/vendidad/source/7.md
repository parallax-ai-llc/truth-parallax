---
title: "Vendidad Chapter 7"
scripture: "vendidad"
chapter: 7
---

**1** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! Is it true that thou, Ahura Mazda, dost send the waters from the sea Vouru-kasha down with the wind and with the clouds?

**2** That thou, Ahura Mazda, dost make them flow down to the corpses? That thou, Ahura Mazda, dost make them flow down to the Dakhmas? That thou, Ahura Mazda, dost make them flow down to the unclean remains?

**3** Ahura Mazda answered: It is so as thou hast said, O righteous Zarathushtra! I, Ahura Mazda, send the waters from the sea Vouru-kasha down with the wind and with the clouds.

**4** I make them flow down to the corpses; I make them flow down to the Dakhmas; I make them flow down to the unclean remains.

**5** I, Ahura Mazda, purify the water, I purify the earth, I purify the plants.

**6** For if I were not to purify the earth, the earth would become full of dead matter. For if I were not to purify the waters, the waters would become full of dead matter.

**7** I, Ahura Mazda, created the plants that grow from the earth, that they may purify the earth. I created the star Tishtrya, the brightest and most glorious, to purify the waters.

**8** Therefore, O Zarathushtra! shall the faithful purify the waters and the earth and the plants. Through their purification shall Asha increase, and through Asha shall the creation of Ahura Mazda be furthered.
