---
title: "Vendidad Chapter 12"
scripture: "vendidad"
chapter: 12
---

**1** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! How long shall mourning for the dead last?

**2** Ahura Mazda answered: Mourning for the father or the mother shall last thirty days; for the brother or the sister, for the son or the daughter, it shall last thirty days.

**3** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! How long shall mourning for the next of kin last?

**4** Ahura Mazda answered: Mourning for the next of kin shall last thirty days.

**5** During the days of mourning the faithful shall not eat meat, nor drink wine. They shall pray and offer sacrifice for the souls of the departed.

**6** He who mourns for the righteous dead with too great sorrow sins against Ahura Mazda; for he calls into question the wisdom of the Lord.

**7** The soul of the righteous man goes upward to the House of Song, to the abode of Ahura Mazda, to the abode of the Amesha Spentas, to the abode of the righteous ones.

**8** Therefore let the mourning be with moderation, and let the faithful remember that the righteous dead dwell in happiness with Ahura Mazda.
