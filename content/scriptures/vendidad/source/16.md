---
title: "Vendidad Chapter 16"
scripture: "vendidad"
chapter: 16
---

**1** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! If a woman in her sickness be touched by a faithful man, how shall he be purified?

**2** Ahura Mazda answered: He shall wash his body three times with gomez, three times with water. He shall be clean after this purification.

**3** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! Shall the woman during her sickness be separated from the household?

**4** Ahura Mazda answered: She shall be separated. She shall sit in a place apart. She shall not touch the Fire of Ahura Mazda, nor the water, nor the earth, nor the plants.

**5** She shall eat food from metal vessels, and she shall not eat meat during those days.

**6** After her purification, she shall wash her body and her clothes, and she shall be clean.

**7** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! Can the clothes that she wore during her sickness be purified?

**8** Ahura Mazda answered: They can, O Spitama Zarathushtra! They shall be washed three times with gomez, three times with water, and they shall be exposed to the light of the sun for three months.
