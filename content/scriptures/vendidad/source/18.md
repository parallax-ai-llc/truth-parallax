---
title: "Vendidad Chapter 18"
scripture: "vendidad"
chapter: 18
---

**1** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! Who is the Righteous One among men?

**2** Ahura Mazda answered: It is he, O Spitama Zarathushtra, who, after having been taught the Religion of Mazda, teaches it to others and converts them.

**3** He who recites the word of the Religion and teaches it, and he who works righteousness and furthers the creation of Ahura Mazda — these are the best of men.

**4** He who tells a lie knowingly is as guilty as he who commits murder. The lie destroys the world, the lie oppresses the righteous.

**5** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! What is the most powerful spell to destroy the Daevas?

**6** Ahura Mazda answered: The Ahuna Vairya, O Spitama Zarathushtra! He who recites it once destroys the power of the Daevas. He who recites it thrice utterly overwhelms them.

**7** O Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! What is the most powerful way to destroy the Drug?

**8** Ahura Mazda answered: The confession of the Mazdayasnian Faith, the practice of Asha, the rejection of evil thoughts, evil words, and evil deeds.

**9** He who walks in Asha, who speaks truth, who acts in justice — he is the most righteous of men, the strongest foe of the Daevas.
