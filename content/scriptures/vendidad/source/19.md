---
title: "Vendidad Chapter 19"
scripture: "vendidad"
chapter: 19
---

**1** Zarathushtra asked Ahura Mazda: O Ahura Mazda, most beneficent Spirit, Maker of the material world, thou Holy One! Who was he who first of the healthful, the wise, the happy, the wealthy, the glorious, the strong?

**2** Ahura Mazda answered: Yima the fair, O Spitama Zarathushtra.

**3** O Maker of the material world! What became of Yima?

**4** Ahura Mazda answered: He began to find delight in words of falsehood and untruth. Then the Glory departed from him, in the shape of a bird.

**5** When the Glory had departed from Yima, he was smitten with grief. He wandered about the earth in concealment and sorrow, a prey to the Drug.

**6** Then the serpent Dahaka seized upon the sovereignty that Yima had held. The serpent Dahaka was of the three mouths, the three heads, the six eyes, who had a thousand senses, that most powerful Drug.

**7** But the hero Thraetaona struck the serpent Dahaka. He smote him upon the skull, upon the shoulders, upon the heart. But no blood flowed from his wounds; only snakes and lizards and frogs and toads came forth.

**8** Then Thraetaona feared that if he cut open the body, the whole world would be filled with serpents. So he bound the serpent Dahaka in chains on Mount Demavand.

**9** There shall Dahaka lie bound until the end of the world, when Keresaspa shall slay him.

**10** And the Glory that had departed from Yima was seized by Mithra, and by Thraetaona, and by the righteous ones, and it shall remain with them until the coming of the Saoshyant.

**11** When the Saoshyant shall come, then shall the dead rise. Then shall the living become immortal. Then shall Asha triumph over the Drug. Then shall Ahura Mazda reign supreme.

**12** At that time Angra Mainyu shall be destroyed, utterly and forever. The Drug shall vanish. The Lie shall perish. And the whole creation of Ahura Mazda shall be purified in the molten metal.

**13** Then shall the world be made perfect and immortal, free from old age, from death, from corruption, living forever, growing forever, ruling at will. The dead shall rise; the living shall become immortal; and creation shall flourish in the deathless state.
