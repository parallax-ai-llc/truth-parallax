## Overview

Tablet 7, "The Fifty Names of Marduk," examined through the lens of theological analysis, reveals dimensions of the text that are illuminated by attention to religious meaning, divine nature, and soteriological significance. This tablet of the Enuma Elish addresses themes that have attracted sustained theological attention.

Theologians approach this tablet with particular interest in its contribution to understanding the Enuma Elish as a whole. The content of this section — dealing with the fifty names of marduk — raises important questions that doctrinal and systematic methods are well suited to address.

Within the broader framework of the Enuma Elish, this tablet occupies a significant position. Its themes connect to wider patterns in ancient Mesopotamian religious and literary tradition, while also speaking to concerns that transcend any single cultural context.
