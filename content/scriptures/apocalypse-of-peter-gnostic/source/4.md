---
title: "Apocalypse of Peter (Gnostic) Section 4"
scripture: "apocalypse-of-peter-gnostic"
chapter: 4
---

**1** And Peter was filled with spirit, and he spoke with boldness, saying: The Savior has shown me what I must come to understand, and what was revealed is the depth of the great mystery.

**2** For the crucifixion that took place was not the crucifixion of truth, but of ignorance, for the one they thought they were destroying is beyond all destruction.

**3** They did not nail the living one to the tree; they nailed only his likeness. For the substitute was put to shame before them, while the living one stood apart, laughing at their blindness.

**4** For the mortal cannot grasp the immortal, nor can the creature of the archons comprehend the light which is above all heavens and all powers.

**5** He said to me: Peter, you must be strengthened through these revelations. Do not share them with the children of this age, who neither understand nor have ears to hear.

**6** For there shall come among them those who will lead astray the multitudes, teaching error in my name. They will say: We are servants of his word, but in truth they serve only the dead God.

**7** And they will create churches and altars, but they will be altars of the dead. And the name of the dead man will they invoke over the offerings of the dead, not understanding that the living one stands apart.

**8** But the little ones, those to whom it is given to know these things, they shall find rest from their labors and shall know the imperishable Father and the truth which cannot be defiled.

**9** For the Savior is not he who hangs upon the tree, but he who was released from all suffering, who stands in joy, who laughs at the blindness of the world.

**10** And when Peter had received these revelations, he kept them in his heart, and he went forth to proclaim the living word to those who had ears to hear, the children of the immovable race.

**11** These are the mysteries which were given to Peter, the apocalypse of the truth, that those who seek may find, and those who knock may enter the door of the imperishable light.
