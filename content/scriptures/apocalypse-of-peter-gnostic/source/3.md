---
title: "Apocalypse of Peter (Gnostic) Section 3"
scripture: "apocalypse-of-peter-gnostic"
chapter: 3
---

**1** So then the one susceptible to suffering shall come, since the body is the substitute. But what they released was my incorporeal body.

**2** But I am the intellectual spirit filled with radiant light. He whom you saw coming to me is our intellectual pleroma, which unites the perfect light with my holy spirit.

**3** These things, then, which you saw you shall present to those of another race who are not of this age. For there will be no honor in any man who is not immortal, but only in those who were chosen from an immortal substance, which has shown that it is able to contain him who gives his abundance.

**4** Therefore I said: Every one who has, it will be given to him, and he will have plenty. But he who has not—that is, the man of this place, who is completely dead, who is removed from the planting of the tree of good—that which is from him will be taken from him and added to the one who is.

**5** You therefore, be courageous and do not fear at all. For I shall be with you in order that none of your enemies may prevail over you. Peace be to you. Be strong!

**6** When he had said these things, he came to himself. And Peter came to himself and saw that the Savior had departed from him.

**7** And there were others standing near the temple who were priests, saying blasphemous words about him, that he was begotten in sin and that he was not from God.

**8** But Peter, standing up, was filled with courage and the knowledge of the Savior. And he went forth from the temple to the heights and to the gardens.

**9** And he perceived that the blind ones, in their opinions, were deceived by the archons of fate and the principalities and the powers. And he rejoiced in the revelation that had been given to him.

**10** For he understood that the truth is not in the form of a bodily nature, nor does it derive from the creations of this world, but from the imperishable realm of the Great Light.
