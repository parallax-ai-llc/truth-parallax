---
title: "Rig Veda - Chapter 8"
scripture: "rig-veda"
chapter: 8
---

**1** Come, Indra, and delight thee with the juice at all the Soma feasts, protector, mighty in thy strength.

**2** To thee the bounteous, the strong, the mighty one, we sing our hymns of praise, O Indra, slayer of Vritra.

**3** Indra, whose right hand wields the bolt, we worship. He, foremost among warriors, bestows upon his worshippers all blessings.

**4** Thou art the lord of Soma juice, O Indra. Thou art the lord of strength. No one is mightier than thou.

**5** Let us invoke the lord of hosts, the chieftain Indra, most heroic. He who listens and gives aid in battle, who slays the foe.

**6** The Maruts, the storm gods, the sons of Rudra, come rushing with their shining spears through the sky, bringing rain and power.

**7** The Maruts drive the clouds before them; they shake the mountains with their might. They are the companions of Indra in his victories.

**8** With their golden ornaments, with their shining weapons, the Maruts travel through the sky. They bring the rain, they bring the storm.

**9** Self-luminous are the Maruts, mighty in their valour. They are the lords of the thunderstorm, and no enemy can withstand them.

**10** O Maruts, come from heaven, from the air, from the near regions of the sky. Sit upon the grass and drink the Soma.

**11** The Ashvins, the divine physicians, come riding in their golden chariot at the break of dawn. They bring healing and renewal to all.

**12** O Ashvins, lords of lustre, come to us with your healing powers. Grant us health and vigour, grant us life and prosperity.
