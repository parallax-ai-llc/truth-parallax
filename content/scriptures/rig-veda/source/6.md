---
title: "Rig Veda - Chapter 6"
scripture: "rig-veda"
chapter: 6
---

**1** I praise Agni, the chosen Priest, God, minister of sacrifice, the hotar, lavishest of wealth.

**2** Worthy is Agni to be praised by living as by ancient seers. He shall bring hitherward the gods.

**3** Through Agni man obtains wealth, yea, plenty waxing day by day, most rich in heroes, glorious.

**4** Agni, the perfect sacrifice which thou encompassest about, verily goes to the gods.

**5** Agni, the bright, the holy one, before the dawn, burning, shall approach each morning with the light of day, as the sun approaches from the east.

**6** We approach thee, O Agni, day by day, O lord of tribes, bringer of light, with prayer, bearing our homage unto thee.

**7** O Parjanya, lord of rain, thou art like a father to us. Grant us rain, send us the waters from heaven. Refresh the earth, refresh all creatures.

**8** The bull roars and thunders, impregnating the earth with his seed of rain. The trees bend before his might, and the clouds pour forth their burden.

**9** He strikes the evil-doers with his thunderbolt, and all beings tremble before him. Even the sinless flee before the mighty Parjanya.

**10** May Parjanya send us rain to nourish the grass, to fill the rivers, to refresh the parched earth. May he be gracious unto us.

**11** The herbs and plants spring up under his rain. All life is nourished by his waters. May the rain of Parjanya bring us abundance.

**12** Thunder and roar, O Parjanya; cast thy waters down. Drive forward thy chariot full of water. Pour forth the hollowed bucket downward. Let the heights and hollows be made level.
