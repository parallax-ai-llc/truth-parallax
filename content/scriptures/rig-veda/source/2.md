---
title: "Rig Veda - Chapter 2"
scripture: "rig-veda"
chapter: 2
---

**1** Come, Vayu, that most sweet draught is prepared for thee. Drink of the Soma, hear our call. Come hither.

**2** O Vayu, the singers hymn thee with their latest hymn. They who know the days sing to thee with their songs.

**3** O Vayu, thy satisfying stream goes to the worshipper, wide as the path of the Soma on its way to the sieve.

**4** To Indra and Vayu these Soma juices, pressed and purified, are offered. Come hither with your chariot.

**5** Vayu and Indra, these Soma juices are prepared — come with your celestial favours to the feast.

**6** Mitra I call, of holy strength, and Varuna the foe-destroyer. They bestow grace through their powers.

**7** By Truth, Mitra and Varuna, lovers of Truth, having attained to their great power, were born in strength.

**8** The kings Mitra and Varuna, the great, the powerful, give us efficacious shelter and manifold protection.

**9** Indra, bring us wealth mighty and unsubdued, shining, conquering all, glorious.

**10** With a new hymn let us call to that Indra, the most generous giver, who rules alone with power over all treasures.

**11** Never failing in battle, conqueror of all, never vanquished — that Indra we invoke in fight after fight. May he, our nearest friend, be good to us.

**12** None rivals him, none equals him. No one has reached his might. Indra excels all beings in power and in strength.
