---
title: "Yasna 12 – The Mazdayasnian Creed"
chapter: 12
---

**1.** I curse the Daevas. I declare myself a Mazda-worshipper, a supporter of Zarathushtra, hostile to the Daevas, fond of Ahura's teaching, a praiser of the Amesha Spentas, a worshipper of the Amesha Spentas.

**2.** I ascribe all good to Ahura Mazda, the holy, the resplendent, the glorious, and whatever is of the best; whose is the Ox, whose is Asha, whose are the stars, in whose light the glorious beings and objects are clothed.

**3.** I choose the good Spenta Armaiti. May she be mine. I renounce the theft and robbery of the Ox, and the damage and plunder of the Mazdayasnian communities.

**4.** I vow to give freedom of movement and freedom of life to those righteous beings who dwell upon this earth with their cattle. With reverence for Asha and with offerings, I vow this: I shall nevermore damage or plunder the Mazdayasnian settlements, even if I have to risk life and limb.

**5.** I reject the authority of the Daevas, the wicked, the evil-ruling, the maleficent, the most Drug-like of beings, the foulest of beings, the most damaging of beings.

**6.** I reject the Daevas and their comrades, I reject the sorcerers and their comrades; I reject any who harm beings. I reject them with my thoughts, words, and deeds. I reject them with my spirit.

**7.** Even as Zarathushtra rejected the Daevas at all times and places, so do I reject the Daevas.

**8.** As a Mazda-worshipper of Zarathushtra's order, so do I declare myself. I praise the well-thought, well-spoken, and well-performed thoughts, words, and deeds.

**9.** I embrace the good Mazdayasnian Religion, which overthrows the yoke of the Daevas, which teaches the precepts of Ahura, of Zarathushtra — the Religion of Ahura.

**10.** To Ahura Mazda I ascribe all good. This is the creed of the Mazdayasnian Religion.
