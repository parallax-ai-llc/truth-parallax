---
title: "Yasna 20"
chapter: 20
---

**1.** We sacrifice unto the bountiful Amesha Spentas, the shining ones, the lordly ones, the good, the powerful.

**2.** We sacrifice unto Vohu Manah, the Good Mind, who presides over the beneficent animals.

**3.** We sacrifice unto Asha Vahishta, the Best Righteousness, who presides over Fire.

**4.** We sacrifice unto Khshathra Vairya, the Desired Dominion, who presides over metals.

**5.** We sacrifice unto Spenta Armaiti, Holy Devotion, who presides over the Earth.

**6.** We sacrifice unto Haurvatat, Wholeness, who presides over the Waters.

**7.** We sacrifice unto Ameretat, Immortality, who presides over the Plants.
