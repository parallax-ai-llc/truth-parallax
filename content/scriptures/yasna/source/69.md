---
title: "Yasna 69"
chapter: 69
---

**1.** We worship the Ahuna Vairya and the Ashem Vohu and the Yenghe Hatam. These are the three holiest prayers.

**2.** We worship the Staota Yesnya with its completeness. We worship the Gathas of Zarathushtra.

**3.** We worship the entire Mazdayasnian Religion, the law against the Daevas, the Zarathushtrian law.
