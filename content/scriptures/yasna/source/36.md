---
title: "Yasna 36 – Yasna Haptanghaiti"
chapter: 36
---

**1.** We praise the well-thought thoughts, the well-spoken words, and the well-done deeds.

**2.** These deeds of ours — the offerings and the Zaothras — we place before Ahura Mazda and the Amesha Spentas, that they may be acceptable and well-pleasing.

**3.** We worship the souls of the beneficent animals. We worship the souls of the righteous ones. We worship the Fravashis of the faithful.

**4.** Through this worship and through this offering may we come near unto the Best Existence, unto the throne of Ahura Mazda, unto the assembly of the righteous.

**5.** We worship the entire creation of the righteous Spirit, the bountiful, which is Asha-owning and Mazda-made.
