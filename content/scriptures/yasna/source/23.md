---
title: "Yasna 23"
chapter: 23
---

**1.** I desire for this offering to approach the Fravashis of the righteous, the good, the strong, the beneficent.

**2.** For the Fravashis of the righteous I offer these Zaothras, these offerings, these dedications, these offerings with all their completeness and exactness.

**3.** We worship the Fravashis of the righteous who have been, who are, and who shall be. We worship them in all lands and in all times.

**4.** May the Fravashis of the righteous come to our aid. May they protect us from the Evil One and from all the works of the Lie.
