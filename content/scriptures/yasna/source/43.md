---
title: "Yasna 43 – Ushtavaiti Gatha"
chapter: 43
---

**1.** Bliss be to the man who seeks the Right and follows Good Thought. As the holy one I recognized thee, O Mazda Ahura, when thy Spirit came to me and taught me the way.

**2.** This I ask Thee, tell me truly, Ahura: who is the Father of the Right? Who determined the path of the sun and the stars? Who is it by whom the moon waxes and wanes? This and more I desire to know.

**3.** Through Good Thought I approach Thee, O Mazda. Through the Right I beseech Thee. Grant me the blessings of both worlds, the bodily and the spiritual.

**4.** He who advances Righteousness with full dedication, to him shall be given the rewards of the Best Existence. So hath Ahura Mazda ordained through his Dominion.

**5.** Now will I proclaim to those who will hear: Hear with your ears the best things, look upon them with clear-seeing thought. Each man for himself, before the great consummation, must choose between the two paths.
