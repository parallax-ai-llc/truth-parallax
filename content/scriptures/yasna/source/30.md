---
title: "Yasna 30 – Ahunavaiti Gatha"
chapter: 30
---

**1.** This is the Gatha Ahunavaiti, Yasna 30, the words of Zarathushtra spoken in the presence of Ahura Mazda, concerning the Right and the Good Mind and the Dominion.

**2.** Through the Right, O Mazda, shall the righteous man prosper. Through Good Thought shall the faithful man find the path. Through the Dominion shall the meek be protected.

**3.** I who would serve Thee, O Ahura Mazda, with all my heart and all my mind — grant me the blessing of the Right, that I may teach the nations the way of Righteousness.

**4.** The Liar and the Righteous shall be judged at the last. The Liar shall fall into the House of the Lie, and the Righteous shall cross to the Best Existence.

**5.** May the Right be our guide, may Good Thought be our counsel, may the Dominion be our shield. So shall we prevail against the Lie and advance the cause of Ahura Mazda.
