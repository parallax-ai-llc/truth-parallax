---
title: "Yasna 40 – Yasna Haptanghaiti"
chapter: 40
---

**1.** We worship Ahura Mazda. We worship the Amesha Spentas. We worship the entire creation of Ahura Mazda.

**2.** And now we worship the master of the house, and the lord of the community, and the lord of the province, and the righteous king who upholds the Religion.

**3.** We worship the man whose good thoughts and good words and good deeds are an offering to Ahura Mazda.

**4.** We worship the woman whose faithfulness and devotion are a blessing upon her household and her community.

**5.** May the blessings of Ahura Mazda be upon all the faithful, upon the men and the women, upon the masters and the servants, upon the priests and the warriors and the farmers.
