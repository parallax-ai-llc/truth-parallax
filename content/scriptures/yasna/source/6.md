---
title: "Yasna 6 – Praise to the Luminaries"
chapter: 6
---

**1.** I will praise the stars, moon, and sun, with the Baresman spread before me. I will praise the good and mighty luminaries of Ahura Mazda.

**2.** I will praise Tishtar, the brightest and most glorious of stars. I will praise Sataves and Vanand and Haftoring, the chieftains of the four quarters.

**3.** I will praise the sun, the swift-horsed, the radiant, the immortal, which first among the creations of the Spirit brings light and heat to the world.

**4.** I will praise the moon, whose light is gathered from the sun, and which waxes and wanes, marking the months and seasons.

**5.** I will praise the whole firmament, the fortress of the good creation against the powers of darkness. May the luminaries guard us against the Evil One.
