---
title: "Yasna 3 – The Dedication of the Offering"
chapter: 3
---

**1.** With this offering I dedicate and complete my Yasna to Ahura Mazda. I offer unto the fire of Ahura Mazda, the great, the well-tended, the appointed. I offer these Zaothras to Ahura Mazda.

**2.** And I offer to the sacred waters that flow, and to the plants that grow, and to the wholesome, rich-in-pasture cattle, and to the earth and the sky.

**3.** I offer to the holy wind that works on high, to the stars, moon, and sun that move in their courses, and to the endless Lights.

**4.** I offer to all the holy Yazads, celestial and terrestrial, and to the Fravashis of the righteous, and to the souls of the righteous that have departed.

**5.** And I offer this Yasna with completeness and with holiness, offering the flesh and the Haoma, the Myazda, and the Zaothra.

**6.** Let our offering rise as sweet incense before the throne of Ahura Mazda. Let it be heard and received in the realm of the holy ones.
