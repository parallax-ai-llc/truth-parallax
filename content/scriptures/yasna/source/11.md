---
title: "Yasna 11 – Praise to Haoma (Conclusion)"
chapter: 11
---

**1.** Let the Haoma-drinking man be blessed, for he is righteous. Let him obtain the blessings of Ahura Mazda, of the Amesha Spentas, and of all the Yazads.

**2.** Haoma, I call thee with my prayers to the offering. Come to me with thy blessings. Come to me with thy healing.

**3.** The pressing of the Haoma is an act of worship. The drinking of the Haoma is an act of devotion. Through the Haoma the faithful man draws near to Ahura Mazda.

**4.** Haoma has ten thousand blessings. He is the bringer of health, the giver of strength, the protector of the righteous.

**5.** We praise thee, O Haoma, and we offer thee the sacred Zaothra. May thy blessings be upon us and upon all the righteous.

**6.** And we praise all the Haoma plants that grow upon the mountains, golden-hued and sacred, the creation of Ahura Mazda, the remedy for all ills.
