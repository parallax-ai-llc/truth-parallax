---
title: "Yasna 15"
chapter: 15
---

**1.** I will praise the sacrifice and the prayer, the Yasna and the Vahmya, the strength and the vigor, of the sacrificing priest and of the righteous man.

**2.** I will praise the body of the Kine and its soul; I will praise the soul of the righteous man and woman. I will praise the souls of the righteous that have been, that are, and that shall be.

**3.** I will praise the good men and the good women of all time, of every land and every nation, who practice good thought and good word and good deed.

**4.** I will praise the Mathra Spenta, the law against the Daevas, the Zarathushtrian law, the long-of-tradition, the Asha-owning.
