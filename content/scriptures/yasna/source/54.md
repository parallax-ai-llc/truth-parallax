---
title: "Yasna 54"
chapter: 54
---

**1.** May the Airyema Ishyo come to the help of the men and women of Zarathushtra, to the help of the Good Mind. Through Righteousness may the faithful man earn the reward that he desires.

**2.** I pray for the prize of Asha, which is desired by Ahura Mazda. May Asha grant the best of all.

**3.** The Airyema Ishyo prayer is the most healing of all prayers. Through it the dead shall rise at the Renovation. Through it all disease shall be overcome.
