---
title: "Yasna 35 – Yasna Haptanghaiti"
chapter: 35
---

**1.** We worship Ahura Mazda, who made the Ox and the Waters, who made the Plants and the Light, who made the Earth and all things good.

**2.** We worship him with good thoughts, with good words, with good deeds. We worship him with the Zaothras and with the Baresman.

**3.** We worship the Amesha Spentas who preside over the good creation: the Good Mind, the Best Right, the Desired Dominion, the Holy Devotion, the Wholeness, and the Immortality.

**4.** We worship these with our body, with our breath, and with our soul. We worship these with our offering and with our praise.

**5.** And we worship all the good, holy, righteous things of the creation, those of Ahura Mazda, those which have Asha in them.
