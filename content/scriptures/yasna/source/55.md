---
title: "Yasna 55"
chapter: 55
---

**1.** We worship the Gathas. We worship the Staota Yesnya. We worship Zarathushtra, the lord of Righteousness.

**2.** We worship the Ahuna Vairya and the Ashem Vohu and the Airyema Ishyo. These are the three mightiest prayers.

**3.** Through these prayers the demons are confounded, the sick are healed, and the Renovation is advanced.
