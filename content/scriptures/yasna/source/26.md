---
title: "Yasna 26 – To the Fravashis"
chapter: 26
---

**1.** We worship the Fravashis of the righteous, the good, the powerful, the overwhelming. We worship the Fravashis of Gayomard, the first of men. We worship the Fravashis of Zarathushtra Spitama.

**2.** We worship the Fravashis of Kavi Vishtaspa, and of Isat-vastra, and of the family of Zarathushtra, and of all the righteous.

**3.** We worship the Fravashis of the righteous women, of Hutaosa, and of all the holy women who have upheld the Religion.

**4.** We worship the Fravashis of all times and all lands, who fight against the Lie and advance the cause of Righteousness.

**5.** May the good, strong Fravashis of the righteous come to our help. May they protect us in this world and guide us in the next.
