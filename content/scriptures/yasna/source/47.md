---
title: "Yasna 47 – Spenta Mainyu Gatha"
chapter: 47
---

**1.** For the holy Spirit, the Best, Mazda Ahura clothes with the hardest stones of heaven those who are in Good Thought and in deed and confession united with the Right.

**2.** By this most holy Spirit and by the Fire, through the Right and Good Thought, Thou givest the twofold promise of Welfare and Immortality to the righteous.

**3.** Of this most holy Spirit the father is Ahura Mazda, and Good Thought is his companion. Armaiti, the worker of good, is their offspring, and the all-seeing Lord is not to be deceived.

**4.** By this most holy Spirit hast Thou declared those things which are best. Through the divine Wisdom, through the knowledge of Right and the Dominion of Good Thought, Thou dost ordain the destiny of all things.
