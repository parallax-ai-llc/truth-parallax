---
title: "Yasna 4 – The Offering with Homage"
chapter: 4
---

**1.** And I will worship thee with my offering, O Ahura Mazda! I will complete my Yasna with my offering. These good offerings, these offerings of Righteousness, these offerings given with the sacred word.

**2.** I will worship the waters and the lands, and these the Zaothra offerings, the body of the Kine, and its soul. I will worship thee with the milk and the Haoma.

**3.** All these I will worship, and I will complete my Yasna with them. All these I will worship together with these that are the Fravashis of the righteous ones.

**4.** And I will worship with the Frasasti, the memorized recital of Asha, by the sacrificer's body and word, and with the best gifts of this my earthly life.

**5.** We worship the good mind of Ahura Mazda. We worship the faith of the good Religion. We worship the lordship and rule of Ahura Mazda.

**6.** We dedicate these offerings to thee, O Ahura Mazda, who art the greatest of the great, the best of the best, the most righteous of the righteous.
