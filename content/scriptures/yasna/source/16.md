---
title: "Yasna 16"
chapter: 16
---

**1.** We worship Ahura Mazda, the lord of Righteousness. We worship the Amesha Spentas, the lords of the good creation.

**2.** We worship the Gathas of Zarathushtra, the most righteous, the most sacred, the most efficacious words.

**3.** We worship the Staota Yesnya, with its completeness and its perfection. We worship these with our offering and our praise.

**4.** May this Yasna be complete and acceptable. May the merit of this service accrue to the faithful and to the good creation of Ahura Mazda.
