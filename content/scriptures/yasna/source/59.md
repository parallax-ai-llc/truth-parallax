---
title: "Yasna 59"
chapter: 59
---

**1.** We worship the good, the strong, the beneficent Fravashis of the righteous, who are mighty and overwhelming.

**2.** They are the helpers of Ahura Mazda. They sustain the sky above and the earth below. They cause the waters to flow and the plants to grow.

**3.** Had not the Fravashis of the righteous given their help, the creation could not have withstood the assault of the Destructive Spirit.

**4.** We worship them with our best offerings, with our best thoughts, with our best words, with our best deeds.
