---
title: "Yasna 1 – The Invocation"
chapter: 1
---

**1.** I announce and I will complete my Yasna to Ahura Mazda, the Creator, the radiant, the glorious, the greatest and the best, the most beautiful, the most firm, the wisest, of most perfect form, the highest in Righteousness.

**2.** I announce and I will complete my Yasna to the Good Mind, to Righteousness the Best, to the Sovereignty which is the desired, to Devotion the bountiful, to Salvation and to Immortality.

**3.** I announce and I will complete my Yasna to the Maker Ahura Mazda, to Mithra of the wide pastures, to Sraosha the righteous, to Rashnu the just, to the Fravashis of the faithful.

**4.** I announce and I will complete my Yasna to the five watches of the day and night, to Hawan, to Rapithwin, to Uzerin, to Aiwisruthrem, to Ushahin.

**5.** I announce and I will complete my Yasna to the yearly festivals, the Gahanbars, which are lords of Righteousness: to Maidyozarem, to Maidyoshahem, to Paitishahem, to Ayathrem, to Maidyarem, to Hamaspathmaidyem.

**6.** I announce and I will complete my Yasna to all the lords of Righteousness, the thirty-three lords who are nearest to the times of the Hawan.

**7.** I announce and I will complete my Yasna to thee, the Fire, O son of Ahura Mazda, together with all the fires. I announce and I will complete my Yasna to the good waters, to all the waters made by Mazda, to all the plants made by Mazda.

**8.** I announce and I will complete my Yasna to the Mathra Spenta, the holy, the effective, the Righteous, the law against the Daevas, the Zarathushtrian law.

**9.** I announce and I will complete my Yasna to Mount Ushi-darena, made by Mazda, the seat of holy happiness, and to all the mountains made by Mazda, shining with holiness, to the Royal Glory, to the Glory unconsumed.
