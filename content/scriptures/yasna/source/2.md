---
title: "Yasna 2 – The Offering"
chapter: 2
---

**1.** I desire to approach with my offering the lords of the houses, the lords of the villages, the lords of the communities, the lords of the provinces, and the Zarathushtrotema, the lord of the ritual.

**2.** I desire to approach with my offering the good, strong, beneficent Fravashis of the righteous, who are heroic and victorious.

**3.** I desire to approach with my offering the Haoma and the libations. I desire to approach with my offering the sacred Zaothra and the Baresman.

**4.** I desire to approach with my offering the sacred meat and the sacred bread, and the Haoma mixed with milk, the holy offering for the service of Ahura Mazda.

**5.** We offer these offerings with righteousness, with good thought, and with good word. We offer them to Ahura Mazda, to the Amesha Spentas, and to all the Yazads.

**6.** May these offerings be acceptable. May they bring blessing upon the faithful who offer them, and upon all the good creation of Ahura Mazda.

**7.** With this offering I desire to approach all the lords of the ritual, all the good Yazads, celestial and terrestrial, who are worthy of worship and worthy of prayer, because of their Righteousness.
