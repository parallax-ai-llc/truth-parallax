---
title: "Yasna 61"
chapter: 61
---

**1.** We worship the Ahuna Vairya, the prayer that is the mightiest of all. We worship the Ashem Vohu, the prayer that is the most beautiful of all.

**2.** We worship the bountiful Gathas, the words of Zarathushtra spoken in the presence of Ahura Mazda.

**3.** We worship the entire Yasna, with all its prayers and offerings and rituals, with all its completeness and perfection.

**4.** May this worship be acceptable to Ahura Mazda and bring blessing upon all the faithful.
