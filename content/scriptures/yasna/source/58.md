---
title: "Yasna 58"
chapter: 58
---

**1.** We worship the Fravashis of the righteous. We worship the good, the strong, the beneficent Fravashis.

**2.** We worship the Fravashis of the first teachers, of Zarathushtra, of the righteous kings, of all the faithful of all times.

**3.** The Fravashis chose to enter the material world to fight against the Lie. Through their power the creation of Ahura Mazda is sustained.

**4.** May the Fravashis come to our aid. May they protect the good creation. May they advance the cause of Righteousness.
