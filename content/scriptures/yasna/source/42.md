---
title: "Yasna 42 – Yasna Haptanghaiti"
chapter: 42
---

**1.** We worship the good mind, Vohu Manah, who dwells in the minds of the righteous and guides them in the path of Asha.

**2.** We worship the bountiful Devotion, the holy Armaiti, who counsels the faithful and keeps them firm in the Religion.

**3.** We worship the sacred Fire of Ahura Mazda, the son of Ahura Mazda, which purifies and protects.

**4.** We worship with the Yasna Haptanghaiti, the seven-chaptered Yasna, which is the oldest prayer of the Mazdayasnian Religion next to the Gathas themselves.

**5.** May this prayer be complete and acceptable. May the merit of this prayer accrue to the faithful and to the good creation.
