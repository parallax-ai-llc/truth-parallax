---
title: "Yasna 38 – Yasna Haptanghaiti"
chapter: 38
---

**1.** We worship this Earth, which bears us, together with the Women. We worship those Women who are thine, O Ahura, who belong to the Good Mind.

**2.** We worship the good, holy, righteous Daena. We worship the Religion of Mazda-worship. We worship the bond between mother and child.

**3.** We worship the waters, the good waters, the Mazda-made waters, the Asha-owning waters. We worship the plants and all growing things.

**4.** And we worship the souls of the righteous, those who have walked in the path of Asha, and whose Fravashis are mighty and victorious.

**5.** Let the earth and the waters and the plants and the righteous souls rejoice in our worship and in our offering.
