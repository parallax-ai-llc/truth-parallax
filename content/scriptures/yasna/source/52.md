---
title: "Yasna 52 – Prayer for Blessings"
chapter: 52
---

**1.** I pray for the best life, the life of Righteousness. I pray that the righteous man may be blessed in body and in soul, in this world and in the next.

**2.** I pray for the Good Mind, that it may dwell in my heart. I pray for the Right, that it may guide my steps. I pray for the Dominion, that it may protect the meek.

**3.** I pray for Devotion, that it may strengthen my will. I pray for Wholeness, that it may heal my body. I pray for Immortality, that it may preserve my soul.

**4.** May the blessings of Ahura Mazda be upon all who seek the Right. May the Amesha Spentas be their guides and protectors.

**5.** May the Renovation come speedily. May the Lie be vanquished. May the creation of Ahura Mazda be made perfect and eternal.
