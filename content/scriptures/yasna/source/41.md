---
title: "Yasna 41 – Yasna Haptanghaiti"
chapter: 41
---

**1.** We worship the Righteousness of the righteous man, and we worship the soul of the righteous man. We worship the holy Fravashi of the righteous man.

**2.** And we praise the good, the wise, the happy, the holy Zarathushtra. We praise the holy Zarathushtrian law.

**3.** We worship the entire bountiful Mathra, the entire system of worship, with all of its acts of praise.

**4.** Therefore we would worship these: Ahura Mazda and the Amesha Spentas, and all the good and holy and bountiful beings.

**5.** We worship the good Mazdayasnian Religion, all that is well-thought, well-spoken, well-done — Mazda-made and Asha-owning.
