---
title: "Yasna 57"
chapter: 57
---

**1.** We worship Sraosha, the holy, the lord of Righteousness. We worship Sraosha who smites with the blow of victory.

**2.** The first who chanted the five Gathas of Zarathushtra was Sraosha. He is the teacher of the Religion. He is the guardian of the faithful.

**3.** Sraosha keeps the world against the assault of the demons. Without Sraosha the creation could not endure.

**4.** We worship Sraosha with our best thoughts and words and deeds. May he carry our prayers to the ears of Ahura Mazda.
