---
title: "Yasna 72"
chapter: 72
---

**1.** I desire to worship the Creator Ahura Mazda, the brilliant, the glorious. I desire to worship the Amesha Spentas.

**2.** I desire to worship all good thoughts, and all good words, and all good deeds — those done and those to come.

**3.** I desire to worship the entire creation of the righteous Spirit. I desire to worship the holy Mathra Spenta, the law against the Daevas.

**4.** May this Yasna, which we have offered from beginning to end, be acceptable to Ahura Mazda. May all our prayers, all our offerings, all our worship rise to the Endless Light.

**5.** Praise be to Ahura Mazda. Praise be to Asha. Praise be to Vohu Manah. Ashem Vohu: Asha is the best of all good. Happiness, happiness to him who is righteous for the sake of the Best Righteousness.
