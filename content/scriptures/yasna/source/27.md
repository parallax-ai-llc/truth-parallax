---
title: "Yasna 27 – The Sacred Prayers"
chapter: 27
---

**1.** This is the will of the Lord — the judgment of Truth — the judgment of Right: that the power of deeds done for Mazda shall be given unto him who works in this world for the meek.

**2.** The Ahuna Vairya: As the Master, so is the Judge to be chosen in accord with Truth. Establish the power of acts arising from the life lived with Good Mind, for Mazda and for the lord whom they made pastor for the meek.

**3.** Asha is the best of all good. Happiness, happiness is to him — namely, to him who is righteous for the sake of the Best Righteousness.

**4.** The one of beings, male or female, whom Ahura Mazda knows to be the best for worship according to Righteousness — we worship those.

**5.** We worship the Ahuna Vairya. We worship the Asha Vahishta. We worship all that is well done, past and present, and that which shall be well done hereafter.

**6.** We worship the entire bountiful Mathra, even the entire Mazdayasnian law. We worship Asha the Best.

**7.** As a worshipper of Mazda, as one of the Religion of Zarathushtra, I profess the well-thought thought, the well-spoken word, and the well-done deed.

**8.** I embrace the Mazdayasnian Religion, which throws off the yoke of the Daevas, which upholds the doctrine of Ahura. I praise Asha. Praise be to Asha, the Best.

**9.** We choose the bountiful Spenta Armaiti. May she be ours. We choose Asha, the most beautiful. We choose the Good Mind. We choose the Dominion that is desired.

**10.** We worship the word of Zarathushtra. We worship all the Yasna with its fullness and its ritual. We worship these with the Good Mind and with the Good Word and with the Good Deed.

**11.** Praise to thee, Ahura Mazda! Praise to thee, Asha! Praise to thee, Vohu Manah! Thine is the Dominion, thine is the Power, thine is the Glory, O Ahura Mazda, for ever and ever.
