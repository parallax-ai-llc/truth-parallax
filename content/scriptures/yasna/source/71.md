---
title: "Yasna 71"
chapter: 71
---

**1.** We worship the Yasna with its completeness and its perfection, with the Gathas and the sacred words, with the Haoma and the Zaothra.

**2.** If any part of this Yasna has been imperfectly performed, may the Amesha Spentas perfect it. If any word has been wrongly spoken, may Ahura Mazda accept our intention.

**3.** The faithful priest who performs the Yasna with devotion and correctness earns great merit. His Yasna nourishes the good creation and weakens the Lie.

**4.** We worship the entire Yasna, from beginning to end, with all its prayers and offerings. May it be complete and acceptable.
