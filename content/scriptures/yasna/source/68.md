---
title: "Yasna 68"
chapter: 68
---

**1.** We worship the good waters, the Mazda-made, the Asha-owning waters. We worship them with the most beautiful worship.

**2.** May the waters come to us with healing and with health. May they come to us with strength and with prosperity.

**3.** May they come to us with the glory of Ahura Mazda, with the blessing of the Amesha Spentas, with the protection of the Fravashis of the righteous.

**4.** We worship the waters. Praise be to the waters. May the waters be blessed for ever and ever.
