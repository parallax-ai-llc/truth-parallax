---
title: "Yasna 37 – Yasna Haptanghaiti"
chapter: 37
---

**1.** So we worship Ahura Mazda, who is Righteous and the Master of Righteousness. We worship the Amesha Spentas, the good lords who rule aright.

**2.** We worship the whole world of the righteous, the heavens and the earth, the waters and the plants, the animals and the men.

**3.** And we worship those men and women whose good conscience guides them in the paths of Righteousness. We worship all who live by the law of Good Thought.

**4.** We worship with our offering and our praise, with the Gathas and the sacred words, with the Haoma and the Zaothra.

**5.** May our worship rise as the dawn rises, bringing light to the world. May our offering be as the rain that nourishes the earth.
