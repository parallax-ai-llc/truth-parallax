---
title: "Yasna 67"
chapter: 67
---

**1.** We worship the good waters that Ahura Mazda created. We worship them with the Yasna and the Zaothra.

**2.** The waters are the veins of the earth. Through them life flows to every creature. Without water there is no life, no growth, no renewal.

**3.** We worship the waters and offer them our praise, that they may continue to flow and to sustain the creation of Ahura Mazda.
