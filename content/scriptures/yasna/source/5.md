---
title: "Yasna 5 – Prayer to the Gathas"
chapter: 5
---

**1.** I will worship these Gathas, which are the best of all, which are the most righteous, which are the most efficacious for prayer, which were composed by Zarathushtra, the righteous, the prophet of Ahura Mazda.

**2.** I will worship these words revealed from the mouth of Ahura Mazda, spoken in conference with Zarathushtra, the most righteous of all words.

**3.** I will worship the verses of the Ahunavaiti Gatha, and the verses of the Ushtavaiti Gatha, and the verses of the Spenta Mainyu Gatha, and the verses of the Vohu Khshathra Gatha, and the verses of the Vahishtoishti Gatha.

**4.** I will worship these Gathas that are the praises and the prayers and the teachings of Zarathushtra to Ahura Mazda.

**5.** I will worship every Gatha with the recitation of the Ahunavar, and with the devotion of good thought, good word, and good deed.
