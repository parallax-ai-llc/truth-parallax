---
title: "Yasna 63"
chapter: 63
---

**1.** We worship the good waters of Ahura Mazda, the holy, the healing, the purifying.

**2.** We worship Ardvi Sura Anahita, the wide-flowing. We worship the sea Vourukasha and all the rivers and streams.

**3.** May the good waters bring us health and prosperity. May they nourish the earth and sustain the creation of Ahura Mazda.
