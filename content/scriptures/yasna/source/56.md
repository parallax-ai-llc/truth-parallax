---
title: "Yasna 56"
chapter: 56
---

**1.** We worship Sraosha, the righteous, the beautiful, the strong, the tall, who is the companion of Asha.

**2.** Sraosha protects the world by night when the demons are most active. He drives away Aeshma with his mighty mace.

**3.** We worship Sraosha with our Yasna and our offering. May he protect us from the works of the Lie.
