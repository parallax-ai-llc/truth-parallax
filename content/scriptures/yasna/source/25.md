---
title: "Yasna 25"
chapter: 25
---

**1.** We worship the bountiful Gathas and the law of the Mazdayasnian Religion.

**2.** We worship the Ahunavaiti Gatha with the entire Yasna. We worship the Ushtavaiti Gatha. We worship the Spenta Mainyu Gatha. We worship the Vohu Khshathra Gatha. We worship the Vahishtoishti Gatha.

**3.** I will worship these Gathas as the first and the best, as the most righteous and the most beautiful, the most victorious and the most healing.

**4.** May the merit of these Gathas come to us. May their teaching guide us. May their power protect us from the Lie.
