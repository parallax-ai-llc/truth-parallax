---
title: "Yasna 19"
chapter: 19
---

**1.** This is the Zand, the commentary, of the Ahuna Vairya: Ahura Mazda spoke it to Zarathushtra.

**2.** He spoke it before the creation of the sky, before the creation of the water, before the creation of the earth, before the creation of the plants, before the creation of the four-footed cattle, before the birth of the righteous man.

**3.** He spoke it before the creation of the two-footed man, before the sun rose with its body above the mountains.

**4.** The Ahuna Vairya is the greatest of all words. Through it the Evil Spirit was confounded and fell into stupor. Through it the creation of Ahura Mazda was established.
