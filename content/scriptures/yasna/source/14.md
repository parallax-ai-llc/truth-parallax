---
title: "Yasna 14"
chapter: 14
---

**1.** I will worship the Haoma, pressed with the mortar and the cups, with the spread Baresman, with good word and good deed.

**2.** And I will worship the holy Zaothra-waters, and the Baresman spread with sanctity. And I offer with complete offering the sacred Haoma to the Amesha Spentas.

**3.** Let me worship these offerings with the Ahuna Vairya. Let me worship with the prayer that the Maker Ahura Mazda taught to Zarathushtra.

**4.** May the Fire of Ahura Mazda receive this offering with joy, and may the merit of this offering advance the cause of Righteousness.
