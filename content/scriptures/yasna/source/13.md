---
title: "Yasna 13 – Invocations and Dedications"
chapter: 13
---

**1.** I invoke and I announce my Yasna to the creators of the creatures: to Ahura Mazda, to the Bountiful Spirit, to the Fire of Ahura Mazda, to the Earth of Ahura Mazda.

**2.** I invoke and I announce my Yasna to the good waters, to the faithful who bear their children well, to the Fravashis of the righteous.

**3.** And I invoke the good Ashi, the good Parendi, the good Daena of the worshippers of Mazda.

**4.** I invoke the good, holy Arshtat, who increases Asha, and the good, holy Zamyat, who increases Asha.

**5.** And I invoke the entire creation of the righteous Spirit, the bountiful, the beneficent, Mazda-made, Asha-owning.
