---
title: "Yasna 8 – To the Earth and the Sacred Waters"
chapter: 8
---

**1.** This offering do I make to thee, O sacred Earth! I offer it to thee with homage and with praise, with this Yasna, with this Zaothra, and with these offerings.

**2.** And I offer this Zaothra to the good waters, the waters of Ahura Mazda, the holy, the healing, that flow from the mountains to the seas.

**3.** We worship the good waters, the strong waters, the mighty, the pure, that flow in channels to the fields, that nourish the grass and the grain.

**4.** We worship the waters of Ardvi Sura Anahita, the wide-flowing, the Daeva-opposing, the purifying, the health-giving.

**5.** May the good waters bring us health and strength and righteousness. May they purify our bodies and our souls. May they sustain the good creation of Ahura Mazda.

**6.** And we offer this Zaothra to the Earth, the holy, the bountiful Spenta Armaiti, who bears all good things and sustains all life.
