---
title: "Yasna 21"
chapter: 21
---

**1.** We worship the Ahuna Vairya: "As the Master, so is the Judge to be chosen in accord with Truth. Establish the power of acts arising from the life lived with Good Mind, for Mazda and for the lord whom they made pastor for the meek."

**2.** We worship the Ashem Vohu: "Asha is the best of all good. It is radiance, it is glory. Righteous is the man who is righteous by the Best Righteousness."

**3.** We worship the Yenghe Hatam: "The one of beings, male or female, whom Ahura Mazda knows the best for worship according to Asha — we worship those."

**4.** These three prayers are the foundation of the Religion. Through them the world was created, through them the Lie is vanquished, and through them the Renovation shall come.
