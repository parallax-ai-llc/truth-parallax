---
title: "Yasna 17"
chapter: 17
---

**1.** To the Fire, the son of Ahura Mazda! We worship thee, O Fire, son of Ahura Mazda, with the Yasna and the Vahmya of the righteous.

**2.** And we worship all the fires, both those upon the earth and those in the heavens, and the fire that burns within the bodies of men and animals.

**3.** We worship the fire Berezi-savangha, which burns before Ahura Mazda; and the fire Vohu-fryana, which burns within the bodies of men; and the fire Urvazishta, which burns in the plants; and the fire Vazishta, which is the lightning; and the fire Spenishta, the most holy fire.

**4.** We worship the fire with the sacred wood and with the incense. We worship the fire with the Haoma and with the Zaothra. May the fire receive our offering with joy.
