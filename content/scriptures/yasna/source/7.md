---
title: "Yasna 7 – Presentation of Offerings"
chapter: 7
---

**1.** I will present the offering with this Yasna and with this homage to Ahura Mazda, the lord of Righteousness, and to the Amesha Spentas.

**2.** I present the offering to the Fire, the son of Ahura Mazda, and to all fires. I present the offering to the good waters, and to all the waters of Mazda.

**3.** I present the offering to the fertile earth, and to all the plants that grow upon it, and to all the good trees, the nourishing, the sweet-scented.

**4.** I present the offering to the soul of the Kine, and to the souls of all the beneficent animals, and to the Fravashis of the righteous.

**5.** I present the offering with the sacred Zaothra and the Baresman, with the holy meat and the Haoma, with good thought and good word and good deed.

**6.** May our offerings be pleasing to Ahura Mazda. May they nourish the good creation and increase Righteousness in the world.
