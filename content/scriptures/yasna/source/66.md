---
title: "Yasna 66"
chapter: 66
---

**1.** We worship the waters and the plants with the Zaothra offering.

**2.** We worship the waters of the mountains and the waters of the plains. We worship the waters that nourish the roots of the sacred Haoma.

**3.** May the waters be blessed and bring blessing. May the plants grow strong and nourish the faithful.
