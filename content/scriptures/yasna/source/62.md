---
title: "Yasna 62"
chapter: 62
---

**1.** We worship the sacred Fire of Ahura Mazda, with reverence and with praise.

**2.** The Fire is the son of Ahura Mazda. Through the Fire the Renovation shall be accomplished. Through the Fire the wicked shall be purified and the righteous confirmed in their blessedness.

**3.** We tend the Fire with clean wood, with incense, with the fat of animals offered in sacrifice. We honor the Fire and keep it burning.

**4.** O Fire, son of Ahura Mazda! Thou art worthy of worship, worthy of praise. May thy light shine upon us and protect us from the darkness.
