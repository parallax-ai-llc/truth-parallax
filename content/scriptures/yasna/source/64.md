---
title: "Yasna 64"
chapter: 64
---

**1.** We worship the bountiful Gathas with the entire Yasna. We worship the Ahunavaiti Gatha and the Ushtavaiti Gatha and the Spenta Mainyu Gatha and the Vohu Khshathra Gatha and the Vahishtoishti Gatha.

**2.** These are the words of the Prophet Zarathushtra, spoken in the light of Ahura Mazda. Through them the faithful man gains wisdom and strength.

**3.** May the merit of these Gathas come to us. May their teaching illuminate our minds and guide our deeds.
