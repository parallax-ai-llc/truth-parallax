---
title: "Yasna 18"
chapter: 18
---

**1.** We worship the waters, the good waters, the best, the Mazda-made, the Asha-owning.

**2.** We worship the sea Vourukasha, and all the rivers and streams that flow from it, and the rain that falls from the clouds, and the snow upon the mountains.

**3.** We worship Ardvi Sura Anahita, the wide-flowing, the healing, the Daeva-opposing, the purifying. We worship all the waters of Ahura Mazda.

**4.** May the good waters bring us health and strength and righteousness. May they purify us and sustain us and nourish the good creation.
