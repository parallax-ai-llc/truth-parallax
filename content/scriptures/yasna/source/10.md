---
title: "Yasna 10 – The Hom Yasht (Part 2)"
chapter: 10
---

**1.** O golden-hued Haoma! I call upon thee, I who am Zarathushtra. Thou art the most righteous of all the creations of the Spirit. Thou art the most powerful for healing.

**2.** Through thee men gain strength. Through thee the warriors conquer. Through thee the sick are healed. Through thee the old become young.

**3.** All other intoxications are accompanied by Wrath, the demon with the bloody club. But the intoxication of Haoma is accompanied by Righteousness. The intoxication of Haoma is joyful and health-giving.

**4.** Haoma makes the poor man's thoughts as great as those of the rich. Haoma makes the coward as brave as the hero. Haoma makes the lowly as lofty as the noble.

**5.** I call upon thee, O Haoma, who grows upon the mountains, who grows upon the heights, golden-hued, possessed of wisdom. Come to me for my protection, for my strength, for my victory, for my health, for my healing.

**6.** Grant me, O Haoma, of the strength that thou gavest to the kings, the mighty power and the ten thousandfold wisdom.

**7.** Grant me, O Haoma, health and strength, victory and bliss, the increase of Righteousness and the subjugation of the Lie.

**8.** I praise thee, O Haoma, I who am Zarathushtra. We praise thee with our bodies and our lives, with our bones and our forms of life.
