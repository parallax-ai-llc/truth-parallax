---
title: "Yasna 39 – Yasna Haptanghaiti"
chapter: 39
---

**1.** We worship the souls of the beneficent animals, of the cattle and the horses, of the dogs that guard the homesteads.

**2.** We worship the soul of the Ox, who cried out for protection and received Zarathushtra as the protector of the faithful.

**3.** We worship the souls of all the righteous, men and women, who have upheld the Religion and advanced the cause of Asha.

**4.** We worship the Fravashis of the first teachers: of Zarathushtra, of Maidhyoimaongha, of Isat-vastra, and of all the disciples of the Prophet.

**5.** May the souls of the righteous receive our praise and our offering, and may they intercede for us before the throne of Ahura Mazda.
