---
title: "Yasna 65"
chapter: 65
---

**1.** We worship the good waters that Ahura Mazda created, the waters that flow from the mountain-tops to the sea.

**2.** We worship the waters of Ardvi Sura Anahita, who purifies the seed of men and the womb of women, who gives milk to mothers.

**3.** We worship all waters: the rain-waters, the river-waters, the spring-waters, the well-waters. All are the creation of Ahura Mazda and are holy.
