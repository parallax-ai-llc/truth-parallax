---
title: "Yasna 22"
chapter: 22
---

**1.** We worship Ahura Mazda, the Creator, the holy and the good, the Master of Righteousness.

**2.** We worship all the Amesha Spentas, who are the lords of the good creation. We worship their creation, their body, their strength, their firmness, their Fravashis.

**3.** We sacrifice to the good, the strong, the beneficent Fravashis of the righteous, who are overwhelming and victorious.

**4.** We worship the creatures of the beneficent Spirit. We worship the entire creation of the righteous Spirit, the bountiful, the good.
