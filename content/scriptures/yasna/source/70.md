---
title: "Yasna 70"
chapter: 70
---

**1.** We worship the Amesha Spentas with this Yasna. We worship Ahura Mazda with this Yasna. We worship the Fravashis of the righteous with this Yasna.

**2.** We have completed the Yasna with its fullness and its ritual. We have offered the prayers and the offerings.

**3.** May this completed Yasna be acceptable to Ahura Mazda. May its merit advance the cause of Righteousness and hasten the Renovation.
