---
title: "Yasna 51 – Vohu Khshathra Gatha"
chapter: 51
---

**1.** The Good Dominion is to be chosen. It is the best lot. A blessing it is, a blessing shall it be unto the man who is righteous. Through the holiest Spirit, through the deeds done with Good Thought and with the truth of Mazda Ahura.

**2.** This man it is who by his action, word, and conscience fulfils the ordinance of Ahura Mazda. His thought and mind are in accord with Good Thought. He is the truest friend of the Right.

**3.** He is the reformer, the prophet, the lord that is mighty, strong in devotion, who through the Right holds the land in peace.

**4.** He that advances Devotion and Righteousness is the friend of Mazda Ahura. Through Good Thought shall he teach the way to the abode where Mazda dwells.

**5.** What reward Thou hast for giving, what for receiving — by the Fire of Ordeal, through the molten metal — O Ahura, make it plain to our souls, that we may know it.
