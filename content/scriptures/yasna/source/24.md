---
title: "Yasna 24"
chapter: 24
---

**1.** We worship Ahura Mazda with this Yasna and these offerings. We worship the Amesha Spentas. We worship the fravashis of the righteous.

**2.** We worship the waters, the lands, the plants, the heavens, the earth, the wind, the Fire of Ahura Mazda, the soul of the ox, the luminaries, and all things righteous.

**3.** We worship the Mathra Spenta, the holy word, the law against the Daevas, the Zarathushtrian law.

**4.** We worship the good, holy Sraosha, and Rashnu, and Mithra of the wide pastures.

**5.** We worship the Royal Glory and the Glory not yet seized, the Glory of the Aryan lands and of the righteous of all nations.
