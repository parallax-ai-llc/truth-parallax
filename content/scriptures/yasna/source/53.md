---
title: "Yasna 53 – Vahishtoishti Gatha: The Wedding Hymn"
chapter: 53
---

**1.** The best prayer of Zarathushtra Spitama is known. Mazda Ahura through the Right will give him the blessings of Good Thought, and also the Dominion, the holy Piety whose works bring joy — and Welfare and Immortality.

**2.** He who is most obedient to Mazda through worship — the Lord himself shall give him the way of Good Thought. He is the lord of Right and Good Thought, the blessed one.

**3.** Now to thee, O maiden Pouruchista, youngest daughter of Zarathushtra — thee hath he given as husband one who walks in Good Thought and Right and Mazda. Take counsel with thine own understanding, practice deeds of good devotion.

**4.** So I say unto you brides and bridegrooms: take these words to heart. Apprehend them with understanding. Practice the law of devotion and Right. Let each strive to excel the other in the Right. So shall it be a happy life.

**5.** Those who follow the teachings of the Lie shall find sorrow. Those who give power to the wicked destroy life. But for the servants of the Right there shall be the best reward.
