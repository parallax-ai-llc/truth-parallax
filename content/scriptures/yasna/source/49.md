---
title: "Yasna 49 – Gatha"
chapter: 49
---

**1.** If through the Right the Lie is to be vanquished, then shall the faithful have power. Who among those who listen is righteous, and who is a liar? The wise man shall discern between these two.

**2.** He that is most good to the righteous man shall dwell in the pasture of Right and Good Thought. Through the Dominion of Ahura Mazda shall the righteous man prosper.

**3.** Can my soul count on anyone for help? Who is there for my herd, who for myself a protector, other than the Right and Ahura Mazda and the Best Thought?

**4.** The wise whom thou knowest as worthy, for their good doing and their good thought — for them do thou fulfil their longing. Through Good Thought grant the righteous their reward.

**5.** May the Right be our guide and Good Thought our counsel. Through the Dominion of Ahura Mazda shall the Renovation come, and creation shall be made perfect.
