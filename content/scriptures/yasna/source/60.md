---
title: "Yasna 60"
chapter: 60
---

**1.** We pray for the blessing of the house, for the increase of the family, for the prosperity of the community, for the peace of the land.

**2.** May righteousness prevail in our houses, in our villages, in our provinces, in all the lands of the earth.

**3.** May the fire burn bright in every hearth. May the cattle thrive in every pasture. May the children grow strong in every family.

**4.** We pray for these blessings through the power of Ahura Mazda, through the Amesha Spentas, through the merit of good thoughts, good words, and good deeds.
