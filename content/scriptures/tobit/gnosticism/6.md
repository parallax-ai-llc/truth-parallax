A Gnostic reading of Tobit 6 would interpret the fish and its organs as symbols of hidden knowledge extracted from the natural world by the divine revealer. The fish that attacks from the depths of the river represents the hostile powers of the material world, and Raphael's instruction to preserve its organs and use them for healing symbolizes the Gnostic practice of extracting salvific knowledge from within the material prison itself.

The specific instructions on using the organs against demons and blindness would be read as esoteric teaching, passwords and ritual knowledge necessary for passing through the archonic barriers that separate the soul from the Pleroma. In Gnostic texts, the ascending soul must know the correct words, symbols, and practices to overcome each archon. Raphael's revelation of these secrets to Tobias mirrors the Gnostic revealer who imparts the hidden knowledge necessary for liberation.
