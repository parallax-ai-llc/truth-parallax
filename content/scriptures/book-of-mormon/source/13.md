---
title: "Book of Mormon Section 13: Ether"
scripture: "book-of-mormon"
chapter: 13
---

The book of Ether contains the abridgment by Moroni of the record of the Jaredites, a people who came to the promised land at the time of the great tower, when the Lord confounded the language of the people. The brother of Jared, a large and mighty man, highly favored of the Lord, cried unto the Lord that He would not confound the language of his family and friends.

The Lord heard his plea and directed them to gather seeds of every kind, flocks and herds, and to build barges in which to cross many waters. When they reached the great sea that divided the lands, the Lord instructed the brother of Jared to build eight barges that were tight like unto a dish, to carry them across the ocean.

The brother of Jared went upon the mount and did molten out of a rock sixteen small stones, white and clear, even as transparent glass, and cried unto the Lord: Touch these stones, O Lord, with thy finger, and prepare them that they may shine forth in darkness. And the Lord stretched forth His hand and touched the stones one by one with His finger, and the veil was taken from off the eyes of the brother of Jared and he saw the finger of the Lord, and it was as the finger of a man, like unto flesh and blood.

And the Lord showed Himself unto him and said: Because of thy faith thou hast seen that I shall take upon me flesh and blood. Never has man come before me with such exceeding faith as thou hast. Because thou knowest these things ye are redeemed from the fall, therefore ye are brought back into my presence, therefore I show myself unto you.

The Jaredite civilization rose to great power in the promised land but eventually destroyed itself through cycles of wickedness, secret combinations, and civil war. The last survivor, Coriantumr, was found by the people of Zarahemla, fulfilling the prophecy of the prophet Ether that he would live to see another people receive the land as their inheritance.
