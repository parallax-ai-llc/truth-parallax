---
title: "Mozi - Chapter 1"
scripture: "mozi"
chapter: 1
---

**1** Master Mo said: The gentlemen of the world today know only trifles but not what is important. How do we know this?

**2** We know it from the way a man conducts himself in the family. If a man in the family commits some offense against the head of the family, he still has neighbouring families to flee to.

**3** Yet father warns son, elder brother warns younger brother, saying: "Be careful! Be circumspect! If you are not careful and circumspect in your conduct within the family, how will you get along in the state?"

**4** If a man in the state commits some offense against the ruler, he still has neighbouring states to flee to.

**5** Yet father warns son, elder brother warns younger brother, saying: "Be careful! Be circumspect! It will not do for one who conducts himself in the state not to be careful and circumspect!"

**6** When it comes to the case of all men in the world conducting themselves before Heaven, however, there is nowhere to flee to.

**7** Yet men do not know enough to warn one another. This is how I know that the gentlemen of the world know only trifles but not what is important.

**8** Now Master Mo said: What does Heaven desire, and what does Heaven abominate? Heaven desires righteousness and abominates unrighteousness.

**9** How do we know that Heaven desires righteousness and abominates unrighteousness? I say: With righteousness the world lives and without it the world dies; with it the world becomes rich and without it the world becomes poor; with it the world becomes orderly and without it the world becomes chaotic.

**10** And Heaven likes to have the world live and dislikes to have it die, likes to have it rich and dislikes to have it poor, likes to have it orderly and dislikes to have it in chaos. This is how I know Heaven desires righteousness and abominates unrighteousness.
