**Section VI: Immortality Granted**

**1** An and Enlil spoke kindly to Ziusudra.
**2** They gave him breath of life, like a god.
**3** They gave him eternal life, like a god.

**4** At that time, Ziusudra the king,
**5** the preserver of the name of vegetation and of the seed of mankind,
**6** in the land of crossing, the land of Dilmun,
**7** the place where the sun rises, they caused to dwell.

**8** [Gap in text]

**9** After the flood had swept over the land,
**10** and kingship had descended from heaven,
**11** the kingship was in Kish.

**12** [The text connects to the Sumerian King List]

**13** An and Enlil cherished Ziusudra.
**14** Life like a god they gave him.
**15** Breath eternal like a god they gave him.

**16** Then Ziusudra the king,
**17** the preserver of the name of all vegetation
**18** and of the seed of mankind,
**19** in the land of Dilmun,
**20** the place where the sun rises,
**21** they caused him to dwell.

**22** After the flood,
**23** kingship descended from heaven once more.
**24** The kingship was in Kish.

**25** In Kish, Gashur became king; he ruled—years.
**26** [The list of post-diluvian kings continues]

**27** [Gap in text]

**28** The flood had swept over.
**29** All had been made new.
**30** The rites and the divine powers were established anew.
**31** The cities were rebuilt.
**32** The people multiplied once more upon the land.

**33** [Thus ends the Eridu Genesis.]
