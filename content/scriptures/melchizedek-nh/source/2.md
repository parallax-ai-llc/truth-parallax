---
title: "Melchizedek - Chapter 2"
scripture: "melchizedek-nh"
chapter: 2
---

**1** Then the holy ones continued to speak to Melchizedek concerning the nature of the sacrifice and the priesthood:

**2** "You, Melchizedek, are the priest forever, after the order not of Aaron but of the Most High. For Aaron's priesthood passes away, but yours endures forever."

**3** "The bread and wine that you offer are not mere bread and wine, but the body and blood of the truth, the substance of the divine light."

**4** "When you offer these gifts, you participate in the offering that takes place in the heavens above, in the holy of holies of the Pleroma."

**5** "For as above, so below; and the earthly offering is the image of the heavenly offering, and the earthly priest is the image of the heavenly priest."

**6** Then they revealed to him the mystery of the death and resurrection of the Savior:

**7** "The Savior shall be delivered up by the rulers of this world. They shall nail him to a tree. They shall mock him and scourge him."

**8** "But on the third day he shall rise again, for the power of the Most High shall raise him. And death shall be swallowed up in victory."

**9** "And those who believe in him shall also rise, for the resurrection of the Savior is the first-fruits of the resurrection of all who belong to the light."

**10** "The dead shall not remain dead, and the imprisoned shall not remain imprisoned. For the Savior has broken the gates of the underworld and has opened the way of the ascent."

**11** And Melchizedek received these words and was baptized in the name of the invisible spirit, and in the name of the truth, and in the name of the light.

**12** And the water of the baptism became fire, and the fire became light, and the light filled him and he was transformed.

**13** And the holy ones placed upon him the garment of the priesthood, which is woven of light, and they anointed his head with the oil of gladness.
