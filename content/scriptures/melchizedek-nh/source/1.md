---
title: "Melchizedek - Chapter 1"
scripture: "melchizedek-nh"
chapter: 1
---

**1** Jesus Christ, the Son of God, who has descended from the Pleroma, who is Melchizedek, the great high priest, the one who is holy, the one who presides over the holy of holies:

**2** The revelation which was given to Melchizedek, the priest of God Most High, concerning the mysteries of the heavens and the hidden things of the divine world.

**3** Melchizedek, the great receiver of the light, was taken up and shown the things that are above. And the holy ones spoke to him, saying:

**4** "Be strong, O Melchizedek, great high priest of God Most High, for the archons who are over the world shall wage war against you."

**5** "But they shall not prevail against you, for the power of the invisible spirit is with you, and the authority of the great aeons is upon you."

**6** "You are the image of the great heavenly high priest. As the earthly priest offers sacrifices below, so you shall offer the sacrifice of truth above."

**7** "For the sacrifice that is pleasing to the Most High is not the blood of bulls and goats, but the offering of knowledge and the oblation of truth."

**8** Then Melchizedek was shown the mysteries of the cosmos: how the archons had fashioned the material world, and how they had imprisoned sparks of the divine light within bodies of clay.

**9** And he was shown how the Savior would descend from the Pleroma to liberate those who had been imprisoned, not through force but through knowledge and truth.

**10** "For the Savior shall put on the garment of flesh and enter the realm of the archons. And they shall not recognize him, for he shall be disguised as one of them."

**11** "And he shall speak the words of truth, and those who have ears shall hear, and they shall be awakened from their sleep of ignorance."

**12** "And the archons shall be angry and shall seek to destroy him. But death shall have no power over him, for he is the life that was before death."
