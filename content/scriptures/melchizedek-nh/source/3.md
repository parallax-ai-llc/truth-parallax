---
title: "Melchizedek - Chapter 3"
scripture: "melchizedek-nh"
chapter: 3
---

**1** Then Melchizedek was instructed concerning the end of the age and the final victory of the light over the darkness:

**2** "In the last days, the powers of darkness shall gather their strength against the children of the light. There shall be wars and rumors of wars, and great tribulation."

**3** "But fear not, for these things must come to pass before the end. The Savior shall return in glory, and with him the hosts of the aeons."

**4** "And the archons shall be cast down from their thrones, and their power shall be taken from them, and they shall be bound in the chains of darkness forever."

**5** "And the souls of the righteous, who have kept the faith and held fast to the truth, shall be gathered up and shall enter the Pleroma."

**6** "And they shall be clothed in garments of light, and they shall receive crowns of glory, and they shall dwell forever in the presence of the invisible spirit."

**7** "And there shall be no more death, no more sorrow, no more pain, no more ignorance. For the old things shall have passed away, and all things shall be made new."

**8** And Melchizedek gave praise, saying: "Holy, holy, holy is the Lord God Most High, who was and is and is to come. Blessed be the invisible spirit, the source of all things."

**9** "Blessed be the Savior, who descended to redeem the lost. Blessed be the truth, which sets the captive free."

**10** "I, Melchizedek, priest of God Most High, do bear witness to these things. I have seen them with my own eyes and heard them with my own ears."

**11** "Let those who have ears hear. Let those who have eyes see. Let those who have understanding know that the time of the restoration is at hand."

**12** "For the invisible spirit has not forgotten its own. The sparks of light shall be gathered. The fragments shall be made whole. And all shall be one, as it was in the beginning."

**13** This is the revelation given to Melchizedek, the priest of God Most High, in the holy place. Let it be preserved and transmitted to those who are worthy.
