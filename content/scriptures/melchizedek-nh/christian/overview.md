## Overview

For Christian readers, the Nag Hammadi Melchizedek engages with one of the most mysterious figures in the Bible. The Epistle to the Hebrews (chapters 5-7) develops an elaborate Melchizedek Christology: Christ is 'a priest forever in the order of Melchizedek' (Hebrews 5:6). The Gnostic text develops this connection in a different direction, presenting Melchizedek not merely as a type of Christ but as an active participant in the cosmic drama of salvation. The text raises questions about the diversity of early Christian engagements with the Melchizedek tradition.
