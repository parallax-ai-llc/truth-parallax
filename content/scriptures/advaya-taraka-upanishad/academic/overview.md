## Overview

The Advaya Taraka Upanishad is classified among the minor Upanishads and is generally dated to the early centuries CE. Its teachings on non-duality, taraka yoga, liberation provide important evidence for the development of philosophical and practical traditions within the broader Upanishadic corpus.

## Research Themes

Scholars study the Advaya Taraka Upanishad for its contribution to understanding the diversity of Upanishadic thought beyond the principal Upanishads. The text reflects the ongoing synthesis of Vedic philosophy, devotional theism, and practical spiritual disciplines that characterized the late and post-Vedic period.
