Madhva's Dvaita school reads this section's teachings on the realization of non-duality within the framework of the eternal distinction between God, souls, and the material world. The Advaya Taraka Upanishad's philosophical content is understood as revealing Vishnu's supremacy and the soul's dependent relationship to the Lord.

For Dvaita, the knowledge presented here is valuable as preparation for devotion — it clarifies the soul's position in relation to God and removes misconceptions that obstruct the development of genuine love for the Lord.
