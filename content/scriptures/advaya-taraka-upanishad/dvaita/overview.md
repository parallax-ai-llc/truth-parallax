## Overview

Madhva's Dvaita school reads the Advaya Taraka Upanishad's teachings within the framework of the eternal distinction between God, souls, and the world. The text's themes of non-duality, taraka yoga, liberation are understood as revealing aspects of Vishnu's supreme nature and the soul's dependent relationship to Him.

## Theological Emphases

For Dvaita, the Advaya Taraka Upanishad's philosophical content supports the understanding that all Upanishadic teachings ultimately point to Vishnu's supremacy and the soul's need for divine grace. Its practical instructions serve as devotional disciplines that prepare the soul for receiving God's liberating grace.
