---
title: "Mugyeong - Chapter 1"
scripture: "mugyeong"
chapter: 1
---

**1** In the beginning, before the heavens and the earth were separated, there was only the Great Void, formless and without limit.

**2** From the Great Void arose the primordial breath, the ki that gives life to all things. The bright ki rose and became Heaven; the heavy ki sank and became Earth.

**3** Between Heaven and Earth the spirits were born, and they moved through the visible and invisible worlds like wind through the pine forests.

**4** The Supreme Spirit, Haneullim, the Lord of Heaven, looked down upon the Earth and saw that it was without order.

**5** So Haneullim sent down the first mudang, the shaman who walks between worlds, to carry messages between the spirits and the people.

**6** The first mudang received the sacred drum and the sacred bells, and with these she called upon the spirits of the four directions.

**7** From the East came the Blue Dragon spirit, guardian of the spring and new beginnings. From the South came the Red Phoenix spirit, guardian of summer and prosperity.

**8** From the West came the White Tiger spirit, guardian of autumn and courage. From the North came the Black Tortoise spirit, guardian of winter and wisdom.

**9** Together they established the order of the world, and the people learned to honour the spirits through ritual and offering.

**10** This is the foundation of the mudang's way: to serve as a bridge between Heaven and Earth, between the living and the ancestors, between the visible and the invisible.
