---
title: "Mugyeong - Chapter 2"
scripture: "mugyeong"
chapter: 2
---

**1** The spirits speak through the mudang, and the mudang speaks to the people. This is the sacred chain that has continued since the time of the first ancestors.

**2** When the spirits descend upon the mudang, her body trembles like a willow in the wind, and her voice becomes the voice of the other world.

**3** The ancestors come first, for they are closest to the living. They come to give counsel, to warn of danger, and to bless their descendants.

**4** After the ancestors come the mountain spirits, the sansin, who dwell upon the high peaks and guard the forests and streams.

**5** The mountain spirits are powerful and ancient. They were old when the first kingdoms were young. They know the secrets of healing herbs and the paths of underground waters.

**6** After the mountain spirits come the generals of Heaven, the great warrior spirits who protect the land from calamity and invasion.

**7** Last come the messengers of Haneullim himself, bearing the will of the Supreme Spirit to the world below.

**8** Each spirit must be greeted with the proper words, the proper offerings, and the proper music. The drum calls them forth; the bells guide them; the songs honour them.

**9** If the rituals are performed with sincerity and devotion, the spirits bestow their blessings: health, prosperity, peace, and the continuation of the family line.

**10** If the rituals are neglected, the spirits withdraw their protection, and misfortune follows like shadow follows substance.
