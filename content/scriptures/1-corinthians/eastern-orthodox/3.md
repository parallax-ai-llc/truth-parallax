## Christ the Foundation

Paul's declaration that "no one can lay a foundation other than Jesus Christ" (3:11) is affirmed by Orthodox ecclesiology. The Church is built on Christ, and the apostolic succession preserves this foundation through the continuity of faith, worship, and sacramental life.

## Tested by Fire

Paul's image of work tested by fire (3:13-15) is read in Orthodox theology as referring to the purifying judgment at the end of time. While Orthodoxy does not formally define purgatory as a distinct state, it affirms prayers for the dead and the possibility of post-mortem purification through God's mercy.

## The Community as Temple

Paul's teaching that "you are God's temple" (3:16) is central to Orthodox liturgical theology. The church building itself is understood as an icon of the heavenly temple, and the gathered community becomes the living temple through its Eucharistic worship.
