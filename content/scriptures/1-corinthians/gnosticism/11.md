## The Sacred Meal as Mystery

Paul's institution narrative (11:23-26) was read by Gnostics as describing a mystery rite with deeper meaning than the orthodox church recognized. The "body" broken is the Pleromatic Christ descending into matter; the "blood" of the new covenant is the divine substance shared with the pneumatic elect. The command to "do this in remembrance" preserves the gnosis of Christ's true nature and mission.

## Discerning the Body

Paul's warning about "discerning the body" (11:29) was interpreted by Gnostics as the ability to distinguish between the material body of Jesus and the spiritual Christ. Those who fail to discern eat and drink judgment because they confuse the Demiurge's material realm with the Pleromatic reality. True discernment is gnosis.

## Tradition and Secret Teaching

Paul's statement "I received from the Lord what I also delivered to you" (11:23) was cited as evidence that Paul received his teaching directly from the spiritual Christ, not from the other apostles. This direct transmission grounded the Gnostic claim to a parallel apostolic tradition, bypassing the psychic church's chain of succession.
