## Overview

For Christian readers, the Concept of Our Great Power engages with biblical apocalyptic themes — the periodization of history, the coming of the Savior, the final judgment — while developing them in a distinctly Gnostic direction. The tripartite division of history resonates with Christian dispensationalist thought and with Joachim of Fiore's three ages, though the theological content differs significantly. The text's vision of progressive spiritualization — from flesh through soul to spirit — can be read as a radical version of Paul's own hierarchy (1 Corinthians 15:44-46).
