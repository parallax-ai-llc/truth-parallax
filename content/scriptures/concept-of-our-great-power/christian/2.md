## The Age of the Psychic — Christian Engagement

For Christian readers, this section of Concept of Our Great Power offers both challenge and opportunity for theological reflection. For Christian readers, the Concept of Our Great Power engages with biblical apocalyptic themes — the periodization of history, the coming of the Savior, the final judgment — while developing them in a distinctly Gnostic direction.

The section "The Age of the Psychic" engages with themes that remain central to Christian theology, though it develops them in directions that diverge from creedal orthodoxy. The divergence itself is instructive: by encountering a coherent alternative theology that works with much of the same scriptural and theological raw material, Christian readers can deepen their understanding of their own tradition's distinctive commitments.

The patristic response to Gnostic teachings — from Irenaeus's systematic refutation to Clement of Alexandria's more nuanced engagement — demonstrates that the early church took the Gnostic challenge with profound seriousness.
