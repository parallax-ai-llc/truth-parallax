## The Coming of the Pneumatic Age — Gnostic Reading

This section of Concept of Our Great Power addresses themes central to the Gnostic understanding of the divine realm, the soul's liberation, and the path to gnosis. For Gnostic readers, the Concept of Our Great Power maps the progressive spiritualization of the cosmos through three ages: the age of flesh (dominated by material existence), the age of the psychic (dominated by the soul principle), and the age of the pneumatic (dominated by the spirit).

The Gnostic reader approaches this section — "The Coming of the Pneumatic Age" — with the conviction that its deepest meaning is accessible only through direct spiritual experience. The passage invites not merely intellectual comprehension but a transformation of consciousness in which the reader recognizes truths already latent within the divine spark.

The themes developed here participate in the broader Gnostic drama of emanation, fall, and return. The reader who grasps this pattern experientially — not merely conceptually — has already begun the journey of liberation from material entanglement and return to the fullness of the Pleroma.
