## Overview

For Gnostic readers, the Concept of Our Great Power maps the progressive spiritualization of the cosmos through three ages: the age of flesh (dominated by material existence), the age of the psychic (dominated by the soul principle), and the age of the pneumatic (dominated by the spirit). This tripartite scheme reflects the Gnostic conviction that history has a direction — it moves from material density toward spiritual transparency, from ignorance toward knowledge. The current psychic age, inaugurated by the coming of the Savior, will give way to the final pneumatic age in which the spiritual elect will be fully liberated from material entanglement.
