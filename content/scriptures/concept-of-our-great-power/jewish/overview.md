## Overview

Jewish readers recognize in the Concept of Our Great Power a transformation of Jewish apocalyptic periodization. The division of history into ages — each characterized by a dominant principle and culminating in a decisive divine intervention — draws on the tradition of apocalyptic periodization found in Daniel (the four kingdoms), 1 Enoch (the Apocalypse of Weeks), and 4 Ezra. The text transforms this Jewish heritage by replacing the covenantal framework with a cosmological one: the movement is not from promise to fulfillment but from matter to spirit.
