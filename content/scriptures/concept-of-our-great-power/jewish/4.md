## The Final Consummation — Jewish Perspectives

Jewish readers encounter in this section of Concept of Our Great Power evidence of how Jewish traditions were appropriated and transformed in the Gnostic context. Jewish readers recognize in the Concept of Our Great Power a transformation of Jewish apocalyptic periodization.

The section "The Final Consummation" draws on Jewish sources — Genesis narratives, prophetic imagery, wisdom traditions, and possibly early mystical speculation — but deploys them within a framework that diverges from rabbinic Judaism. This transformation is not simple distortion but a creative engagement with traditions that remained fluid and contested in the second and third centuries.

Reading this passage against rabbinic parallels illuminates both shared concerns and fundamental divergences — revealing what was at stake theologically when Jewish concepts entered Gnostic systems.
