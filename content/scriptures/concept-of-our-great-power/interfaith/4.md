## The Final Consummation — Interfaith Reflection

This section of Concept of Our Great Power addresses questions that transcend the boundaries of any single religious tradition. The Concept of Our Great Power addresses a widespread religious intuition: that history is not random but moves toward a spiritual transformation.

The section "The Final Consummation" speaks from within a specific tradition — Gnostic Christianity of the second and third centuries — but its questions are universal. Every contemplative tradition has grappled with the themes explored here, and the Gnostic answers illuminate dimensions of the human spiritual search that resonate across cultures.

Interfaith readers may discover in this passage both the familiar and the strange — points of contact with their own traditions that create space for dialogue, alongside genuine differences that deepen mutual understanding and respect.
