## The Coming of the Pneumatic Age — Comparative Perspectives

This section of Concept of Our Great Power addresses themes that resonate across the world's religious and philosophical traditions. The Concept of Our Great Power's division of history into three progressive ages resonates with cyclical and progressive cosmologies worldwide.

The section "The Coming of the Pneumatic Age" invites cross-cultural comparison while respecting the irreducible particularity of the Gnostic context. The questions addressed here — about the nature of ultimate reality, the human condition, and the possibility of liberation — are shared by traditions as diverse as Vedanta, Buddhism, Neoplatonism, Kabbalah, and Sufism.

Comparative method at its best illuminates without flattening: genuine parallels create space for mutual understanding, while differences remind us that each tradition develops its insights within a particular historical and cultural matrix.
