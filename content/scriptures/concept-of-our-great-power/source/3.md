---
title: "Concept of Our Great Power — Section 3: The Coming of the Logos"
scripture: "concept-of-our-great-power"
chapter: 3
---

**1** He revealed himself and taught. And many followed him. And those who opposed him were blind. They did not see the light, for they were in the darkness.

**2** He gave power to those who were his own, and they became lights in the world. And the powers were troubled, for they saw a new light in the world.

**3** And the archon of the east summoned the powers, and they plotted against him. They said, "Let us seize him and kill him." But they did not know who he was.

**4** He was seized and brought to the ruler. And they passed judgment on him, the Lord of Glory. And he was crucified, and he died — not that he who is life could die, but it was the psychic soul that died.

**5** And he was placed in a tomb. And after three days he left the tomb. And the psychic region was in confusion, for it did not know what had happened.

**6** Then the Logos of the great power spoke to the powers and the psychic souls, and he gave them the knowledge of the truth.

**7** And many were saved by the word of knowledge, and they were taken up to the rest of the great aeon.
