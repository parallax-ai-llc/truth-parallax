---
title: "Concept of Our Great Power — Section 4: The Aeon of the Spirit"
scripture: "concept-of-our-great-power"
chapter: 4
---

**1** The third aeon is the immutable aeon, the aeon of beauty and the spirit. In this aeon the spirit of silence reigns.

**2** And the souls that are perfect will be taken up to the aeon of the spirit. They will rest in the great power which is above all powers.

**3** The fire that is in them will consume the archons and the powers. And they will pass away, and the works of the archons will be destroyed.

**4** For the great power will gather his flock that has gone astray. And he will save the whole of the spirit. And the All will be purified from evil.

**5** Then the great light will appear, the light that has no shadow, and the mind of the great power will fill the whole aeon with glory.

**6** And there will be no more darkness, and no more death, and no more suffering. But all will rest in the spirit of the great power forever.

**7** This is the concept of the Great Power. Blessed is the soul that knows these things, for it shall never taste death.
