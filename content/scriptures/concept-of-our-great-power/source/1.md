---
title: "Concept of Our Great Power — Section 1: The Aeon of the Flesh"
scripture: "concept-of-our-great-power"
chapter: 1
---

**1** He who knew the great powers did not know that the Great Power existed before them. Therefore he is called the perfection, for he was great, and every power exists in him.

**2** The great power is the mind of those who are appointed. He is the Father of every power. He willed the souls that are in the world to be saved.

**3** Now I have spoken to you about the three aeons. The first aeon is that of the flesh. The second is the psychic one. The third aeon is the aeon of the spirit.

**4** Now, the first aeon is the aeon of the flesh, and its ruler is the archon of the flesh. He made the elements that are in the world.

**5** He set up kingdoms. He caused wars to happen. He sowed wickedness. He brought about the shedding of blood.

**6** For in that aeon the darkness ruled, together with ignorance. He who was born before the flood, Noah, preached for one hundred and twenty years, and no one listened to him.

**7** And the great eternal one looked down from the heavens of heavens, and called through the Holy Spirit. He sent a power and the flood came upon the earth.
