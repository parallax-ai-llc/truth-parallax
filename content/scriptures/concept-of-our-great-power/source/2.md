---
title: "Concept of Our Great Power — Section 2: The Aeon of the Psyche"
scripture: "concept-of-our-great-power"
chapter: 2
---

**1** The second aeon is the psychic one. And a soul was revealed in the psychic region, and it entered the thought of those who were to receive a form from the psychic aeon.

**2** And the archon of the west was appointed. He was given power over the fire. And he made his dwelling place in the west.

**3** And Moses was sent as a servant, and he who was appointed was the one who received the Law. And through him the kingdoms of the earth were distributed.

**4** And the aeon of the psyche was established in kingship, and through the Law the psychic world was governed.

**5** But the great power knew that the psychic aeon was dissolving. And it sent the word of knowledge to the world.

**6** Then the Logos came from the aeon above, who is in the heart of the Father. He came to the psychic region. And he put on the soul of the psyche.

**7** And the powers did not know him. They thought he was a mere man. But he spoke with them, and they did not know that he was the Lord.
