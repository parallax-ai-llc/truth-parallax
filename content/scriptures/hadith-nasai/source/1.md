---
title: "Sunan an-Nasa'i - Chapter 1"
scripture: "hadith-nasai"
chapter: 1
---

**1** It was narrated that Umar ibn al-Khattab said: I heard the Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, say: "Actions are judged by intentions, and every person will be rewarded according to his intention."

**2** It was narrated that Abu Hurairah said: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "Were it not that I would place a hardship on my nation, I would have commanded them to use the tooth-stick before every prayer."

**3** It was narrated that Anas said: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "I have urged you repeatedly to use the tooth-stick."

**4** It was narrated that Aisha said: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "The tooth-stick is a purification for the mouth and is pleasing to the Lord."

**5** It was narrated from Abu Hurairah that the Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "When one of you performs ablution, let him put water in his nose, then blow it out. Whoever cleans himself with stones, let him use an odd number."

**6** It was narrated from Uthman ibn Affan that the Prophet, peace be upon him, performed ablution three times each, and said: "Whoever performs ablution like this, his previous sins will be forgiven."

**7** It was narrated from Ali that the Prophet, peace be upon him, said: "The key to prayer is purification, its beginning is the takbir, and its ending is the taslim."

**8** It was narrated that Abu Hurairah said: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "The prayer of one who breaks his ablution is not accepted until he performs ablution again."
