---
title: "Sunan an-Nasa'i - Chapter 4"
scripture: "hadith-nasai"
chapter: 4
---

**1** It was narrated from Abu Hurairah that the Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "Charity does not diminish wealth. Allah does not increase a servant who forgives others except in honor. Whoever humbles himself for the sake of Allah, Allah will raise him."

**2** It was narrated from Abu Hurairah that the Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "Whoever gives in charity the equivalent of a date from lawful earnings, and Allah accepts only that which is pure, Allah will take it in His right hand and nurture it for its owner, as one of you nurtures his foal, until it becomes like a mountain."

**3** It was narrated from Aisha that the Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "When a woman gives in charity from her husband's food, without being extravagant, she will receive the reward for what she spent, her husband will be rewarded for what he earned, and the storekeeper will receive a similar reward."

**4** It was narrated from Abu Hurairah that the Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "The upper hand is better than the lower hand. The upper hand is the one that gives, and the lower hand is the one that takes."

**5** It was narrated from Asma bint Abu Bakr that the Prophet, peace be upon him, said to her: "Spend, and do not count, lest Allah counts against you. Do not hoard, lest Allah withholds from you. Spend what you can."

**6** It was narrated from Abu Hurairah that the Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "There is no day on which the servants wake but two angels descend, and one of them says: 'O Allah, give to the one who spends a replacement,' and the other says: 'O Allah, give the one who withholds destruction.'"

**7** It was narrated from Abu Hurairah that a man came to the Prophet, peace be upon him, and said: "O Messenger of Allah, which charity has the greatest reward?" He said: "That you give charity while you are healthy, feeling miserly, fearing poverty and hoping for wealth. Do not delay until death is at your throat and then say: 'Give to so-and-so, give to so-and-so.'"

**8** It was narrated from Mu'adh ibn Jabal that the Prophet, peace be upon him, said: "Charity extinguishes sin as water extinguishes fire."
