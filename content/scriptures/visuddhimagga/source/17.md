---
title: "Visuddhimagga Chapter 17: Panna-bhumi-niddesa (The Soil of Understanding - Dependent Origination)"
scripture: "visuddhimagga"
chapter: 17
---

**1** Dependent origination is the most profound teaching of the Blessed One. It shows how all phenomena arise in dependence upon conditions and cease when those conditions cease. "When this exists, that comes to be; with the arising of this, that arises. When this does not exist, that does not come to be; with the cessation of this, that ceases."

**2** With ignorance as condition, formations arise. Ignorance is the non-knowledge of the Four Noble Truths. Because beings do not understand suffering, its origin, its cessation, and the path, they engage in wholesome and unwholesome formations.

**3** With formations as condition, consciousness arises. The consciousness conditioned by formations is the rebirth-consciousness — the first consciousness in a new existence, carrying the imprint of the kamma of the previous life.

**4** With consciousness as condition, name-and-form arise. Name is the mental aggregates: feeling, perception, mental formations, consciousness. Form is the material body. At conception, consciousness and name-and-form arise together in mutual dependence.

**5** With name-and-form as condition, the six sense-bases arise. As the embryo develops, the six sense-bases — eye, ear, nose, tongue, body, and mind — gradually come into being, providing the doors for contact with the world.

**6** With the six sense-bases as condition, contact arises. Contact is the meeting of the internal base, the external base, and consciousness. With contact as condition, feeling arises — pleasant, painful, or neutral.

**7** With feeling as condition, craving arises. When pleasant feeling arises, craving for more pleasure arises. When painful feeling arises, craving for the removal of pain arises. This craving is the origin of suffering.

**8** With craving as condition, clinging arises. Clinging is of four kinds: clinging to sensual pleasures, clinging to views, clinging to rules and rituals, and clinging to a doctrine of self. With clinging as condition, becoming arises — the process of generating a new existence.

**9** With becoming as condition, birth arises. Birth is the arising of the aggregates in a new existence. With birth as condition, ageing-and-death arise, and sorrow, lamentation, pain, grief, and despair. Thus is the arising of this whole mass of suffering.

**10** With the cessation of ignorance, formations cease. With the cessation of formations, consciousness ceases. And so on, through the entire chain, until: with the cessation of birth, ageing-and-death cease, and sorrow, lamentation, pain, grief, and despair cease. Thus is the cessation of this whole mass of suffering. This is the liberating insight of dependent origination.
