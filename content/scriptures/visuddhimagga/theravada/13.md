Theravada commentary on this chapter treats the complete Abhidhamma analysis of the aggregates of clinging. Buddhaghosa's presentation draws on the entire Pali canon and earlier Sinhalese commentaries to provide the most detailed and authoritative treatment of the systematic analysis of the five aggregates of experience available in the Theravada tradition. The chapter's systematic approach reflects Buddhaghosa's conviction that the path to liberation requires precise understanding of each component of practice.

For Theravada practitioners, this chapter provides essential guidance for cultivating the wisdom that constitutes the third stage of the threefold training. The detailed instructions have shaped Theravada meditation practice for over fifteen centuries, providing the framework within which teachers across Southeast Asia continue to guide their students.
