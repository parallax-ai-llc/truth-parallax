## Overview

The Comparative reading of 3 Enoch examines the text's angelic hierarchies, heavenly ascent narrative, and mediator-figure alongside parallel traditions. Metatron invites comparison with Gnostic demiurgical figures, the Islamic angel Jibril (Gabriel), and the Christian Logos. The Hekhalot architecture parallels the Gnostic aeon-system and Dante's celestial spheres. The heavenly liturgy resonates with Orthodox Christian and Islamic accounts of angelic worship.
