## Overview

The Interfaith reading of 3 Enoch explores the text as a meeting point for Jewish, Christian, Islamic, and broader mystical traditions. The figure of Metatron has parallels in the Christian Logos, the Islamic concept of the Nur Muhammadi (Light of Muhammad), and the Gnostic demiurge. The heavenly ascent motif is shared across all three Abrahamic traditions and beyond. This reading examines how 3 Enoch's themes of angelic mediation, divine vision, and cosmic order resonate across religious boundaries.
