## Overview

The Academic study of 3 Enoch situates the text within the broader corpus of Hekhalot and Merkabah literature, examining its date, provenance, literary structure, and relationship to earlier and later Jewish mystical traditions. Scholars analyze the text's complex redaction history, the development of the Enoch-Metatron identification, and the social setting of the mystical practitioners who produced and used these texts.
