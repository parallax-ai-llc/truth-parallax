---
title: "3 Enoch Section 11"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 11
---

**1** Rabbi Ishmael said: Metatron, the angel, the Prince of the Presence, said to me: "What is the measure of the Holy One, blessed be He, who is hidden from all creatures?

**2** The soles of His feet fill the whole world; their height is thirty million parasangs. The height of the right sole is thirty million parasangs, and the height of the left sole is thirty million parasangs.

**3** From the sole of His feet to His ankles is a journey of one hundred and fifty million parasangs. From His ankles to His knees is a journey of one hundred and ninety million parasangs.

**4** From His knees to His thighs is a journey of one hundred and eighty million parasangs. And thus for every limb, measure upon measure, beyond all reckoning.

**5** These measures are those of the body, but the measure of His glory who can know? The glory of His countenance who can comprehend? The measure of His beauty who can tell?"
