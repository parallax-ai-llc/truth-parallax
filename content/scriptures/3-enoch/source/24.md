---
title: "3 Enoch Section 24"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 24
---

**1** Rabbi Ishmael said: Metatron, the angel, the Prince of the Presence, said to me: "Come, and I will show thee the curtain of the Omnipresent One, which is spread before the Holy One, blessed be He,

**2** on which are engraved all the generations of the world and all their deeds, whether done or to be done, unto the last generation.

**3** And I went, and Metatron showed me, and I saw written thereon: Adam and his generation, their deeds and their thoughts; Noah and his generation, their deeds and their thoughts;

**4** the generation of the Flood and their deeds and their thoughts; Shem, Elam, Ashur, Arpachshad, and all their generations, and their deeds;

**5** Abraham and his generation, Isaac and his generation, Jacob and his generation, and all their deeds;

**6** the redemption of Israel in the days of the Messiah, the war of Gog and Magog, and the rest of the deeds and struggles which the nations of the world will do."
