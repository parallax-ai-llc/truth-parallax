---
title: "3 Enoch Section 7"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 7
---

**1** The Holy One, blessed be He, fashioned for me a kingly crown in which forty-nine refulgent stones were placed, each like the orb of the sun,

**2** and its brilliance went forth in the four directions of Arabot of the firmament, and in the seven heavens, and in the four directions of the world.

**3** And He set it upon my head and He called me "The Lesser YHWH" in the presence of His whole household in the height, as it is written: "My name is in him."

**4** He made for me a garment of glory on which all kinds of lights were set, and He clothed me in it. And He made for me a robe of honor in which brightness, splendor, brilliance, and luster of every kind were fixed.

**5** And all the secrets of the world and all the order of nature stood revealed before me as they stand revealed before the Creator. And from that time onward I looked and beheld deep secrets and wonderful mysteries.
