---
title: "3 Enoch Section 38"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 38
---

**1** Rabbi Ishmael said: Metatron, the angel, the Prince of the Presence, said to me: "Come, and I will show thee the right hand of the Omnipresent One, from which all manner of blessings flow.

**2** It is stretched out behind Him because of the destruction of the Holy Temple. And from each of its fingers flow four rivers of fire.

**3** These rivers pass through the gates of Gehinnom, through the abodes of death, through the gates of darkness, through the paths of the shadow of death.

**4** And these rivers of fire flow on and pass before Satan and his attendants, before Sammael and his attendants.

**5** They tremble and are terrified and overawed. And they fall upon their faces before the rivers of fire. For the fire of those rivers is greater than all the fire of Gehinnom."
