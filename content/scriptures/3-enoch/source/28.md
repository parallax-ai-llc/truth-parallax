---
title: "3 Enoch Section 28"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 28
---

**1** Rabbi Ishmael said: Metatron, the angel, the Prince of the Presence, said to me: "When the Holy One, blessed be He, sits on the Throne of Judgment,

**2** Justice stands on His right, and Mercy on His left, and Truth stands directly before Him.

**3** And when man enters before Him for judgment, a staff as it were of fire goes forth from the splendor of the Shekinah, and approaches the man.

**4** And the angels of mercy and the angels of justice write down the merits and the transgressions.

**5** And the Throne of Glory weighs out the merits against the transgressions, and the whole world is judged by lovingkindness.

**6** When the merits are found to be greater, mercy prevails; when the transgressions are found to be greater, justice prevails."
