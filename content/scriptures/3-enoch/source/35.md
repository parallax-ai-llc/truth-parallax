---
title: "3 Enoch Section 35"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 35
---

**1** Rabbi Ishmael said: Metatron, the angel, the Prince of the Presence, said to me: "There is a great and terrible prince whose name is Dumiel.

**2** He is the angel of silence, the prince appointed over Gehinnom. And a thousand thousand and a myriad of myriads of angels of destruction are under his authority.

**3** He stands at the gate of Gehinnom, and guards the gate. And in the hour when the wicked are delivered to Gehinnom, he takes them from the hand of the angels of mercy.

**4** Three times each day he casts them into the burning fire, and three times each day he brings them out again.

**5** And each time he brings them out, he leads them before the Holy One, blessed be He, and asks: 'What is Thy pleasure concerning these?' And the Holy One, blessed be He, answers according to His righteous judgment."
