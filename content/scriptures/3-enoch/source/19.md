---
title: "3 Enoch Section 19"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 19
---

**1** Rabbi Ishmael said: Metatron, the angel, the Prince of the Presence, said to me: "In each Raqia there are Chayyot standing over against each other. Above them are the wheels of the Merkabah.

**2** Between each Raqia and Raqia are doors of fire; doors of the whirlwind; doors of the tempest; doors of lightning; doors of flames; doors of fire; doors of thunders; doors of storms; doors of dread; doors of terror.

**3** At every door there are seven watchers standing at the opening; at each gate there are seven princes guarding the entrance.

**4** The height of each Raqia is a journey of five hundred years; and the width thereof is a journey of five hundred years.

**5** And between each Raqia and the next there is a space of a journey of five hundred years."
