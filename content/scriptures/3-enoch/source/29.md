---
title: "3 Enoch Section 29"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 29
---

**1** Rabbi Ishmael said: Metatron, the angel, the Prince of the Presence, said to me: "In the hour when the ministering angels wish to chant the Song,

**2** Hashmal of the consuming fire arises, and flames go forth from the river of fire.

**3** Between each angel and the next, fiery clouds and clouds of the consuming fire dwell.

**4** All the heavenly hosts tremble and fear, and the Chayyot retreat to their standing places, and the Ophanim are silent.

**5** And the Prince of the World proclaims: 'Be silent, all flesh, before the Lord, for He is raised up out of His holy habitation.'

**6** Then all the ministering angels offer their song and sing: 'Holy, holy, holy, is the Lord of Hosts; the whole earth is full of His glory.'"
