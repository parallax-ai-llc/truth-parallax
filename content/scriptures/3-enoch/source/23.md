---
title: "3 Enoch Section 23"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 23
---

**1** Rabbi Ishmael said: Metatron, the angel, the Prince of the Presence, said to me: "There are seventy-two names written on the Throne of Glory, for the seventy-two languages.

**2** And all the names that the Holy One, blessed be He, showed to Moses on Mount Sinai are all carved with a pen of flame on the Throne of Glory.

**3** And sparks go out from them and rays of splendor. And they cover all the chambers of Arabot.

**4** From each spark there go out three hundred and sixty-five thousand rays, and each ray is like the orb of Venus.

**5** And these sparks and rays encompass and surround the Holy One, blessed be He, from the right and from the left.

**6** And these are the explicit names that go out from the Throne of Glory, three times every day, from each spark, in Arabot of the firmament of the highest heaven."
