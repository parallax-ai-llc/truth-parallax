---
title: "3 Enoch Section 4"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 4
---

**1** At first I sat upon a great Throne at the door of the seventh hall, and I judged all the denizens of the heights on the authority of the Holy One, blessed be He.

**2** And I assigned greatness, royalty, rank, sovereignty, glory, praise, diadem, crown, and honor to all the princes of kingdoms, when I sat in the heavenly court.

**3** And the princes of kingdoms stood beside me, on my right and on my left, by authority of the Holy One, blessed be He.

**4** But when Acher came to behold the vision of the Merkabah and set eyes upon me, he was afraid and trembled before me.

**5** His soul was alarmed to the point of leaving him, because of the fear, dread, and terror of me, when he saw me seated upon a throne like a king, with ministering angels standing beside me as servants and all the princes of kingdoms crowned with crowns surrounding me.

**6** Then he opened his mouth and said: "There are indeed two powers in heaven!" Forthwith a divine voice went out from the presence of the Shekinah and said: "Come back to Me, ye backsliding children — except Acher."
