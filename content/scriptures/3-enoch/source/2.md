---
title: "3 Enoch Section 2"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 2
---

**1** In that moment the Holy One, blessed be He, opened to me three hundred and ten thousand gates of understanding, three hundred and ten thousand gates of prudence, three hundred and ten thousand gates of life, three hundred and ten thousand gates of grace and favor, three hundred and ten thousand gates of love, three hundred and ten thousand gates of Torah, three hundred and ten thousand gates of meekness, three hundred and ten thousand gates of sustenance, three hundred and ten thousand gates of mercy, three hundred and ten thousand gates of reverence.

**2** Forthwith I was strengthened and encouraged, and I stood upon my feet; and they sang praises before the Holy One, blessed be He, saying:

**3** "Blessed be the Lord, the God of Israel, from everlasting to everlasting, and blessed be the name of His glory for ever and ever."

**4** And from the splendor of the Shekinah, from the beauty of its glory, my face shone, and my eyes were strengthened, and all fear and trembling left me.

**5** And Metatron, the angel, the Prince of the Presence, said to me: "Behold, the merit of your prayers has prevailed before the throne of glory."
