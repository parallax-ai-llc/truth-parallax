---
title: "3 Enoch Section 34"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 34
---

**1** Rabbi Ishmael said: Metatron, the angel, the Prince of the Presence, said to me: "In every single heaven there is a prince who governs the angels of that heaven.

**2** And each prince has under him subordinate princes, who attend upon the host of that heaven.

**3** The prince distributes to each angel his duties, his watch, and his station, according to the glory of each angel and the rank of each prince.

**4** Those of higher rank stand closer to the Throne, those of lower rank stand farther away, but all serve with joy and gladness before the Holy One, blessed be He.

**5** And none disputes the authority of the prince set over him, for order is the foundation of the heavenly service."
