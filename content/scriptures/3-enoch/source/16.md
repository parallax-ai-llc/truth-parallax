---
title: "3 Enoch Section 16"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 16
---

**1** Rabbi Ishmael said: Metatron, the angel, the Prince of the Presence, said to me: "Before the Holy One, blessed be He, appointed me to attend the Throne of Glory,

**2** He opened to me three hundred thousand gates of prudence, three hundred thousand gates of subtlety, three hundred thousand gates of life, three hundred thousand gates of lovingkindness,

**3** three hundred thousand gates of love, three hundred thousand gates of Torah, three hundred thousand gates of meekness, three hundred thousand gates of sustenance, three hundred thousand gates of mercy.

**4** At that time the Holy One, blessed be He, appointed Metatron to be His servant over all the treasuries and storehouses of every heaven.

**5** The keys of each heaven He committed to his charge."
