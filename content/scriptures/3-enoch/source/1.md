---
title: "3 Enoch Section 1"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 1
---

**1** Rabbi Ishmael said: When I ascended on high to behold the vision of the Merkabah, and had entered the six halls, one within the other,

**2** as soon as I reached the door of the seventh hall, I stood still in prayer before the Holy One, blessed be He, and lifting up my eyes on high, I said:

**3** "Lord of the Universe, I pray Thee, that the merit of Aaron, the son of Amram, the lover of peace and pursuer of peace, who received the crown of priesthood from Thy Glory on Mount Sinai, be valid for me at this hour, so that Suriel, the Prince of the Presence, and the angels that are with him, may not get power over me and cast me down from the heavens."

**4** Forthwith the Holy One, blessed be He, sent to me Metatron, His servant, the angel, the Prince of the Presence, and he spread his wings and came out to meet me with great joy, in order to save me from their hand.

**5** And he took me by his hand before their eyes and said to me: "Enter in peace before the high and exalted King, to behold the likeness of the Merkabah."

**6** Then I entered the seventh hall, and he led me to the camps of the Shekinah and placed me before the Holy One, blessed be He, to behold the Merkabah.

**7** But when the princes of the Merkabah and the flaming Seraphim perceived me, they fixed their gaze upon me and lo, they trembled and fell back from me.
