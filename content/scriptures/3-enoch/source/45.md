---
title: "3 Enoch Section 45"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 45
---

**1** Rabbi Ishmael said: Metatron, the angel, the Prince of the Presence, said to me: "Come, and I will show thee the souls of the angels, how they stand before the Throne of Glory.

**2** And I looked and I saw the souls of the ministering angels standing on thrones of splendor by the river of fire.

**3** And their garments were garments of glory, and their crowns were crowns of majesty, and their bodies were full of eyes.

**4** And the light of each angel's countenance was like the light of the sun, and the brilliance of each one was like the splendor of the Throne of Glory.

**5** And they stood in rows, company after company, and praised the Holy One, blessed be He, with a single voice and a single utterance."
