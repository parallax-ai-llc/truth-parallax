---
title: "3 Enoch Section 3"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 3
---

**1** I asked Metatron and said to him: "Why art thou called by the name of thy Creator, by seventy names? Thou art greater than all the princes, more exalted than all the angels, more beloved than all the ministers."

**2** He answered and said to me: "Because I am Enoch, the son of Jared. When the generation of the Flood sinned and turned to evil deeds, and said to God: 'Depart from us, for we desire not the knowledge of Thy ways,'

**3** the Holy One, blessed be He, removed me from their midst to be a witness against them in the heavenly height to all the inhabitants of the world, that they may not say, 'The Merciful One is cruel.'

**4** What did the Holy One, blessed be He, do to me? He took me from the generation of the Flood, and carried me on the wings of the wind of Shekinah to the highest heaven, and brought me into the great palaces on the height of the seventh heaven Arabot,

**5** where are the Throne of the Shekinah and the Merkabah, the bands of anger and the hosts of wrath, the Shinanim of the fire, the Cherubim of the flaming torches, the Ophanim of the fiery coals, the servants of the flames, the Seraphim of the lightning, and where I was placed to attend the Throne of Glory day after day."
