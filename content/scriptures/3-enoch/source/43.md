---
title: "3 Enoch Section 43"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 43
---

**1** Rabbi Ishmael said: Metatron, the angel, the Prince of the Presence, said to me: "Come, and I will show thee the letters by which heaven and earth were created,

**2** the letters by which the seas and rivers were created, the letters by which the mountains and hills were created, the letters by which the trees and herbs were created,

**3** the letters by which the planets and the constellations were created, the letters by which the orb of the moon and the orb of the sun were created,

**4** the letters by which the ministering angels were created, the letters by which the Throne of Glory and the wheels of the Merkabah were created.

**5** All these letters are flaming and sparkling before the Throne of Glory, and they fly on the wings of the wind, and they circle the heavens in four hundred and ninety-six thousand myriads of camps."
