---
title: "3 Enoch Section 32"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 32
---

**1** Rabbi Ishmael said: Metatron, the angel, the Prince of the Presence, said to me: "In the hour when the ministering angels sing the Song, the heavenly streams and springs of fire and the rivers of fire and the springs of flame become strong with a great roar.

**2** Thousands of thousands and myriads of myriads of troops of fire and flame surround the Throne of Glory.

**3** And the Holy One, blessed be He, sits on the Throne, and the Throne flames with fire and sparks, and is all studded with sapphires and carbuncles.

**4** And around the Throne, covering it like a tent, stand columns of fire and streams of luminous light.

**5** And all the paths of the seventh heaven are ablaze with the splendor of the Shekinah."
