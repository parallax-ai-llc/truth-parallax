---
title: "3 Enoch Section 15"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 15
---

**1** Rabbi Ishmael said: Metatron, the angel, the Prince of the Presence, said to me: "When the Holy One, blessed be He, took me to serve the Throne of Glory and the wheels of the Merkabah and the needs of the Shekinah,

**2** forthwith my flesh turned to flame, my sinews to blazing fire, my bones to juniper coals, my eyelashes to lightning flashes, my eyeballs to fiery torches,

**3** the hairs of my head to hot flames, all my limbs to wings of burning fire, and the substance of my body to blazing fire.

**4** On my right those who cleave flames of fire, on my left burning brands, round about me swept wind, tempest, and storm; and the roar of earthquake upon earthquake was before and behind me.

**5** The Holy One, blessed be He, placed His hand upon me, and endowed me with one thousand three hundred and sixty-five thousand myriads of blessings,

**6** and I was raised and enlarged to the size of the length and width of the world."
