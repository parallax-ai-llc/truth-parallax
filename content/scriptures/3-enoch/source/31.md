---
title: "3 Enoch Section 31"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 31
---

**1** Rabbi Ishmael said: Metatron, the angel, the Prince of the Presence, said to me: "Whenever the great Beth Din sits in the Raqia of Arabot on high, none opens his mouth save those who utter the Song,

**2** for only the great ones who are called YHWH by the name of the Holy One, blessed be He, may open their mouths to give praise and song.

**3** They praise, laud, glorify, sing, extol, sanctify, and ascribe sovereignty to the name of the King of kings of kings, the Holy One, blessed be He.

**4** The first company begins and says: 'Let the name of His glorious kingdom be blessed for ever and ever.'

**5** And the second company answers and says: 'Holy, holy, holy, is the Lord of Hosts; the whole earth is full of His glory.'"
