---
title: "3 Enoch Section 39"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 39
---

**1** Rabbi Ishmael said: Metatron, the angel, the Prince of the Presence, said to me: "Come, and I will show thee the right hand of the Omnipresent One, which is stretched out behind Him.

**2** From it all manner of blessings flow forth upon Israel and upon the righteous of the world, and from it streams of lovingkindness issue.

**3** And there are three hundred and sixty-five thousand ministering angels who stand by it, and they protect the right hand of the Holy One, blessed be He.

**4** And the right hand of the Holy One, blessed be He, will in the future be revealed to the nations of the world.

**5** And when it is revealed, the nations shall tremble and fall, but Israel shall rejoice, for the redemption shall come upon them."
