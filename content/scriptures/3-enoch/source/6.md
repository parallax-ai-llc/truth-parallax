---
title: "3 Enoch Section 6"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 6
---

**1** When the Holy One, blessed be He, took me to serve the Throne of Glory, the wheels of the Merkabah, and all the needs of the Shekinah,

**2** at once my flesh turned to flame, my sinews to blazing fire, my bones to juniper coals, my eyelashes to lightning flashes, my eyeballs to fiery torches,

**3** the hairs of my head to hot flames, all my limbs to wings of burning fire, and the substance of my body to blazing fire.

**4** On my right those who cleave flames of fire, on my left burning brands, round about me swept wind, tempest, and storm; and the roar of earthquake upon earthquake was before and behind me.

**5** And the Holy One, blessed be He, set me on a throne, and made a proclamation concerning me in every heaven saying: "I have appointed My servant Metatron as prince and ruler over all the princes of My kingdoms and over all the children of heaven,

**6** excepting only the eight great princes, the honored and revered ones, who are called by the name of the Holy One, blessed be He, by the name which comprises seventy names."
