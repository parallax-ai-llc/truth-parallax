---
title: "3 Enoch Section 37"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 37
---

**1** Rabbi Ishmael said: Metatron, the angel, the Prince of the Presence, said to me: "Come, and I will show thee the souls of the wicked and the souls of the sinners."

**2** And he showed me the souls of the wicked, and I beheld them descending to Sheol.

**3** And I saw the wicked standing at the gates of Gehinnom, and the angels of destruction smiting them with blows of fire.

**4** And the sinners cried out and said: "Woe unto us! Would that we had never been created, that we had never come forth from the womb!"

**5** And I saw that each punishment was measured according to the transgressions, and each sentence was decreed according to the deeds done in the flesh."
