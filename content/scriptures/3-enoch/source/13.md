---
title: "3 Enoch Section 13"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 13
---

**1** Rabbi Ishmael said: Metatron, the angel, the Prince of the Presence, said to me: "Above the Chayyot there is one great prince, dread and honored, mighty and exalted, revered, ancient, and powerful.

**2** His name is Keraspiel YHWH. He is a great prince who causes the Shekinah to dwell in the seventh heaven.

**3** He ministers before the Throne of Glory and puts order among the holy Chayyot. All the princes of the Merkabah who walk with the Shekinah, he causes to stand before the Throne of Glory.

**4** He arranges the Throne, puts order among the wheels of the Merkabah, and attends to the needs of the Shekinah.

**5** And why is he called Keraspiel? Because the crown of sovereignty which is on his head is like the crown of the Holy One, blessed be He."
