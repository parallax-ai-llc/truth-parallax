---
title: "3 Enoch Section 30"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 30
---

**1** Rabbi Ishmael said: Metatron, the angel, the Prince of the Presence, said to me: "The angels of the first heaven, when they behold their prince, they dismount from their horses and fall on their faces.

**2** And the prince of the first heaven, when he beholds the prince of the second heaven, he removes the glorious crown from his head and falls prostrate.

**3** And the prince of the second heaven, when he beholds the prince of the third heaven, he removes the glorious crown from his head and falls prostrate.

**4** And so each prince in succession, from heaven to heaven, until the seventh heaven, the highest.

**5** And the prince of the seventh heaven, when he beholds the Seraphim, he removes his crown and falls prostrate, for the ministering angels are greater than the princes of kingdoms."
