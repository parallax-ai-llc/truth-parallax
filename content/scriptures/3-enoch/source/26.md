---
title: "3 Enoch Section 26"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 26
---

**1** Rabbi Ishmael said: Metatron, the angel, the Prince of the Presence, said to me: "Come, and I will show thee where the spirits of the wicked stand, and where the spirits of the wicked descend."

**2** And he showed me, and I saw the angels of destruction standing by the rivers of fire at the gate of Gehinnom.

**3** Their height is like the height of mountains. Their breadth is as great as a wide valley. Their faces are like the faces of vipers.

**4** In their hands they hold rods of fire. They smite the spirits of the wicked and drive them into Gehinnom.

**5** And from each stroke there goes forth a blast of fire that consumes a thousand mountains, and the sound of the strokes reaches from one end of the world to the other."
