---
title: "3 Enoch Section 21"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 21
---

**1** Rabbi Ishmael said: Metatron, the angel, the Prince of the Presence, said to me: "The Chayyot that bear the Throne of Glory — there are four Chayyot standing by each Throne, and each Chayyot has four faces and each one has four wings.

**2** How many are the wings that the world contains? There are in all six hundred and four thousand and eight wings, corresponding to the six hundred and four thousand and eight letters of the Torah.

**3** And when the time draws nigh for the recitation of the Song, all the Chayyot stand up with their wings on high and with their wings they chant the Song to the Holy One, blessed be He.

**4** Some sing: 'Holy, holy, holy, is the Lord of Hosts; the whole earth is full of His glory.' And some cry: 'Blessed be the glory of the Lord from His place.'

**5** And some answer: 'The Lord shall reign for ever and ever.'"
