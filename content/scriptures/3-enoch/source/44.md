---
title: "3 Enoch Section 44"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 44
---

**1** Rabbi Ishmael said: Metatron, the angel, the Prince of the Presence, said to me: "Come, and I will show thee the generations of the future, what shall befall Israel in each generation."

**2** And he took me and showed me, and I saw the princes of Israel in each generation, and I saw the tribulations and the sorrows that would come upon Israel in every age.

**3** And I saw the teachers of Torah in every generation, and the scribes and the righteous, and the wicked, and the merits and the transgressions of each.

**4** And Metatron said to me: "All this is written in the book of records that stands before the Throne of Glory."

**5** And I said: "How long shall these things last?" And he answered: "Until the time is fulfilled and the measure is complete."
