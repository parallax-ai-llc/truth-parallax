---
title: "3 Enoch Section 48"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 48
---

**1** Rabbi Ishmael said: Metatron, the angel, the Prince of the Presence, said to me: "The Holy One, blessed be He, revealed to me from that time forth all the mysteries of the Torah, all the secrets of wisdom, all the depths of the perfect law, and all the thoughts of the hearts of living creatures.

**2** And all the mysteries of the world, and all the orders of nature stood open before me, as they stand open before the Creator.

**3** And from that time onward I looked and beheld deep secrets and wonderful mysteries. Before a man thinks in secret, I see his thought; before he acts, I see his act. There is nothing in heaven above or deep beneath that is hidden from me.

**4** And I saw the light that was before the world was created. I saw the light of the Torah that was given on Sinai. I saw the light of the Messiah and of his generation.

**5** And all of these are concealed in the palaces of the seventh heaven of Arabot, and all of them are hidden under the Throne of Glory.

**6** Blessed be the name of His glorious kingdom for ever and ever. Amen and Amen."
