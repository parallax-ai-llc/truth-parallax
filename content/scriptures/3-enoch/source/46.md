---
title: "3 Enoch Section 46"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 46
---

**1** Rabbi Ishmael said: Metatron, the angel, the Prince of the Presence, said to me: "Come, and I will show thee what is above and what is beneath, what is before and what is behind, what was and what will be.

**2** And I looked and saw the workmanship of the world, from the beginning to the end, from the uppermost height to the nethermost depth.

**3** And I beheld the arrangement of the luminaries, the course of the sun and the path of the moon, the circuit of the stars and the revolution of the constellations.

**4** All was ordered by the word of the Holy One, blessed be He, and nothing was out of place.

**5** And I saw that all the works of creation testify to the glory of their Creator, and all the hosts of heaven declare the work of His hands."
