---
title: "3 Enoch Section 20"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 20
---

**1** Rabbi Ishmael said: Metatron, the angel, the Prince of the Presence, said to me: "In Arabot, in the seventh heaven, there are nine hundred and fifty-five thousand myriads of chariots, like the chariots of fire.

**2** And in each chariot there are four horses of fire, and in each horse's mouth there are four thousand fires, and in each fire there are four thousand coals.

**3** And each coal casts out four thousand flames, and each flame casts out four thousand burning embers.

**4** From each burning ember there goes forth a flash of lightning, and between one flash and the next, clouds of loving-kindness dwell.

**5** And the flashes of lightning surround them, and clouds of mercy encircle them. And this is what stands before the Throne of Glory."
