---
title: "3 Enoch Section 47"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 47
---

**1** Rabbi Ishmael said: Metatron, the angel, the Prince of the Presence, said to me: "In the time to come, the Holy One, blessed be He, will sit in the heavenly court, and the books of the living and the books of the dead will be opened before Him.

**2** And all the hosts of the height will stand before Him in awe and dread and trembling.

**3** And the Holy One, blessed be He, will sit on the Throne of Judgment, clothed in the garment of judgment, and will judge all the world with justice and the inhabitants of the world with equity.

**4** And the ministering angels will speak before Him, each bearing witness concerning the deeds of men.

**5** And Michael, the great prince, will open the book of merits, and Gabriel will open the book of transgressions, and the scales of justice will be set up before the Throne of Glory.

**6** And a single word shall not be overlooked, neither for good nor for ill."
