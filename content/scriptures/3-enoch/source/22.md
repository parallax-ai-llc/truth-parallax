---
title: "3 Enoch Section 22"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 22
---

**1** Rabbi Ishmael said: Metatron, the angel, the Prince of the Presence, said to me: "The Ophanim surround the Throne of the Shekinah on every side.

**2** And the Seraphim surround the Ophanim, and the Chayyot surround the Seraphim, on every side. And the ministering angels surround all of them.

**3** And the Holy One, blessed be He, sits on the Throne, high and exalted, in the highest height, and His great glory adorns the Throne of Glory.

**4** And when the Chayyot see the likeness of the Throne of Glory, they fall upon their faces and recite: 'Blessed be the name of His glorious kingdom for ever and ever.'

**5** And the Ophanim, when they behold the holy Chayyot, they fall from the height of their standing. And when they fall, the ten firmaments tremble at the resounding of their fall."
