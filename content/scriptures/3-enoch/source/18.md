---
title: "3 Enoch Section 18"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 18
---

**1** Rabbi Ishmael said: Metatron, the angel, the Prince of the Presence, said to me: "In each heaven there are seventy-two princes of kingdoms, besides the prince of the world who speaks in favor of the world before the Holy One, blessed be He, every day,

**2** at the hour when the book is opened in which is recorded every deed done in the world. As it is written: 'And the books were opened.'

**3** And there are seventy-two princes of kingdoms on high corresponding to the seventy-two nations in the world.

**4** And all of them are crowned with royal crowns, and all of them are clothed in royal vestments.

**5** And all of them ride on royal horses, and all of them hold royal scepters in their hands. And before each one of them, when he travels in the Raqia, royal servants run, just as upon earth."
