---
title: "3 Enoch Section 25"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 25
---

**1** Rabbi Ishmael said: Metatron, the angel, the Prince of the Presence, said to me: "Come, and I will show thee the spirits of the righteous who have already been created and have returned, and the spirits of the righteous who have not yet been created."

**2** And he carried me and showed me, and I saw splendor, pomp, majesty, glory, beauty, grace, and lovingkindness in their faces.

**3** And the spirits of the righteous sat adorned in great glory, with crowns on their heads.

**4** And I asked Metatron: "Whose are these?" He answered: "These are the spirits of the righteous who dwell in the Garden of Eden, and who have been fashioned in the Workshop of the Holy One, blessed be He.

**5** These are destined to revive the dead in the days of the Messiah."
