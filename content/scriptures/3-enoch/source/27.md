---
title: "3 Enoch Section 27"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 27
---

**1** Rabbi Ishmael said: Metatron, the angel, the Prince of the Presence, said to me: "Come, and I will show thee the judgment that the Holy One, blessed be He, executes upon the hosts of the height.

**2** In the hour when the ministering angels wish to sing the Song, the fiery river Dinur rises with its great flames and with mighty force,

**3** and with a tremendous noise it falls upon the heads of the wicked who are judged in Gehinnom.

**4** The wicked tremble and fear, and they are brought down to the lowest abyss. And their bodies and souls are consumed in rivers of flame.

**5** But the Holy One, blessed be He, saves the righteous from this judgment and shelters them in the shadow of His lovingkindness."
