---
title: "3 Enoch Section 42"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 42
---

**1** Rabbi Ishmael said: Metatron, the angel, the Prince of the Presence, said to me: "Come, and I will show thee where the souls of the righteous are fashioned, and where the souls of the wicked are cast down.

**2** The souls of the righteous, who are to be created in the future, are fashioned in the seventh heaven, in Arabot.

**3** And the spirits and the souls that are yet to be created stand in the treasury of souls in the seventh heaven.

**4** And when the time comes for each soul to descend into the world, the Holy One, blessed be He, commands Metatron, and Metatron delivers the soul to Gabriel, and Gabriel to the angel appointed over the womb.

**5** And the soul is shown all that it will experience from the day of its birth to the day of its death, and all is done according to the decree of the Almighty."
