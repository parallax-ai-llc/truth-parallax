---
title: "3 Enoch Section 40"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 40
---

**1** Rabbi Ishmael said: Metatron, the angel, the Prince of the Presence, said to me: "Come, and I will show thee the spirits of the stars.

**2** All of them stand in the Raqia of the heaven, bound with chains of fire, with fetters of flame.

**3** And they are the stars which transgressed the commandment of the Omnipresent One before the Flood, and were punished. And He set them in chains until their iniquity is completed.

**4** And they stand at the places of fire and at the places of the whirlwind, some of them in rivers of fire, and some of them in mountains of fire, and some of them in hills of fire.

**5** And each one of them is greater in size than the whole world. Each one has seventy thousand chains upon it. And each chain weighs one hundred and twenty-one thousand talents of iron."
