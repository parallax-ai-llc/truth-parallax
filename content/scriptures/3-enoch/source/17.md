---
title: "3 Enoch Section 17"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 17
---

**1** Rabbi Ishmael said: Metatron, the angel, the Prince of the Presence, the glory of all heaven, said to me: "There are seven great, beautiful, wonderful, and honored princes who are appointed over the seven heavens. They are:

**2** Michael, Gabriel, Shatqiel, Baradiel, Shachaqiel, Baraqiel, and Sidriel.

**3** Each one of them is the prince of one heaven. And each one of them is accompanied by four hundred and ninety-six thousand myriads of ministering angels.

**4** Michael, the great prince, is appointed over the seventh heaven, the highest, which is in Arabot.

**5** Gabriel, the prince of the host, is appointed over the sixth heaven, which is in Makon.

**6** Shatqiel, the prince of the host, is appointed over the fifth heaven, which is in Maon.

**7** Baradiel, the prince of the host, is appointed over the fourth heaven, which is in Zebul.

**8** Shachaqiel, the prince of the host, is appointed over the third heaven, which is in Shechaqim.

**9** Baraqiel, the prince of the host, is appointed over the second heaven, which is in the height of Raqia.

**10** Sidriel, the prince of the host, is appointed over the first heaven, which is in Shamayim, the lowest."
