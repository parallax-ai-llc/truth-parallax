---
title: "3 Enoch Section 36"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 36
---

**1** Rabbi Ishmael said: Metatron, the angel, the Prince of the Presence, said to me: "Come, and I will show thee the souls of the righteous who have already been created, and the souls of those who have not yet been born."

**2** And he took me by his hand, and we ascended, and he showed me the souls of the righteous, each in its appointed place and in its position of glory.

**3** And I saw the souls of the Patriarchs, Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob, and the souls of the righteous sitting in great glory in the Garden of Eden.

**4** And all the righteous souls were adorned with crowns of light and clothed in garments of glory.

**5** And I asked: "How long must the souls of the righteous wait until the time of the resurrection?" And he answered: "Until the number of the righteous is complete."
