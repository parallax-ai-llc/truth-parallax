---
title: "3 Enoch Section 12"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 12
---

**1** Rabbi Ishmael said: Metatron, the angel, the Prince of the Presence, the glory of all heaven, said to me:

**2** "The Throne of Glory is set on high and exalted over all at the gate of the seventh hall, and it rises above all the holy Chayyot by seventy thousand parasangs on high.

**3** The King of all kings, the Holy One, blessed be He, sits upon it, and His robe fills the whole Temple, as it is said: 'His train filled the Temple.'

**4** The appearance of His countenance is like the appearance of two Cherubim. And the crown on His head is like the brightness of the orb of the sun.

**5** The brilliance of His glory shines from the highest heavens. From the seven abysses, from the height of the heights, from the dwelling place on high, the splendor of His majesty streams forth."
