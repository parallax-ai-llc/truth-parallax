---
title: "3 Enoch Section 14"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 14
---

**1** Rabbi Ishmael said: Metatron, the angel, the Prince of the Presence, said to me: "There is one great prince in the highest heaven whose name is Gallisur YHWH.

**2** He reveals all the secrets of the Torah, and the secrets of all mysteries are placed in his hand. And every day he stands in attendance before the Holy One, blessed be He.

**3** He unrolls the book of records and reads all that is written therein, and he delivers the decree of the Holy One, blessed be He.

**4** When he opens the book, all the heavenly hosts are silenced, and the ministering angels become mute. When he speaks, fire goes forth from his mouth.

**5** The words that he speaks are as coals of fire, and from the words of his mouth lightnings flash forth, and it causes those who hear it to tremble."
