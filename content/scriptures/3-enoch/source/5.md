---
title: "3 Enoch Section 5"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 5
---

**1** Then Anaphiel, the Prince, the honored, glorified, beloved, wonderful, terrible, and dread one, was sent by command from the Holy One, blessed be He.

**2** He came and smote me with sixty lashes of fire and made me stand on my feet.

**3** But I was not worthy of this punishment, for it was done to make atonement for what Acher had said, that there are two powers in heaven.

**4** After the Holy One, blessed be He, had taken me from among the generation of the Flood, He carried me on the wings of the Shekinah to the highest heaven, to be a witness to all the generations that would arise after the Flood.

**5** And the Holy One, blessed be He, assigned me to attend the Throne of Glory to keep guard over the wheels of the Merkabah and all the holy creatures.
