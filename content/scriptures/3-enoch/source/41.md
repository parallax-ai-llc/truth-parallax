---
title: "3 Enoch Section 41"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 41
---

**1** Rabbi Ishmael said: Metatron, the angel, the Prince of the Presence, said to me: "Come, and I will show thee where the waters above are suspended.

**2** And I looked and saw that the waters above were suspended in the height of Arabot by the name of the Holy One, blessed be He.

**3** They are suspended in the upper air and cover the upper heavens. And their height is like the height of the whole world, and their width is like the width of the whole world.

**4** And from them the rain descends upon the world, as it is written: 'Who watereth the mountains from His upper chambers.'

**5** And all the springs, and all the rivers, and all the brooks, and all the streams go forth from them, and they flow on and water the whole face of the earth."
