---
title: "3 Enoch Section 33"
scripture: "3-enoch"
chapter: 33
---

**1** Rabbi Ishmael said: Metatron, the angel, the Prince of the Presence, said to me: "At the time when the ministering angels offer the Qedushah,

**2** all the pillars of the heavens and their sockets tremble, and the gates of the halls of Arabot of the firmament shake, and the foundations of Sheol and Abaddon are shaken.

**3** The world totters, and all the dead who are in Sheol hear and tremble, and all the wicked who are in Gehinnom cry out.

**4** And the seas and rivers flee, and the great sea roars, and Leviathan trembles. The whole world is shaken.

**5** But the Holy One, blessed be He, says: 'Do not fear, for I am with you. Be not dismayed, for I am your God.'"
