## Overview

The Catholic reading of 3 Enoch engages with the text's detailed angelic hierarchies and heavenly liturgy in light of the Catholic tradition of angelological reflection, particularly as developed by Pseudo-Dionysius the Areopagite. The text's nine-fold angelic hierarchy (though differently organized) resonates with the Dionysian system that became normative in Catholic theology. The heavenly Kedushah (trisagion) connects to the Sanctus of the Mass.
