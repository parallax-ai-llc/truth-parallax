## Overview

The Eastern Orthodox reading of 3 Enoch resonates with core Orthodox theological themes: the vision of divine glory, the angelic liturgy that the earthly liturgy mirrors, and the transformative encounter with the divine presence. The text's description of Enoch's transformation into a being of fire and light parallels Orthodox accounts of theosis and the experience of uncreated light in the hesychast tradition.
