This section addresses the sacred stone tablets and petroglyphs that encode Hopi prophetic knowledge. The most well-known is Prophecy Rock near Oraibi, which depicts two paths for humanity — the path of materialism leading to destruction, and the path of spiritual balance leading to survival. The stone tablets given to the Hopi at the time of emergence serve as title deeds to the land and as prophetic roadmaps, with specific symbols representing future events and choices facing humanity.

From an Indigenous perspective, The Prophecy Stones and Tablets carries living significance for the community's ongoing spiritual and cultural practice. This is not a text to be analyzed from the outside but a sacred narrative that continues to inform daily life, ceremonial practice, and the community's relationship with the land and with each other. The knowledge contained in this section is maintained by specific custodians and transmitted through established protocols that ensure its integrity across generations.

Indigenous engagement emphasizes the inseparability of this narrative from the broader web of cultural practice, language, and identity. The themes addressed here are not abstract philosophical concepts but practical guides for living in right relationship with the community, the natural world, and the spiritual forces that sustain existence.
