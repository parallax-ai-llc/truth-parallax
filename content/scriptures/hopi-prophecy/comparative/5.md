This section catalogs the prophetic signs that indicate the approach of the Great Purification (Day of Purification). These signs include the coming of white-skinned people, the appearance of 'cobwebs in the sky' (power lines), roads like rivers of stone (highways), the 'gourd of ashes' (nuclear weapons), and other specific predictions that Hopi elders have identified as having been fulfilled. The accumulation of these signs indicates humanity's proximity to a critical turning point.

Comparative analysis of The Signs of Purification reveals both parallels with and distinctive departures from similar themes in other world traditions. The motifs and structures found in this section connect to widespread patterns in human religious and mythological expression while retaining features that are specific to this particular cultural and spiritual context.

Cross-cultural comparison illuminates the universal dimensions of the human religious imagination while also highlighting the unique contributions of this tradition to the global tapestry of spiritual thought. However, comparative analysis must be conducted with care to avoid reducing distinctive traditions to mere instances of universal patterns, thereby obscuring what is most valuable and specific in each tradition.
