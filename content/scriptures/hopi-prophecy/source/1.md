---
title: "Hopi Prophecy - Chapter 1"
scripture: "hopi-prophecy"
chapter: 1
---

**1** The Hopi were told by the Great Spirit Massau'u at the time of their emergence from the underworld: "You are to be the caretakers of this land. Guard it well, for it is sacred."

**2** In the beginning, the Creator made the earth and all living things upon it. He created man and woman, and gave them instructions for living upon the land.

**3** The Creator said: "I am going to give you the knowledge and the wisdom to live in harmony with all of creation. Do not forget these teachings."

**4** He divided the people into four groups, each given a different color of skin and each sent to a different direction, and each given a guardian spirit and a set of stone tablets.

**5** To the Hopi, the Guardian of the South, were given the sacred stone tablets upon which were inscribed the laws and prophecies of the Creator.

**6** The Creator told the people: "If you live according to these instructions, you will live long and happy lives. But if you forget them, you will bring destruction upon yourselves."

**7** The first world was called Tokpela, the Endless Space. In it the people were pure and lived in harmony with the Creator. But eventually they forgot his teachings and the world was destroyed by fire.

**8** The second world was called Tokpa, the Dark Midnight. Again the people forgot the teachings, and this world was destroyed by ice.

**9** The third world was called Kuskurza. The people built great cities and developed many technologies, but they became wicked and warlike. This world was destroyed by a great flood.

**10** The fourth world, Tuwaqachi, the Complete World, is the world in which we now live. The Creator warned: "This is the last world. If you destroy this one, there will be no more."
