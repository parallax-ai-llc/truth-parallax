---
title: "Hopi Prophecy - Chapter 8"
scripture: "hopi-prophecy"
chapter: 8
---

**1** The final teaching of the elders concerns the fifth world that is to come after the purification. This world will be a world of peace, harmony, and spiritual understanding.

**2** In the fifth world, the people will once again live according to the Creator's plan. They will walk softly upon the earth and take only what they need.

**3** The corn will grow tall and strong, and the rains will come in their proper seasons. The springs will flow with clear water, and the land will be green and abundant.

**4** There will be no more war, for the people will have learned that war destroys both the victor and the vanquished. They will settle their differences through council and prayer.

**5** The animals will no longer fear man, for man will have learned to live in harmony with all creatures. The eagle will nest upon the mesa, and the deer will drink from the spring without fear.

**6** The children will be taught the old ways: how to plant, how to pray, how to listen to the earth. And the elders will be honored for their wisdom.

**7** The ceremonies will be performed with renewed devotion, and the kachinas will dance with great joy, for the people will have returned to the path of the Creator.

**8** All the races of mankind will come together as one family, sharing their gifts and their knowledge. The circle of life will be complete.

**9** The elders say: "We do not know when the purification will come. It may come in our lifetime, or in our children's lifetime, or in a time far distant. But it will come."

**10** "Therefore, live each day as if it were the last. Plant your corn. Say your prayers. Love your children. Care for your elders. Walk in beauty. Walk in balance. Walk in the way of the Creator. This is the Hopi way. This is the way of life."
