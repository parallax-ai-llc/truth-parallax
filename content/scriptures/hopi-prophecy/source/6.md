---
title: "Hopi Prophecy - Chapter 6"
scripture: "hopi-prophecy"
chapter: 6
---

**1** The elders spoke of the Pahana, the lost white brother, who would return in the time of the purification. He would come bearing the missing corner of the sacred stone tablet.

**2** "When the Pahana returns, he will bring with him the dawn of the fifth world. He will come from the east, and his coming will be like the rising of the sun."

**3** "But beware of the false Pahana. Many will come claiming to be the true brother, but they will bring only destruction. The true Pahana will be known by the stone tablet he carries."

**4** "If the true Pahana comes, he will bring the two great helpers: one who bears the swastika, the symbol of purity and the four directions, and one who bears the sun, the symbol of truth."

**5** "The true Pahana will not come with weapons. He will come with wisdom and compassion. He will restore the balance that has been lost."

**6** "The Hopi were told to wait for the Pahana and to keep the sacred tablets safe until his coming. They were told not to resist the invaders with violence, but to hold fast to the way of peace."

**7** "For violence breeds only more violence, and hatred breeds only more hatred. But peace and prayer have the power to heal the world."

**8** "The Creator said: 'Be patient, my children. The dawn is coming. The long night of suffering will end, and a new day will begin.'"

**9** "When the purification is complete, the good people of all nations will come together. They will form one family, as it was in the beginning."

**10** "The earth will be renewed. The rivers will run clean. The air will be pure. The animals will return. And the people will live in peace, as the Creator intended."
