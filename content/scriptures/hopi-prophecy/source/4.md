---
title: "Hopi Prophecy - Chapter 4"
scripture: "hopi-prophecy"
chapter: 4
---

**1** The elders spoke of signs that would come before the great purification. These signs were given so that the people might prepare themselves.

**2** The first sign: "A white-skinned people will come to this land. They will strike the earth with thunder sticks and claim the land as their own."

**3** The second sign: "The land will be crossed by spinning wheels filled with voices." This was the coming of the settlers in their covered wagons.

**4** The third sign: "A strange beast like a buffalo but with great long horns will overrun the land in large numbers." This was the longhorn cattle of the ranchers.

**5** The fourth sign: "The land will be crossed by snakes of iron." This was the coming of the railroad.

**6** The fifth sign: "The land shall be criss-crossed by a giant spider's web." This was the coming of the telephone and power lines.

**7** The sixth sign: "The land shall be criss-crossed with rivers of stone that make pictures in the sun." This was the coming of the concrete highways and their mirages.

**8** The seventh sign: "You will hear of the sea turning black, and many living things dying because of it." This was the oil spills upon the ocean.

**9** The eighth sign: "You will see many youth, who wear their hair long like our people, come and join the tribal nations, to learn our ways and wisdom."

**10** The ninth and final sign: "You will hear of a dwelling-place in the heavens, above the earth, that shall fall with a great crash. It will appear as a blue star."
