---
title: "Hopi Prophecy - Chapter 5"
scripture: "hopi-prophecy"
chapter: 5
---

**1** The elders spoke of two paths that lie before humanity. One path is the path of the Creator, the path of harmony and balance. The other is the path of materialism and destruction.

**2** On the sacred tablets, two paths are drawn. The upper path is jagged and uncertain, leading to destruction. The lower path is smooth and even, leading to everlasting life.

**3** Those who follow the upper path will build great things, but they will lose touch with the earth. Their heads will be separated from their bodies, meaning they will lose their connection to the spiritual.

**4** Those who follow the lower path will remain close to the earth. They will live simply and in harmony with creation. Their path leads to the future.

**5** The elders said: "A time will come when the people must choose between the two paths. This choice will determine the fate of the world."

**6** "If humanity chooses the path of materialism, the earth will be purified by great suffering. The purification will come through fire, flood, earthquake, and storm."

**7** "But if humanity returns to the path of the Creator, the purification will be gentle, and a new era of peace and harmony will begin."

**8** "The choice is not made by governments or nations, but by each individual. Each person must look into their own heart and choose which path to walk."

**9** The elders taught: "The time of choosing is upon us. Look at the signs around you. The earth is speaking. The weather is changing. The animals are disappearing. These are the warnings."

**10** "Return to the old ways. Plant your corn. Pray to the Creator. Live simply. Care for the earth. This is the only way to survive the great purification."
