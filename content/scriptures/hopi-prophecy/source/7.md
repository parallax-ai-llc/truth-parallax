---
title: "Hopi Prophecy - Chapter 7"
scripture: "hopi-prophecy"
chapter: 7
---

**1** The elders spoke of the house of mica, a great building where the leaders of all nations would gather to speak of peace. This was the United Nations.

**2** "The Hopi were told to go to this house of mica and deliver their message to the world. Four times they must try to enter, and four times they may be turned away."

**3** "But on the fourth attempt, the door will open, and the world will hear the message of the Creator. The Hopi must not give up."

**4** "The message is this: Return to the spiritual path. Abandon the ways of materialism and greed. Care for the earth, for the earth is your mother."

**5** "All the races of mankind were created by one Creator. All are brothers and sisters. The divisions between nations and peoples are illusions created by man, not by God."

**6** "The earth does not belong to man. Man belongs to the earth. Whatever befalls the earth befalls the children of the earth. If men spit upon the ground, they spit upon themselves."

**7** "The Hopi carry this message not because they think they are better than others, but because this is the responsibility given to them by the Creator. Every people has a responsibility."

**8** "The people of the four directions must come together and share their knowledge. The red people have the knowledge of the earth. The yellow people have the knowledge of the wind. The black people have the knowledge of the water. The white people have the knowledge of the fire."

**9** "When all four types of knowledge are combined, a great power will be released that can heal the world. But if this knowledge is used for destruction, the world will end."

**10** "This is why the Hopi pray. This is why the ceremonies continue. This is why the corn is planted. For as long as the Hopi pray and plant, there is hope for the world."
