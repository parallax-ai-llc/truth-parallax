This section catalogs the prophetic signs that indicate the approach of the Great Purification (Day of Purification). These signs include the coming of white-skinned people, the appearance of 'cobwebs in the sky' (power lines), roads like rivers of stone (highways), the 'gourd of ashes' (nuclear weapons), and other specific predictions that Hopi elders have identified as having been fulfilled. The accumulation of these signs indicates humanity's proximity to a critical turning point.

Interfaith engagement with The Signs of Purification offers opportunities for cross-traditional dialogue on shared human concerns expressed through this tradition's distinctive spiritual vocabulary. The themes addressed in this section — creation, transformation, moral order, the relationship between humans and the natural world — are concerns that resonate across the world's spiritual traditions while being expressed here in uniquely powerful ways.

Respectful interfaith dialogue with this material requires acknowledging both the common ground it shares with other traditions and its irreducible distinctiveness. When approached with humility and genuine openness, this section offers resources for enriching one's own spiritual understanding while deepening appreciation for the breadth and depth of human spiritual experience.
