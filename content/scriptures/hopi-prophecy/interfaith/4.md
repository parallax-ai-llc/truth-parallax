This section addresses the sacred stone tablets and petroglyphs that encode Hopi prophetic knowledge. The most well-known is Prophecy Rock near Oraibi, which depicts two paths for humanity — the path of materialism leading to destruction, and the path of spiritual balance leading to survival. The stone tablets given to the Hopi at the time of emergence serve as title deeds to the land and as prophetic roadmaps, with specific symbols representing future events and choices facing humanity.

Interfaith engagement with The Prophecy Stones and Tablets offers opportunities for cross-traditional dialogue on shared human concerns expressed through this tradition's distinctive spiritual vocabulary. The themes addressed in this section — creation, transformation, moral order, the relationship between humans and the natural world — are concerns that resonate across the world's spiritual traditions while being expressed here in uniquely powerful ways.

Respectful interfaith dialogue with this material requires acknowledging both the common ground it shares with other traditions and its irreducible distinctiveness. When approached with humility and genuine openness, this section offers resources for enriching one's own spiritual understanding while deepening appreciation for the breadth and depth of human spiritual experience.
