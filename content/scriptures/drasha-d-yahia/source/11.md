---
title: "Drasha d-Yahia Section 11"
scripture: "drasha-d-yahia"
chapter: 11
---

**1** Yahia speaks concerning the rites of the Mandaeans. He says: These are the commandments which the Great Life has given through me.

**2** The baptism in the Living Water: let it be performed in flowing water, in a river or a stream. Let the priest immerse the believer three times, speaking the sacred formulas.

**3** The masiqta, the ascent of the soul: when a Mandaean dies, let the priest perform the masiqta. Let him prepare the sacred meal and speak the prayers that the soul may ascend through the watch-houses.

**4** The sacred meal: let the priest prepare the bread called pihta and the sacred drink called mambuha. Let them be consumed with reverence and prayer.

**5** The Sunday observance: let the Mandaeans rest on the Sunday and perform their devotions. Let them go to the mandi and pray facing the north.

**6** Marriage: let the Mandaeans marry within the community. Let the priest perform the ceremony. Let husband and wife live in righteousness.

**7** The dead: let the dead be washed and wrapped in white garments. Let the masiqta be performed for them. Let the living remember the dead with prayers and offerings.

**8** And Yahia said: Whoever keeps these commandments shall live in the light. Whoever abandons them shall dwell in darkness until they repent.
