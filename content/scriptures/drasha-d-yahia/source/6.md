---
title: "Drasha d-Yahia Section 6"
scripture: "drasha-d-yahia"
chapter: 6
---

**1** And Anush Uthra came to Yahia and said: Yahia, your time in the world draws near its end. The Great Life calls you home.

**2** And Yahia said: I have performed my task. I have baptized those who came to me. I have taught the way of truth. Let me depart in peace.

**3** And Anush Uthra said: You have served faithfully. The Great Life is pleased with your work. Your place in the world of light is prepared.

**4** And Yahia called his disciples together and said: I am departing. Continue in the way that I have taught you. Baptize in the Living Water. Keep the commandments.

**5** Do not let the teaching die. Pass it from generation to generation. Let the priests perform the rituals. Let the people come to the mandi for prayer.

**6** And his disciples wept and said: Master, who shall lead us when you are gone?

**7** And Yahia said: The Great Life shall lead you. He is the true teacher. I am only His messenger. Follow His light and you shall not go astray.

**8** And Yahia raised his eyes to heaven and said: Great Life, into your hands I commend my spirit. Receive me in your world of light, where there is no end.
