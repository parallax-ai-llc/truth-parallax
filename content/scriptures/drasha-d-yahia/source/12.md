---
title: "Drasha d-Yahia Section 12"
scripture: "drasha-d-yahia"
chapter: 12
---

**1** And Yahia spoke his final words. He said: The Great Life is one. There is no god beside Him.

**2** He is the source of all light and the origin of all life. From Him all things come, and to Him all things return.

**3** The world of light is eternal and unchanging. The material world is temporary and passing away. Cling not to that which passes away, but seek that which endures.

**4** The body is a prison for the soul. The soul longs to be free. Through the baptism and the keeping of the commandments, the soul is prepared for its return.

**5** Do not fear death, for death is the release of the soul. The righteous soul rises to the world of light. Only the wicked soul is detained in darkness.

**6** Love one another, as the Great Life loves you. Give to the poor, comfort the afflicted, speak truth, shun lies. These are the marks of the children of light.

**7** And remember: the Great Life sees all. Nothing is hidden from Him. Every deed, every word, every thought is known to Him. Therefore walk in righteousness.

**8** In the name of the Great Life, the sublime Light be praised. The words of Yahia are ended. Life is victorious over death. Light is victorious over darkness. The Great Life endures forever and ever.
