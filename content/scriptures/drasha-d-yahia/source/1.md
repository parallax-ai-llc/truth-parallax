---
title: "Drasha d-Yahia Section 1"
scripture: "drasha-d-yahia"
chapter: 1
---

**1** In the name of the Great Life, the sublime Light be praised.

**2** Yahia proclaims in the nights, Yuhana on the evenings of the nights. Yahia proclaims and says:

**3** In the name of the Great Life, I have been chosen. The Great Life has selected me. He has sent me to the world of darkness, to the habitation in which the sons of Adam dwell.

**4** The Great Life said to me: Go, baptize the souls in the Living Water. Teach them the prayers and the sacred formulas. Set them on the path of truth.

**5** And I, Yahia, descended to the banks of the Jordan. I stood in the Living Water and called out to the sons of Adam.

**6** I said: Come to the Living Water, come to the baptism of truth. Leave the ways of darkness and walk in the ways of light.

**7** And many came to me and I baptized them in the name of the Great Life. I placed my hand upon their heads and spoke the prayers over them.

**8** And the Great Life was pleased with my work. He sent his Uthras to stand beside me and protect me from the powers of darkness.
