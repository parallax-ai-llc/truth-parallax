---
title: "Drasha d-Yahia Section 7"
scripture: "drasha-d-yahia"
chapter: 7
---

**1** And the soul of Yahia departed from his body and rose up through the firmaments. At each watch-house the guardians saw the light of his soul and they trembled.

**2** And they said: Who is this that ascends with such radiance? And the Uthras answered: This is Yahia, the messenger of the Great Life, who baptized in the Jordan.

**3** And the guardians opened the gates and let him pass. And his soul ascended through the seven firmaments until it reached the Great Place of Light.

**4** And the Great Life received Yahia and said: Welcome, faithful messenger. You have done well. Enter into the joy of the world of light.

**5** And Yahia was clothed in the garment of radiance and crowned with the crown of light. And he dwelt among the Uthras in the Great Place of Light.

**6** And from the world of light, Yahia watches over the Mandaeans, his people. He intercedes for them before the Great Life.

**7** And he says: Great Life, protect my people. Keep them in the way of truth. Let the Living Water flow for them. Let the baptism continue until the end of days.

**8** And the Great Life hears Yahia and sends his blessings upon the Mandaeans who keep the commandments and perform the rites.
