---
title: "Drasha d-Yahia Section 5"
scripture: "drasha-d-yahia"
chapter: 5
---

**1** And Yahia spoke concerning the end of days. He said: A time shall come when darkness shall cover the earth, and the children of light shall be few.

**2** The temples of the Great Life shall be abandoned, and the baptism of the Living Water shall be neglected. Men shall follow after lies and worship false gods.

**3** But in that time, the Great Life shall send another messenger, and he shall restore the ancient teaching and renew the baptism.

**4** And the souls of the righteous who have kept the way shall be gathered together, and the Great Life shall bring them home to the Place of Light.

**5** And Yahia said: I am not the last messenger. After me shall come others. For the Great Life does not abandon His children.

**6** As long as there is one soul that remembers the truth, the Great Life shall send help. As long as there is one who performs the baptism, the Living Water shall flow.

**7** And the powers of darkness shall rage and seek to extinguish the light. But the light cannot be extinguished, for it comes from the Great Life, who is eternal.

**8** And Yahia raised his hands toward the north, toward the Place of Light, and prayed. And the Great Life heard his prayer and strengthened him.
