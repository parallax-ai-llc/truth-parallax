---
title: "Drasha d-Yahia Section 10"
scripture: "drasha-d-yahia"
chapter: 10
---

**1** And Adam lived in the world and begat sons and daughters. And the souls of light multiplied in the world.

**2** But the powers of darkness sought to ensnare the souls of light. They sent the planets and zodiac signs to lead men astray.

**3** They taught men to worship idols and images. They taught them magic and sorcery. They turned men from the way of truth.

**4** And the Great Life saw that His children were going astray. And He sent messengers to call them back.

**5** He sent Shitil, the son of Adam, to teach the way of truth. And Shitil taught men the baptism and the prayers.

**6** And after Shitil came Anush, and after Anush came Sam, the son of Nuh. Each messenger renewed the teaching and called men back to the way.

**7** And the Great Life said: As long as the world endures, I shall send messengers. The souls of light shall not be abandoned.

**8** For every soul of light is precious to me, and I shall not rest until all my children have returned to the Great Place of Light.
