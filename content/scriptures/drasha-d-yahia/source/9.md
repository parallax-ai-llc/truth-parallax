---
title: "Drasha d-Yahia Section 9"
scripture: "drasha-d-yahia"
chapter: 9
---

**1** And Hibil Ziwa descended to the world of darkness, to the realm of the lord of darkness, whose name is Ur.

**2** And the lord of darkness saw Hibil Ziwa and was filled with rage. He gathered his armies and came against Hibil Ziwa.

**3** But Hibil Ziwa spoke the name of the Great Life, and the armies of darkness were scattered. And the lord of darkness trembled before the light.

**4** And Hibil Ziwa said: I have come in the name of the Great Life. No power of darkness can stand against the name of the Great Life.

**5** And he bound the lord of darkness and placed limits upon his power. He set boundaries which darkness could not cross.

**6** And Hibil Ziwa established the firmaments as barriers between the world of light and the world of darkness. He set the sun and the moon and the stars in their courses.

**7** And then he formed the body of Adam from the dust of the earth, and the Great Life breathed into it a soul of light. And Adam awoke and saw the world.

**8** And Hibil Ziwa said to Adam: Remember your origin. You are from the world of light. This material world is not your home. Seek always to return to the Great Life.
