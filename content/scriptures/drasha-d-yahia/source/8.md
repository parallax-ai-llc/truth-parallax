---
title: "Drasha d-Yahia Section 8"
scripture: "drasha-d-yahia"
chapter: 8
---

**1** The teaching of Yahia concerning the creation. In the beginning there was the Great Life, and beside Him there was nothing.

**2** And the Great Life existed in the Great Place of Light, in radiance and splendor, in purity and perfection.

**3** And the Great Life brought forth emanations of light, the Uthras, the celestial beings. And each Uthra received a portion of the light.

**4** And among the Uthras there arose one who desired to create a world of his own. And he descended into the darkness and formed the material world.

**5** But the material world was imperfect, for it was formed from darkness mixed with sparks of light. And in it there was suffering and death.

**6** And the Great Life saw that the souls of light were trapped in the material world. And He said: I will send my messengers to rescue them.

**7** And He sent Hibil Ziwa into the darkness, and Hibil Ziwa descended through the watch-houses and came to the material world.

**8** And Hibil Ziwa taught the first humans the way of return, the path from darkness to light, from the material world to the Great Place of Light.
