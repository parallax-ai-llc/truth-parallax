---
title: "Drasha d-Yahia Section 3"
scripture: "drasha-d-yahia"
chapter: 3
---

**1** And it came to pass in those days that Yeshu Meshiha came to the Jordan and said to Yahia: Baptize me in your baptism and speak over me your sacred formulas.

**2** And Yahia said to Yeshu: You have come with lies and deceived the Jews. You have cut off the seed of men and brought the worship of the planets and zodiac.

**3** But Yeshu spoke with humility and said: I ask for your baptism. And Yahia answered him and said:

**4** If you receive the baptism, then receive it truly, and do not corrupt the Living Water. Do not turn the people astray from the way of the Great Life.

**5** And a voice came from the Great Life, and it said to Yahia: Baptize Yeshu Meshiha. His spirit descends from the house of the mighty.

**6** And Yahia baptized him in the Jordan. And when Yeshu came up from the water, a radiance shone about him.

**7** But the Great Life said: He will go his way and you will go your way. His teaching is not your teaching. His path is not your path.

**8** And Yahia continued to baptize at the Jordan, teaching the souls of men the way of truth and the path of the Great Life.
