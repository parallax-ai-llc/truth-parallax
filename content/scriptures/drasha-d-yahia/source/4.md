---
title: "Drasha d-Yahia Section 4"
scripture: "drasha-d-yahia"
chapter: 4
---

**1** Yahia teaches in Jerusalem, Yuhana in the city of the Jews. He raises his voice and proclaims:

**2** O Jews, do not cut off the seed of men. Do not worship the Lord of this world. Do not bow down before idols and images.

**3** Turn to the Great Life, the source of all light. Perform the baptism in the Living Water. Speak the prayers which I have taught you.

**4** The body is a garment which the soul puts on. When the garment is worn out, the soul casts it off and ascends to its home.

**5** Therefore care for the soul more than for the body. For the body returns to dust, but the soul returns to the Great Life.

**6** Keep the commandments: do not steal, do not lie, do not commit adultery, do not murder. Give to the poor and comfort the afflicted.

**7** And Yahia said: Blessed are those who keep the Mandaean way, who baptize in the Living Water, who remember their Creator.

**8** For they shall pass through the watch-houses without fear, and the guardians shall let them pass, and they shall enter the world of light where there is no more pain or death.
