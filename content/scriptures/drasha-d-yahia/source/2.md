---
title: "Drasha d-Yahia Section 2"
scripture: "drasha-d-yahia"
chapter: 2
---

**1** Yahia baptizes in the Jordan, Yuhana in the waters of the river. He baptizes and speaks formulas over the Jordan.

**2** And the Jordan trembles and its waters are agitated. And the fish of the sea gather together and come to Yahia.

**3** They listen to his voice and they hear his discourse. They hear the name of the Great Life which Yahia pronounces over the Jordan.

**4** And Yahia says: Listen to me, O fish of the sea and birds of the air and beasts of the earth. All who have ears, hear my voice.

**5** I am the messenger of the Great Life. I have come to bring truth to the world. I have come to illumine those who dwell in darkness.

**6** The Great Life has sent me with the sign of the Living Water. Whoever is baptized in my baptism shall be purified. Whoever receives my teaching shall be saved.

**7** And the planets and the zodiac signs looked upon Yahia with displeasure, for his teaching threatened their dominion over the souls of men.

**8** But the Great Life protected Yahia and said: No harm shall come to my messenger. For I have placed my seal upon him, and the powers of darkness cannot prevail against him.
