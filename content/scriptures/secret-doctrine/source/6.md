---
title: "Secret Doctrine - Chapter 6"
scripture: "secret-doctrine"
chapter: 6
---

**1** The Secret Doctrine teaches concerning the evolution of humanity that which the ancient wisdom has always maintained.

**2** The Universe is worked and guided from within outward. As above so below, as in heaven so on earth.

**3** Every atom in the Universe has the potentiality of self-consciousness in it, and is, like the Monads, a Universe in itself and for itself.

**4** The whole order of nature evinces a progressive march towards a higher life. There is design in the action of seemingly blind forces.

**5** The Universe is called, with everything in it, Maya, because all is temporary therein, from the ephemeral life of a firefly to that of the Sun.

**6** There is a predestination in the geological life of our globe, as in the history, past and future, of races and nations. This is closely connected with what we call Karma.

**7** Matter is eternal. It is the upadhi, or physical basis, for the One infinite Universal Mind to build thereon its ideations.

**8** The fundamental Law in the Secret Doctrine is the One homogeneous divine Substance-Principle, the one radical cause.

**9** Spirit and Matter are the two facets of the unknowable Unity, their apparently contrasted natures depending on the degree of their differentiation.

**10** This is the end of the teaching on this subject. The student must now meditate upon these truths and discover their meaning within.
