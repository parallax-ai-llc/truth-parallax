---
title: "Secret Doctrine - Chapter 3"
scripture: "secret-doctrine"
chapter: 3
---

**1** Stanza II: "Where were the builders, the luminous sons of manvantaric dawn? In the unknown darkness in their Ah-hi Paranishpanna."

**2** "The producers of form from no-form — the root of the world — lay in the bliss of non-being."

**3** Stanza III: "The last vibration of the seventh eternity thrills through infinitude. The Mother swells, expanding from within without, like the bud of the lotus."

**4** "The vibration sweeps along, touching with its swift wing the whole universe and the germ that dwelleth in darkness: the darkness that breathes over the slumbering waters of life."

**5** "Darkness radiates light, and light drops one solitary ray into the waters, into the mother-deep. The ray shoots through the virgin egg; the ray causes the eternal egg to thrill, and drop the non-eternal germ, which condenses into the world-egg."

**6** These stanzas describe the awakening of the Universe from Pralaya.

**7** The first throb of cosmic life stirs the sleeping matter. The One becomes Two; Spirit and Matter begin their eternal interplay.

**8** From the One All-Presence emerge the manifold forms of the Universe, as waves arise from the ocean.

**9** Yet the waves are never separate from the ocean; the forms are never separate from the One.

**10** This is the fundamental teaching: all differentiation is apparent, not real. The Many are the One, and the One is the Many.
