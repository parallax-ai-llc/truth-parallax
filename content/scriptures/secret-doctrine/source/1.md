---
title: "Secret Doctrine - Chapter 1"
scripture: "secret-doctrine"
chapter: 1
---

**1** An Archaic Manuscript — a collection of palm leaves made impervious to water, fire, and air, by some specific unknown process — is before the writer's eye.

**2** On the first page is an immaculate white disk within a dull black ground. On the following page, the same disk, but with a central point.

**3** The first illustrates Kosmos in Eternity, before the re-awakening of still slumbering Energy, the emanation of the Word in later systems.

**4** The point in the hitherto immaculate Disk, Space and Eternity in Pralaya, denotes the dawn of differentiation.

**5** It is the Point in the Mundane Egg, the germ within the latter which will become the Universe, the All, the boundless, periodical Kosmos.

**6** The one circle is divine Unity, from which all proceeds, whither all returns. Its circumference — a forcibly limited symbol, in view of the limitation of the human mind — indicates the abstract, ever incognisable Presence.

**7** Its plane represents Spirit and Matter, the two poles of every phenomenon.

**8** The Secret Doctrine establishes three fundamental propositions: First, an Omnipresent, Eternal, Boundless, and Immutable Principle on which all speculation is impossible, since it transcends the power of human conception.

**9** Second, the Eternity of the Universe in toto as a boundless plane; periodically the playground of numberless Universes incessantly manifesting and disappearing.

**10** Third, the fundamental identity of all Souls with the Universal Over-Soul, the latter being itself an aspect of the Unknown Root; and the obligatory pilgrimage for every Soul through the Cycle of Incarnation.
