## Comparative Reading

Comparative study of the seventh Stanza's account of humanity's spiritual origins reveals parallels with creation myths across cultures: the Promethean gift of fire/reason in Greek mythology, the biblical creation of Adam in the divine image, the Qur'anic account of God teaching Adam the names, the Chinese myth of Fuxi bringing civilization, and Australian Aboriginal creation narratives. Each tradition describes a moment when human beings received the distinctive qualities that set them apart from other creatures — a universal myth that the Secret Doctrine reinterprets in evolutionary terms.
