## Comparative Reading

Comparative study of the third Stanza's emanation cosmology reveals parallels with Neoplatonic procession (from the One through Nous and Psyche to the material world), Kabbalistic *seder hishtalshelut* (the chain of emanation from *Ein Sof* through the *sefirot*), Vedantic *vivartavada* (apparent transformation of Brahman into the world), and Sufi concepts of *tajalli* (divine self-disclosure). Each tradition describes the emergence of multiplicity from unity through intermediate stages.
