## Comparative Reading

The second Stanza's imagery of cosmic awakening invites comparison with creation narratives from diverse traditions: the Egyptian emergence of Atum from Nun, the biblical 'Let there be light,' the Hindu *hiranyagarbha* (golden egg), the Chinese emergence of yang from primordial chaos, and the Maori separation of Rangi and Papa. Comparative analysis examines the shared motif of emergence from darkness or chaos into light and order.
