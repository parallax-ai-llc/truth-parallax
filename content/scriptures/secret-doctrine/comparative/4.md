## Comparative Reading

The fourth Stanza's spiritual hierarchies are compared with analogous concepts across traditions: Christian angelology (Pseudo-Dionysius's nine orders), Islamic cosmology's angelic ranks, Buddhist cosmology's *devas* and *bodhisattvas*, Hindu *devas*, Zoroastrian *Amesha Spentas*, and Kabbalistic *sefirot*. Comparative analysis examines the cross-cultural tendency to populate the space between the Absolute and the material world with hierarchies of spiritual beings.
