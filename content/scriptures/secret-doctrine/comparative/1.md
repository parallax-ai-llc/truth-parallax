## Comparative Reading

Comparative study of the first Stanza places the concept of cosmic dissolution alongside parallel accounts in other traditions: the Hindu *pralaya*, the Norse *Ragnarok* and subsequent renewal, the Buddhist concept of the cosmic cycle's end, the Stoic *ekpyrosis* (cosmic conflagration), and the Kabbalistic *tzimtzum*. Each tradition describes a state of cosmic rest or dissolution that precedes a new creation, suggesting a deep structural parallel in cosmogonic thinking across cultures.
