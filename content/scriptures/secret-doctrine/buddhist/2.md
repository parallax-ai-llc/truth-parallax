## Buddhist Reading

The second Stanza's imagery of the 'Germ of Life' awakening within the Absolute is examined critically from the Buddhist perspective. Buddhist dependent origination (*pratityasamutpada*) teaches that all phenomena arise through the interdependent co-arising of conditions, not through the activation of a pre-existing potential within an Absolute. However, certain Mahayana and Vajrayana concepts — such as *tathagatagarbha* (buddha-nature) as the potential for awakening inherent in all beings — offer points of contact with the Stanza's imagery.
