## Buddhist Reading

The fourth Stanza's Septenary Hierarchies are examined in relation to Buddhist cosmology's elaborate hierarchy of beings — *devas*, *brahmas*, *bodhisattvas*, and *buddhas* — who inhabit the various realms of existence. While the Buddhist cosmological hierarchy does bear a structural resemblance to Blavatsky's scheme, the Buddhist understanding differs fundamentally: in Buddhism, these beings are themselves subject to impermanence and are not eternal cosmic agents but simply sentient beings at various stages of the cyclic existence (*samsara*).
