## Buddhist Reading

The third Stanza's emanation cosmology is assessed by the Buddhist reader in relation to Buddhist concepts of cosmic arising. Buddhist philosophy generally rejects the concept of creation or emanation from a first cause, teaching instead that the universe arises through the beginningless, interdependent process of *karma* and *vipaka* (action and result). However, the Stanza's description of the progressive unfolding of increasingly complex levels of reality does resonate with the Abhidharma analysis of the progressive arising of mental and material phenomena.
