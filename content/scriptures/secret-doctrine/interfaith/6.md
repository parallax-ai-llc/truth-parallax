## Interfaith Reading

The sixth Stanza's vision of the earth within a chain of worlds invites interfaith reflection on the place of human existence within the larger cosmos. In an age of space exploration and scientific cosmology, the traditional cosmographies of the world's religions acquire new significance as symbolic expressions of the conviction that the visible world is not all there is — that human life is situated within a larger spiritual context.
