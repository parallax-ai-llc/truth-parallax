## Interfaith Reading

The third Stanza's emanation cosmology opens interfaith dialogue about the relationship between the One and the Many — the universal metaphysical question of how multiplicity arises from unity. Whether understood as creation *ex nihilo*, emanation from the Absolute, or the spontaneous arising of interdependent conditions, the emergence of the cosmos from its source is a mystery that every tradition addresses and that none can fully explain.
