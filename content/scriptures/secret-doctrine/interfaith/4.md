## Interfaith Reading

The fourth Stanza's spiritual hierarchies invite interfaith conversation about the nature and role of non-physical beings — angels, devas, bodhisattvas, and other spiritual entities — in the cosmic order. The widespread belief in such beings across traditions suggests either a common human tendency to populate the cosmos with intermediate agents or a genuine perception of spiritual realities that different traditions name and categorize differently.
