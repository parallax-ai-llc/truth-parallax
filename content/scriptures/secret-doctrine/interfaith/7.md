## Interfaith Reading

The seventh Stanza's account of humanity's spiritual origins opens interfaith dialogue about what it means to be human — the question of human nature, human purpose, and the distinctive qualities that characterize human existence. Every tradition affirms that human beings possess a capacity for spiritual awareness that sets them apart from other creatures, and the Secret Doctrine's mythological account of this capacity's origin provides a starting point for exploring how different traditions understand the mystery of human consciousness.
