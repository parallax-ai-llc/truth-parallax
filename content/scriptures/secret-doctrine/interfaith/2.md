## Interfaith Reading

The second Stanza's imagery of cosmic awakening invites interfaith reflection on the mystery of creation itself — the question of why there is something rather than nothing. Every major tradition offers its own account of this primordial beginning, from the divine command ('Let there be light') to the spontaneous arising of conditions, and the Stanzas of Dzyan provide a distinctive perspective that seeks to encompass and reconcile these diverse accounts.
