## Theosophical Reading

The sixth Stanza describes the formation of the earth and its place within the 'planetary chain' — a series of seven globes at different levels of materiality through which the wave of evolution passes. The Theosophist reads this as a description of the earth's spiritual anatomy: our visible, physical earth is only the fourth and most material globe in a chain that includes three globes of progressively subtler matter on either side. Humanity evolves through all seven globes, developing different aspects of consciousness on each.
