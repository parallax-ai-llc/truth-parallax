## Theosophical Reading

The second Stanza describes the first stirrings of cosmic awakening — the emergence of the 'Germ of Life' from the slumbering Absolute. The Theosophist reads this as the beginning of a new *manvantara* (period of cosmic activity), when the impulse of divine will initiates a new cycle of emanation and evolution. The Stanza's imagery of vibration, light, and the awakening of dormant potencies is understood as a symbolic description of processes occurring on planes of existence far beyond the physical — processes that Blavatsky's Masters could perceive through their advanced spiritual faculties.
