## Theosophical Reading

The seventh and final Stanza describes the spiritual origins of humanity — the 'Parents' from whom the human race descended. The Theosophical reading identifies these Parents as the *Manasaputras* ('Sons of Mind') — advanced spiritual beings from previous evolutionary cycles who incarnated in early humanity to awaken the faculty of mind (*manas*). This 'lighting of the mental fire' is understood as the event that transformed animal-like proto-humans into self-conscious beings capable of spiritual evolution.
