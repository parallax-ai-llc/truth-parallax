## Theosophical Reading

The fifth Stanza describes the progressive evolution of the elements — from the most subtle and spiritual to the most dense and material. The Theosophical reading understands this as the process of 'involution' by which spirit descends into matter through increasingly dense states of substance, creating the material basis for the subsequent 'evolution' of consciousness back toward its spiritual source. The seven elements described correspond to the seven planes of existence in Theosophical cosmology.
