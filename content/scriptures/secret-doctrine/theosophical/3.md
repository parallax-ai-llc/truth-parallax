## Theosophical Reading

The third Stanza describes the full awakening of Kosmos — the emanation of the universe from the Absolute through the agency of the 'Third Logos.' The Theosophical reading understands this as the process by which the One becomes the Many without ceasing to be One — a metaphysical paradox that lies at the heart of esoteric cosmology. The Stanza's description of the 'Web of Being' woven by the cosmic Logos is understood as the fundamental matrix of existence from which all forms, forces, and consciousness arise.
