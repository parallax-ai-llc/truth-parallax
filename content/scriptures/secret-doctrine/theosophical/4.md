## Theosophical Reading

The fourth Stanza introduces the Septenary Hierarchies — the seven orders of spiritual beings who serve as the agents of cosmic evolution. The Theosophist understands these Hierarchies as the 'Builders' and 'Architects' of the universe, working under the direction of cosmic law (Karma) to guide the evolution of matter and consciousness. These are identified with the angelic hierarchies of Christianity, the *Dhyani-Buddhas* of esoteric Buddhism, and the *devas* of Hinduism — different cultural expressions of the same metaphysical reality.
