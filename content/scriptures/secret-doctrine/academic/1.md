## Academic Reading

Academic analysis of the first Stanza examines Blavatsky's sources for the concept of cosmic dissolution and rest. Scholars trace the imagery to the Hindu concepts of *pralaya* (dissolution) and the 'Night of Brahma' as described in the Vishnu Purana and other texts available in 19th-century English translation. Neoplatonic concepts of the return of all things to the One are also identified as significant influences, along with the Kabbalistic concept of *tzimtzum* (divine contraction). The claim that the Stanzas derive from an independent Tibetan source is assessed against the available evidence.
