## Academic Reading

The sixth Stanza's concept of the planetary chain is analyzed as one of Blavatsky's most distinctive cosmological innovations. Scholars trace its roots in earlier esoteric systems (particularly the works of A.P. Sinnett) while noting its originality as a cosmological model. The concept is compared with Buddhist cosmology's multiple world-systems and Hindu Puranic accounts of the structure of the universe, with attention to both genuine parallels and Blavatsky's creative interpolations.
