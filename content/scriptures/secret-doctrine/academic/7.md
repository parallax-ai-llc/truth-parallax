## Academic Reading

Academic analysis of the seventh Stanza examines the concept of humanity's spiritual origins in relation to both mythological and scientific accounts of human origins current in the late 19th century. The *Manasaputra* concept is traced to Hindu mythology (the *Prajapatis* and *Kumaras*) while its evolutionary implications are compared with Darwinian and Lamarckian theories of human development. Scholars assess the Stanza as a creative myth that attempts to reconcile spiritual and scientific accounts of human origins.
