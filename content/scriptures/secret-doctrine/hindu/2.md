## Hindu Reading

The second Stanza's concept of the cosmic awakening resonates with the Hindu concept of *hiranyagarbha* — the 'golden embryo' or cosmic egg from which the universe emerges. The Chandogya Upanishad's description of the primordial self (*atman*) willing itself into manifestation finds a parallel in the Stanza's description of the Germ of Life stirring within the Absolute. Hindu readers appreciate Blavatsky's engagement with these concepts while noting that her framework ultimately subordinates Hindu categories to a syncretic scheme.
