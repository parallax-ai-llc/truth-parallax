## Hindu Reading

Hindu readers recognize in the fourth Stanza's spiritual hierarchies a creative reinterpretation of the Vedic *devas* and Puranic cosmological beings. The Septenary Hierarchies correspond, in broad outline, to the Hindu cosmological schema of *devas*, *asuras*, and other celestial beings who participate in the cosmic drama. However, Hindu scholars note that Blavatsky's evolutionary framework — in which these beings represent stages in progressive development — differs from the traditional Hindu understanding of the *devas* as eternal cosmic principles.
