## Hindu Reading

Hindu readers engage with the sixth Stanza's planetary chain concept through the lens of Hindu cosmography, which describes a multi-layered universe of *lokas* (worlds) at different levels of subtlety. While the specific concept of a seven-globed planetary chain is not found in traditional Hindu cosmology, the idea that the visible world is surrounded by invisible realms of varying degrees of materiality is thoroughly Hindu. The concept of cyclic evolution through successive worlds resonates with the Puranic concept of *manvantaras*.
