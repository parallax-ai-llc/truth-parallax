## Hindu Reading

The seventh Stanza's account of humanity's spiritual origins is read through the Hindu concept of the *Manasaputras* (Sons of Mind) — a term Blavatsky borrows directly from Hindu mythology. The Puranic tradition describes the *Kumaras* and *Prajapatis* as spiritual beings who played a role in the creation and spiritual awakening of humanity. Hindu readers find in this Stanza a recognizable, if freely adapted, version of the Puranic creation narrative, reframed within Blavatsky's evolutionary cosmology.
