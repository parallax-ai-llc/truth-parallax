## Hindu Reading

The fifth Stanza's account of elemental evolution resonates with the Hindu Samkhya philosophy's description of the evolution of the *mahabhutas* (great elements) from *prakriti* (primordial nature) through progressively grosser stages of manifestation. The concept of subtler elements giving rise to denser ones corresponds to the Samkhya theory of *tanmatras* (subtle elements) producing the gross elements. Hindu readers find in this Stanza the most directly Indic elements of the Secret Doctrine's cosmology.
