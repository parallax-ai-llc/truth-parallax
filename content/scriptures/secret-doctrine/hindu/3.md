## Hindu Reading

The third Stanza's account of cosmic emanation is read through the lens of Vedantic *srishti* (creation/emanation) theory. The concept of the universe emanating from the Absolute through intermediate stages corresponds to the *parinamavada* (transformation theory) of some Vedantic schools, though Blavatsky's Neoplatonic framework of successive Logoi differs from the strictly Indian categories. Hindu scholars recognize the Upanishadic echoes in the Stanza's language while noting the syncretistic character of the overall cosmological scheme.
