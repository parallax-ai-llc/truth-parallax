## Second Stele: Praise to Barbelo — Academic Analysis

Scholarly analysis of this stele situates it within the broader context of Gnostic literary production and the specific theological tradition to which Three Steles of Seth belongs. The Three Steles of Seth provides the clearest evidence of Sethian liturgical practice in the Nag Hammadi library.

This section — "Second Stele: Praise to Barbelo" — raises questions about genre, literary composition, and theological classification. Its relationship to other Nag Hammadi texts, to the heresiological reports of the Church Fathers, and to the broader religious landscape of late antiquity illuminates both the text's distinctiveness and its participation in shared traditions of thought.
