## Overview

For Gnostic readers, the Three Steles of Seth is a liturgical treasure: three hymns mapping a progressive contemplative ascent from the Self-Generated (Autogenes) through Barbelo to the Invisible Spirit. Each stele addresses a higher level of divine reality and invokes it through praise, petition, and doxology. The instructions for communal recitation at the text's conclusion reveal that these were not private meditations but community prayers, chanted together by Sethian worshippers who understood their praise as participation in the divine reality they celebrated.
