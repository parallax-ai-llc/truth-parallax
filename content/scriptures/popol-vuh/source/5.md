---
title: "Popol Vuh - Chapter 5"
scripture: "popol-vuh"
chapter: 5
---

**1** And then the Maker and Modeler carved their figures out of wood. They looked like people, talked like people, and populated the surface of the earth. They existed and multiplied; they had daughters and sons, these wooden figures, but they had no heart and no mind. They did not remember their Maker, their Modeler.

**2** They went about heedlessly. They did not remember the Heart of Sky. And so they fell. They were merely an experiment, merely a design for people. They talked at first but their faces were dry. Their limbs were not fleshed out. They had no blood, no body fluids. They had no sweat, no fat.

**3** Their faces were blank. They were not thinking of their Maker, their Modeler, who made them, who cared for them. These were the first humans to exist in numbers on the face of the earth.

**4** And so a great flood was made; it came down upon the heads of the wooden figures. The Heart of Sky devised a great calamity. A heavy resin rained down from the sky. Their eyes were gouged out by the creatures of the earth.

**5** They were pounded down to the bones and tendons, smashed and pulverized even to the bones. Their faces were smashed because they were incapable of understanding before the face of their mother and the face of their father, the Heart of Sky, named Hurricane. The earth was blackened because of this; the black rainstorm began, rain all day and rain all night.
