---
title: "Popol Vuh - Chapter 8"
scripture: "popol-vuh"
chapter: 8
---

**1** And then the twins Hunahpu and Xbalanque were conceived from the blood of their father One Hunahpu, miraculously conceived in the womb of Blood Moon, the maiden daughter of Blood Gatherer, one of the lords of Xibalba.

**2** "You are not to die, my daughter. What is in your belly is merely a sign of the word of my son, One Hunahpu," said the skull. And Blood Moon conceived, and the lords of Xibalba saw that she was with child.

**3** So Blood Moon was driven out. She climbed up here above the earth and went to the grandmother of the twins. "I have come, mother. I am your daughter-in-law," she said.

**4** The grandmother was suspicious, but a sign was given through the harvest. Blood Moon went to the garden and called upon the food to come. The net was full. The grandmother was amazed: "Where did all the food come from? You must truly be my daughter-in-law."

**5** And so were born the Hero Twins, Hunahpu and Xbalanque. They grew up in their grandmother's house, becoming great hunters and ballplayers, destined to avenge their father in Xibalba.
