---
title: "Popol Vuh - Chapter 7"
scripture: "popol-vuh"
chapter: 7
---

**1** And here is the account of the underworld, called Xibalba. And the messengers of One Death and Seven Death came to summon One Hunahpu and Seven Hunahpu to play ball in Xibalba.

**2** "Come," said the messengers. "The lords say you must come play ball with them. In seven days they want your opponents," they said when they came to One Hunahpu and Seven Hunahpu.

**3** "Very well," said One Hunahpu and Seven Hunahpu, and they left their game and set out on the road to Xibalba. They entered on the path, which descended steeply. They went down until they came to the banks of several canyons.

**4** They passed through the places of spiked crossings, the narrow canyons. They passed through Scorpion Rapids. They passed the river filled with blood, the river filled with pus. They crossed these without being destroyed.

**5** But when they arrived in Xibalba, the lords of the underworld made them sit upon a hot stone bench, and they burned. Then One Hunahpu and Seven Hunahpu were put to death in Xibalba, sacrificed for their failure.
