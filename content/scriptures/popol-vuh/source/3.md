---
title: "Popol Vuh - Chapter 3"
scripture: "popol-vuh"
chapter: 3
---

**1** And then the deer and birds were told by the Maker, Modeler, Bearer, Begetter: "Talk, cry out. Don't just squawk, and don't just be silent. Please talk, each to each, within each kind, within each group," they were told.

**2** "Name now our names, praise us. We are your mother, we are your father. Speak now: 'Hurricane, Newborn Thunderbolt, Raw Thunderbolt, Heart of the Sky, Heart of the Earth, Maker, Modeler, Bearer, Begetter,' speak, pray to us, keep our days," the animals were told.

**3** But it didn't turn out that they spoke like people: they just squawked, they just chattered, they just howled. It wasn't apparent what language they spoke; each one gave a different cry.

**4** When the Maker, Modeler heard that it was impossible for them to talk to each other, they said among themselves: "It is not yet possible to say our names, the names of us, their makers. This is not good," said the Bearers, Begetters to each other.

**5** So their flesh was brought low. They were allowed to be eaten. The animals on the face of the earth were designated for slaughter. This was the test, the first human work, designed by the Maker, Modeler.
