---
title: "Popol Vuh - Chapter 2"
scripture: "popol-vuh"
chapter: 2
---

**1** Then they planned the creation, and the growth of the trees and the thickets and the design of life and humankind, in the blackness, in the early dawn, by the Heart of Sky, named Hurricane. Thunderbolt Hurricane comes first, the second is Newborn Thunderbolt, and the third is Raw Thunderbolt.

**2** "Let it be this way, think about it: this water should be removed, emptied out for the formation of the earth's own plate and target. Then comes the sowing, the dawning of the sky-earth."

**3** "Earth!" they said, and instantly it was made. Like a cloud, like a mist, now forming, unfolding. The mountains were brought forth from the water. All at once the great mountains came forth. By their genius alone, by their cutting edge alone they carried out the conception of the mountain-Loss, whose name means "earth ripple."

**4** The channels of water were separated; their branches wound their ways among the mountains. The waters were divided when the great mountains appeared.

**5** Such was the formation of the earth when it was brought forth by the Heart of Sky, Heart of Earth, as they are called, since they were the first to think of it. The sky was set apart, and the earth was set apart in the midst of the waters.
