## Critique of Orthodox Practice — Comparative Perspectives

This section of Testimony of Truth addresses themes that resonate across the world's religious and philosophical traditions. The Testimony of Truth exemplifies the pattern of internal reform and critique that appears in virtually every religious tradition.

The section "Critique of Orthodox Practice" invites cross-cultural comparison while respecting the irreducible particularity of the Gnostic context. The questions addressed here — about the nature of ultimate reality, the human condition, and the possibility of liberation — are shared by traditions as diverse as Vedanta, Buddhism, Neoplatonism, Kabbalah, and Sufism.

Comparative method at its best illuminates without flattening: genuine parallels create space for mutual understanding, while differences remind us that each tradition develops its insights within a particular historical and cultural matrix.
