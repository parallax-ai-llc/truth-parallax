---
title: "Living Gospel Section 3"
scripture: "living-gospel"
chapter: 3
---

1. The First Man descended into the abyss to make war against the Darkness, and the battle was fierce beyond all reckoning.

2. The Archons of Darkness came against him in waves: first the Archon of Smoke, then the Archon of Burning Fire, then the Archon of Destructive Wind, then the Archon of Poisonous Water, and last the Archon of Darkness itself.

3. The First Man fought valiantly, but the Darkness was vast and hungry. One by one, the Archons devoured his five elements.

4. The Light Air was swallowed by Smoke. The Light Wind was consumed by Destructive Wind. The Light Fire was engulfed by Burning Fire. The Light Water was poisoned by Dark Water. The Light Ether was buried in Darkness.

5. The First Man was defeated. He lay unconscious in the deep, stripped of his armor, alone in the realm of the enemy.

6. But his sacrifice was not in vain, for by giving his Light to the Darkness, he accomplished the Father's hidden purpose.

7. The Darkness, having consumed the Light, was now pacified, as a wild beast that has gorged itself grows drowsy.

8. The Light within the Darkness began its secret work of transformation, like leaven placed within dough.

9. Meanwhile the First Man awoke in the pit and remembered who he was. He sent forth a prayer, seven times, to the Father of Greatness.

10. The prayer ascended through the layers of Darkness, through fire and smoke, until it reached the ears of the Father.

11. The Father heard the prayer and was moved with compassion. He called forth a second emanation: the Friend of Light, and from the Friend of Light came the Great Builder, and from the Great Builder came the Living Spirit.

12. The Living Spirit stood at the border of the worlds and called out to the First Man with a great voice.

13. The First Man heard the Call, and he answered. The Call and the Answer embraced, and together they ascended.

14. The Living Spirit reached down his right hand and grasped the First Man and drew him out of the abyss, back to the Kingdom of Light.
