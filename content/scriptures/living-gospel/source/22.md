---
title: "Living Gospel Section 22"
scripture: "living-gospel"
chapter: 22
---

1. The Living Gospel concludes with the vision of the restored Paradise and the final word of the Apostle.

2. When the Darkness is sealed and the cosmos is no more, the Kingdom of Light shall be as it was in the Former Time—but greater.

3. For in the Former Time, the Father dwelt in the Paradise with his Aeons and his angels. But now, all the Light that was scattered in the world has returned.

4. The First Man, who gave his Light to save the Kingdom, shall stand complete, his five elements restored, his glory renewed.

5. Every soul that was imprisoned in matter shall be free, clothed in garments of Light, crowned with glory, dwelling in the Paradise forever.

6. There shall be no more suffering, no more birth, no more death, no more separation. The Light shall be one, as it was in the beginning.

7. The Father shall rejoice, and the Mother of Life shall rejoice, and the First Man shall rejoice, and all the Aeons and angels shall sing.

8. The hymn of restoration shall echo through the Paradise, a song of thanksgiving for the long labor of salvation that is finally complete.

9. And the Paradise shall expand, for it has grown by all the Light that was recovered from the Darkness. The Father's kingdom shall be greater than before.

10. This is the hope of the Living Gospel: that the Light shall prevail, that the Darkness shall be overcome, that every particle of the divine shall return to its source.

11. I, Mani, the Apostle of Jesus Christ, have written this Gospel by the hand of truth. What I have written, I have received from the Twin, my heavenly counterpart.

12. The Twin has guided me since my youth, revealing to me the mysteries of the Two Principles and the Three Times.

13. I have concealed nothing. I have added nothing. I have written the truth as it was shown to me.

14. Let those who have ears hear, and those who have eyes see, and those who have hearts understand. The Light calls to you. Answer, and be saved.

15. This is the Living Gospel. It shall endure until the Last Day, a witness to the truth, a lamp in the Darkness, a guide for every soul that seeks the Light.
