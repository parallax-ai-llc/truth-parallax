---
title: "Living Gospel Section 9"
scripture: "living-gospel"
chapter: 9
---

1. The Living Gospel teaches concerning the Cross of Light: "The Light is crucified in all the world, in every living thing."

2. The Cross of Light is not a cross of wood. It is the suffering of the Light that is stretched across the cosmos, pinned to matter, aching for release.

3. In every fruit that is picked, the Light on the Cross trembles. In every animal that is killed, the Light on the Cross cries out.

4. In every soul that forgets its origin, the Light on the Cross weeps in silence.

5. Jesus knew this Cross, for he bore it in his own body. His crucifixion was the visible sign of the invisible truth: that the Light suffers in matter.

6. When Jesus hung upon the cross of wood, he showed the world what the Light endures in every creature, in every blade of grass, in every grain of sand.

7. But the cross of wood was also the promise of liberation, for Jesus rose from death, showing that the Light cannot be destroyed by Darkness.

8. The resurrection of Jesus is the pattern for every soul. As he rose from the tomb, so shall every particle of Light rise from the tomb of matter.

9. Therefore, when you see suffering in the world, do not say it is meaningless. It is the suffering of the Light, and through it, the Light is being purified.

10. And when you act with compassion, when you ease the suffering of another, you participate in the liberation of the Light from its cross.

11. The Elect, through their purity, take upon themselves a portion of the Cross of Light, for by consuming only plants and fruits, they liberate the Light within.

12. The Hearers, through their generosity, also share in the Cross, for by supporting the Elect, they enable the work of liberation to continue.

13. All who serve the Light bear the Cross, and all who bear the Cross shall share in the resurrection.
