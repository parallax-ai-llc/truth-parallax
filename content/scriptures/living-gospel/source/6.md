---
title: "Living Gospel Section 6"
scripture: "living-gospel"
chapter: 6
---

1. The Archons, seeing that their Light was being drawn from them, devised a counter-plan to trap the Light beyond the reach of the Sun and Moon.

2. The chief of the Archons, whom some call Saklas and others call the Demiurge, summoned his fellow Archons and said:

3. "Let us create a being in the image of that glorious form we saw descending from the Light, and let us imprison within him all the Light we can gather."

4. So the Archons took their own substance—matter and desire and ignorance—and fashioned from it the body of the first human, Adam.

5. They formed his body in the image of the First Man, whom they had seen, copying his form but filling it with Darkness.

6. Into this body they concentrated the greatest portion of Light they could seize, hoping to hide it within flesh so that the ships of Light could not reach it.

7. Adam was cast upon the earth, and the Light within him was stupefied by the weight of the body. He lay as one dead, without knowledge.

8. The Archons rejoiced, thinking they had secured their treasure. But the Father of Greatness saw what they had done.

9. The Father sent the Luminous Jesus, the Jesus of Light, to awaken Adam from his sleep of forgetfulness.

10. Jesus descended through the heavens, passing the watchposts of the Archons in disguise, until he reached the earth.

11. He found Adam lying on the ground and touched him and said: "Adam, arise! Remember who you are. You are not of this world. Your true home is the Paradise of Light."

12. Adam opened his eyes and saw himself, and he understood for the first time the nature of his captivity and the hope of his liberation.
