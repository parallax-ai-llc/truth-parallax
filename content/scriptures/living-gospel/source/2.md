---
title: "Living Gospel Section 2"
scripture: "living-gospel"
chapter: 2
---

1. In the Former Time, the Kingdom of Light and the Kingdom of Darkness were wholly separate, each in its own territory, with a border between them.

2. The Father of Greatness had no knowledge of evil, for evil had never approached his realm. The Darkness had no knowledge of good, for good had never entered its domain.

3. But the Darkness was restless, for its nature is disorder, and it moved chaotically within its own borders.

4. In its chaotic movement, the King of Darkness rose up to the borders of his realm and there beheld, for the first time, the Light.

5. When the Darkness saw the Light, it was seized with desire. It coveted the beauty and peace of the Light, though it could not comprehend it.

6. The Darkness gathered its forces—all its Archons, all its demons, all its powers—and launched an assault upon the border of Light.

7. The attack shook the foundations of the Paradise of Light. The Aeons trembled, and the angels cried out to the Father.

8. The Father of Greatness perceived the assault, but he could not go forth himself, for his nature is peace and he cannot make war.

9. Therefore the Father called forth from himself an emanation, the Mother of Life, and the Mother of Life called forth the First Man.

10. The First Man was named Ohrmazd, and he was beautiful and mighty, the firstborn of the Light's defense.

11. The Mother of Life armed the First Man with five elements: the Light Air, the Light Wind, the Light Fire, the Light Water, and the Light Ether.

12. These five elements were his sons, his armor, his weapons. Clothed in them, the First Man descended to the border to meet the onslaught of Darkness.
