---
title: "Living Gospel Section 18"
scripture: "living-gospel"
chapter: 18
---

1. The Gospel teaches concerning the community of Light and how the faithful shall live together.

2. The Elect shall dwell in monasteries, which are houses of Light, where they devote themselves to prayer, to study, to copying the sacred books, and to painting the sacred images.

3. The Elect shall own nothing of their own. All things are held in common, for possessions bind the soul to the world.

4. The Elect shall wear white garments, for white is the color of Light, and their clothing proclaims their allegiance.

5. The Hearers shall live in the world, among the nations, conducting their affairs with honesty and compassion.

6. The Hearers shall give one-tenth of their income to the support of the Elect, and they shall bring food to the monasteries daily.

7. The Hearers shall gather each week on the appointed day to hear the teaching, to confess their sins, and to renew their commitment.

8. The Teachers shall travel from community to community, carrying the word to new lands. They shall learn the languages of the peoples and translate the sacred books.

9. The Bishops shall settle disputes, ordain new Elect, and ensure that the teaching is preserved without corruption.

10. The Presbyters shall lead the daily prayers, minister to the sick, and comfort the dying.

11. In all things, the community shall be united. There shall be no quarreling over rank, for all serve the same Light.

12. The community of Light is the body of the Apostle on earth. When it functions in harmony, the work of liberation proceeds swiftly. When it is divided, the Darkness gains ground.

13. Love one another, children of Light, as the Father loves you. Support one another, as the elements of Light supported the First Man. Be patient with one another, as the Light is patient in its long captivity.
