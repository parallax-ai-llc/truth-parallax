---
title: "Living Gospel Section 10"
scripture: "living-gospel"
chapter: 10
---

1. The Gospel of Life teaches concerning the Column of Glory, which is the Perfect Man.

2. The Column of Glory is visible to those whose eyes are opened. It stretches from the earth to the heavens, a pillar of pure Light.

3. Through this Column, the liberated particles of Light ascend from the world to the Moon, from the Moon to the Sun, and from the Sun to the Paradise of Light.

4. The Column is called the Perfect Man because it is composed of all the purified souls that have been freed from matter.

5. Each soul that ascends becomes part of the Column, adding to its brilliance, strengthening its power to draw other souls upward.

6. The Column is also the Milky Way, which the ancients knew as the path of souls. It is no mere arrangement of stars but the visible trace of the ascending Light.

7. When a righteous person dies and their Light ascends, it enters the Column and joins the great river of Light flowing back to the Father.

8. The Column is the spine of the cosmos, the axis around which all things turn. Without it, the cosmos would collapse into Darkness.

9. In prayer, the faithful direct their thoughts toward the Column, for it is the pathway of communication between the world and the Father.

10. The prayers of the righteous ascend the Column like fragrant smoke, and the blessings of the Father descend it like gentle rain.

11. At the end of all things, when the last particle of Light has ascended, the Column will be complete, and the Perfect Man will stand whole before the Father.

12. Then the cosmos will fall away like a discarded garment, and the Perfect Man—the totality of redeemed Light—will enter the Paradise and be one with the Father forever.
