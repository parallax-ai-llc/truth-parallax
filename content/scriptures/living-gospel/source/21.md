---
title: "Living Gospel Section 21"
scripture: "living-gospel"
chapter: 21
---

1. The Living Gospel speaks concerning the Great Fire and the final separation.

2. After the judgment, the Great Fire shall be kindled. This fire is not a punishment but a purification.

3. The cosmos itself shall burn, for it was made from the bodies of the Archons, and it shall return to its base nature.

4. The heavens that were stretched from the skins of the Archons shall shrivel in the heat. The mountains made from their bones shall melt.

5. The earth that was formed from their flesh shall be consumed. Nothing of the material world shall remain.

6. In this fire, the last particles of Light that are still trapped in matter shall be released. The fire shall extract every trace of the divine substance.

7. This fire shall burn for one thousand four hundred and sixty-eight years, until every last spark of Light has risen free.

8. The liberated Light shall ascend the Column of Glory for the last time, and the Column shall blaze with a brilliance never before seen.

9. When the last particle of Light has ascended, the fire shall go out, for there will be no more Light to sustain it.

10. The remnant of matter—pure Darkness, devoid of any Light—shall collapse upon itself and fall into the great Pit.

11. The Pit is the eternal prison of the Darkness. It is boundless in its depth, and nothing that enters it shall ever emerge.

12. A great stone shall be set upon the Pit, sealing it forever. And the Darkness shall gnaw upon itself for eternity, but it shall never again touch the Light.

13. This is the Last Day, the final moment of the Third Time, when the separation that began at the beginning of the world is at last complete.
