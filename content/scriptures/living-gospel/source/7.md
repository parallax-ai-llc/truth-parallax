---
title: "Living Gospel Section 7"
scripture: "living-gospel"
chapter: 7
---

1. The Luminous Jesus spoke to Adam and revealed to him the knowledge of his origin and his destiny.

2. "Adam, you are a being of two natures. Your body is of the Darkness, fashioned by the Archons. But your soul is of the Light, a fragment of the First Man."

3. "The Archons made your body to be a prison, but the Father has sent me to show you the way of escape."

4. "Look upward, Adam, and see the Sun. It is the ship that will carry your Light home. Look at the Moon. It is the gate through which the Light passes."

5. "Within you is a treasure more precious than all the gold and jewels of the world. It is the Light, the divine spark, the soul that came from the Father."

6. "The Archons will try to make you forget. They will fill your mind with pleasures and pains, with desires and fears, so that you will not remember."

7. "But I will send my messengers in every age to remind you, to call you back to the truth."

8. "Guard the Light within you, Adam. Do not let the body consume it. Do not let desire scatter it. Do not let ignorance bury it."

9. Adam wept and said: "Lord, how shall I guard the Light when the body is so heavy and the Darkness so strong?"

10. Jesus answered: "Through knowledge. Through gnosis. When you know who you are, where you came from, and where you are going, the Darkness has no power over you."

11. "Know that you are Light dwelling in Darkness. Know that you are a stranger in this world. Know that your home awaits you above."

12. "And when you depart from this body, if you have kept the knowledge, you shall rise through the heavens and the Archons shall not be able to hold you."

13. "For the Call that awakened you shall also guide you, and the Answer that you gave shall be your password through the gates."
