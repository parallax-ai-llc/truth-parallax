---
title: "Living Gospel Section 12"
scripture: "living-gospel"
chapter: 12
---

1. The Gospel teaches concerning the Three Seals that the Elect must keep, which are the marks of those who have chosen the Light.

2. The First Seal is the Seal of the Mouth. By this seal, the Elect governs what enters and what leaves the mouth.

3. No flesh shall pass the lips of the Elect, for in flesh the Light is deeply trapped and the act of killing scatters it further.

4. No wine shall the Elect drink, for wine inflames the passions of the Old Man and clouds the Light Mind.

5. No falsehood shall the Elect speak, for lies are the language of Darkness, and every lie strengthens the Archons' hold on the world.

6. The Elect shall speak truth, and pray, and sing hymns, for these purify the mouth and make it a vessel of Light.

7. The Second Seal is the Seal of the Hands. By this seal, the Elect governs all actions of the body.

8. The Elect shall not kill any living creature, for all living things contain the Light, and to kill is to wound the Cross of Light.

9. The Elect shall not uproot plants or break branches, for the trees are vessels of Light and feel pain as we do.

10. The Elect shall not engage in any labor that harms the Light. They shall not plow, nor reap, nor build, nor destroy.

11. The Third Seal is the Seal of the Breast. By this seal, the Elect governs the desires of the heart.

12. The Elect shall not engage in sexual union, for procreation is the device of the Archons to scatter the Light.

13. The Elect shall not covet possessions, for attachment to the things of the world binds the soul to matter.

14. By these Three Seals, the Elect becomes a living vessel of purification, through whom the Light of the world is liberated and ascends to the Father.
