---
title: "Living Gospel Section 1"
scripture: "living-gospel"
chapter: 1
---

1. This is the Living Gospel, written by the hand of Mani, the Apostle of Jesus Christ, by the will of the Father of Greatness, the blessed God of Truth.

2. In the beginning, before heaven and earth, before the visible and the invisible, there existed Two Natures: the Nature of Light and the Nature of Darkness.

3. The Nature of Light is the Father of Greatness, who dwells in the Paradise of Light. His kingdom has no boundary, and his glory has no measure.

4. The Father possesses five Shekhinas, which are his dwelling-places of glory: Mind, Knowledge, Reason, Thought, and Understanding.

5. From these five Shekhinas proceed twelve Aeons, and from the twelve Aeons proceed the countless hosts of angels, who sing the praise of the Father eternally.

6. The Paradise of Light is a realm of unending peace, where there is no suffering, no conflict, no decay, and no death.

7. The Nature of Darkness is the realm of the King of Darkness, who dwells in the deep with his five worlds: the World of Smoke, the World of Fire, the World of Wind, the World of Water, and the World of Darkness.

8. In these five worlds dwell the Archons, who are beings of confusion, of appetite, of destruction. They war against one another ceaselessly.

9. The Darkness has no wisdom, no order, no peace. It is ruled by blind desire and insatiable hunger.

10. These Two Natures have existed from eternity. The Light did not create the Darkness, nor did the Darkness create the Light. They are co-eternal, co-existent, and opposed.

11. This is the first teaching of the Gospel of Life: know the Two Roots from which all things proceed, and you shall understand everything that follows.
