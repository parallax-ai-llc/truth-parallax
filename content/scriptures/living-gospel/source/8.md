---
title: "Living Gospel Section 8"
scripture: "living-gospel"
chapter: 8
---

1. After the Luminous Jesus awakened Adam, the Archons grew fearful and devised further schemes to recapture the Light.

2. They created Eve from the substance of Adam, placing within her a portion of the Light, but also infusing her with desire.

3. The Archons intended that through the union of Adam and Eve, the Light would be further scattered through procreation, divided into countless fragments too small to recover.

4. For each child born carries a portion of the parents' Light, and so the Light is divided again and again, generation after generation.

5. The Archon of Desire, whom they call Ashaqlun, entered into Eve and stirred up lust, so that she tempted Adam.

6. Adam, forgetting the warning of Jesus, joined with Eve, and so the cycle of birth began—the great trap of the Archons.

7. Through procreation, the Light that was concentrated in Adam was scattered into his descendants, like a jewel broken into dust.

8. Each human being carries a spark of that original Light, but it is small and dim, buried under layers of flesh and desire.

9. This is why the path of the Elect requires abstinence from procreation—to stop the further scattering of the Light.

10. But the knowledge that Jesus gave to Adam was not lost. It was passed from generation to generation, from Seth to his descendants.

11. Seth, the righteous son of Adam, received the gnosis and kept it pure. Through his lineage, the truth was preserved.

12. And in every generation, some souls remember the call of Jesus and turn toward the Light, even when the Darkness presses upon them from every side.

13. The Gospel of Life calls to every soul: "Remember! You are not of this world. The Light within you is your true self. Do not let the Archons deceive you."
