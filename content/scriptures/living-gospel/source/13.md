---
title: "Living Gospel Section 13"
scripture: "living-gospel"
chapter: 13
---

1. The Living Gospel teaches concerning the Hearers, who are the foundation upon which the Church of Light stands.

2. Not all can bear the full burden of the Three Seals, for the Darkness within many is still too strong.

3. But the Father does not reject those who cannot yet be Elect. He offers them the path of the Hearer, which is the path of service and preparation.

4. The Hearer shall keep the Ten Commandments, which are the lesser seals, suitable for those still bound to the world.

5. The first commandment: Do not worship any image or idol, for the Archons work through images to deceive.

6. The second: Do not bear false witness, for truth is the foundation of the Light.

7. The third: Do not be greedy, but share what you have with the Elect and with those in need.

8. The fourth: Do not kill a human being, for in every person dwells a portion of the Light.

9. The fifth: Do not commit adultery, for unfaithfulness scatters the Light of the heart.

10. The sixth: Do not steal, for taking what belongs to another disturbs the balance of the world.

11. The seventh: Do not practice deceit or sorcery, for these are the arts of the Archons.

12. The eighth: Do not doubt the teaching of the Apostle, for doubt is the tool of the Old Man.

13. The ninth: Be diligent in your duties, for the slothful soul grows cold and the Darkness creeps in.

14. The tenth: Pray daily and keep the appointed fasts, for these are the means by which the Hearer draws closer to the Light.

15. By keeping these commandments and by serving the Elect, the Hearer purifies their own Light, and in time they may be born as Elect in a future life.
