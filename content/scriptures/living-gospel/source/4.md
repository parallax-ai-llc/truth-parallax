---
title: "Living Gospel Section 4"
scripture: "living-gospel"
chapter: 4
---

1. Though the First Man was saved, his five sons—his elements of Light—remained captive in the Darkness, mixed with the substance of the enemy.

2. The Living Spirit and his five sons descended to begin the work of creation, which is the work of separating the Light from the Darkness.

3. They slew the Archons and took their bodies, and from these they fashioned the visible world.

4. From the skins of the Archons, they stretched out the heavens—ten heavens, one above another.

5. From their bones, they piled up the mountains. From their flesh and their filth, they formed the eight layers of the earth.

6. The Living Spirit took the purest Light that he could extract and placed it in the Sun and the Moon.

7. The Sun he set as the great luminary, the ship of the day, to carry the purified Light upward to the Paradise.

8. The Moon he set as the lesser luminary, the ship of the night, to receive the Light and channel it to the Sun.

9. The Light that was less pure, but still partially free, he set as the stars, each one a lamp of Light in the canopy of Darkness.

10. Then he set three great wheels in motion beneath the heavens: the Wheel of Fire, the Wheel of Water, and the Wheel of Wind, to drive the machinery of the cosmos.

11. All of creation—the heavens, the earth, the sun, the moon, the stars—is a vast apparatus designed for one purpose: to extract the Light from the Darkness and return it to the Father.

12. This is the truth of the world: it is not your home. It is a refinery, a crucible. You are the Light passing through it on your way back to the Father.
