---
title: "Living Gospel Section 5"
scripture: "living-gospel"
chapter: 5
---

1. Then the Father sent the Third Messenger, who established himself in the Ship of the Sun, to begin the active work of liberation.

2. The Third Messenger revealed himself in two forms: as a beautiful youth to the female Archons, and as the Maiden of Light to the male Archons.

3. When the male Archons bound in the heavens beheld the Maiden of Light, desire overcame them, and in their passion they released the Light they had consumed.

4. The Light streamed forth from them and fell upon the earth like rain, and from it sprang all green and growing things.

5. The female Archons, beholding the beautiful youth, were likewise seized with desire, and they cast forth their offspring—the demons and monstrous forms.

6. These abortive creatures fell to the earth and became the five races of demons: bipeds, quadrupeds, birds, fish, and creeping things.

7. Thus even the device of desire, which belongs to Darkness, was turned to the service of Light, for through it the imprisoned Light was shaken loose.

8. The vegetation that sprang from the fallen Light is filled with the divine substance. The trees and the fruits and the grains are vessels of Light.

9. This is why the Elect treat all growing things with reverence, for in them the Light is present, waiting to be freed.

10. The Cross of Light is everywhere—in every tree, in every plant, in every creature. The Living Soul suffers in all things.

11. When you see a tree bending under the weight of its fruit, know that the Light within is heavy and longs to ascend.

12. When you see grain being ground into flour, know that the Light within cries out. But through the meal of the Elect, it shall be liberated.
