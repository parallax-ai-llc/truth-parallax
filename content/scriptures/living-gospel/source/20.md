---
title: "Living Gospel Section 20"
scripture: "living-gospel"
chapter: 20
---

1. The Gospel teaches concerning the Great War and the end of the present age.

2. As the work of separation nears its end, the Darkness will become ever more frantic, for it feels its power waning.

3. Wars shall multiply upon the earth. Kingdom shall rise against kingdom. The seas shall rage and the mountains shall tremble.

4. The Church of Light shall be persecuted, and many of the faithful shall be put to death. The sacred books shall be burned, and the monasteries shall be destroyed.

5. False prophets shall arise, claiming to speak for the Light but serving the Darkness. They shall deceive many with signs and wonders.

6. But the faithful remnant shall endure. Though few in number, they shall keep the Three Seals and preserve the teaching.

7. Then Jesus the Judge shall descend from the Sun, seated upon his throne of glory, surrounded by the angels of Light.

8. He shall call all souls before him—the living and the dead, from every nation and every age.

9. To those on his right, he shall say: "Come, children of Light, enter the Paradise that was prepared for you before the foundation of the world."

10. To those on his left, he shall say: "Depart from me into the Darkness, for you have chosen the Darkness over the Light."

11. The righteous shall ascend in glory, their Light restored in full, and they shall enter the Kingdom of Light forever.

12. The wicked shall be bound with the Darkness and cast into the pit from which there is no return.

13. And Jesus shall deliver the Kingdom to the Father, and the Father shall receive it, and there shall be no more mixture, no more suffering, no more separation.
