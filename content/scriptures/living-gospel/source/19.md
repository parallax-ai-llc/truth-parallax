---
title: "Living Gospel Section 19"
scripture: "living-gospel"
chapter: 19
---

1. The Living Gospel speaks concerning the destiny of the soul and the journey after death.

2. When the Elect dies, the bonds of the body are loosed, and the Light within is free at last. Three angels descend to greet the departing soul.

3. The first angel bears a garment of Light, woven from the good deeds of the Elect. The soul puts on this garment and is clothed in glory.

4. The second angel bears a crown, fashioned from the prayers and hymns of the Elect. The soul is crowned and honored.

5. The third angel bears a wreath of victory, for the soul has conquered the Darkness. It is placed upon the soul as a sign of triumph.

6. The Luminous Jesus appears as guide, and the Maiden of Light leads the way upward.

7. The soul ascends through the heavens. At each gate, the Archons challenge it, but it speaks the passwords of Light and they cannot hold it.

8. It passes the Moon, where its remaining impurities are cleansed. It passes the Sun, where it is filled with glory.

9. It rises along the Column of Glory until it reaches the New Paradise, the realm prepared for the redeemed.

10. There the soul rests in the presence of the Father, its long journey complete, its Light restored to its source.

11. But the soul of the Hearer follows a different path. At death, it is weighed in the balance. The Light within is measured against the Darkness.

12. If the Light outweighs the Darkness, the soul is permitted to rest briefly and then is born again into favorable circumstances, closer to the truth.

13. If the Darkness outweighs the Light, the soul falls into lower forms of existence, further from the truth, deeper in matter.

14. And the soul of the wicked, who rejected the Light entirely, is cast into outer Darkness, where it wanders in torment until the end of all things.
