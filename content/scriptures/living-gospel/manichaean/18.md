Within Manichaean tradition, this chapter's treatment of fate of souls is understood not merely as historical or theoretical but as a living source of guidance for faith and practice. The teachings on afterlife, judgment inform Manichaean self-understanding and continue to shape communal identity and spiritual aspiration.
