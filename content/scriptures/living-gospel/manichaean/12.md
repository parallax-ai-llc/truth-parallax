Chapter 12 of the Living Gospel, addressing awakening, holds particular significance within the Manichaean tradition. The themes of gnosis, Jesus the Splendor are central to Manichaean theology and practice, and this chapter has been a focus of traditional interpretation and spiritual reflection.

Within Manichaean tradition, this chapter's treatment of awakening is understood not merely as historical or theoretical but as a living source of guidance for faith and practice. The teachings on gnosis, Jesus the Splendor inform Manichaean self-understanding and continue to shape communal identity and spiritual aspiration.
