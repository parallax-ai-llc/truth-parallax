Chapter 3 of the Living Gospel, addressing realm of light, holds particular significance within the Manichaean tradition. The themes of paradise, aeons are central to Manichaean theology and practice, and this chapter has been a focus of traditional interpretation and spiritual reflection.

Within Manichaean tradition, this chapter's treatment of realm of light is understood not merely as historical or theoretical but as a living source of guidance for faith and practice. The teachings on paradise, aeons inform Manichaean self-understanding and continue to shape communal identity and spiritual aspiration.
