The themes of eschatology, cosmic conflict are central to Manichaean theology and practice, and this chapter has been a focus of traditional interpretation and spiritual reflection.

Within Manichaean tradition, this chapter's treatment of great war is understood not merely as historical or theoretical but as a living source of guidance for faith and practice. The teachings on eschatology, cosmic conflict inform Manichaean self-understanding and continue to shape communal identity and spiritual aspiration.
