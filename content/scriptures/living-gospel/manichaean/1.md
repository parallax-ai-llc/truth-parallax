Chapter 1 of the Living Gospel, addressing two principles, holds particular significance within the Manichaean tradition. The themes of light, darkness are central to Manichaean theology and practice, and this chapter has been a focus of traditional interpretation and spiritual reflection.

Within Manichaean tradition, this chapter's treatment of two principles is understood not merely as historical or theoretical but as a living source of guidance for faith and practice. The teachings on light, darkness inform Manichaean self-understanding and continue to shape communal identity and spiritual aspiration.
