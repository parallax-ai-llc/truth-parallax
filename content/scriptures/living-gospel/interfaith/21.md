Interfaith engagement with this chapter emphasizes shared human concerns and complementary insights, while respecting the distinctive theological context of Manichaeism. The themes addressed here — restoration, paradise regained, final victory — resonate across religious boundaries and invite collaborative reflection on questions of ultimate significance.
