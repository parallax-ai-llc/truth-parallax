## Overview

An interfaith reading of the Living Gospel seeks to build bridges of understanding between Manichaeism and other religious communities, fostering mutual respect and appreciation through engagement with sacred texts. Illustrates the universalist aspirations of Mani's prophetic mission. This approach emphasizes shared values, complementary insights, and the potential for enriching dialogue.

Interfaith engagement with the Living Gospel addresses common misconceptions, highlights the depth and sophistication of Manichaeism theological thought, and identifies points of convergence with other traditions' scriptures and teachings. Such readings contribute to a more inclusive and informed understanding of humanity's diverse spiritual heritage.
