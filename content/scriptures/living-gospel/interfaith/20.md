An interfaith reading of Chapter 20 of the Living Gospel seeks to build bridges of understanding around the themes of last judgment. The chapter's teachings on judgment, separation offer points of contact with other traditions' reflections on similar questions.

Interfaith engagement with this chapter emphasizes shared human concerns and complementary insights, while respecting the distinctive theological context of Manichaeism. The themes addressed here — judgment, separation, light from darkness — resonate across religious boundaries and invite collaborative reflection on questions of ultimate significance.
