An interfaith reading of Chapter 9 of the Living Gospel seeks to build bridges of understanding around the themes of sun and moon. The chapter's teachings on luminaries, light vessels offer points of contact with other traditions' reflections on similar questions.

Interfaith engagement with this chapter emphasizes shared human concerns and complementary insights, while respecting the distinctive theological context of Manichaeism. The themes addressed here — luminaries, light vessels, purification — resonate across religious boundaries and invite collaborative reflection on questions of ultimate significance.
