---
title: "Corpus Hermeticum Section 2"
scripture: "corpus-hermeticum"
chapter: 2
---

**1** Hermes: Tell me, O Mind, about the things that are. Declare to me the nature of the universe and the nature of God.

**2** Mind: Receive what thou dost wish. I am the Mind of the First God, who was before the moist Nature that appeared out of the Darkness; and the Word that came from the Light is the Son of God.

**3** Hermes: What meanest thou?

**4** Mind: Learn this: that which in thee sees and hears is the Word of the Lord, but thy Mind is God the Father. They are not separated from each other, for their union is life.

**5** Hermes: I give thanks to thee. But tell me more of the things that are.

**6** Mind: The God who is Mind, being both male and female, being Life and Light, brought forth by His Word another Mind, the Maker; who, being god of Fire and of Spirit, fashioned Seven Rulers, who encompass with their orbits the world perceived by the senses; and their governance is called Destiny.

**7** Forthwith the Word of God leapt from the downward-moving elements upward to the pure creation of Nature, and was united with the Maker-Mind; for the Word was of one substance with that Mind. And the downward-moving elements of Nature were left devoid of reason, so as to be mere Matter.
