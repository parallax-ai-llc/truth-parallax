## Gnostic Reading

The Gnostic engagement with The Mixing Bowl (Kratēr) examines the Hermetic treatment of mixing bowl, nous, baptism of mind, gnosis within the broader context of Gnostic scholarship and practice. This treatise presents teachings attributed to Hermes Trismegistus on the nature of the divine, the cosmos, and the human soul's path to gnosis, offering material for sustained Gnostic reflection and analysis.
