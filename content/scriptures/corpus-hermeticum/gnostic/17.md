## Gnostic Reading

The Gnostic engagement with From Tat to the King (fragment) examines the Hermetic treatment of fragment, kingship, body, soul within the broader context of Gnostic scholarship and practice. This treatise presents teachings attributed to Hermes Trismegistus on the nature of the divine, the cosmos, and the human soul's path to gnosis, offering material for sustained Gnostic reflection and analysis.
