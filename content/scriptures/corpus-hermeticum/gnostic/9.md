## Gnostic Reading

The Gnostic engagement with On Thought and Sense examines the Hermetic treatment of thought, sense, God, mind, perception within the broader context of Gnostic scholarship and practice. This treatise presents teachings attributed to Hermes Trismegistus on the nature of the divine, the cosmos, and the human soul's path to gnosis, offering material for sustained Gnostic reflection and analysis.
