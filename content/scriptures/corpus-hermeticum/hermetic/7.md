## Hermetic Reading

The Hermetic engagement with That the Greatest Evil in Mankind Is Ignorance of God examines the Hermetic treatment of ignorance, evil, knowledge of God, salvation within the broader context of Hermetic scholarship and practice. This treatise presents teachings attributed to Hermes Trismegistus on the nature of the divine, the cosmos, and the human soul's path to gnosis, offering material for sustained Hermetic reflection and analysis.
