## Hermetic Reading

The Hermetic engagement with On Truth (fragment) examines the Hermetic treatment of truth, fragment, reality, being within the broader context of Hermetic scholarship and practice. This treatise presents teachings attributed to Hermes Trismegistus on the nature of the divine, the cosmos, and the human soul's path to gnosis, offering material for sustained Hermetic reflection and analysis.
