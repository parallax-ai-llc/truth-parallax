## Hermetic Reading

The Hermetic engagement with A Sacred Discourse of Hermes examines the Hermetic treatment of sacred discourse, creation, cosmos, elements within the broader context of Hermetic scholarship and practice. This treatise presents teachings attributed to Hermes Trismegistus on the nature of the divine, the cosmos, and the human soul's path to gnosis, offering material for sustained Hermetic reflection and analysis.
