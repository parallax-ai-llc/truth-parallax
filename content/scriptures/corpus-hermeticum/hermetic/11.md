## Hermetic Reading

The Hermetic engagement with Mind (Nous) to Hermes examines the Hermetic treatment of Nous, mind, God, cosmos, emanation within the broader context of Hermetic scholarship and practice. This treatise presents teachings attributed to Hermes Trismegistus on the nature of the divine, the cosmos, and the human soul's path to gnosis, offering material for sustained Hermetic reflection and analysis.
