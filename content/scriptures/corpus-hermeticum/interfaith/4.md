## Interfaith Reading

The Interfaith engagement with The Mixing Bowl (Kratēr) examines the Hermetic treatment of mixing bowl, nous, baptism of mind, gnosis within the broader context of Interfaith scholarship and practice. This treatise presents teachings attributed to Hermes Trismegistus on the nature of the divine, the cosmos, and the human soul's path to gnosis, offering material for sustained Interfaith reflection and analysis.
