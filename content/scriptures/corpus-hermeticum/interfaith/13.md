## Interfaith Reading

The Interfaith engagement with A Secret Dialogue of Hermes and Tat on the Mountain: On Regeneration examines the Hermetic treatment of regeneration, rebirth, powers, divinization, secret teaching within the broader context of Interfaith scholarship and practice. This treatise presents teachings attributed to Hermes Trismegistus on the nature of the divine, the cosmos, and the human soul's path to gnosis, offering material for sustained Interfaith reflection and analysis.
