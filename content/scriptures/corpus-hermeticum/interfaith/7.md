## Interfaith Reading

The Interfaith engagement with That the Greatest Evil in Mankind Is Ignorance of God examines the Hermetic treatment of ignorance, evil, knowledge of God, salvation within the broader context of Interfaith scholarship and practice. This treatise presents teachings attributed to Hermes Trismegistus on the nature of the divine, the cosmos, and the human soul's path to gnosis, offering material for sustained Interfaith reflection and analysis.
