## Interfaith Reading

The Interfaith engagement with To Asclepius examines the Hermetic treatment of Asclepius, cosmos, God, motion, eternity within the broader context of Interfaith scholarship and practice. This treatise presents teachings attributed to Hermes Trismegistus on the nature of the divine, the cosmos, and the human soul's path to gnosis, offering material for sustained Interfaith reflection and analysis.
