## Interfaith Reading

The Interfaith engagement with On the Common Mind examines the Hermetic treatment of common mind, reason, humanity, divine gift within the broader context of Interfaith scholarship and practice. This treatise presents teachings attributed to Hermes Trismegistus on the nature of the divine, the cosmos, and the human soul's path to gnosis, offering material for sustained Interfaith reflection and analysis.
