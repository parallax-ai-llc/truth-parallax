---
title: "Pesher Habakkuk - Chapter 1"
scripture: "pesher-habakkuk"
chapter: 1
---

**1** The oracle which Habakkuk the prophet received in a vision. How long, O Lord, shall I cry for help, and thou wilt not hear?

**2** The interpretation of this concerns the beginning of the final generation, when the cry goes up to God because of the oppression of the wicked.

**3** Why dost thou show me iniquity and cause me to look upon trouble? Destruction and violence are before me; there is strife, and contention arises.

**4** The interpretation of this concerns those who are unfaithful together with the Man of Lies, for they did not listen to the words of the Teacher of Righteousness from the mouth of God.

**5** So the law is slackened and justice never goes forth. For the wicked surround the righteous, so justice goes forth perverted.

**6** The interpretation of this: the wicked is the Wicked Priest, and the righteous is the Teacher of Righteousness.

**7** Look among the nations, and see; wonder and be astounded. For I am doing a work in your days that you would not believe if told.

**8** The interpretation of this concerns those who were unfaithful together with the Man of Lies, for they did not believe that which the Teacher of Righteousness told them from the mouth of God.

**9** For behold, I am rousing the Chaldeans, that bitter and hasty nation. The interpretation concerns the Kittim, who are swift and valiant in war.

**10** And the wicked shall not believe. They shall rule over many peoples, and they shall not have faith in the statutes of God.
