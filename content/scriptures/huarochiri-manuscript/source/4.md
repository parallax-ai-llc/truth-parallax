---
title: "What Cuniraya Did After This"
scripture: "huarochiri-manuscript"
chapter: 4
---

**1** After Cavillaca became stone, Cuniraya Viracocha went to Pachacamac. There he found the two daughters of Pachacamac, guarded by a serpent.

**2** The mother of the daughters, Urpay Huachac, had gone to the sea to visit Cavillaca. While she was away, Cuniraya seduced the elder daughter.

**3** When he tried to approach the younger daughter, she changed into a pigeon and flew away. This is why pigeons exist.

**4** At that time, there were no fish in the sea. Only Urpay Huachac raised some fish in a small pond near her home.

**5** Cuniraya, angry at Urpay Huachac, overturned the pond. The fish spilled into the sea. This is why there are now fish in the ocean. Before this, there had been none.

**6** Cuniraya then went along the coast, playing tricks on the people, sometimes helping them, sometimes confounding them. He was both creator and trickster.
