---
title: "The Priesthood of Pariacaca"
scripture: "huarochiri-manuscript"
chapter: 24
---

**1** The priests of Pariacaca were chosen from among the people of the surrounding communities. Each ayllu sent one priest in rotation.

**2** The priest served for one year. During that time, he lived at the shrine on the mountain. He maintained the sacred fire, swept the shrine, and performed the daily offerings.

**3** The priest fasted frequently and abstained from salt and chili during his service. He was not permitted to have sexual relations or to eat certain foods.

**4** In return, his community was exempted from certain labor obligations. His fields were worked by the other members of his ayllu.

**5** When his year of service was ended, the priest returned to his community and resumed his normal life. But he was respected ever after as one who had served Pariacaca.
