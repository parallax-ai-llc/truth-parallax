---
title: "The Story of Coniraya and Huatyacuri"
scripture: "huarochiri-manuscript"
chapter: 21
---

**1** In another version of the Coniraya story, it is told that after his pursuit of Cavillaca, Coniraya went to the highlands and met with Huatiacuri.

**2** They recognized each other as father and son in spirit, for both wandered the world in the guise of poor men, and both possessed hidden power.

**3** Coniraya taught Huatiacuri the arts of healing and divination. He gave him the knowledge to hear the conversations of animals and to interpret their words.

**4** Huatiacuri used this knowledge to cure the great lord Tamtañamca and to win the contests against the rich man. All of this power came from Coniraya.

**5** This shows the connection between the old gods and the new. Coniraya was the ancient creator; Pariacaca and his sons were the new order. But the wisdom passed from the old to the new.
