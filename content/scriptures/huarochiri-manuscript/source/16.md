---
title: "The Pilgrimage to Pariacaca"
scripture: "huarochiri-manuscript"
chapter: 16
---

**1** The pilgrimage to the mountain of Pariacaca was undertaken three times each year: at the planting season, at the harvest, and at the great festival.

**2** Pilgrims prepared themselves by fasting for five days before the journey. They abstained from salt, chili peppers, and sexual relations.

**3** The journey to the summit took two days. The pilgrims walked in procession, carrying their offerings and singing the ancient hymns.

**4** At the summit, each pilgrim placed their offering at the shrine and spoke their prayer. They asked for rain, for good harvests, for healthy children, for protection from enemies.

**5** The descent was joyful. The pilgrims believed that Pariacaca had heard their prayers and would answer them in the coming season.
