---
title: "The Khalsa Ideal"
scripture: "rehatnama"
chapter: 8
---

**1** The Khalsa is the pure, the chosen, the saint-soldier of the Guru. The Khalsa belongs to God, and all victory belongs to God.

**2** The true Khalsa is one who fights injustice but never attacks the innocent. Who protects the weak but never bullies the strong. Who serves all but bows to none but God.

**3** The true Khalsa sees God in all creation. Every human being is a child of God, regardless of religion, race, or nationality.

**4** The true Khalsa lives by the teachings of the ten Gurus and the eternal Guru, the Guru Granth Sahib. The scriptures are the living Guru, and their word is the command of God.

**5** The true Khalsa is ready to give their life for the faith, as the Gurus gave theirs. Martyrdom for righteousness is the highest honor.

**6** The Khalsa shall endure until the end of time. The flame lit by Guru Nanak and passed through ten Gurus shall never be extinguished. Waheguru Ji Ka Khalsa, Waheguru Ji Ki Fateh.
