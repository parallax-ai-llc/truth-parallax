---
title: "The Conduct of the Gurdwara"
scripture: "rehatnama"
chapter: 5
---

**1** The Gurdwara is the house of God, open to all people regardless of caste, creed, or station. No one shall be turned away.

**2** In the Gurdwara, the Guru Granth Sahib shall be installed with honor. It shall be read and recited with devotion.

**3** The Langar, the communal kitchen, shall serve food to all who come, without distinction. This is the practice of equality before God.

**4** All who enter the Gurdwara shall remove their shoes and cover their heads as a sign of respect.

**5** The congregation shall sit on the floor, all at the same level, for before God there is no high or low.

**6** The Gurdwara shall be maintained by the community through voluntary service, seva, which is the highest form of worship.
