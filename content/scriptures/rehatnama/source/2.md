---
title: "The Five Articles of Faith"
scripture: "rehatnama"
chapter: 2
---

**1** Every Amritdhari Sikh shall wear the five articles of faith, known as the Five Ks.

**2** Kesh: uncut hair, a sign of acceptance of God's will. The hair shall be kept clean and covered with a turban.

**3** Kangha: a wooden comb, worn in the hair, a sign of cleanliness and discipline. The Sikh shall comb the hair twice daily.

**4** Kara: a steel bracelet, worn on the right wrist, a symbol of restraint and of the bond with God. It reminds the Sikh that all actions must be righteous.

**5** Kachera: a specific undergarment, a symbol of self-control and chastity.

**6** Kirpan: a ceremonial sword, a symbol of courage and the duty to protect the weak. The Sikh shall defend the helpless and fight injustice.
