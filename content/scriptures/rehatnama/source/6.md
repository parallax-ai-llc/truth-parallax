---
title: "Marriage and Family"
scripture: "rehatnama"
chapter: 6
---

**1** The Sikh marriage ceremony, the Anand Karaj, is performed in the presence of the Guru Granth Sahib. The couple walks around the holy scripture four times.

**2** Marriage is a union of two souls, not two families. The consent of the bride and groom is essential. Forced marriage is forbidden.

**3** Husband and wife shall be equal partners. They shall support each other in spiritual growth and in the duties of life.

**4** Children shall be raised in the Sikh faith. They shall be taught to read the scriptures, to serve the community, and to live with honor.

**5** Dowry is forbidden. The practice of giving or demanding dowry is a corruption of the sacred bond of marriage.

**6** The family is the foundation of the community. A strong family produces strong Sikhs, and strong Sikhs build a strong Khalsa.
