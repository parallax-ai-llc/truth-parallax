---
title: "The Sikh's Daily Life"
scripture: "rehatnama"
chapter: 4
---

**1** Upon rising, the Sikh shall recite the name of God: Waheguru, Waheguru. The first thoughts of the day shall be of the Lord.

**2** After bathing, the Sikh shall sit in meditation and recite the Nitnem, the daily prayers.

**3** The Sikh shall earn an honest living. Whether farmer, merchant, soldier, or scholar, the work of the hands is blessed by God.

**4** The Sikh shall eat in moderation and avoid excess. Food is a gift from God and must not be wasted.

**5** The Sikh shall be truthful in all dealings. A lie, even a small one, separates the soul from God.

**6** Before sleeping, the Sikh shall recite Kirtan Sohila and reflect upon the day's actions. Have I served? Have I been truthful? Have I remembered God?
