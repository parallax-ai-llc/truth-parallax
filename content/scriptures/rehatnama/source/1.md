---
title: "The Rehat of Guru Gobind Singh"
scripture: "rehatnama"
chapter: 1
---

**1** The Rehatnama is the code of conduct given by Guru Gobind Singh to the Khalsa. These are the rules by which a Sikh must live.

**2** A Sikh shall rise before dawn, bathe, and recite the morning prayers: Japji Sahib, Jaap Sahib, and the Ten Savaiyes.

**3** A Sikh shall earn an honest living by the sweat of the brow. Fraud, theft, and deception are forbidden.

**4** A Sikh shall share earnings with those in need. One-tenth of all income shall be given in the service of the community and the Guru.

**5** A Sikh shall not worship idols, nor make pilgrimages to Hindu temples, nor follow superstitions. The one true God alone is worthy of worship.

**6** The Khalsa belongs to God, and all victory belongs to God. Waheguru Ji Ka Khalsa, Waheguru Ji Ki Fateh.
