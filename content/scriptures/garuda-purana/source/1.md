---
title: "Garuda Purana - Chapter 1"
scripture: "garuda-purana"
chapter: 1
---

**1** Suta said: I shall relate to you that sacred Purana which the Lord Vishnu narrated to Garuda, the king of birds, concerning the nature of life, death, and the hereafter.

**2** Garuda asked: "O Lord, tell me what becomes of the soul after death. What are the experiences of the righteous and the wicked? What is the path to liberation?"

**3** The Lord said: The soul is eternal, unborn, undying. It is not slain when the body is slain. It passes from body to body, as a man discards worn-out garments and puts on new ones.

**4** At the time of death, the vital breaths gather around the soul. The soul, accompanied by the subtle body and the impressions of its actions, departs from the gross body.

**5** The righteous soul, which has lived a life of virtue and devotion, departs in peace. It is attended by the messengers of Vishnu, who guide it through the heavenly path.

**6** The soul that has accumulated merit through charity, worship, and righteous living passes through pleasant regions. It sees beautiful gardens, flowing rivers, and celestial beings.

**7** The soul that has attained the knowledge of Brahman is not touched by death. For such a soul, death is merely the dropping of the outer garment. It merges with the Supreme and is free forever.

**8** But the soul that has lived a life of sin and cruelty — that soul departs in agony. It is seized by the messengers of Yama and dragged along the dark path.

**9** Therefore, O Garuda, let every man live a life of righteousness. Let him worship the Lord with devotion, give charity to the poor, speak the truth, and practise non-violence.

**10** He who remembers the Lord at the time of death attains the Lord. Therefore, let a man practise the remembrance of the Lord throughout his life, so that at the moment of death, the Lord's name is on his lips.
