## Overview

The Prayer of Thanksgiving demonstrates a truth recognized across all religious traditions: that gratitude is the foundation of the spiritual life, and that sharing food in community is one of humanity's most powerful sacred acts. The prayer's thanks for the gift of knowledge resonates with every tradition that values enlightenment, wisdom, or divine revelation. The communal meal that follows embodies the universal principle that spiritual insight must be lived in community, not hoarded in isolation.
