## Overview

For Christian readers, the Prayer of Thanksgiving provides important context for understanding the Eucharist within the broader landscape of ancient Mediterranean sacred meal practices. The combination of thanksgiving prayer, communal embrace, and shared food parallels the structure of early Christian worship (1 Corinthians 11:17-34). The vegetarian character of the meal differs from the Eucharistic bread and wine but resonates with early Christian communities that practiced abstinence from blood (Acts 15:20).
