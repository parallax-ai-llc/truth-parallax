## Overview

The Prayer of Thanksgiving's combination of ecstatic prayer and communal meal resonates with sacred meal traditions across religions. The Christian Eucharist, the Jewish Kiddush and Passover Seder, the Sikh Langar, the Zoroastrian Yasna ritual meal, and the communal feasts of indigenous traditions all embody the principle that sharing food in a sacred context creates and sustains spiritual community. The vegetarian character of the Hermetic meal connects to traditions of vegetarian sacred eating in Hinduism, Buddhism, and Pythagorean philosophy.
