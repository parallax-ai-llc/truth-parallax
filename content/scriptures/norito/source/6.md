---
title: "Norito - Chapter 6"
scripture: "norito"
chapter: 6
---

**1** I humbly speak before the assembled Deities concerning the ceremony of the Great New Food Offering.

**2** By the divine command of the Sovereign, we gather in the sacred precincts to speak the ancient words of prayer and supplication.

**3** Hear, O Deities of Heaven and Earth, the words which we speak with reverence. Accept the offerings which we present with purified hands and a sincere heart.

**4** The white cloth and the bright cloth, the sake and the first-fruits, the fish of the sea and the herbs of the mountain, all are arranged upon the offering tables in the ancient manner.

**5** As the sacred sakaki tree stands evergreen upon the mountain, so may the blessings of the Deities endure without ceasing.

**6** As the pure water flows from the mountain springs, so may the divine favour wash away all impurity and misfortune.

**7** We pray that the Sovereign's reign may continue in peace, that the harvests may be abundant, and that the people may dwell in safety and contentment.

**8** Let the Deities of the four quarters guard this land. Let the ancestral spirits watch over their descendants. Let all things prosper under the benevolent light of the Sun-Goddess.

**9** With these words of reverence and gratitude, we conclude the ritual of the ceremony of the Great New Food Offering. May the Deities accept our offerings and bestow their blessings upon this sacred land.
