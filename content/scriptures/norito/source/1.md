---
title: "Norito - Chapter 1"
scripture: "norito"
chapter: 1
---

**1** I humbly speak in the presence of the Sovereign Deities whose praises are fulfilled in the great shrine of the Deity of Kasuga.

**2** The Great Deity who dwells in the inner shrine, august child of the Heavenly Deities, hear these words of praise and supplication.

**3** By the command of the Sovereign Grandchild, I, the priest of the august shrine, humbly speak these liturgical words.

**4** As the morning sun rises in splendour, and as the evening sun sinks in glory, so may the reign of the Sovereign be eternal and unchanging.

**5** We offer these first-fruits of the field, the produce of the mountains and the seas, placing them in abundance upon the offering tables.

**6** The white cloth and the blue cloth, the bright cloth and the coarse cloth, we present as offerings to the Great Deity.

**7** May the Sovereign's reign endure as long as Heaven and Earth, as firm as the rocks, as eternal as the pines upon the mountain.

**8** Hear these words, O Great Deity, with a clear and tranquil heart, and bestow thy divine favour upon this land and its people.
