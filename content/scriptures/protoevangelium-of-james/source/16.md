---
title: "Protoevangelium of James - Chapter 16"
scripture: "protoevangelium-of-james"
chapter: 16
---

**1** And he found a cave there, and brought her into it, and left his sons beside her, and went forth to seek a midwife of the Hebrews in the region of Bethlehem.

**2** And I, Joseph, was walking about, and yet I did not walk. And I looked up to the vault of heaven, and saw it standing still, and I looked up unto the air, and saw the air in amazement, and the birds of the heaven without motion.

**3** And I looked upon the earth, and saw a dish set, and workmen lying by it, and their hands were in the dish; and those who were chewing chewed not, and those who were lifting the food lifted it not, and those who put it to their mouth put it not thereto, but the faces of all of them were looking upward.

**4** And behold, there were sheep being driven, and they went not forward, but stood still; and the shepherd lifted his hand to smite them with his staff, and his hand remained up.

**5** And I looked upon the stream of the river, and saw the mouths of the kids upon the water and they drank not. And of a sudden all things moved onward in their course.
