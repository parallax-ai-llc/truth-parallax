---
title: "Protoevangelium of James - Chapter 21"
scripture: "protoevangelium-of-james"
chapter: 21
---

**1** And Herod searched for John, and sent officers to Zacharias at the altar, saying unto him: Where hast thou hidden thy son?

**2** And he answered and said unto them: I am a minister of God, and a servant at the temple of the Lord: I know not where my son is.

**3** And the officers departed, and told all these things to Herod. And Herod was wroth, and said: His son is to be king over Israel.

**4** And he sent to him again, saying: Tell the truth; where is thy son? For thou knowest that thy blood is under my hand.

**5** And the officers departed, and told him all these things.

**6** And Zacharias said: I am a martyr of God if thou shed my blood; for my spirit the Lord shall receive, because thou sheddest innocent blood in the forecourt of the temple of the Lord.

**7** And about the dawning of the day Zacharias was slain. And the sons of Israel knew not that he had been slain.
