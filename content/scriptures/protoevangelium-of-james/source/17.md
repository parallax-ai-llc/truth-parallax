---
title: "Protoevangelium of James - Chapter 17"
scripture: "protoevangelium-of-james"
chapter: 17
---

**1** And behold, a woman coming down from the hill country, and she said to me: Man, where goest thou? And I said: I seek a midwife of the Hebrews.

**2** And she responded and said unto me: Art thou of Israel? And I said unto her: Yes.

**3** And she said: And who is she that brings forth in the cave? And I said: She who is betrothed unto me.

**4** And she said to me: Is she not thy wife? And I said to her: It is Mary that was brought up in the temple of the Lord; and I received her by lot as my wife, and she is not my wife, but she has conception by the Holy Spirit.

**5** And the midwife said unto him: Is this true? And Joseph said unto her: Come hither, and see.

**6** And the midwife went with him. And they stood in the place of the cave; and behold, a bright cloud overshadowed the cave. And the midwife said: My soul is magnified this day, because mine eyes have seen marvellous things; for salvation is born unto Israel.

**7** And immediately the cloud withdrew itself out of the cave, and a great light appeared in the cave so that our eyes could not endure it. And by little and little that light withdrew itself until the young child appeared; and it went and took the breast of its mother Mary.

**8** And the midwife cried aloud and said: Great unto me is this day, in that I have seen this new sight.
