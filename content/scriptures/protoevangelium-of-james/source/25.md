---
title: "Protoevangelium of James - Chapter 25"
scripture: "protoevangelium-of-james"
chapter: 25
---

**1** And grace was upon all them that heard these things, and they glorified God and praised his name.

**2** And the account of the infancy of the Lord and of his mother Mary was kept among the secret writings, and was treasured by the faithful.

**3** For the angel had said: These things shall be made known in the fullness of time, when the Lord shall choose to reveal them to his people.

**4** And they who had seen and heard these things marvelled, and kept all these words in their hearts, pondering them.

**5** And when the time of Herod's death was come, there was peace again in the land of Judea, and the child grew in wisdom and in stature.

**6** And Joseph and Mary returned from the place where they had fled, and dwelt in the land of Israel, as the angel of the Lord had commanded.

**7** And the child Jesus grew, and the grace of God was upon him.

**8** These are the words of James, the brother of the Lord, concerning the birth of the holy God-bearer Mary, and the infancy of our Lord Jesus Christ. Glory be to God, who reigns forever. Amen.
