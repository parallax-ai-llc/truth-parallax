---
title: "Protoevangelium of James - Chapter 14"
scripture: "protoevangelium-of-james"
chapter: 14
---

**1** And the high priest said: Give back the virgin whom thou didst receive out of the temple of the Lord. And Joseph burst into tears.

**2** And the high priest said: I will give you to drink of the water of the conviction of the Lord, and it will make manifest your sins before your eyes.

**3** And the high priest took it, and gave it to Joseph to drink, and sent him to the hill country; and he returned whole. And he made Mary also drink, and sent her to the hill country; and she returned whole.

**4** And all the people marvelled, because sin did not appear in them. And the high priest said: If the Lord God has not made manifest your sins, neither do I condemn you. And he released them.

**5** And Joseph took Mary, and departed unto his house, rejoicing and glorifying the God of Israel.
