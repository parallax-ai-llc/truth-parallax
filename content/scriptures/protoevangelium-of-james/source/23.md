---
title: "Protoevangelium of James - Chapter 23"
scripture: "protoevangelium-of-james"
chapter: 23
---

**1** And I, James, who wrote this history in Jerusalem, when there arose a tumult when Herod died, withdrew myself to the wilderness until the tumult ceased in Jerusalem.

**2** Glorifying the Lord God, who gave me the gift and the wisdom to write this history.

**3** And grace shall be with them that fear our Lord Jesus Christ, to whom be glory for ever and ever. Amen.
