---
title: "Protoevangelium of James - Chapter 22"
scripture: "protoevangelium-of-james"
chapter: 22
---

**1** And at the hour of the salutation the priests were going away, and Zacharias did not come forth to meet them with a blessing, according to his custom.

**2** And the priests stood waiting for Zacharias, to salute him with prayer, and to glorify the Most High God.

**3** But when he delayed coming, they were all afraid. And one of them took courage, and went into the sanctuary, and he saw beside the altar of the Lord coagulated blood.

**4** And a voice said: Zacharias has been slain, and his blood shall not be wiped up until his avenger come.

**5** And when he heard these words he was afraid, and went forth and told the priests.

**6** And they took courage, and went in, and saw what had been done; and the panels of the temple cried aloud, and they rent their clothes from the top even to the bottom.

**7** And his body they found not, but his blood they found turned into stone.

**8** And they were afraid, and went forth and told all the people that Zacharias had been slain. And all the tribes of the people heard it, and they mourned for him, and lamented him three days and three nights.

**9** And after the three days the priests took counsel as to whom they should put in his place; and the lot fell upon Simeon. Now it was he to whom it had been revealed by the Holy Spirit that he should not see death until he should see the Christ in the flesh.
