---
title: "Protoevangelium of James - Chapter 20"
scripture: "protoevangelium-of-james"
chapter: 20
---

**1** And when Herod perceived that he had been mocked by the wise men, he was wroth, and sent murderers, saying to them: Kill the children from two years old and under.

**2** And when Mary heard that the children were being killed, she was afraid, and took the young child, and swaddled him, and put him into a stall of oxen.

**3** And Elizabeth, hearing that they were searching for John, took him and went up into the hill country, and looked around to see where she could hide him.

**4** And there was no hiding place. And Elizabeth groaned aloud, and said: O mountain of God, receive a mother with a child. For Elizabeth could not go up further.

**5** And immediately the mountain was cleft, and received her. And that mountain made a light to gleam for her; for an angel of the Lord was with them, watching over them.
