---
title: "Protoevangelium of James - Chapter 5"
scripture: "protoevangelium-of-james"
chapter: 5
---

**1** And on the morrow he brought his offerings, saying in himself: If the Lord God be reconciled to me, the plate that is upon the priest's forehead will make it manifest to me. And Joachim brought his offerings, and observed earnestly the priest's plate when he went up to the altar of the Lord, and he saw no sin in himself.

**2** And Joachim said: Now I know that the Lord God is reconciled unto me, and hath remitted all my sins. And he went down from the temple of the Lord justified, and departed to his own house.

**3** And her months were fulfilled, and in the ninth month Anna brought forth. And she said to the midwife: What have I brought forth? And she said: A girl. And said Anna: My soul hath been magnified this day. And she laid it down. And the days were fulfilled, and Anna was purified, and gave the breast to the child, and called her name Mary.
