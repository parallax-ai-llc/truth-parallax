Chapter 8 of the Nine Ways of Bon, addressing primordial shen, holds particular significance within the Bon tradition. The themes of highest tantra, supreme yoga are central to Bon theology and practice, and this chapter has been a focus of traditional interpretation and spiritual reflection.

Within Bon tradition, this chapter's treatment of primordial shen is understood not merely as historical or theoretical but as a living source of guidance for faith and practice. The teachings on highest tantra, supreme yoga inform Bon self-understanding and continue to shape communal identity and spiritual aspiration.
