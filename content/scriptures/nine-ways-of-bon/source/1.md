---
title: "Nine Ways of Bon - Chapter 1"
scripture: "nine-ways-of-bon"
chapter: 1
---

**1** In the name of Tonpa Shenrab Miwoche, the great teacher of the Bon tradition, who brought the light of wisdom to the world of men:

**2** The first way is called Chashen, the Way of the Shen of Prediction. This is the path of divination, astrology, and the reading of omens.

**3** By observing the stars, the movements of birds, the patterns of smoke, and the casting of lots, the Shen of Prediction discerns the will of the gods and the course of future events.

**4** The diviner examines the cracks in heated bones and the patterns of thrown grain. He reads the flight of vultures and the direction of the wind.

**5** Through these arts, illness is diagnosed, enemies are identified, lost objects are found, and the proper time for action is determined.

**6** The Shen of Prediction also practices the art of calculating — the science of the elements and their interactions, the cycles of time, and the influences of the planets.

**7** He determines the auspicious and inauspicious days for marriages, journeys, building, and all important undertakings.

**8** This is the foundation of all the ways, for without knowledge of the unseen forces, one cannot navigate the world of appearances.

**9** The Shen of Prediction serves the community by revealing the hidden causes of suffering and by prescribing the appropriate rituals and remedies.

**10** Thus the first way establishes the ground upon which the higher ways are built, connecting the visible world with the invisible, the human realm with the realm of the gods.
