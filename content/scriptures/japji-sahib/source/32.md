---
title: "Pauri 32 - One Tongue"
scripture: "japji-sahib"
chapter: 32
---

If one tongue became one hundred thousand, and one hundred thousand became twenty million, each tongue would repeat the Name of the One Lord hundreds of thousands of times. Along this path to the Lord, the steps ascend. Hearing of the celestial realm, even worms long to come back home. O Nanak, by His grace He is obtained. False are the boasters.
