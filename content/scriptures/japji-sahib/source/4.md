---
title: "Pauri 4 - The True Lord"
scripture: "japji-sahib"
chapter: 4
---

Sachaa sahib saach naa-ay bhaakhi-aa bhaa-o apaar.
Aakhahi mangahi deh deh daat karay daataar.

True is the Master, True is His Name; it is spoken with infinite love.
People beg and beg, and the Giver keeps on giving.
So what can we offer Him that we might see His court?
What words can we utter that He might bestow His love?
In the ambrosial hours of the morning, contemplate the true Name and His greatness.
By the karma of past actions, the robe of this physical body is obtained; by His grace, the gate of liberation is found.
O Nanak, know this: the True One Himself is all.
