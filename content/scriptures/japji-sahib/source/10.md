---
title: "Pauri 10 - By Listening"
scripture: "japji-sahib"
chapter: 10
---

Sunni-ai sat santokh gi-aan.
Sunni-ai athsath kaa isnaan.

By listening, truth, contentment, and spiritual wisdom are obtained.
By listening, it is like bathing at the sixty-eight sacred shrines.
By listening, one receives honor in the divine court.
By listening, one intuitively grasps meditation.
O Nanak, the devotees are forever in bliss.
By listening, sorrow and sin are erased.
