---
title: "Pauri 29 - Divine Knowledge"
scripture: "japji-sahib"
chapter: 29
---

Let divine knowledge be your food, and compassion your attendant. In each and every heart, the celestial music resounds. He Himself is the Lord and Master. He Himself is the one who listens. The creation and destruction of the universe belong to the One Lord. He watches over all, and with His glance of grace, He bestows happiness. He Himself is the Giver, the Lord of the world.
