---
title: "Pauri 8 - By Listening"
scripture: "japji-sahib"
chapter: 8
---

Sunni-ai sidh peer sur naath.
Sunni-ai dharat dhaval aakaas.

By listening, one becomes a spiritual hero, a divine being, a Nath.
By listening, one understands the earth, its support, and the sky.
By listening, one knows the continents, the worlds, and the nether regions.
By listening, death cannot touch you.
O Nanak, the devotees are forever in bliss.
By listening, sorrow and sin are erased.
