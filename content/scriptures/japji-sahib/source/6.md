---
title: "Pauri 6 - The Sacred Shrines"
scripture: "japji-sahib"
chapter: 6
---

Tirath naavaa jay tis bhaavaa vin bhaanay ke naa-ay karee.
Jaytee sirath upaa-ee vaykhaa vin karmaa ke milay la-ee.

If it pleases Him, I bathe at sacred shrines. If it does not please Him, what is the use?
In all the created universe that I behold, without good karma, what can anyone obtain?
Within the mind are precious gems, jewels, and rubies, if one listens to even one teaching of the Guru.
The Guru has given me this one understanding: there is only the One, the Giver of all souls. May I never forget Him!
