---
title: "Pauri 19 - Countless Names"
scripture: "japji-sahib"
chapter: 19
---

Countless are His names, countless His places. Inaccessible and unapproachable are His countless heavenly realms. Even to call them countless is a burden on the head. By words we name Him, by words we praise Him. By words we know, we sing, we write. In words our destiny is recorded on our foreheads. But the One who writes these words of destiny—no words are carved on His forehead. As He ordains, so do we receive.
