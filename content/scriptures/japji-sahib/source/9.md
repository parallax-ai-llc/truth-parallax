---
title: "Pauri 9 - By Listening"
scripture: "japji-sahib"
chapter: 9
---

Sunni-ai eesar barmaa ind.
Sunni-ai mukh saalaahan mand.

By listening, one becomes like Shiva, Brahma, and Indra.
By listening, even the lowly can offer praise.
By listening, the technology of Yoga and the secrets of the body are revealed.
By listening, one understands the Shastras, the Simritees, and the Vedas.
O Nanak, the devotees are forever in bliss.
By listening, sorrow and sin are erased.
