---
title: "Pauri 37 - The Realm of Grace"
scripture: "japji-sahib"
chapter: 37
---

In the realm of grace, the word is power. Nothing else prevails there. The bravest warriors and the greatest heroes dwell there. They are totally saturated with the Lord. They cannot be described. They do not die, and they are not deceived. The devotees of many worlds dwell there. They are in bliss because the True Lord is in their hearts.
