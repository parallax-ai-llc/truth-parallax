---
title: "Pauri 40 - Salok - The Final Verse"
scripture: "japji-sahib"
chapter: 40
---

As the goldsmith melts gold in the furnace and tests it on the touchstone, so the Lord tests His creatures. By the True Lord's grace, the pure are known. The impure are beaten and cast into the furnace again. O Nanak, by His glance of grace, He bestows His blessings on those who are pleasing to His will. Nanak, the lowly, speaks: by the grace of the True Guru, the Lord is found. Air is the Guru, water the father, the great earth the mother. Day and night are the two nurses, male and female, in whose lap the whole world plays. Good deeds and bad—the record is read out in the presence of the Lord of Dharma. According to our own actions, some are near and some are far. Those who have meditated on the Name, their toils shall end. O Nanak, their faces shall glow, and many shall be saved along with them.
