---
title: "Pauri 28 - The Yogi's Horn"
scripture: "japji-sahib"
chapter: 28
---

Make contentment your earrings, humility your begging bowl, and meditation the ashes you apply to your body. Let the remembrance of death be the patched coat you wear. Let purity be your way in the world, and faith your walking stick. Let the brotherhood of all be the highest order of Yogis. Know that to conquer the mind is to conquer the world. Hail! Hail to Him! The Primal One, the Immaculate, the Imperishable, the Unchanging through all ages.
