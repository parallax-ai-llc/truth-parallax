---
title: "Pauri 11 - By Listening"
scripture: "japji-sahib"
chapter: 11
---

By listening, one dives deep into virtue. By listening, one becomes a Sheikh, a Pir, a spiritual king. By listening, the blind find the path. By listening, the unfathomable becomes fathomable. O Nanak, the devotees are forever in bliss. By listening, sorrow and sin are erased.
