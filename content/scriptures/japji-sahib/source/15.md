---
title: "Pauri 15 - The Faithful"
scripture: "japji-sahib"
chapter: 15
---

The faithful have power; the faithful have wisdom. The faithful know all the worlds. The faithful shall not be struck in the face. The faithful do not have to go with the Messenger of Death. Such is the path of the faithful, that they walk with the Lord upon their foreheads. O Nanak, the faithful merge imperceptibly into the Lord.
