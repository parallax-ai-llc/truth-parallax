---
title: "Pauri 13 - By Knowing"
scripture: "japji-sahib"
chapter: 13
---

By knowing Him, the mind is uplifted and enlightened. By knowing Him, all the world and its underworlds are known. By knowing Him, one is not struck by death. Such is the power of the Name of the Immaculate One. Only those who have faith know the joy of the mind.
