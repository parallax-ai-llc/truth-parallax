---
title: "Pauri 16 - The Five Chosen"
scripture: "japji-sahib"
chapter: 16
---

The five chosen ones are accepted and approved. The five chosen ones are distinguished in the court of the Lord. The five chosen ones are beautiful in the court of the Lord. The five chosen ones meditate single-mindedly on the Guru. No matter how much anyone speaks and tries to describe them, their actions cannot be recounted. The bull of Dharma is the offspring of compassion.
