---
title: "Pauri 17 - Countless Praises"
scripture: "japji-sahib"
chapter: 17
---

Countless are His praises, countless the ways to speak them. Countless are His deeds, countless His gifts. Countless His sights, countless His sounds. Countless His forms of existence, countless His mysteries. Even to speak of this, countless have tried. But the beings have not been able to say enough.
