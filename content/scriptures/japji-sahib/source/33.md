---
title: "Pauri 33 - No Power"
scripture: "japji-sahib"
chapter: 33
---

I have no power to speak, and no power to be silent. I have no power to beg, and no power to give. I have no power to live, and no power to die. I have no power to rule with wealth and worldly power. I have no power to meditate and gain spiritual wisdom. I have no power to escape the world. He alone has the power, He watches over all. O Nanak, no one is high or low.
