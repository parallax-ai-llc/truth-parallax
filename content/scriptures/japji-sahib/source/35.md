---
title: "Pauri 35 - The Realm of Dharma"
scripture: "japji-sahib"
chapter: 35
---

In the realm of Dharma, Dharma is practiced. In the realm of knowledge, knowledge is the way. In the realm of beauty, how wondrous are the forms. In the realm of grace, the power is in the Word of the Lord. The realms of power and bliss are beyond description.
