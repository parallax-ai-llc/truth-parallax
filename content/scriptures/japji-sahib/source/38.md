---
title: "Pauri 38 - The Forge"
scripture: "japji-sahib"
chapter: 38
---

Let self-control be the furnace, and patience the goldsmith. Let understanding be the anvil, and spiritual wisdom the tools. With the Fear of God as the bellows, fan the flames of devotion. In the crucible of love, melt the Nectar of the Name. In this true mint, forge the Word of the Lord. This is the labor of those upon whom He has cast His glance of grace. O Nanak, the Gracious Lord, by His grace, uplifts and exalts them.
