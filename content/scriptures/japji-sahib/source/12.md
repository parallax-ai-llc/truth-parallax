---
title: "Pauri 12 - The State of the Faithful"
scripture: "japji-sahib"
chapter: 12
---

The state of the faithful cannot be described. Whoever tries to describe it shall regret the attempt. No paper, no pen, no scribe can record the state of the faithful. Such is the power of the immaculate Name. Only one who has faith knows the state of faith within the mind.
