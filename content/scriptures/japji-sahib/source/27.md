---
title: "Pauri 27 - The Lord's Seat"
scripture: "japji-sahib"
chapter: 27
---

In what house, in what mansion do You sit? In that place, all are cared for and watched over. Countless instruments and countless harmonies play; countless are the players. Countless are the Ragas, countless the singers. They sing to You, O Lord. Wind, water, and fire sing to You. The Righteous Judge of Dharma sings at Your door. Chitr and Gupt, the angels of the conscious and the subconscious, sing. They know all and write the account, over which the Righteous Judge presides. Shiva, Brahma, and the Goddess of Beauty sing. Indra seated on his throne sings, and so do the Siddhas in meditation. The celibates, the truthful, the contented, and the fearless warriors all sing. The Pandits, the most senior religious scholars, sing, along with the Rishis from age to age.
