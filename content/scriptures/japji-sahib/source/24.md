---
title: "Pauri 24 - Endless Praises"
scripture: "japji-sahib"
chapter: 24
---

Endless are His praises, endless those who speak them. Endless are His deeds, endless His gifts. Endless His sights, endless His sounds. Endless the mysteries of His mind. Endless the creation, endless the expanse. Many struggle to find His limits, but His limits cannot be found. No one knows these limits. The more you say about them, the more there remains to be said. Great is the Lord, high is His position. Higher than the highest is His Name. Only one as great as He can know His lofty state. O Nanak, only He knows Himself. By His glance of grace, He bestows His blessings.
