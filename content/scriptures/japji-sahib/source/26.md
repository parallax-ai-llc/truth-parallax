---
title: "Pauri 26 - The Priceless Merchandise"
scripture: "japji-sahib"
chapter: 26
---

Priceless is His praise, priceless is His adoration. Priceless are His dealers, priceless their trade. Priceless are the weights, priceless the measures. Priceless are His blessings, priceless His signs. Priceless is His mercy, priceless His command. How priceless He is cannot be stated. Those who try to describe Him remain absorbed in Him. The scholars of the Vedas speak of Him. The teachers speak, and try to describe Him. Brahma speaks, Indra speaks. The Gopis and Krishna speak. Shiva speaks, the Siddhas speak. Many Buddhas speak. The demons speak, the demigods speak. The spiritual warriors, the divine beings, the silent sages, the humble servants all speak. Many speak and try to describe Him. Many have spoken and many will speak. He is not made less by any amount of speaking. He is the Giver; all others are beggars. O Nanak, what is there left to say?
