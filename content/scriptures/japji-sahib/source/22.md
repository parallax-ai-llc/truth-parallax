---
title: "Pauri 22 - Countless Worlds"
scripture: "japji-sahib"
chapter: 22
---

Countless are the worlds below, and countless the skies above. One grows weary trying to explore their limits. The Vedas say there are 18,000 worlds—in reality, there is only the One. If it could be written, it would have been; but people have exhausted themselves writing. O Nanak, call Him great! He Himself knows Himself.
