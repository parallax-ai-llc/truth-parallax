---
title: "Pauri 31 - His Seat"
scripture: "japji-sahib"
chapter: 31
---

He has His seat in every world, in every realm. His storerooms are overflowing. Whatever He has to place there, He has placed once and for all. Having created the creation, the Creator Lord watches over it. O Nanak, true is the creation of the True Lord. Hail to the Primal, the Immaculate.
