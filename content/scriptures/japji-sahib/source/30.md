---
title: "Pauri 30 - The One Mother"
scripture: "japji-sahib"
chapter: 30
---

From the One Mother, the world was born. She bore three disciples: one, the creator of the world; one, the sustainer; and one, the destroyer. He makes things happen according to the pleasure of His will. He watches over all, but no one sees Him. How wonderful this is! Hail to the Lord of all!
