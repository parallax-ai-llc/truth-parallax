---
title: "Pauri 25 - His Bounty"
scripture: "japji-sahib"
chapter: 25
---

His bounty is so great that it cannot be written. The Great Giver does not hold back anything. How many great warriors beg at His door. So many consume and waste His gifts; so many keep on receiving them. So many are ruined by their own foolishness. So many receive, but deny receiving. So many foolish consumers keep on consuming. So many endure distress, deprivation, and abuse. Even these are Your gifts, O Great Giver.
