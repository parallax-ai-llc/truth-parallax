---
title: "Pauri 14 - The Path"
scripture: "japji-sahib"
chapter: 14
---

The path cannot be described in words. Whoever tries shall regret the attempt. No power of speech, no power of silence, no power of asking, no power of giving. No power of living, no power of dying. All power belongs to the Lord. O Nanak, no one is higher or lower.
