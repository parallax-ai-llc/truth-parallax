---
title: "Pauri 21 - Pilgrimage and Austerity"
scripture: "japji-sahib"
chapter: 21
---

Pilgrimages, austere discipline, compassion, and charity—these bring only a tiny bit of merit. Listening, believing, and loving the Name—cleansing by bathing at the sacred shrine deep within. All virtues are Yours, Lord; I have none. Without virtuous actions, devotional worship cannot be performed.
