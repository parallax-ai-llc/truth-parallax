---
title: "Pauri 18 - Countless Fools"
scripture: "japji-sahib"
chapter: 18
---

Countless are the fools, blind in their ignorance. Countless are the thieves and cheaters. Countless impose their will by force. Countless the cut-throats and tyrants. Countless the sinners who keep on sinning. Countless the liars, wandering in their falsehood. Countless the filthy, consuming filth. Countless the slanderers, carrying the weight of their evil on their heads.
