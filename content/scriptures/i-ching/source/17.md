**Hexagram 17: Sui — Following**

☱☳

**The Judgment:** Sui indicates that, under its conditions, there will be great progress and success. But it will be advantageous to be firm and correct. There will then be no error.

**The Image:** Thunder in the middle of the lake. Thus the superior man at nightfall goes indoors for rest and recuperation.

**Line 1 (Initial Nine):** The standard is changing. Perseverance brings good fortune. To go out of the door in company produces deeds.

**Line 2 (Six in the Second Place):** If one clings to the small boy, one loses the strong man.

**Line 3 (Six in the Third Place):** If one clings to the strong man, one loses the small boy. Through following one finds what one seeks. It furthers one to remain persevering.

**Line 4 (Nine in the Fourth Place):** Following creates success. Perseverance brings misfortune. To go one's way with sincerity brings clarity. How could there be blame in this?

**Line 5 (Nine in the Fifth Place):** Sincere in the good. Good fortune.

**Line 6 (Top Six):** He meets with firm allegiance and is still further bound. The king introduces him to the Western Mountain.
