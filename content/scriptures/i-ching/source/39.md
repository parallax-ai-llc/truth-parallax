**Hexagram 39: Jian — Obstruction**

☵☶

**The Judgment:** Jian intimates that the south-west will be advantageous. The north-east will not be advantageous. It will be advantageous to meet with the great man. Firmness and correctness will lead to good fortune.

**The Image:** Water on the mountain. Thus the superior man turns his attention to himself and moulds his character.

**Line 1 (Initial Six):** Going leads to obstructions, coming meets with praise.

**Line 2 (Six in the Second Place):** The king's servant is beset by obstruction upon obstruction, but it is not his own fault.

**Line 3 (Nine in the Third Place):** Going leads to obstructions; hence he comes back.

**Line 4 (Six in the Fourth Place):** Going leads to obstructions, coming leads to union.

**Line 5 (Nine in the Fifth Place):** In the midst of the greatest obstructions, friends come.

**Line 6 (Top Six):** Going leads to obstructions, coming leads to great good fortune. It furthers one to see the great man.
