**Hexagram 22: Bi — Grace**

☶☲

**The Judgment:** Bi indicates that there should be free course in what it denotes. There will be little advantage however if it be allowed to advance and take the lead.

**The Image:** Fire at the foot of the mountain. Thus the superior man proceeds to clarify all current affairs, but he dare not decide great controversies in this way.

**Line 1 (Initial Nine):** He lends grace to his toes, leaves the carriage, and walks.

**Line 2 (Six in the Second Place):** Lends grace to the beard on his chin.

**Line 3 (Nine in the Third Place):** Graceful and moist. Constant perseverance brings good fortune.

**Line 4 (Six in the Fourth Place):** Grace or simplicity? A white horse comes as if on wings. He is not a robber; he will woo at the proper time.

**Line 5 (Six in the Fifth Place):** Grace in hills and gardens. The roll of silk is meagre and small. Humiliation, but in the end good fortune.

**Line 6 (Top Nine):** Simple grace. No blame.
