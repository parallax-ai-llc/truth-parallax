**Hexagram 9: Xiao Chu — The Taming Power of the Small**

☴☰

**The Judgment:** Xiao Chu intimates that, under its conditions, there will be progress and success. Dense clouds, but no rain from our borders in the west.

**The Image:** The wind drives across heaven. Thus the superior man refines the outward aspect of his nature.

**Line 1 (Initial Nine):** Return to the way. How could there be blame in this? Good fortune.

**Line 2 (Nine in the Second Place):** He allows himself to be drawn into returning. Good fortune.

**Line 3 (Nine in the Third Place):** The spokes burst out of the wagon wheels. Man and wife roll their eyes.

**Line 4 (Six in the Fourth Place):** If you are sincere, blood vanishes and fear gives way. No blame.

**Line 5 (Nine in the Fifth Place):** If you are sincere and loyally attached, you are rich in your neighbour.

**Line 6 (Top Nine):** The rain comes, there is rest. This is due to the lasting effect of character. Perseverance is dangerous for the woman. The moon is nearly full. If the superior man persists, misfortune comes.
