**Hexagram 57: Xun — The Gentle/Wind**

☴☴

**The Judgment:** Xun intimates that, under the conditions which it denotes, there will be some little attainment and progress. There will be advantage in movement onward in whatever direction. It will be advantageous also to see the great man.

**The Image:** Winds following one upon another: the image of the Gently Penetrating. Thus the superior man spreads his commands abroad and carries out his undertakings.

**Line 1 (Initial Six):** In advancing and in retreating, the perseverance of a warrior furthers.

**Line 2 (Nine in the Second Place):** Penetration under the bed. Priests and magicians are used in great number. Good fortune. No blame.

**Line 3 (Nine in the Third Place):** Repeated penetration. Humiliation.

**Line 4 (Six in the Fourth Place):** Remorse vanishes. During the hunt three kinds of game are caught.

**Line 5 (Nine in the Fifth Place):** Perseverance brings good fortune. Remorse vanishes. Nothing that does not further. No beginning, but an end. Before the change, three days. After the change, three days. Good fortune.

**Line 6 (Top Nine):** Penetration under the bed. He loses his property and his axe. Perseverance brings misfortune.
