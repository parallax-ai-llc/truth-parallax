**Hexagram 16: Yu — Enthusiasm**

☳☷

**The Judgment:** Yu indicates that it will be advantageous to install feudal princes and to put the hosts in motion.

**The Image:** Thunder comes resounding out of the earth. Thus the ancient kings made music in order to honour merit, and offered it with splendour to the Supreme Deity, inviting their ancestors to be present.

**Line 1 (Initial Six):** Enthusiasm that expresses itself brings misfortune.

**Line 2 (Six in the Second Place):** Firm as a rock. Not a whole day. Perseverance brings good fortune.

**Line 3 (Six in the Third Place):** Enthusiasm that looks upward creates remorse. Hesitation brings remorse.

**Line 4 (Nine in the Fourth Place):** The source of enthusiasm. He achieves great things. Doubt not. You gather friends around you as a hair clasp gathers the hair.

**Line 5 (Six in the Fifth Place):** Persistently ill, and still does not die.

**Line 6 (Top Six):** Deluded enthusiasm. But if after completion one changes, there is no blame.
