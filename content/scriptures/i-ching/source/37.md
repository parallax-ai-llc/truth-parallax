**Hexagram 37: Jia Ren — The Family/The Clan**

☴☲

**The Judgment:** Jia Ren, or the household, shows that what is most advantageous in it is that the wife be firm and correct.

**The Image:** Wind comes forth from fire. Thus the superior man has substance in his words and duration in his way of life.

**Line 1 (Initial Nine):** Firm seclusion within the family. Remorse disappears.

**Line 2 (Six in the Second Place):** She should not follow her whims. She must attend within to the food. Perseverance brings good fortune.

**Line 3 (Nine in the Third Place):** When tempers flare up in the family, too great severity brings remorse. Good fortune nonetheless. When woman and child dally and laugh, it leads in the end to humiliation.

**Line 4 (Six in the Fourth Place):** She is the treasure of the house. Great good fortune.

**Line 5 (Nine in the Fifth Place):** As a king he approaches his family. Fear not. Good fortune.

**Line 6 (Top Nine):** His work commands respect. In the end good fortune comes.
