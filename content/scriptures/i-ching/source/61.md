**Hexagram 61: Zhong Fu — Inner Truth**

☴☱

**The Judgment:** Zhong Fu, or inner truth, moves even pigs and fish, and leads to good fortune. There will be advantage in crossing the great stream. There will be advantage in being firm and correct.

**The Image:** Wind over the lake: the image of Inner Truth. Thus the superior man discusses criminal cases in order to delay executions.

**Line 1 (Initial Nine):** Being prepared brings good fortune. If there are secret designs, it is disquieting.

**Line 2 (Nine in the Second Place):** A crane calling in the shade. Its young answers it. I have a good goblet. I will share it with you.

**Line 3 (Six in the Third Place):** He finds a comrade. Now he beats the drum, now he stops. Now he sobs, now he sings.

**Line 4 (Six in the Fourth Place):** The moon nearly at the full. The team horse goes astray. No blame.

**Line 5 (Nine in the Fifth Place):** He possesses truth, which links together. No blame.

**Line 6 (Top Nine):** Cockcrow penetrating to heaven. Perseverance brings misfortune.
