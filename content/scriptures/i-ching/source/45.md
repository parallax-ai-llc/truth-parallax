**Hexagram 45: Cui — Gathering Together/Massing**

☱☷

**The Judgment:** Cui indicates that, in the state which it denotes, the king will repair to his ancestral temple. It will be advantageous to meet with the great man; and then there will be progress and success, though the advantage must come through firm correctness. The use of great victims will conduce to good fortune. It will be advantageous to go forward in any direction.

**The Image:** Over the earth, the lake. Thus the superior man renews his weapons in order to meet the unforeseen.

**Line 1 (Initial Six):** If you are sincere, but not to the end, there will sometimes be confusion, sometimes gathering together. If you call out, then after one grasp of the hand you can laugh again. Regret not. Going is without blame.

**Line 2 (Six in the Second Place):** Letting oneself be drawn brings good fortune and remains without blame. If one is sincere, it furthers one to bring even a small offering.

**Line 3 (Six in the Third Place):** Gathering together amid sighs. Nothing that would further. Going is without blame. Slight humiliation.

**Line 4 (Nine in the Fourth Place):** Great good fortune. No blame.

**Line 5 (Nine in the Fifth Place):** If in gathering together one has position, this brings no blame. If there are some who are not yet sincerely in the work, sublime and enduring perseverance is needed. Then remorse disappears.

**Line 6 (Top Six):** Lamenting and sighing, floods of tears. No blame.
