**Hexagram 46: Sheng — Pushing Upward**

☷☴

**The Judgment:** Sheng indicates that, in the state which it denotes, there will be great progress and success. Seeking by the qualities implied in it to meet with the great man, its subject need have no anxiety. Advance to the south will be fortunate.

**The Image:** Within the earth, wood grows. Thus the superior man of devoted character heaps up small things in order to achieve something high and great.

**Line 1 (Initial Six):** Pushing upward that meets with confidence brings great good fortune.

**Line 2 (Nine in the Second Place):** If one is sincere, it furthers one to bring even a small offering. No blame.

**Line 3 (Nine in the Third Place):** One pushes upward into an empty city.

**Line 4 (Six in the Fourth Place):** The king offers him Mount Qi. Good fortune. No blame.

**Line 5 (Six in the Fifth Place):** Perseverance brings good fortune. One pushes upward by steps.

**Line 6 (Top Six):** Pushing upward in darkness. It furthers one to be unremittingly persevering.
