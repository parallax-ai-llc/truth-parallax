**Hexagram 35: Jin — Progress**

☲☷

**The Judgment:** Jin denotes a state in which the strong prince is honoured with horses in large numbers, and the interviews with the sovereign are frequent, taking place three times in a single day.

**The Image:** The sun rises over the earth. Thus the superior man himself brightens his bright virtue.

**Line 1 (Initial Six):** Progressing, but turned back. Perseverance brings good fortune. If one meets with no confidence, one should remain calm. No mistake.

**Line 2 (Six in the Second Place):** Progressing, but in sorrow. Perseverance brings good fortune. Then one obtains great happiness from one's ancestress.

**Line 3 (Six in the Third Place):** All are in accord. Remorse disappears.

**Line 4 (Nine in the Fourth Place):** Progress like a hamster. Perseverance brings danger.

**Line 5 (Six in the Fifth Place):** Remorse disappears. Take not gain and loss to heart. Undertakings bring good fortune. Everything serves to further.

**Line 6 (Top Nine):** Making progress with the horns is permissible only for the purpose of punishing one's own city. To be conscious of danger brings good fortune. No blame. Perseverance brings humiliation.
