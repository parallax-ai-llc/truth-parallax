**Hexagram 18: Gu — Work on What Has Been Spoiled/Decay**

☶☴

**The Judgment:** Gu indicates great progress and success to him who deals properly with the condition represented by it. It will be advantageous to cross the great stream. He should weigh well, however, the events of three days before the turning point, and those to be done three days after it.

**The Image:** The wind blows low on the mountain. Thus the superior man stirs up the people and strengthens their spirit.

**Line 1 (Initial Six):** Setting right what has been spoiled by the father. If there is a son, no blame rests upon the departed father. Danger. In the end, good fortune.

**Line 2 (Nine in the Second Place):** Setting right what has been spoiled by the mother. One must not be too persevering.

**Line 3 (Nine in the Third Place):** Setting right what has been spoiled by the father. There will be a little remorse. No great blame.

**Line 4 (Six in the Fourth Place):** Tolerating what has been spoiled by the father. In continuing one sees humiliation.

**Line 5 (Six in the Fifth Place):** Setting right what has been spoiled by the father. One meets with praise.

**Line 6 (Top Nine):** He does not serve kings and princes, sets himself higher goals.
