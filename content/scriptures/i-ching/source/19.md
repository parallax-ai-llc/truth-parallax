**Hexagram 19: Lin — Approach**

☷☱

**The Judgment:** Lin indicates that, under the conditions supposed by it, there will be great progress and success, while it will be advantageous to be firmly correct. In the eighth month there will be evil.

**The Image:** The earth above the lake. Thus the superior man is inexhaustible in his will to teach, and without limits in his tolerance and protection of the people.

**Line 1 (Initial Nine):** Joint approach. Perseverance brings good fortune.

**Line 2 (Nine in the Second Place):** Joint approach. Good fortune. Everything furthers.

**Line 3 (Six in the Third Place):** Comfortable approach. Nothing that would further. If one is induced to grieve about it, one becomes free of blame.

**Line 4 (Six in the Fourth Place):** Complete approach. No blame.

**Line 5 (Six in the Fifth Place):** Wise approach. This is right for a great prince. Good fortune.

**Line 6 (Top Six):** Great-hearted approach. Good fortune. No blame.
