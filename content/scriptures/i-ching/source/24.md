**Hexagram 24: Fu — Return/The Turning Point**

☷☳

**The Judgment:** Fu intimates that there will be free course and progress in what it denotes. The subject of it finds no one to distress him in his exits and entrances. Friends come to him, and no error is committed. He returns and repeats his proper course. In seven days comes his return. There will be advantage in whatever direction movement is made.

**The Image:** Thunder within the earth. Thus the kings of old closed the passes at the time of the solstice. Merchants and strangers did not go about, and the ruler did not travel through the provinces.

**Line 1 (Initial Nine):** Return from a short distance. No need for remorse. Great good fortune.

**Line 2 (Six in the Second Place):** Quiet return. Good fortune.

**Line 3 (Six in the Third Place):** Repeated return. Danger. No blame.

**Line 4 (Six in the Fourth Place):** Walking in the midst of others, one returns alone.

**Line 5 (Six in the Fifth Place):** Noble-hearted return. No remorse.

**Line 6 (Top Six):** Missing the return. Misfortune. Misfortune from within and without. If armies are set marching in this way, one will in the end suffer a great defeat, disastrous for the ruler of the country. For ten years it will not be possible to attack again.
