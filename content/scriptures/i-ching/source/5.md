**Hexagram 5: Xu — Waiting/Nourishment**

☵☰

**The Judgment:** Xu intimates that, with the sincerity which is indicated by it, there will be brilliant success. With firmness there will be good fortune. It will be advantageous to cross the great stream.

**The Image:** Clouds rise up to heaven. Thus the superior man eats and drinks, is joyous and of good cheer.

**Line 1 (Initial Nine):** Waiting in the meadow. It furthers one to abide in what endures. No blame.

**Line 2 (Nine in the Second Place):** Waiting on the sand. There is some gossip. The end brings good fortune.

**Line 3 (Nine in the Third Place):** Waiting in the mud. He brings about the arrival of the enemy.

**Line 4 (Six in the Fourth Place):** Waiting in blood. Get out of the pit.

**Line 5 (Nine in the Fifth Place):** Waiting at meat and drink. Perseverance brings good fortune.

**Line 6 (Top Six):** One falls into the pit. Three uninvited guests arrive. Honour them, and in the end there will be good fortune.
