**Hexagram 23: Bo — Splitting Apart**

☶☷

**The Judgment:** Bo indicates that in the state which it symbolises it will not be advantageous to make a movement in any direction whatever.

**The Image:** The mountain rests on the earth. Thus those above can ensure their position only by giving generously to those below.

**Line 1 (Initial Six):** The leg of the bed is split. Those who persevere are destroyed. Misfortune.

**Line 2 (Six in the Second Place):** The bed is split at the edge. Those who persevere are destroyed. Misfortune.

**Line 3 (Six in the Third Place):** He splits with them. No blame.

**Line 4 (Six in the Fourth Place):** The bed is split up to the skin. Misfortune.

**Line 5 (Six in the Fifth Place):** A string of fishes. Favour comes through the ladies of the court. Everything acts to further.

**Line 6 (Top Nine):** There is a large fruit still uneaten. The superior man receives a carriage. The house of the inferior man is split apart.
