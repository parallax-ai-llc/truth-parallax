**Hexagram 14: Da You — Possession in Great Measure**

☲☰

**The Judgment:** Da You indicates that, under the circumstances which it implies, there will be great progress and success.

**The Image:** Fire in heaven above. Thus the superior man curbs evil and promotes good, and thereby obeys the benevolent will of heaven.

**Line 1 (Initial Nine):** No relationship with what is harmful; there is no blame in this. If one remains conscious of difficulty, one remains without blame.

**Line 2 (Nine in the Second Place):** A big wagon for loading. One may undertake something. No blame.

**Line 3 (Nine in the Third Place):** A prince offers it to the Son of Heaven. A petty man cannot do this.

**Line 4 (Nine in the Fourth Place):** He makes a difference between himself and his neighbour. No blame.

**Line 5 (Six in the Fifth Place):** He whose truth is accessible, yet dignified, has good fortune.

**Line 6 (Top Nine):** He is blessed by heaven. Good fortune. Nothing that does not further.
