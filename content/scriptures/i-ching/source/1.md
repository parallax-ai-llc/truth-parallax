**Hexagram 1: Qian — The Creative/Heaven**

☰☰

**The Judgment:** Qian intimates that, under the conditions which it denotes, there will be great progress and success. It will be advantageous to be firm and correct.

**The Image:** The movement of heaven is full of power. Thus the superior man makes himself strong and untiring.

**Line 1 (Initial Nine):** The dragon lies hid in the deep. It is not the time for active doing.

**Line 2 (Nine in the Second Place):** The dragon appears in the field. It will be advantageous to meet with the great man.

**Line 3 (Nine in the Third Place):** The superior man is active and vigilant all the day, and in the evening still careful and apprehensive. The position is dangerous, but there will be no mistake.

**Line 4 (Nine in the Fourth Place):** He seems to be leaping up, but is still in the deep. There will be no mistake.

**Line 5 (Nine in the Fifth Place):** The dragon is on the wing in the sky. It will be advantageous to meet with the great man.

**Line 6 (Top Nine):** The dragon exceeds the proper limits. There will be occasion for repentance.
