**Hexagram 8: Bi — Holding Together/Union**

☵☷

**The Judgment:** Bi indicates that, under the conditions which it supposes, there is good fortune. Let the principal party intended in it re-examine himself, as if by divination, whether his virtue be great, unintermitting, and firm. If it be so, there will be no error. Those who have not rest will then come to him; and with those who are too late in coming it will be ill.

**The Image:** On the earth is water. Thus the kings of antiquity bestowed the different states as fiefs and cultivated friendly relations with the feudal lords.

**Line 1 (Initial Six):** Hold to him in truth and loyalty; this is without blame. Truth, like a full earthen bowl: thus in the end good fortune comes from without.

**Line 2 (Six in the Second Place):** Hold to him inwardly. Perseverance brings good fortune.

**Line 3 (Six in the Third Place):** You hold together with the wrong people.

**Line 4 (Six in the Fourth Place):** Hold to him outwardly also. Perseverance brings good fortune.

**Line 5 (Nine in the Fifth Place):** Manifestation of holding together. In the hunt, the king uses beaters on three sides only and forgoes game that runs off in front. The citizens need no warning. Good fortune.

**Line 6 (Top Six):** He finds no head for holding together. Misfortune.
