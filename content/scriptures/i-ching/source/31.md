**Hexagram 31: Xian — Influence/Wooing**

☱☶

**The Judgment:** Xian intimates that, on the fulfilment of the conditions implied in it, there will be free course and success. Its advantageousness will depend on the being firm and correct, as in marrying a young lady. There will be good fortune.

**The Image:** A lake on the mountain. Thus the superior man encourages people to approach him by his readiness to receive them.

**Line 1 (Initial Six):** The influence shows itself in the big toe.

**Line 2 (Six in the Second Place):** The influence shows itself in the calves of the legs. Misfortune. Tarrying brings good fortune.

**Line 3 (Nine in the Third Place):** The influence shows itself in the thighs. Holds to that which follows it. To continue is humiliating.

**Line 4 (Nine in the Fourth Place):** Perseverance brings good fortune. Remorse disappears. If a man is agitated in mind, and his thoughts go hither and thither, only those friends on whom he fixes his conscious thoughts will follow.

**Line 5 (Nine in the Fifth Place):** The influence shows itself in the back of the neck. No remorse.

**Line 6 (Top Six):** The influence shows itself in the jaws, cheeks, and tongue.
