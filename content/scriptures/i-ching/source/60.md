**Hexagram 60: Jie — Limitation**

☵☱

**The Judgment:** Jie intimates progress and attainment. But if the regulations which it denotes be severe and difficult, they cannot be permanent.

**The Image:** Water over the lake: the image of Limitation. Thus the superior man creates number and measure, and examines the nature of virtue and correct conduct.

**Line 1 (Initial Nine):** Not going out of the door and the courtyard is without blame.

**Line 2 (Nine in the Second Place):** Not going out of the gate and the courtyard brings misfortune.

**Line 3 (Six in the Third Place):** He who knows no limitation will have cause to lament. No blame.

**Line 4 (Six in the Fourth Place):** Contented limitation. Success.

**Line 5 (Nine in the Fifth Place):** Sweet limitation brings good fortune. Going brings esteem.

**Line 6 (Top Six):** Galling limitation. Perseverance brings misfortune. Remorse disappears.
