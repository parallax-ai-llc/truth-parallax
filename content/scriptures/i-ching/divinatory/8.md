The divinatory tradition reads the final hexagrams as addressing the most profound situations a questioner can face. Hexagram 63, Ji Ji (After Completion), paradoxically carries a warning: when everything seems to be in order, the seeds of future difficulty are already present. The oracle counsels vigilance and attention to small details at the very moment when the temptation to relax is greatest.

Hexagram 64, Wei Ji (Before Completion), as the oracle's final word, teaches that every consultation, like every situation, contains the potential for further development. The divinatory tradition understands this hexagram as a reminder that the oracle does not provide fixed answers but dynamic guidance. Each reading opens a conversation between the questioner and the cosmos that continues beyond the moment of consultation, as the situation evolves and the hexagram's guidance unfolds through time.
