In divinatory practice, the third group's hexagrams address situations of decline, transition, and renewal. Hexagram 23, Bo (Splitting Apart), warns the questioner that current structures are disintegrating and that resistance is futile — the appropriate response is to let go and wait for the natural turning point. Hexagram 24, Fu (Return), arriving in consultation signals that the turning point has come, and the questioner should act on the first stirrings of new possibility.

The divinatory tradition teaches that changing lines are particularly significant in this group because they reveal the precise point in the cycle of decline and return where the questioner currently stands. A changing line in the first position of Hexagram 24 indicates the very earliest stage of return, requiring patience; a changing line in the sixth position suggests that the opportunity for return has been missed and further decline must be endured.
