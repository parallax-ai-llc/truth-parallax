The seventh group's hexagrams provide oracular guidance for situations of radical change and transition. Hexagram 49, Ge (Revolution), in consultation, indicates that fundamental change is necessary but must be carefully timed. The oracle counsels the questioner to prepare thoroughly, establish trust and support, and act only when the time is clearly right. Premature action will fail; properly timed action will succeed.

Hexagram 51, Zhen (The Arousing, Thunder), received in divination, indicates a sudden shock or disruption that may be frightening but is ultimately beneficial. The oracle counsels the questioner to maintain composure in the face of the unexpected. The shock serves to wake the questioner from complacency and to restore awareness. The changing lines indicate the specific nature of the shock and whether the questioner's response should be active or receptive.
