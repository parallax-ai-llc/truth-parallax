The fifth group's hexagrams provide oracular guidance for situations involving power dynamics, progress, and liberation. Hexagram 34, Da Zhuang (The Power of the Great), warns the questioner against the misuse of strength. When power is at its peak, the temptation to overreach is greatest, and the oracle counsels restraint and adherence to what is right rather than what is merely possible.

Hexagram 40, Jie (Deliverance), is one of the most welcome hexagrams to receive in divination, indicating that a period of difficulty is ending and freedom is at hand. The oracle counsels swift, decisive action to capitalize on the moment of release — hesitation will allow the old difficulties to reconsolidate. If no specific action is needed, the hexagram counsels returning to normal life without dwelling on the difficulties that have passed.
