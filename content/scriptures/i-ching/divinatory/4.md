Divinatory reading of the fourth group offers guidance for situations requiring perseverance and integrity. Hexagram 25, Wu Wang (Innocence), received in consultation, advises the questioner to act from genuine sincerity without calculating outcomes. The oracle indicates that manipulation and strategic thinking will backfire; only authentic, uncontrived response will succeed in the present situation.

Hexagram 29, Kan (The Abysmal, Water), is one of the most serious hexagrams to receive in divination, indicating danger and difficulty. The oracle does not counsel avoidance — the danger cannot be escaped — but rather sincerity of heart in the face of peril. The doubling of the water trigram (danger upon danger) suggests that the questioner is in a situation requiring sustained courage and faith. The changing lines specify the nature and intensity of the danger and the appropriate response.
