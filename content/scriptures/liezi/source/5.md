---
title: "Liezi - Chapter 5"
scripture: "liezi"
chapter: 5
---

**1** Tang asked Chi whether there had been a primal beginning to things, or whether things had always existed.

**2** Chi replied: If in the beginning there was nothing, how did the something of today come into being? And if someone in a later age were to maintain that there is nothing existing now, how could he be refuted?

**3** Tang said: Then is there no before or after in the creation of things?

**4** Chi replied: The end and the beginning of things have no precise limit. Beginning may be end, and end may be beginning. Who can draw the line between them?

**5** But that which lies beyond things and beyond events, we are not able to know.

**6** Tang said: And what about the limits of space? Does space come to an end, or does it stretch on for ever?

**7** Chi replied: Looking into the void beyond, how can we set limits? Beyond the limitless there is again the limitless; within the infinite there is again the infinite.

**8** It is this that I know: that the limitless and infinite exist. But beyond that, I know nothing.

**9** The universe in which our world is contained -- is there anything outside it, or not? This is a question to which no one in the world can give an answer.
