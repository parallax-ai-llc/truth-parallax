## Feminist Reading

The Feminist engagement with The Words of the Great Mother examines Valiente's treatment of Great Mother, freedom, ecstasy, nature, sacred body within the broader context of Feminist scholarship and practice. This section of the Charge presents the Goddess's self-revelation in language that has become central to modern Pagan liturgy, offering material for sustained Feminist reflection and analysis.
