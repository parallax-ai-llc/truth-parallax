---
title: "Charge of the Goddess Section 2"
scripture: "charge-of-the-goddess"
chapter: 2
---

**1** I am the Gracious Goddess, who gives the gift of joy unto the heart of man. Upon earth, I give the knowledge of the spirit eternal; and beyond death, I give peace and freedom and reunion with those who have gone before.

**2** Nor do I demand sacrifice; for behold, I am the Mother of all living, and my love is poured out upon the earth.

**3** I am the beauty of the green earth, and the white moon among the stars, and the mystery of the waters, and the desire of the heart of man.

**4** Call unto thy soul, arise, and come unto me. For I am the soul of Nature, who gives life to the universe. From me all things proceed, and unto me all things must return.

**5** And before my face, beloved of Gods and of men, let thine innermost divine self be enfolded in the rapture of the infinite.

**6** Let my worship be within the heart that rejoiceth; for behold, all acts of love and pleasure are my rituals.

**7** And therefore let there be beauty and strength, power and compassion, honor and humility, mirth and reverence within you.
