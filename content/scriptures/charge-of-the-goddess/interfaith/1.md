The Interfaith engagement with The Invocation and Descent of the Goddess examines Valiente's treatment of invocation, descent, moon, Goddess presence, ritual opening within the broader context of Interfaith scholarship and practice. This section of the Charge presents the Goddess's self-revelation in language that has become central to modern Pagan liturgy, offering material for sustained Interfaith reflection and analysis.
