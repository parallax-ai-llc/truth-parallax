## Overview

The Interpretation of Knowledge speaks to a universal challenge of religious community life: how to celebrate the diversity of members' gifts without creating division and jealousy. Every community of practice — Buddhist sangha, Christian congregation, Sufi order, Jewish synagogue, Hindu ashram — must navigate the tension between recognizing exceptional gifts and maintaining communal equality. The text's solution — deeper knowledge of the underlying unity that encompasses all diversity — represents one enduring answer to this perennial challenge.
