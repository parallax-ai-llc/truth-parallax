## Overview

Jewish readers find in the Interpretation of Knowledge an engagement with the concept of communal solidarity that resonates with the Jewish idea of kol Yisrael arevim zeh ba-zeh ('all Israel is responsible for one another,' Talmud Shevuot 39a). The text's body metaphor — the community as one body with diverse but essential members — parallels the rabbinic understanding of the community of Israel as an organic whole in which each member contributes to the collective well-being.
