## Overview

The Interpretation of Knowledge addresses a challenge that arises in every spiritual community: how to maintain unity amid the diversity of members' gifts and abilities. The Buddhist sangha's struggle with hierarchy and equality, the Sufi order's tension between the sheikh's authority and the murid's individual experience, the Hindu ashram's management of diverse spiritual practices within a unified community, and the Jewish congregation's allocation of religious honors all grapple with the same fundamental question this text addresses.
