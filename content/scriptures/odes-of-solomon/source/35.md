---
title: "Odes of Solomon - Ode 35"
scripture: "odes-of-solomon"
chapter: 35
---

**1** I opened my heart to the Lord, and his love gave me life. He helped me and I found rest.

**2** He became my sure foundation, and set me upon his rock where there is no shaking.

**3** The Lord is my light, and in him there is no darkness. By his truth he guided my steps.

**4** He lifted me up from the depths, and from the pit of darkness he drew me forth.

**5** His grace descended upon me like the dew upon the grass, and his mercy covered me like a mantle.

**6** I put off darkness and clothed myself with light. My soul acquired members free from sorrow, free from affliction, free from pain.

**7** And abundantly helpful to me was the thought of the Lord, and his fellowship in incorruption.

**8** I was lifted up in the light, and I passed before his face. And I was constantly near him, while praising and confessing him.

**9** He caused my heart to overflow, and it was found in my mouth; and it sprang forth upon my lips.

**10** Then upon my face increased the exultation of the Lord and his praise. Hallelujah.
