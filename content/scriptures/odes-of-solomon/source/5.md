---
title: "Odes of Solomon - Ode 5"
scripture: "odes-of-solomon"
chapter: 5
---

**1** I give thanks unto thee, O Lord, because I love thee.

**2** O Most High, forsake me not, for thou art my hope.

**3** Freely did I receive thy grace, let me live by it.

**4** My persecutors will come and not see me.

**5** A cloud of darkness shall fall on their eyes, and an air of thick darkness shall darken them.

**6** And they shall have no light to see; they cannot therefore seize me.

**7** Let their counsel become thick darkness, and what they have cunningly devised, let it return upon their own heads.

**8** For they have devised a counsel, and it did not succeed.

**9** They prepared themselves wickedly, and were found to be vain.

**10** My trust, O Lord, is in thee. I shall not fear. And because the Lord is my salvation, I shall not fear.

**11** And he is as a garland on my head, and I shall not be moved.

**12** Even if everything should be shaken, I shall stand firm.

**13** And though all things visible should perish, I shall not die; because the Lord is with me, and I am with him. Hallelujah.
