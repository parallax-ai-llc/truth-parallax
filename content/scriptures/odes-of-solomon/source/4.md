---
title: "Odes of Solomon - Ode 4"
scripture: "odes-of-solomon"
chapter: 4
---

**1** No man, O my God, changes thy holy place; and it is not possible that he should change it and put it in another place; because he has no power over it.

**2** For thy sanctuary thou didst design before thou didst make special places.

**3** The ancient one shall not be changed by those which are inferior to it. Thou hast given thy heart, O Lord, to thy believers.

**4** Never wilt thou be idle nor shalt thou be without fruits.

**5** For one hour of thy faith is more excellent than all days and years.

**6** For who shall put on thy grace and be rejected?

**7** For thy seal is known; and thy creatures are known to it.

**8** And thy hosts possess it, and the elect archangels are clothed with it.

**9** Thou hast given to us thy fellowship, not that thou wast in need of us, but that we are in need of thee.

**10** Sprinkle upon us thy dews, and open thy rich springs which pour forth milk and honey for us.

**11** For there is no repentance with thee, that thou shouldst repent of anything which thou hast promised; and the end was revealed before thee.

**12** For what thou gavest, thou gavest freely, so that no longer wilt thou draw back and take them again.

**13** For all was manifest to thee as God, and was set in order from the beginning before thee.

**14** And thou, O Lord, hast made all things. Hallelujah.
