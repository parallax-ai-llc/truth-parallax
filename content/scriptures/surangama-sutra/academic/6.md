Avalokitesvara's method of perfect penetration through hearing has been studied as one of the most detailed meditation instructions in Buddhist literature. Scholars have analyzed its phenomenological precision — the progressive dissolution of the subject-object structure of hearing — in relation to both Buddhist theories of perception and contemporary phenomenological philosophy. The chapter's selection of Avalokitesvara's method as supreme has also been studied in relation to the development of Avalokitesvara devotion in East Asian Buddhism.
