The path structure described in this chapter has been compared with other Buddhist systematizations of the bodhisattva path, including the Dasabhumika Sutra, the Bodhisattvabhumi, and the Abhisamayalamkara. Scholars have analyzed how the Surangama's 57-level scheme relates to these earlier and parallel systematizations, noting both continuities and innovations. The chapter's integration of sudden and gradual elements in the path structure reflects ongoing debates within Chinese Buddhism about the nature of enlightenment.
