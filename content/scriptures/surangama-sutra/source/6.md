---
title: "Surangama Sutra - Chapter 6"
scripture: "surangama-sutra"
chapter: 6
---

**1** The Buddha then spoke to Ananda and the great assembly concerning the formation of the world: "Listen carefully and I will explain."

**2** "All beings from beginningless time have turned in the cycle of birth and death because they do not know the bright, true mind, the nature of original purity."

**3** "Instead they make use of false thinking. It is these false thoughts that are not true. Therefore the cycle of birth and death continues."

**4** "Now you wish to investigate the unsurpassed Bodhi and discover your true nature. You should answer my questions with a straightforward mind, for the Tathagatas of the ten directions escaped from birth and death by this straightforward mind."

**5** "The mind is like a vast ocean — deep, still, and clear. When the winds of ignorance arise, waves of consciousness appear on its surface."

**6** "But the nature of water does not change. The waves are not other than the ocean, and the ocean is not other than the waves."

**7** "In the same way, your true mind does not change. The thoughts that arise are not other than the mind, and the mind is not other than the thoughts."

**8** "When you understand this, you will see that birth and death have no hold on you, and that you have been fundamentally free from the very beginning."

**9** "This is the supreme, wonderful, perfect enlightenment of all the Buddhas."

**10** "Practise this teaching diligently, and you will surely attain the fruit of the Way."
