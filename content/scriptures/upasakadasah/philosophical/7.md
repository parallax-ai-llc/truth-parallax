## Sadhana

Philosophical interpretation examines this teaching on Sadhana's practice of non-violence in daily life as a contribution to systematic philosophy. The text raises fundamental questions about the nature of reality, knowledge, and ethics that remain relevant to contemporary philosophical discussion.
