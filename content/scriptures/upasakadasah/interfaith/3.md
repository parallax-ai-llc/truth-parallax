## Chulanipiya

Interfaith interpretation finds in this teaching on Chulanipiya's balance of family duties and dharma resonances with the concerns of multiple religious traditions. The text addresses universal human questions about meaning, morality, and liberation that transcend sectarian boundaries and invite constructive dialogue.
