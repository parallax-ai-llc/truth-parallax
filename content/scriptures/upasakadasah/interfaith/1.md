## Ananda

Interfaith interpretation finds in this teaching on Ananda as the ideal Jain householder resonances with the concerns of multiple religious traditions. The text addresses universal human questions about meaning, morality, and liberation that transcend sectarian boundaries and invite constructive dialogue.
