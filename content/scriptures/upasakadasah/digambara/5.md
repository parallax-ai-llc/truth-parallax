## Chulanisaddha

The Digambara tradition reads this Chulanisaddha's faithful spiritual progress with characteristic doctrinal strictness, emphasizing the necessity of complete renunciation and the precise application of Jain principles. Digambara interpreters stress that Chulanisaddha's faithful spiritual progress must be understood within the framework of absolute non-attachment and rigorous ascetic practice that defines the Digambara path.
