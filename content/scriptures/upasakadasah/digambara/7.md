## Sadhana

The Digambara tradition reads this Sadhana's practice of non-violence in daily life with characteristic doctrinal strictness, emphasizing the necessity of complete renunciation and the precise application of Jain principles. Digambara interpreters stress that Sadhana's practice of non-violence in daily life must be understood within the framework of absolute non-attachment and rigorous ascetic practice that defines the Digambara path.
