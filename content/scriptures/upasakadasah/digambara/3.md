## Chulanipiya

The Digambara tradition reads this Chulanipiya's balance of family duties and dharma with characteristic doctrinal strictness, emphasizing the necessity of complete renunciation and the precise application of Jain principles. Digambara interpreters stress that Chulanipiya's balance of family duties and dharma must be understood within the framework of absolute non-attachment and rigorous ascetic practice that defines the Digambara path.
