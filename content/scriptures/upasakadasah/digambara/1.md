## Ananda

The Digambara tradition reads this Ananda as the ideal Jain householder with characteristic doctrinal strictness, emphasizing the necessity of complete renunciation and the precise application of Jain principles. Digambara interpreters stress that Ananda as the ideal Jain householder must be understood within the framework of absolute non-attachment and rigorous ascetic practice that defines the Digambara path.
