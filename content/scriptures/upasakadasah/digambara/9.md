## Nandini

The Digambara tradition reads this Nandini's devotion and spiritual aspiration with characteristic doctrinal strictness, emphasizing the necessity of complete renunciation and the precise application of Jain principles. Digambara interpreters stress that Nandini's devotion and spiritual aspiration must be understood within the framework of absolute non-attachment and rigorous ascetic practice that defines the Digambara path.
