## On the Duties of Friendship and Brotherhood

**1.** Brotherhood in God is one of the fruits of faith, for the Prophet said: "None of you truly believes until he loves for his brother what he loves for himself."

**2.** The duties of brotherhood are six: to give your brother what he needs before he asks, to keep his secrets, to overlook his faults, to visit him when he is ill, to attend his funeral when he dies, and to pray for him in his absence.

**3.** The friend who tells you your faults is better than the friend who flatters you, for the first is a mirror and the second is a painting.

**4.** Choose your friends as you choose your food—with care. For the companion influences you more than the teacher, and the friend shapes your character more than the book.

**5.** The Prophet said: "A person is upon the religion of his friend, so let each of you consider whom he befriends."

**6.** Forgive your friend's mistakes, for you will make mistakes too. The friendship that cannot survive a mistake was never a friendship.

**7.** Do not cut off your brother for a single fault, for if you do, you will find yourself without a friend in the world.

**8.** The rights of the neighbor are almost equal to the rights of the brother. The Prophet said: "Gabriel continued to advise me about the neighbor until I thought he would make him an heir."

**9.** Visit your brother when he is well, not only when he is sick. The one who visits only in sickness is a doctor, not a friend.

**10.** Be generous with your brother in word and in deed. The kind word is a charity, and the smile in the face of your brother is a charity.

**11.** Do not burden your friend with your problems unless he asks, and when he shares his problems with you, carry them as if they were your own.

**12.** The best friend is the one whose presence reminds you of God. If your friend's company draws you toward the world, he is a companion on the road to ruin.

**13.** Brotherhood is tested in times of hardship. The friend who stands by you when the world has turned against you is the true friend. The one who disappears is the false one.

**14.** Pray for your friends, for the prayer of a friend for a friend is answered. The angels say: "And the same for you."

**15.** Know that the bond of brotherhood in God is stronger than the bond of blood, for the bond of blood ends with death, but the bond of faith extends into the next world.
