## On Anger, Envy, and Hatred

**1.** Anger is a flame that burns first the one who harbors it. The angry man is a prisoner of his own fire.

**2.** The Prophet said: "The strong man is not the one who can wrestle others to the ground. The strong man is the one who controls himself when angry."

**3.** Anger arises from pride, and pride arises from ignorance. The one who truly knows himself has no cause for anger, for he knows that he is dust, and dust does not boast.

**4.** When anger rises within you, seek refuge in God from the accursed devil, for anger is the devil's wind, and it fans the flames of the ego.

**5.** If you are standing when anger comes, sit down. If you are sitting, lie down. If the anger still persists, perform ablution with cold water, for water extinguishes fire.

**6.** The remedy for anger is forbearance, and forbearance is the noblest of virtues. The Prophet said: "God loves forbearance and gentleness."

**7.** Envy is to wish that another person lose what he has been given. It is the ugliest of the diseases of the heart, for it is a complaint against God's distribution.

**8.** The envier says, in effect: "God, You have given to the wrong person. You should have given to me." This is a presumption against the wisdom of the All-Knowing.

**9.** The remedy for envy is gratitude. When you are grateful for what you have, you do not covet what others have.

**10.** The Prophet said: "Look at those who are below you and do not look at those who are above you, for this is more likely to prevent you from despising God's blessings."

**11.** Hatred is the fruit of anger and envy. When anger is not controlled and envy is not cured, they produce hatred, which is the poison of the soul.

**12.** The one who harbors hatred harms himself more than he harms his enemy. Hatred is a fire that burns the vessel that contains it.

**13.** The remedy for hatred is to pray for the one you hate. This is almost impossible at first, but with persistence, the prayer softens the heart and the hatred dissolves.

**14.** The Prophet said: "Do not hate each other, do not envy each other, do not turn away from each other. Be servants of God, as brothers."

**15.** The purification of the heart from anger, envy, and hatred is the work of a lifetime, but every moment of effort is rewarded, and every small victory is celebrated by the angels.
