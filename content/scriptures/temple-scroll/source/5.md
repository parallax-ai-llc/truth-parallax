---
title: "Temple Scroll - Chapter 5"
scripture: "temple-scroll"
chapter: 5
---

**1** And the Lord spoke further concerning the city of the sanctuary, saying: These are My statutes which you shall observe.

**2** You shall keep My commandments and do them; I am the Lord your God who brought you out of the land of Egypt.

**3** You shall not do as the nations do who dwell in the land, for they have done all these abominations and the land has become defiled.

**4** Keep all My statutes and all My judgments and do them, that the land to which I bring you to dwell in may not reject you.

**5** You shall be holy, for I the Lord your God am holy. And you shall keep My sabbaths and reverence My sanctuary.

**6** You shall make no idols for yourselves, nor set up for yourselves any carved image or sacred pillar.

**7** If you walk in My statutes and keep My commandments and do them, I will give your rains in their season, and the land shall yield its produce.

**8** You shall eat your bread to the full and dwell in your land safely. And I will give peace in the land.

**9** But if you do not obey Me and do not observe all these commandments, I will bring upon you sudden terror, consumption, and fever.

**10** Yet for all that, when they are in the land of their enemies, I will not reject them utterly, nor will I break My covenant with them.
