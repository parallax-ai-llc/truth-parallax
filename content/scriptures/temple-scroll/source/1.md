---
title: "Temple Scroll - Chapter 1"
scripture: "temple-scroll"
chapter: 1
---

**1** And the Lord spoke to Moses saying: Command the children of Israel and say unto them, when you come into the land which I give to you as an inheritance, you shall build for Me a sanctuary.

**2** You shall make it according to all that I show you, the plan of the sanctuary and the plan of all its furnishings.

**3** And you shall make the altar of burnt offering of acacia wood, five cubits long and five cubits wide. The altar shall be square and its height shall be three cubits.

**4** And you shall make its horns on its four corners; its horns shall be of one piece with it, and you shall overlay it with bronze.

**5** And you shall make the court of the sanctuary. For the south side there shall be hangings for the court of fine twisted linen, one hundred cubits long.

**6** And its pillars shall be twenty, with twenty bases of bronze. The hooks of the pillars and their bands shall be of silver.

**7** And likewise for the north side there shall be hangings one hundred cubits long, with twenty pillars and twenty bases of bronze.

**8** The width of the court on the west side shall have hangings of fifty cubits, with ten pillars and ten bases.

**9** The width of the court on the east side shall be fifty cubits. And the gate of the court shall be a screen of twenty cubits.

**10** And all the pillars around the court shall be banded with silver, their hooks of silver, and their bases of bronze.
