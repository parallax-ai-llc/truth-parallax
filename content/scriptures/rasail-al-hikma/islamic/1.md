Volume 1 of the Rasa'il al-Hikma, with its focus on cosmic intellect, can be examined in relation to Islamic theological and philosophical traditions. The themes of al-Aql, divine unity resonate with discussions in the Quran, hadith literature, and Islamic philosophy.

Islamic scholars and theologians have engaged with these themes from multiple perspectives — kalamic, philosophical, mystical, and legal. The volume's treatment of cosmic intellect invites consideration of both convergences and divergences with mainstream Islamic thought, enriching understanding of the broader landscape of monotheistic theology.
