## Overview

An interfaith reading of the Rasa'il al-Hikma seeks to build bridges of understanding between Druze Faith and other religious communities, fostering mutual respect and appreciation through engagement with sacred texts. Explored as a bridge between Abrahamic monotheism and philosophical mysticism. This approach emphasizes shared values, complementary insights, and the potential for enriching dialogue.

Interfaith engagement with the Rasa'il al-Hikma addresses common misconceptions, highlights the depth and sophistication of Druze Faith theological thought, and identifies points of convergence with other traditions' scriptures and teachings. Such readings contribute to a more inclusive and informed understanding of humanity's diverse spiritual heritage.
