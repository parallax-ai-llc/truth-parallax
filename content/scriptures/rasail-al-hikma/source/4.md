## The Epistle of Accusation and Periods

**1.** This epistle concerns the periods of time and the cycles of history through which the truth is alternately revealed and concealed.

**2.** There are periods of manifestation, when the truth is openly proclaimed and the people of wisdom walk freely, and periods of concealment, when the truth must be hidden and the people of wisdom must guard their knowledge.

**3.** In the periods of concealment, the wise protect their knowledge not from miserliness but from necessity, for the truth given to the unprepared is a poison, not a remedy.

**4.** The seed must be planted in the right season. The farmer who plants in winter will reap nothing. The teacher who teaches before the student is ready will sow confusion.

**5.** The accusation against the people of wisdom has always been the same: that they hide the truth, that they speak in riddles, that they wrap the simple in complexity.

**6.** But the wise do not hide the truth from those who seek it sincerely—they hide it from those who would misuse it.

**7.** The sword in the hand of the just protects the innocent. The same sword in the hand of the tyrant slaughters them. Knowledge is like the sword.

**8.** The cycles of history move between light and darkness, between knowledge and ignorance, between justice and oppression. These cycles are not random—they are governed by a wisdom that the short-sighted cannot perceive.

**9.** In every period of darkness, a seed of light is preserved. And in every period of light, a seed of darkness is growing. This is the nature of the world of duality.

**10.** The wise man does not despair in the period of darkness, for he knows that dawn follows night. Nor does he become complacent in the period of light, for he knows that night follows day.

**11.** The purpose of the periods is the testing of the soul. The soul that maintains its faith in the period of darkness is more precious than the soul that believes only in the period of light.

**12.** The hidden truth is not less true for being hidden. The sun behind the clouds is still the sun. The truth behind the veil is still the truth.

**13.** Guard the truth in your heart during the periods of concealment, and share it freely during the periods of manifestation. Both guarding and sharing are forms of service to the truth.

**14.** The cycles will continue until the final manifestation, when the truth will be revealed in its fullness, and there will be no more concealment, no more cycles, no more darkness—only the eternal light of the One.

**15.** Until that day, be patient, be vigilant, and be true. The truth endures, though the periods change. The star endures, though the clouds pass before it.
