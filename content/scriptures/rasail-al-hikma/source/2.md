## The Epistle of the Admonition and the Rebuke

**1.** O you who are heedless of the truth, awake! The night of ignorance is long, but the dawn of knowledge is breaking.

**2.** You have busied yourselves with the accumulation of wealth and the pursuit of status, while the treasure of wisdom lies neglected at your feet.

**3.** What profit has the man who gains the whole world and loses his own soul? The world that you grasp will slip through your fingers, but the wisdom you acquire will accompany you beyond the grave.

**4.** Look at the generations that came before you: where are their palaces, their armies, their treasures? All have been swallowed by the dust. Only the words of the wise endure.

**5.** The wise man builds his house upon the rock of knowledge, and the fool builds his upon the sand of wealth. When the floods come, one stands and the other falls.

**6.** Do not be deceived by the appearance of permanence, for nothing in the world of matter is permanent. The mountains themselves are being ground to dust by the patience of the wind.

**7.** The body is a garment that the soul wears for a time. When the garment is worn out, the soul discards it and puts on another. Do not confuse the garment with the wearer.

**8.** The soul that clings to its garment clings to its prison. The soul that releases it freely rises to the world of light.

**9.** Prepare your soul for the ascent by purifying it of the impurities of this world: greed, envy, hatred, and ignorance. These are the weights that keep the soul earthbound.

**10.** The admonition is this: you will not live forever in this form. Use the time you have been given to acquire wisdom, for wisdom is the only provision that does not perish.

**11.** The rebuke is this: you have been given reason and yet you behave as if you have none. You have been given the light of the intellect and yet you choose to walk in darkness.

**12.** The animal follows its instincts and is blameless. But you, who have been given the power of choice, will be called to account for every choice you make.

**13.** Choose wisdom over ignorance, truth over falsehood, light over darkness, and the eternal over the temporal. These choices are the choices that define the human being.

**14.** The time of heedlessness is passing. The caravan of life moves on, and the road behind you cannot be traveled again.

**15.** Awake, O sleeper, before the morning comes and finds you still in the darkness of your dreams.
