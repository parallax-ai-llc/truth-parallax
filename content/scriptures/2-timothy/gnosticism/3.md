## All Scripture Is God-Breathed

Gnostic interpretation of 2 Timothy 3 centers on esoteric knowledge and the liberation of the divine spark. The key passages — difficult times in the last days (3:1), all Scripture is God-breathed (3:16), equipped for every good work (3:17) — have been read through the lens of spiritual awakening, the duality of matter and spirit, and hidden meanings, generating rich theological reflection across centuries of commentary.

Ancient Gnostic teachers and modern scholars of Nag Hammadi texts gave significant attention to this chapter, exploring how its themes illuminate the broader message of 2 Timothy. The chapter's teaching on all scripture is god-breathed has shaped Gnostic understanding of the Christian life, ecclesiology, and eschatology in important ways.

Modern Gnostic scholarship continues to engage this chapter with fresh exegetical insight, examining how 2 Timothy 3 speaks to contemporary theological and ethical questions while remaining grounded in the interpretive traditions that have sustained the community of faith.
