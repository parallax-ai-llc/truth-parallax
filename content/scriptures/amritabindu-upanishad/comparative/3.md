The Amritabindu's teaching that ultimate reality lies beyond the mind parallels apophatic traditions across the world. The Christian Cloud of Unknowing teaches that God is reached not through thought but through the abandonment of thought. Meister Eckhart's concept of *Gelassenheit* (releasement) describes the letting go of all mental content to encounter the divine ground. The Zen Buddhist emphasis on *mu-shin* (no-mind) and the Daoist concept of *wu-wei* (non-action of the mind) both point to the same recognition: the deepest reality is accessed not through mental effort but through mental quieting.

The metaphor of *amrita* (nectar of immortality) compares with the Christian "living water" (John 4:14), the Daoist elixir of immortality, and the Buddhist *amrita* (deathless nectar) that is nirvana.
