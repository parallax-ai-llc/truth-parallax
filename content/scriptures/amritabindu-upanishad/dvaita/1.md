Madhva reads Section 1 as a practical teaching on mental discipline. The mind's attachment to sense objects is a real problem — not a mere illusion — that prevents the soul from recognizing its dependence on God. Freeing the mind from these attachments through devotion and discipline creates the conditions for receiving Vishnu's grace.

For Dvaita, the mind does not create bondage or liberation by itself — God is the ultimate agent. But the mind's orientation determines whether the soul is receptive to God's grace (when freed from attachments) or closed to it (when bound by desires).
