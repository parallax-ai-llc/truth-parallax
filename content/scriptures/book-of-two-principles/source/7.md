---
title: "Book of Two Principles Section 7"
scripture: "book-of-two-principles"
chapter: 7
---

**1** In conclusion, we affirm that the doctrine of two principles is the only doctrine that preserves the perfect goodness of the true God while explaining the manifest existence of evil in the world.

**2** We affirm that the good God is the Father of spirits, the Lord of the invisible world, the source of all light, truth, and love.

**3** We affirm that the evil principle is the lord of this visible world, the creator of matter, the source of darkness, falsehood, and corruption.

**4** We affirm that our souls are creatures of the good God, imprisoned in bodies of flesh by the evil principle, and that the purpose of our life is to free ourselves from this imprisonment through the knowledge of truth and the reception of the Consolamentum.

**5** We affirm that Christ came as the messenger of the good God to teach the way of liberation, and that His Church, the Church of the Good Christians, preserves the true teaching handed down from the Apostles.

**6** We affirm that in the end, all souls shall be freed, the evil principle shall be overthrown, and the good God shall reign over all in perfect peace and light for ever and ever. Amen.
