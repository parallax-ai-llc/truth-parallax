---
title: "Book of Two Principles Section 3"
scripture: "book-of-two-principles"
chapter: 3
---

**1** Concerning the nature of these two principles, we must understand that the good principle is eternal, infinite, and immutable, and from it proceed all good things: angels, souls, light, truth, and love.

**2** The evil principle is also eternal, for if it had been created by the good God, then the good God would be the author of evil, which is impossible.

**3** But though the evil principle is eternal in duration, it is not equal to the good principle in power or dignity, for the good shall ultimately prevail over evil.

**4** The good principle created the invisible world, the world of spirit, of light, and of truth. The evil principle created the visible world, the world of matter, of darkness, and of corruption.

**5** Our souls are creatures of the good God, imprisoned in bodies of flesh fashioned by the evil principle. And the purpose of the good God's dispensation is to free these souls from their imprisonment.

**6** Christ came not in true flesh, for flesh is the creation of the evil principle, but He appeared in the likeness of flesh to teach souls the way of liberation from this world of corruption.
