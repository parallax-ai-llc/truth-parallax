## The Bezel of the Wisdom of Power in the Word of Lot

**1.** Lot was given the wisdom of power, and power here means the fortification that God provides for those who take refuge in Him.

**2.** Lot said: "Would that I had power against you, or that I could take refuge in a strong pillar." The strong pillar is God Himself.

**3.** The Prophet Muhammad said, upon hearing this verse: "God have mercy on Lot—he was indeed taking refuge in a strong pillar." For there is no pillar stronger than God.

**4.** The wisdom of power teaches that true strength is not in armies or fortresses but in the connection to the Source of all power.

**5.** The weak man connected to God is stronger than the strong man disconnected from Him. The reed that bends in the wind survives; the oak that resists is uprooted.

**6.** Lot stood alone against his people, and his people were many. But the one who stands with God stands with the only Majority that matters.

**7.** When the angels came to Lot's house, the people tried to break down the door. But the door of God cannot be broken, for it is guarded by the Power that created the universe.

**8.** The destruction of Lot's people came from above—stones of baked clay rained upon them. The power of heaven overwhelmed the power of earth.

**9.** This is the teaching: no earthly power can stand against the power of heaven. Every Pharaoh meets his sea; every Nimrod meets his gnat.

**10.** The power of God is not the power of coercion—it is the power of truth. Truth does not need to force; it only needs to reveal itself, and all falsehood crumbles.

**11.** Lot's wife looked back because her heart was still attached to the city. The body followed the spirit, and the spirit was still with the doomed.

**12.** This teaches that the power of God protects those whose hearts are turned toward Him, not those whose bodies merely accompany the righteous.

**13.** Power in the spiritual sense is the capacity to remain firm in the truth when all the world opposes you. This is the greatest power a human can possess.

**14.** The strong pillar that Lot sought is available to every soul. It is the pillar of faith, and it cannot be shaken by any earthquake of circumstance.

**15.** Take refuge in the strong pillar, and the storms of the world will pass over you like clouds passing over a mountain.
