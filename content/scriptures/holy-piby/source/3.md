---
title: "Holy Piby - Chapter 3"
scripture: "holy-piby"
chapter: 3
---

**1** The Almighty commanded: "Love one another, for love is the fulfillment of the law. He who loveth not his brother abideth in darkness."

**2** "Give unto the poor, and the Lord shall repay thee. For blessed is the man who considereth the needy; the Lord shall deliver him in the day of trouble."

**3** "Let not thy heart be hardened against thy brother, for ye are all children of one Father. The blood that floweth in thy veins floweth also in his."

**4** "Judge not thy brother by the color of his skin, but by the content of his heart. For the Lord looketh not upon the outward appearance, but upon the heart."

**5** "Be faithful in all thy doings, for the eyes of the Lord are upon the righteous, and his ears are open unto their cry. But the face of the Lord is against them that do evil."

**6** "Teach thy children the ways of the Lord, that they may walk in truth all the days of their lives. For a child trained in the way he should go, when he is old, he will not depart from it."

**7** "Seek wisdom, for wisdom is the principal thing; therefore get wisdom: and with all thy getting get understanding. She shall give to thine head an ornament of grace."

**8** "Be not deceived by the vanities of this world, for the world passeth away, and the lust thereof: but he that doeth the will of God abideth forever."

**9** "Let thy speech be with grace, seasoned with truth. For by thy words thou shalt be justified, and by thy words thou shalt be condemned."

**10** "The Lord requireth of thee: to do justly, and to love mercy, and to walk humbly with thy God. This is the whole of the law."
