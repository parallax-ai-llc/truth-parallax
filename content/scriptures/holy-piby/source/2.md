---
title: "Holy Piby - Chapter 2"
scripture: "holy-piby"
chapter: 2
---

**1** And the children of Ethiopia were scattered among the nations, and they dwelt in lands that were not their own. But the Lord God did not forget them.

**2** For the Lord said: "Though my people be scattered to the four corners of the earth, yet shall I gather them again. And they shall return to the land of their fathers."

**3** And the oppressors said: "These are a people without hope, a people without a future." But the Lord said: "I shall raise them up, and they shall be a great nation."

**4** Woe unto those who have enslaved the children of Ethiopia, for the Lord shall require their blood at the hands of the oppressors. The chains of bondage shall be broken.

**5** And the Lord sent prophets among the people, to comfort them in their affliction and to remind them of the promises of God. And the prophets spoke the words of the Almighty.

**6** "Fear not," saith the Lord, "for I am with thee. Be not dismayed, for I am thy God. I will strengthen thee; yea, I will help thee; yea, I will uphold thee with the right hand of my righteousness."

**7** And the people heard the words of the prophets, and their hearts were encouraged. And they said: "The Lord our God liveth, and he shall deliver us."

**8** Let the children of Ethiopia remember their heritage and walk in the ways of the Almighty. For the Lord hath chosen them, and his purpose shall be fulfilled.

**9** Blessed is the man who putteth his trust in the Lord God of Ethiopia, for he shall never be put to shame. His children and his children's children shall inherit the earth.

**10** The Lord God of Ethiopia shall reign forever. His kingdom shall have no end. All nations shall come to know his glory.
