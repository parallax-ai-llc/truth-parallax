---
title: "Holy Piby - Chapter 1"
scripture: "holy-piby"
chapter: 1
---

**1** The heaven and the earth were created by the Almighty God of Ethiopia. In the beginning God created all things that were created.

**2** And God set the Garden of Life in Ethiopia, and there he placed man whom he had created; and all things were given unto man for his use.

**3** And God said unto man: "Of all that I have created thou mayest freely partake, only thou shalt not abuse my gifts, for the day thou abusest them, suffering shall come upon thee."

**4** And man was obedient unto God, and kept his commandments, and was blessed in all his ways. And God saw that it was good.

**5** The children of Ethiopia walked in the ways of righteousness, and the light of the Almighty was upon them. They knew no want, for the land was bountiful.

**6** And God appointed leaders among the people, to guide them in the ways of truth. And these leaders were faithful, and the people prospered.

**7** But there arose among the nations those who forgot the commandments of God, and turned aside to worship false things. And they went forth to make war upon the children of Ethiopia.

**8** And God said: "Those who oppress my children shall be brought low. For I am the God of Ethiopia, and I shall deliver my people from bondage."

**9** And the Almighty sent his spirit upon the faithful, that they might endure their trials with patience and courage. For the day of deliverance was appointed.

**10** Thus saith the Lord God of Ethiopia: "I am the beginning and the end. My word shall not return unto me void, but shall accomplish that for which I sent it."
