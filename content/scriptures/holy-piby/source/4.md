---
title: "Holy Piby - Chapter 4"
scripture: "holy-piby"
chapter: 4
---

**1** And the Lord God of Ethiopia spoke concerning the latter days: "Behold, I shall send my anointed one, and he shall be a leader unto my people."

**2** "He shall gather the scattered children of Ethiopia from every land, and he shall restore them unto their inheritance. And they shall dwell in safety."

**3** "The nations that have oppressed my people shall tremble, for the arm of the Lord is not shortened that it cannot save, neither is his ear heavy that it cannot hear."

**4** "In that day, the children of Ethiopia shall lift up their heads, for their redemption draweth nigh. And they shall praise the name of the Lord their God."

**5** "And the Lord shall make a new covenant with his people: I will write my law in their hearts, and I will be their God, and they shall be my people."

**6** "There shall be peace in the land, and prosperity shall flow as a river. The children shall play in the streets, and the elders shall sit in the gates."

**7** "And the knowledge of the Lord shall cover the earth as the waters cover the sea. All flesh shall know that I am the Lord God of Ethiopia, and there is none other beside me."

**8** "Blessed are those who have waited for this day, for they shall see the glory of the Lord. And blessed are those who have kept the faith, for they shall receive their reward."

**9** "Let the heavens rejoice and let the earth be glad, for the Lord reigneth. Let the fields be joyful, and all that is therein. Let all the trees of the wood rejoice."

**10** "For the Lord cometh to judge the earth: he shall judge the world with righteousness, and the peoples with his truth. Amen."
