## Ethiopian Orthodox Reading

The Ethiopian Orthodox engagement with Precepts and Commandments examines Rogers's treatment of commandments, ethics, conduct, law within the broader context of Ethiopian Orthodox scholarship and practice. This book presents foundational themes that shape the Holy Piby's vision of divine purpose for people of African descent, offering rich material for Ethiopian Orthodox reflection and analysis.
