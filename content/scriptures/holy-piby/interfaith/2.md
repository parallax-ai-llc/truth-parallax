## Interfaith Reading

The Interfaith engagement with Aggregation: A Name Given to the Ethiopian Church examines Rogers's treatment of Ethiopian Church, aggregation, Black solidarity, institution within the broader context of Interfaith scholarship and practice. This book presents foundational themes that shape the Holy Piby's vision of divine purpose for people of African descent, offering rich material for Interfaith reflection and analysis.
