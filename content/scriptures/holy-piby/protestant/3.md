## Protestant Reading

The Protestant engagement with The Facts of the Apostles examines Rogers's treatment of apostles, mission, diaspora, witness within the broader context of Protestant scholarship and practice. This book presents foundational themes that shape the Holy Piby's vision of divine purpose for people of African descent, offering rich material for Protestant reflection and analysis.
