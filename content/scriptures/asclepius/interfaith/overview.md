## Overview

The Asclepius celebrates human dignity in terms that resonate across all religious traditions. The concept of humanity as a 'great miracle' — uniquely positioned between the divine and the earthly, capable of either ascending to the gods or descending to the beasts — speaks to every tradition that affirms the extraordinary potential of the human being. The prophecy of spiritual decline and the promise of restoration address the universal religious experience of living in an age perceived as diminished, and the equally universal hope that renewal will come.
