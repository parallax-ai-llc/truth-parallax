---
title: "Legend of Five Suns - Chapter 2"
scripture: "legend-of-five-suns"
chapter: 2
---

**1** This Sun, the third Sun, its sign was Four Rain. It was called the Rain Sun, the Rain of Fire. In this era it happened that fire rained down, that everything that existed was burned. And it rained gravel and stones.

**2** They say that in this Sun the great stones that we now see were scattered about, and the rough lava stone called tezontle seethed and boiled into being. And the red cliffs were formed.

**3** This Sun lasted four hundred and sixty-eight years, and the people of this era ate a seed called acecentli, a water maize.

**4** The day sign of their destruction was Four Rain. They were destroyed by rain of fire. Everything burned. All their houses burned. And so the people were changed into birds.

**5** When the Sun burned, they flew up and were saved. And all the children became birds. All of them soared away singing, leaving behind the burning earth.

**6** Thus ended the third Sun, the Rain of Fire Sun. The gods were not yet satisfied, for the creation was not yet complete.

**7** This Sun, the fourth Sun, its sign was Four Wind. It was called the Wind Sun. Everything was carried away by the wind in this era. The people all became monkeys.

**8** They were scattered through the forests, the monkey-people. They lived in the trees and ate forest fruits. This Sun lasted five hundred and twelve years.

**9** Their food was the seed called tzentzontli, the Four Hundred Seed, which grows wild in the forests. The day sign of their destruction was Four Wind.

**10** Everything was carried away by the wind. The people became monkeys, and they were scattered across the mountains and forests. Their descendants are the monkeys who live among the trees.

**11** Thus ended the fourth Sun, the Wind Sun. And the earth was still not complete, and the gods gathered to decide what must be done.

**12** And the gods spoke among themselves, saying: "Four times the world has been created, and four times it has been destroyed. What shall we do? The world must be made anew, and a new sun must rise."

**13** And so the gods assembled at Teotihuacan, the place of the gods, to create the fifth Sun, the Sun under which we now live.
