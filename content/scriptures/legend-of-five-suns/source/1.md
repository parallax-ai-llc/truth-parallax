---
title: "Legend of Five Suns - Chapter 1"
scripture: "legend-of-five-suns"
chapter: 1
---

**1** Here is the oral account of what is known of how the earth was founded long ago. One by one, here are its various foundations.

**2** How it began, how the first Sun had its beginning two thousand five hundred and thirteen years ago, thus it is known today.

**3** This Sun, the first Sun, its sign was Four Water. It was called the Sun of Water. In this Sun it happened that water carried everything away. Everything vanished. The people were changed into fish.

**4** The sky came down upon the water. In a single day everything perished. The day sign was Four Water. Everything that existed was destroyed. All the mountains perished. The water lasted fifty-two springs.

**5** Then the years of this first Sun came to an end. And it is known that it lasted four hundred and seventy-six years.

**6** Thus perished the first people, who were giants. They had been created by the gods at the beginning. Their food was the water maize called acicintli. This was the era when the water came.

**7** And it is remembered that when the water lasted, the sky fell upon the earth. And when they perished, they became all manner of fish. It was then that the sky fell and the water covered everything in a single day.

**8** The mountains vanished. And the water prevailed for fifty-two years. And so it was that their years came to an end.

**9** This Sun, the second Sun, its sign was Four Jaguar. It was called the Jaguar Sun. In this Sun it happened that the sky was crushed, that the Sun could not follow its course. When the Sun reached midday, it immediately became night.

**10** When it became dark, the people were devoured. And in this Sun, jaguars ate the people. Giants lived in this era.

**11** The old ones said that the greeting of the giants was: "Do not fall down," for whoever fell down fell forever. This was the era of giants who greeted one another thus.

**12** This Sun lasted five hundred and seventy-six years, and then the jaguars ate the people, and they perished. Their food was pine nuts. The day sign of their destruction was Four Jaguar.

**13** In this Sun, the giants were destroyed and perished. Their bones were scattered, and the hills were formed from them. And this is how the second Sun came to an end.
