## Patthana

The Theravada tradition regards this section on the Patthana's monumental analysis of 24 types of conditional relations as essential canonical teaching. Theravada interpreters emphasize its direct relevance to meditation practice and the cultivation of wisdom, reading it as part of the complete path from morality through concentration to liberating insight.
