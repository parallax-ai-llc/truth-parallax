## Vibhanga

Zen practitioners appreciate this section on the Vibhanga's detailed analysis of the aggregates, sense bases, and key doctrinal categories for its emphasis on direct experience and practical application. The teaching's focus on immediate awareness and the transformation of consciousness aligns with the Zen aim of awakening to one's true nature in this very moment.
