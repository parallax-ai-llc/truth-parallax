## Yamaka

From the Mahayana perspective, this section on the Yamaka's paired questions clarifying logical relationships between categories preserves important foundational teachings that the Mahayana tradition inherits and develops. Mahayana scholars identify in these early discourses the seeds of later philosophical developments in Madhyamaka and Yogachara thought.
