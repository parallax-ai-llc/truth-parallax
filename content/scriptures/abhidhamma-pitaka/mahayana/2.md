## Vibhanga

From the Mahayana perspective, this section on the Vibhanga's detailed analysis of the aggregates, sense bases, and key doctrinal categories preserves important foundational teachings that the Mahayana tradition inherits and develops. Mahayana scholars identify in these early discourses the seeds of later philosophical developments in Madhyamaka and Yogachara thought.
