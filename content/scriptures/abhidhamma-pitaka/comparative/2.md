## Vibhanga

This section on the Vibhanga's detailed analysis of the aggregates, sense bases, and key doctrinal categories provides rich material for cross-traditional analysis. Comparative scholars examine how the Buddhist approach to these questions relates to and differs from the approaches of other major religious and philosophical traditions.
