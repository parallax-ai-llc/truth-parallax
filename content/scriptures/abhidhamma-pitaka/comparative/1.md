## Dhammasangani

Comparative interpretation of this teaching on the Dhammasangani's fundamental enumeration of all mental and material phenomena reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar teachings in Hindu, Jain, Christian, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Buddhist perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
