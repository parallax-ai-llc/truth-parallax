## Patthana

Comparative interpretation of this teaching on the Patthana's monumental analysis of 24 types of conditional relations reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar teachings in Hindu, Jain, Christian, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Buddhist perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
