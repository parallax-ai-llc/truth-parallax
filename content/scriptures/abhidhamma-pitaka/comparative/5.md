## Kathavatthu

Comparative interpretation of this teaching on the Kathavatthu's debates between Buddhist schools on disputed doctrinal questions reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar teachings in Hindu, Jain, Christian, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Buddhist perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
