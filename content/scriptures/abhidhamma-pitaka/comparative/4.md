## Puggalapannatti

This section on the Puggalapannatti's classification of individuals by their spiritual attainments provides rich material for cross-traditional analysis. Comparative scholars examine how the Buddhist approach to these questions relates to and differs from the approaches of other major religious and philosophical traditions.
