## Dhatukatha

Comparative interpretation of this teaching on the Dhatukatha's discussion of how elements relate to aggregates and sense bases reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar teachings in Hindu, Jain, Christian, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Buddhist perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
