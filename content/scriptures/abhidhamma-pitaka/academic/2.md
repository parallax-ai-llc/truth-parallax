## Vibhanga

From an academic perspective, this teaching on the Vibhanga's detailed analysis of the aggregates, sense bases, and key doctrinal categories is examined for its compositional history, its relationship to parallel versions in other Buddhist canons, and its significance for reconstructing the teachings of the historical Buddha.
