## Dhatukatha

Academic scholars analyze this section on the Dhatukatha's discussion of how elements relate to aggregates and sense bases using the methods of textual criticism, historical contextualization, and comparative study. The text provides evidence for understanding the development of early Buddhist thought and its relationship to the broader intellectual culture of ancient India.
