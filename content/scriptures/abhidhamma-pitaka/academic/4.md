## Puggalapannatti

From an academic perspective, this teaching on the Puggalapannatti's classification of individuals by their spiritual attainments is examined for its compositional history, its relationship to parallel versions in other Buddhist canons, and its significance for reconstructing the teachings of the historical Buddha.
