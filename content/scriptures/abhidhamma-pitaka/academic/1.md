## Dhammasangani

Academic scholars analyze this section on the Dhammasangani's fundamental enumeration of all mental and material phenomena using the methods of textual criticism, historical contextualization, and comparative study. The text provides evidence for understanding the development of early Buddhist thought and its relationship to the broader intellectual culture of ancient India.
