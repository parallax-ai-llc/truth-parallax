## Kathavatthu

Academic scholars analyze this section on the Kathavatthu's debates between Buddhist schools on disputed doctrinal questions using the methods of textual criticism, historical contextualization, and comparative study. The text provides evidence for understanding the development of early Buddhist thought and its relationship to the broader intellectual culture of ancient India.
