## Overview

Tibetan Buddhism places Abhidharma study (mngon pa) as one of the five major topics in the monastic curriculum. Tibetan scholars study primarily through Vasubandhu's Abhidharmakosa and its commentarial tradition, which synthesizes Sarvastivada analysis with insights that bridge toward Mahayana philosophy.

## Key Themes

Tibetan Abhidharma study focuses on the classification of mental and material phenomena, the analysis of the twelve links of dependent origination, and the mapping of cosmological realms. This knowledge provides the foundation for more advanced study of Madhyamaka emptiness philosophy.

## Relevance to Tibetan Study

Abhidharma mastery is required for the Geshe degree in Gelug monasteries and is considered essential for understanding the philosophical arguments that occupy higher levels of Tibetan Buddhist education.
