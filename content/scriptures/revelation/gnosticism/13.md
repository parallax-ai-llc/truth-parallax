## The Beasts as Archontic Manifestations

Revelation 13's two beasts were read in Gnostic cosmology as manifestations of archontic power in its political (beast from the sea) and religious-ideological (beast from the earth) forms. The Demiurge rules through these two instruments: brute force and deceptive persuasion. The first beast demands bodily submission; the second beast captures the mind and spirit through false worship and counterfeit miracles. Together, they represent the totalizing grip of the material cosmos on the unawakened soul.

## The Mark of the Beast and Material Entanglement

The "mark of the beast" required for buying and selling (13:16-17) was interpreted by Gnostic readers as the stamp of material entanglement — the binding of the soul to the economic, social, and political systems of the Demiurge's world. To bear the mark is to be fully integrated into the material order, unable to conceive of any reality beyond it. The pneumatic, by contrast, refuses the mark — not through political resistance but through *gnosis*, the recognition that the material system is ultimately unreal.

## 666 and the Imperfect Creation

The number 666 was read through Gnostic numerology as a triple repetition of six — the number of imperfection, falling short of the divine seven. The beast's number represents the ultimate expression of the Demiurge's creation: an accumulation of imperfection that aspires to completeness but can never achieve it. The material world, however elaborately constructed, is irredeemably deficient because it lacks the divine fullness (*pleroma*) that only the true God possesses.
