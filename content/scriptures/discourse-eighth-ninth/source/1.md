---
title: "Discourse on the Eighth and Ninth Section 1"
scripture: "discourse-eighth-ninth"
chapter: 1
---

**1** My father, yesterday you promised me that you would bring my mind into the eighth and afterwards you would bring me into the ninth. You said that this is the order of the tradition.

**2** My son, indeed this is the order. But the promise was according to human nature. For I told you when I initiated the promise: If you hold in mind each one of the steps.

**3** After I had received the spirit through the power, I set forth the action for you. Indeed the understanding dwells in you. In me it is as though the power were pregnant.

**4** For when I conceived from the fountain that flowed to me, I gave birth.

**5** My father, you have spoken every word well to me. But I am amazed at this statement that you have just made. For you said: The power that is in me.

**6** He said: I gave birth to it as children are born.

**7** Then, my father, I have many brothers, if I am to be numbered among the offspring?

**8** Right, my son! This good thing is numbered by being born. And this is the same thing that happened to you. For you were born in this understanding.

**9** My father, I have questions I want to ask, and I hope you will not be angry with me. Do you not wish to answer me?

**10** The father said: Ask, my son. I will answer with pleasure.

**11** My father, is there a being in this place of the eighth which is beyond the beauty that I have experienced in the seven heavens?

**12** My son, I will tell you that which no tongue can tell, and no hand can write. For the incorporeal is not grasped by the corporeal. The invisible is not held by the visible. The formless is not bound by form.

**13** Come, pray first with me and give thanks to God, the Father of all things, through whom the All is one.
