## Overview

For Gnostic readers, the Discourse on the Eighth and Ninth is one of the most precious texts in the Nag Hammadi library: a record of an actual mystical initiation in which a Hermetic master guides his student through the highest stages of spiritual ascent. The journey beyond the seven planetary spheres — the domain of fate and the archons — to the Eighth (the realm of the fixed stars and divine powers) and the Ninth (the supreme God) represents the culmination of Hermetic spiritual practice. The text describes not abstract theology but lived experience: ecstatic prayer, visionary encounter, tears of joy, and the silence of union.
