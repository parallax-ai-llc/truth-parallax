## Overview

The Comparative reading of the Psalms of Solomon places their messianic expectations alongside royal ideologies from the ancient Near East, Zoroastrian Saoshyant expectations, and later Islamic Mahdi traditions. The collection's theology of righteous suffering through divine discipline resonates with Buddhist teachings on purification through hardship and Hindu concepts of karmic refinement.
