## Overview

The Interfaith reading of the Psalms of Solomon draws on their universal themes of longing for just governance, hope for divine intervention against oppression, and trust in divine faithfulness. The collection provides common ground for Jewish, Christian, and Muslim reflection on the nature of righteous leadership and the role of messianic hope in sustaining communities under foreign domination.
