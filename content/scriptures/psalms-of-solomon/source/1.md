---
title: "Psalms of Solomon Section 1"
scripture: "psalms-of-solomon"
chapter: 1
---

**1** I cried out to the Lord when I was in great distress, unto God when sinners assailed me.

**2** Suddenly the clamor of war was heard before me; I said, He will hear me, for I am full of righteousness.

**3** I thought in my heart that I was full of righteousness, because I had prospered and had many children.

**4** Their wealth was spread over the whole earth, and their glory unto the ends of the earth.

**5** They were exalted unto the stars; they said they would never fall.

**6** But they were arrogant in their prosperity, and they were without understanding.

**7** Their sins were in secret, and I knew them not. Their transgressions exceeded those of the heathen before them; they profaned the sanctuary of the Lord.
