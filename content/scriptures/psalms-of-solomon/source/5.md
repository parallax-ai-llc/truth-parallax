---
title: "Psalms of Solomon Section 5"
scripture: "psalms-of-solomon"
chapter: 5
---

**1** Lord God, I will praise Thy name with joy, in the midst of them that know Thy righteous judgments.

**2** For Thou art good and merciful, the refuge of the poor. When I cry unto Thee, do not be silent toward me.

**3** For no man takes spoil from a mighty man; and who is there that can take from all that Thou hast made, except Thou Thyself givest?

**4** For a man and his lot lie before Thee in the balance; he cannot add to more than Thy judgment, O God.

**5** When we are in distress we call upon Thee for help, and Thou dost not turn away our petition, for Thou art our God.

**6** Cause not Thy hand to be heavy upon us, lest through necessity we sin.

**7** Even though Thou restorest us not, we shall not keep away, but unto Thee will we come. For if I hunger, unto Thee will I cry, O God; and Thou wilt give to me.

**8** Birds and fish dost Thou nourish, when Thou givest rain to the steppe that green grass may spring up, to provide pasture in the steppe for every living thing; and if they hunger, unto Thee do they lift up their face.
