---
title: "Psalms of Solomon Section 3"
scripture: "psalms-of-solomon"
chapter: 3
---

**1** Why sleepest thou, O my soul, and blessest not the Lord? Sing a new song unto God, who is worthy to be praised.

**2** Sing and be wakeful concerning His return, for good is a psalm to God from a glad heart.

**3** The righteous remember the Lord at all times, with thanksgiving and declaration of the righteousness of the Lord's judgments.

**4** The righteous despises not the chastening of the Lord. His will is ever before the Lord.

**5** The righteous stumbles and holds the Lord righteous; he falls and looks to what God will do for him; he seeks the source whence his salvation will come.

**6** The steadfastness of the righteous is from God their Savior. There lodges not in the house of the righteous sin upon sin.

**7** The righteous continually searches his house, to remove utterly all iniquity done by him in error. He makes atonement for sins of ignorance by fasting and afflicting his soul; and the Lord counts guiltless every pious man and his house.
