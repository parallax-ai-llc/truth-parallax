---
title: "Psalms of Solomon Section 2"
scripture: "psalms-of-solomon"
chapter: 2
---

**1** In his arrogance the sinner broke down the strong walls with a battering ram, and Thou didst not prevent him.

**2** Alien nations ascended Thine altar; they trampled it proudly with their sandals.

**3** Because the sons of Jerusalem had defiled the holy things of the Lord, had profaned with iniquity the offerings of God.

**4** Therefore He said: Cast them far from Me; I take no delight in them.

**5** The beauty of His glory was despised before God; it was utterly dishonored.

**6** The sons and daughters were in grievous captivity, sealed was their neck, branded was it among the nations.

**7** According to their sins He dealt with them, for He left them in the hands of them that prevailed.

**8** He turned away His face from pitying them, young and old and their children alike, for they had sinned grievously in not hearkening. And the heaven was moved to wrath, and the earth abhorred them.
