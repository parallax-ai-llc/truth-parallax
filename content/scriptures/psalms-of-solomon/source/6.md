---
title: "Psalms of Solomon Section 6"
scripture: "psalms-of-solomon"
chapter: 6
---

**1** Happy is the man whose heart is fixed to call upon the name of the Lord; when he remembers the name of the Lord, he will be saved.

**2** His ways are made even by the Lord, and the works of his hands are preserved by the Lord his God.

**3** At what he sees in his bad dreams, his soul shall not be troubled; when he passes through rivers and the tossing of the seas, he shall not be dismayed.

**4** He arises from his sleep and blesses the name of the Lord. When his heart is at peace, he sings to the name of his God.

**5** And he entreats the Lord for all his house, and the Lord hears the prayer of everyone that fears God.

**6** And every petition of the soul that hopes in Him is heard by the Lord. Blessed is the Lord, who shows mercy to those who love Him in truth.
