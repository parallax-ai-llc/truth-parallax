---
title: "Psalms of Solomon - Chapter 12"
scripture: "psalms-of-solomon"
chapter: 12
---

**1** O Lord, deliver my soul from the wicked and sinful man, from the tongue that is lawless and slanderous, and speaketh lies.

**2** In manifold and cunning craft are the words of the tongue of the sinful man, like among a people the fire that burneth up their beauty.

**3** His coming filleth the homes with the lying tongue, he cutteth down the trees of gladness, setting firebrands.

**4** He maketh desolate homes by means of a slanderous tongue; may God remove far from the innocent the lips of the lawless in confusion, and the bones of the slanderers scatter far from them that fear the Lord.

**5** In flaming fire perish the slanderous tongue, far from the pious!

**6** May the Lord protect the quiet soul that hateth the unrighteous; and may the Lord establish the man that followeth peace at home.

**7** The salvation of the Lord be upon Israel his servant for ever; and let the sinners perish together at the presence of the Lord; but let the Lord's pious ones inherit the promises of the Lord.
