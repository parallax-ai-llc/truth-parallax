## Overview

The Protestant engagement with the Psalms of Solomon focuses on their importance as background for understanding New Testament messianic claims. The detailed portrait of the Davidic Messiah in Psalm 17 illuminates the expectations Jesus both fulfilled and transformed. The collection also provides essential context for understanding Pharisaic theology as encountered in the Gospels and Paul's letters.
