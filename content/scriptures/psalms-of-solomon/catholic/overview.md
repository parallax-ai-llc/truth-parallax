## Overview

The Catholic reading of the Psalms of Solomon values the collection as a witness to the developing Jewish prayer tradition and messianic expectation that prepared for the coming of Christ. The psalms' emphasis on God's covenant faithfulness, the purification of Jerusalem, and the righteous king resonates with Catholic theology of the kingdom and the Church's liturgical prayer tradition.
