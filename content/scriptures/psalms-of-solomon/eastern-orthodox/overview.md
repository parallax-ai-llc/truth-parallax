## Overview

The Eastern Orthodox encounter with the Psalms of Solomon recognizes continuity between these Second Temple hymns and the rich hymnographic tradition of Orthodoxy. The psalms' liturgical character, their interweaving of lament and praise, and their eschatological hope for divine restoration resonate with the themes of Orthodox worship and the theology of theosis.
