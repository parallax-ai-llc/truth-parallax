---
title: "Visperad Chapter 3"
scripture: "visperad"
chapter: 3
---

**1** We worship the creator Ahura Mazda with our Yasna. We worship the Amesha Spentas with our Yasna. We worship all the righteous beings with our Yasna.

**2** We worship all the lords of the ritual. We worship the Zarathushtrotema, the head of the ritual lords.

**3** We worship the Fire of Ahura Mazda and the waters Mazda-made. We worship the Mathra Spenta and the Mazdayasnian Religion.

**4** We worship all good deeds, done and yet to be done. We worship the entire creation of the righteous spirit.

**5** We worship all the lords of the Yasna and of the Visperad who are righteous.
