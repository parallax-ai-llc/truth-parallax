---
title: "Visperad Chapter 15"
scripture: "visperad"
chapter: 15
---

**1** We worship all good thoughts, all good words, all good deeds, which are and which shall be. We worship all the good things of the Righteous creation.

**2** We worship the Bountiful Gathas, the Ahunavaiti, the Ushtavaiti, the Spenta-Mainyu, the Vohu-Khshathra, the Vahishtoishti. We worship them all with our sacrifice.

**3** We worship each Gatha with its Ha and its ritual. We worship the lords of each Gatha.

**4** We worship the Ahuna Vairya and the Ashem Vohu and the Yenghe Hatam, the holy ritual chiefs.

**5** We worship the entire collection of the ritual with the Visperad. Ashem Vohu.
