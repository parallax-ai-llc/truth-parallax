---
title: "Visperad Chapter 19"
scripture: "visperad"
chapter: 19
---

**1** I announce the Visperad of the lords of the ritual to the day-lords and to the month-lords and to the year-lords and to those of the seasons.

**2** I announce and complete the entire Visperad for the propitiation of Ahura Mazda and the Amesha Spentas.

**3** I announce and complete the Visperad for the propitiation of all the lords of the ritual who are righteous.

**4** I announce and complete the Visperad for the propitiation of the Fravashis of the saints.

**5** I announce and complete the entire Visperad. Ashem Vohu.
