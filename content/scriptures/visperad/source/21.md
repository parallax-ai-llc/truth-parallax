---
title: "Visperad Chapter 21"
scripture: "visperad"
chapter: 21
---

**1** We sacrifice to all the lords of the ritual, of the Yasna, of the Visperad, of the Vendidad.

**2** We sacrifice to all the holy words of Zarathushtra, and to all good deeds done and yet to be done.

**3** We sacrifice to the Mazdayasnian Religion, and to the Mathra Spenta, and to the holy Zarathushtra.

**4** We sacrifice to all the good Yazads, celestial and terrestrial. We sacrifice to the Fravashis of the saints.

**5** We sacrifice to the entire righteous creation of Ahura Mazda. Ashem Vohu.
