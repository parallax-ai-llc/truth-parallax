---
title: "Visperad Chapter 24"
scripture: "visperad"
chapter: 24
---

**1** I am a Mazdayasnian, a follower of Zarathushtra. I profess this faith and I praise it. I praise the well-thought thought, the well-spoken word, and the well-done deed.

**2** I praise the good Mazdayasnian Religion, which overtakes the Drug and puts it down, which is righteous. Of all religions that exist or shall be, it is the greatest, the best, and the most beautiful.

**3** I ascribe all good to Ahura Mazda, and I celebrate the entire Yasna, and the entire Visperad, and all the lords of the ritual.

**4** As the lord is all-powerful, so is the spiritual guide through Righteousness. The gifts of Vohu Manah are for the doer of good for the Lord of Life. The sovereign power belongs to Ahura Mazda who gives sustenance to the poor.

**5** Ashem Vohu Vahishtem asti. Righteousness is the best of all good. It is also happiness. Happy the man who is righteous for the sake of the best Righteousness.

**6** We celebrate and complete the entire Visperad, and the entire Yasna. We worship Ahura Mazda and the Amesha Spentas. We worship all the lords of the ritual. Yatha Ahu Vairyo. Ashem Vohu.
