**1** We worship Ahura Mazda, and we worship the Amesha Spentas, and we worship all the lords of the ritual with the Visperad.

**2** We worship the Mathra Spenta with the Visperad. We worship the Mazdayasnian Religion with the Visperad.

**3** We worship the Fire of Ahura Mazda with the Visperad. We worship all good creatures of Ahura Mazda with the Visperad.

**4** We worship the entire Yasna and Visperad with all their lords and ritual chiefs.

**5** We worship all that is righteous and good. Ashem Vohu.
