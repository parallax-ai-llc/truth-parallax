---
title: "Visperad Chapter 17"
scripture: "visperad"
chapter: 17
---

**1** I celebrate the lords of the Visperad with the Yasna, and I celebrate the Yasna with the Visperad.

**2** I celebrate the Amesha Spentas with the Visperad and the Yasna. I celebrate the Fravashis of the saints.

**3** I celebrate the creators of the creatures, male and female. I celebrate all the good Yazads, celestial and terrestrial.

**4** I celebrate the entire Mazdayasnian Religion. I celebrate the sacred Mathra and the holy Zarathushtra.

**5** I celebrate all the lords of the ritual who are righteous. Ashem Vohu.
