---
title: "Visperad Chapter 1"
scripture: "visperad"
chapter: 1
---

**1** I announce and I (will) complete (my Yasna) to the lords of the days, and of the days in their dawns and their evenings, to the lords of the months, of the years, and of the seasons.

**2** I announce and I complete to the Asnya, the day-lords of the ritual, to Havani, to Savanghi, to Visya, to Rapithwina, to Fradat-fshu, to Zantuma.

**3** I announce and I complete to Uzayeirina, to Fradat-vira, to Dahvyuma, to Aiwisruthrem, to Fradat-vispam-hujyaiti, to Zarathushtrotema.

**4** I announce and I complete to Ushahina, to Berejya, to Nmanya, and to all the lords of the ritual who are the best.

**5** I announce and I complete to the Mahya, monthly festivals, to the new moon and the waning moon, and to the full moon which scatters night.

**6** I announce and I complete to all the lords of the ritual order who are to be worshipped at the time of Havani.
