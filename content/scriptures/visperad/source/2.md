---
title: "Visperad Chapter 2"
scripture: "visperad"
chapter: 2
---

**1** I desire to approach with my worship the lords of all the ritual, the holy lords of the ritual order. I worship all the good Yazads, celestial and terrestrial.

**2** I desire to approach with my worship the Fravashis of the saints, the mighty, the overwhelming. And I desire to approach the Haoma-water and the holy Zaothra.

**3** And I desire to approach with my worship these offered Zaothras and the Baresman spread with sanctity, and the holy flesh, and the Haoma and the milk.

**4** I desire to approach with my worship the Fravashis of all the saints, the righteous, the mighty, the overwhelming, of all lands and nations.

**5** I desire to approach and worship all the lords of the ritual who are the best, who are most righteous, who dispose of all aright.
