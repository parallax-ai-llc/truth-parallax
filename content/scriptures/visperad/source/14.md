---
title: "Visperad Chapter 14"
scripture: "visperad"
chapter: 14
---

**1** I announce the Visperad to all the lords of the ritual, to those of the Yasna, and to those who are most righteous.

**2** I celebrate the entire Yasna with its offerings and its prayers, and I celebrate the Visperad with its lords and ritual chiefs.

**3** I celebrate the bountiful Gathas, the holy, the ruling, and the Staota Yesnya, and the Mathra Spenta.

**4** I celebrate all the good Mazda-made creatures, and all the righteous creation of Ahura Mazda.

**5** I celebrate all the lords of the ritual with the Visperad. Ashem Vohu.
