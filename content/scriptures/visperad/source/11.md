---
title: "Visperad 11 – Praise to the Earth"
chapter: 11
---

**1.** I desire to approach with my praise the Earth, the bountiful, the holy Spenta Armaiti, who bears all good things.

**2.** She bears the crops and the forests, the waters and the cattle, the homes of men and the temples of fire. She is the mother of all that lives upon her.

**3.** We worship thee, O Earth, who art the creation of Ahura Mazda. We worship thee with righteousness and with good thought.

**4.** Blessed is the man who tills the earth with diligence, who waters the dry ground, who drains the wet ground, who sows the seed and reaps the harvest.

**5.** He who cares for the earth cares for the creation of Ahura Mazda. He who neglects the earth neglects the creation of Ahura Mazda.

**6.** May the earth be fruitful, may the pastures be green, may the cattle prosper, may the homes of the faithful be filled with blessing.
