---
title: "Visperad 9 – Praise to the Haoma"
chapter: 9
---

**1.** I desire to approach with my praise the Haoma, the holy, the lord of Righteousness. The Haoma that is golden-hued, that grows upon the mountain-tops.

**2.** The Haoma that was first pressed by Vivanghvant, the father of Yima. He pressed it and gained the blessing of Ahura Mazda.

**3.** The Haoma that was pressed by Athwya, the father of Thraetaona. He pressed it and gained the strength to smite Azhi Dahaka, the three-headed, the three-mouthed.

**4.** The Haoma that was pressed by Thrita of the family of Sama. He pressed it and gained the art of healing, and became the first physician.

**5.** The Haoma that was pressed by Pourushaspa, the father of Zarathushtra. He pressed it and gained the blessing of the Prophet.

**6.** All these pressed the Haoma with righteousness, with prayer, with the sacred word. And the Haoma gave them strength and wisdom and long life.

**7.** I praise the Haoma, the golden-hued, the lord of the mountain-tops. May its strength come to us, may its blessing be upon us, we who worship Ahura Mazda.
