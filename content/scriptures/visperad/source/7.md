---
title: "Visperad 7 – Invocation to the Waters"
chapter: 7
---

**1.** I desire to approach with my praise the good waters, made by Mazda, holy, and all the waters made by Mazda, holy, and all the plants made by Mazda, holy.

**2.** I desire to approach with my praise the sea Vourukasha, and the standing waters and the running waters, the spring waters and the rain waters.

**3.** I desire to approach with my praise Ardvi Sura Anahita, the wide-flowing, the healing, the Daeva-opposing, the faithful to the law of Ahura.

**4.** She it is who purifies the seed of men, who purifies the womb of women, who makes the birth easy, who gives milk to the mothers.

**5.** I desire to approach with my praise the good, strong, beneficent Fravashis of the faithful, who are strong and victorious.

**6.** All waters I praise, all plants I praise, all righteous men I praise, all righteous women I praise.
