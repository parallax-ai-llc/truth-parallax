---
title: "Visperad 10 – Praise to the Fire"
chapter: 10
---

**1.** I desire to approach with my praise the Fire of Ahura Mazda, the son of Ahura Mazda, the lord of Righteousness.

**2.** We praise the fire Berezi-savangha. We praise the fire Vohu-fryana. We praise the fire Urvazishta. We praise the fire Vazishta. We praise the fire Spenishta, the most holy.

**3.** We praise the fire that burns in the homes of men, that warms the body and cooks the food. We praise the fire that burns in the temples, the Bahram fire, the highest of all fires.

**4.** The fire is the representative of Ahura Mazda on earth. He who tends the fire with clean wood and sweet incense serves Ahura Mazda.

**5.** We praise the fire that is in the body, that gives life and warmth. We praise the fire that is in the plants, that makes them grow. We praise the fire that is in the clouds, the lightning.

**6.** All these fires are one fire, the fire of Ahura Mazda. They are his creation and his glory.
