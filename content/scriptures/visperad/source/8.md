---
title: "Visperad 8 – Praise to Asha"
chapter: 8
---

**1.** Asha Vahishta, the Best Righteousness! With all my heart I desire to approach thee with my praise, O beautiful, bountiful, most desired Asha!

**2.** We worship Righteousness, the best of all good, the most beautiful. It is radiant, it is glorious, it is desirable, it is the most powerful of all things for our help.

**3.** Ashem Vohu Vahishtem Asti — Righteousness is the best of all good. It is radiance and glory. Righteous is he who is righteous for the sake of the Best Righteousness.

**4.** This is the word of the holy Zarathushtra: that Asha is the highest good, that Asha is the brightest beauty, that Asha is the surest path to the Best Existence.

**5.** Let every worshipper of Mazda embrace Asha with all his heart, with all his mind, with all his soul. Through Asha shall the Lie be vanquished.

**6.** I praise Asha, the Best, the Most Beautiful. With all the blessings of Asha I desire to approach my Yasna.
