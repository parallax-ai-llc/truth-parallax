---
title: "Visperad Chapter 4"
scripture: "visperad"
chapter: 4
---

**1** I announce my Yasna to the lord Ahura Mazda, to the Amesha Spentas, and to all the ritual lords. And I announce my Yasna for the Visperad, the entire collection of the lords of the ritual.

**2** And I announce the entire Yasna service for all the good things that are of Mazda. I announce the Yasna for the Fravashis of the saints.

**3** I announce my Visperad and my Yasna to the creators of the creatures, male and female, who are worthy of worship.

**4** And I announce my Visperad to the entire creation of the righteous spirit, the bountiful, the good, Mazda-made.

**5** And I announce the Visperad for the entire Mazdayasnian religion, and for all the words of Zarathushtra.
