---
title: "Visperad Chapter 20"
scripture: "visperad"
chapter: 20
---

**1** We worship the entire Visperad, with the Yasna, in the stead of all good things. We worship the lords of the Visperad.

**2** We worship the Ahuna Vairya prayer, the most potent of all. We worship the Ashem Vohu prayer. We worship the Yenghe Hatam prayer.

**3** We worship the bountiful Gathas and the Staota Yesnya and the Mathra Spenta.

**4** We worship the entire creation of the righteous spirit. We worship all the Fravashis of the saints.

**5** We worship the entire Visperad and Yasna. Ashem Vohu.
