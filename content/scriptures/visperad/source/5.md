---
title: "Visperad Chapter 5"
scripture: "visperad"
chapter: 5
---

**1** We worship the bountiful Gathas. We worship the Ahunavaiti Gatha with our Yasna, the Ushtavaiti Gatha, the Spenta-Mainyu Gatha, the Vohu-Khshathra Gatha, and the Vahishtoishti Gatha.

**2** We worship each Gatha in its proper place. We worship the five Gathas as the five great prayers of Zarathushtra.

**3** We worship the Staota Yesnya with its complete offering. We worship the Ahuna Vairya and the Ashem Vohu and the Yenghe Hatam.

**4** We worship these holy words which are the most potent of all words, the most victorious, the most healing, the most fiend-smiting.

**5** We worship them with our body and with our life and with the best worship of the Yasna.
