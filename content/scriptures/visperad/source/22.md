---
title: "Visperad Chapter 22"
scripture: "visperad"
chapter: 22
---

**1** I celebrate the lords of the Visperad with the lords of the Yasna, with the lords of all the ritual.

**2** I celebrate the Amesha Spentas, the righteous, the mighty. I celebrate all the Fravashis of the saints.

**3** I celebrate the entire Mazdayasnian Religion, the worship and the praise and the adoration.

**4** I celebrate the Fire of Ahura Mazda and the good waters and the wholesome plants.

**5** I celebrate and complete the entire Visperad. Ashem Vohu.
