---
title: "Visperad Chapter 13"
scripture: "visperad"
chapter: 13
---

**1** We worship all the lords of the ritual, of the Yasna and of the Visperad, with our sacrifice and prayer. We worship the Zarathushtrotema.

**2** We worship the Fravashis of the righteous, those who dwell in the heavens and those who dwell upon the earth.

**3** We worship the entire creation of Ahura Mazda, the righteous and the good. We worship the Mathra Spenta with our sacrifice.

**4** We worship Asha Vahishta, the best Righteousness. We worship the Kine-soul and the Kine-body.

**5** We celebrate the entire Visperad. Ashem Vohu.
