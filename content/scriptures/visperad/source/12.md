---
title: "Visperad 12 – On the Offering"
chapter: 12
---

**1.** We offer this Yasna, this homage, this adoration, and this praise to Ahura Mazda, the righteous lord of Righteousness.

**2.** We offer this Yasna to the Amesha Spentas, who are the lords of the good creation, who protect and sustain all righteous beings.

**3.** We offer this Yasna with the sacred meat, with the sacred bread, with the Haoma and the milk. These are the offerings of the faithful.

**4.** We offer this Yasna with the sacred words, with the Gathas, with the Ahunavar and the Ashem Vohu. These are the offerings of the spirit.

**5.** May our offerings be acceptable to Ahura Mazda. May they bring blessing upon our homes, our families, and our communities.

**6.** Through these offerings we strengthen the good creation and weaken the Lie. Through these offerings we advance the cause of Righteousness.
