---
title: "Visperad Chapter 6"
scripture: "visperad"
chapter: 6
---

**1** I celebrate the Haoma-drink with our Yasna, the Haoma offered with sanctity. I celebrate the milk, the meat, and the holy Zaothra.

**2** I celebrate the Baresman spread with sanctity, and the Haoma juice, and the Parahaoma.

**3** I celebrate the Fire of Ahura Mazda, the son of Ahura Mazda. I celebrate the waters which are Mazda-made. I celebrate all the good creatures of Ahura Mazda.

**4** I celebrate the holy Fravashis of the righteous. I celebrate the souls of the righteous who are dead, who are living, and who are yet to be born.

**5** I celebrate the entire Visperad and the entire Yasna with all the lords of the ritual order.
