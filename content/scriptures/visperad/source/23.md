---
title: "Visperad Chapter 23"
scripture: "visperad"
chapter: 23
---

**1** We worship Ahura Mazda with our sacrifice, the holy lord of Asha. We worship the Amesha Spentas with our sacrifice.

**2** We worship all the lords of the Yasna and the Visperad with our sacrifice. We worship the entire righteous creation.

**3** We worship the good, the heroic, the bountiful Fravashis of the saints. We worship the Mathra Spenta.

**4** We worship the Fire of Ahura Mazda. We worship all that is righteous and good.

**5** We celebrate and complete the entire Visperad. Yatha Ahu Vairyo. Ashem Vohu.
