---
title: "Visperad Chapter 16"
scripture: "visperad"
chapter: 16
---

**1** We worship the bountiful holy Gathas. We worship the Yasna Haptanghaiti. We worship the entire Staota Yesnya.

**2** We worship all the good Mathra, all the holy words of Zarathushtra. We worship all the good deeds, done and yet to be done.

**3** We worship the Mazdayasnian Religion, the upholder of the Righteous Order, the smiter of the Drug.

**4** We worship the Fire of Ahura Mazda and the waters and the earth and the plants and the heavens and the holy wind.

**5** We worship the entire creation of the righteous spirit with the Visperad and the Yasna. Ashem Vohu.
