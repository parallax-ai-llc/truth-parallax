This chapter amplifies the essential Way (dao) through filial piety, teaching that the way of filial devotion encompasses teaching the people respect through reverence for one's father, teaching them compliance through reverence for one's elder brother, and teaching them loyalty through reverence for one's ruler. Confucian interpreters see here the mechanism by which personal virtue becomes public transformation.

The chapter demonstrates the Confucian method of moral extension: beginning with the most intimate relationships and systematically broadening the circle of moral concern until it encompasses all of society.
