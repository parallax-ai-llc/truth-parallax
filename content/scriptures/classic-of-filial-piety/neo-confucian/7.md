The trinity chapter receives extensive Neo-Confucian commentary because it most directly addresses the metaphysical dimensions of filial piety. The teaching that xiao connects heaven, earth, and humanity confirms the Neo-Confucian understanding of reality as a unified system of principle. Zhu Xi interprets the cosmic efficacy of filial piety through the doctrine of the Great Ultimate (taiji), which generates yin and yang and all phenomena.

For Neo-Confucian readers, this chapter demonstrates that filial piety is not a merely human invention but the expression of the deepest structure of reality.
