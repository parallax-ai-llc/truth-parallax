**1** The Master said, "The teaching of filial piety by the ancient kings consisted in this: that they governed themselves according to the rules of propriety; that they established themselves by the practice of righteousness; that their loyalty to the state was constant; and that their obedience to their parents was complete."

**2** Zeng said, "I am not gifted with intelligence, but I beg leave to receive your words and hold them fast."
