title: "Classic of Filial Piety - Chapter 9"
scripture: "classic-of-filial-piety"
chapter: 9
---

**1** The Master said, "The government of the intelligent kings by filial piety was as follows: They served with filial piety, and therefore all fathers under heaven were held in honour. They served their elders with fraternal duty, and therefore all elder brothers under heaven were held in honour. They served their rulers with loyalty, and therefore all rulers under heaven were held in honour."

**2** "It is said in the Book of Poetry: 'The happy and courteous prince is the parent of the people.' If it were not a perfect virtue, how could it be recognized as such by the people?"
