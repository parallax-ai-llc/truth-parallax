---
title: "Classic of Filial Piety - Chapter 7"
scripture: "classic-of-filial-piety"
chapter: 7
---

**1** Zeng said, "Immense indeed is the greatness of filial piety!"

**2** The Master said, "Yes, filial piety is the constant method of Heaven, the righteousness of Earth, and the practical duty of Man."

**3** "Heaven and earth invariably pursue their course, and the people take it as their pattern. The brilliant luminaries of heaven are imitated by the people, and the natural advantages of earth are turned to their benefit."

**4** "The ancient kings, seeing that their teaching could transform the people, set before them an example of the most extended love, and none of the people neglected their parents."

**5** "They set before them the highest example of virtue and righteousness, and the people roused themselves to the practice of it. They showed an example of respectful yielding, and the people had no contentions."

**6** "They led them on by the rules of propriety and by music, and the people were concordant. They showed them what they liked and what they disliked, and the people understood their prohibitions."

**7** "It is said in the Book of Poetry: 'Awe-inspiring are you, O Grand-Master Yin, and the people all look up to you.'"
