## Overview

Theological engagement with the Atrahasis brings distinctive interpretive methods and concerns to this foundational text. This approach illuminates dimensions of the text that may be less visible from other perspectives.

## Key Interpretive Approaches

### Systematic Reflection
Theological interpreters examine the Atrahasis for its contributions to systematic understanding of the divine nature, the human condition, and the relationship between God (or gods) and humanity.

### Cosmological Analysis
The Atrahasis's creation narratives and cosmological framework are analyzed for their theological implications about the origin, nature, and purpose of the created order.

### Theodicy
The Atrahasis's treatment of suffering, divine justice, and the problem of evil invites sustained theological reflection.
