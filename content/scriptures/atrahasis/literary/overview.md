## Overview

Literary engagement with the Atrahasis brings distinctive interpretive methods and concerns to this foundational text. This approach illuminates dimensions of the text that may be less visible from other perspectives.

## Key Interpretive Approaches

### Narrative Analysis
Literary scholars examine the Atrahasis's narrative techniques, including plot structure, characterization, point of view, and the use of repetition and variation to create meaning.

### Poetic Form
The Atrahasis's formal properties — meter, imagery, symbolism, and rhetorical devices — are central to literary interpretation. The relationship between form and content is a key concern.

### Intertextuality
Literary analysis traces the Atrahasis's relationships with other texts, examining how it draws upon, transforms, and responds to earlier and contemporary literary traditions.
