Academic analysis of Risala al-Qushayriyya section 9, "Knowledge and Gnosis," examines the text's historical context, its sources, and its contribution to the development of Islamic mystical thought. Scholars trace Qushayri's material to earlier authors and analyze his distinctive contributions to the systematization of Sufi doctrine and practice.

This section has been studied for its preservation of early Sufi teachings, its role in standardizing mystical terminology, and its evidence for the institutional and intellectual dynamics of eleventh-century Islamic culture. Comparative scholars have explored parallels between Qushayri's framework and systematic treatments of mystical experience in other traditions, including Christian and Buddhist contemplative literature.
