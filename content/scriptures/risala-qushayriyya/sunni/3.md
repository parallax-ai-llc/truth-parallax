Sunni reading of Risala al-Qushayriyya section 3, "The Stations of the Path," evaluates Qushayri's teaching against the standards of Sunni orthodoxy. The Sunni tradition appreciates this section for its demonstration that the spiritual concepts and practices described are grounded in the Quran, Sunnah, and the consensus of the scholarly community. Qushayri's careful documentation of the early masters' adherence to Islamic law provides assurance that the mystical path does not deviate from normative practice.

This section contributes to the Sunni understanding that the inner dimensions of faith — sincerity, presence of heart, and awareness of God — are not optional additions but essential components of complete religious life. Qushayri's balance between mystical aspiration and legal fidelity offers a model that the Sunni tradition has embraced for nearly a millennium.
