Sufi interpretation of Risala al-Qushayriyya section 1, "The Lives of the Sufi Masters," reads Qushayri's teaching as practical guidance for the spiritual journey. The Sufi tradition values this section for its clarity in mapping the stages of the path and its fidelity to the experiential wisdom of the early masters. Each concept and distinction presented here is not merely theoretical but reflects the accumulated experience of generations of practitioners.

The Sufi reader approaches this section as a mirror for self-examination: Where am I on the path? Which stations have I traversed and which remain before me? What states has the divine granted me, and how should I respond to them? Qushayri's systematic presentation allows the practitioner to orient themselves within the landscape of the spiritual journey with precision and humility.
