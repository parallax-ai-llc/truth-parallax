---
title: "Sirach - Chapter 18"
scripture: "sirach"
chapter: 18
---

**1** The wise man shall inherit glory among his people, and his name shall be perpetual.

**2** He that feareth the Lord will do good; and he that hath the knowledge of the law shall obtain her.

**3** The Lord is patient, and full of mercy, and forgiveth sins, and saveth in time of affliction.

**4** A faithful friend is a strong defence: and he that hath found such an one hath found a treasure.

**5** In all thy works keep to thyself the preeminence; leave not a stain in thine honour.

**6** My son, glorify thy soul in meekness, and give it honour according to the dignity thereof.

**7** The heart of the prudent will understand a parable; and an attentive ear is the desire of a wise man.

**8** Water will quench a flaming fire; and alms maketh an atonement for sins.

**9** The discourse of a godly man is always with wisdom; but a fool changeth as the moon.

**10** The lips of talkers will be telling such things as pertain not unto them: but the words of such as have understanding are weighed in the balance.

**11** Open not thine heart to every man, lest he requite thee with a shrewd turn.

**12** Blessed is the man that doth meditate good things in wisdom, and that reasoneth of holy things by his understanding.

**13** The fear of the Lord is the first step to be accepted of him: and wisdom obtaineth his love.

**14** Give unto the most High according as he hath enriched thee; and as thou hast gotten, give with a cheerful eye.

**15** The wisdom of a learned man cometh by opportunity of leisure: and he that hath little business shall become wise.

**16** How can he get wisdom that holdeth the plough, and that glorieth in the goad?

**17** But they will maintain the state of the world, and all their desire is in the work of their craft.

**18** Praise the Lord in all his works, and magnify his name with thanksgiving.

**19** O how great is he that findeth wisdom! yet is there none above him that feareth the Lord.

**20** The fear of the Lord passeth all things: blessed is he that holdeth it.

**21** The wise man shall inherit glory among his people, and his name shall be perpetual.

**22** He that feareth the Lord will do good; and he that hath the knowledge of the law shall obtain her.

**23** The Lord is patient, and full of mercy, and forgiveth sins, and saveth in time of affliction.

**24** A faithful friend is a strong defence: and he that hath found such an one hath found a treasure.

**26** My son, glorify thy soul in meekness, and give it honour according to the dignity thereof.

**28** Water will quench a flaming fire; and alms maketh an atonement for sins.

**30** The lips of talkers will be telling such things as pertain not unto them: but the words of such as have understanding are weighed in the balance.
