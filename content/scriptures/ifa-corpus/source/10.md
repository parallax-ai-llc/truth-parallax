---
title: "Ifa Corpus - Osa Meji"
scripture: "ifa-corpus"
chapter: 10
---

**1** Osa Meji is the Odu of Oya, the orisha of the wind, of change, of the marketplace. When Osa Meji appears, winds of change are blowing.

**2** Osa Meji speaks of transformation and movement. Nothing stands still in this world. Those who try to hold everything in place will be overwhelmed by change.

**3** Orunmila teaches: The market of life is always changing. What is valuable today may be worthless tomorrow. Invest in character and knowledge, for these never lose their value.

**4** Osa Meji says: The wind sweeps away the dead leaves, but the living tree remains. Be like the living tree. Let the wind carry away what is no longer needed.

**5** The sacrifice for Osa Meji: she-goat, eggplant, shea butter. Those who embrace change shall prosper.

**6** Osa Meji warns: Do not cling to the past. The river that stops flowing becomes a stagnant pool. Move forward, always forward.
