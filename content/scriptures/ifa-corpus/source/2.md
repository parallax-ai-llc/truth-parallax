---
title: "Ifa Corpus - Oyeku Meji"
scripture: "ifa-corpus"
chapter: 2
---

**1** Oyeku Meji is the Odu of darkness, of night, of mystery. Where Eji Ogbe is the day, Oyeku Meji is the night. Together they form the complete cycle.

**2** Oyeku Meji speaks of death and transformation. Death is not the end but the beginning of a new journey. The ancestors dwell in the realm of Oyeku Meji.

**3** When Oyeku Meji appears in divination, it speaks of hidden matters, of secrets, of things not yet revealed. The client must be patient and wait for understanding.

**4** Orunmila says: Do not fear the darkness, for in the darkness the seeds germinate. Do not fear death, for death is the doorway to the ancestors.

**5** The sacrifice for Oyeku Meji: black cloth, black hen, palm oil. Those who honor the ancestors shall be protected by them.

**6** Oyeku Meji teaches: Every ending is a beginning. What appears to be destruction is often transformation. Trust in the wisdom of Olodumare, who sees the whole pattern.
