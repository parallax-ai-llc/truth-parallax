---
title: "Ifa Corpus - Ofun Meji"
scripture: "ifa-corpus"
chapter: 16
---

**1** Ofun Meji is the last and the beginning, the Odu of purity, of return to the source. It is the Odu that completes the circle and begins it again.

**2** Ofun Meji speaks of Obatala, the orisha of creation, of purity, of wisdom. Obatala is the father of all the orishas and the shaper of human destiny.

**3** Orunmila teaches through Ofun Meji: In the end, all returns to the beginning. The journey of the soul is circular. We come from Olodumare and we return to Olodumare.

**4** Ofun Meji says: Purity of heart is the highest attainment. The person whose heart is pure stands in the presence of Olodumare without shame.

**5** The sacrifice for Ofun Meji: white cloth, snails, shea butter, and efun (white chalk). Those who seek purity of heart shall find it.

**6** Ofun Meji teaches: This is not the end of the Ifa corpus, but a doorway to deeper understanding. Beyond the sixteen major Odu are two hundred and forty minor Odu, and within each Odu are thousands of verses. The study of Ifa is the work of a lifetime.
