---
title: "Ifa Corpus - Irete Meji"
scripture: "ifa-corpus"
chapter: 14
---

**1** Irete Meji is the Odu of persistence, of determination, of the long road. When Irete Meji appears, the client is on a long journey and must not give up.

**2** Irete Meji teaches: The tortoise reaches its destination, though it moves slowly. The person who persists shall achieve their goal, even if the world says it is impossible.

**3** Orunmila said: Irete Meji went to heaven and came back. The journey was long and the road was hard, but Irete did not turn back. This is why Irete is blessed.

**4** Irete Meji says: Do not compare your journey to the journey of others. Each person has their own path, their own pace, their own destination. Walk your own road.

**5** The sacrifice for Irete Meji: snails, pigeons, and yams. Those who persist in righteousness shall be rewarded in the end.

**6** Irete Meji warns: Impatience is the enemy of success. The farmer who pulls up the seedling to see if it has taken root will kill it. Let things grow in their own time.
