---
title: "Ifa Corpus - Okanran Meji"
scripture: "ifa-corpus"
chapter: 8
---

**1** Okanran Meji is the Odu of conflict, of confrontation, of the need for courage. When Okanran Meji appears, the client must face a challenge head-on.

**2** Okanran Meji is associated with Ogun, the orisha of iron, of war, of technology. Ogun is the pathfinder, the one who clears the way through the forest.

**3** Orunmila teaches: There are times when conflict cannot be avoided. When injustice stands in the way, the righteous person must fight. But fight with honor, not with treachery.

**4** Okanran Meji says: The sword of truth is sharper than the sword of iron. Fight first with words, then with actions. But if you must fight, fight to win.

**5** The sacrifice for Okanran Meji: dog, rooster, iron implements. Those who face their challenges with courage shall overcome them.

**6** Okanran Meji warns: Do not start fights unnecessarily. The person who is always fighting will one day meet someone stronger. Choose your battles wisely.
