---
title: "Ifa Corpus - Obara Meji"
scripture: "ifa-corpus"
chapter: 7
---

**1** Obara Meji is the Odu of wealth, of kingship, of authority. When Obara Meji appears, matters of power and prosperity are at hand.

**2** Obara Meji is associated with the orisha Shango, lord of thunder and lightning, king of kings. Shango embodies the power of righteous authority.

**3** Orunmila teaches through Obara Meji: Power without justice is tyranny. Wealth without generosity is poverty of the soul. The true king serves his people.

**4** Obara Meji says: The person who is generous shall never lack. The river that gives its water to the land receives it back from the rain.

**5** The sacrifice for Obara Meji: ram, red cloth, thunder-stone. Those who use their power justly shall be honored by both the living and the dead.

**6** Obara Meji warns: Pride goes before a fall. The person who forgets that all power comes from Olodumare shall be humbled.
