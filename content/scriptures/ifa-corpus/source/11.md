---
title: "Ifa Corpus - Ika Meji"
scripture: "ifa-corpus"
chapter: 11
---

**1** Ika Meji is the Odu of protection, of watchfulness, of awareness of danger. When Ika Meji appears, the client must be alert.

**2** Ika Meji teaches: Not everyone who smiles at you is your friend. Not every opportunity is genuine. Be discerning, and you will avoid the traps that are set for you.

**3** Orunmila said through Ika Meji: The gazelle that stops watching for the leopard will be eaten. Stay alert, but do not live in fear. Awareness and fear are different things.

**4** Ika Meji is associated with medicine and healing. The same plant that can heal can also poison. Knowledge must be used wisely.

**5** The sacrifice for Ika Meji: tortoise, snail, and herbs. Those who are watchful shall be protected from harm.

**6** Ika Meji says: Prevention is better than cure. The person who sees danger and avoids it is wiser than the person who walks into it and must fight their way out.
