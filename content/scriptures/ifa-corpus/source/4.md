---
title: "Ifa Corpus - Odi Meji"
scripture: "ifa-corpus"
chapter: 4
---

**1** Odi Meji is the Odu of the womb, of creation, of new beginnings. It speaks of fertility, of birth, of the creative power that brings new things into the world.

**2** Odi Meji is associated with the orisha Yemoja, mother of the waters, mother of all the orishas. When Odi Meji appears, new life is stirring.

**3** Orunmila teaches through Odi Meji: Respect women, for they are the bearers of life. Honor mothers, for through them the human race continues.

**4** Odi Meji warns: Do not block the path of creation. Do not prevent what is meant to be born. The creative force of the universe cannot be stopped, only delayed.

**5** The sacrifice for Odi Meji: she-goat, white cloth, honey. Those who honor the creative forces shall be blessed with abundance.

**6** Odi Meji says: Every person carries within them the seed of greatness. Nurture the seed, water it with effort, and it will grow into a mighty tree.
