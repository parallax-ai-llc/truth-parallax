---
title: "Ifa Corpus - Oturupon Meji"
scripture: "ifa-corpus"
chapter: 12
---

**1** Oturupon Meji is the Odu of sickness and healing, of Babalaye, the orisha of disease and medicine. When Oturupon Meji appears, matters of health are foremost.

**2** Oturupon Meji teaches: The body is the temple of the spirit. Care for your body, and your spirit will be strong. Neglect your body, and your spirit will weaken.

**3** Orunmila said: The sickness of the body begins in the spirit. Heal the spirit first, and the body will follow. A bitter heart breeds a sick body.

**4** Oturupon Meji says: There is no shame in illness. It comes to all. But there is shame in refusing help, in refusing to change the habits that cause sickness.

**5** The sacrifice for Oturupon Meji: dried corn, beans, sesame. Those who care for their bodies and spirits shall enjoy long life.

**6** Oturupon Meji teaches: Compassion for the sick is a sacred duty. The person who turns away from the suffering of others turns away from the orishas.
