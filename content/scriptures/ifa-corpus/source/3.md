---
title: "Ifa Corpus - Iwori Meji"
scripture: "ifa-corpus"
chapter: 3
---

**1** Iwori Meji is the Odu of inner vision, of the heart, of character. It speaks of what is hidden within a person, the true nature that lies beneath the surface.

**2** Iwori Meji teaches: Character is destiny. Iwa, good character, is the most important thing a person can possess. Without good character, all other blessings are meaningless.

**3** Orunmila said: When they asked what brings blessing from heaven, I said it is good character. Good character is the finest beauty. It does not fade with age.

**4** Iwori Meji warns against hypocrisy. The person who appears good on the outside but is rotten within shall be exposed. The person who appears humble but is proud shall fall.

**5** The sacrifice for Iwori Meji: pigeon, palm oil, and kola nuts. Those who cultivate good character shall be blessed in all their endeavors.

**6** Iwori Meji says: Look within before you look without. Judge yourself before you judge others. The person who knows himself is wiser than the person who knows the world.
