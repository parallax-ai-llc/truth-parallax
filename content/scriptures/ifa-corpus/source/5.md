---
title: "Ifa Corpus - Irosun Meji"
scripture: "ifa-corpus"
chapter: 5
---

**1** Irosun Meji is the Odu of blood, of lineage, of ancestry. It speaks of the connection between the living and the dead, between parent and child, between past and future.

**2** Irosun Meji teaches: Honor your ancestors. Make offerings at their shrines. Remember their names and tell their stories to your children.

**3** The blood that flows in your veins is the same blood that flowed in the veins of your ancestors. You are connected to them by an unbreakable chain.

**4** Orunmila said: The tree that forgets its roots will be blown down by the wind. The person who forgets their ancestors will be lost in the world.

**5** The sacrifice for Irosun Meji: rooster, red palm oil, kola nuts. Those who honor their lineage shall be strengthened by the power of their ancestors.

**6** Irosun Meji warns: The sins of the parents can affect the children. Therefore live righteously, so that your descendants may be blessed.
