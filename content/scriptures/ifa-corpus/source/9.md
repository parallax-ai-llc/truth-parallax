---
title: "Ifa Corpus - Ogunda Meji"
scripture: "ifa-corpus"
chapter: 9
---

**1** Ogunda Meji is the Odu of Ogun, the opener of the way, the one who clears the path. When Ogunda Meji appears, obstacles will be removed.

**2** Ogunda Meji speaks of truth and justice. Ogun is the sworn enemy of liars and oath-breakers. Those who swear falsely by Ogun shall suffer.

**3** Orunmila teaches through Ogunda Meji: An oath is sacred. A promise made must be kept. The person who breaks their word shall lose the trust of gods and men.

**4** Ogunda Meji says: The blacksmith forges iron in the fire. In the same way, character is forged in the fire of hardship. Do not avoid difficulty, for it makes you strong.

**5** The sacrifice for Ogunda Meji: palm wine, rooster, iron nails. Those who keep their oaths shall be protected by Ogun.

**6** Ogunda Meji teaches: Clear the path for others, and your own path will be cleared. The person who helps others will be helped by the universe.
