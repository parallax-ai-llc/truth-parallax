---
title: "Ifa Corpus - Eji Ogbe"
scripture: "ifa-corpus"
chapter: 1
---

**1** Eji Ogbe is the first and greatest of the Odu. It is the Odu of light, of clarity, of the beginning. When Eji Ogbe appears, the path is open and the blessings of Olodumare flow freely.

**2** Orunmila, the prophet of Ifa, received this Odu first among all the Odu. It is the foundation upon which all other Odu rest.

**3** Eji Ogbe teaches: Honesty is the foundation of all virtue. The person who speaks truth in all matters shall prosper. The liar shall be exposed in time.

**4** When the world was new, Olodumare sent the sixteen principal Odu to earth. Eji Ogbe came first and established the order of things. He set the sun in the sky and the rivers in their courses.

**5** The sacrifice for Eji Ogbe: white cloth, white kola nut, shea butter, and cool water. Those who make the proper sacrifice shall find their path cleared of obstacles.

**6** Eji Ogbe says: The dawn always follows the darkness. No matter how long the night, the morning will come. Be patient, be truthful, and your reward is certain.
