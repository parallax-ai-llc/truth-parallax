---
title: "Ifa Corpus - Owonrin Meji"
scripture: "ifa-corpus"
chapter: 6
---

**1** Owonrin Meji is the Odu of change, of revolution, of the unexpected. When Owonrin Meji appears, nothing is as it seems. The world is about to turn upside down.

**2** Owonrin Meji is associated with Eshu, the divine messenger, the trickster, the keeper of the crossroads. Eshu reminds us that the universe is not predictable.

**3** Orunmila teaches: Be flexible. The rigid tree breaks in the storm, but the flexible tree bends and survives. Expect the unexpected and you will not be caught off guard.

**4** Owonrin Meji says: What goes up must come down. What is down must come up. The wheel of fortune turns, and those on top today may be on the bottom tomorrow.

**5** The sacrifice for Owonrin Meji: rooster, palm oil, and gin for Eshu. Those who honor Eshu shall navigate the changes successfully.

**6** Owonrin Meji teaches: Do not judge a situation by its appearance. What seems like disaster may be opportunity. What seems like opportunity may be a trap. Look deeper.
