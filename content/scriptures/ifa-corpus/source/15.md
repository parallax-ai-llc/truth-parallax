---
title: "Ifa Corpus - Ose Meji"
scripture: "ifa-corpus"
chapter: 15
---

**1** Ose Meji is the Odu of abundance, of blessings, of Oshun, the orisha of sweet water, love, and fertility. When Ose Meji appears, blessings are flowing.

**2** Ose Meji teaches: The sweetness of life is a gift from Olodumare. Do not reject it out of false pride. Enjoy the blessings that come to you, and share them with others.

**3** Orunmila said through Ose Meji: Honey is sweet, but too much honey makes the stomach sick. Enjoy the good things of life in moderation.

**4** Ose Meji is associated with love and marriage. When Ose Meji appears in a reading about relationships, the prognosis is good. Love will flourish.

**5** The sacrifice for Ose Meji: honey, oranges, cinnamon, and five eggs. Those who give generously in love shall receive abundantly.

**6** Ose Meji says: The river of blessings flows from Olodumare through the orishas to the people. Keep the channel open through prayer, sacrifice, and good character.
