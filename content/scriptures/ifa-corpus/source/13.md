---
title: "Ifa Corpus - Otura Meji"
scripture: "ifa-corpus"
chapter: 13
---

**1** Otura Meji is the Odu of wisdom, of divination, of the knowledge that comes from Ifa itself. It is the Odu of Orunmila, the prophet.

**2** Otura Meji speaks of the importance of divination. When in doubt, consult Ifa. The wisdom of Ifa is older than the world and deeper than the ocean.

**3** Orunmila said through Otura Meji: I was present when Olodumare created the world. I was the witness to creation. This is why I know the past, the present, and the future.

**4** Otura Meji teaches: True wisdom is humble. The more you know, the more you realize how much you do not know. The wise person asks questions; the fool thinks he has all the answers.

**5** The sacrifice for Otura Meji: two pigeons, shea butter, and white cloth. Those who seek wisdom through Ifa shall find their path illuminated.

**6** Otura Meji says: Knowledge without application is useless. Wisdom without action is empty. Learn, and then act upon what you have learned.
