## Overview

Within the Yoruba religious tradition, the Ifa corpus occupies a position of supreme authority as the direct revelation of Orunmila, the deity of wisdom and divination. The babalawo (Ifa priest) undergoes years of intensive training to memorize the verses (*ese*) of the Odu and master the techniques of divination. Ifa consultation is central to Yoruba religious life, guiding decisions about marriage, childbirth, business, travel, governance, and spiritual practice.

The Yoruba understanding of Ifa emphasizes its role as the guide to fulfilling one's destiny (*ori*). Before birth, each soul selects its destiny before Olodumare (the Supreme Being) and the witnesses of heaven. Through Ifa divination, individuals learn what sacrifices (*ebo*) to offer, which Orisha to honor, and what conduct to follow in order to align with their chosen destiny. The corpus thus functions as a comprehensive guide to living in harmony with cosmic and social order (*iwa pele*, gentle character).
