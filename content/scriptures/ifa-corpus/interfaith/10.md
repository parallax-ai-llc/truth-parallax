Odu 10 — Osa Meji — Water and Spiritual Power — offers valuable perspectives for interfaith dialogue, presenting Yoruba spiritual wisdom on water and spiritual power that resonates with the concerns of the world's major religious traditions. The Odu's teachings on these themes contribute an essential African voice to global interreligious conversation, enriching dialogue that has too often been limited to Abrahamic and Asian perspectives.

Interfaith engagement with Osa Meji reveals shared human concerns addressed in different ways across traditions: the search for guidance in the face of uncertainty, the role of sacrifice and offering in the relationship between humans and the divine, and the ethical foundations of a meaningful life. The Odu's integration of practical wisdom with spiritual insight — its refusal to separate the sacred from the everyday — offers a distinctive perspective that challenges Western tendencies to compartmentalize religious and secular knowledge.
