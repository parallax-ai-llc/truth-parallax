---
title: "Vimalakirti Sutra Chapter 13"
scripture: "vimalakirti-sutra"
chapter: 13
---

**1.** At that time the Buddha expounded the Dharma in the land called Abhirati in the eastern quarter. The Buddha of that land was called Akshobhya — the Immovable.

**2.** Vimalakirti said to the assembly: "Good sirs, have you ever seen the land of the Buddha Akshobhya and the Pure Land Abhirati?"

**3.** Manjusri said: "I have not seen it. I wish the householder would show it to us."

**4.** Then Vimalakirti, without rising from his seat, used his right hand to reach into the eastern quarter, and grasped the entire world-system Abhirati, including its mountains, rivers, oceans, continents, the Buddha Akshobhya and all his Bodhisattvas and Sravakas, its gods, men, asuras, and all beings.

**5.** He brought the whole world-system and placed it in the palm of his right hand, as a potter lifts a wheel, and brought it to this Saha world to show the assembly.

**6.** The beings in the land Abhirati who possessed the divine eye cried out: "World-Honoured One, who is carrying us away? Save us!" The Buddha Akshobhya said: "It is not I who does this. It is the Bodhisattva Vimalakirti who is performing this act."

**7.** Those who did not possess the divine eye did not know they had been moved, and the world Abhirati was in no way diminished.

**8.** Then the Lord Sakyamuni said to the assembly: "Do you see the world Abhirati and the Buddha Akshobhya?" They said: "Yes, we see it."

**9.** The Buddha said: "Bodhisattvas who wish to obtain a Pure Land such as this should study the practices of the Buddha Akshobhya."

**10.** When the world Abhirati was shown, fourteen nayutas of persons in this Saha world produced the thought of supreme, perfect enlightenment and all aspired to be reborn in the world-system Abhirati.

**11.** The Buddha gave them a prediction: "You shall all be reborn in that world."

**12.** Then Vimalakirti returned the world Abhirati to its original location in the east, without any of its inhabitants being aware of having moved.

**13.** The Buddha said to Sariputra: "Did you see this world Abhirati and the Buddha Akshobhya?"

**14.** Sariputra said: "Yes, I saw it, World-Honoured One. May all beings obtain a Pure Land as pure as the world of the Buddha Akshobhya, and may they all obtain supernatural powers like those of Vimalakirti."

**15.** The Buddha said: "Excellent! Excellent! As you say, all beings should aspire to Pure Lands, and know that the inconceivable liberation of the Bodhisattva enables even worlds to be moved and placed in the palm of the hand, with no one the wiser. Such is the liberation beyond thought."
