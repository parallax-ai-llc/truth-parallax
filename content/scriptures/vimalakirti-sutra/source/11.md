---
title: "Vimalakirti Sutra Chapter 11"
scripture: "vimalakirti-sutra"
chapter: 11
---

**1.** At that time the Buddha at the garden of Amrapali was preaching the Dharma. Suddenly the ground became pure and adorned, and the whole assembly turned golden in colour.

**2.** Ananda said: "World-Honoured One, what is the cause of this auspicious sign — the ground becoming pure and the assembly turning golden?"

**3.** The Buddha said: "This is because Vimalakirti and Manjusri, with their great assembly, are about to come here."

**4.** At that time Vimalakirti said to Manjusri: "Let us go together to see the Buddha and allow the Bodhisattvas to pay homage to him."

**5.** Vimalakirti then used his supernatural powers to place all the members of the assembly — Bodhisattvas, great disciples, and others — on the palm of his right hand, and transported them all to where the Buddha was.

**6.** Having arrived, he set them down on the ground. They bowed at the Buddha's feet, and then circled him to the right seven times. Having made their obeisance, they sat down to one side.

**7.** The Buddha said to Sariputra: "Did you see the acts of the Bodhisattva Vimalakirti and his great supernatural power?"

**8.** Sariputra said: "Yes, I saw them, World-Honoured One. They are beyond what I could have imagined. They are beyond the reach of thought and calculation."

**9.** Ananda then asked the Buddha: "World-Honoured One, the fragrance I smell now is one I have never smelled before. What fragrance is this?"

**10.** The Buddha said: "Ananda, this fragrance comes from the pores of the bodies of these Bodhisattvas." Sariputra said: "The fragrance also comes from our pores — from the fragrant rice that Vimalakirti provided."

**11.** Ananda asked Vimalakirti: "How long will this fragrance last?"

**12.** Vimalakirti said: "Until the rice is digested." Ananda said: "How long will it take to digest?" Vimalakirti said: "The power of this rice will last seven days. After seven days it will be digested."

**13.** "Moreover, Ananda, if a monk who has not yet reached the final determination eats this rice, he will not digest it until he has reached the final determination. If one who has already reached the final determination eats it, he will not digest it until his mind is liberated."

**14.** "If anyone who has not yet produced the thought of supreme enlightenment eats this rice, he will not digest it until he has produced that thought."

**15.** The Buddha said: "Excellent! Excellent! The work of Vimalakirti is inconceivable. He can make a single room contain the whole universe. He can make a single meal lead beings to liberation. Such is the power of the Bodhisattva who dwells in inconceivable liberation."
