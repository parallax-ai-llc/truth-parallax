---
title: "Thought of Norea - Chapter 2"
scripture: "thought-of-norea"
chapter: 2
---

**1** It is Norea who entreats them for help. She entreats the Father of the All, whose dominion extends over the entirety, and whose power extends over every invisible and visible realm.

**2** He heard her entreaty, and the four Luminaries came to her, and they gave her guidance.

**3** They said to her, "Cry out to God the Father, the Mother of the All, for you are from the superior stock."

**4** And Norea cried out to the God of the All, saying, "Hear me, O God of the All, for I am the one who came forth from the All."

**5** She said, "It is I who first ascended to the holy place. It is I who received the power of the incorruptible generation."

**6** She said, "It is I who have been sown in the world through the will of the Father of the All."

**7** And the four Luminaries stood before her and said to her, "What is your request?"

**8** She said, "I ask that I may know the nature of my own being, in order that I may comprehend the truth."

**9** They said to her, "Surely you have come to know the truth from the beginning, and you seek the manner of the truth."

**10** She said, "I desire to know the power and the mystery of the All."

**11** And the four Luminaries spoke to her and revealed to her the mystery of her birth, and the mystery of the tree of knowledge.

**12** And they said, "The tree of knowledge is the power of God. From it came the insight of the pure light."

**13** And they said, "Norea, you are the daughter of the Father of the All. Your seed is the holy seed."

**14** And they said, "Your generation is from the imperishable light. No power will be able to attack your seed."

**15** And they said, "For your sake a voice came forth from the incorruptible aeon. Through it there arose the God of truth and justice, the Father of the All."

**16** They said, "You shall return to the light. You shall find your place of rest in the imperishable Aeon."

**17** And thus they instructed Norea, and she was placed in the rank of the five seals, and she received the power of the Father.

**18** And she was given authority over every invisible and visible creature. And she was made to rule over the holy seed.

**19** And through her, the holy seed was preserved, that it might be brought into the light of the imperishable Father.
