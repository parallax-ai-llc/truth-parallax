---
title: "Thought of Norea - Chapter 1"
scripture: "thought-of-norea"
chapter: 1
---

**1** Father of All, Ennoia of the Light, dwelling in the heights above the regions below, Light dwelling in the heights, Voice of Truth, upright Nous, untouchable Logos, and ineffable Voice, incomprehensible Father!

**2** It is Norea who cries out to them. They heard, and they received her into her place forever.

**3** They gave it to her in the Father of Nous, Adamas, as well as the voice of the Holy Ones, in order that she might rest in the ineffable Father, in order that she might regenerate herself in this generation, having found the holy seed which was sown in her.

**4** For Norea is she who came forth from the power of the imperishable, from the generation of the Autogenes.

**5** She is the one who went forth to become the helpmate for Adamas, the one who gave her the power of light.

**6** For Adam was a laughingstock, since he was made a counterfeit type by the Hebdomad, as if he had become stronger than I and my brothers.

**7** We are innocent; we have not sinned.

**8** The four holy helpers who are set over the four holy fires were established from the stock of the Great Seth: Gamaliel, Gabriel, Samblo, and Abrasax.

**9** They are the ones who are set over the four fires, and over the four elements: water and earth and fire and air.

**10** And they are the ones who guard holy Norea: Harmozel, Oroiael, Daveithe, and Eleleth.

**11** And the four Luminaries are the ones who watch over the holy seed of the great Seth.

**12** They are the ones from whom the living soul comes, and the life and the breath and the movement and the understanding and the desire and the power and the spirit that gives glory.
