## Overview

For Gnostic readers, the Thought of Norea is a compact but powerful hymn celebrating the divine feminine. Norea — daughter of Eve, sister-wife of Seth, and representative of the spiritual race — cries out to the Father of All when threatened by the archons, and receives immediate divine assistance through the four luminaries (Harmozel, Oroiael, Daveithai, Eleleth). The text affirms that the feminine figure has direct access to the highest God without male mediation, and that her cry is heard and answered. In a tradition often dominated by male revealer figures, Norea represents the irreducible feminine dimension of Sethian gnosis.
