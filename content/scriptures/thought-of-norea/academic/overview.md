## Overview

The Thought of Norea is studied for its place within the broader Norea/Noria tradition attested in multiple sources (the Hypostasis of the Archons, On the Origin of the World, Epiphanius's account of the Borborites). Scholars have debated Norea's identity and function: is she primarily the daughter of Eve, the wife of Noah (as in some Jewish legends), or a purely mythological figure? The text's brevity and hymnic character have led to discussions about whether it served a liturgical function in Sethian communities or was a literary composition meant for private meditation.
