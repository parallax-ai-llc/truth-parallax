---
title: "The Creation and the Flood"
scripture: "book-of-invasions"
chapter: 1
---

**1** In the beginning God created heaven and earth and the elements, and he made the light and the darkness, and the sea and the land.

**2** And God created Adam, the first man, from the clay of the earth, and from Adam came all the peoples of the world.

**3** From Adam to the Flood were two thousand two hundred and forty-two years. And the Flood came upon the earth because of the wickedness of men.

**4** Noah entered the Ark with his three sons: Shem, Ham, and Japheth. And from these three the whole earth was repeopled after the Flood.

**5** To Japheth, the eldest, was given the north of the world, and from his descendants came the peoples of Europe, and among them the Gaels, who would one day come to Ireland.

**6** The learned men of the Gaels trace their ancestry thus: from Japheth son of Noah, through Magog, through many generations, down to Mil Espaine, from whom the sons of Mil took their name.
