---
title: "The Coming of Nemed"
scripture: "book-of-invasions"
chapter: 4
---

**1** Nemed son of Agnomain came to Ireland from Scythia. He sailed with forty-four ships, but only one ship survived the journey, with Nemed and his four sons.

**2** During the time of Nemed, twelve plains were cleared and four lakes burst forth. They built two royal forts: Rath Ciombaoth and Rath Chinneich.

**3** The Fomorians oppressed the people of Nemed. Their kings Morc and Conand demanded a heavy tribute: two-thirds of their children, their corn, and their milk, to be delivered every Samhain.

**4** The Nemedians rose up against the Fomorians and attacked their tower on Tory Island. They slew Conand and many of his people, but Morc brought the sea upon them.

**5** In the great battle, nearly all the Nemedians perished. Only thirty survived in one ship. They scattered: one group went to Greece, one to the north of the world, and one to Britain.

**6** Those who went to Greece became the Fir Bolg. Those who went to the north became the Tuatha De Danann. They would return to Ireland in their own times.
