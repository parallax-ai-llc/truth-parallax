---
title: "The Coming of the Tuatha De Danann"
scripture: "book-of-invasions"
chapter: 6
---

**1** The Tuatha De Danann came to Ireland in dark clouds. They landed on the mountains of Connacht, and they brought a darkness over the sun for three days and three nights.

**2** They burned their ships so that they could not retreat. They demanded battle or kingship from the Fir Bolg.

**3** The two peoples met in the First Battle of Mag Tuired. The battle was fierce, and the Fir Bolg fought bravely. Eochaid their king was slain, and his champion Sreng cut off the hand of Nuada, king of the Tuatha De.

**4** Because Nuada had lost his hand, he could not be king, for no blemished man could rule. Bres, who was half-Fomorian, was made king in his place.

**5** But Bres was a tyrant. He taxed the Tuatha De heavily and reduced their chiefs to servitude. The Dagda was made to dig ditches, and Ogma to carry firewood.

**6** The physician Dian Cecht made a silver hand for Nuada, and later his son Miach grew a hand of flesh upon him. Nuada was restored as king, and Bres went to the Fomorians to raise an army.

**7** At the Second Battle of Mag Tuired, the Tuatha De Danann fought the Fomorians. Lugh of the Long Arm led them, and he slew Balor of the Evil Eye, his own grandfather, with a sling-stone through his eye. The Fomorians were driven into the sea forever.
