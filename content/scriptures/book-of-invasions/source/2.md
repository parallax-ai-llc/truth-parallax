---
title: "The Coming of Cessair"
scripture: "book-of-invasions"
chapter: 2
---

**1** The first taking of Ireland after the Flood was by Cessair daughter of Bith son of Noah. She came to Ireland forty days before the Flood.

**2** Noah had refused her a place in the Ark, and so she fled to the western edge of the world, to an island that had never known sin, hoping to escape the waters.

**3** She came with fifty women and three men: Bith, Ladra, and Fintan. They divided the women among them, but Ladra died, and then Bith died.

**4** The Flood overwhelmed them all, and only Fintan survived. He lived through many ages in the forms of a salmon, an eagle, and a hawk, and he remembered all the history of Ireland.

**5** Ireland lay empty after the Flood for three hundred and twelve years, until the next people came to take it.
