---
title: "Agni Purana - Chapter 7"
scripture: "agni-purana"
chapter: 7
---

**1** Agni said: I shall now describe the geography of the universe as revealed by the Supreme Lord.

**2** The earth is divided into seven continents, each surrounded by an ocean. The central continent is Jambudvipa, in the centre of which stands the golden mountain Meru.

**3** Mount Meru rises eighty-four thousand yojanas above the earth, and descends sixteen thousand yojanas below. Its summit is the abode of Brahma and the gods.

**4** Around Meru are arranged the four sub-continents. To the south lies Bharatavarsha, the land of action, where beings reap the fruits of their karma.

**5** Bharatavarsha alone among all the lands is the karma-bhumi, the land of action. Here alone can beings perform actions that lead to liberation. Even the gods desire to be born here.

**6** The seven oceans that surround the seven continents are made respectively of salt water, sugarcane juice, wine, clarified butter, curds, milk, and fresh water.

**7** Below the earth are the seven nether worlds: Atala, Vitala, Sutala, Rasatala, Talatala, Mahatala, and Patala. They are the abodes of the Danavas, the Daityas, and the Nagas.

**8** Above the earth are the seven upper worlds: Bhuloka, Bhuvarloka, Svarloka, Maharloka, Janaloka, Tapoloka, and Satyaloka. Each successively is more sublime than the one below.

**9** Satyaloka, the world of truth, is the highest of all. There Brahma dwells, and there the liberated souls who have attained the knowledge of Brahman reside in eternal bliss.

**10** Beyond all these worlds, beyond the manifested universe, is the Supreme Abode of Vishnu, called Vaikuntha, which is eternal, self-luminous, and imperishable. Those who reach it never return to the cycle of birth and death.
