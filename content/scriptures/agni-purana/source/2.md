---
title: "Agni Purana - Chapter 2"
scripture: "agni-purana"
chapter: 2
---

**1** Agni said: I shall now describe to you the incarnations of Vishnu, by which the lord protects the world in every age.

**2** In the form of the Fish, the Lord saved the Vedas from the great deluge. He guided Manu's ship through the raging waters and preserved the seeds of all creation.

**3** In the form of the Tortoise, the Lord bore upon His back the great mountain Mandara, while the gods and demons churned the ocean of milk for the nectar of immortality.

**4** In the form of the Boar, the Lord plunged into the cosmic waters and lifted the earth upon His tusks, rescuing her from the demon who had dragged her down.

**5** In the form of the Man-Lion, the Lord burst forth from the pillar to slay the demon Hiranyakashipu, who could not be killed by man or beast, by day or night, within or without.

**6** In the form of the Dwarf, the Lord approached the great king Bali and, with three strides, measured the entire universe, reclaiming the three worlds for the gods.

**7** In the form of Parashurama, the Lord, bearing the axe, rid the earth of tyrannical warriors and restored the order of righteousness.

**8** In the form of Rama, the Lord demonstrated the ideal of kingship, the sanctity of truth, and the path of dharma through His perfect life and conduct.

**9** In the form of Krishna, the Lord appeared in His full divine nature, spoke the Bhagavad Gita, and established the eternal principles of devotion, knowledge, and action.

**10** In the form of Buddha, the Lord appeared to teach compassion and non-violence to all living beings, turning them away from harmful rituals and toward the path of mercy.

**11** At the end of the Kali age, the Lord shall appear as Kalki, riding a white horse, bearing a blazing sword, to destroy the wicked and restore the age of truth.

**12** He who hears of these incarnations with faith and devotion is freed from all sins and attains the supreme abode of Vishnu.
