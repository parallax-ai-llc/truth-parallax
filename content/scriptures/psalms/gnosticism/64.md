## The Archons' Schemes Unraveled

Psalm 64's depiction of enemies plotting secretly and shooting arrows from ambush (vv. 2-4) is read in Gnostic exegesis as describing the archons' primary strategy: deception. The *Hypostasis of the Archons* (NHC II,4) narrates how the cosmic rulers operate through concealment and misdirection, keeping the divine sparks ignorant of their true origin. The secret plots are the systems of ignorance — false religions, material desires, social hierarchies — that the archons have constructed to prevent the pneumatic soul from awakening to *gnosis*.

The divine reversal — "God shoots his arrow at them... their own tongues are turned against them" (vv. 7-8) — describes the moment when *gnosis* penetrates the archontic deception and the entire system collapses upon itself. The *Second Treatise of the Great Seth* narrates how the archons' attempt to crucify Christ — their ultimate weapon — became the very instrument of their defeat, as Christ's death and descent into the underworld liberated the imprisoned sparks. The archons' weapons always ultimately backfire, because deception cannot withstand the force of truth.

The psalm's conclusion — "all mankind fears; they tell what God has brought about" (v. 9) — is read as the universal recognition that follows the revelation of *gnosis*. Even the psychics and hylics, who cannot fully comprehend the pneumatic truth, will witness the archons' downfall and recognize that a power greater than the cosmic rulers has intervened. The "righteous one" who "rejoices in the LORD" (v. 10) is the pneumatic who has already attained this recognition and whose joy is the unshakeable confidence that the true God's victory over the archontic system is certain and final.
