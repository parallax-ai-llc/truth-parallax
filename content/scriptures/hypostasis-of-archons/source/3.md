---
title: "Hypostasis of the Archons - Chapter 3"
scripture: "hypostasis-of-archons"
chapter: 3
---

**1** Then Eleleth, the great angel, spoke to me. "It is I," he said, "who am understanding. I am one of the four light-givers, who stand in the presence of the great invisible spirit."

**2** "Do you think these rulers have any power over you? None of them can prevail against the root of truth; for on its account he appeared in the final ages, and these authorities will be restrained. And these authorities cannot defile you and that generation; for your abode is in incorruptibility, where the virgin spirit dwells, who is superior to the authorities of chaos and to their universe."

**3** But I said, "Sir, how long will the authorities rule over the earth?" He said, "Until the true man, within a modeled form, reveals the existence of the spirit of truth, which the father has sent."

**4** "Then he will teach them about everything, and he will anoint them with the unction of the eternal life, given him from the undominated generation."

**5** "Then they will be freed from blind thought, and they will trample underfoot death, which is of the authorities, and they will ascend into the limitless light where this sown element belongs."

**6** "Then the authorities will relinquish their ages, and their angels will weep over their destruction, and their demons will lament their death."

**7** "Then all the children of the light will be truly acquainted with the truth and their root, and the father of the entirety and the holy spirit. They will all say with a single voice, 'The father's truth is just, and the son presides over the entirety,' and from everyone unto the ages of ages, 'Holy — holy — holy! Amen!'"

**8** The reality of the authorities. As it is written, these are the words of the great apostle, inspired by the spirit of the father of truth, about the authorities of the darkness.

**9** This is the end of the treatise. The grace of the great invisible spirit be with you always. Amen.

**10** The Hypostasis of the Archons.
