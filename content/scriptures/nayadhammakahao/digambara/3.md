## The Tale of Dhanya

The Digambara tradition reads this the futility of worldly attachment with characteristic doctrinal strictness, emphasizing the necessity of complete renunciation and the precise application of Jain principles. Digambara interpreters stress that the futility of worldly attachment must be understood within the framework of absolute non-attachment and rigorous ascetic practice that defines the Digambara path.
