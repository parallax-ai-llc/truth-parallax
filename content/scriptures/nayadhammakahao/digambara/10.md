## The Tale of Liberation

From the Digambara perspective, this teaching on achieving liberation through right conduct reinforces the tradition's emphasis on uncompromising spiritual discipline. The Digambara commentarial tradition elaborates these teachings with philosophical precision, connecting them to the broader framework of strict karma theory and the path of total renunciation.
