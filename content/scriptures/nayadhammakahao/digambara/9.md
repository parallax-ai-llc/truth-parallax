## The Tale of the Ascetic

The Digambara tradition reads this endurance and spiritual victory of the ascetic with characteristic doctrinal strictness, emphasizing the necessity of complete renunciation and the precise application of Jain principles. Digambara interpreters stress that endurance and spiritual victory of the ascetic must be understood within the framework of absolute non-attachment and rigorous ascetic practice that defines the Digambara path.
