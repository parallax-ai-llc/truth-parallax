## The Tale of Prince Meghakumara

The Digambara tradition reads this Prince Meghakumara's renunciation of royal life with characteristic doctrinal strictness, emphasizing the necessity of complete renunciation and the precise application of Jain principles. Digambara interpreters stress that Prince Meghakumara's renunciation of royal life must be understood within the framework of absolute non-attachment and rigorous ascetic practice that defines the Digambara path.
