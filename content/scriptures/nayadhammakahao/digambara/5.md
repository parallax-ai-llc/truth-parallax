## The Tale of the Monk

The Digambara tradition reads this the power of monastic austerity and discipline with characteristic doctrinal strictness, emphasizing the necessity of complete renunciation and the precise application of Jain principles. Digambara interpreters stress that the power of monastic austerity and discipline must be understood within the framework of absolute non-attachment and rigorous ascetic practice that defines the Digambara path.
