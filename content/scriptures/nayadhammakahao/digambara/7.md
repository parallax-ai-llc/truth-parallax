## The Tale of Compassion

The Digambara tradition reads this non-violence and compassion in action with characteristic doctrinal strictness, emphasizing the necessity of complete renunciation and the precise application of Jain principles. Digambara interpreters stress that non-violence and compassion in action must be understood within the framework of absolute non-attachment and rigorous ascetic practice that defines the Digambara path.
