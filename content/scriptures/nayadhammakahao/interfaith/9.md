## The Tale of the Ascetic

Interfaith interpretation finds in this teaching on endurance and spiritual victory of the ascetic resonances with the concerns of multiple religious traditions. The text addresses universal human questions about meaning, morality, and liberation that transcend sectarian boundaries and invite constructive dialogue.
