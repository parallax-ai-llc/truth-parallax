## The Tale of Compassion

Interfaith interpretation finds in this teaching on non-violence and compassion in action resonances with the concerns of multiple religious traditions. The text addresses universal human questions about meaning, morality, and liberation that transcend sectarian boundaries and invite constructive dialogue.
