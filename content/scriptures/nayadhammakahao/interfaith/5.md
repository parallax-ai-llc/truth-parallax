## The Tale of the Monk

Interfaith interpretation finds in this teaching on the power of monastic austerity and discipline resonances with the concerns of multiple religious traditions. The text addresses universal human questions about meaning, morality, and liberation that transcend sectarian boundaries and invite constructive dialogue.
