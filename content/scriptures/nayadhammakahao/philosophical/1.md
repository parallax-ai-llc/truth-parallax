## The Tale of Prince Meghakumara

Philosophical interpretation examines this teaching on Prince Meghakumara's renunciation of royal life as a contribution to systematic philosophy. The text raises fundamental questions about the nature of reality, knowledge, and ethics that remain relevant to contemporary philosophical discussion.
