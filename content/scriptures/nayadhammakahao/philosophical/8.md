## The Tale of the King

From a philosophical standpoint, this section on the impermanence of royal power and wealth presents arguments and positions that engage with perennial questions in metaphysics, epistemology, and moral philosophy. The Jain approach offers distinctive solutions to problems debated across philosophical traditions.
