## The Tale of the Merchant

From a philosophical standpoint, this section on karmic consequences in the life of a merchant presents arguments and positions that engage with perennial questions in metaphysics, epistemology, and moral philosophy. The Jain approach offers distinctive solutions to problems debated across philosophical traditions.
