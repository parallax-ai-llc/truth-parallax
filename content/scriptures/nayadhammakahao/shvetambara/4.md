## The Tale of the Merchant

Shvetambara scholars read this section on karmic consequences in the life of a merchant as authoritative canonical teaching that guides both monastic and lay practice. The tradition's inclusive approach ensures that these teachings remain relevant and applicable to the entire Jain community.
