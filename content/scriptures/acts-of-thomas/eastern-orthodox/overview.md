## Overview

The Eastern Orthodox encounter with the Acts of Thomas recognizes the text's importance for the Syriac Christian tradition that contributed significantly to Orthodox theology and liturgy. The sacramental descriptions preserve early liturgical forms, and the Thomas tradition connects to the living Christian communities of India that trace their origin to the apostle. The text's mystical theology resonates with Orthodox spiritual tradition.
