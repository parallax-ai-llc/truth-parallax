## Overview

The Catholic reading of the Acts of Thomas values the text for its elaborate sacramental theology — its detailed descriptions of baptism with anointing, the eucharistic prayer, and the seal of initiation. The missionary narrative resonates with Catholic missionary tradition, and Thomas's martyrdom exemplifies the apostolic model of witness unto death. The Indian Thomas tradition remains significant for the Saint Thomas Christians of Kerala.
