## Overview

The Academic study of the Acts of Thomas examines the text's composition in early third-century Syriac Christianity, its encratite theology, the literary genre of apocryphal Acts, and the relationship between the Syriac and Greek recensions. Scholars investigate the Hymn of the Pearl's Gnostic or proto-Gnostic affinities, the historical kernel behind the Indian mission tradition, and the text's evidence for early Syriac baptismal and eucharistic practices.
