---
title: "Acts of Thomas — Act 8: The Wild Asses"
scripture: "acts-of-thomas"
chapter: 8
---

**1** And as the apostle went on his way, there came a herd of wild asses feeding in the plain. And the apostle called one of them and said to it: Of you that are of this herd, let one come and carry me until I enter the city.

**2** And one of the wild asses came willingly and stood before him, and the apostle mounted upon it and rode, and the others followed after.

**3** And when they had gone a little way, the apostle dismissed the other asses and said to them: Go to your pastures. And they departed quietly till they were no longer seen.

**4** And as Thomas rode upon the colt and drew near to the city gates, the colt said unto him with a human voice: Apostle of the Most High, twin brother of Christ, thou that art privy to the hidden word, thou that receivest the secret oracles of him that is the truth — thou art he that worketh great wonders by the will of him that sent thee.

**5** And when they entered the city, the people marvelled, seeing the wild ass bearing the apostle, and they came together from every part to see this wonder.

**6** And the apostle descended from the colt and said: This grace hath been given unto the beasts, who by nature are without speech, because of your unbelief; for had ye believed, not the dumb beasts but the very stones would have spoken and proclaimed the glory of God.

**7** And the apostle laid his hands upon the sick that were gathered, and healed them every one. And the colt, when the apostle had descended from it, fell down at his feet and died.

**8** And the apostle was grieved and said: Lord Jesus, the strength of all creatures — why hast thou suffered this colt to die, which bore me faithfully? And a voice came from heaven, saying: Grieve not, Thomas; this colt hath fulfilled his service and shall rest. His obedience hath been written in the heavens.

**9** And the multitude believed because of the wonder of the wild asses, and many were added to the faith.
