## Maha Vagga (I)

The Theravada tradition regards this section on the Mahaparinibbana and Mahanidana Suttas on the Buddha's final days and dependent origination as essential canonical teaching. Theravada interpreters emphasize its direct relevance to meditation practice and the cultivation of wisdom, reading it as part of the complete path from morality through concentration to liberating insight.
