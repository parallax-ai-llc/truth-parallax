## Maha Vagga (II)

In the Theravada tradition, this teaching on the Sakkapanha Sutta's divine questions and the Mahasatipatthana's foundations of mindfulness holds a position of central importance for both doctrinal understanding and practical application. Monks and scholars study this section as part of the comprehensive training in the Buddha's teaching, connecting its insights to the daily practice of mindfulness and ethical conduct.
