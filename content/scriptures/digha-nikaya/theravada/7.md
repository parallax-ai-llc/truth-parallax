## Patika Vagga (II)

The Theravada tradition regards this section on the Cakkavatti Sutta on the ideal ruler and the Sigalovada on lay ethics as essential canonical teaching. Theravada interpreters emphasize its direct relevance to meditation practice and the cultivation of wisdom, reading it as part of the complete path from morality through concentration to liberating insight.
