## Maha Vagga (III)

The Theravada tradition regards this section on Buddhist cosmology and the Agganna Sutta's account of social origins as essential canonical teaching. Theravada interpreters emphasize its direct relevance to meditation practice and the cultivation of wisdom, reading it as part of the complete path from morality through concentration to liberating insight.
