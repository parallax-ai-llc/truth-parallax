## Additional Discourses (III)

In the Theravada tradition, this teaching on the final collection of long discourses and their synthetic teachings holds a position of central importance for both doctrinal understanding and practical application. Monks and scholars study this section as part of the comprehensive training in the Buddha's teaching, connecting its insights to the daily practice of mindfulness and ethical conduct.
