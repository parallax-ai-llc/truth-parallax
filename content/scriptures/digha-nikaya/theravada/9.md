## Additional Discourses (II)

The Theravada tradition regards this section on philosophical debates with Brahmins and other religious teachers as essential canonical teaching. Theravada interpreters emphasize its direct relevance to meditation practice and the cultivation of wisdom, reading it as part of the complete path from morality through concentration to liberating insight.
