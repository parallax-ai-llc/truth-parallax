## Silakkhandha Vagga (II)

In the Theravada tradition, this teaching on the gradual training from morality through concentration to wisdom holds a position of central importance for both doctrinal understanding and practical application. Monks and scholars study this section as part of the comprehensive training in the Buddha's teaching, connecting its insights to the daily practice of mindfulness and ethical conduct.
