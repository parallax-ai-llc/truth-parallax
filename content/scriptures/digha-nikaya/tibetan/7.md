## Patika Vagga (II)

Tibetan Buddhism engages with this teaching on the Cakkavatti Sutta on the ideal ruler and the Sigalovada on lay ethics as part of the foundational sutra vehicle upon which the complete Tibetan Buddhist path is constructed. These teachings inform the analytical meditation practices and the stages-of-the-path (lam-rim) literature that structures Tibetan Buddhist education.
