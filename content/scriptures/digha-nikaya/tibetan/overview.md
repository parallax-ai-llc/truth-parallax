## Overview

Tibetan Buddhism engages with the Digha Nikaya's teachings through Mulasarvastivada parallels preserved in the Tibetan Kangyur and through the broader framework of Buddhist scholastic study. The collection's cosmological and doctrinal discourses provide foundational material that Tibetan Buddhist philosophy builds upon.

## Key Themes

Tibetan scholars value the Digha Nikaya's systematic cosmological teachings, which provide the basis for the Abhidharma cosmology studied in Tibetan monastic curricula. The collection's discourses on meditation stages inform the preliminary practices (ngondro) that precede advanced tantric training.

## Relevance to Tibetan Study

In the Tibetan scholastic tradition, the early discourses provide the foundational understanding of Buddhist doctrine upon which Madhyamaka and Yogachara philosophy are constructed.
