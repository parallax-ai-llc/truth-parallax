## Silakkhandha Vagga (I)

Tibetan Buddhism engages with this teaching on the Brahmajala Sutta's net of views and the foundations of moral conduct as part of the foundational sutra vehicle upon which the complete Tibetan Buddhist path is constructed. These teachings inform the analytical meditation practices and the stages-of-the-path (lam-rim) literature that structures Tibetan Buddhist education.
