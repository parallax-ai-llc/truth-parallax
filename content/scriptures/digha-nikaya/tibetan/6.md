## Patika Vagga (I)

In the Tibetan scholastic tradition, this section on the Patika and Udumbarika Suttas on asceticism and debate provides essential doctrinal foundations studied in the monastic curriculum. Tibetan scholars connect these teachings to the broader framework of Buddhist philosophy, from Abhidharma analysis through Madhyamaka and Yogachara to tantric practice.
