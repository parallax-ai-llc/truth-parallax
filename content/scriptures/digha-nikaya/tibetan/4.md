## Maha Vagga (II)

In the Tibetan scholastic tradition, this section on the Sakkapanha Sutta's divine questions and the Mahasatipatthana's foundations of mindfulness provides essential doctrinal foundations studied in the monastic curriculum. Tibetan scholars connect these teachings to the broader framework of Buddhist philosophy, from Abhidharma analysis through Madhyamaka and Yogachara to tantric practice.
