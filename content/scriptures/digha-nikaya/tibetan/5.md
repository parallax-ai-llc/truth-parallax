## Maha Vagga (III)

Tibetan Buddhism engages with this teaching on Buddhist cosmology and the Agganna Sutta's account of social origins as part of the foundational sutra vehicle upon which the complete Tibetan Buddhist path is constructed. These teachings inform the analytical meditation practices and the stages-of-the-path (lam-rim) literature that structures Tibetan Buddhist education.
