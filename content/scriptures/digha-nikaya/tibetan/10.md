## Additional Discourses (III)

In the Tibetan scholastic tradition, this section on the final collection of long discourses and their synthetic teachings provides essential doctrinal foundations studied in the monastic curriculum. Tibetan scholars connect these teachings to the broader framework of Buddhist philosophy, from Abhidharma analysis through Madhyamaka and Yogachara to tantric practice.
