## Additional Discourses (II)

Academic scholars analyze this section on philosophical debates with Brahmins and other religious teachers using the methods of textual criticism, historical contextualization, and comparative study. The text provides evidence for understanding the development of early Buddhist thought and its relationship to the broader intellectual culture of ancient India.
