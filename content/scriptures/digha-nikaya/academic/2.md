## Silakkhandha Vagga (II)

From an academic perspective, this teaching on the gradual training from morality through concentration to wisdom is examined for its compositional history, its relationship to parallel versions in other Buddhist canons, and its significance for reconstructing the teachings of the historical Buddha.
