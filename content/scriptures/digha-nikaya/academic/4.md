## Maha Vagga (II)

From an academic perspective, this teaching on the Sakkapanha Sutta's divine questions and the Mahasatipatthana's foundations of mindfulness is examined for its compositional history, its relationship to parallel versions in other Buddhist canons, and its significance for reconstructing the teachings of the historical Buddha.
