## Maha Vagga (III)

Academic scholars analyze this section on Buddhist cosmology and the Agganna Sutta's account of social origins using the methods of textual criticism, historical contextualization, and comparative study. The text provides evidence for understanding the development of early Buddhist thought and its relationship to the broader intellectual culture of ancient India.
