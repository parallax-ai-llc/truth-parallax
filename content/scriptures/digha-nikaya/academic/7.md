## Patika Vagga (II)

Academic scholars analyze this section on the Cakkavatti Sutta on the ideal ruler and the Sigalovada on lay ethics using the methods of textual criticism, historical contextualization, and comparative study. The text provides evidence for understanding the development of early Buddhist thought and its relationship to the broader intellectual culture of ancient India.
