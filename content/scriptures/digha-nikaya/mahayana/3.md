## Maha Vagga (I)

Mahayana Buddhism engages with this teaching on the Mahaparinibbana and Mahanidana Suttas on the Buddha's final days and dependent origination through its parallel Agama traditions and recognizes it as foundational material upon which later Mahayana developments build. The emphasis on compassion, wisdom, and the welfare of all beings connects these early teachings to the bodhisattva ideal.
