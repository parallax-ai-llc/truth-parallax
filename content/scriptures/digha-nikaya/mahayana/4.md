## Maha Vagga (II)

From the Mahayana perspective, this section on the Sakkapanha Sutta's divine questions and the Mahasatipatthana's foundations of mindfulness preserves important foundational teachings that the Mahayana tradition inherits and develops. Mahayana scholars identify in these early discourses the seeds of later philosophical developments in Madhyamaka and Yogachara thought.
