## Additional Discourses (I)

From the Mahayana perspective, this section on various longer dialogues with Brahmin interlocutors preserves important foundational teachings that the Mahayana tradition inherits and develops. Mahayana scholars identify in these early discourses the seeds of later philosophical developments in Madhyamaka and Yogachara thought.
