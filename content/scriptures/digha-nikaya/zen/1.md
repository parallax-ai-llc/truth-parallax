## Silakkhandha Vagga (I)

Zen Buddhism finds in this teaching on the Brahmajala Sutta's net of views and the foundations of moral conduct a directness and practicality that resonates with Zen values. While Zen emphasizes experience beyond textual study, these teachings provide foundations for the mindful awareness and direct insight that are central to Zen practice.
