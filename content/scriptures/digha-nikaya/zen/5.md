## Maha Vagga (III)

Zen Buddhism finds in this teaching on Buddhist cosmology and the Agganna Sutta's account of social origins a directness and practicality that resonates with Zen values. While Zen emphasizes experience beyond textual study, these teachings provide foundations for the mindful awareness and direct insight that are central to Zen practice.
