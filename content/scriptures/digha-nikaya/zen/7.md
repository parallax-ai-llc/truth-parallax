## Patika Vagga (II)

Zen Buddhism finds in this teaching on the Cakkavatti Sutta on the ideal ruler and the Sigalovada on lay ethics a directness and practicality that resonates with Zen values. While Zen emphasizes experience beyond textual study, these teachings provide foundations for the mindful awareness and direct insight that are central to Zen practice.
