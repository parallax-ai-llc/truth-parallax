## Maha Vagga (I)

Zen Buddhism finds in this teaching on the Mahaparinibbana and Mahanidana Suttas on the Buddha's final days and dependent origination a directness and practicality that resonates with Zen values. While Zen emphasizes experience beyond textual study, these teachings provide foundations for the mindful awareness and direct insight that are central to Zen practice.
