## Additional Discourses (II)

Zen Buddhism finds in this teaching on philosophical debates with Brahmins and other religious teachers a directness and practicality that resonates with Zen values. While Zen emphasizes experience beyond textual study, these teachings provide foundations for the mindful awareness and direct insight that are central to Zen practice.
