## Maha Vagga (I)

Comparative interpretation of this teaching on the Mahaparinibbana and Mahanidana Suttas on the Buddha's final days and dependent origination reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar teachings in Hindu, Jain, Christian, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Buddhist perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
