## Additional Discourses (II)

Comparative interpretation of this teaching on philosophical debates with Brahmins and other religious teachers reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar teachings in Hindu, Jain, Christian, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Buddhist perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
