## Patika Vagga (II)

Comparative interpretation of this teaching on the Cakkavatti Sutta on the ideal ruler and the Sigalovada on lay ethics reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar teachings in Hindu, Jain, Christian, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Buddhist perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
