## Silakkhandha Vagga (II)

This section on the gradual training from morality through concentration to wisdom provides rich material for cross-traditional analysis. Comparative scholars examine how the Buddhist approach to these questions relates to and differs from the approaches of other major religious and philosophical traditions.
