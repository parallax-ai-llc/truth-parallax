## Patika Vagga (I)

This section on the Patika and Udumbarika Suttas on asceticism and debate provides rich material for cross-traditional analysis. Comparative scholars examine how the Buddhist approach to these questions relates to and differs from the approaches of other major religious and philosophical traditions.
