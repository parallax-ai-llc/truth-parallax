## Maha Vagga (III)

Comparative interpretation of this teaching on Buddhist cosmology and the Agganna Sutta's account of social origins reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar teachings in Hindu, Jain, Christian, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Buddhist perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
