## Maha Vagga (II)

This section on the Sakkapanha Sutta's divine questions and the Mahasatipatthana's foundations of mindfulness provides rich material for cross-traditional analysis. Comparative scholars examine how the Buddhist approach to these questions relates to and differs from the approaches of other major religious and philosophical traditions.
