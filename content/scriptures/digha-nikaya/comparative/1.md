## Silakkhandha Vagga (I)

Comparative interpretation of this teaching on the Brahmajala Sutta's net of views and the foundations of moral conduct reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar teachings in Hindu, Jain, Christian, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Buddhist perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
