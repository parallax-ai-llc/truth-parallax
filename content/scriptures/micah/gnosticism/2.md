## The Archons' Exploitation

Gnostic interpretation reads the oppressors of Micah 2 as representatives of the archontic powers — the rulers of the material world who "covet fields and seize them." Their systematic dispossession of the poor mirrors the archons' systematic exploitation of the divine sparks trapped in matter. The material world is structured for extraction: the powerful consume the weak, and this is not an aberration but the fundamental design of the Demiurge's cosmos.

## The Silencing of Gnosis

The demand to "stop preaching" is read as the archons' attempt to suppress *gnosis* — the liberating knowledge that threatens their control. Every system of material power has a vested interest in preventing the truth from being spoken. The false prophets who prophesy "wine and strong drink" represent counterfeit spiritual teaching that keeps the soul intoxicated and docile within the material prison.

## The Pneumatic Remnant

The gathering of the remnant (2:12-13) is read as the preservation of the *pneumatic* elect — those who possess the divine spark and who will eventually be gathered out of the material cosmos into the Pleroma.
