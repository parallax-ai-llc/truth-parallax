## The Archontic Rulers Exposed

Gnostic interpretation of Micah 3 reads the corrupt rulers, prophets, and priests as manifestations of the archontic powers. The judges who "abhor justice and pervert all equity" (3:9) are the Demiurge's agents, maintaining a system designed for exploitation. The prophets who prophesy for money are purveyors of false knowledge — *pseudognosis* — that keeps the soul bound to the material order by offering counterfeit spiritual comfort.

## True Gnosis vs. False Teaching

Micah's Spirit-empowered declaration (3:8) is contrasted with the hired prophets' false pronouncements. The Gnostic reading identifies Micah as a bearer of genuine *gnosis* — knowledge that disrupts the archontic system and exposes its corruption. True spiritual knowledge is always dangerous to the established order because it reveals the prison for what it is.

## The Destruction of the Temple

The prophecy that Zion will become "a heap of ruins" (3:12) is read with approval: the destruction of the material temple represents the dissolution of the archontic worship center. The Gnostic tradition sees in this not tragedy but liberation — the breaking of the Demiurge's most powerful symbol of control over the religious imagination.
