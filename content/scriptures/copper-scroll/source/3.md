---
title: "Copper Scroll Section 3"
scripture: "copper-scroll"
chapter: 3
---

**5:1** In the reservoir which is in Beth-Kerem, as you enter, on the left: vessels of silver, and gold bars.

**5:4** In the Hoard next to the water channel, on the southern side, at the second level going down from the top: nine talents.

**5:6** In the cistern of the esplanade which is on the east, in the northern section: chests of silver and their treasure, being seven talents.

**5:9** In the outer valley, at the stone in the middle of the sheepcote: seventeen talents.

**5:12** At the monument at the head of the ravine of Beth Tamar, at the mouth of the outlet: bars of silver.

**6:1** In the aqueduct of the northern reservoir having four sides, measured out on its four sides: twenty-two talents.

**6:3** In the cave next to the cold-chamber belonging to the house of Ahub: six silver bars.

**6:6** At Dok, under the eastern corner of the guardhouse: gold, and sixty talents of silver.

**6:9** In the cave of the pillar having two entrances facing east, at the northern entrance: three cubits in from the entrance, there is a pitcher. In it is one scroll. Under it: forty-two talents.

**6:12** In the cave at the base of the crag facing east: vessels of the sons of Aaron and sacred scrolls.

**6:14** Total: approximately four thousand six hundred talents of silver, and approximately one thousand three hundred talents of gold, plus vessels, sacred scrolls, and vestments beyond counting.
