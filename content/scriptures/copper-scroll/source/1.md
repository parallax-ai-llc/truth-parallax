---
title: "Copper Scroll Section 1"
scripture: "copper-scroll"
chapter: 1
---

**1:1** In the ruin which is in the Valley of Achor, under the steps leading to the east, forty long cubits: a chest of silver and its vessels with a weight of seventeen talents.

**1:3** In the sepulchral monument, in the third course of stones: 100 bars of gold.

**1:5** In the big cistern which is in the court of the peristyle, in a recess at the bottom of it, hidden in a hole in front of the upper opening: nine hundred talents.

**1:7** In the hill of Kohlit: vessels of tribute of the Master of the Peoples, and sacred vestments. Total of the tribute and of the seventh treasure: a second tithe rendered unclean. Its opening lies on the edge of the canal on its northern side, six cubits before the immersion pool.

**1:9** In the plastered cistern of Manos, going down to the left, at a height of three cubits from the bottom: forty talents of silver.

**1:11** In the cistern which is below the rampart, on the east side, in a place carved out of the rock: six hundred bars of silver.

**2:1** In the cistern which is under the wall on the east side, in the tooth of the rock: six hundred bars of silver and gold.

**2:3** In the underground passage which is in Secacah, to the north of Kohlit, which opens towards the north with graves at its mouth: a copy of this document with an explanation and its measurements and an inventory of each thing and other things.
