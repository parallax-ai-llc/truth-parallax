---
title: "Copper Scroll Section 2"
scripture: "copper-scroll"
chapter: 2
---

**3:1** In the gutter which is at the bottom of the water tank: seventy-one talents of silver, and vessels of precious wood.

**3:4** In the pit next to the overflowing well: a purse of holiness, and one ephah of sacred wood and vestments of holiness.

**3:6** In the upper opening of the rocky slope, facing west, opposite the garden of Zadok, under the great stone at the head of the water channel: a sealed vessel with one book inside.

**3:9** In the tomb of the sons of the Third Great Settlement: vessels of silver and the garments of the Temple.

**4:1** In the cave of the old Washer's Chamber, on the third terrace: sixty-five bars of gold.

**4:4** In the underground chamber which faces east, at the northern entrance: chests of tribute of the house of tribute, and vessels of tribute, all of them of silver.

**4:6** In the vault which is at the mouth of the Secacah pool, when you go down from the north side: in a hole sealed with plaster there are four talents.

**4:9** In the cavity of the smooth rock north of Kohlit, whose opening faces north with tombs at its mouth: a copy of this document.

**4:12** In the irrigation ditch of the vineyard of the estate of the great cedar: vessels of tribute of the lord of the people, consecrated vessels, and tithes.
