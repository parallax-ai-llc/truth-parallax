## Final Discourses

The Theravada tradition regards this section on the final discourses of the collection and their synthetic reflections on the path as essential canonical teaching. Theravada interpreters emphasize its direct relevance to meditation practice and the cultivation of wisdom, reading it as part of the complete path from morality through concentration to liberating insight.
