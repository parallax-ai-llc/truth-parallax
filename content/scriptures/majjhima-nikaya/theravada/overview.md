## Overview

The Majjhima Nikaya is regarded in the Theravada tradition as the single most important collection for understanding the complete path of practice. Its 152 suttas provide detailed instructions for meditation, comprehensive doctrinal teachings, and practical guidance for both monastic and lay practitioners.

## Key Themes

Theravada interpreters treasure the Majjhima Nikaya for its balanced combination of doctrine and practice. The Satipatthana Sutta (MN 10) is considered the foundational text for vipassana meditation. The Sammaditthi Sutta (MN 9) provides the definitive analysis of right view. The Anapanasati Sutta (MN 118) gives complete instructions for mindfulness of breathing.

## Practice and Study

In Theravada monasteries across Southeast Asia, the Majjhima Nikaya forms the core curriculum for doctrinal study and meditation practice. Its suttas are frequently cited in meditation retreats and Dhamma talks.
