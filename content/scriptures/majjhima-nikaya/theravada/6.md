## Discourses to Householders

In the Theravada tradition, this teaching on teachings addressed to householders on ethical conduct and spiritual practice in daily life holds a position of central importance for both doctrinal understanding and practical application. Monks and scholars study this section as part of the comprehensive training in the Buddha's teaching, connecting its insights to the daily practice of mindfulness and ethical conduct.
