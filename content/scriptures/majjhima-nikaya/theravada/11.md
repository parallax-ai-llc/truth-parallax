## Aggregates and Elements

The Theravada tradition regards this section on analysis of the five aggregates and the elements of experience as essential canonical teaching. Theravada interpreters emphasize its direct relevance to meditation practice and the cultivation of wisdom, reading it as part of the complete path from morality through concentration to liberating insight.
