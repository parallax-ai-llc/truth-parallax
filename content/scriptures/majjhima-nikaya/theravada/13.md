## Action and Rebirth

The Theravada tradition regards this section on the analysis of intentional action, its consequences, and the mechanics of rebirth as essential canonical teaching. Theravada interpreters emphasize its direct relevance to meditation practice and the cultivation of wisdom, reading it as part of the complete path from morality through concentration to liberating insight.
