## Final Discourses

Academic scholars analyze this section on the final discourses of the collection and their synthetic reflections on the path using the methods of textual criticism, historical contextualization, and comparative study. The text provides evidence for understanding the development of early Buddhist thought and its relationship to the broader intellectual culture of ancient India.
