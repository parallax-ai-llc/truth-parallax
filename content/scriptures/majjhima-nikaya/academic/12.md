## Emptiness and Non-Self

From an academic perspective, this teaching on the teachings on emptiness, non-self, and the wisdom that leads to liberation is examined for its compositional history, its relationship to parallel versions in other Buddhist canons, and its significance for reconstructing the teachings of the historical Buddha.
