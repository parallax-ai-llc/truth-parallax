## Destruction of Craving

Academic scholars analyze this section on the destruction of craving as the essential goal of Buddhist practice using the methods of textual criticism, historical contextualization, and comparative study. The text provides evidence for understanding the development of early Buddhist thought and its relationship to the broader intellectual culture of ancient India.
