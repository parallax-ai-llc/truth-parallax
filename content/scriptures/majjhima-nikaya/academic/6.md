## Discourses to Householders

From an academic perspective, this teaching on teachings addressed to householders on ethical conduct and spiritual practice in daily life is examined for its compositional history, its relationship to parallel versions in other Buddhist canons, and its significance for reconstructing the teachings of the historical Buddha.
