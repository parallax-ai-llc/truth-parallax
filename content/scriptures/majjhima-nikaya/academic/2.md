## Similes and Foundations

From an academic perspective, this teaching on foundational teachings using the similes of the cloth, the heartwood, and other vivid metaphors is examined for its compositional history, its relationship to parallel versions in other Buddhist canons, and its significance for reconstructing the teachings of the historical Buddha.
