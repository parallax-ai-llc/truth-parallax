## Monks and Meditation

Academic scholars analyze this section on teachings on monastic conduct, meditation practice, and the cultivation of concentration using the methods of textual criticism, historical contextualization, and comparative study. The text provides evidence for understanding the development of early Buddhist thought and its relationship to the broader intellectual culture of ancient India.
