## Root Discourses on Right View

Academic scholars analyze this section on right view as the foundation of the entire Buddhist path using the methods of textual criticism, historical contextualization, and comparative study. The text provides evidence for understanding the development of early Buddhist thought and its relationship to the broader intellectual culture of ancient India.
