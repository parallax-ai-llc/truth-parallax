## Kings and Brahmins

Academic scholars analyze this section on encounters with kings and Brahmins on dharma, governance, and truth using the methods of textual criticism, historical contextualization, and comparative study. The text provides evidence for understanding the development of early Buddhist thought and its relationship to the broader intellectual culture of ancient India.
