## Lion's Roar Discourses

Academic scholars analyze this section on the lion's roar discourses proclaiming the distinctive excellence of the Buddha's teaching using the methods of textual criticism, historical contextualization, and comparative study. The text provides evidence for understanding the development of early Buddhist thought and its relationship to the broader intellectual culture of ancient India.
