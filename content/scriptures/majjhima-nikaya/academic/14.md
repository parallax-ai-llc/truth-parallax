## Stages of Meditation

From an academic perspective, this teaching on detailed descriptions of the stages of meditation from access to the formless attainments is examined for its compositional history, its relationship to parallel versions in other Buddhist canons, and its significance for reconstructing the teachings of the historical Buddha.
