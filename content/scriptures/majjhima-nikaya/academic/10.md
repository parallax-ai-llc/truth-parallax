## Advanced Meditation

From an academic perspective, this teaching on advanced teachings on meditation, the jhanas, and the development of insight is examined for its compositional history, its relationship to parallel versions in other Buddhist canons, and its significance for reconstructing the teachings of the historical Buddha.
