## Paired Discourses

From an academic perspective, this teaching on paired discourses analyzing various doctrinal topics from complementary perspectives is examined for its compositional history, its relationship to parallel versions in other Buddhist canons, and its significance for reconstructing the teachings of the historical Buddha.
