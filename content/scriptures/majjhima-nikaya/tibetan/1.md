## Root Discourses on Right View

Tibetan Buddhism engages with this teaching on right view as the foundation of the entire Buddhist path as part of the foundational sutra vehicle upon which the complete Tibetan Buddhist path is constructed. These teachings inform the analytical meditation practices and the stages-of-the-path (lam-rim) literature that structures Tibetan Buddhist education.
