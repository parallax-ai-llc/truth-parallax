## Action and Rebirth

Tibetan Buddhism engages with this teaching on the analysis of intentional action, its consequences, and the mechanics of rebirth as part of the foundational sutra vehicle upon which the complete Tibetan Buddhist path is constructed. These teachings inform the analytical meditation practices and the stages-of-the-path (lam-rim) literature that structures Tibetan Buddhist education.
