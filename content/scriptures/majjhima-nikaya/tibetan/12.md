## Emptiness and Non-Self

In the Tibetan scholastic tradition, this section on the teachings on emptiness, non-self, and the wisdom that leads to liberation provides essential doctrinal foundations studied in the monastic curriculum. Tibetan scholars connect these teachings to the broader framework of Buddhist philosophy, from Abhidharma analysis through Madhyamaka and Yogachara to tantric practice.
