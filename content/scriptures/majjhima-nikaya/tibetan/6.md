## Discourses to Householders

In the Tibetan scholastic tradition, this section on teachings addressed to householders on ethical conduct and spiritual practice in daily life provides essential doctrinal foundations studied in the monastic curriculum. Tibetan scholars connect these teachings to the broader framework of Buddhist philosophy, from Abhidharma analysis through Madhyamaka and Yogachara to tantric practice.
