## Stages of Meditation

In the Tibetan scholastic tradition, this section on detailed descriptions of the stages of meditation from access to the formless attainments provides essential doctrinal foundations studied in the monastic curriculum. Tibetan scholars connect these teachings to the broader framework of Buddhist philosophy, from Abhidharma analysis through Madhyamaka and Yogachara to tantric practice.
