## Advanced Meditation

In the Tibetan scholastic tradition, this section on advanced teachings on meditation, the jhanas, and the development of insight provides essential doctrinal foundations studied in the monastic curriculum. Tibetan scholars connect these teachings to the broader framework of Buddhist philosophy, from Abhidharma analysis through Madhyamaka and Yogachara to tantric practice.
