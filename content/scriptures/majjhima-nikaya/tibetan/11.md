## Aggregates and Elements

Tibetan Buddhism engages with this teaching on analysis of the five aggregates and the elements of experience as part of the foundational sutra vehicle upon which the complete Tibetan Buddhist path is constructed. These teachings inform the analytical meditation practices and the stages-of-the-path (lam-rim) literature that structures Tibetan Buddhist education.
