## Kings and Brahmins

Tibetan Buddhism engages with this teaching on encounters with kings and Brahmins on dharma, governance, and truth as part of the foundational sutra vehicle upon which the complete Tibetan Buddhist path is constructed. These teachings inform the analytical meditation practices and the stages-of-the-path (lam-rim) literature that structures Tibetan Buddhist education.
