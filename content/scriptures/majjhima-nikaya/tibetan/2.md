## Similes and Foundations

In the Tibetan scholastic tradition, this section on foundational teachings using the similes of the cloth, the heartwood, and other vivid metaphors provides essential doctrinal foundations studied in the monastic curriculum. Tibetan scholars connect these teachings to the broader framework of Buddhist philosophy, from Abhidharma analysis through Madhyamaka and Yogachara to tantric practice.
