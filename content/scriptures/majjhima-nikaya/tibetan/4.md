## Paired Discourses

In the Tibetan scholastic tradition, this section on paired discourses analyzing various doctrinal topics from complementary perspectives provides essential doctrinal foundations studied in the monastic curriculum. Tibetan scholars connect these teachings to the broader framework of Buddhist philosophy, from Abhidharma analysis through Madhyamaka and Yogachara to tantric practice.
