## Destruction of Craving

Tibetan Buddhism engages with this teaching on the destruction of craving as the essential goal of Buddhist practice as part of the foundational sutra vehicle upon which the complete Tibetan Buddhist path is constructed. These teachings inform the analytical meditation practices and the stages-of-the-path (lam-rim) literature that structures Tibetan Buddhist education.
