## Wanderers and Ascetics

In the Tibetan scholastic tradition, this section on dialogues with wanderers and ascetics of other traditions provides essential doctrinal foundations studied in the monastic curriculum. Tibetan scholars connect these teachings to the broader framework of Buddhist philosophy, from Abhidharma analysis through Madhyamaka and Yogachara to tantric practice.
