## Emptiness and Non-Self

This section on the teachings on emptiness, non-self, and the wisdom that leads to liberation provides rich material for cross-traditional analysis. Comparative scholars examine how the Buddhist approach to these questions relates to and differs from the approaches of other major religious and philosophical traditions.
