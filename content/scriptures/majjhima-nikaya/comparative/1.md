## Root Discourses on Right View

Comparative interpretation of this teaching on right view as the foundation of the entire Buddhist path reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar teachings in Hindu, Jain, Christian, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Buddhist perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
