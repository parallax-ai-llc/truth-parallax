## Stages of Meditation

This section on detailed descriptions of the stages of meditation from access to the formless attainments provides rich material for cross-traditional analysis. Comparative scholars examine how the Buddhist approach to these questions relates to and differs from the approaches of other major religious and philosophical traditions.
