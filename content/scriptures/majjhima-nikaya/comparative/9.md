## Kings and Brahmins

Comparative interpretation of this teaching on encounters with kings and Brahmins on dharma, governance, and truth reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar teachings in Hindu, Jain, Christian, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Buddhist perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
