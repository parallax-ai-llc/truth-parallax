## Advanced Meditation

This section on advanced teachings on meditation, the jhanas, and the development of insight provides rich material for cross-traditional analysis. Comparative scholars examine how the Buddhist approach to these questions relates to and differs from the approaches of other major religious and philosophical traditions.
