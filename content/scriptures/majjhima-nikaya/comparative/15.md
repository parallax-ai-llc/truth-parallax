## Final Discourses

Comparative interpretation of this teaching on the final discourses of the collection and their synthetic reflections on the path reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar teachings in Hindu, Jain, Christian, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Buddhist perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
