## Action and Rebirth

Comparative interpretation of this teaching on the analysis of intentional action, its consequences, and the mechanics of rebirth reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar teachings in Hindu, Jain, Christian, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Buddhist perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
