## Discourses to Householders

This section on teachings addressed to householders on ethical conduct and spiritual practice in daily life provides rich material for cross-traditional analysis. Comparative scholars examine how the Buddhist approach to these questions relates to and differs from the approaches of other major religious and philosophical traditions.
