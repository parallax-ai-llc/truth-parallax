## Destruction of Craving

Comparative interpretation of this teaching on the destruction of craving as the essential goal of Buddhist practice reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar teachings in Hindu, Jain, Christian, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Buddhist perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
