## Lion's Roar Discourses

Comparative interpretation of this teaching on the lion's roar discourses proclaiming the distinctive excellence of the Buddha's teaching reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar teachings in Hindu, Jain, Christian, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Buddhist perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
