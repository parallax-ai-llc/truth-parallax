## Aggregates and Elements

Comparative interpretation of this teaching on analysis of the five aggregates and the elements of experience reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar teachings in Hindu, Jain, Christian, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Buddhist perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
