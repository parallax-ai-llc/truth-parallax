## Similes and Foundations

This section on foundational teachings using the similes of the cloth, the heartwood, and other vivid metaphors provides rich material for cross-traditional analysis. Comparative scholars examine how the Buddhist approach to these questions relates to and differs from the approaches of other major religious and philosophical traditions.
