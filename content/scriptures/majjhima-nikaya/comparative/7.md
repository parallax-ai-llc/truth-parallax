## Monks and Meditation

Comparative interpretation of this teaching on teachings on monastic conduct, meditation practice, and the cultivation of concentration reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar teachings in Hindu, Jain, Christian, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Buddhist perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
