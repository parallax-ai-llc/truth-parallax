## Lion's Roar Discourses

Zen Buddhism finds in this teaching on the lion's roar discourses proclaiming the distinctive excellence of the Buddha's teaching a directness and practicality that resonates with Zen values. While Zen emphasizes experience beyond textual study, these teachings provide foundations for the mindful awareness and direct insight that are central to Zen practice.
