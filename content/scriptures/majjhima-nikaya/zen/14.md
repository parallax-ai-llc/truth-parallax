## Stages of Meditation

Zen practitioners appreciate this section on detailed descriptions of the stages of meditation from access to the formless attainments for its emphasis on direct experience and practical application. The teaching's focus on immediate awareness and the transformation of consciousness aligns with the Zen aim of awakening to one's true nature in this very moment.
