## Similes and Foundations

Zen practitioners appreciate this section on foundational teachings using the similes of the cloth, the heartwood, and other vivid metaphors for its emphasis on direct experience and practical application. The teaching's focus on immediate awareness and the transformation of consciousness aligns with the Zen aim of awakening to one's true nature in this very moment.
