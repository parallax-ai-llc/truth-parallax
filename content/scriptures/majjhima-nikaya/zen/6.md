## Discourses to Householders

Zen practitioners appreciate this section on teachings addressed to householders on ethical conduct and spiritual practice in daily life for its emphasis on direct experience and practical application. The teaching's focus on immediate awareness and the transformation of consciousness aligns with the Zen aim of awakening to one's true nature in this very moment.
