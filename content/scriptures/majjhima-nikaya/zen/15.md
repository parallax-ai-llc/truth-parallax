## Final Discourses

Zen Buddhism finds in this teaching on the final discourses of the collection and their synthetic reflections on the path a directness and practicality that resonates with Zen values. While Zen emphasizes experience beyond textual study, these teachings provide foundations for the mindful awareness and direct insight that are central to Zen practice.
