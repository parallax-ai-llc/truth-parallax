## Root Discourses on Right View

Zen Buddhism finds in this teaching on right view as the foundation of the entire Buddhist path a directness and practicality that resonates with Zen values. While Zen emphasizes experience beyond textual study, these teachings provide foundations for the mindful awareness and direct insight that are central to Zen practice.
