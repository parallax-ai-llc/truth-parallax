## Destruction of Craving

Zen Buddhism finds in this teaching on the destruction of craving as the essential goal of Buddhist practice a directness and practicality that resonates with Zen values. While Zen emphasizes experience beyond textual study, these teachings provide foundations for the mindful awareness and direct insight that are central to Zen practice.
