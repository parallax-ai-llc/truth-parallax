## Kings and Brahmins

Zen Buddhism finds in this teaching on encounters with kings and Brahmins on dharma, governance, and truth a directness and practicality that resonates with Zen values. While Zen emphasizes experience beyond textual study, these teachings provide foundations for the mindful awareness and direct insight that are central to Zen practice.
