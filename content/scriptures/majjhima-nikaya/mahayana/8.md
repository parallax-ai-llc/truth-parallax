## Wanderers and Ascetics

From the Mahayana perspective, this section on dialogues with wanderers and ascetics of other traditions preserves important foundational teachings that the Mahayana tradition inherits and develops. Mahayana scholars identify in these early discourses the seeds of later philosophical developments in Madhyamaka and Yogachara thought.
