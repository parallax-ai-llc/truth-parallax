## Action and Rebirth

Mahayana Buddhism engages with this teaching on the analysis of intentional action, its consequences, and the mechanics of rebirth through its parallel Agama traditions and recognizes it as foundational material upon which later Mahayana developments build. The emphasis on compassion, wisdom, and the welfare of all beings connects these early teachings to the bodhisattva ideal.
