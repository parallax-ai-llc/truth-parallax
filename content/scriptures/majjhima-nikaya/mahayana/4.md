## Paired Discourses

From the Mahayana perspective, this section on paired discourses analyzing various doctrinal topics from complementary perspectives preserves important foundational teachings that the Mahayana tradition inherits and develops. Mahayana scholars identify in these early discourses the seeds of later philosophical developments in Madhyamaka and Yogachara thought.
