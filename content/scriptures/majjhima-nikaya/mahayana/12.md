## Emptiness and Non-Self

From the Mahayana perspective, this section on the teachings on emptiness, non-self, and the wisdom that leads to liberation preserves important foundational teachings that the Mahayana tradition inherits and develops. Mahayana scholars identify in these early discourses the seeds of later philosophical developments in Madhyamaka and Yogachara thought.
