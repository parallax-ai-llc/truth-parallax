## Final Discourses

Mahayana Buddhism engages with this teaching on the final discourses of the collection and their synthetic reflections on the path through its parallel Agama traditions and recognizes it as foundational material upon which later Mahayana developments build. The emphasis on compassion, wisdom, and the welfare of all beings connects these early teachings to the bodhisattva ideal.
