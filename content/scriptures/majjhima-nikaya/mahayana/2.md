## Similes and Foundations

From the Mahayana perspective, this section on foundational teachings using the similes of the cloth, the heartwood, and other vivid metaphors preserves important foundational teachings that the Mahayana tradition inherits and develops. Mahayana scholars identify in these early discourses the seeds of later philosophical developments in Madhyamaka and Yogachara thought.
