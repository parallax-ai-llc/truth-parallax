## Discourses to Householders

From the Mahayana perspective, this section on teachings addressed to householders on ethical conduct and spiritual practice in daily life preserves important foundational teachings that the Mahayana tradition inherits and develops. Mahayana scholars identify in these early discourses the seeds of later philosophical developments in Madhyamaka and Yogachara thought.
