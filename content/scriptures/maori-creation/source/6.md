---
title: "Maori Creation - Chapter 6"
scripture: "maori-creation"
chapter: 6
---

**1** These are the karakia, the sacred chants, for the ordering of the world:

**2** "From the first, from Te Kore, the Void, the Nothing, came Te Po, the Night, the Darkness."

**3** "From Te Po came Te Ao, the Light, the World. From Te Ao came Te Ao-marama, the World of Light."

**4** "From the Void came the first glimmering. From the first glimmering came the first light. From the first light came the great light."

**5** "From Te Kore-te-whiwhia, the Void in which nothing is possessed, came Te Kore-te-rawea, the Void in which nothing is felt, came Te Kore-te-tamaua, the Void in which nothing is bound."

**6** "From the nothingness came the first growth, from the first growth came the increase, from the increase came the waxing full, from the waxing full came the thought, from the thought came the remembrance."

**7** "From the remembrance came the consciousness, from the consciousness came the desire, from the desire came the word — the living word."

**8** "The word became fruitful, and it dwelt with the feeble glimmering, and it brought forth night — Te Po-nui, the Great Night; Te Po-roa, the Long Night; Te Po-uriuri, the Dark Night; Te Po-kerekere, the Intense Night; Te Po-tiwhatiwha, the Gloom of Night."

**9** "From the night came Rangi-nui, the Great Sky, and Papa-tu-a-nuku, the Earth Spread Out."

**10** "These are the words of the ancients, handed down from generation to generation, from mouth to ear, in the whare wananga, the house of learning."

**11** "Let the words be preserved. Let the knowledge be kept sacred. For in the beginning was Te Kore, and from Te Kore came all that is."

**12** "E Io e! Tu mai ra koe! Ko koe anake te matua! Ko koe te taki — te ingoa nui — te ingoa roa!" — "O Io! Thou standest! Thou alone art parent! Thou art the reciter — the great name — the long name!"

**13** These are the sacred words of creation, guarded by the tohunga in the house of learning, passed from teacher to pupil in the sacred rites. Thus is the story of the beginning.
