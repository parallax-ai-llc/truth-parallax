## Islamic Reading

The Islamic reader of the Bundahishn's creation account of the earth discovers the pre-Islamic Persian cosmogonic tradition that influenced Islamic creation narratives. The Qur'anic creation account, while rooted in the Abrahamic tradition, also reflects the cosmological environment of the late antique Near East in which Zoroastrian, Jewish, and Christian creation theologies circulated and interacted. The Bundahishn provides the fullest account of the Zoroastrian cosmogonic tradition that formed part of this shared environment.
