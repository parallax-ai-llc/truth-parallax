## Islamic Reading

The Islamic reader of the Bundahishn's historical narrative of history of the kayanid dynasty encounters the legendary history of pre-Islamic Iran — the kings and heroes who populated the Persian national epic tradition preserved in Ferdowsi's Shahnameh. This legendary history was not rejected by Islamic Persian culture but incorporated into the broader Islamic understanding of human history, with pre-Islamic Persian kings sometimes identified as prophets or righteous rulers in the Islamic schema.
