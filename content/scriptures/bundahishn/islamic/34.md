## Islamic Reading

The Islamic reader of the Bundahishn's eschatological teaching on coming of the saoshyant encounters the Zoroastrian eschatological tradition that demonstrably influenced Islamic descriptions of the end times. Islamic concepts including the *Sirat* (bridge of judgment, from the Zoroastrian Chinvat), the *Mizan* (scales), the physical descriptions of paradise and hell, and the concept of the Mahdi (possibly influenced by the Saoshyant) all bear the marks of Zoroastrian influence. The Bundahishn provides the pre-Islamic context for understanding these Islamic eschatological concepts.
