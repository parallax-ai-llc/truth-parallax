## Islamic Reading

The Islamic reader of the Bundahishn's treatment of nature of demons and their works encounters the Zoroastrian demonological tradition that influenced Islamic concepts of *jinn*, *shayatin*, and evil spirits. The Qur'anic and hadith accounts of the unseen world of spiritual beings reflect a cosmological environment in which Zoroastrian, Jewish, and Christian demonological traditions circulated and interacted. The Bundahishn provides the fullest account of the Zoroastrian component of this shared demonological heritage.
