## Protestant Reading

The Protestant reader of the Bundahishn's treatment of remedies against evil creatures encounters a systematic demonology that parallels and may have influenced the development of biblical and Christian understanding of evil spiritual forces. The Zoroastrian classification of demons by their specific functions and methods of attack resonates with the New Testament's language about 'principalities and powers' and the Protestant tradition's recognition of spiritual warfare as a dimension of the Christian life.
