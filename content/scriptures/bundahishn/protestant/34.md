## Protestant Reading

The Protestant reader of the Bundahishn's eschatological teaching on coming of the saoshyant discovers ancient parallels to core Christian eschatological convictions: the intensification of evil before the end, the coming of a divine savior, the resurrection of the dead, and the renewal of creation. These parallels raise important theological questions about the relationship between general revelation and special revelation — whether God communicated eschatological truth to Zoroaster as well as to the biblical prophets.
