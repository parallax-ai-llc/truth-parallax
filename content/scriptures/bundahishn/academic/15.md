## Academic Reading

Scholars analyze the Bundahishn's cosmographic treatment of the planets in relation to ancient Near Eastern, Greek, and Indian geographical and astronomical knowledge. The text is examined as evidence of the Sasanian-era Zoroastrian engagement with Ptolemaic astronomy and Aristotelian natural philosophy, transmitted through Syriac and Pahlavi scholarly traditions. Comparative analysis reveals both borrowed elements and distinctively Zoroastrian interpretations.
