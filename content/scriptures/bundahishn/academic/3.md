## Academic Reading

Academic study of the material creation chapter examines the Bundahishn's concept of matter as inherently good — a position that distinguishes Zoroastrianism from Gnostic and Manichaean systems that regarded materiality as negative. Scholars analyze the relationship between the Bundahishn's creation account and the lost Avestan creation texts that it may preserve in Pahlavi summary, using comparative evidence from surviving Avestan passages to assess the text's antiquity.
