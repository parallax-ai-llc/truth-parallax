## Academic Reading

Academic analysis of the Bundahishn's treatment of remedies against evil creatures examines the text's demonological system in relation to comparative demonology across ancient Near Eastern and Indo-European traditions. Scholars trace specific demonic figures to Avestan sources while noting parallels with Babylonian, Jewish, and early Christian demonological traditions. The practical prescriptions are studied as evidence of Zoroastrian ritual practice and its response to perceived spiritual threats.
