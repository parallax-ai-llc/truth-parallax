## Academic Reading

Scholars analyze the Bundahishn's eschatological teaching on coming of the saoshyant in relation to the development of apocalyptic and eschatological thought across ancient Near Eastern religions. The chapter is compared with Jewish apocalyptic literature (Daniel, 1 Enoch), Christian Revelation, and Islamic eschatological texts to assess the direction and extent of mutual influence. The text is studied as evidence of Zoroastrian eschatological traditions that may predate and have influenced the Abrahamic apocalyptic tradition.
