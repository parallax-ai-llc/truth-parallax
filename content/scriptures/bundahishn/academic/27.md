## Academic Reading

Scholars analyze the Bundahishn's historical narrative concerning history of the kayanid dynasty as a blend of mythological tradition, legendary history, and possible historical memory. The text is compared with Greek, Indian, and biblical accounts of legendary kings and dynastic founders to identify shared Indo-European mythological patterns and culturally specific elaborations. The chapter's relationship to the lost Avestan historical texts summarized in the Denkard is assessed.
