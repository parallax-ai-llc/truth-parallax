## Academic Reading

Academic analysis of the Bundahishn's opening chapter examines its account of spiritual creation in relation to other ancient Near Eastern cosmogonies and Indo-Iranian creation myths. Scholars trace the concept of a primordial spiritual realm to Avestan sources (particularly Yasna 30 and Yasht 13) while noting Neoplatonic and Manichaean parallels that may reflect later influences. The text's Pahlavi vocabulary is analyzed for evidence of both archaic Avestan concepts and post-Sasanian developments.
