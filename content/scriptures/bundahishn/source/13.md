---
title: "Chapter 13 – On the Nature of the Races of Mankind"
chapter: 13
---

**1.** On the races of mankind it is declared in the Religion that from Fravak came fifteen pairs of children, and from each pair a race descended, and they spread across the seven regions of the earth.

**2.** The Iranians are from the pair Hooshang and Guzhak. They were born in Iranvej, the noblest of lands, which is in the middle of the seven regions.

**3.** The Turanians are from another pair, and they settled in the region of Turan. And other peoples settled in other regions, each according to their appointed place.

**4.** Among the Iranians, the noblest are those of the seed of Manushchihr, the grandson of Iraj. They are the chosen people of Ohrmazd, for through them the Religion was revealed.

**5.** They say that all mankind are of one stock, being descended from Mashya and Mashyane, and the differences among them came through the different regions and climates in which they dwelt.

**6.** The people of each region have their own tongue, their own customs, and their own manner of dress. But the Religion of Ohrmazd is for all mankind, and the duty of good thoughts, good words, and good deeds belongs to every person.

**7.** From Hooshang, who was the first king, descended the line of kings of Iran, through whom civilization and law were established. Hooshang was the first to discover fire, the first to smelt metals, and the first to teach men to till the earth.

**8.** After Hooshang came Tahmuras, who subjugated the demons and rode upon the back of the Destructive Spirit. He taught men the art of writing, and under his reign the world prospered.

**9.** After Tahmuras came Jamshid, the most glorious of kings. He divided mankind into four classes: the priests, the warriors, the farmers, and the artisans. Under his rule there was no death, no hunger, no thirst, no old age.

**10.** But Jamshid became proud and claimed divinity for himself. Then his royal glory departed from him, and the world fell into the power of Dahak, the servant of the Destructive Spirit.
