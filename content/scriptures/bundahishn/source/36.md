---
title: "Chapter 36 – The Final Renovation"
chapter: 36
---

**1.** On the Renovation, the Frashokereti, it is declared in the Religion that when Soshyant has raised the dead and prepared the elixir of immortality, all creation shall be transformed.

**2.** The fire and the angel Airyaman shall melt all the metal in the hills and mountains, and it shall flow over the earth like a mighty river.

**3.** All mankind shall be made to pass through that river of molten metal. For the righteous it shall feel as warm milk; for the wicked it shall burn like fire. But all shall emerge purified.

**4.** Then father and son, brother and sister, friend and stranger shall be reunited, and they shall praise Ohrmazd together in joy.

**5.** The Destructive Spirit and all the demons shall be utterly destroyed. Ohrmazd himself shall seize Ahriman. The earth shall swallow hell, and the molten metal shall fill the cavity where hell was, and no trace of evil shall remain.

**6.** The earth shall be made flat and smooth, without mountain or valley. There shall be one plain, fair and green, where all mankind shall dwell.

**7.** All men shall speak one tongue and belong to one nation. There shall be no more division of race or country. All shall follow the Good Religion.

**8.** There shall be no more darkness, for the light of Ohrmazd shall fill all things. There shall be no more death, no hunger, no thirst, no old age, no disease.

**9.** Men shall have bodies that cast no shadow. They shall be immortal, ever youthful, ever joyful. They shall desire good things, and their desire shall be fulfilled.

**10.** This is the purpose for which Ohrmazd created the world and the creatures: that through the battle with evil, righteousness should be proved, and through the Renovation, all things should be made perfect and eternal.

**11.** Blessed is the man who in this present life practices good thoughts, good words, and good deeds, for he hastens the Renovation and adds to the army of Ohrmazd.

**12.** Blessed is the man who upholds the Religion, who teaches the truth, who protects the righteous, and who destroys the Lie. His reward shall be great at the Renovation, and in the renewed world he shall dwell in bliss for ever and ever.

**13.** So it is declared in the Good Religion of the Mazdayasnians. So it was, so it is, and so it shall be. In the name of Ohrmazd, the Creator, the Righteous One, the Lord of Wisdom.
