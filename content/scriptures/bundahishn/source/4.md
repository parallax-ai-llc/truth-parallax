---
title: "Chapter 4 – The Onslaught of the Planets"
chapter: 4
---

**1.** The Destructive Spirit produced the planets against the luminaries. Seven planets he brought forth against the seven luminaries, to disturb the order and arrangement of the constellations.

**2.** As the Destructive Spirit rushed upon the creation, the chieftains of the constellations, together with the planets and many demons, fell upon the earth. The luminaries were in conflict with the whole sky.

**3.** The Destructive Spirit made the planets rush against the firmament, and they mixed the constellations. He bound the planet Jupiter to the constellation of the Ram, the planet Venus to the Bull, the planet Mercury to the Twins, the planet Mars to the Crab, the planet Saturn to the Balance, and there was much disturbance.

**4.** He set in the sky the Thief, the Mushpar, and other planets of the Destructive Spirit, and they have their motion contrary to the sun, as the sun moves from east to west, and the Thief moves from west to east.

**5.** The moon and the sun and the fixed stars wage war against the planets. They say: "Every disturbance and ailment and damage and destruction that comes to the creatures of Ohrmazd proceeds from the planets."

**6.** Tishtar, the chieftain of the east, with Sataves and Vanand and Haftoring, the four generals of the four quarters, together with the other luminaries, war against the planets, and keep back much affliction from the creation of Ohrmazd.

**7.** The Destructive Spirit also let loose upon the earth the comet-demons, that they might attack the fortification of the sky and mingle with the luminaries, and the whole sky and earth were disturbed by them.

**8.** But the firmament revolves around the world, and the luminaries fight continuously, so that the Destructive Spirit and his crew may be kept at bay until the Renovation shall come.
