---
title: "Chapter 33 – On the Resurrection and Future Body"
chapter: 33
---

**1.** On the resurrection and the future body it is declared in the Religion that when the time of the Renovation arrives, the dead shall rise.

**2.** First the bones of Gayomard shall rise, then those of Mashya and Mashyane, then the rest of mankind.

**3.** In fifty-seven years Soshyant shall raise all the dead. Every person shall rise in the place where death overtook him, and he shall recognize his kin.

**4.** Then all shall be assembled, and each shall see the other. The righteous man shall see the wicked, and the wicked shall see the righteous, and each shall say: "This is my father, this is my mother, this is my brother, this is my wife."

**5.** Then shall come the great assembly, and each shall look upon his own good and evil deeds. The righteous shall be as conspicuous among the wicked as a white sheep among black.

**6.** The wicked man shall cry: "Alas! Where was my friend? Where was my kinsman? Why did he not teach me the Right? Why did he not show me the Good?" And the righteous man shall weep for the wicked.

**7.** Then the righteous and the wicked shall be separated, and the righteous shall be raised to the star station, and the wicked shall be cast into hell. For three days the righteous shall enjoy the bliss of heaven, and for three days the wicked shall suffer in hell.

**8.** After these three days, all shall pass through the river of molten metal. The righteous shall pass as through warm milk; for the wicked it shall be torment. But through the torment, all sin shall be burned away.

**9.** Then Soshyant, with his helpers, shall perform the final sacrifice. The ox Hadhayas shall be slain, and from its fat and the white Hom the drink of immortality shall be prepared, and it shall be given to all men, and all men shall become immortal.

**10.** The bodies of all men shall be made young again. Those who died as old men shall be restored to the age of forty; those who died as children to the age of fifteen. And each shall receive back his wife and children.

**11.** The earth shall give up the bone, the water the blood, the fire the warmth, and the wind the spirit. From these the body shall be reconstituted, as it was in the beginning.

**12.** And then creation shall be renewed. The fire and the angel Airyaman shall melt the metals in the hills and mountains, and the molten metal shall flow over the earth like a river.

**13.** All creation shall pass through that river. To the righteous it shall be as warm milk; to the wicked it shall be as burning flame. And all shall emerge purified, and sin and evil shall be no more.

**14.** Ohrmazd shall seize the Destructive Spirit, Vahman shall seize Akoman, Ardwahisht shall seize Indar, Shahrevar shall seize Savar, Spandarmad shall seize Taromat, Hordad and Amurdad shall seize Tairev and Zairicha.

**15.** Ahriman, stripped of power and followers, shall be cast into the darkness and the passage sealed with molten metal, and the darkness itself shall be burned away.

**16.** Then shall creation abide in bliss: immortal, undecaying, forever youthful. And Ohrmazd and all creation shall rest in joy that has no end.
