---
title: "Chapter 35 – On the Coming of the Saoshyants"
chapter: 35
---

**1.** On the coming of the Saoshyants it is declared in the Religion that three deliverers shall come from the seed of Zarathushtra to accomplish the Renovation.

**2.** The first is Hushedar, who shall come at the beginning of the last three thousand years. When he appears, the sun shall stand still at the zenith for ten days and nights, and all men shall know that the time of deliverance has begun.

**3.** Hushedar shall restore the Religion to its purity. He shall teach men to forsake evil and to embrace the Right. Under his guidance, men shall eat only once in two days, and noxious creatures shall diminish.

**4.** After Hushedar, a thousand years shall pass. Then Hushedar-Mah shall come. When he appears, the sun shall stand still for twenty days and nights.

**5.** Under Hushedar-Mah, the demon Az shall be defeated, and men shall need to eat only once in three days. The wolf and the sheep shall dwell together, and men shall do no harm to one another.

**6.** After Hushedar-Mah, another thousand years shall pass. Then Soshyant, the greatest of the three, shall come. When he appears, the sun shall stand still for thirty days and nights.

**7.** Soshyant shall raise all the dead and prepare the drink of immortality. He shall perform the final sacrifice and render the Destructive Spirit powerless.

**8.** Under Soshyant, all evil shall cease, and the earth shall be made new, and mankind shall be immortal. The good creation shall endure in bliss, in the light of Ohrmazd, for ever and ever.

**9.** This is the promise of Ohrmazd to his creation: that the darkness shall not endure, that the Lie shall be vanquished, and that all things shall be made right. This is the Renovation, the Frashokereti, which is the purpose of all creation.
