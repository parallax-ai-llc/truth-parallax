---
title: "Chapter 32 – On the Conflict of Ohrmazd and Ahriman"
chapter: 32
---

**1.** On the conflict of Ohrmazd and Ahriman it is declared in the Religion that the battle shall endure for nine thousand years, and in the end Ohrmazd shall prevail.

**2.** In the first three thousand years, Ohrmazd is supreme, and creation exists in its spiritual form, pure and uncorrupted.

**3.** In the second three thousand years, the creation is brought into material form, and the Destructive Spirit lies in stupor.

**4.** In the third three thousand years, the conflict rages in the material world, and good and evil are mingled together. This is the time in which we live.

**5.** In the last three thousand years, the three Saoshyants shall come, each a thousand years apart. The first, Hushedar, shall restore the Religion and diminish the power of the demons.

**6.** The second, Hushedar-Mah, shall further weaken the Destructive Spirit and bring about the slaying of the demon Az, so that men shall need to eat only once in three days.

**7.** The third, Soshyant, the greatest of the three, shall raise the dead and accomplish the Renovation. Through him the final sacrifice shall be performed, and from the fat of the bull Hadhayas and the juice of the white Hom, an elixir of immortality shall be prepared.

**8.** All the dead shall rise from their graves, and each shall recognize the other. The righteous and the wicked shall be separated, and all shall pass through a river of molten metal.

**9.** To the righteous, the molten metal shall feel as warm milk. To the wicked, it shall burn, but through this burning they shall be purified. Three days and three nights they shall endure it, and then all sin shall be washed from them.

**10.** The Destructive Spirit, seeing all his followers destroyed and his power broken, shall flee back to the darkness through the passage by which he entered. The passage shall be sealed with molten metal, and the Destructive Spirit shall be imprisoned forever.

**11.** Then creation shall be made new. The earth shall be leveled, the mountains flattened. All shall be immortal, undecaying, without hunger, without thirst. The bodies of men shall cast no shadow, for there shall be no darkness.

**12.** And Ohrmazd shall reign over all, and the Good Religion shall be the law of all creation for ever and ever.
