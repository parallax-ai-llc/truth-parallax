---
title: "Chapter 30 – On the Var of Yima"
chapter: 30
---

**1.** On the Var of Yima it is declared in the Religion that when Ohrmazd warned Yima that a deadly winter would come upon the world, with snow and ice and flood, Yima built a Var, an enclosure, to preserve the best of every creation.

**2.** Ohrmazd said to Yima: "There shall fall upon the material world a winter of great severity, with thick snow and terrible cold. One-third of cattle and men and dogs and birds and fires shall perish."

**3.** "Make thee a Var, the length of a riding-ground on every side of the square. Thither bring the seeds of sheep and oxen, of men, of dogs, of birds, and of red blazing fires."

**4.** "Bring thither the seeds of the best and finest men and women; the seeds of every kind of cattle; the seeds of every kind of tree; the seeds of every kind of fruit."

**5.** "Two of every kind shalt thou bring, to be kept inexhaustible there, so long as those men shall stay in the Var."

**6.** So Yima made the Var. He brought into it the best of men and women, the finest of animals, the most fragrant of plants, and the most delicious of foods. He brought the sacred fire, and he brought the bird Karshipt to maintain the Religion.

**7.** Within the Var there is no strife, no disease, no old age, no death. Those who dwell there live in happiness, and the light within is both created and uncreated.

**8.** They produce children once in every forty years, and the Var shall endure until the Renovation, when all that was preserved within it shall come forth to replenish the renewed world.

**9.** The entrance to the Var was sealed, and its location is hidden from men. Only at the Renovation shall it be opened again.
