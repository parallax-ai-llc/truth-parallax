---
title: "Chapter 10 – On the Nature of Fire"
chapter: 10
---

**1.** On the nature of fire it is declared in the Religion that fire was created in five kinds: the Berezi-savangha fire, the Vohu-fryana fire, the Urvazishta fire, the Vazishta fire, and the Spenishta fire.

**2.** The Berezi-savangha fire is that which blazes before Ohrmazd in the spiritual realm. Its manifestation in the material world is the fire which blazes on the earth and is set in the body of all living beings.

**3.** The Vohu-fryana fire is that which blazes in the bodies of men and animals. Through its power the body is kept warm and the food is digested.

**4.** The Urvazishta fire is that which blazes in the plants. Through its power the plants grow and bear fruit and grain.

**5.** The Vazishta fire is that which blazes in the clouds. Its manifestation is the lightning, which smites the darkness of the Destructive Spirit.

**6.** The Spenishta fire is the Bahram fire, which blazes in the spiritual realm before Ohrmazd. Its manifestation is the sacred fire that is established in the fire temples.

**7.** All these five fires are interconnected and perform the work of Ohrmazd. The fire which is in the body keeps life and soul together; the fire of the plants makes them grow; the fire in the clouds brings the rain.

**8.** The sacred fire must be maintained with care and reverence, for it is the representative of Ohrmazd's power on earth. He who tends the fire with devotion and righteous care acquires great merit.

**9.** At the Renovation, all these five fires shall unite, and the molten metal shall flow over the earth for the purification of all creation. To the righteous it shall feel as warm milk; to the wicked it shall burn.

**10.** Fire is the son of Ohrmazd, and through fire the Renovation shall be accomplished. The wicked shall be purified, and creation shall be made immortal, undecaying, and free from all evil.
