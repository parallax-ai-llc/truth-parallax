---
title: "Chapter 18 – On the Nature of the Spiritual Beings"
chapter: 18
---

**1.** On the nature of the spiritual beings it is declared in the Religion that Ohrmazd first created the Amesha Spentas, the Holy Immortals, who are six in number, and with Ohrmazd they are seven.

**2.** Vahman, who is Good Thought, is the first. He protects the beneficent animals. Through Good Thought shall men come to the knowledge of Ohrmazd.

**3.** Ardwahisht, who is Best Righteousness, is the second. He protects fire and all luminaries. Through Righteousness shall the Lie be vanquished.

**4.** Shahrevar, who is the Desired Dominion, is the third. He protects metals. Through the Dominion shall the righteous rule.

**5.** Spandarmad, who is Holy Devotion, is the fourth. She protects the earth. Through Devotion shall men serve Ohrmazd with humility.

**6.** Hordad, who is Wholeness, is the fifth. He protects the waters. Through Wholeness shall the creatures be healed.

**7.** Amurdad, who is Immortality, is the sixth. He protects the plants. Through Immortality shall death be defeated.

**8.** Below the Amesha Spentas are the Yazads, the spiritual beings worthy of worship. They are many: Mithra, the lord of wide pastures; Anahita, the lady of the waters; Sraosha, the lord of obedience; Rashnu, the lord of justice; Ashi, the lady of reward; Tishtar, the lord of rain.

**9.** The Fravashis are the guardian spirits of all men. They existed before the material creation, and they chose to be incarnated in the world to fight against the Destructive Spirit.

**10.** Before creation, Ohrmazd showed the Fravashis the future: the assault of the Destructive Spirit, the suffering of the world, and the final Renovation. He asked: "Will you enter the material world to fight?" And the Fravashis said: "We will."

**11.** So the Fravashis entered the material world, and in every generation they are born as men and women to fight the battle of the Right against the Lie. At the Renovation they shall all be gathered together, and creation shall be restored.
