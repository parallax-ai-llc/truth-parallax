---
title: "Chapter 20 – On the Succession of Generations from Gayomard"
chapter: 20
---

**1.** On the succession of generations it is declared in the Religion that from Gayomard came Mashya and Mashyane, and from them came the seven pairs from whom all mankind descended.

**2.** From Siamak came Fravak, and from Fravak came fifteen pairs, and they became the peoples of the seven regions.

**3.** Hooshang son of Fravak was the first king. He discovered fire by striking stones together, and he taught men to smelt metals and to irrigate the fields.

**4.** Tahmuras son of Vivanghan was the second king. He bound Ahriman and rode upon him as a horse for thirty years. He taught men the art of writing and clothed mankind in garments of wool and silk.

**5.** Jamshid son of Vivanghan was the third king. He divided mankind into four classes: priests, warriors, farmers, and artisans. Under his rule, for three hundred years there was no disease, no death, no hunger, no thirst.

**6.** But Jamshid grew proud and claimed to be the creator. His royal glory departed from him, and he fled. Dahak, the servant of Ahriman, seized the throne and ruled for a thousand years with tyranny and oppression.

**7.** Dahak had three mouths, six eyes, and three heads. On his shoulders grew two serpents, which had to be fed daily with the brains of young men.

**8.** At last Faridun, of the line of Jamshid, arose and overthrew Dahak. He bound him in chains on Mount Damavand, where he shall remain until the Renovation.

**9.** Faridun divided the earth among his three sons: Salm received the west, Tur received the east, and Iraj received Iran. But Salm and Tur, out of envy, slew Iraj.

**10.** From Iraj descended Manushchihr, who avenged Iraj by slaying Salm and Tur. From Manushchihr descended the line of the kings of Iran, through whom the Religion was preserved.

**11.** In the time of King Vishtaspa, Zarathushtra the Prophet came forth and proclaimed the Religion of Ohrmazd. Vishtaspa accepted the Religion, and through his authority it was spread throughout the world.

**12.** From Zarathushtra's seed shall come the three Saoshyants, the Future Deliverers, who at the end of time shall bring about the Renovation and the defeat of the Destructive Spirit.
