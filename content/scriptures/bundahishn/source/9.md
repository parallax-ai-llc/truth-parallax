---
title: "Chapter 9 – On the Nature of Plants"
chapter: 9
---

**1.** On the nature of plants it is declared in the Religion that before the coming of the Destructive Spirit, there was no thorn on the plants, no bark on the trees, and no hard rind on the fruits. But after the assault of the Destructive Spirit, all these came into being.

**2.** Of plants there are many species. Ohrmazd created one hundred thousand species of plants, and among them are one hundred and twenty thousand species that are for remedies.

**3.** From the tree of many seeds, which is in the sea of Vourukasha, every kind of plant received its seed. And Amurdad, who is the guardian of plants, pounds the plants and mixes them with the water that Tishtar takes up, so that the rain may scatter the seeds of all plants over the earth.

**4.** Close to that tree the white Hom has grown, which is the preparer of the dead — that is, its juice gives immortality to those who drink of it at the Renovation.

**5.** The tree of many seeds has grown in the middle of the sea of Vourukasha, and every seed of every plant is on it. Some say it is the tree of all remedies, and its seeds are the remedy of every disease.

**6.** There is a bird called the Karshipt, which sits on that tree. When it rises, a thousand twigs grow from the tree; when it alights, it breaks off a thousand twigs and scatters the seeds from them.

**7.** The bird Chamrosh dwells near that tree, and its work is to gather the scattered seeds, which the bird Karshipt has shaken from the tree, and carry them to where Tishtar shall take up the water, so that Tishtar may take the seeds of all plants with the water and rain them upon the earth.

**8.** Of the plants that grow on the earth, grain is the chief. Of grain, wheat is the most important, for it is what men need most. Barley is second, and the others after them.

**9.** All the plants of Ohrmazd are free from thorns and fragrant, pleasing to the sight and good for food. What is harmful in them has come from the Destructive Spirit.

**10.** At the Renovation all plants shall grow without thorn or bark, fragrant and pleasing, as they were in the beginning before the assault.
