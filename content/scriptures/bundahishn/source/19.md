---
title: "Chapter 19 – On the Nature of the Demons"
chapter: 19
---

**1.** On the nature of the demons it is declared in the Religion that the Destructive Spirit created the demons from his own essence of darkness and evil. They are innumerable, but the chief among them are known.

**2.** Akoman, Evil Thought, is the foremost demon. He is opposed to Vahman, Good Thought. His work is to make men think evil, to turn their minds from Ohrmazd to the Lie.

**3.** Indar is the demon who freezes the minds of creatures against the practice of righteousness and good deeds. He opposes Ardwahisht.

**4.** Savar is the demon of misgovernment and drunkenness. He opposes Shahrevar, the Desired Dominion, and through him rulers become tyrants.

**5.** Naonhaithya, called Taromat, is the demon of presumption and contempt. She opposes Spandarmad, Holy Devotion, and makes men arrogant and disrespectful.

**6.** Tairev is the demon of corruption. He opposes Hordad, and his work is to mingle poison with the plants and waters.

**7.** Zairicha is the demon who makes poison. He opposes Amurdad, and his work is to blight the crops and wither the plants.

**8.** Aeshma, the demon of wrath, is the most violent of all demons. He has a bloody spear, and he rushes upon men to incite them to violence and war and murder. His chief opponent is Sraosha, who through obedience and prayer keeps wrath at bay.

**9.** Az is the demon of greed, who is never satisfied. He makes men covet that which is not theirs, and through greed all manner of evil deeds are done.

**10.** Bushyasta is the long-handed demon of sloth. She lies upon men at dawn and makes them sleep on, so that they neglect their prayers and duties.

**11.** Jahi is the demon of corruption, the mother of all female demons. She corrupts the minds and bodies of men with lustful desires.

**12.** Apaosha is the demon of drought. He takes the form of a black horse and fights against Tishtar. When he prevails, the rain ceases and the earth is parched.

**13.** The Drug, the Lie itself, is the essence of all demons. All falsehood, all evil, all corruption proceeds from the Drug. To speak truth and act with righteousness is to fight against the Drug.

**14.** At the Renovation all demons shall be destroyed. The Destructive Spirit himself shall be rendered powerless, and creation shall be free from evil for all time.
