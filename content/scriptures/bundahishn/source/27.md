---
title: "Chapter 27 – On the Nature of the Wolf"
chapter: 27
---

**1.** On the nature of the wolf species it is declared in the Religion that the wolf is of the creation of the Destructive Spirit and the mightiest of noxious creatures on land.

**2.** The wolf was created to prey upon the flocks and to attack the cattle of righteous men. He hunts by night and brings terror to the homesteads.

**3.** Of wolves there are several kinds: the mountain wolf, the forest wolf, and the desert wolf. Each is cunning and savage, and each seeks to destroy the good creation.

**4.** Against the wolf stands the dog, who was created by Ohrmazd to guard the flocks and the homesteads. The shepherd's dog is the foremost of dogs, for his work is to protect the sheep.

**5.** It is the duty of every man to keep dogs and to train them against wolves, for in this manner the creation of Ohrmazd is defended against the creation of the Destructive Spirit.

**6.** He who feeds a dog with care and gives it shelter gains great merit. He who strikes or injures a dog without cause commits a grievous sin, for the dog is a righteous creature.

**7.** At the Renovation, all wolves shall be destroyed, and the flocks shall graze without fear in the pastures of the renewed world.
