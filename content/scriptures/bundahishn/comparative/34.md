## Comparative Reading

Comparative study of the Bundahishn's eschatological teaching on coming of the saoshyant reveals its pivotal position in the development of end-time expectations across traditions. The Zoroastrian concepts presented here — the signs of the end, the coming savior, the resurrection of the dead, the final judgment, and the renovation of creation — influenced the development of Jewish, Christian, and Islamic eschatology. Comparative analysis traces these shared motifs while noting each tradition's distinctive elaborations.
