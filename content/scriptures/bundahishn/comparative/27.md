## Comparative Reading

Comparative analysis of the Bundahishn's historical narrative of history of the kayanid dynasty reveals shared patterns with legendary histories from other civilizations: the biblical patriarchs, the Hindu Puranic kings, the Chinese legendary emperors, and the Greek age of heroes. Each tradition constructs a sacred history that connects the present community with primordial origins through a chain of legendary figures who embody the tradition's foundational values.
