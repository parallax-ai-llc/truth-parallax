## Zoroastrian Reading

The Bundahishn's classification of animals divides them into categories based on their relationship to the cosmic struggle: beneficial animals (*gospand*) that serve Ahura Mazda's purposes, and noxious creatures (*khrafstar*) that are the creations of Angra Mainyu. This moral classification of the animal kingdom is distinctively Zoroastrian and has practical implications for the treatment of different species.
