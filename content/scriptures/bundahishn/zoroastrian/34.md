## Zoroastrian Reading

The coming of the Saoshyant (Savior) is the climactic event of Zoroastrian eschatology. The Bundahishn describes how the Saoshyant — born from the miraculously preserved seed of Zoroaster — will lead the final battle against Angra Mainyu, raise the dead, and inaugurate the *frashokereti* (renovation). The Zoroastrian community's endurance through centuries of adversity is sustained by this eschatological promise.
