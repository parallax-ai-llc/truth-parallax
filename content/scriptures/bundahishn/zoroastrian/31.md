## Zoroastrian Reading

The Bundahishn's demonology provides a systematic account of the forces of evil — their names, their specialties, and their methods of attack. The demons (*divs*) are led by Angra Mainyu and include beings responsible for specific forms of suffering: Aeshma (wrath), Nasu (pollution), Az (greed), and others. This classification provides the Zoroastrian with a comprehensive map of the moral dangers they face.
