## Zoroastrian Reading

The Bundahishn's account of the signs of the end times describes the progressive intensification of the cosmic struggle as history approaches its culmination. The Zoroastrian reader finds in these signs not cause for despair but for hope — the very intensity of evil's assault signals the approaching victory of good.
