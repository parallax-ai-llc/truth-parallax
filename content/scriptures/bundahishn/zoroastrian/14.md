## Zoroastrian Reading

The sun is described as the supreme celestial ally of Ahura Mazda — the source of light, warmth, and life that drives back the darkness associated with Angra Mainyu. The Bundahishn's solar theology provides the cosmic context for the Zoroastrian practice of praying facing a source of light and the reverence for fire as the sun's earthly representative.
