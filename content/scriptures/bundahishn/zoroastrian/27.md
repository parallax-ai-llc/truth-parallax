## Zoroastrian Reading

The Kayanid dynasty's history connects the legendary past with the historical Zoroastrian kingdoms — providing a narrative of continuity that links the community's present with its mythical origins. The Kayanid kings — Kay Kavad, Kay Khosrow, and others — are presented as righteous rulers who defended the faith and advanced the cause of good against evil.
