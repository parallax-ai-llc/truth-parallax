## Zoroastrian Reading

The creation of Gayomard, the primal human, establishes the Zoroastrian understanding of humanity's cosmic role. Gayomard is created as Ahura Mazda's chief ally in the battle against evil — a conscious being endowed with *xrad* (reason) and *fravashi* (pre-existent soul) who can choose to align with either good or evil. The Zoroastrian reader finds in Gayomard's story the origin of human moral responsibility and the basis of Zoroastrian ethics.
