## Zoroastrian Reading

The Bundahishn's final chapter describes the eternal state that follows the *frashokereti* — a perfected material world in which death, disease, aging, and all other effects of Angra Mainyu's assault have been permanently eliminated. The Zoroastrian reader finds in this vision the ultimate promise of the Good Religion: not an escape from the material world but its transfiguration into eternal goodness.
