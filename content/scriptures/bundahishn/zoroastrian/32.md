## Zoroastrian Reading

The Bundahishn describes the ongoing battle between good and evil in the natural world — in the struggle between beneficial and noxious animals, between fertile and barren land, between clean and polluted water. The Zoroastrian reader understands every natural phenomenon as a manifestation of the cosmic struggle and every act of cultivation, purification, and healing as participation in Ahura Mazda's cause.
