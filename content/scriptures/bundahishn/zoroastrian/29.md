## Zoroastrian Reading

The Bundahishn's classification of evil creatures (*khrafstars*) — snakes, scorpions, frogs, ants, and other noxious beings — reflects the Zoroastrian understanding that evil has manifest in specific material forms. These creatures are understood as the creations of Angra Mainyu, designed to corrupt and destroy the good creation. The faithful Zoroastrian's duty to destroy *khrafstars* is a practical expression of cosmic ethics.
