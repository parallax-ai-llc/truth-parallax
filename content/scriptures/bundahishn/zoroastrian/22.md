## Zoroastrian Reading

The Bundahishn's account of human faculties — reason (*xrad*), soul (*urvan*), pre-existent soul (*fravashi*), vital force (*jan*), and body (*tan*) — provides the Zoroastrian anthropological framework. The human being is understood as a complex unity of spiritual and material elements, each serving a specific function in the soul's journey through the cosmic struggle.
