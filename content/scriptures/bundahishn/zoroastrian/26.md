## Zoroastrian Reading

The genealogy of Zoroaster traces the prophet's lineage back to the primordial couple, establishing his place within the sacred history of creation. The Zoroastrian reader understands Zoroaster not as a historical accident but as a figure prepared by Ahura Mazda from the beginning of time — the culmination of a divine plan that spans the entire history of creation.
