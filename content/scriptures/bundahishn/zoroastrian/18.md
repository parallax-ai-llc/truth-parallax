## Zoroastrian Reading

The Bundahishn's classification of trees and plants reflects the Zoroastrian understanding that vegetation is a divine gift — one of Ahura Mazda's primary weapons against the barrenness and death brought by the Destructive Spirit. Each plant species has a cosmic purpose, and the cultivation of the earth is understood as a sacred act of participation in the divine creative work.
