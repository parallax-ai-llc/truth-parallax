## Zoroastrian Reading

The creation of plants represents the life force with which Ahura Mazda endowed the material world. The Bundahishn describes the primordial plant from which all vegetation derives, and the Zoroastrian reader understands agriculture and gardening as sacred acts — participation in Ahura Mazda's creative work against the barrenness and death introduced by Angra Mainyu.
