## Zoroastrian Reading

The Bundahishn's account of the stars and constellations reveals the Zoroastrian understanding of the celestial realm as a cosmic battlefield. The fixed stars, under the generalship of Tishtrya (Sirius), wage perpetual war against the planetary demons (*stargaran*) that seek to disrupt the cosmic order. The constellations are organized into a celestial army, each assigned a specific sector of the sky to defend against the forces of chaos.
