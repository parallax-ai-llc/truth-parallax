## Zoroastrian Reading

The Bundahishn provides remedies and protections against evil creatures, reflecting the Zoroastrian conviction that the faithful are not helpless before the forces of evil but are equipped by Ahura Mazda with effective means of resistance. Sacred prayers, ritual purity, and practical measures all serve as weapons in the daily battle against the Destructive Spirit's agents.
