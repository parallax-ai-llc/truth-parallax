## Zoroastrian Reading

The Bundahishn's classification of humanity by region and lineage traces all human diversity back to the primal couple, Mashya and Mashyana, who arose from the body of Gayomard. The Zoroastrian reader understands human diversity as a product of the cosmic plan — different peoples occupy different *karshvars* (regions) and serve different functions in the cosmic order, but all share the same divine origin and moral responsibility.
