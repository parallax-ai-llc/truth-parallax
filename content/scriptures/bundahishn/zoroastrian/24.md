## Zoroastrian Reading

The Bundahishn's calendar section describes the Zoroastrian religious year — its festivals (*gahanbars*), holy days, and the assignment of each day and month to the care of a specific divine being (*yazata*). The calendar is understood not as a human invention but as a reflection of cosmic order — each day a renewal of Ahura Mazda's creative act.
