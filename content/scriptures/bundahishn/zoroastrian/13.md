## Zoroastrian Reading

The moon's nature and cycles are described in relation to the cosmic struggle — its waxing representing the growth of good and its waning the temporary advance of evil. The Bundahishn's lunar cosmology reflects the Zoroastrian calendar's intimate connection with celestial phenomena and the conviction that astronomical regularity is a manifestation of divine order.
