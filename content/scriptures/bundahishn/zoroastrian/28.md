## Zoroastrian Reading

The Bundahishn's account of the faith's history through subsequent centuries traces the fortunes of Zoroastrianism through the Achaemenid, Parthian, and Sasanian periods — a narrative of preservation, challenge, and renewal that provides the community with a sense of historical identity and purpose.
