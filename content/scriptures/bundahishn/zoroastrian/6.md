## Zoroastrian Reading

The creation of the earth reveals the Zoroastrian understanding of geography as spiritually significant. The Bundahishn describes seven *karshvars* (regions) of the earth, with the central region (*Xwaniratha*) — identified with Iran — as the most important arena of the cosmic struggle. Mountains, valleys, and plains are understood as the result of Angra Mainyu's assault on the originally smooth and perfect earth.
