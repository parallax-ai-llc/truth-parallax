## Zoroastrian Reading

Water, the second material creation, is treasured in Zoroastrian thought as a sacred element associated with the divine being Aredvi Sura Anahita. The Bundahishn describes how Angra Mainyu attacked the waters, introducing salt and pollution, and how the waters continue to resist this corruption through their natural purifying action. The Zoroastrian reverence for water purity — reflected in elaborate ritual protections — is grounded in this cosmogonic teaching.
