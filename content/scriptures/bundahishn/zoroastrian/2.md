## Zoroastrian Reading

The assault of Angra Mainyu (the Destructive Spirit) upon the good creation is the pivotal event that sets the entire cosmic drama in motion. The Zoroastrian reader understands this assault not as a failure of divine planning but as a calculated risk — Ahura Mazda permitted the Destructive Spirit to enter the creation in order to trap evil within material boundaries where it can be ultimately defeated. The chapter reveals the strategic genius of Ahura Mazda's plan: creation itself is a weapon against evil.
