## Zoroastrian Reading

The creation of the sky as a crystalline shell enclosing the world provides the Zoroastrian cosmographic framework — a protective barrier that limits the destructive influence of the outer darkness and contains the arena of the cosmic battle. The sky's association with specific materials (stone, metal) and its defensive function reflect the Zoroastrian understanding of creation as a fortified space within which the forces of good wage their campaign against evil.
