## Zoroastrian Reading

The creation of the primal bull (*Gaw-i Ewag-dad*, the 'uniquely created bull') is one of the most distinctive elements of Zoroastrian cosmogony. The bull's death at the hands of Angra Mainyu — and the miraculous generation of all animal species from its body — is a foundational myth that expresses the Zoroastrian understanding of sacrifice and regeneration: from the destruction wrought by evil, good brings forth new life.
