## Zoroastrian Reading

The *frashokereti* (final renovation) is the Zoroastrian vision of the ultimate triumph of good over evil — the complete purification of creation and the restoration of the material world to its original perfection. The Bundahishn describes this event in vivid detail: the dead will rise, the mountains will melt, a river of molten metal will purify the earth, and evil will be destroyed forever.
