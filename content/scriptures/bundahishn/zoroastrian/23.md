## Zoroastrian Reading

The Bundahishn's account of the sacred fires (*atash*) describes their cosmic origin, their spiritual nature, and their ritual functions. The three great fires of Zoroastrian tradition — Farnbag, Gushnasp, and Burzen-Mihr — are described as divisions of the primordial fire created by Ahura Mazda, each associated with a social class and a sacred function.
