## Jewish Reading

The Jewish reader of the Bundahishn's historical narrative of reign of the legendary kings finds parallels with the biblical narrative of sacred history and the post-biblical Jewish tradition of *aggadah* (legendary narrative). Both traditions construct sacred histories that connect the present community with primordial origins and that understand historical events as the unfolding of divine purpose. The comparison illuminates the shared Near Eastern patterns of historical narrative that shaped both Jewish and Zoroastrian self-understanding.
