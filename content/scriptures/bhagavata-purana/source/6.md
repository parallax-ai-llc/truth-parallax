---
title: "Bhagavata Purana - Chapter 6"
scripture: "bhagavata-purana"
chapter: 6
---

**1** The Supreme Lord said: Under the supervision of the Supreme Lord and according to the result of their work, the living entities, the individual souls, are created, maintained, and annihilated again and again in the material world.

**2** My dear Uddhava, the material body is endowed with senses for perceiving the world. But the intelligent person does not identify the self with the body and its possessions.

**3** Because of ignorance the living entity identifies himself with the material body and thus becomes subject to birth and death, just as a man in a dream sees himself as having a head that is being cut off.

**4** As a man who has been sleeping and dreaming returns to his full consciousness upon awakening, so the soul returns to his true nature when freed from material identification.

**5** The living entity who has become free from attachment, fear, and anger, who is fully absorbed in Me, and who takes refuge in Me, is purified by the fire of knowledge and attains My nature.

**6** As a fire, kindled from wood, burns that very wood to ashes, so knowledge, kindled by devotion, burns all karmic reactions to ashes.

**7** For one who worships Me, giving up all other activities and dedicating all efforts to Me — for such a person, drowning in the ocean of birth and death, I am the swift deliverer.

**8** Fix your mind upon Me, the Supreme Lord, present in the heart of every living being. Offering obeisances and worshipping Me with devotion, you shall certainly come to Me.

**9** This I promise you, for you are My very dear friend. Surrender unto Me. I shall deliver you from all sins. Do not grieve.

**10** The devotee who knows Me as the Supreme Lord, as the friend of all living entities, and as the enjoyer of all sacrifices, attains peace from the pangs of material miseries.
