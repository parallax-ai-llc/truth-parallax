## Overview

Dvaita Vedanta reads the ninth Skandha as historical evidence for Vishnu's governance of the world through royal dynasties and divine descents. The genealogical records are taken as substantially historical, and the avatar narratives as descriptions of real divine interventions.

The Ambarisha episode is read as a straightforward demonstration of the supremacy of Vishnu's devotees over all other powers. Durvasa's yogic power, however great, is no match for Vishnu's Sudarshana chakra, which protects the true devotee. This narrative supports the Dvaita teaching that devotion to Vishnu surpasses all other spiritual attainments.

Madhvacharya's treatment of the Rama narrative in his Mahabharata Tatparya Nirnaya draws on the Bhagavata's account alongside the Ramayana, presenting Rama as a fully divine avatar whose every action serves Vishnu's cosmic purposes.

## Key Themes

- **Historical Governance**: Vishnu's real governance through real dynastic succession
- **Devotion's Supremacy**: The Ambarisha narrative proving bhakti's power over yogic attainment
- **Real Avatar Activity**: Rama's life as genuine divine intervention in human history
- **Taratamya in Kingship**: The hierarchical ranking of kings based on devotion to Vishnu
- **Sudarshana's Protection**: Vishnu's active, physical protection of his devotees

## Key Verses and Passages

### Ambarisha and Sudarshana

Madhvacharya reads the Sudarshana chakra's protection of Ambarisha as literal divine intervention — Vishnu's weapon physically pursues Durvasa, demonstrating the real power of Vishnu's grace to overcome even the greatest yogic achievements.

### Rama's Full Divinity

The Dvaita tradition insists on Rama's full divinity throughout his earthly life. His apparent sorrow over Sita's abduction is read as divine play, not genuine ignorance or suffering.

## Commentary Tradition

Dvaita commentary integrates the ninth Skandha's content with Madhvacharya's broader historical and theological framework, treating the dynastic narratives as part of the real cosmic history governed by Vishnu.
