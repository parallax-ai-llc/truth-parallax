---
title: "Book of Documents — The Announcement of King Wu"
scripture: "book-of-documents"
chapter: 5
---

**1** In the first month, on the day Jen-chen, the King came in the morning to the great assembly of the western capital.

**2** The King spoke to the following effect: Oh, how great is the decree of Heaven! And how dangerous! How is its renewal not to be feared!

**3** I have heard that the sovereign of Shang had a subject shrinking from great virtue, so that he was appointed to be a prince by the side of the sovereign.

**4** Heaven has rejected and put an end to the decree in favor of the great state of Yin. There are many former wise kings of Yin in Heaven, and the later kings and people here managed their decrees.

**5** But in the end, wise men were in hiding, and the people were in abject terror. He was not able to reform himself.

**6** Therefore Heaven searched and looked, and its decree lighted on King Wen, to terminate the mandate of Yin, and to receive the great appointment.

**7** The way of Heaven is not unchanging. On the good-doer it sends down all blessings, and on the evil-doer all miseries. Oh, be reverent!
