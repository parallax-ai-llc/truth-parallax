---
title: "Marsanes - Chapter 1"
scripture: "marsanes"
chapter: 1
---

**1** The discourse of Marsanes. He who speaks, having been taken up in vision, receiving revelation from the invisible spirit:

**2** The first level of knowledge is the material world, where forms are shaped and named. The letters of the alphabet correspond to the powers of the soul, and each letter has a power.

**3** The vowels correspond to the gods, and the consonants to the angels. The semi-vowels correspond to the powers that are between.

**4** The first shape is the round shape, which is the form of the cosmos. The second shape is the long shape. The third shape is the short shape. These three shapes correspond to the three levels of existence.

**5** He who knows the powers of the letters knows the powers of the soul. He who knows the powers of the soul knows the powers of the divine world.

**6** The first level is the corporeal, where the soul is bound in matter. The second level is the psychic, where the soul begins to awaken. The third level is the spiritual, where the soul is freed.

**7** There are thirteen seals of the invisible spirit, corresponding to the thirteen levels of ascent. Each seal must be broken in order for the soul to ascend.

**8** The first seal is the seal of the body. The second seal is the seal of desire. The third seal is the seal of anger. The fourth seal is the seal of ignorance.

**9** When these seals are broken, the soul ascends beyond the material world and enters the realm of the psychic powers.

**10** The shapes of the letters reveal the shapes of the soul in its various states. The round shape is the shape of the soul at rest. The long shape is the shape of the soul in motion. The short shape is the shape of the soul in contemplation.

**11** Know that the invisible spirit has placed these signs within the letters as a guide for those who seek the ascent.

**12** Thus Marsanes received the first revelation concerning the shapes and powers of the letters and the levels of the soul.
