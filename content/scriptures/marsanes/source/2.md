---
title: "Marsanes - Chapter 2"
scripture: "marsanes"
chapter: 2
---

**1** Concerning the ascent of the soul through the thirteen levels: the soul must pass through each aeon, receiving knowledge at each level.

**2** At the fifth level, the soul encounters the powers of the archons, who seek to prevent its ascent. But the soul that possesses knowledge passes through unharmed.

**3** At the sixth level, the soul encounters the realm of the Self-Generated One, where it receives the baptism of the spirit.

**4** At the seventh level, the soul enters the realm of the Triple-Powered One, where it is purified and made ready for the higher ascent.

**5** The eighth level is the level of Barbelo, the first thought of the invisible spirit, the perfect mind, the forethought of all.

**6** At the ninth level, the soul enters the realm of the truly existent ones, where all duality ceases and the soul knows itself as part of the All.

**7** The tenth level is the level of the repentance, where the soul turns wholly toward the light and is freed from the last traces of materiality.

**8** The eleventh level is the realm of the invisible spirit itself, where silence reigns and all names cease.

**9** The twelfth level is the Unknown One, concerning whom nothing can be said, for it is beyond speech and beyond thought.

**10** The thirteenth level is the level of the Unknown God who is above all aeons, who is before all things, who is beyond existence and non-existence.

**11** Those who attain the thirteenth level are united with the invisible spirit and know themselves as they truly are: emanations of the One, sparks of the divine light.

**12** This is the path of the ascent as revealed to Marsanes by the invisible spirit, that those who have ears may hear and those who have eyes may see.
