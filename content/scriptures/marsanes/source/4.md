---
title: "Marsanes - Chapter 4"
scripture: "marsanes"
chapter: 4
---

**1** Concerning the end of all things and the restoration of all souls: when the last soul has been awakened and has ascended to the divine world, the material world will be dissolved.

**2** For the material world exists only because souls have fallen into it. When all souls have returned to their source, there will be no need for the world of matter.

**3** The archons know this, and they strive to keep souls in ignorance, for when all souls have ascended, the power of the archons will be destroyed.

**4** But the invisible spirit is greater than all the archons, and the light is stronger than the darkness, and knowledge will prevail over ignorance.

**5** In the end, all things will be gathered back into the One — the Unknown God who is before all things. All emanations will return to their source, all sparks will be reunited with the flame.

**6** This is the promise of the invisible spirit to all souls: "You shall return to me. Though you have wandered far, though you have been lost in darkness, I have not forgotten you."

**7** "The light within you is my own light. The knowledge that awakens you is my own knowledge. When you know yourselves, you know me."

**8** And Marsanes, having received these revelations, was filled with joy and with peace. And he descended from the vision and spoke these things to those who had ears to hear.

**9** He said: "Brothers, do not be afraid of the darkness. The light is within you. Do not be troubled by the powers of this world. The invisible spirit is with you."

**10** "Seek knowledge, for knowledge is the path of ascent. Seek the baptism of the spirit, for it is the washing away of ignorance. Seek the company of the awakened ones, for they will guide you."

**11** "And when you have ascended through all the levels and passed through all the seals, you will know the truth: that you were never truly separated from the One."

**12** "For the One is all, and all is the One, and there is nothing outside the invisible spirit."

**13** This is the teaching of Marsanes, the prophet of the ascent, who received revelation from the invisible spirit and spoke it to the chosen ones. Peace be with all who hear these words and keep them.
