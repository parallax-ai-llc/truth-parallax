---
title: "Marsanes - Chapter 3"
scripture: "marsanes"
chapter: 3
---

**1** Concerning the nature of the soul and its relation to the divine world: the soul is a spark of the divine light that has fallen into the material world.

**2** The soul in its original state was one with the invisible spirit. But through a fall — through forgetfulness and ignorance — it became clothed in matter and forgot its true nature.

**3** The body is the garment of the soul, woven by the archons of this world. But the soul is not the body, just as the wearer is not the garment.

**4** The passions are the chains that bind the soul to the body. Desire, anger, fear, and ignorance — these are the four chains.

**5** When the soul awakens and remembers its divine origin, it begins to loosen these chains. Knowledge is the key that unlocks the prison of matter.

**6** The soul that has received knowledge says: "I am not this body. I am not these passions. I am a child of the light, a spark of the invisible spirit."

**7** And when the soul speaks these words with understanding, the chains fall away, and the archons of this world have no more power over it.

**8** The baptism of the spirit is the washing away of ignorance. The anointing is the restoration of the divine light. The sealing is the protection against the powers of darkness.

**9** He who has been baptized, anointed, and sealed shall ascend without hindrance through all the levels of the aeons.

**10** For the invisible spirit has placed within each soul a spark of its own light, and that spark cannot be extinguished, though it may be hidden beneath the ashes of ignorance.

**11** When the spark is fanned into flame by knowledge and remembrance, the soul blazes forth in its original glory and ascends to its place in the divine world.

**12** This is the mystery of salvation as taught by Marsanes: not the salvation of the body, but the salvation of the soul; not through works, but through knowledge; not through fear, but through love.
