## The Lesser Division

From the Mahayana perspective, this section on disciplinary procedures, the handling of schism, and accounts of the early Councils preserves important foundational teachings that the Mahayana tradition inherits and develops. Mahayana scholars identify in these early discourses the seeds of later philosophical developments in Madhyamaka and Yogachara thought.
