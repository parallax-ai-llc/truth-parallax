## The Great Division

Mahayana Buddhism engages with this teaching on ordination procedures, the uposatha ceremony, the rains retreat, and major communal practices through its parallel Agama traditions and recognizes it as foundational material upon which later Mahayana developments build. The emphasis on compassion, wisdom, and the welfare of all beings connects these early teachings to the bodhisattva ideal.
