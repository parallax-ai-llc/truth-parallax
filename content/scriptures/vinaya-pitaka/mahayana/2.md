## Rules for Nuns

From the Mahayana perspective, this section on the 311 rules of the Bhikkhuni Patimokkha governing the order of nuns preserves important foundational teachings that the Mahayana tradition inherits and develops. Mahayana scholars identify in these early discourses the seeds of later philosophical developments in Madhyamaka and Yogachara thought.
