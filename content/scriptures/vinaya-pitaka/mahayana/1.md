## Rules for Monks

Mahayana Buddhism engages with this teaching on the 227 rules of the Bhikkhu Patimokkha with their origin stories and case law through its parallel Agama traditions and recognizes it as foundational material upon which later Mahayana developments build. The emphasis on compassion, wisdom, and the welfare of all beings connects these early teachings to the bodhisattva ideal.
