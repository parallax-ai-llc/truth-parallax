## The Appendix

Mahayana Buddhism engages with this teaching on summaries, classificatory analyses, and reference guides for the entire Vinaya through its parallel Agama traditions and recognizes it as foundational material upon which later Mahayana developments build. The emphasis on compassion, wisdom, and the welfare of all beings connects these early teachings to the bodhisattva ideal.
