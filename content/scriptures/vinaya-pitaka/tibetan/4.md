## The Lesser Division

In the Tibetan scholastic tradition, this section on disciplinary procedures, the handling of schism, and accounts of the early Councils provides essential doctrinal foundations studied in the monastic curriculum. Tibetan scholars connect these teachings to the broader framework of Buddhist philosophy, from Abhidharma analysis through Madhyamaka and Yogachara to tantric practice.
