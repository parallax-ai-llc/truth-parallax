## The Great Division

Tibetan Buddhism engages with this teaching on ordination procedures, the uposatha ceremony, the rains retreat, and major communal practices as part of the foundational sutra vehicle upon which the complete Tibetan Buddhist path is constructed. These teachings inform the analytical meditation practices and the stages-of-the-path (lam-rim) literature that structures Tibetan Buddhist education.
