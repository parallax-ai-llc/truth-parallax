## The Great Division

Zen Buddhism finds in this teaching on ordination procedures, the uposatha ceremony, the rains retreat, and major communal practices a directness and practicality that resonates with Zen values. While Zen emphasizes experience beyond textual study, these teachings provide foundations for the mindful awareness and direct insight that are central to Zen practice.
