## Rules for Monks

Zen Buddhism finds in this teaching on the 227 rules of the Bhikkhu Patimokkha with their origin stories and case law a directness and practicality that resonates with Zen values. While Zen emphasizes experience beyond textual study, these teachings provide foundations for the mindful awareness and direct insight that are central to Zen practice.
