## The Lesser Division

This section on disciplinary procedures, the handling of schism, and accounts of the early Councils provides rich material for cross-traditional analysis. Comparative scholars examine how the Buddhist approach to these questions relates to and differs from the approaches of other major religious and philosophical traditions.
