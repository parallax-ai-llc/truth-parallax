## Rules for Monks

Comparative interpretation of this teaching on the 227 rules of the Bhikkhu Patimokkha with their origin stories and case law reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar teachings in Hindu, Jain, Christian, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Buddhist perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
