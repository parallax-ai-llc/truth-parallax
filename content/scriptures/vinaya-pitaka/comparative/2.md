## Rules for Nuns

This section on the 311 rules of the Bhikkhuni Patimokkha governing the order of nuns provides rich material for cross-traditional analysis. Comparative scholars examine how the Buddhist approach to these questions relates to and differs from the approaches of other major religious and philosophical traditions.
