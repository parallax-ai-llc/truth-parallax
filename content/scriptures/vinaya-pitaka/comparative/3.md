## The Great Division

Comparative interpretation of this teaching on ordination procedures, the uposatha ceremony, the rains retreat, and major communal practices reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar teachings in Hindu, Jain, Christian, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Buddhist perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
