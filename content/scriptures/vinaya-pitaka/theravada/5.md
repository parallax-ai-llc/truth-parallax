## The Appendix

The Theravada tradition regards this section on summaries, classificatory analyses, and reference guides for the entire Vinaya as essential canonical teaching. Theravada interpreters emphasize its direct relevance to meditation practice and the cultivation of wisdom, reading it as part of the complete path from morality through concentration to liberating insight.
