## Rules for Monks

The Theravada tradition regards this section on the 227 rules of the Bhikkhu Patimokkha with their origin stories and case law as essential canonical teaching. Theravada interpreters emphasize its direct relevance to meditation practice and the cultivation of wisdom, reading it as part of the complete path from morality through concentration to liberating insight.
