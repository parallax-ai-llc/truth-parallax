## Rules for Nuns

In the Theravada tradition, this teaching on the 311 rules of the Bhikkhuni Patimokkha governing the order of nuns holds a position of central importance for both doctrinal understanding and practical application. Monks and scholars study this section as part of the comprehensive training in the Buddha's teaching, connecting its insights to the daily practice of mindfulness and ethical conduct.
