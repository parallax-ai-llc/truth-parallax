## The Great Division

The Theravada tradition regards this section on ordination procedures, the uposatha ceremony, the rains retreat, and major communal practices as essential canonical teaching. Theravada interpreters emphasize its direct relevance to meditation practice and the cultivation of wisdom, reading it as part of the complete path from morality through concentration to liberating insight.
