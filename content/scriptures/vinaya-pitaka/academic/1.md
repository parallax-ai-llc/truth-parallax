## Rules for Monks

Academic scholars analyze this section on the 227 rules of the Bhikkhu Patimokkha with their origin stories and case law using the methods of textual criticism, historical contextualization, and comparative study. The text provides evidence for understanding the development of early Buddhist thought and its relationship to the broader intellectual culture of ancient India.
