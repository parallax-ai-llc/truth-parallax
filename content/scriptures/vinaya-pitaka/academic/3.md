## The Great Division

Academic scholars analyze this section on ordination procedures, the uposatha ceremony, the rains retreat, and major communal practices using the methods of textual criticism, historical contextualization, and comparative study. The text provides evidence for understanding the development of early Buddhist thought and its relationship to the broader intellectual culture of ancient India.
