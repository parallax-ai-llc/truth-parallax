## Rules for Nuns

From an academic perspective, this teaching on the 311 rules of the Bhikkhuni Patimokkha governing the order of nuns is examined for its compositional history, its relationship to parallel versions in other Buddhist canons, and its significance for reconstructing the teachings of the historical Buddha.
