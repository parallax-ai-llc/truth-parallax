## The Lesser Division

From an academic perspective, this teaching on disciplinary procedures, the handling of schism, and accounts of the early Councils is examined for its compositional history, its relationship to parallel versions in other Buddhist canons, and its significance for reconstructing the teachings of the historical Buddha.
