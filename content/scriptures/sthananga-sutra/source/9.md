---
title: "Sthananga Sutra - Chapter 9"
scripture: "sthananga-sutra"
chapter: 9
---

**1** There are 9 fundamental categories that the wise must understand on the path to liberation.

**2** There are 9 qualities that mark the true seeker of truth.

**3** There are 9 disciplines that a monk must master.

**4** There are 9 types of beings that inhabit the universe.

**5** There are 9 stages of spiritual development.

**6** There are 9 aspects of right conduct that must be cultivated.

**7** There are 9 objects of meditation that lead to purification.

**8** There are 9 forms of charity that bring great merit.

**9** There are 9 kinds of knowledge that lead to understanding.

**10** This is the teaching concerning nines, as declared by the venerable Mahavira.
