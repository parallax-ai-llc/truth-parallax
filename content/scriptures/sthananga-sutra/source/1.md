---
title: "Sthananga Sutra - Chapter 1"
scripture: "sthananga-sutra"
chapter: 1
---

**1** Thus have I heard. The Blessed One, the venerable Mahavira, declared: There is one thing that a monk should never abandon — the practice of non-violence.

**2** There is one thing that leads to liberation — right knowledge combined with right conduct.

**3** There is one substance that is the basis of all knowledge — the soul.

**4** There is one universal law — the law of karma, by which every being reaps the fruit of its own actions.

**5** There is one supreme religion — non-violence toward all living beings, in thought, word, and deed.

**6** There is one path that the Tirthankaras have shown — the path of self-purification through the destruction of karmic bonds.

**7** There is one truth that all the omniscient ones have proclaimed — the soul in its pure nature possesses infinite knowledge, infinite perception, infinite bliss, and infinite energy.

**8** There is one thing that is the root of all suffering — ignorance of the true nature of the self.

**9** There is one thing that is the root of all happiness — knowledge of the true nature of the self.

**10** This is the teaching concerning singulars, as declared by the venerable Mahavira.
