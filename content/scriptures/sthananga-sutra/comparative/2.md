The section's systematic pairing of concepts invites comparison with dualistic frameworks across traditions: Buddhist samsara-nirvana, Chinese yin-yang, Zoroastrian Ahura Mazda-Angra Mainyu, and Gnostic spirit-matter. Each tradition structures reality through fundamental polarities.
