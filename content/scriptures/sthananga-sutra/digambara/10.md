The Digambara tradition culminates the enumeration with the ten supreme virtues: supreme forgiveness, humility, uprightness, contentment, truthfulness, self-restraint, austerity, renunciation, non-attachment, and celibacy. These represent the complete moral ideal of the Digambara path.
