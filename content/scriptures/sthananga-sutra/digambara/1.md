The Digambara tradition reads this section's enumeration of singular concepts with doctrinal precision. Each 'unit of one' -- the one soul, the one liberation, the one truth -- is understood with characteristic Digambara strictness, emphasizing the absolute and uncompromising nature of these fundamental categories.
