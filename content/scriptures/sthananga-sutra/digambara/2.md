Digambara interpreters emphasize the fundamental duality of jiva and ajiva as the cornerstone of Jain metaphysics. The paired categories of bondage and liberation, virtue and vice, knowledge and ignorance are understood with the precision that characterizes Digambara philosophical analysis.
