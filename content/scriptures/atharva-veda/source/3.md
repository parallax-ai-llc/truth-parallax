---
title: "Atharva Veda - Chapter 3"
scripture: "atharva-veda"
chapter: 3
---

**1** May Agni, the lord of vows, unite our hearts. May the lord of nourishment unite our hearts. May the lord of waters unite our hearts. May Indra and Vayu unite our hearts. May the creator unite our hearts.

**2** Let the heavens be peaceful, let the atmosphere be peaceful, let the earth be peaceful. Let the waters flow peacefully, let the herbs and plants grow peacefully. Let all the gods bring me peace. Let Brahman be peaceful. Let everything be peaceful and peaceful only. May that peace, real peace, be mine.

**3** Om. May there be peace in heaven, may there be peace in the sky, may there be peace on earth. May the waters be peaceful, may the herbs be peaceful, may the trees be peaceful. May all the gods bring me peace, may Brahman be peaceful, may all things be peaceful. And may that peace come to me.

**4** The night has passed and the dawn has come. The sun, who illumines all, rises. Let me not fall into dispute with anyone. Let me not fall into dispute with thee, O Varuna. We invoke Mitra, we invoke Varuna. We invoke that which brings no conflict.

**5** Let the wind blow healing upon us. Let the sun shine healing upon us. Let the days pass healing upon us. Let the nights bring us healing. Let the rain fall healing upon us.

**6** May every creature look upon us with the eye of a friend. May we look upon every creature with the eye of a friend. May we look upon one another with the eye of a friend.

**7** Sweet be the earth to us, sweet be the air, sweet be the heaven, our father. May the trees be full of sweetness for us, may the sun be sweet, and may the cows yield us sweet milk.

**8** Vayu blows healing; the rivers pour healing. May the herbs be healing unto us. May night and dawn be healing, and may the dust of the earth be healing.

**9** Healing be both heaven and earth, healing be our father the sky. May the plants, the trees, the herbs — may they all pour healing upon us.

**10** May Indra give me healing; may the Asvins, the divine physicians, give me healing. May the plants and herbs give me healing. May the thunderbolt give me healing.
