---
title: "Amma and the Cosmic Egg"
scripture: "dogon-cosmogony"
chapter: 1
---

**1** In the beginning there was Amma, the supreme god, and nothing else existed. Amma was a round, cosmic egg, and within this egg were the seeds of all things.

**2** Amma vibrated seven times, and from these vibrations came the first seeds of creation. Within the egg were four elements: earth, water, fire, and air, and four cardinal directions.

**3** The egg contained two pairs of twins, each pair consisting of a male and a female. These were the Nommo, the first divine beings, the monitors of the universe.

**4** Amma began to create the universe by drawing signs in space. These signs were the first words, the first language, the foundation of all creation.

**5** The signs of Amma are written in the stars, in the patterns of the earth, in the markings on the tortoise shell. Those who can read the signs can understand the mind of Amma.
