---
title: "The Eight Ancestors"
scripture: "dogon-cosmogony"
chapter: 4
---

**1** From the Nommo came the eight ancestors of humanity. They were four pairs of twins, male and female, and from them descended all the peoples of the earth.

**2** The first ancestor was the master of speech. He received the first word from the Nommo and passed it to his descendants. Speech is the gift of the Nommo to humankind.

**3** The second ancestor was the master of weaving. From the Nommo he learned to weave cloth, and in the pattern of the weave is encoded the word of Amma.

**4** The third ancestor was the master of the granary. He built the first granary in the shape of the cosmic egg, with eight compartments for the eight seeds of creation.

**5** Each ancestor received a portion of the sacred knowledge. Together, the eight ancestors possessed the complete knowledge of creation. Separately, each possessed only a part.

**6** From these eight ancestors came the eight original families of the Dogon. Each family preserves the knowledge given to its ancestor. Together, the eight families preserve the complete knowledge.
