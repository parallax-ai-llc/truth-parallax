---
title: "The Sacrifice of Nommo"
scripture: "dogon-cosmogony"
chapter: 3
---

**1** To purify the earth that Ogo had defiled, Amma sacrificed the other male Nommo. The Nommo was killed, and his body was cut into pieces.

**2** The pieces of the Nommo were scattered throughout the universe. Each piece became a part of creation: his blood became the rain, his body became the trees and plants, his bones became the stones and mountains.

**3** From the sacrifice of the Nommo, the stars were arranged in the sky. The Sirius star system, which the Dogon call Sigi Tolo, was placed at the center of the sky-world.

**4** Around Sigi Tolo revolves a smaller, denser star, Po Tolo, which the Dogon say is the heaviest thing in the universe. Po Tolo completes its orbit every fifty years.

**5** After the sacrifice, Amma resurrected the Nommo. The Nommo was restored to life and descended to earth in an ark, bringing with him the seeds of all living things.

**6** The ark landed on the earth, and the Nommo organized the world. He taught the first humans the arts of civilization: agriculture, weaving, ironworking, and the sacred rituals.
