---
title: "Damascus Document Section 4"
scripture: "damascus-document"
chapter: 4
---

**6:11** The well is the Law, and those who dug it were the converts of Israel who went out of the land of Judah to sojourn in the land of Damascus.

**6:14** God called them all "princes" because they sought Him, and their renown was disputed by no man. The Staff is the Interpreter of the Law.

**6:19** And all the members of the Covenant who have transgressed the boundary of the Law, when the glory of God is revealed to Israel, they shall be cut off from the camp and with them all the wicked of Judah who did evil in the days of testing.

**7:4** And during all those years Belial shall be unleashed against Israel, as He spoke by the hand of Isaiah the Prophet, the son of Amoz, saying, "Terror and the pit and the snare are upon you, O inhabitant of the land."

**7:9** The interpretation: the three nets of Belial with which Levi son of Jacob said that he catches Israel by setting them up as three kinds of righteousness. The first is fornication, the second is riches, the third is profanation of the Temple.

**7:14** Whoever escapes the first is caught in the second, and whoever saves himself from the second is caught in the third.

**8:1** But all those who have been brought into the Covenant shall not enter the Temple to kindle fire on the altar in vain. They shall be the ones who "shut the door."

**8:4** And all who despise the commandments and the statutes shall have the reward of the wicked visited upon them, when God visits the land, when the word is fulfilled which was written among the words of Isaiah the Prophet.

**8:9** The Star is the Interpreter of the Law who shall come to Damascus; as it is written, "A star shall come out of Jacob and a scepter shall rise out of Israel."

**8:12** The scepter is the Prince of the whole congregation, and when he comes he shall smite all the children of Sheth. These escaped in the age of the first visitation, but those who turned back were delivered to the sword.
