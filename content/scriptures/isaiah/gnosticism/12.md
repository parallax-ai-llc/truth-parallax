## The Hymn of the Awakened

Isaiah 12's songs of praise are read by Gnostic interpreters as the hymns of those who have received *gnosis*. The *Hymn of the Pearl* in the *Acts of Thomas* provides a parallel: the awakened soul sings with joy upon recognizing its true identity and origin. Isaiah's doxology expresses the same ecstatic recognition.

## The Wells of Gnosis

"With joy you will draw water from the wells of salvation" (12:3). Gnostic soteriology identifies these wells with the sources of *gnosis* — the hidden teachings, the secret traditions, the mystical experiences that provide access to divine knowledge. Water is a common Gnostic symbol for spiritual knowledge: the *Gospel of Philip* speaks of "living water" that nourishes the pneumatic being.

## Proclamation of the Hidden Name

"Make known among the nations what he has done; proclaim that his name is exalted" (12:4). Gnostic interpretation reads "his name" as the secret divine name — the true name of the transcendent God, hidden from the archons and the Demiurge. The proclamation of this name among the nations is the dissemination of *gnosis* — the hidden knowledge that liberates the divine sparks.
