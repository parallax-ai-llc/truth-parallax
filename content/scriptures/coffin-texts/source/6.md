---
title: "Coffin Texts - Chapter 6"
scripture: "coffin-texts"
chapter: 6
---

**1** Spell for going forth by day in the realm of the dead. I have opened for myself every path that is in the sky and on the earth, for I am the well-favoured son of Osiris. I am noble, I am a spirit, I am equipped. O all you gods and all you spirits, make a way for me.

**2** I go in like the Hawk and I come forth like the Phoenix, the morning star of Re. I open up my path, and I drive away the darkness. I find the way, and I traverse the firmament.

**3** I am one who goes past the guards. The gatekeepers make way for me. I have traversed the house of darkness. I have appeared as one who is glorified. My face is open, my heart is upon its seat, and the crown of every hour is upon me.

**4** Spell for breathing air in the realm of the dead. O Atum, give me the sweet breath which is in your nostrils. I am the egg which is in the Great Cackler. I keep watch over that great place which Geb has spoken of. I live and it lives; I am hale and it is hale; I breathe air and it breathes air.

**5** I am he who splits the iron. I am he who traverses the sky. I have come upon the roads of the jackal-god. O you who are over the hours, open the ways for me. I am equipped with millions of years of life. My food is from the Eye of Horus.

**6** O you four winds of heaven, the north wind is for Osiris, the east wind is for the opening of the windows of the sky, the west wind is for the equipment of the spirit, the south wind is for the purification of passages. Give me the north wind, for I am the lord of air.

**7** Spell for not perishing and for being alive in the realm of the dead. I shall not perish. I shall enter in truth and I shall go forth in truth. The gods are about me, and I am about the gods. I sit among the great gods.

**8** I am the scribe of the divine book, who says what is and determines what is not. I am lord of life, the knotter of the cord. I have come into being, I have become glorious, I am strong, I am holy. I am equipped in my place, I am established upon my throne.
