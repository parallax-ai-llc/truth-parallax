---
title: "Coffin Texts - Chapter 18"
scripture: "coffin-texts"
chapter: 18
---

**1** O you who are alive upon the earth, who shall pass by this tomb, as you love life and hate death, so offer to me that which is in your hands.

**2** If nothing is in your hands, then speak the words: A thousand of bread, a thousand of beer, a thousand of oxen, a thousand of fowl, a thousand of all good and pure things for the spirit of the Osiris, justified.

**3** I am a spirit, I am equipped, I am a soul. I have come forth from the Duat. I have come that I may take possession of my throne and that I may receive my dignity.

**4** For I was the lord of it before there was given to me the status of a spirit and the status of one who is in the earth.

**5** I am the outflow of Osiris. I have come forth from the water. I have grown from it. I have rotted in it. I have fallen on my side in it, in accordance with the wish of the Great Outflower.

**6** The sky is given to me, the earth is given to me, the Field of Rushes is given to me. The two long regions and the two great regions fear me.

**7** I eat with my mouth, I chew with my jaws, for I am indeed the god who is lord of the Duat. I am given the things that endure.

**8** I have come forth by day. I have opened every path in the sky and upon the earth, for I am the well-equipped spirit. The place that I will is where my heart desires to be.
