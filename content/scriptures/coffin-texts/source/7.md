---
title: "Coffin Texts - Chapter 7"
scripture: "coffin-texts"
chapter: 7
---

**1** Spell for knowing the souls of the holy places. I know the souls of the East. They are Horakhty, the Kheper-beetle, and the Morning Star. I know the souls of Heliopolis. They are Re, Shu, and Tefnut.

**2** I know the souls of Hermopolis. They are Thoth, the moon, and the Ibis. I know the souls of Pe. They are Horus, Imsety, and Hapy. I know the souls of Nekhen. They are Horus, the Scorpion, and the One of the Brazier.

**3** I know the souls of the West. They are Atum, Sobek lord of Bakhu, and Hathor lady of the Evening. I know the souls of Buto. They are the heron and the dwarf. I know the secret of Hermopolis: it is the eight primordial gods.

**4** Spell for knowing the souls of the sacred cities. I know why the djed-pillar was raised in Busiris. It was because Osiris told Horus to look at what his father had suffered. And Horus tore open his Eye and gave it to Osiris. Then Osiris said to him: "Take this djed-pillar." And so the djed was raised.

**5** Spell for entering the great mansion. I enter the great mansion with my soul upon my head and my terror in my arms. They open a path for me. They shut the door after me. They say: "He is one of us."

**6** I know the field of reeds. Its wall is of iron. The barley there is five cubits high, with ears of two cubits and stalks of three cubits. The emmer there is seven cubits high, with ears of three cubits and stalks of four cubits. It is the blessed ones, each nine cubits tall, who reap them beside the Souls of the East.

**7** I know the road. I have travelled it in peace. It is the road to the place where Re is. I have entered and I have been given bread. I have been given beer. I have been given a portion of meat. I have sat down in the place of my heart's desire.

**8** O Osiris, I am your son Horus. I have come to you. I have brought you the Eye. I have found it complete, counted whole, that nothing was lacking from it. I give it to you complete, I add it to you as a whole, sound and complete before you.
