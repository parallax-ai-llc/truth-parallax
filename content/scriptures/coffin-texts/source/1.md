---
title: "Coffin Texts - Chapter 1"
scripture: "coffin-texts"
chapter: 1
---

**1** O Atum, this your son is here, Osiris, whom you have caused to live and to endure. He lives, and so this king lives; he does not die, and so this king does not die; he is not destroyed, and so this king is not destroyed.

**2** I have come to you, O my father, I have come to you, O Re. I have come to you, O my father, I have come to you, O Osiris. I am your son, I am Horus, I have come that I may purify you, that I may cleanse you, that I may revive you, that I may assemble for you your bones.

**3** The sky is given to you, the earth is given to you. The Field of Rushes is given to you, and the two great regions that are in awe of you. I have come to you, my father Osiris. I have opened your eyes for you. I am Horus, the avenger of his father.

**4** O Re, commend me to Mahaf, the ferryman of the Winding Waterway, so that he may bring me his ferryboat which belongs to the Winding Waterway, in which he ferries the gods to yonder side of the Winding Waterway, to the eastern side of the sky.

**5** I know you, I know your name. The name of the right-hand oar is "Finger of Horus." The name of the left-hand oar is "Hand of Isis." I am he whom the gods commanded to row, and I row without wearying.

**6** Raise yourself, O my father! Receive these your libations which I make for you. I have come to bring you the Eye of Horus, that your heart may be refreshed. I bring it to you under your sandals. Receive the efflux which issues from you.

**7** I am Horus. I have come to you that I may purify you and cleanse you, that I may make you live, that I may assemble for you your bones, that I may collect for you your swimming parts, that I may assemble for you your dismembered limbs.

**8** For I am Nut. I have come that I may enfold and protect you from all evil things. I have placed you within me. My arms are about you, my protection is over you, eternally and ever.
