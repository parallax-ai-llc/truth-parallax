---
title: "Coffin Texts - Chapter 9"
scripture: "coffin-texts"
chapter: 9
---

**1** Spell for going to the great god. I have gone to the great god, lord of heaven, lord of earth, lord of water, lord of the mountains, lord of the sea. I have brought him right and truth. I have driven out wickedness for him.

**2** I am one of those four gods who sit at the side of the great god, who sit on the eastern side of the sky, who judge the poor and the rich, who give satisfaction to the weary, who give air to him who is in need of it.

**3** The doors of the horizon are opened, its bolts are slid back. He has come to you, O Red Crown. He has come to you, O Great One. He has come to you, O Flame. He has come cleansed and purified, for he has driven out evil from himself for you.

**4** Spell for opening the tomb. The earth is hacked up. The offering is placed upon it. The earth of Geb is opened. I come forth with my head held high. Nut has opened the beautiful paths. The paths of the Jackal are opened for me. I am guided upon fair roads. I am a follower of Osiris.

**5** Spell for knowing the powers of the West. I know the powers of the West. I know Atum, I know Shu, I know Tefnut. I know Geb, I know Nut. I know Osiris, I know Isis, I know Nephthys. I know Horus the Elder, I know Horus the Child.

**6** I know the gates and I know the paths. I know the names of the gods who guard them. I am one of you, O gods who are in the following of the lord of right and truth. I have been purified. My front is washed, my back is cleansed, my interior is in the Pool of Truth.

**7** Spell for knowing the Eastern Souls. I know the gate of the field of rushes from which Re goes forth into the east of the sky. Its south is at the Lake of Waterfowl, its north is at the Waters of Geese, the place where Re navigates by wind and by rowing.

**8** I am the keeper of the halyard in the boat of the god. I am the oarsman who rows and does not tire in the barque of Re. I know that sycamore of turquoise from which Re goes forth. I know those two trees of turquoise between which Re goes forth, which have grown up at the Supports of Shu, at the door of the lord of the East, through which Re goes forth into the sky.
