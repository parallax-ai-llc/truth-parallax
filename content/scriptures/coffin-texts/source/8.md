---
title: "Coffin Texts - Chapter 8"
scripture: "coffin-texts"
chapter: 8
---

**1** Spell for the ferrying over. O you ferryman who ferry over the just who have no boat, the keeper of the Winding Waterway: I have come to you that you may ferry me over in this boat in which you ferry over the gods. I have come to the region of the great ones.

**2** I have come from the place of the Lion, I have come from the place of the Lynx, and the Lion has sent me and the Lynx has given me. I have come on account of the matter of right and truth. I bring it, I announce it. I am the messenger, I am sent.

**3** O Mahaf, bring me this, the ferryboat of the Winding Waterway. My brow is like the brow of Re. My face is like the face of Hathor. My plumes are like the plumes of Horus. I am arrayed in the regalia of the great ones.

**4** Spell for not walking upside-down. I am the plant of life which comes forth from Osiris, which grows upon the ribs of Osiris, which allows the people to live, which makes the gods divine, which spiritualizes the spirits. I am he who lives.

**5** I shall not walk upside-down for Shu, the possessor of the plumes, the son of Atum, who created order. I shall not be delivered to the fingers of him who would walk upside-down. My abomination is excrement. I shall not eat it. My detestation is filth. It shall not enter my body. I shall live on bread of white emmer, my beer shall be of red barley.

**6** Spell for bringing a boat in the realm of the dead. O you who bring, bring me this. I am he who made it. I am he who came forth from the Eye of Horus. I am he who closed the Eye of Horus at the right moment. I am equipped with his Eye.

**7** I am he whom the gods fear when they see the harpoon of the Eye of Horus gleaming. The sky reels, the earth quakes, Horus comes, Thoth appears. They raise me up from my left side and set me upon my right side. I have risen like Nefertem from the lotus to the nostrils of Re. I go forth by day and I appear among the gods.

**8** I am the seed-grain of every god, the beautiful one who comes forth from the lord of mankind. I am he whom the Sole Lord made before there existed two things in this land. I am the protection which he commanded. I am the knot of the god within the tamarisk. I am the one who worships that which is to come.
