---
title: "Coffin Texts - Chapter 3"
scripture: "coffin-texts"
chapter: 3
---

**1** Spell for becoming Tefnut. I am Tefnut, the moisture that came forth from Atum. I was not borne by a womb, I was spat forth. I and my brother Shu were created together in the primeval waters of Nun.

**2** The Eye of Atum was sent to seek us when we were lost in the dark waters. When it found us and brought us back, my father Atum wept, and from his tears mankind came into being.

**3** I am the moisture of the sky. I am she who pours out the rain and the dew. The plants grow green because of me. The fields flourish by my gifts. I am the breath of life in the nostrils of the living.

**4** Spell for not dying a second death. O you who preside over the hours, who go before Re, make a path for me, that I may pass by in the following of the great god. I am an equipped spirit; let me not be turned away from the gate of the Duat.

**5** I am he who was born before the flood, before the serpent and the worm came into being, before anything had been established. If I see the way, I walk upon it. If I find the barrier, I shall break through it. I have power, I have might.

**6** O you wardens of the ways, who guard the paths to Osiris, who bar the way to the approaches of Osiris, who draw the bolts of the earth by the command of Geb, listen to me, for I know you, I know your names.

**7** Let me pass by you, for I am a follower of Re. I go forth with the word of Atum. I am the serpent which came into being in the First Time. My food is what the gods live on. I will not be destroyed.

**8** My heart is not taken from me. I am sound, I am sound. My flesh has not decayed. I shall not be turned back. I shall go forth with my own legs. I am Atum, the creator. The gods shall rise for me, for I am the lord of life, the knotter of the cord.
