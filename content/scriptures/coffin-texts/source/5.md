---
title: "Coffin Texts - Chapter 5"
scripture: "coffin-texts"
chapter: 5
---

**1** Spell for not dying in the realm of the dead. I am the double Lion-god, older than all the gods. If I die, Osiris dies. If I live, Osiris lives. I am lord of the boundary. I keep watch over the borders of the sky.

**2** O you doors, O you who keep the doors because of Osiris, O you who guard them and who report the affairs of the Two Lands to Osiris every day, I know you and I know your names.

**3** I was conceived in the great flood and born in the great flood. My mother is Nut, my nurse is Nut. I have breathed in the breath of life. I shall not be destroyed in this land. I shall not die a second time.

**4** Spell for opening the way. O you gate-keepers, make way for me. I am one like you. I have come forth by day. I walk on my feet. I have the power of my own two legs. I am he who goes forth from the flame, the survivor of the fire.

**5** The road is opened for me by the openers of the way. The bolts are drawn back. The doors are thrown open for me by him who is in the booth. I walk, I am made to walk.

**6** I am the bull of the sky. My heart feeds on the existence of the great gods who are in the realm of the dead. I have passed by the judgment. The watchers on their thrones are content with me.

**7** I eat of that on which they eat. I drink of that which they drink. My bread is in the sky with Re, my bread is in the earth with Geb. The southern path and the northern path bring me bread from the fields.

**8** I am equipped, I am equipped, I am a spirit. I shall not be turned back. I shall not be repelled. My place is far from him who would harm me. I am Horus, the avenger, who emerged from the Eye of Horus. I have come, I have become glorious, I have become a soul, I have become mighty, I have become equipped. I am complete.
