---
title: "Coffin Texts - Chapter 4"
scripture: "coffin-texts"
chapter: 4
---

**1** Spell for becoming the god Atum. I am Atum, I who was alone in Nun. I am Re who appeared in glory, the ruler of that which he made. I am the great god who came into being of himself, who created his names, the lord of the Ennead. No god can withstand me.

**2** I am yesterday, and I know tomorrow. The battle-ground of the gods was made when I spoke. I know the name of the great god who is therein.

**3** I am that great Phoenix which is in Heliopolis, the examiner of what exists. What is it? It is Osiris. As for the examiner of what exists, it is eternity and everlastingness. Eternity is the day, everlastingness is the night.

**4** I am Min at his coming forth. I have set the two plumes upon my head. My mother Isis nursed me, and my nurse was Nephthys. She who suckled me was Sekhat-Hor. I have appeared as king. I have taken the White Crown.

**5** Spell for having power in the heart of a man in the realm of the dead. My heart is with me, and it shall not be taken away. I am the lord of hearts, the slayer of the heart's assailant. I live by right and truth, and I have my being therein.

**6** I am Horus, the dweller in hearts, the dweller in the body. I live by saying what is in my heart, and it shall not be taken from me. My heart obeys me, I am its master. It is in my body, it has not fallen to the will of any tyrannical judge.

**7** O you who seize hearts and tear out the heart of the Osiris, you shall not carry off my heart. For I am the lord of hearts, stout of heart, who lives by right and truth.

**8** Here I am, sound of heart, sound of breast, furnished with all the parts of the heart of a lion. I have come into being, I have become glorious, I have been initiated, I am a spirit. My heart is with me, and it shall never depart from me.
