## Overview

Hermetic interpretation of the Coffin Texts focuses on the democratization of esoteric knowledge — the moment when the secret wisdom of the royal priesthood began to be shared with a wider circle of initiates. This process prefigures the later Hermetic tradition's own tension between secrecy and transmission.

## Key Interpretive Approaches

### Democratized Gnosis
The extension of funerary knowledge beyond royalty is read as a paradigm for the dissemination of gnosis — salvific knowledge that was once restricted to an elite but becomes available to all sincere seekers.

### The Book of Two Ways as Initiatory Map
The Book of Two Ways is interpreted as an initiatory guide — a map of the spiritual journey that the initiate must navigate, with its two paths representing different approaches to divine knowledge.

### Transformation as Theurgic Practice
The transformation spells are understood as theurgic operations in which the practitioner assumes divine forms through ritual identification with the gods.
