title: "1 Enoch Chapter 30"
scripture: "book-of-enoch"
chapter: 30
---

**1** And beyond these, I went afar towards the east, and I saw another place, a valley full of water.

**2** And therein there was a tree, the colour of fragrant trees such as the mastic.

**3** And on the sides of those valleys I saw fragrant cinnamon. And beyond these I proceeded towards the east.
