---
title: "1 Enoch Chapter 16"
scripture: "book-of-enoch"
chapter: 16
---

**1** 'And now as to the Watchers who have sent thee to intercede for them, who had been aforetime in heaven, say to them:

**2** "You have been in heaven, but all the mysteries had not yet been revealed to you, and you knew worthless ones, and these in the hardness of your hearts you have made known to the women, and through these mysteries women and men work much evil on earth."

**3** Say to them therefore: "You have no peace."'
