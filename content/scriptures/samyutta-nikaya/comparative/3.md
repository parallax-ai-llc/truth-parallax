## Dependent Origination

Comparative interpretation of this teaching on the twelve links of dependent origination and the analysis of conditioned arising reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar teachings in Hindu, Jain, Christian, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Buddhist perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
