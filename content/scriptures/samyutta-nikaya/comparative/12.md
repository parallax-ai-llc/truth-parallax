## Stream-Entry and the Truths

This section on stream-entry, the four noble truths, and the stages of noble attainment provides rich material for cross-traditional analysis. Comparative scholars examine how the Buddhist approach to these questions relates to and differs from the approaches of other major religious and philosophical traditions.
