## Verses with Nuns and Brahmins

This section on verses and encounters with nuns, Brahmins, and royal figures provides rich material for cross-traditional analysis. Comparative scholars examine how the Buddhist approach to these questions relates to and differs from the approaches of other major religious and philosophical traditions.
