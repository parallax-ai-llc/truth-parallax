## Verses with Devas and Mara

Comparative interpretation of this teaching on verses exchanged with devas, Mara, and celestial beings reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar teachings in Hindu, Jain, Christian, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Buddhist perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
