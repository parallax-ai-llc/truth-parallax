## Powers and Faculties

Comparative interpretation of this teaching on the spiritual powers, faculties, and strengths that support the path to awakening reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar teachings in Hindu, Jain, Christian, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Buddhist perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
