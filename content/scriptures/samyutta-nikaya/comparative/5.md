## The Five Aggregates

Comparative interpretation of this teaching on the five aggregates of clinging and their impermanent, unsatisfactory nature reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar teachings in Hindu, Jain, Christian, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Buddhist perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
