## The Noble Eightfold Path

Comparative interpretation of this teaching on the Noble Eightfold Path as the complete way from suffering to liberation reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar teachings in Hindu, Jain, Christian, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Buddhist perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
