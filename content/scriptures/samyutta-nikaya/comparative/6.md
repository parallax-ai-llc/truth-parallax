## Non-Self and Clinging

This section on the teaching of non-self and the release from clinging to the aggregates provides rich material for cross-traditional analysis. Comparative scholars examine how the Buddhist approach to these questions relates to and differs from the approaches of other major religious and philosophical traditions.
