## The Six Sense Bases

Comparative interpretation of this teaching on the six sense bases as the arena of experience and the arising of perception reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar teachings in Hindu, Jain, Christian, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Buddhist perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
