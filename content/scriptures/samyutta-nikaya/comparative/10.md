## Mindfulness and Right Effort

This section on the four foundations of mindfulness and the practice of right effort provides rich material for cross-traditional analysis. Comparative scholars examine how the Buddhist approach to these questions relates to and differs from the approaches of other major religious and philosophical traditions.
