## Verses with Nuns and Brahmins

From the Mahayana perspective, this section on verses and encounters with nuns, Brahmins, and royal figures preserves important foundational teachings that the Mahayana tradition inherits and develops. Mahayana scholars identify in these early discourses the seeds of later philosophical developments in Madhyamaka and Yogachara thought.
