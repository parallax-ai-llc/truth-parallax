## The Noble Eightfold Path

Mahayana Buddhism engages with this teaching on the Noble Eightfold Path as the complete way from suffering to liberation through its parallel Agama traditions and recognizes it as foundational material upon which later Mahayana developments build. The emphasis on compassion, wisdom, and the welfare of all beings connects these early teachings to the bodhisattva ideal.
