## Contact and Craving

From the Mahayana perspective, this section on how contact through the senses gives rise to feeling, craving, and suffering preserves important foundational teachings that the Mahayana tradition inherits and develops. Mahayana scholars identify in these early discourses the seeds of later philosophical developments in Madhyamaka and Yogachara thought.
