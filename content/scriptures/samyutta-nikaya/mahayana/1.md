## Verses with Devas and Mara

Mahayana Buddhism engages with this teaching on verses exchanged with devas, Mara, and celestial beings through its parallel Agama traditions and recognizes it as foundational material upon which later Mahayana developments build. The emphasis on compassion, wisdom, and the welfare of all beings connects these early teachings to the bodhisattva ideal.
