## Non-Self and Clinging

From the Mahayana perspective, this section on the teaching of non-self and the release from clinging to the aggregates preserves important foundational teachings that the Mahayana tradition inherits and develops. Mahayana scholars identify in these early discourses the seeds of later philosophical developments in Madhyamaka and Yogachara thought.
