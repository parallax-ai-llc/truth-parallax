## The Six Sense Bases

Mahayana Buddhism engages with this teaching on the six sense bases as the arena of experience and the arising of perception through its parallel Agama traditions and recognizes it as foundational material upon which later Mahayana developments build. The emphasis on compassion, wisdom, and the welfare of all beings connects these early teachings to the bodhisattva ideal.
