## Stream-Entry and the Truths

From the Mahayana perspective, this section on stream-entry, the four noble truths, and the stages of noble attainment preserves important foundational teachings that the Mahayana tradition inherits and develops. Mahayana scholars identify in these early discourses the seeds of later philosophical developments in Madhyamaka and Yogachara thought.
