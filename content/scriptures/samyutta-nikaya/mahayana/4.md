## Elements and Feeling

From the Mahayana perspective, this section on the elements of experience, the nature of feeling, and the chain of causation preserves important foundational teachings that the Mahayana tradition inherits and develops. Mahayana scholars identify in these early discourses the seeds of later philosophical developments in Madhyamaka and Yogachara thought.
