## Mindfulness and Right Effort

From the Mahayana perspective, this section on the four foundations of mindfulness and the practice of right effort preserves important foundational teachings that the Mahayana tradition inherits and develops. Mahayana scholars identify in these early discourses the seeds of later philosophical developments in Madhyamaka and Yogachara thought.
