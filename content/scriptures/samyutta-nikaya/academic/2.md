## Verses with Nuns and Brahmins

From an academic perspective, this teaching on verses and encounters with nuns, Brahmins, and royal figures is examined for its compositional history, its relationship to parallel versions in other Buddhist canons, and its significance for reconstructing the teachings of the historical Buddha.
