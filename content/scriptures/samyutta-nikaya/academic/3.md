## Dependent Origination

Academic scholars analyze this section on the twelve links of dependent origination and the analysis of conditioned arising using the methods of textual criticism, historical contextualization, and comparative study. The text provides evidence for understanding the development of early Buddhist thought and its relationship to the broader intellectual culture of ancient India.
