## Non-Self and Clinging

From an academic perspective, this teaching on the teaching of non-self and the release from clinging to the aggregates is examined for its compositional history, its relationship to parallel versions in other Buddhist canons, and its significance for reconstructing the teachings of the historical Buddha.
