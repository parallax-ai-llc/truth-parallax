## The Six Sense Bases

Academic scholars analyze this section on the six sense bases as the arena of experience and the arising of perception using the methods of textual criticism, historical contextualization, and comparative study. The text provides evidence for understanding the development of early Buddhist thought and its relationship to the broader intellectual culture of ancient India.
