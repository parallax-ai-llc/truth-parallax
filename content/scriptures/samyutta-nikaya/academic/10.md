## Mindfulness and Right Effort

From an academic perspective, this teaching on the four foundations of mindfulness and the practice of right effort is examined for its compositional history, its relationship to parallel versions in other Buddhist canons, and its significance for reconstructing the teachings of the historical Buddha.
