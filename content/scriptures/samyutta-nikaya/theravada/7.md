## The Six Sense Bases

The Theravada tradition regards this section on the six sense bases as the arena of experience and the arising of perception as essential canonical teaching. Theravada interpreters emphasize its direct relevance to meditation practice and the cultivation of wisdom, reading it as part of the complete path from morality through concentration to liberating insight.
