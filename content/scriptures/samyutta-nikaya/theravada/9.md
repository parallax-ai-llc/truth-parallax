## The Noble Eightfold Path

The Theravada tradition regards this section on the Noble Eightfold Path as the complete way from suffering to liberation as essential canonical teaching. Theravada interpreters emphasize its direct relevance to meditation practice and the cultivation of wisdom, reading it as part of the complete path from morality through concentration to liberating insight.
