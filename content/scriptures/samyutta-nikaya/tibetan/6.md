## Non-Self and Clinging

In the Tibetan scholastic tradition, this section on the teaching of non-self and the release from clinging to the aggregates provides essential doctrinal foundations studied in the monastic curriculum. Tibetan scholars connect these teachings to the broader framework of Buddhist philosophy, from Abhidharma analysis through Madhyamaka and Yogachara to tantric practice.
