## Elements and Feeling

In the Tibetan scholastic tradition, this section on the elements of experience, the nature of feeling, and the chain of causation provides essential doctrinal foundations studied in the monastic curriculum. Tibetan scholars connect these teachings to the broader framework of Buddhist philosophy, from Abhidharma analysis through Madhyamaka and Yogachara to tantric practice.
