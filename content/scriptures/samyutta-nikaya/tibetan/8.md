## Contact and Craving

In the Tibetan scholastic tradition, this section on how contact through the senses gives rise to feeling, craving, and suffering provides essential doctrinal foundations studied in the monastic curriculum. Tibetan scholars connect these teachings to the broader framework of Buddhist philosophy, from Abhidharma analysis through Madhyamaka and Yogachara to tantric practice.
