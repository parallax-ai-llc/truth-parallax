## The Five Aggregates

Tibetan Buddhism engages with this teaching on the five aggregates of clinging and their impermanent, unsatisfactory nature as part of the foundational sutra vehicle upon which the complete Tibetan Buddhist path is constructed. These teachings inform the analytical meditation practices and the stages-of-the-path (lam-rim) literature that structures Tibetan Buddhist education.
