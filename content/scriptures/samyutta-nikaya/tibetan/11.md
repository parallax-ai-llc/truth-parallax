## Powers and Faculties

Tibetan Buddhism engages with this teaching on the spiritual powers, faculties, and strengths that support the path to awakening as part of the foundational sutra vehicle upon which the complete Tibetan Buddhist path is constructed. These teachings inform the analytical meditation practices and the stages-of-the-path (lam-rim) literature that structures Tibetan Buddhist education.
