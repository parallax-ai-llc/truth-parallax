## Overview

Tibetan Buddhism regards the Samyutta Nikaya's doctrinal content as foundational for the entire Buddhist philosophical tradition. The twelve-link chain of dependent origination, as analyzed in the Connected Discourses, is a central topic of study in Tibetan monastic education.

## Key Themes

Tibetan scholars emphasize the Samyutta Nikaya's dependent origination teachings as the basis for Nagarjuna's Madhyamaka philosophy and the Prasangika analysis of emptiness. The collection's analysis of the five aggregates informs the Tibetan Buddhist understanding of personal identity and its deconstruction.

## Relevance to Tibetan Study

The twelve links of dependent origination, as taught in the Samyutta Nikaya, are depicted on the Tibetan Wheel of Life and form a core topic in the Tibetan lam-rim curriculum.
