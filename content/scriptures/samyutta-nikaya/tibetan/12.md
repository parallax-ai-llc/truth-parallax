## Stream-Entry and the Truths

In the Tibetan scholastic tradition, this section on stream-entry, the four noble truths, and the stages of noble attainment provides essential doctrinal foundations studied in the monastic curriculum. Tibetan scholars connect these teachings to the broader framework of Buddhist philosophy, from Abhidharma analysis through Madhyamaka and Yogachara to tantric practice.
