## The Six Sense Bases

Tibetan Buddhism engages with this teaching on the six sense bases as the arena of experience and the arising of perception as part of the foundational sutra vehicle upon which the complete Tibetan Buddhist path is constructed. These teachings inform the analytical meditation practices and the stages-of-the-path (lam-rim) literature that structures Tibetan Buddhist education.
