## Verses with Devas and Mara

Zen Buddhism finds in this teaching on verses exchanged with devas, Mara, and celestial beings a directness and practicality that resonates with Zen values. While Zen emphasizes experience beyond textual study, these teachings provide foundations for the mindful awareness and direct insight that are central to Zen practice.
