## Non-Self and Clinging

Zen practitioners appreciate this section on the teaching of non-self and the release from clinging to the aggregates for its emphasis on direct experience and practical application. The teaching's focus on immediate awareness and the transformation of consciousness aligns with the Zen aim of awakening to one's true nature in this very moment.
