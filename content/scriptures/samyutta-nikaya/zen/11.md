## Powers and Faculties

Zen Buddhism finds in this teaching on the spiritual powers, faculties, and strengths that support the path to awakening a directness and practicality that resonates with Zen values. While Zen emphasizes experience beyond textual study, these teachings provide foundations for the mindful awareness and direct insight that are central to Zen practice.
