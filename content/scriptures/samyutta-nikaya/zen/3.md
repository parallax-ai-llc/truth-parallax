## Dependent Origination

Zen Buddhism finds in this teaching on the twelve links of dependent origination and the analysis of conditioned arising a directness and practicality that resonates with Zen values. While Zen emphasizes experience beyond textual study, these teachings provide foundations for the mindful awareness and direct insight that are central to Zen practice.
