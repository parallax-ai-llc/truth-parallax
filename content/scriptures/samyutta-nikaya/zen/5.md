## The Five Aggregates

Zen Buddhism finds in this teaching on the five aggregates of clinging and their impermanent, unsatisfactory nature a directness and practicality that resonates with Zen values. While Zen emphasizes experience beyond textual study, these teachings provide foundations for the mindful awareness and direct insight that are central to Zen practice.
