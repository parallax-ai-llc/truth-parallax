## Verses with Nuns and Brahmins

Zen practitioners appreciate this section on verses and encounters with nuns, Brahmins, and royal figures for its emphasis on direct experience and practical application. The teaching's focus on immediate awareness and the transformation of consciousness aligns with the Zen aim of awakening to one's true nature in this very moment.
