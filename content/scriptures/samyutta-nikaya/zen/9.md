## The Noble Eightfold Path

Zen Buddhism finds in this teaching on the Noble Eightfold Path as the complete way from suffering to liberation a directness and practicality that resonates with Zen values. While Zen emphasizes experience beyond textual study, these teachings provide foundations for the mindful awareness and direct insight that are central to Zen practice.
