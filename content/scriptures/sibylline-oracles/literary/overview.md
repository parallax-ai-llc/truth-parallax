## Overview

The Literary reading of the Sibylline Oracles attends to the texts' sophisticated literary strategies: the adoption of the Sibylline persona, the use of Greek hexameter verse, the deployment of vaticinia ex eventu (prophecy after the fact), and the blending of mythological, historical, and apocalyptic modes. The collection represents a remarkable case of cross-cultural literary ventriloquism.
