## Overview

The Philosophical reading of the Sibylline Oracles engages with the texts' implicit claims about divine knowledge, the moral structure of history, and the relationship between prophecy and human freedom. The Oracles present a vision of history as divinely ordered and morally meaningful, in which empires rise and fall according to their adherence to justice and proper worship.
