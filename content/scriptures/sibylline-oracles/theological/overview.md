## Overview

The Theological reading of the Sibylline Oracles examines the collection's development of Jewish and Christian theological themes: the oneness of God, the coming judgment, messianic expectation, and the ultimate vindication of the righteous. The Oracles represent an important witness to the development of eschatological thought from Second Temple Judaism through early Christianity.
