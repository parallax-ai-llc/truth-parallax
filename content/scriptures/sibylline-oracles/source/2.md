---
title: "Sibylline Oracles - Chapter 2"
scripture: "sibylline-oracles"
chapter: 2
---

**1** And then God shall send from the land of the sunrise a king, who shall make every land cease from evil war.

**2** To some he shall bring peace, to others war. But in all things he shall do not his own counsels, but obey the good counsels of the great God.

**3** And the temple of the great God shall be laden with exceeding beautiful wealth, with gold and silver and purple ornaments.

**4** And the fruitful earth shall bear yet more abundant fruits, many and sweet. And the cities shall be full of good things, and the fields shall be fat.

**5** And there shall be no sword throughout the land nor battle din, nor shall the earth groan heavily any more, nor shall there be war, nor drought throughout the land, nor famine, nor hail to waste the fruits.

**6** But there shall be a great peace throughout all the earth, and king shall be friend to king until the end of the age.

**7** And the immortal God who dwells in heaven shall establish a common law for all men throughout all the earth.

**8** And then shall all the sons of the great God live peacefully around the temple, rejoicing in those things which the Creator, righteous Judge and sole Ruler, shall give them.

**9** For He alone shall protect them, standing beside them in the night and in the early morning, giving reverence to this great God.

**10** And they shall offer sacrifice upon the great altar of God, making burnt offerings of fat flesh of bulls, choice rams, firstborn sheep, and great fat offerings of lambs.

**11** And then the peoples of the great God, made strong with wealth, shall be as a crown of beauty and shall live in peace.

**12** O blessed shall those mortals be upon the earth who shall live to see that day among the days of the great God, blessed above all things.
