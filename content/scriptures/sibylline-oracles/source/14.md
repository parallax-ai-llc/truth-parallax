---
title: "Sibylline Oracles - Chapter 14"
scripture: "sibylline-oracles"
chapter: 14
---

**1** Thus speaks the Sibyl with unerring tongue, declaring things revealed by God most high.

**2** Hear now, O nations, what the Lord declares through mortal lips unto all the earth.

**3** For God who thunders from the heights of heaven commands that I should prophesy to men.

**4** Woe unto those who turn aside from righteousness and walk in crooked paths of wickedness.

**5** But blessed are the righteous who keep the commandments of the Most High God forever.

**6** The time shall come when heaven and earth shall pass, yet not one word of God shall fail.

**7** Turn now, O mortals, from your evil ways, and cleanse your hearts before the living God.

**8** For there is one God, maker of all things, who measures out the rain and wind and fire.

**9** He sits enthroned above the starry vault and looks upon the deeds of mortal men.

**10** The earth shall tremble and the mountains quake when God arises in His wrath to judge.

**11** But unto those who love the truth and walk in paths of justice, He gives life eternal.

**12** Thus ends the oracle, the words of God delivered through the Sibyl, His instrument.
