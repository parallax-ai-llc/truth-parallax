---
title: "Sibylline Oracles - Chapter 4"
scripture: "sibylline-oracles"
chapter: 4
---

**1** Hear ye, O people of proud Asia and of Europe, how many things by my many-toned utterance I am about to proclaim.

**2** Prophesying from the mouth of God, things true and things that shall yet come to pass for all mortals.

**3** God did not reveal all things at once to those of earlier times, but in due course, for He is the beginning and the end.

**4** Then shall come great woe to all mortals upon the earth, and among all nations shall arise confusion.

**5** There shall come a time when the great broad river Euphrates shall be stained with blood because of the multitudes of the slain.

**6** And fire shall rain from heaven upon the earth, and great signs shall there be in every land.

**7** The sun shall prophesy by showing itself darkened at midday, and the rays of the moon shall become visible.

**8** Earth shall give signs: dripping with blood shall the rocks give their warning, and a great column of fire men shall see in heaven.

**9** Then shall God who dwells on high roll up the heaven as a scroll is rolled, and the whole firmament in its varied forms shall fall upon the divine earth and on the sea.

**10** And then shall flow a ceaseless stream of blazing fire, burning earth, burning sea, burning sky, burning air. And the whole world shall melt into one clean thing and become pure.

**11** And then shall all the spirits of men, gnashing with their teeth, be burned. And they shall know that the judgment of God is true.

**12** O wretched mortals, change these things! Do not lead the great God to all manner of anger. Lay aside swords and groanings, murders and outrages, and wash your whole bodies in the rivers that never fail.
