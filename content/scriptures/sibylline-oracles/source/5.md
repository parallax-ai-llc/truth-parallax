---
title: "Sibylline Oracles - Chapter 5"
scripture: "sibylline-oracles"
chapter: 5
---

**1** But come, hear me recount a mournful tale of the line of kings. After the first kingdom and the reign of the Assyrians comes the rule of the Medes.

**2** And after them the Persians shall hold dominion, and then the Macedonians. And the power of Rome shall rule over all.

**3** An evil time shall come for the land of Sodom and for Gomorrha, and for the cities round about them.

**4** And then God shall send from heaven His judgment, and there shall be sulphur and fire and brimstone, and stones and hail.

**5** And in that time a great star shall come from heaven into the terrible ocean and shall burn up the deep sea, and Babylon, and the land of Italy, by reason of which many saints of the Hebrews perished and the truth was no more among them.

**6** But when the fated time of the phoenix shall come upon the earth, there shall come one who shall rule, having a sign — a man carrying war among the peoples.

**7** Woe to thee, city of the Latin land, unclean in all things. Viper-filled, thou shalt sit a widow, and the years shall waste thee.

**8** O dark and gloomy, no more for thee shall the light of day shine, nor shall there be honour to thee. Gone art thou, thou wretched one.

**9** But when the end of all things on earth shall come, and the last day, God in heaven shall judge all things, both the pious and the impious.

**10** And He shall cast the impious into the darkness, and they shall know how great was their impiety. But the pious shall still live on the earth, God giving them at once both breath and grace and life.

**11** And they shall all see each other, beholding the sun's sweet light that never sets, and blessed shall that man be who shall live in those days.

**12** For from the starry heaven all blessedness shall come upon them, one and all. The judgment of God shall never fail. Blessed is the man who shall live to see those things.
