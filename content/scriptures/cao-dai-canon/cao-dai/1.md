## Cao Dai Reading

The Cao Dai community receives the Divine Messages (*Thánh Ngôn*) as the direct communication of the Supreme Being to humanity for the Third Era of salvation. These messages, received through *cơ bút* (spirit writing) séances conducted between 1925 and 1934, constitute the theological heart of the Cao Dai Canon. The Supreme Being speaks in the first person, identifying Himself as the one God who has been known by many names — Cao Đài, God, Allah, Brahman — and who now reveals the final, unified truth.

The Divine Messages establish the central doctrines of Cao Dai: the unity of all religions, the reality of the spirit world, the law of karma and reincarnation, and the divine plan for universal salvation. Each message was received under carefully controlled ritual conditions and authenticated by the community's spiritual leaders before being incorporated into the canon. The Cao Dai faithful study these messages as the Supreme Being's personal instruction to each believer.
