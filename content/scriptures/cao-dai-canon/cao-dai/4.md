## Cao Dai Reading

The New Canonical Codes (*Tân Luật*) provide the updated regulations governing the ethical, social, and religious life of the Cao Dai community. These codes address matters ranging from personal morality and family life to communal governance and the responsibilities of the clergy, establishing a comprehensive legal framework for the Cao Dai faithful.

The community understands the New Canonical Codes as the practical application of the Supreme Being's teaching to the concrete circumstances of daily life. The codes reflect the Cao Dai commitment to moral excellence — prescribing vegetarianism for senior adherents, prohibiting alcohol and opium, requiring charitable giving, and establishing standards for family life that reflect both Confucian and Buddhist moral traditions.
