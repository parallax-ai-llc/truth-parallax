## Interfaith Reading

The interfaith reading of the Divine Messages highlights their radical ecumenism — the Supreme Being's explicit declaration that all religions are divine in origin and that the Third Era of salvation will unite them into a single Great Way. This teaching represents one of the strongest statements of religious unity in any scripture, moving beyond mere tolerance or mutual respect to affirm the fundamental identity of all genuine religious paths.

Interfaith practitioners find in the Divine Messages a prophetic challenge to exclusivism in all traditions. The Supreme Being's claim to have revealed through Moses, the Buddha, Jesus, Muhammad, Laozi, and Confucius asserts a divine perspective that transcends the boundaries drawn by human religious institutions. The messages invite dialogue about the possibility of a meta-religious truth that encompasses and fulfills all particular traditions.
