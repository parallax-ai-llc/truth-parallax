## Academic Reading

Academic study of the Prayers and Rituals section examines the Cao Dai liturgy as a remarkable example of ritual syncretism. Scholars analyze how the prayer texts draw upon Buddhist mantras, Taoist invocations, Confucian moral maxims, and Catholic prayer forms, weaving them into a unified liturgical structure that functions coherently within Cao Dai worship.

The ritual calendar — incorporating lunar observances from Vietnamese folk tradition, Buddhist festival days, and commemorations of Cao Dai saints — demonstrates the complexity of syncretic liturgical construction. Scholars note that the prayers and rituals are not merely juxtaposed but integrated through the overarching theological framework of the Supreme Being's universal revelation.
