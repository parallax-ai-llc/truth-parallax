## Buddhist-Vietnamese Reading

The Buddhist-Vietnamese reading of the Prayers and Rituals section identifies substantial Buddhist liturgical elements within the Cao Dai worship system. The use of mantras, meditation techniques, and the concept of merit-making through devotional practice all reflect the deep influence of Vietnamese Buddhist worship on Cao Dai liturgy.

Buddhist-Vietnamese scholars note that Cao Dai prayers incorporate adapted versions of Buddhist sutras and invocations, particularly from the Pure Land and Thiền traditions. The ritual emphasis on purification, compassion, and spiritual cultivation resonates with Buddhist devotional ideals while being redirected toward the Supreme Being as the ultimate object of worship.
