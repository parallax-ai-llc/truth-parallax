---
title: "Cao Dai Canon Section 4"
scripture: "cao-dai-canon"
chapter: 4
---

**1** The Supreme Being declared: "In this Third Amnesty, I unite all previous revelations. The truth that was given in parts shall now be given whole.

**2** The Left Eye represents the Yang, the active principle. The Right Eye represents the Yin, the passive principle. But the Divine Eye, the Eye of Cao Dai, represents the unity of both, the supreme principle that transcends all duality.

**3** My children, do not quarrel over names and forms. Whether you call Me God, Allah, Brahman, the Tao, or the Supreme Being, I am One. The names differ but the reality is the same.

**4** The souls of Victor Hugo, Sun Yat-sen, Trang Trinh, and many other spirits have spoken through the corbeille to confirm this teaching and to guide the faithful.

**5** Love one another as I love you all. Treat every person as a brother or sister, regardless of race or creed. For all souls come from Me and to Me all souls shall return.

**6** Practice charity, prayer, and meditation. Purify your hearts. Free yourselves from the attachments of the material world. And when the time comes, your soul shall ascend through the spheres of heaven and return to its source in the Divine.

**7** I am Cao Dai. I am the Supreme Being. I am the Father of all. My grace is infinite. My love is eternal. Follow My way, and you shall find peace."
