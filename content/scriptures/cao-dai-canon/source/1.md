---
title: "Cao Dai Canon Section 1"
scripture: "cao-dai-canon"
chapter: 1
---

**1** The Supreme Being, Cao Dai, the Most High, the Creator of all beings, has sent His divine message to the world through the mediumistic séances at the Holy See in Tay Ninh.

**2** Cao Dai declared: "In the past, I sent Buddha, Confucius, Jesus Christ, and many other prophets to guide humanity. But men distorted their teachings through division and dogma.

**3** Now I come Myself, using this new means of communication, to establish the Third Universal Redemption, uniting all faiths into one great religion of truth.

**4** The First Redemption was given through Moses and the prophets. The Second Redemption was given through Christ, Buddha, and Muhammad. The Third Redemption I bring Myself, for in the Third Amnesty, I shall reveal all truths that were hidden.

**5** I am the Father of all souls. All religions are from Me. I do not make distinctions between races, between religions, between the noble and the common. All are My children."

**6** Thus the Supreme Being established the Great Way of the Third Amnesty, uniting Confucianism, Taoism, Buddhism, Christianity, and Islam under one divine truth.
