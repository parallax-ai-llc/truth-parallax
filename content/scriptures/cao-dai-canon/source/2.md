---
title: "Cao Dai Canon Section 2"
scripture: "cao-dai-canon"
chapter: 2
---

**1** The Supreme Being taught: "The universe is governed by the law of cause and effect. Every soul progresses through many incarnations, ascending or descending according to its merits.

**2** The human soul passes through many lifetimes, in many forms, learning the lessons of existence. Through virtuous conduct, the soul ascends. Through vice, it descends.

**3** The highest goal is to return to Me, to unite the individual soul with the universal soul, from which it first emanated.

**4** To achieve this goal, the devotee must practice the Five Prohibitions: do not kill, do not steal, do not commit adultery, do not become intoxicated, do not speak falsely.

**5** The devotee must also practice the Five Virtues: humanity, righteousness, propriety, wisdom, and faithfulness. These are the foundations of moral conduct.

**6** Furthermore, the devotee should practice vegetarianism at least ten days each month, for the killing of living creatures creates karmic debts that impede the soul's progress.

**7** Prayer and meditation are essential. The devotee shall pray four times daily: at six in the morning, at noon, at six in the evening, and at midnight."
