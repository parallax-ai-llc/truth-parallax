## Sunni Reading

The Sunni reading grounds Sa'di's praise of contentment in Qur'anic teaching and Prophetic *hadith*. The Qur'an's assurance that God provides sustenance (*rizq*) to all creation undergirds Sa'di's tales of those who found peace by accepting their portion. The Prophet's statement that 'richness is not in having many possessions but richness is the richness of the soul' provides the theological foundation for the contentment Sa'di celebrates. Sunni scholars use this chapter to teach the virtue of moderation (*wasatiyya*) — the middle path between miserliness and extravagance that characterizes the balanced Islamic life.
