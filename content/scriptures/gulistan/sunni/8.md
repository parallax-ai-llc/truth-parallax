## Sunni Reading

The Sunni reading of Sa'di's final chapter finds in his practical maxims a literary expression of Islamic *adab* — the comprehensive system of refined conduct that governs social relations in the Muslim community. Sa'di's counsel on friendship, hospitality, speech, and generosity aligns with the Prophetic model of social ethics and the Sunni juristic tradition of *mu'ashara* (social dealings). The chapter serves as a capstone for the Gulistan's moral project, demonstrating that Islamic ethics is not confined to ritual practice but extends to every dimension of human interaction.
