## Shia Reading

The Shia reading of Sa'di's dervish tales is informed by the tradition of *irfan* (gnosis) — the Shia mystical tradition that traces its spiritual authority through the Imams to the Prophet. The authentic dervishes in Sa'di's stories embody qualities attributed to the Imams: inner purity, detachment from worldly power, and a direct relationship with divine truth. The fraudulent dervishes whom Sa'di satirizes represent those who claim spiritual authority without the legitimacy that comes through the chain of *walaya* (spiritual guardianship) anchored in the Ahl al-Bayt.
