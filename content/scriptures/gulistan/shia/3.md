## Shia Reading

The Shia reader finds in Sa'di's praise of contentment an echo of Imam Husayn's supreme example of *rida* — acceptance of God's will even unto martyrdom at Karbala. Contentment in the Shia reading is not passive resignation but active alignment with divine justice, even when that alignment demands the sacrifice of worldly comfort and safety. Sa'di's stories of those who chose inner freedom over external wealth resonate with the Shia reverence for the Ahl al-Bayt, who consistently chose spiritual integrity over political compromise.
