## Shia Reading

The Shia tradition reads Sa'di's chapter on silence through the distinctive Shia concept of *taqiyya* — the prudent concealment of one's true beliefs in situations of danger. Sa'di's stories of the disasters caused by unguarded speech resonate with the historical Shia experience of persecution, where careless words could bring destruction upon the entire community. Imam Ja'far al-Sadiq's teaching that 'our matter is a secret within a secret' provides the theological context for the Shia appreciation of Sa'di's wisdom on the power and protection of silence.
