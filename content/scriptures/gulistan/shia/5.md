## Shia Reading

The Shia reading of Sa'di's love poetry draws on the rich tradition of *irfan* in which love (*ishq*) for the divine is expressed through the imagery of earthly beauty. In the Shia mystical tradition, the Beloved is often identified with the Imams, whose spiritual beauty reflects the divine light (*nur*) transmitted through the Prophet's lineage. Sa'di's celebration of beauty and his anguish at separation find deep resonance in Shia devotional culture, where longing for the Hidden Imam and grief over the martyrs of Karbala constitute a powerful emotional and spiritual current.
