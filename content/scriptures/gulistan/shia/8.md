## Shia Reading

The Shia reading of Sa'di's practical maxims connects them to the ethical teachings of Imam Ali as recorded in the Nahj al-Balagha and other Shia sources. Sa'di's counsel on generosity, truthfulness, and social responsibility aligns with the Alid ethical tradition that sees every human interaction as an opportunity to embody divine justice. The Gulistan's closing chapter, in the Shia reading, serves as a practical guide for living the values that the Imams taught and exemplified — a handbook for transforming the ideals of *walaya* into the daily conduct of the faithful.
