## Overview

The Hávamál is one of the most intensively studied poems in the Old Norse corpus. Academic scholarship examines the poem's composite structure, seeking to identify the originally independent poems that were combined into the extant text. Philological analysis focuses on the Old Norse language, ljóðaháttr meter, and textual transmission. Scholars debate the dating of individual sections, the relationship between oral tradition and written composition, and the degree to which the wisdom sayings reflect actual pre-Christian Norse ethical thought.
