## Overview

The Hávamál is a masterpiece of Old Norse wisdom poetry, employing the ljóðaháttr meter characteristic of instructional verse. Its literary artistry lies in the compression and memorability of its gnomic stanzas, the dramatic power of the Rúnatal narrative, and the skillful integration of diverse poetic forms into a unified whole attributed to the voice of Odin. Literary analysis examines the poem's imagery, structural patterns, rhetorical strategies, and its relationship to the broader tradition of Germanic and world wisdom poetry.
