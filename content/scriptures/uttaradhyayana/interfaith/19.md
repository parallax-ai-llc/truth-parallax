## The Nature of Karma

Interfaith interpretation finds in this teaching on the mechanics and nature of karmic bondage resonances with the concerns of multiple religious traditions. The text addresses universal human questions about meaning, morality, and liberation that transcend sectarian boundaries and invite constructive dialogue.
