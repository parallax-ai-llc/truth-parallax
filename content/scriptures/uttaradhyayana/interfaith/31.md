## The Parable of the Lotus

Interfaith interpretation finds in this teaching on the parable of the lotus and spiritual purity resonances with the concerns of multiple religious traditions. The text addresses universal human questions about meaning, morality, and liberation that transcend sectarian boundaries and invite constructive dialogue.
