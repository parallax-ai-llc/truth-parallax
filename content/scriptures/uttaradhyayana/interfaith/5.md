## The Rarity of Human Birth

Interfaith interpretation finds in this teaching on the precious rarity of human birth resonances with the concerns of multiple religious traditions. The text addresses universal human questions about meaning, morality, and liberation that transcend sectarian boundaries and invite constructive dialogue.
