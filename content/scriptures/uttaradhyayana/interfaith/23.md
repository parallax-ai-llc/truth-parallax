## The Realms of Existence

Interfaith interpretation finds in this teaching on the different realms of existence and their inhabitants resonances with the concerns of multiple religious traditions. The text addresses universal human questions about meaning, morality, and liberation that transcend sectarian boundaries and invite constructive dialogue.
