## The Story of King Nami

Interfaith interpretation finds in this teaching on King Nami's renunciation of royal power resonances with the concerns of multiple religious traditions. The text addresses universal human questions about meaning, morality, and liberation that transcend sectarian boundaries and invite constructive dialogue.
