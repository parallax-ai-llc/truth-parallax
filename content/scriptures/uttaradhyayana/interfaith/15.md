## On Celibacy

Interfaith interpretation finds in this teaching on celibacy and the purification of desire resonances with the concerns of multiple religious traditions. The text addresses universal human questions about meaning, morality, and liberation that transcend sectarian boundaries and invite constructive dialogue.
