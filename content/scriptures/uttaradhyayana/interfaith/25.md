## On Right Conduct

Interfaith interpretation finds in this teaching on right conduct as the practical expression of wisdom resonances with the concerns of multiple religious traditions. The text addresses universal human questions about meaning, morality, and liberation that transcend sectarian boundaries and invite constructive dialogue.
