## On the Soul

Interfaith interpretation finds in this teaching on the nature of the soul and its inherent perfection resonances with the concerns of multiple religious traditions. The text addresses universal human questions about meaning, morality, and liberation that transcend sectarian boundaries and invite constructive dialogue.
