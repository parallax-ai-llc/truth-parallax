## On the Ten Virtues

Interfaith interpretation finds in this teaching on the ten supreme virtues of the Jain path resonances with the concerns of multiple religious traditions. The text addresses universal human questions about meaning, morality, and liberation that transcend sectarian boundaries and invite constructive dialogue.
