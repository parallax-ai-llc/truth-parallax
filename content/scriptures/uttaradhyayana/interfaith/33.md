## On Equanimity

Interfaith interpretation finds in this teaching on equanimity and balanced awareness in all circumstances resonances with the concerns of multiple religious traditions. The text addresses universal human questions about meaning, morality, and liberation that transcend sectarian boundaries and invite constructive dialogue.
