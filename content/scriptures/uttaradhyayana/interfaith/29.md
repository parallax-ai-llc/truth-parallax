## The Path to Liberation

Interfaith interpretation finds in this teaching on the complete path from bondage to liberation resonances with the concerns of multiple religious traditions. The text addresses universal human questions about meaning, morality, and liberation that transcend sectarian boundaries and invite constructive dialogue.
