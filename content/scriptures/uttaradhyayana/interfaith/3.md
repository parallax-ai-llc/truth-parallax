## The Four Requisites

Interfaith interpretation finds in this teaching on the four requisites of the monastic life resonances with the concerns of multiple religious traditions. The text addresses universal human questions about meaning, morality, and liberation that transcend sectarian boundaries and invite constructive dialogue.
