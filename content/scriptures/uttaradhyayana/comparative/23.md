## The Realms of Existence

Comparative analysis of this teaching on the different realms of existence and their inhabitants reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar discussions in Buddhist, Hindu, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Jain perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
