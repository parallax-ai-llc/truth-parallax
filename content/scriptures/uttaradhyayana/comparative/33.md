## On Equanimity

Comparative analysis of this teaching on equanimity and balanced awareness in all circumstances reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar discussions in Buddhist, Hindu, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Jain perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
