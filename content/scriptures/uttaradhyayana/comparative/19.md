## The Nature of Karma

Comparative analysis of this teaching on the mechanics and nature of karmic bondage reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar discussions in Buddhist, Hindu, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Jain perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
