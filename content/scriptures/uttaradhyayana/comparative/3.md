## The Four Requisites

Comparative analysis of this teaching on the four requisites of the monastic life reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar discussions in Buddhist, Hindu, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Jain perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
