## On Bondage

From a philosophical standpoint, this section on the causes and conditions of spiritual bondage presents arguments and positions that engage with perennial questions in metaphysics, epistemology, and moral philosophy. The Jain approach offers distinctive solutions to problems debated across philosophical traditions.
