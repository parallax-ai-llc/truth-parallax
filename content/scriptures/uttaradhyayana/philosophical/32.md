## The Last Instructions

From a philosophical standpoint, this section on Mahavira's last instructions to his disciples presents arguments and positions that engage with perennial questions in metaphysics, epistemology, and moral philosophy. The Jain approach offers distinctive solutions to problems debated across philosophical traditions.
