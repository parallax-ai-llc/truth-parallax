## On Knowledge

From a philosophical standpoint, this section on types of knowledge and the possibility of omniscience presents arguments and positions that engage with perennial questions in metaphysics, epistemology, and moral philosophy. The Jain approach offers distinctive solutions to problems debated across philosophical traditions.
