## The Liberated Soul

From a philosophical standpoint, this section on the nature and bliss of the liberated soul presents arguments and positions that engage with perennial questions in metaphysics, epistemology, and moral philosophy. The Jain approach offers distinctive solutions to problems debated across philosophical traditions.
