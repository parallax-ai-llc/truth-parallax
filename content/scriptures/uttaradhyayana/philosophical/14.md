## On Non-Stealing

From a philosophical standpoint, this section on non-stealing and the integrity of the ascetic presents arguments and positions that engage with perennial questions in metaphysics, epistemology, and moral philosophy. The Jain approach offers distinctive solutions to problems debated across philosophical traditions.
