## The Leaf of the Tree

From a philosophical standpoint, this section on the parable of the leaf and its teaching on transience presents arguments and positions that engage with perennial questions in metaphysics, epistemology, and moral philosophy. The Jain approach offers distinctive solutions to problems debated across philosophical traditions.
