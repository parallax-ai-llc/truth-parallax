## The True Monk

From a philosophical standpoint, this section on the qualities and conduct of the true monk presents arguments and positions that engage with perennial questions in metaphysics, epistemology, and moral philosophy. The Jain approach offers distinctive solutions to problems debated across philosophical traditions.
