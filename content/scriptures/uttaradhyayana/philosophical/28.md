## On Discipline

From a philosophical standpoint, this section on discipline and self-control in monastic life presents arguments and positions that engage with perennial questions in metaphysics, epistemology, and moral philosophy. The Jain approach offers distinctive solutions to problems debated across philosophical traditions.
