## On Non-Violence

Philosophical interpretation examines this teaching on non-violence as the supreme ethical principle as a contribution to systematic philosophy. The text raises fundamental questions about the nature of reality, knowledge, and ethics that remain relevant to contemporary philosophical discussion.
