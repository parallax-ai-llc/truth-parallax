## The Great Departure

From a philosophical standpoint, this section on the great departure into nirvana and eternal freedom presents arguments and positions that engage with perennial questions in metaphysics, epistemology, and moral philosophy. The Jain approach offers distinctive solutions to problems debated across philosophical traditions.
