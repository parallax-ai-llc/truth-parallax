## On Non-Possession

From a philosophical standpoint, this section on non-possession and freedom from material attachment presents arguments and positions that engage with perennial questions in metaphysics, epistemology, and moral philosophy. The Jain approach offers distinctive solutions to problems debated across philosophical traditions.
