## The Story of Rohini

Shvetambara scholars read this section on the narrative of Rohini and its spiritual lessons as authoritative canonical teaching that guides both monastic and lay practice. The tradition's inclusive approach ensures that these teachings remain relevant and applicable to the entire Jain community.
