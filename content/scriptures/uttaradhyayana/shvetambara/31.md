## The Parable of the Lotus

The Shvetambara tradition approaches this teaching on the parable of the lotus and spiritual purity within the context of its broader Agamic canon, reading it as part of the comprehensive teaching transmitted from Mahavira through his disciples. Shvetambara interpreters emphasize both the rigor of the teaching and its accessibility to sincere practitioners at all levels of commitment.
