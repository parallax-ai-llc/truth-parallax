## The Leaf of the Tree

From the Digambara perspective, this teaching on the parable of the leaf and its teaching on transience reinforces the tradition's emphasis on uncompromising spiritual discipline. The Digambara commentarial tradition elaborates these teachings with philosophical precision, connecting them to the broader framework of strict karma theory and the path of total renunciation.
