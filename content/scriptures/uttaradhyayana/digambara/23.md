## The Realms of Existence

The Digambara tradition reads this the different realms of existence and their inhabitants with characteristic doctrinal strictness, emphasizing the necessity of complete renunciation and the precise application of Jain principles. Digambara interpreters stress that the different realms of existence and their inhabitants must be understood within the framework of absolute non-attachment and rigorous ascetic practice that defines the Digambara path.
