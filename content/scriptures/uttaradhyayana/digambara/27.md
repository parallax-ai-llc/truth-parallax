## On Austerity

The Digambara tradition reads this austerity as a means of karmic purification with characteristic doctrinal strictness, emphasizing the necessity of complete renunciation and the precise application of Jain principles. Digambara interpreters stress that austerity as a means of karmic purification must be understood within the framework of absolute non-attachment and rigorous ascetic practice that defines the Digambara path.
