## On the Ten Virtues

The Digambara tradition reads this the ten supreme virtues of the Jain path with characteristic doctrinal strictness, emphasizing the necessity of complete renunciation and the precise application of Jain principles. Digambara interpreters stress that the ten supreme virtues of the Jain path must be understood within the framework of absolute non-attachment and rigorous ascetic practice that defines the Digambara path.
