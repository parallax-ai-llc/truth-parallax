## On Equanimity

The Digambara tradition reads this equanimity and balanced awareness in all circumstances with characteristic doctrinal strictness, emphasizing the necessity of complete renunciation and the precise application of Jain principles. Digambara interpreters stress that equanimity and balanced awareness in all circumstances must be understood within the framework of absolute non-attachment and rigorous ascetic practice that defines the Digambara path.
