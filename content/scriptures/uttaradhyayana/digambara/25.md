## On Right Conduct

The Digambara tradition reads this right conduct as the practical expression of wisdom with characteristic doctrinal strictness, emphasizing the necessity of complete renunciation and the precise application of Jain principles. Digambara interpreters stress that right conduct as the practical expression of wisdom must be understood within the framework of absolute non-attachment and rigorous ascetic practice that defines the Digambara path.
