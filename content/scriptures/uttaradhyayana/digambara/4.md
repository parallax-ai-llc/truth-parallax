## Righteousness

From the Digambara perspective, this teaching on the nature of righteousness and dharmic duty reinforces the tradition's emphasis on uncompromising spiritual discipline. The Digambara commentarial tradition elaborates these teachings with philosophical precision, connecting them to the broader framework of strict karma theory and the path of total renunciation.
