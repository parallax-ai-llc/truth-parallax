## The Path to Liberation

The Digambara tradition reads this the complete path from bondage to liberation with characteristic doctrinal strictness, emphasizing the necessity of complete renunciation and the precise application of Jain principles. Digambara interpreters stress that the complete path from bondage to liberation must be understood within the framework of absolute non-attachment and rigorous ascetic practice that defines the Digambara path.
