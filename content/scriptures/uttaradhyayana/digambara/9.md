## The Story of King Nami

The Digambara tradition reads this King Nami's renunciation of royal power with characteristic doctrinal strictness, emphasizing the necessity of complete renunciation and the precise application of Jain principles. Digambara interpreters stress that King Nami's renunciation of royal power must be understood within the framework of absolute non-attachment and rigorous ascetic practice that defines the Digambara path.
