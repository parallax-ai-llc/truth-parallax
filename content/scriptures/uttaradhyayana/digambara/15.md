## On Celibacy

The Digambara tradition reads this celibacy and the purification of desire with characteristic doctrinal strictness, emphasizing the necessity of complete renunciation and the precise application of Jain principles. Digambara interpreters stress that celibacy and the purification of desire must be understood within the framework of absolute non-attachment and rigorous ascetic practice that defines the Digambara path.
