## The Nature of Karma

The Digambara tradition reads this the mechanics and nature of karmic bondage with characteristic doctrinal strictness, emphasizing the necessity of complete renunciation and the precise application of Jain principles. Digambara interpreters stress that the mechanics and nature of karmic bondage must be understood within the framework of absolute non-attachment and rigorous ascetic practice that defines the Digambara path.
