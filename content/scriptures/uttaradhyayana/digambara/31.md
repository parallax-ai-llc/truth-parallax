## The Parable of the Lotus

The Digambara tradition reads this the parable of the lotus and spiritual purity with characteristic doctrinal strictness, emphasizing the necessity of complete renunciation and the precise application of Jain principles. Digambara interpreters stress that the parable of the lotus and spiritual purity must be understood within the framework of absolute non-attachment and rigorous ascetic practice that defines the Digambara path.
