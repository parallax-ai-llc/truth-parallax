## Harikeshi

From the Digambara perspective, this teaching on Harikeshi's story of endurance and unwavering faith reinforces the tradition's emphasis on uncompromising spiritual discipline. The Digambara commentarial tradition elaborates these teachings with philosophical precision, connecting them to the broader framework of strict karma theory and the path of total renunciation.
