## The Story of Sthulabhadra

The Digambara tradition reads this Sthulabhadra's exemplary renunciation with characteristic doctrinal strictness, emphasizing the necessity of complete renunciation and the precise application of Jain principles. Digambara interpreters stress that Sthulabhadra's exemplary renunciation must be understood within the framework of absolute non-attachment and rigorous ascetic practice that defines the Digambara path.
