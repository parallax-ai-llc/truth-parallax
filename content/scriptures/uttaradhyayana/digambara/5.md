## The Rarity of Human Birth

The Digambara tradition reads this the precious rarity of human birth with characteristic doctrinal strictness, emphasizing the necessity of complete renunciation and the precise application of Jain principles. Digambara interpreters stress that the precious rarity of human birth must be understood within the framework of absolute non-attachment and rigorous ascetic practice that defines the Digambara path.
