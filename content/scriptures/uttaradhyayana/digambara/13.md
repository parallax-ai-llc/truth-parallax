## On Truthfulness

The Digambara tradition reads this truthfulness in thought, word, and deed with characteristic doctrinal strictness, emphasizing the necessity of complete renunciation and the precise application of Jain principles. Digambara interpreters stress that truthfulness in thought, word, and deed must be understood within the framework of absolute non-attachment and rigorous ascetic practice that defines the Digambara path.
