## On the Soul

The Digambara tradition reads this the nature of the soul and its inherent perfection with characteristic doctrinal strictness, emphasizing the necessity of complete renunciation and the precise application of Jain principles. Digambara interpreters stress that the nature of the soul and its inherent perfection must be understood within the framework of absolute non-attachment and rigorous ascetic practice that defines the Digambara path.
