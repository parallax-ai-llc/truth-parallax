## The Four Requisites

The Digambara tradition reads this the four requisites of the monastic life with characteristic doctrinal strictness, emphasizing the necessity of complete renunciation and the precise application of Jain principles. Digambara interpreters stress that the four requisites of the monastic life must be understood within the framework of absolute non-attachment and rigorous ascetic practice that defines the Digambara path.
