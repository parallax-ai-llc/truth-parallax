## On Impermanence

The Digambara tradition reads this impermanence and the urgency of spiritual practice with characteristic doctrinal strictness, emphasizing the necessity of complete renunciation and the precise application of Jain principles. Digambara interpreters stress that impermanence and the urgency of spiritual practice must be understood within the framework of absolute non-attachment and rigorous ascetic practice that defines the Digambara path.
