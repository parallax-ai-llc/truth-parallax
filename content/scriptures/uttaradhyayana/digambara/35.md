## Mahavira's Final Days

The Digambara tradition reads this Mahavira's final days before liberation with characteristic doctrinal strictness, emphasizing the necessity of complete renunciation and the precise application of Jain principles. Digambara interpreters stress that Mahavira's final days before liberation must be understood within the framework of absolute non-attachment and rigorous ascetic practice that defines the Digambara path.
