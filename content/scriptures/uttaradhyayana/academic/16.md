## On Non-Possession

From an academic perspective, this teaching on non-possession and freedom from material attachment is examined for its historical context, compositional layers, and relationship to parallel discussions in Buddhist and Hindu literature. Scholars note both the distinctive Jain contributions and the shared intellectual culture of ancient Indian philosophical debate.
