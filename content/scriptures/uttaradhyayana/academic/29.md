## The Path to Liberation

Academic scholars analyze this section on the complete path from bondage to liberation using the tools of textual criticism, historical contextualization, and comparative analysis. The text provides evidence for understanding the development of Jain thought and its place within the broader intellectual landscape of ancient India.
