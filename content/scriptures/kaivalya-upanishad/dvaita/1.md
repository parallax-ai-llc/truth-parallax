Madhva reads Section 1 as Brahma transmitting Vishnu's teaching on liberation. The path to kaivalya involves renunciation, meditation, and above all, devotion to the Supreme Lord. The instruction to meditate on the Lord in the heart is not a technique for dissolving the self but for intensifying the devotee's awareness of God's presence.

The teaching that ritual alone does not lead to liberation does not negate ritual for Dvaita but establishes that ritual must be accompanied by knowledge and devotion. The path to kaivalya is the path of *bhakti* — loving devotion that culminates in God's gracious gift of liberation.
