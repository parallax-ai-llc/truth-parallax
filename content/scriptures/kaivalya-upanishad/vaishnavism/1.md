Section 1 presents Brahma's instruction to the sage Ashvalayana on the path to *kaivalya* — supreme liberation. Brahma teaches that liberation is attained not through ritual action, wealth, or progeny but through renunciation and knowledge. Vaishnava commentators note that even Brahma, the creator god, teaches as a servant of the Supreme Lord, transmitting knowledge that ultimately comes from Vishnu.

The meditation instructions — concentration on the Lord dwelling in the heart, beyond darkness, brighter than the sun — are read as describing meditation on Vishnu as the inner light. The path to kaivalya runs through devotion, knowledge, and the grace of God.
