---
title: "On the Origin of the World - Chapter 1"
scripture: "on-the-origin-of-world"
chapter: 1
---

**1** Seeing that everybody, gods of the world and mankind, says that nothing existed prior to chaos, I in distinction to them shall demonstrate that they are all mistaken, because they are not acquainted with the origin of chaos, nor with its root.

**2** If it is agreed by all men concerning chaos that it is a kind of darkness, then it is something derived from a shadow. And it was called darkness. But the shadow is something derived from a work existing from the beginning.

**3** So it is obvious that it existed before chaos came into being, and that the latter is posterior to the first work. Now let us consider the facts of the matter.

**4** Before chaos became, there existed the immortal entirety, and there existed the boundless light. And the shadow was called darkness. Darkness came into being from the shadow, and the rulers called the darkness "night."

**5** The curtain of the spirit was established on high. Out of it the waters of darkness came to be, and the chaos took its form from the shadow. And the darkness flowed forth from it, and its foundations came into being from the dark water.
