---
title: "Lotus Sutra - Chapter 23: The Former Affairs of the Bodhisattva Medicine King"
scripture: "lotus-sutra"
chapter: 23
---
**1** At that time the Bodhisattva Star Constellation King Flower said to the Buddha: "World-Honoured One, how does the Bodhisattva Medicine King come and go in this world? What austerities and good practices has he performed?"

**2** The Buddha said: "In the past, a Buddha called Sun Moon Pure Bright Virtue appeared in the world. Under that Buddha there was a Bodhisattva called Gladly Seen by All Living Beings."

**3** "That Bodhisattva practised austerities for twelve thousand years and then, having completed them, thought: 'I have received this Dharma from that Buddha. I should make an offering of my own body.'"

**4** "He ate various fragrances—sandalwood, kunduruka, turushka, prikka, aloes, and resin fragrances. He also drank the oil of fragrant flowers. After twelve years, he anointed his body with fragrant oil and set himself ablaze before the Buddha Sun Moon Pure Bright Virtue as an offering."

**5** "The glow of that fire lit up worlds equal in number to the sands of eighty million Ganges Rivers. The Buddhas in those worlds praised him, saying: 'Excellent! This is true diligence. This is called a true Dharma offering to the Tathagata.'"

**6** "After his body had been consumed by fire, he was reborn in the land of the Buddha Sun Moon Pure Bright Virtue. He appeared in a sitting position in the house of King Pure Virtue, and immediately spoke in verse to his father."

**7** "Then he went to that Buddha and made obeisance. The Buddha was about to enter Nirvana. He entrusted the Dharma to the Bodhisattva, who after the Buddha's passing made eighty-four thousand stupas."

**8** "Then, before the stupas, as an offering, he burned his own arms for seventy-two thousand years."

**9** "Star Constellation King Flower, the Bodhisattva Gladly Seen by All Living Beings is none other than the present Bodhisattva Medicine King."

**10** "If anyone, hearing this chapter on the Bodhisattva Medicine King, is able to respond with joy and praise, then in this present life that person's mouth will constantly emit the fragrance of the blue lotus flower, and his body will constantly emit the fragrance of sandalwood."
