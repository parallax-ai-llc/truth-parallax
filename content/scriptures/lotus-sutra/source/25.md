---
title: "Lotus Sutra - Chapter 25: The Universal Gate of Bodhisattva Avalokiteshvara"
scripture: "lotus-sutra"
chapter: 25
---
**1** At that time the Bodhisattva Inexhaustible Intent rose from his seat, bared his right shoulder, pressed his palms together, and facing the Buddha said: "World-Honoured One, for what reason is the Bodhisattva Avalokiteshvara called Avalokiteshvara, 'Perceiver of the World's Sounds'?"

**2** The Buddha said to Inexhaustible Intent: "Good man, if there are immeasurable hundreds, thousands, ten thousands, millions of living beings who are undergoing various trials and suffering, and if they hear of this Bodhisattva Avalokiteshvara and single-mindedly call his name, then Avalokiteshvara will immediately perceive the sound of their voices and they will all gain deliverance from their trials."

**3** "If a person who holds the name of Bodhisattva Avalokiteshvara should enter a great fire, the fire will be unable to burn him. This is because of the power of this Bodhisattva's authority and supernatural power."

**4** "If a person is carried away by a great flood and calls upon his name, he will immediately find himself in shallow water."

**5** "If hundreds of thousands of ten thousands of millions of beings who are seeking for gold, silver, lapis lazuli, seashell, agate, coral, amber, pearls, and other treasures should set out on the great sea, and if a black wind should blow their ship off course and it should drift to the land of rakshasas, if among them there is even just one person who calls the name of Bodhisattva Avalokiteshvara, all those persons will be delivered from the troubles of the rakshasas."

**6** "If a person who is about to be harmed calls the name of Bodhisattva Avalokiteshvara, the swords and staves held by the attackers will be broken to bits."

**7** "If the three-thousand-great-thousand world is full of yakshas and rakshasas who wish to bring harm, and if they hear the name of Bodhisattva Avalokiteshvara being called, these evil demons will not even be able to look at the person with their evil eyes, much less do him harm."

**8** "If a person, whether guilty or not, is bound in fetters and chains and calls the name of Bodhisattva Avalokiteshvara, the fetters and chains will be broken and he will immediately gain deliverance."

**9** "Bodhisattva Avalokiteshvara manifests in the form of a Buddha to preach to those who need a Buddha, as a Pratyekabuddha to those who need a Pratyekabuddha, as a Brahma king, as Shakra, as a god, as a great general, as a man, as a woman, as a boy or girl—in whatever form is needed to save beings."

**10** "Inexhaustible Intent, this Bodhisattva Avalokiteshvara has accomplished merit as great as this. In many different forms he visits the lands, saving living beings. Therefore you should all wholeheartedly make offerings to Bodhisattva Avalokiteshvara."
