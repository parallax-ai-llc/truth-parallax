---
title: "Lotus Sutra - Chapter 22: The Entrustment"
scripture: "lotus-sutra"
chapter: 22
---
**1** At that time Shakyamuni Buddha rose from his Dharma seat and, manifesting his great supernatural powers, with his right hand patted the heads of the immeasurable Bodhisattvas.

**2** He said: "For immeasurable hundreds of thousands of ten thousands of millions of asamkhya kalpas I have practised this hard-to-attain Dharma of supreme perfect enlightenment. Now I entrust it to you."

**3** "You must wholeheartedly propagate this Dharma and cause it to spread far and wide."

**4** Three times he patted the heads of the Bodhisattvas and said: "For immeasurable hundreds of thousands of ten thousands of millions of asamkhya kalpas I have practised this hard-to-attain Dharma. Now I entrust it to you. Accept it, uphold it, read it, recite it, and broadly proclaim it, so that all living beings everywhere may hear and understand it."

**5** "Why? Because the Tathagata has great compassion. He has no stinginess or fear and is able to bestow on living beings the Buddha's wisdom, the Tathagata's wisdom, the wisdom that comes of itself."

**6** "The Tathagata is a great dispenser of gifts to all living beings. You in turn should follow the example of the Tathagata and not be stingy."

**7** "In future ages, if there is a good man or good woman who has faith in the wisdom of the Tathagata, you should preach this Lotus Sutra to them, so that they may hear and understand it. For in that way you will enable them to gain the Buddha-wisdom."

**8** "If there are living beings who do not believe and accept the sutra, you should use some other profound teaching of the Tathagata to instruct, benefit, and bring joy to them."

**9** "If you can do this, you will have repaid the debt of gratitude you owe to the Buddhas."

**10** At that time the Bodhisattvas all said in a loud voice: "We will respectfully carry out the command of the World-Honoured One. We beg the World-Honoured One to have no concern!" Three times they spoke these words.
