---
title: "Lotus Sutra - Chapter 24: The Bodhisattva Wonderful Sound"
scripture: "lotus-sutra"
chapter: 24
---
**1** At that time the Buddha Shakyamuni emitted a beam of light from the protuberance on his head, one of his special features, and also emitted a beam of light from the tuft of white hair between his eyebrows, illuminating the Buddha-worlds in the eastern direction.

**2** Beyond a number of worlds equal to a hundred and eight ten thousand million nayutas of the sands of the Ganges, there was a world called Adorned with Pure Light. In that world there was a Buddha called Pure Flower Constellation King Wisdom.

**3** Before that Buddha was a Bodhisattva named Wonderful Sound, who had planted the roots of virtue under innumerable Buddhas.

**4** That Bodhisattva Wonderful Sound, without rising from his seat, caused his body to tremble, and at that time eighty-four thousand lotus flowers rained down. Then, accompanied by eighty-four thousand Bodhisattvas, he made his way to this world.

**5** When he reached Mount Gridhrakuta, he bowed to Shakyamuni Buddha and said: "The Buddha Pure Flower Constellation King Wisdom asks whether the World-Honoured One is free from illness and worry, whether his daily life proceeds smoothly."

**6** The Bodhisattva Flower Virtue asked the Buddha: "World-Honoured One, what roots of virtue has this Bodhisattva Wonderful Sound planted that he has these supernatural powers?"

**7** The Buddha said: "In the past there was a Buddha called Cloud Thunder Sound King. The Bodhisattva Wonderful Sound offered twelve thousand varieties of music to that Buddha."

**8** "Because of this offering of music, he was reborn in the land of Pure Flower Constellation King Wisdom and now possesses these supernatural powers."

**9** "This Bodhisattva Wonderful Sound can manifest himself in various forms to preach the Lotus Sutra to beings. He appears as a Brahma king, as Shakra, as a heavenly being, as a great general, as a great king, as a merchant, as a woman, as a boy or girl—in whatever form is needed."

**10** "Even in the hells he manifests himself to save beings. He can transform himself into all these various forms and in this world preach the Lotus Sutra. His supernatural powers and wisdom are as great as this."
