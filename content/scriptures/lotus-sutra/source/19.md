---
title: "Lotus Sutra - Chapter 19: Benefits of the Teacher of the Law"
scripture: "lotus-sutra"
chapter: 19
---
**1** At that time the Buddha said to the Bodhisattva King of the Medicine: "If a good man or good woman embraces the Lotus Sutra, whether reading it, reciting it, explaining it, or copying it, that person will gain eight hundred eye-benefits, twelve hundred ear-benefits, eight hundred nose-benefits, twelve hundred tongue-benefits, eight hundred body-benefits, and twelve hundred mind-benefits."

**2** "With these benefits he will be able to adorn his six sense organs, making all of them pure."

**3** "With the pure eye of flesh given him by his parents, he will see the entire great three-thousand world, with all its mountains, forests, rivers, and seas. He will see down to the Avici hell and up to the Summit of Being."

**4** "In the midst of all this, he will see all the living beings and will understand the causes and conditions of their actions, and the recompense they will receive."

**5** "With the pure ear, he will hear throughout the great three-thousand world all manner of sounds—elephants, horses, cattle, carts, weeping, lamenting, conch shells, drums, bells, laughter, voices of men and women, boys and girls."

**6** "He will hear the voices of those in hell, those in the animal realm, those in the realm of hungry ghosts. He will hear the sounds of the heavenly beings, their music and song. He will hear the monks and nuns reading and reciting the sutras."

**7** "Although he has not yet gained heavenly ears, with the ordinary ears of flesh given him by his parents, he will be able to hear and understand all of this."

**8** "With the pure nose, he will smell throughout the great three-thousand world all manner of fragrances—sandalwood, aloes, tamalapatra, all varieties of flowers, fruits, and trees."

**9** "With the pure tongue, whatever food he eats, whether good or bad, will become a wonderful flavour, like the taste of the Dharma. His tongue will be purified so that whatever he says will accord with truth and will be pleasing to all who hear."

**10** "With the pure body, he will reflect all forms in the great three-thousand world, as a clean mirror reflects all images. With the pure mind, hearing a single verse or phrase, he will penetrate its infinite meaning."
