---
title: "Lotus Sutra - Chapter 17: Distinctions in Benefits"
scripture: "lotus-sutra"
chapter: 17
---
**1** At that time, when the great assembly heard the Buddha describe how his life span was of such a great number of kalpas, immeasurable, boundless asamkhyas of living beings gained a great benefit.

**2** The World-Honoured One said to the Bodhisattva Maitreya: "When I preached on the great length of the Tathagata's life span, living beings as numerous as the sands of six hundred and eighty ten thousand million nayutas of Ganges attained the truth of the birthlessness of all dharmas."

**3** "A thousand times that number of Bodhisattvas gained the ability to hear and hold dharanis. A world full of Bodhisattvas gained the power of eloquent preaching."

**4** "Maitreya, the benefits of those who, hearing that the Buddha's life span is of such length, are able to believe and understand—the benefits of such persons are without limit."

**5** "If a good man or good woman, for the sake of supreme enlightenment, over a period of eight hundred thousand million nayutas of kalpas practises the five paramitas—the paramitas of giving, morality, patience, energy, and meditation—the benefits gained thereby cannot match those gained through faith in the Buddha's long life span."

**6** "A person who is able to believe and accept this teaching gains benefits that are a hundred, a thousand, ten thousand, a million times greater than the benefits of practising the five paramitas for all those kalpas."

**7** "If a person hears this sutra and does not slander or doubt it, and in his heart has deep faith and understanding, the benefits he gains are even greater."

**8** "How much more so for one who reads and recites, embraces, and copies this sutra, and who regards the sutra scroll as the Buddha himself, offering flowers, incense, necklaces, and all manner of offerings."

**9** "Maitreya, if after my extinction there is a good man or good woman who hears the chapter on the life span of the Tathagata and in deep mind believes and understands it, that person shall see the Buddha constantly on Mount Gridhrakuta."

**10** "Maitreya, know this: such a person has already made offerings to countless Buddhas in the past and is now manifesting in this world to hear this sutra and to believe in it."
