An interfaith reading of Chapter 2 of the Kephalaia seeks to build bridges of understanding around the themes of light-nous. The chapter's teachings on divine mind, gnosis offer points of contact with other traditions' reflections on similar questions.

Interfaith engagement with this chapter emphasizes shared human concerns and complementary insights, while respecting the distinctive theological context of Manichaeism. The themes addressed here — divine mind, gnosis, illumination — resonate across religious boundaries and invite collaborative reflection on questions of ultimate significance.
