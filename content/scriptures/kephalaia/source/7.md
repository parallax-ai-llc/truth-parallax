---
title: "Kephalaia Section 7"
scripture: "kephalaia"
chapter: 7
---

1. A disciple asked the Apostle: "Master, tell us about the Light Mind and how it enters a person." The Illuminator answered:

2. "The Light Mind is the presence of the divine within the soul. It is sent by the Third Messenger to dwell in those who turn toward the Light."

3. "When a person hears the call of truth and responds, the Light Mind descends and takes up residence within them, like a king entering his palace."

4. "Before the Light Mind enters, the soul is ruled by the Old Man, which is the mind of Darkness, the mind of the flesh."

5. "The Old Man fills the soul with greed, with lust, with anger, with ignorance, and with all the passions that keep the Light imprisoned."

6. "But when the Light Mind enters, it drives out the Old Man and sets the New Man upon the throne of the soul."

7. "The New Man is gentle, peaceful, full of knowledge, and devoted to the liberation of Light. The New Man keeps the commandments without effort."

8. "Yet the Old Man does not depart willingly. He lurks at the edges of the soul, waiting for a moment of weakness to return."

9. "Therefore the disciple must be vigilant. Through prayer, through fasting, through the study of wisdom, the Light Mind is strengthened."

10. "The Light Mind has five attributes: Love, Faith, Perfection, Patience, and Wisdom. These are the armor of the New Man."

11. "Love drives out hatred. Faith drives out doubt. Perfection drives out corruption. Patience drives out anger. Wisdom drives out ignorance."

12. "When these five attributes are fully developed in the soul, the Light Mind has complete dominion, and the soul is prepared for its ascent."

13. "At death, the soul of such a person rises swiftly through the heavens, past the Archons, past the watchers, and reaches the Paradise of Light."

14. "But the soul in which the Old Man still reigns must return to the world, to be born again, until the Light Mind is awakened within it."
