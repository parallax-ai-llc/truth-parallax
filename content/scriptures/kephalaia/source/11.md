---
title: "Kephalaia Section 11"
scripture: "kephalaia"
chapter: 11
---

1. The Apostle spoke concerning the nature of trees and plants: "Know that the Light is present in all living things, and most abundantly in the fruit-bearing trees."

2. "When you see a tree laden with fruit, know that you behold the Light struggling to free itself from the bonds of matter."

3. "The fruit is the purest concentration of Light in the vegetable kingdom. When the Elect eats the fruit, the Light within it is liberated."

4. "For the body of the Elect is like a furnace of purification. The Light passes through the Elect and rises to the Column of Glory."

5. "This is why the Hearer must bring the choicest fruits and the purest bread to the Elect, for through this offering the Hearer participates in the liberation of Light."

6. "But when fruit falls to the ground and rots, the Light within it weeps, for it is dragged back into the earth, into the Darkness."

7. "When a tree is cut down, the Light within it cries out in pain. Therefore the Elect shall not cut down any tree, nor shall they plow the ground."

8. "The Hearer, however, may farm and harvest, for this is necessary for life in the world. But they should do so with compassion, knowing the cost."

9. "Water also contains Light, for the Living Water that flows through the world carries particles of the divine substance."

10. "When water is heated by fire, the Light in it suffers. When it is cooled, it is at peace. Therefore the Elect drinks water at its natural temperature."

11. "Even the stones and the earth contain some measure of Light, though it is deeply buried and will be the last to be liberated."

12. "At the end of all things, even the Light in the stones shall be freed, and then the cosmos shall collapse, for there will be nothing to hold it together."

13. "The wise person walks gently upon the earth, knowing that beneath their feet the Light is waiting, hoping for the day of its release."
