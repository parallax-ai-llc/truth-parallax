---
title: "Kephalaia Section 5"
scripture: "kephalaia"
chapter: 5
---

1. The Apostle of Light taught concerning the Five Classes of the Church: "The Church of Righteousness is ordered in five ranks, even as the Father has five Greatnesses."

2. "The first rank is that of the Teachers, who are twelve in number, corresponding to the twelve Aeons of Light. They carry the word of truth to all nations."

3. "The second rank is that of the Bishops, who are seventy-two, corresponding to the seventy-two languages of humanity. They oversee the congregations."

4. "The third rank is that of the Presbyters, who are three hundred and sixty, corresponding to the days of the year. They minister to the faithful."

5. "The fourth rank is that of the Elect, who are the pure ones. They keep the Three Seals: the Seal of the Mouth, the Seal of the Hands, and the Seal of the Breast."

6. "The Seal of the Mouth: the Elect shall not consume meat, nor drink wine, nor speak falsehood or blasphemy. Their mouths are sanctified."

7. "The Seal of the Hands: the Elect shall not kill any living thing, nor uproot any plant, nor defile the Light that is in all things. Their hands are pure."

8. "The Seal of the Breast: the Elect shall abstain from all carnal desire, from procreation, and from the pleasures of the body. Their hearts are sealed against Darkness."

9. "The fifth rank is that of the Hearers, who are the greater multitude. They support the Elect with food and shelter, and they keep the Ten Commandments."

10. "The Hearers shall not worship idols. They shall not lie. They shall not be greedy. They shall not kill. They shall not commit adultery."

11. "They shall not steal. They shall not teach falsehood. They shall not practice sorcery. They shall not be of two minds in their faith. They shall not be slothful in their work."

12. "When the Hearer provides food to the Elect, the Light in that food is liberated through the body of the Elect, for their body is a vessel of purification."

13. "Thus the Hearer participates in the liberation of Light through service, and in future births they may be born as Elect, until their Light too is fully gathered."
