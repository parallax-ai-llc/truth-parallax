---
title: "Kephalaia Section 3"
scripture: "kephalaia"
chapter: 3
---

1. The Apostle taught concerning the creation of the world: "After the Living Spirit rescued the First Man, he turned to fashion the cosmos from the bodies of the defeated Archons."

2. "The Living Spirit and his five sons spread out the skins of the Archons and made the ten heavens. From their bones they made the mountains, and from their flesh and excrement they made the eight earths."

3. "The Living Spirit set up three wheels: the Wheel of Fire, the Wheel of Water, and the Wheel of Wind, by which the cosmos is sustained."

4. "Then from the purest Light that he could extract from the Darkness, the Living Spirit fashioned the Sun and the Moon."

5. "The Sun and the Moon are the great ships of Light, the vessels by which the liberated particles of Light ascend to the Paradise of Light."

6. "The Sun is the ship of the day, and the Moon is the ship of the night. They sail across the heavens, gathering Light from the world below."

7. "When the Moon waxes, it fills with Light that has been purified from the world. When it is full, it pours this Light into the Sun, and so the Moon wanes."

8. "The Sun carries the Light upward to the Paradise of Light, where it is restored to the Father of Greatness. This is the Column of Glory."

9. "The Column of Glory is the path of ascending Light, visible to those whose eyes are opened. It is also called the Perfect Man, for it is the image of the redeemed soul."

10. "All the stars in the sky are also repositories of Light, each one holding a measure of the divine substance that was scattered in the primordial war."

11. "Thus the entire cosmos is a great machine for the purification and liberation of Light. Every day, Light ascends through the Column of Glory."

12. "And this process shall continue until all the Light has been gathered, and the cosmos shall then be dissolved, for it will have served its purpose."
