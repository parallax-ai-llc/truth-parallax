---
title: "Kephalaia Section 10"
scripture: "kephalaia"
chapter: 10
---

1. A disciple asked: "Master, how shall we pray?" And the Apostle answered: "Prayer is the voice of the Light within you calling to its source."

2. "You shall pray four times each day: at noon, in the afternoon, in the evening, and at dawn. And before each prayer you shall wash your hands and face."

3. "You shall face the Sun when you pray during the day, for the Sun is the great ship of Light and the dwelling of the Third Messenger."

4. "At night you shall face the Moon, for the Moon too is a vessel of Light, carrying the purified particles to the Sun."

5. "When you prostrate yourself, you lay down the body of Darkness before the throne of Light. When you rise, you enact the resurrection of the soul."

6. "Seven prostrations shall you make at each prayer, and with each prostration you shall contemplate one of the seven steps of ascent."

7. "The first step is repentance, turning away from Darkness. The second is self-examination, knowing the Light within."

8. "The third is confession, acknowledging the hold that Darkness has upon you. The fourth is faith, trusting in the Father's plan of salvation."

9. "The fifth is generosity, releasing the things of the world. The sixth is patience, enduring the trials of the body."

10. "The seventh is wisdom, understanding the mystery of the Two Principles and the Three Times."

11. "Besides the daily prayers, you shall keep the fast of Monday, the day on which the Apostle first received his revelation."

12. "And you shall observe the great fast before the Feast of the Bema, which lasts thirty days, in memory of the suffering of the First Man."

13. "The Feast of the Bema is the holiest day, when we remember the passion and triumph of the Apostle, and when the empty throne awaits his spiritual presence."

14. "On that day, the entire community gathers, and the books of Mani are read aloud, and the faithful renew their devotion to the path of Light."
