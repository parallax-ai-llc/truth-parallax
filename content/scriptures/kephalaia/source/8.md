---
title: "Kephalaia Section 8"
scripture: "kephalaia"
chapter: 8
---

1. The Apostle taught concerning the destiny of the soul after death: "There are three paths that await the soul when it departs from the body."

2. "The first path is for the soul of the Elect, who has kept the Three Seals and has been filled with the Light Mind."

3. "When the Elect dies, three angels come to greet him: an angel bearing a garment of Light, an angel bearing a crown, and an angel bearing a wreath."

4. "The Luminous Jesus appears before the soul as its guide, and with him come the Light Maiden and the three angels."

5. "The soul is clothed in the garment of Light, and it ascends the Column of Glory, past the Moon and the Sun, to the New Paradise."

6. "There it rests in glory with the Father of Greatness for all eternity, and its Light is restored to the Kingdom of Light."

7. "The second path is for the soul of the Hearer, who has kept the Ten Commandments and has served the Elect faithfully."

8. "This soul does not ascend immediately to the Paradise of Light. Instead, it passes through a period of purification."

9. "The Light that it has gathered through service is separated from the Darkness that remains within it. This Light ascends to the Paradise."

10. "But the soul itself is born again into a new body, and it is given another chance to progress further along the path."

11. "If it is fortunate, it may be born into the community of the Elect and keep the Three Seals, and so ascend at its next death."

12. "The third path is for the soul of the sinner, who has rejected the truth and lived in Darkness."

13. "This soul is seized by the demons and dragged through the realms of punishment. It is shown all the evils it has committed."

14. "Then it is cast back into the world in a lower form—as a beast, or a plant—where its Light is trapped ever more deeply in matter."

15. "Therefore the Apostle warns: choose the path of Light while you have the chance, for the opportunity may not come again soon."
