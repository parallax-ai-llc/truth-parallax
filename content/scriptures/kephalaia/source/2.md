---
title: "Kephalaia Section 2"
scripture: "kephalaia"
chapter: 2
---

1. Again the Enlightener spoke concerning the First Man: "When the Darkness rose up against the realm of Light, the Father of Greatness called forth the First Man."

2. "The First Man is the first emanation of the Father, armed with five elements of Light: Air, Wind, Light, Water, and Fire."

3. "These five elements are the armor of the First Man, and he clothed himself in them as a warrior clothes himself for battle."

4. "The First Man descended into the Darkness to make war, and the Archons of Darkness swallowed his Light, his five elements."

5. "When the Archons consumed the Light, they were pacified, even as a beast is pacified when it has eaten. And the Light became mixed with the Darkness."

6. "This was the great sacrifice, for the First Man gave his own soul, his own Light, to be devoured, that the Darkness might be subdued."

7. "And the First Man lay in the deep, stripped of his armor, and he prayed to the Father of Greatness for deliverance."

8. "The Father heard his prayer and sent the Living Spirit, his second emanation, to rescue the First Man from the abyss."

9. "The Living Spirit descended and reached down his right hand to the First Man, and the First Man grasped it, and was drawn up out of the Darkness."

10. "This is the Right Hand of salvation, the greeting of peace, which we practice to this day among the Elect, as a remembrance of that first deliverance."

11. "But though the First Man was rescued, his armor of Light remained trapped in the Darkness, mixed with matter, and it cries out for liberation."

12. "All the Light that is in the world—in the sun, the moon, the stars, in plants and animals and in the souls of human beings—this is the scattered Light of the First Man."

13. "And the great work of the Church of Light is to gather this Light and return it to the Father, particle by particle, until the separation is complete."
