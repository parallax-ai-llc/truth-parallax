---
title: "Kephalaia Section 15"
scripture: "kephalaia"
chapter: 15
---

1. The Apostle spoke his final teaching to his disciples: "I have revealed to you all that the Father of Greatness has given me to reveal."

2. "I have written seven books: the Living Gospel, the Treasure of Life, the Pragmateia, the Book of Mysteries, the Book of Giants, the Letters, and the Psalms and Prayers."

3. "In these books, the entire truth is contained. Guard them and copy them and spread them to every nation under heaven."

4. "I have also given you the Picture Book, which contains the truth in images for those who cannot read."

5. "Other apostles before me spoke the truth but did not write it down, and so their teachings were corrupted. I have written everything, that no corruption may enter."

6. "After I depart from this world, hold fast to the teaching. Do not add to it and do not take away from it."

7. "False teachers will arise among you, who will try to change what I have taught. Recognize them by this: they will say the Darkness does not exist, or they will say the Light is not truly good."

8. "Any teaching that denies the Two Principles is false. Any teaching that confuses Light with Darkness is of the enemy."

9. "Keep the community together. Let the Teachers guide, let the Bishops administer, let the Presbyters minister, let the Elect purify, and let the Hearers support."

10. "If the community is united, the Light within you will grow stronger. If it is divided, the Darkness will enter the cracks."

11. "Remember the Bema. Gather each year and remember my teaching and my suffering. Set the empty chair and know that I am with you in spirit."

12. "For I do not truly die. The Light within me returns to the Father, and from the Father it shines upon you always."

13. "Be lamps in the Darkness. Be ships of Light in the sea of matter. Carry the truth to every creature, that all Light may be gathered."

14. "And when the last particle of Light ascends the Column of Glory, I shall meet you in the Paradise of Light, and there shall be no more separation."

15. "Peace be upon you, children of Light. The Father of Greatness watches over you. The Mother of Life sustains you. The Luminous Jesus guides you. Go forth in Light."
