---
title: "Kephalaia Section 14"
scripture: "kephalaia"
chapter: 14
---

1. A disciple asked: "Master, why do the other religions condemn us and call us heretics?" The Apostle answered:

2. "Because they see only part of the truth, and when they encounter the whole truth, it threatens the part they hold."

3. "The Christians say we deny the goodness of creation. But we do not deny it—we say that what is good in creation is the Light, and what is evil is the Darkness that is mixed with it."

4. "The Zoroastrians say we are too much like the Christians. The Christians say we are too much like the Zoroastrians. This shows that we stand at the center where truth meets truth."

5. "The Jews say we blaspheme their God. But their God is the Archon who created the material world, who is not the Father of Greatness but a lesser power."

6. "The Buddhists say we are attached to doctrines of a personal God. But we honor the Buddha and teach, as he did, the liberation of the soul from the cycle of rebirth."

7. "Each of these religions contains a portion of the Light, for each was founded by a true apostle. But each has been corrupted by the Darkness."

8. "My teaching gathers what is true in all of them and discards what is false. This is why they attack us—for we reveal what they have lost."

9. "Do not be discouraged by persecution. The Apostles of Light have always been persecuted, for the Darkness cannot bear the Light."

10. "Jesus was crucified. Zoroaster was killed. The Buddha was given poison. And I too shall suffer, for this is the way of the world."

11. "But suffering purifies the Light within us. Each wound inflicted on the body of the righteous liberates Light from Darkness."

12. "Therefore, endure persecution with patience and joy, knowing that through your suffering the great work of separation advances."

13. "The day shall come when all persecution ceases, when the Light is fully gathered and the Darkness is sealed away. Until that day, be steadfast."
