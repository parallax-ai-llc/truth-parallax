---
title: "Kephalaia Section 1"
scripture: "kephalaia"
chapter: 1
---

1. The Apostle of Light spoke to his disciples and said: "When I came forth from the Father of Greatness, I brought with me the knowledge of all things."

2. "For the Father sent me into the world that I might reveal the Two Principles and the Three Times, which are the foundation of all wisdom."

3. "The First Principle is Light, which is the Father himself, dwelling in the Paradise of Light with his twelve Aeons and his countless angels of glory."

4. "The Second Principle is Darkness, which has existed from the beginning, dwelling in the deep, filled with smoke and fire and destruction."

5. "Before the creation of heaven and earth, these Two Roots existed, separate from one another, the Light above and the Darkness below."

6. "The Father of Greatness possesses five Greatnesses: Mind, Knowledge, Reason, Thought, and Understanding. These are the limbs of the Father."

7. "And in the land of Darkness there dwell five Archons: the Archon of Smoke, the Archon of Fire, the Archon of Wind, the Archon of Water, and the Archon of Darkness."

8. "Each of these Archons rules over his own realm, and together they form the Kingdom of Darkness, which is disorder and strife."

9. "Now the Three Times are these: the Former Time, when Light and Darkness were separate; the Present Time, when they are mixed; and the Future Time, when they shall be separated again."

10. "In the Former Time, the Father of Greatness dwelt in his kingdom in peace, and the Darkness dwelt in its own realm, and neither knew the other."

11. "But the Darkness rose up and beheld the Light, and desired it, and made war against it. This was the beginning of the mixture."

12. "Therefore the Father sent forth his emanations to defend the Light and to begin the great work of separation, which continues until this day."
