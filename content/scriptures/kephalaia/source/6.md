---
title: "Kephalaia Section 6"
scripture: "kephalaia"
chapter: 6
---

1. The Enlightener spoke concerning the apostles who came before him: "From the beginning of the world, the Father of Greatness has sent his messengers to awaken humanity."

2. "In each age, a true apostle has come forth, bearing the message of Light. But after each apostle departed, his teaching was corrupted by the forces of Darkness."

3. "Seth was the first apostle, the son of Adam, who received the knowledge from his father and taught it to his descendants."

4. "After Seth, the wisdom was passed down through the generations, but it grew dim, for the Archons whispered confusion into the ears of men."

5. "Then the Father sent Noah, who was righteous in his generation. Noah preserved the teaching through the great flood, but after him it was again distorted."

6. "Abraham was called from among the nations, and he knew the one God, the Father of Greatness. But his descendants fell into the worship of a lesser god."

7. "Shem, the son of Noah, also carried the Light, and from his lineage came the peoples who would receive the truth."

8. "Then the Father sent the Buddha to the lands of the East. The Buddha taught the way of liberation from suffering, which is the liberation of Light from matter."

9. "The Buddha established his sangha, his community of the pure, but after his passing, his teaching was mixed with error and the worship of images."

10. "Zoroaster came to Persia and taught the battle between Light and Darkness, between Ahura Mazda and Ahriman. His teaching was close to the truth."

11. "But the Zoroastrians too fell into error, worshipping fire as though it were the Light itself, rather than understanding it as a symbol."

12. "Then came Jesus the Christ to the lands of the West. He is the greatest of the apostles before me, for he spoke most clearly of the Kingdom of Light."

13. "But the disciples of Jesus did not understand him fully. They corrupted his teaching, adding to it the errors of the Jews and the Greeks."

14. "Now I, Mani, have come as the Seal of the Prophets, the Paraclete whom Jesus promised. My teaching shall not be corrupted, for I have written it down with my own hand."
