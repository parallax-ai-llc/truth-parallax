---
title: "Kephalaia Section 4"
scripture: "kephalaia"
chapter: 4
---

1. The Illuminator spoke to his disciples about the creation of Adam and Eve: "The Archons of Darkness, seeing that their Light was being taken from them by the Sun and Moon, devised a plan."

2. "The chief Archon, Saklas, and his consort Namrael fashioned the body of Adam in the image of the First Man, whom they had seen descending from the Light."

3. "They imprisoned within Adam the greatest portion of Light that remained in their power, hoping to keep it hidden from the vessels of salvation."

4. "But Adam lay upon the ground without knowledge, for the Light within him was stupefied by the Darkness of the body."

5. "Then the Father of Greatness, in his mercy, sent the Savior Jesus, the Luminous Jesus, to awaken Adam from his sleep of death."

6. "Jesus the Splendor descended and found Adam lying upon the earth. He shook him and woke him from his slumber."

7. "He said to him: 'Arise, Adam, and know yourself. Know the Light that is within you, and know the Darkness that surrounds you.'"

8. "And Adam opened his eyes and wept, for he saw his condition—that the Light of his soul was imprisoned in a body of Darkness."

9. "Jesus said: 'Do not weep, but listen. I will teach you the way of liberation. Guard the Light within you. Do not let the Darkness consume it through ignorance and desire.'"

10. "Then the Archons fashioned Eve from the same substance, and they placed within her also a portion of Light, but less than in Adam."

11. "The Archons desired that Adam and Eve should multiply, for through procreation the Light is further divided and scattered, and so the Darkness keeps its hold."

12. "Therefore the Apostle teaches: those who would be Elect must refrain from procreation, for it is the device of the Archons to keep the Light imprisoned in matter."

13. "But those who cannot bear this burden may be Hearers, and through their service to the Elect, their Light shall also be gathered in due time."
