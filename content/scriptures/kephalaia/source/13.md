---
title: "Kephalaia Section 13"
scripture: "kephalaia"
chapter: 13
---

1. The Apostle taught his disciples concerning the paintings and the images: "I have painted great pictures upon the scrolls, that the truth may be seen as well as heard."

2. "For the eye understands what the ear alone cannot grasp, and the image teaches what words may fail to convey."

3. "I have painted the Two Kingdoms: the Kingdom of Light with its twelve Aeons and its shining angels, and the Kingdom of Darkness with its five pits and its devouring Archons."

4. "I have painted the First Man going forth to battle, armed in his five elements, and his defeat and his rescue by the Living Spirit."

5. "I have painted the cosmos as it truly is: a great machine of purification, with the Sun and Moon as ships of Light sailing across the heavens."

6. "I have painted the Column of Glory, the path of Light ascending, so that the faithful may see with their eyes what their souls shall one day traverse."

7. "No apostle before me has painted the truth in images. This is my gift to the Church, that the truth may endure uncorrupted."

8. "For words may be twisted and texts may be altered, but the image speaks directly to the soul, bypassing the confusion of language."

9. "When the faithful gather and behold these paintings, let them meditate upon what they see, for in contemplation the Light Mind is strengthened."

10. "The Great Picture Book, which I have prepared, contains the entire teaching in visual form. It shall be shown to kings and commoners alike."

11. "Through these images, even the unlettered may understand the truth of the Two Principles, the Three Times, and the path of salvation."

12. "Guard these images carefully, for they are sacred. Copy them faithfully when they grow old, and let no hand alter what the Apostle has drawn."

13. "For in the Last Days, these images shall testify to the truth, and those who have seen them shall not be able to say they did not know."
