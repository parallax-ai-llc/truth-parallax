---
title: "Kephalaia Section 9"
scripture: "kephalaia"
chapter: 9
---

1. The Illuminator spoke concerning the Third Messenger and the Maiden of Light: "After the Living Spirit fashioned the cosmos, the Father sent the Third Messenger."

2. "The Third Messenger dwells in the Sun, and his task is to set in motion the mechanism by which Light is liberated from matter."

3. "The Third Messenger revealed his image—his male and female forms—to the Archons who were bound in the heavens."

4. "When the male Archons beheld the Maiden of Light in her beauty, they were seized with desire, and in their agitation they released the Light they had swallowed."

5. "This Light fell from them as seed falls from a plant, and it descended to the earth. From it sprang forth all vegetation, all the green life of the world."

6. "When the female Archons beheld the male image of the Third Messenger, they too were agitated, and they cast forth the Darkness within them."

7. "This Darkness fell to earth as abortions, as monstrous forms, which became the demons and evil spirits that plague humanity."

8. "Thus through the device of the Third Messenger, the process of separation was set in motion within the very bodies of the Archons."

9. "The Light that was liberated from the Archons rises upward through the plants and trees, and when the Elect consume fruit and bread, they release this Light through their purified bodies."

10. "The Maiden of Light continues to call to the scattered particles of Light in the world, drawing them upward like a magnet draws iron."

11. "She appears in many forms and in many places, for she is the feminine aspect of salvation, the compassion of the Father."

12. "All beauty in the world is a reflection of the Maiden of Light, but the wise know that one must not cling to the reflection but seek the source."
