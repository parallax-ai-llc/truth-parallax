---
title: "Kephalaia Section 12"
scripture: "kephalaia"
chapter: 12
---

1. The Illuminator spoke concerning the Last Days: "There shall come a time when the great work of separation nears its completion."

2. "In that time, the Church of Light shall have spread to every nation, and the truth shall have been proclaimed in every tongue."

3. "But the forces of Darkness shall also increase in power, for as the Light is gathered, the Darkness grows more desperate."

4. "False prophets shall arise and lead many astray. Wars and calamities shall multiply, for the Darkness rages against the Light."

5. "The Elect shall be persecuted, and the Hearers shall be tested. Many shall fall away, but those who endure shall be crowned with glory."

6. "Then shall come the Great War, the final battle between Light and Darkness, which mirrors the primordial war at the beginning of time."

7. "Jesus the Splendor shall return as the judge, and he shall separate the righteous from the wicked, as the farmer separates wheat from chaff."

8. "The righteous shall be gathered to the right hand, and the Light within them shall be fully liberated and ascend to the Paradise."

9. "The wicked shall be gathered to the left hand, and they shall be bound with the Darkness, for they have chosen Darkness over Light."

10. "Then the Great Fire shall be kindled, and it shall burn for one thousand four hundred and sixty-eight years."

11. "In this fire, the last particles of Light that are trapped in matter shall be released, and they shall rise to join the Kingdom of Light."

12. "When the last particle of Light has been freed, the fire shall consume all matter, and the Darkness shall be sealed in the great Pit."

13. "A great stone shall be rolled over the Pit, and the Darkness shall be imprisoned for all eternity, never again to trouble the Light."

14. "And the Kingdom of Light shall be as it was in the Former Time, but greater, for all the Light shall have been gathered, and the Father shall rejoice."
