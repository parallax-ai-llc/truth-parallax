Chapter 6 of the Kephalaia, with its treatment of zodiac, exemplifies key themes of the gnostic worldview. The motifs of astrology, cosmic wheel are central to gnostic soteriology and cosmology, and this chapter develops them with characteristic depth and narrative power.

The gnostic dimensions of this chapter — particularly its engagement with zodiac — connect to broader patterns in gnostic literature, from the Nag Hammadi texts to Mandaean writings. The emphasis on astrology, cosmic wheel reflects the gnostic conviction that saving knowledge is the key to liberation from the material realm.
