## Overview

The Christian reading of the Kephalaia engages with this text from within the living tradition of Christian thought and practice. Reveals the Manichaean appropriation and reinterpretation of Christian concepts. This perspective brings the accumulated wisdom of Christian interpretation to bear on the text, illuminating dimensions that may not be apparent from external scholarly perspectives alone.

Within the Christian tradition, the Kephalaia is understood not merely as a historical document but as a living source of spiritual guidance and theological reflection. Christian interpreters bring distinctive hermeneutical principles, doctrinal frameworks, and experiential knowledge to their engagement with this text, producing readings that are both deeply informed and spiritually engaged.
