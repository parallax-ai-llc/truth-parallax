## Overview

The Manichaean reading of the Kephalaia engages with this text from within the living tradition of Manichaean thought and practice. A primary source for Manichaean theology in dialogical form. This perspective brings the accumulated wisdom of Manichaean interpretation to bear on the text, illuminating dimensions that may not be apparent from external scholarly perspectives alone.

Within the Manichaean tradition, the Kephalaia is understood not merely as a historical document but as a living source of spiritual guidance and theological reflection. Manichaean interpreters bring distinctive hermeneutical principles, doctrinal frameworks, and experiential knowledge to their engagement with this text, producing readings that are both deeply informed and spiritually engaged.
