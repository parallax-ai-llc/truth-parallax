Chapter 3 of the Kephalaia, addressing five elements, holds particular significance within the Manichaean tradition. The themes of air, wind are central to Manichaean theology and practice, and this chapter has been a focus of traditional interpretation and spiritual reflection.

Within Manichaean tradition, this chapter's treatment of five elements is understood not merely as historical or theoretical but as a living source of guidance for faith and practice. The teachings on air, wind inform Manichaean self-understanding and continue to shape communal identity and spiritual aspiration.
