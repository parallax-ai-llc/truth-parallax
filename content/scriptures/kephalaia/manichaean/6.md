Chapter 6 of the Kephalaia, addressing zodiac, holds particular significance within the Manichaean tradition. The themes of astrology, cosmic wheel are central to Manichaean theology and practice, and this chapter has been a focus of traditional interpretation and spiritual reflection.

Within Manichaean tradition, this chapter's treatment of zodiac is understood not merely as historical or theoretical but as a living source of guidance for faith and practice. The teachings on astrology, cosmic wheel inform Manichaean self-understanding and continue to shape communal identity and spiritual aspiration.
