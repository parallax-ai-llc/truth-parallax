**Section II: The Purification and the Gods**

**1** O Osiris, the justified, born of Nut! You are purified by Horus, you are censed by Thoth.

**2** Your soul is given to you. Your power is given to you. Your mouth is opened. Your eyes see. Your ears hear.

**3** Your flesh is firm. Your bones are joined together. You are content with what has been done for you.

**4** You breathe by the command of Ra. You are protected by Isis and Nephthys.

**5** Your heart is given to you, the heart of your body. You are in your former state. You are in your former position.

**6** You descend into the Hall of the Two Truths. The forty-two gods sit in the presence of the Great God.

**7** The ground of the Hall of the Two Truths is given to you. The Field of Offerings is given to you. The Field of Rushes is given to you.

**8** Hapi comes to you bearing his offerings: bread and beer, oxen and geese, all things good and pure.

**9** The field gives you its food. Offerings are placed before you daily. Water is poured for you.

**10** The north wind comes to you, sweet-smelling. It emerges from the nostrils of Osiris for your nostrils.

**11** You go in and you come out, your heart rejoicing in the favour of the Lord of the Gods. You are given a good burial.

**12** You receive a coffin of gold, your head of lapis lazuli. The sky is above you, the earth is beneath you.

**13** A mat of gold is placed beneath you. You are given the adornments of the goddess Tefnut.

**14** Shu stretches himself out beneath you, that he may raise you to heaven. The stars are at your service.

**15** The portals of the Duat are opened for you. The gates of the West are thrown open for you.

**16** The blessed dead come to you bowing. They worship you. They say to you: "Welcome! Welcome!" when you enter the Duat.

**17** You see Ra in the West. You worship him at his setting. He joins you in the night. He gathers you to the morning.

**18** He says to you: "Welcome, welcome, into this your house of eternity, which I have made for you!"
