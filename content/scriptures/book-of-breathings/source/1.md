**Section I: The Invocation of Isis**

**1** The beginning of the Book of Breathings, which Isis made for her brother Osiris, to make his soul live, to make his body live, to rejuvenate all his limbs again;

**2** that he might join the horizon with his father Ra; that his soul might rise to heaven in the disk of the moon;

**3** that his body might shine in the constellation of Orion, in the womb of Nut;

**4** to make the same for each and every one, to be placed under his left arm.

**5** O Osiris, the justified! Your name endures. Your soul lives. Your body is revivified.

**6** Breathe, breathe, O Osiris, the justified! Your name shall not perish. Your soul shall not be destroyed. You shall breathe as a god.

**7** Isis has come to you to give you breath, to make your throat breathe again, to make your nostrils breathe again.

**8** Hail to you, Osiris, son of Nut! You are crowned as lord of the West. Your soul lives by the decree of the gods.

**9** You live, you live! You rise, you rise! You breathe, you breathe! You exist, you exist!

**10** Your heart beats. Your limbs move. You stir your body. You shake the dust from your flesh.

**11** You are stable. You stand upon your feet. You are purified. You are cleansed.

**12** Hail to you, Osiris! Your ka lives. Your body endures. Your name shall not perish from the earth.

**13** Isis has made this for you. She has given you the breath of life. She has made wind with her wings.

**14** She has cried out, she has mourned, she has restored the breath to your nostrils.

**15** Her word of power is your protection. The gods obey her speech. The doors of the tomb open for you.

**16** Your mummy wrappings are loosened. Your mouth is opened. Your heart is restored to you.

**17** Rise up, O Osiris! You shall breathe forever and ever.
