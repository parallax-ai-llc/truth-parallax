**Section III: The Declaration of Innocence**

**1** O Osiris, the justified! Your soul is with you. Your form is behind you. Your crown is upon your head.

**2** Thoth has made your wrappings. Anubis has strengthened your mummy. Isis has gathered your limbs.

**3** You say: I have done no evil. I have committed no crime. I have done no wrong. I have killed no man.

**4** I have not diminished the offerings in the temples. I have not damaged the bread of the gods.

**5** I have not stolen the food of the spirits. I have not committed adultery. I have not been unchaste in the sanctuary of my god.

**6** I have not added to the weight of the balance. I have not tampered with the plumb-bob of the scales.

**7** I have not taken milk from the mouths of children. I have not driven cattle from their pastures.

**8** I have not trapped the birds of the gods. I have not caught the fish of their marshes.

**9** I have not held back the water in its season. I have not built a dam against the running water.

**10** I have not extinguished the fire in its time. I have not neglected the appointed offerings.

**11** I have not driven away the cattle of the god's property. I have not turned back the god at his appearance.

**12** I am pure! I am pure! I am pure! I am pure!

**13** My purity is the purity of the Great Bennu Bird which is in Heracleopolis.

**14** For I am the nose of the Lord of Breath, who gives life to all the people on that great day of the reckoning in Heliopolis.

**15** I have not perished, and my eye has not perished. I shall not perish; my eye shall not perish.

**16** Osiris breathes. Osiris lives. Osiris has not died. Osiris shall not die.
