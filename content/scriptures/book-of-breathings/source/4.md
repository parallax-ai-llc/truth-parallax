**Section IV: Eternal Life and the Transformation**

**1** Hail, Osiris, the justified! You are the son of Nut. You are the first among the brethren of the gods.

**2** You are the heir of Geb. You are the beloved of your father. Your mother Nut stretches herself above you.

**3** She conceals you from all evil. She protects you from every harm.

**4** She places you among the imperishable stars. You shall not perish, you shall not be destroyed.

**5** Your face is the face of Anubis. Your flesh is the flesh of Atum. Your head is the head of Horus of the horizon.

**6** You fly as a falcon. You alight as a heron. You soar as a crane. You live as a scarab.

**7** You sit upon your throne of iron. You lean upon your staff of copper. The gods come to you bowing.

**8** Geb gives you your two eyes that you may see. He gives you your two ears that you may hear.

**9** He gives you your mouth that you may speak. He gives you your two legs that you may walk.

**10** He gives you your two arms that you may strike down your enemies.

**11** Your throat breathes. Your heart beats. Your members move. You command your limbs. You count your bones.

**12** You come forth to heaven with Ra. You sail in the morning with Khepri. You set in the evening with Atum.

**13** The stars of the sky worship you. The pillars of the earth bow to you.

**14** You eat bread in the presence of Osiris. You drink beer in the presence of the Great God. You are satisfied with the offerings.

**15** Your house endures. Your plantation flourishes. Your name lives upon the earth.

**16** You breathe through your nose. You live through your throat. You are a god. You shall exist for ever and ever.

**17** Your soul breathes wherever it wishes. You are called by name and you answer. You shall not die a second death.

**18** O Osiris, the justified, born of the sky, created of Nut! You are divine. You have become a spirit. You are mighty among the gods.

**19** Anubis, who is upon his mountain, has embalmed you. The god who is in the embalming chamber has treated you.

**20** Your ba-soul goes wherever it wishes. You live again. You are young again, forever and ever.

**21** The Book of Breathings is your protection. Breathe! Breathe! For ever and ever!

**22** [Colophon: Placed beneath the left arm of the deceased, wrapped in royal linen, written on the inside in a column.]
