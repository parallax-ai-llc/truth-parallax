---
title: "The Seven Sisters"
scripture: "dreamtime-stories"
chapter: 5
---

**1** In the Dreaming, seven sisters traveled across the land. They were beautiful, and they were pursued by a man named Wati Nyiru, who desired them.

**2** The sisters ran from east to west, across deserts and mountains. Wherever they stopped, they created features of the landscape: a waterhole here, a rock formation there.

**3** Wati Nyiru followed them, always following, never giving up. He used magic to try to catch them, but the sisters were clever and always escaped.

**4** At last the sisters reached the western sea. They could run no further. So they leaped into the sky and became the stars of the Pleiades.

**5** Wati Nyiru leaped after them and became the star that follows the Pleiades across the sky, always pursuing, never catching.

**6** When the Pleiades rise in the eastern sky, the people know that the season is changing. The Seven Sisters mark the time of ceremony, the time of gathering, the rhythms of the year.
