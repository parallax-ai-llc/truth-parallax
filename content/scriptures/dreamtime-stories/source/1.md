---
title: "The Rainbow Serpent"
scripture: "dreamtime-stories"
chapter: 1
---

**1** In the Dreaming, before the world had form, the Rainbow Serpent lay sleeping beneath the earth. The earth was flat and featureless, without hills or rivers or trees.

**2** When the Rainbow Serpent awoke, she pushed her way through the surface of the earth. She traveled across the land, and wherever she went, she left behind her the marks of her passage.

**3** Where she pushed up the earth, mountains formed. Where she carved through the ground, rivers flowed. Where she rested, great hollows filled with water and became lakes.

**4** The Rainbow Serpent called to the frogs: Come out! And the frogs came out of the ground, their bellies full of water. She tickled their bellies, and the water flowed out of them and filled the rivers and lakes.

**5** Then the grass grew and the trees grew and the flowers bloomed, and the land was covered with life. And the Rainbow Serpent said: Those who keep the law shall be rewarded. Those who break the law shall be turned to stone.

**6** The Rainbow Serpent is still here. She lives in the deep waterholes, in the rivers and the billabongs. When the rainbow appears in the sky after rain, it is the Rainbow Serpent moving from one waterhole to another.
