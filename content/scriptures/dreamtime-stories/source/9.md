---
title: "The Law of the Land"
scripture: "dreamtime-stories"
chapter: 9
---

**1** The law was given in the Dreaming, and it cannot be changed. The law tells the people how to live, how to care for the land, how to treat each other.

**2** The law says: The land does not belong to the people. The people belong to the land. We are the custodians of the land, not its owners.

**3** The law says: Respect the sacred sites. Do not disturb the resting places of the Ancestor Spirits. Do not take from the land more than you need.

**4** The law says: Care for your kinfolk. Share what you have. Help those who are in need. The strong must protect the weak.

**5** The law says: Perform the ceremonies at the proper times. Sing the songs. Dance the dances. Tell the stories to the young. If the ceremonies are not performed, the world will lose its balance.

**6** The law says: When a wrong is done, it must be put right. The elders will decide the punishment. Justice must be fair and must restore the balance.

**7** This is the law of the Dreaming. It has been the law since the beginning, and it will be the law until the end.
