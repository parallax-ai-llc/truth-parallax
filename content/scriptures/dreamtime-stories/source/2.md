---
title: "The Sun Woman"
scripture: "dreamtime-stories"
chapter: 2
---

**1** In the Dreaming, the world was in darkness. The people lived in darkness and felt their way through the cold.

**2** Then the Sun Woman, Yhi, woke from her sleep. She opened her eyes, and light flooded the world. She breathed, and warmth spread across the land.

**3** Yhi walked across the earth, and where she walked, plants sprang up behind her. She walked into the caves, and the insects came out to follow her light. She walked to the frozen lakes, and the fish began to swim in the melting water.

**4** She gave legs to the worms and they became lizards. She gave wings to some creatures and they became birds. She gave fur to some and they became animals.

**5** When she had finished her work, Yhi sank below the western horizon. The world was plunged into darkness again, and the creatures cried out in fear.

**6** But Yhi promised: I will return. Every day I will cross the sky, bringing light and warmth. And when I sink in the west, I will rise again in the east. This is my promise to the world.

**7** And so the Sun Woman crosses the sky each day, and each evening she sinks in the west, and each morning she rises again in the east, and the world has light.
