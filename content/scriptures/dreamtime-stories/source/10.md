---
title: "The Dreaming Endures"
scripture: "dreamtime-stories"
chapter: 10
---

**1** The Dreaming is not the past. The Dreaming is now. The Dreaming is always. It is the eternal present in which the Ancestor Spirits live and act.

**2** When a person goes to a sacred site and performs the proper ceremony, they enter the Dreaming. They stand in the presence of the Ancestor Spirits. They participate in the creation of the world.

**3** The land is alive with the Dreaming. Every rock, every tree, every waterhole, every hill is part of the Dreaming. The land is a sacred text, written by the Ancestor Spirits, and those who know how to read it can understand the meaning of creation.

**4** The Dreaming connects all things: the people and the land, the living and the dead, the past and the present and the future. Nothing is separate. Everything is connected.

**5** This is the oldest continuous culture on earth. For tens of thousands of years, the people have kept the Dreaming alive. They have sung the songs, performed the ceremonies, walked the songlines, and cared for the land.

**6** And as long as the songs are sung and the ceremonies are performed and the law is kept, the Dreaming will endure. The Ancestor Spirits will watch over the land. The land will provide for the people. And the world will continue in its proper balance.
