---
title: "Shepherd of Hermas - Chapter 6"
scripture: "shepherd-of-hermas"
chapter: 6
---

**1** "First of all, believe that God is one, who made all things and completed them, and made all things to be out of that which was not, and contains all things, and is Himself alone uncontained.

**2** "Believe in Him therefore, and fear Him, and in this fear be continent. Keep these things, and you shall cast away all wickedness from you, and shall put on every virtue of righteousness, and shall live to God, if you keep this commandment."

**3** He said to me: "Love truth, and let nothing but truth proceed from your mouth, that the spirit which God has made to dwell in this flesh may be found truthful by all men.

**4** "In this way the Lord, who dwells in you, will be glorified, for the Lord is truthful in every word, and there is no lie in Him."

**5** "Those therefore who lie deny the Lord and become robbers of the Lord, for they do not give back to Him the deposit which they have received. For they received from Him a spirit free from lies. If they return this as a lying spirit, they have defiled the commandment of the Lord and become robbers."

**6** When I heard these things, I wept bitterly. Seeing me weep, he said: "Why do you weep?" "Because, sir," I said, "I know not if I can be saved." "Why?" he said. "Because, sir, I have never in my life spoken a true word, but have lived always cunningly with all men, and described my falsehood as truth to all men. And no one ever contradicted me, but faith was placed in my word."

**7** "Sir," I said, "how then can I live, since I have done these things?" "Your thought is good and true," he said, "for you ought as a servant of God to have walked in truth, and not to have joined an evil conscience with the spirit of truth, nor to have caused grief to the holy and true Spirit."

**8** "But now I tell you: refrain from falsehood, and speak nothing but the truth from this time forward. And the former falsehoods will become trustworthy through the finding of your truth."

**9** "If you keep this commandment, you will be able to attain to life. And whoever keeps this commandment, and abstains from falsehood, shall live to God."

**10** The shepherd, the angel of repentance, said these things to me.
