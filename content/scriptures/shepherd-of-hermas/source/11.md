---
title: "Shepherd of Hermas - Chapter 11"
scripture: "shepherd-of-hermas"
chapter: 11
---

**1** "I charge you," he said, "in the name of the Lord, to guard yourself in this commandment concerning false prophets. For he who masters this commandment shall have life.

**2** "First, fear the Lord and keep His commandments. If you keep the commandments of God, you will be powerful in every action, and every action will be beyond compare.

**3** "For by fearing the Lord you shall do everything well. This is the fear which you must have in order to be saved.

**4** "But do not fear the devil. If you fear the Lord, you will master the devil, because there is no power in him.

**5** "Fear the Lord, therefore, and you will live to Him. And all those who fear Him and keep His commandments shall live to God."

**6** "I said to him: 'Sir, you said to me that he who keeps these commandments shall live to God. I should like to know in what way they shall live and in what way they shall be preserved.'

**7** "He said to me: 'Those who believe in the Lord through His Son, and clothe themselves in these spirits, shall live. For the commandments of the Lord are great, and glorious, and magnificent.'

**8** "I said to him: 'Sir, can these commandments be kept by man?' He said: 'If you set it before yourself that they can be kept, you will easily keep them, and they will not be hard.

**9** "'But if you already take it into your heart that they cannot be kept by man, you will not keep them. But now I say to you: if you do not keep them, but neglect them, you will not be saved, nor your children, nor your household.'

**10** "These things he said to me, the shepherd, the angel of repentance."
