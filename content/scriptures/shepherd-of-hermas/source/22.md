---
title: "Shepherd of Hermas - Chapter 22"
scripture: "shepherd-of-hermas"
chapter: 22
---

**1** He showed me a great vision concerning the stones of the tower and said: "See this great work. I wish to explain these things to you, that you may understand and know all things."

**2** "Hear now," he said. "The faithful are those who have heard the word and received it. They keep the commandments of God, and their life is in these commandments.

**3** "But those who are double-minded and who doubt — they are like the weeds among the wheat. They have not the root of life in themselves.

**4** "Therefore, be single-minded and doubt not at all, and you shall receive from the Lord everything that you ask.

**5** "But those who are double-minded — unless they repent quickly, they shall have no life, for double-mindedness is an earthly spirit from the devil, and has great power.

**6** "Put on therefore faith, which has great power, for faith promises all things and completes all things. But doubt, which has no full faith in itself, fails in all the works it does.

**7** "You see then," he said, "that faith comes from above, from the Lord, and has great power. But doubt is an earthly spirit that has no power."

**8** "Serve faith therefore, which has power, and refrain from doubt, which has no power, and you shall live to God. And all those who think thus shall live to God."

**9** "Blessed are all those who practise righteousness. They shall never be destroyed. But the double-minded shall be rooted out from the earth, for they bear no fruit."

**10** "He who has the Lord in his heart can master all things and all these commandments. But for those who have the Lord on their lips, but whose hearts are hardened and who are far from the Lord — for them these commandments are hard and difficult."
