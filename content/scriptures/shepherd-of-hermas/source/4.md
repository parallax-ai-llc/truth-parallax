---
title: "Shepherd of Hermas - Chapter 4"
scripture: "shepherd-of-hermas"
chapter: 4
---

**1** A vision was shown to me, brethren, as I was fasting and seated on a certain mountain, and giving thanks to the Lord for all that He had done to me.

**2** I saw the aged woman whom I had seen before, walking and reading a little book. She said to me: "Can you report these things to the elect of God?" I said to her: "Lady, I cannot remember so much; but give me the little book to copy." "Take it," she said, "and give it back to me."

**3** I took it and went away to a certain part of the country, and copied it all, letter by letter, for I could not distinguish the syllables.

**4** Then, when I had finished the letters of the little book, it was suddenly snatched out of my hand; but by whom I did not see.

**5** After fifteen days, when I had fasted and prayed much to the Lord, the knowledge of the writing was revealed to me. And this is what was written:

**6** "Your seed, Hermas, has sinned against God, and they have blasphemed the Lord, and in their great wickedness they have betrayed their parents, and they got the name of betrayers of parents, and they did not profit from their betrayal.

**7** "And still they added to their sins wanton deeds and piled up wickedness, and so their transgressions were made complete.

**8** "But make these words known to all your children and to your wife, who shall in future be to you as a sister. For she also does not restrain her tongue, with which she does wickedness; but on hearing these words she will restrain it, and will obtain mercy.

**9** "After you have made known to them these words which the Master has commanded me to reveal to you, then all the sins which they have formerly committed shall be forgiven them, and they shall be forgiven to all the saints who have sinned up to this day, if they repent with their whole heart, and put aside double-mindedness from their hearts.

**10** "For the Master has sworn by His glory concerning His elect: if there is still sin after this day has been fixed as a limit, they shall not find salvation; for repentance for the righteous has an end; the days of repentance for all the saints are fulfilled, but for the heathen repentance is open until the last day."
