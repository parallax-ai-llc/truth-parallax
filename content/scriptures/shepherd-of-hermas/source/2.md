---
title: "Shepherd of Hermas - Chapter 2"
scripture: "shepherd-of-hermas"
chapter: 2
---

**1** After she had spoken these words, the heavens were shut, and I was filled with sorrow and fear. I said to myself: "If this sin is recorded against me, how shall I be saved?"

**2** While I was considering and doubting these things in my heart, I saw before me a great white chair made of snow-white wool. And there came an aged woman in shining garments, holding a book in her hands, and she sat down alone and greeted me: "Hail, Hermas!"

**3** And I, in my grief and weeping, said: "Hail, lady!" And she said to me: "Why are you gloomy, Hermas? You who are patient and good-tempered, who are always laughing, why are you so downcast in appearance and not cheerful?"

**4** I said to her: "Because of a most excellent lady, who says that I sinned against her."

**5** She said: "Far be this thing from the servant of God! But no doubt the desire for her did enter your heart. It is such a desire as this which, for the servants of God, produces sin. For it is an evil and mad purpose in a chaste and already approved spirit, to desire an evil deed."

**6** "But God is not angry with you on account of this, but in order that you should convert your family, which has sinned against the Lord and against you, their parents. But you are indulgent, and do not correct your family, but have allowed them to become corrupt. This is the reason the Lord is angry with you."

**7** "But He will heal all the former evil deeds in your family. For because of their sins and iniquities, you have been destroyed by the affairs of daily life."

**8** "But the great mercy of the Lord has taken pity on you and on your family, and will strengthen you and establish you in His glory."

**9** "Only do not be negligent, but have courage and strengthen your family. For as the smith, hammering his work, overcomes the task he wishes, so also the daily righteous word overcomes all wickedness."

**10** "Therefore do not cease to correct your children, for I know that if they repent with all their heart, they will be enrolled in the books of life with the saints."
