## Overview

The Eastern Orthodox encounter with the Shepherd of Hermas finds deep resonance with the tradition's theology of repentance (metanoia) as an ongoing process of spiritual transformation. The text's vision of the Church as pre-existent and cosmic aligns with Orthodox ecclesiology. The patristic Fathers who commended the Shepherd — Irenaeus, Clement, Origen — are pillars of the Orthodox theological tradition.
