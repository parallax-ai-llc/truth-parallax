## Overview

The Protestant engagement with the Shepherd of Hermas reflects on a text that was nearly included in the New Testament canon. The work provides evidence for early Christian theology before the boundaries of orthodoxy were firmly established, raising important questions about the relationship between Scripture, tradition, and authority. The Mandates offer ethical teaching that resonates with Protestant emphasis on sanctification and holy living.
