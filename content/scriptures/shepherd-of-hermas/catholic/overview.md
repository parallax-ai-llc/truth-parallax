## Overview

The Catholic reading of the Shepherd of Hermas values the text as a foundational witness to the theology of penance that would become central to Catholic sacramental practice. The teaching that post-baptismal sin can be forgiven through repentance anticipates the development of the sacrament of reconciliation. The ecclesiology of the Tower — the Church as pre-existent and cosmic — resonates with Catholic understanding of the Church as both divine institution and pilgrim people.
