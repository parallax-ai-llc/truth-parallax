## Overview

The Táin Bó Cúailnge is the most studied text in the medieval Irish literary corpus. Academic scholarship examines the text's complex manuscript tradition (with two main recensions and numerous interpolations), its relationship to earlier oral tradition, and its place within the Ulster Cycle and the broader context of Celtic heroic literature. Scholars analyze the Old and Middle Irish language of the text, its verse insertions, its narrative structure, and the historical and cultural context of Iron Age and early medieval Ireland that it reflects.
