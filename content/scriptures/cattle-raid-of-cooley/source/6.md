---
title: "Cattle Raid of Cooley Section 6"
scripture: "cattle-raid-of-cooley"
chapter: 6
---

**1** Then the Morrigan came to Cuchulainn in the form of a beautiful young woman and offered him her love and her aid. But Cuchulainn refused her, saying: "I have no time for a woman's love when I am engaged in feats of war."

**2** The Morrigan was angered, and she said: "Then I shall hinder you in battle. When you fight at the ford, I shall come against you in the form of an eel and trip you in the water; I shall come as a grey she-wolf and stampede the cattle into the ford; I shall come as a hornless red heifer and lead the cattle against you."

**3** Cuchulainn replied: "You are no easier for me than for anyone, but I shall strike you with each wound, and those wounds shall not be healed unless I myself bless you."

**4** When next Cuchulainn fought at the ford, the Morrigan came against him as she had threatened, in three shapes. But he wounded her each time: he broke the eel's ribs, he cast a stone that put out the wolf's eye, and he broke the heifer's leg.

**5** Later, the Morrigan came to him as an old woman milking a cow with three teats, and she was wounded in three places. Cuchulainn blessed her thrice for drinks of milk, and each blessing healed one wound, but he did not know it was she.

**6** Thus the Morrigan was healed, but her enmity was turned, and in the final battle she would fight on Cuchulainn's side.
