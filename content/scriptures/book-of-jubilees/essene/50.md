## The Final Revelation

The Essene community understood Jubilees 50 ("The Sabbath Laws and Jubilees") as addressing the culmination of the jubilee chronology and the establishment of definitive law for the end times. The chapter's legal prescriptions — particularly regarding sabbath, festivals, and purity — represented the community's own practice, confirming that they alone faithfully preserved the revealed order.

## Sabbath and Sacred Time

The chapter's detailed sabbath legislation matched the rigorous observance practiced at Qumran, where the community maintained absolute sabbath rest as described in the heavenly tablets. The 364-day calendar ensured that sabbaths and festivals never conflicted, a perfection the community contrasted with the confusion of the lunisolar calendar used by their opponents.

## Eschatological Anticipation

The jubilee framework reaching its conclusion in these chapters confirmed the community's expectation that the present age was approaching its end. The final jubilee would bring divine judgment, the defeat of Mastema and his spirits, and the vindication of the righteous remnant who had maintained the correct calendar and law throughout the period of apostasy.
