## Chapter 25 — Rebecca Instructs Jacob

Jubilees 25 presents Rebecca's instructions to Jacob regarding the birthright and blessing, and her role in guiding the transfer of the spiritual inheritance from Esau to Jacob. Gnostic interpreters see Rebecca here as a Sophia figure orchestrating the transmission of pneumatic election.

## Gnostic Interpretive Lens

Rebecca's active role in Jubilees 25 — instructing Jacob, perceiving the spiritual unfitness of Esau, and engineering the transfer of the blessing — elevates her beyond a passive maternal figure to a wisdom agent. In Gnostic mythology, *Sophia* acts decisively to ensure that the pneumatic seed reaches its proper recipient, even when this requires circumventing the established material order (primogeniture). Rebecca's knowledge of Jacob's spiritual superiority is itself a form of *gnosis* — direct perception of spiritual reality that contradicts surface appearances.

Esau's intermarriage with Canaanite women, which grieves Rebecca in this chapter, represents in Gnostic terms the *hylic* soul's integration into the material world's structures. Esau does not merely marry; he merges with the archontic population, losing whatever spiritual potential he might have possessed. His marriages are the anti-type of pneumatic election — a voluntary binding to the material order that forecloses spiritual ascent.

The tension between Isaac's preference for Esau and Rebecca's knowledge of Jacob's election dramatizes the conflict between *psychic* and *pneumatic* perception. Isaac, operating at the *psychic* level, judges by material criteria — Esau's hunting skill, his provision of savory food. Rebecca perceives at the *pneumatic* level, discerning spiritual destinies invisible to the *psychic* eye. This conflict between two modes of seeing — one bound to appearances, the other penetrating to essence — is a defining feature of Gnostic epistemology.

## Scholarly Assessment

Maddalena Scopello's research on women in Gnostic texts has shown that the biblical matriarchs, particularly Rebecca, served as prototypes for Gnostic Sophia figures who act with decisive agency in the transmission of spiritual knowledge. Jubilees 25's enhanced portrait of Rebecca as the one who perceives and orchestrates spiritual election provided Gnostic interpreters with a narrative in which feminine wisdom overrides patriarchal authority — a reversal that maps directly onto the Sophia myth's dynamic of feminine divine knowledge operating within and against the Demiurge's patriarchal order.
