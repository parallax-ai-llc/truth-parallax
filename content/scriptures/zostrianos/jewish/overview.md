## Overview

Jewish readers find in Zostrianos a text that closely parallels the Jewish Hekhalot (heavenly palaces) mystical tradition. The multi-stage ascent through heavenly realms, the successive purifications and sealings at each level, the encounter with divine beings at each stage, and the culminating vision of the highest God all mirror the structure of the Hekhalot literature (Hekhalot Rabbati, Hekhalot Zutarti, 3 Enoch). The parallel is sufficiently close that scholars have debated direct influence, shared sources, or parallel development within a common cultural environment.
