---
title: "Zostrianos Section 7"
scripture: "zostrianos"
chapter: 7
---

**1** And I descended through the aeons, receiving back the garments that I had removed during the ascent. But now the garments were transparent to me, for I knew them for what they were.

**2** And as I descended through each aeon, I spoke to the beings who dwelt there, and I shared with them the knowledge that I had received.

**3** And in the aeon of the Autogenes, I spoke to the four great lights and their attendants. And they rejoiced, for they perceived in my words a confirmation of the truth that they already knew.

**4** And in the aeon of Protophanes, I spoke of the nature of Being. And the minds that dwell there received my words and were strengthened.

**5** And in the lower aeons, I spoke to the souls that were still clothed in the garments of ignorance. And some of them heard and were awakened, and some did not hear, for their garments were too thick.

**6** And the angel said: Do not grieve for those who do not hear. The Father is patient, and the messengers will come again. No soul is abandoned, though some require many messengers and many lifetimes before they awaken.

**7** And I returned to the material world, and I put on again the garment of flesh. And the garment was heavy and dark, but I knew that beneath it, I was light.

**8** And I opened my eyes and saw the world of matter, and it appeared to me as it truly is: a dream, a shadow, a reflection of reflections. But within the dream, there are dreamers who can awaken.

**9** And I resolved to speak the truth to those who would hear. I would not force the truth upon anyone, for the truth cannot be forced. But I would make it available, like a spring of water in the desert.

**10** And those who thirst would come to the spring, and those who are satisfied with sand would pass by. But the spring would always be there, waiting for the thirsty.
