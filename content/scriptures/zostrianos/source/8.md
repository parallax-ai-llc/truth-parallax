---
title: "Zostrianos Section 8"
scripture: "zostrianos"
chapter: 8
---

**1** And I, Zostrianos, wrote down all that I had seen and heard. I inscribed it on three tablets of wood and left them as a testimony.

**2** And these are the things that must be known: that the Father exists above all things, invisible and unknowable, and yet he is the source of all that is.

**3** That the aeons proceed from the Father through the triple-powered one, and they constitute the fullness of the divine life.

**4** That the soul has descended from the fullness into the material world, and that it is clothed in garments of ignorance, and that it has forgotten its origin.

**5** That the way of return is through knowledge, through the baptisms of purification, through the removal of the garments of ignorance, and through the recovery of the original light.

**6** That the Father has not abandoned the soul but has sent messengers to awaken it, and that the messengers continue to come until every soul has been awakened.

**7** And these are the warnings that I leave: Beware of those who teach that the material world is the whole of reality. They are blind, and they lead others into blindness.

**8** Beware of those who teach that the God of this world is the true God. He is the Demiurge, the craftsman, not the Father. The Father is above the Demiurge, as the sun is above the candle.

**9** Beware of the false baptisms, which use water but do not confer knowledge. The true baptism is the baptism of the mind, the washing of the soul in the light of truth.

**10** And seek always the spark within you, for it is your guide and your compass. It points always toward the Father, even when you do not know the way. Follow the spark, and you will find the light.

**11** This is the teaching of Zostrianos, set down for those who seek the truth. May the great invisible Spirit, the Father of all things, grant knowledge and rest to all who read these words.

**12** Zostrianos. Words of Truth of Zostrianos. God of Truth. Words of Zostrianos.
