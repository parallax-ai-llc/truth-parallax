---
title: "Zostrianos Section 2"
scripture: "zostrianos"
chapter: 2
---

**1** Then the angel led me upward, through the first aeon and into the second. And I perceived that each aeon was greater than the one below it, more luminous and more full of power.

**2** And in the second aeon I was baptized. I was baptized in the name of the Autogenes, the self-begotten one. And the baptism was not with water but with knowledge.

**3** And after the baptism, I was given a new garment, brighter than the first. And the angel said: You have been washed of the first ignorance. Now you may ascend further.

**4** And I ascended through the second aeon, and I saw the powers of the Autogenes. They were four great lights: Harmozel, Oroiael, Daveithai, and Eleleth. And each light governed its own realm.

**5** And in the presence of these lights, I was baptized a second time. And this baptism was in the name of the great invisible Spirit. And my garment became still brighter.

**6** And the angel said: The baptisms are purifications. Each one removes a layer of ignorance and clothes you in a deeper knowledge. There are many baptisms on the way to the truth.

**7** And I said: How many baptisms must I receive?

**8** He said: As many as there are aeons. For each aeon reveals a new aspect of the truth, and each baptism prepares you to receive that revelation.

**9** And I ascended into the third aeon, the aeon of the triple-powered one. And there I beheld Protophanes, the first-appearing one, the mind of the divine.

**10** And Protophanes spoke to me, saying: Zostrianos, you who seek the truth, behold the mind of the Father. For the mind contains all things, and all things proceed from the mind, and all things return to the mind.
