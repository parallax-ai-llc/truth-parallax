---
title: "Zostrianos Section 5"
scripture: "zostrianos"
chapter: 5
---

**1** And the angel led me into the region of the great invisible Spirit. And there was no form and no image, but only light, boundless and without qualification.

**2** And I received the fifth baptism, the baptism of the great invisible Spirit himself. And this baptism dissolved the last vestiges of separateness within me.

**3** And I perceived that the great invisible Spirit is not a being among beings, not even the highest being. He is the source of being, the ground of all existence, the silence from which all speech proceeds.

**4** And in his presence, all the aeons that I had passed through were revealed as his emanations, his self-expressions, his thoughts made manifest. And I saw that the entire hierarchy of the aeons is the self-unfolding of the one Father.

**5** And I said: Is there anything beyond this?

**6** And the angel said: There is the unknowable. The Father himself does not exhaust the depth of his own being. There is always more, always deeper, always further. The ascent has no final destination, for the Father is infinite.

**7** And I was astonished, for I had thought that the ascent would end in a fixed point, a final resting place. But the angel revealed to me that rest itself is not static but dynamic. It is the eternal movement of the Father within himself.

**8** And the great invisible Spirit communicated to me without words, without images, without concepts. He communicated through the direct touch of being upon being.

**9** And in that communication, I understood all things at once. I understood the origin of the aeons, the fall of the soul, the nature of matter, and the way of return. All these things were contained in a single, instantaneous knowing.

**10** And the knowing was not mine. It was the Father knowing himself through me. I was the instrument of his self-knowledge, and in being his instrument, I found my own truth.
