---
title: "Zostrianos Section 6"
scripture: "zostrianos"
chapter: 6
---

**1** And having received the knowledge of the great invisible Spirit, I was instructed concerning the nature of the soul and its descent into the material world.

**2** The soul is a portion of the divine light. It descends through the aeons, passing through Kalyptos and Protophanes and the Autogenes, and at each level it receives a garment.

**3** The garment of the spiritual world is pure light. The garment of the psychic world is a mixture of light and shadow. The garment of the material world is the body of flesh.

**4** And the soul, clothed in these garments, forgets its origin. It identifies with its garments and thinks itself to be a creature of the material world.

**5** But the spark of the divine remains within the soul, hidden beneath the garments. And the spark cries out to the Father, though the soul does not know why it cries.

**6** The restlessness of the human being, the longing for something beyond the material world, the dissatisfaction with all earthly things—this is the cry of the spark, seeking its origin.

**7** And the Father hears the cry, and he sends messengers to awaken the soul. The messengers speak in parables and symbols, for the soul in its garments cannot receive the naked truth.

**8** But to those who are ready, the messengers speak plainly. They say: You are not of this world. Your origin is in the light. Your destiny is to return to the light. Put off the garments of ignorance and clothe yourself in the garments of knowledge.

**9** And the soul that hears this message and receives it begins the ascent. It passes through the baptisms, and at each baptism it removes a garment, until at last it stands naked before the Father, clothed only in the light that is its true nature.

**10** And the angel said to me: Zostrianos, you have experienced this ascent. Now you must return and bear witness to those who dwell below.
