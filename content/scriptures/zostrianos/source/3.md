---
title: "Zostrianos Section 3"
scripture: "zostrianos"
chapter: 3
---

**1** And in the aeon of Protophanes I was baptized a third time. And I received the knowledge of Being, as it truly is, not as it appears in the world of forms.

**2** And I perceived that Being is not a thing but an activity. The Father does not exist as a static object but as an eternal process of self-knowing. He is the thinking that thinks itself.

**3** And Protophanes said: The ignorant ones below think that existence is mere extension in space and time. But true Being is the activity of knowing, the light that illumines itself.

**4** And I asked: What is the relationship between the Being that is above and the beings that are below?

**5** He said: The beings below are images, reflections of the true Being. They have existence insofar as they participate in the Being above. But in themselves they are nothing.

**6** It is like a mirror: the reflection exists, but it has no substance of its own. Remove the mirror, and the reflection vanishes. But the original remains.

**7** And I said: Then the material world is a reflection?

**8** He said: It is a reflection of a reflection. The first reflection is the psychic world, which reflects the spiritual. The second reflection is the material world, which reflects the psychic. Each reflection is more distant from the original, and more distorted.

**9** And I understood that the ascent through the aeons is the return from reflection to original, from image to reality, from distortion to truth.

**10** Then I was taken higher, beyond the aeon of Protophanes, into the aeon of Kalyptos, the hidden one. And in that aeon, the light was so great that I could scarcely endure it.
