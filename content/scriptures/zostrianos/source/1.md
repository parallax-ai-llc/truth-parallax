---
title: "Zostrianos Section 1"
scripture: "zostrianos"
chapter: 1
---

**1** The words of truth of Zostrianos. God of truth. Words of the teaching of Zostrianos. An angel of the knowledge of the eternal light spoke to me.

**2** I was pondering great matters. I was seeking the God of truth, whether he truly exists, and whether the great aeons are as they are, and whether the truth is different from what appears.

**3** When I was deeply disturbed and despairing, I was about to deliver myself to destruction. Then a messenger of the knowledge of the eternal light appeared to me.

**4** He said to me: Zostrianos, why have you gone mad as if you are ignorant of the great eternal ones who are above? Speak, that I may teach you about the things which you have not known.

**5** He said: How long will you search as though you are one who is mortal? The truth is not in the visible world. Seek the invisible.

**6** And I said: I have been searching, but I have not found. What is the way to the truth?

**7** He said: Come, and I will show you. I will take you through the aeons, and you shall see the truth with your own eyes. But first you must leave behind the garment of flesh.

**8** And I left behind the body of darkness. I separated myself from the psychic God and his angels. And I stood in the region between the visible and the invisible.

**9** Then I saw the first aeon, and I perceived the powers that dwell within it. And the angel said to me: This is the first heaven, the dwelling place of the righteous souls.

**10** And I saw the souls that dwell there, and they were clothed in garments of light, dimmer than the light above but brighter than the darkness below. And they were at rest, but they had not yet attained the fullness.
