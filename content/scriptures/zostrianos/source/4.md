---
title: "Zostrianos Section 4"
scripture: "zostrianos"
chapter: 4
---

**1** And Kalyptos, the hidden one, is the aeon that conceals and reveals. He conceals the Father from those who are not ready, and he reveals the Father to those who have been prepared through the baptisms.

**2** And in the aeon of Kalyptos I was baptized a fourth time, in the name of the hidden one. And this baptism was a baptism of silence, for no words were spoken and no images were shown.

**3** And in the silence, I perceived that which cannot be perceived through sound or sight. I perceived the presence of the unknowable, the one who is beyond all names and all forms.

**4** And the angel said: You are now in the realm of the triple-powered one: Existence, Vitality, and Mentality. These three are one, and the one is three. And they proceed from the Father, who is beyond the three.

**5** And I asked: How can the three be one?

**6** He said: Existence is the foundation. Vitality is the movement. Mentality is the knowing. But there is no existence without vitality, and no vitality without mentality, and no mentality without existence. They are three aspects of one reality.

**7** And I saw that the triple-powered one is the bridge between the Father and the aeons. The Father is beyond all determination, but through the triple-powered one, he becomes accessible to those who ascend.

**8** And the angel said: You have now passed through the four baptisms. You have been purified of the material ignorance, the psychic ignorance, the intellectual ignorance, and the ignorance of the hidden. There remains one more ascent.

**9** And I trembled, for the light was overwhelming. And the angel strengthened me, saying: Do not fear. The Father does not destroy those who approach him. He receives them.

**10** And I prepared myself for the final ascent, into the region of the great invisible Spirit, the Father of all things.
