## Overview

For Gnostic readers, Zostrianos maps the most complete and detailed spiritual journey in the Nag Hammadi library. Beginning with the prophet's despair — he contemplates suicide, overwhelmed by the futility of material existence — the text traces his rescue by an angelic guide, his progressive ascent through the heavenly realms, his successive baptisms and transformations at each level, and his culminating encounter with the Triple-Powered Invisible Spirit. The text functions as a roadmap for the Gnostic initiate: each level of ascent corresponds to a stage of spiritual development, each baptism to a new degree of purification, and each sealing to a new level of spiritual identity.
