## Overview

Zostrianos's multi-stage ascent through heavenly realms represents one of the most elaborate versions of a pattern found across the world's mystical traditions. The Jewish Hekhalot literature describes passage through seven heavenly palaces with transformative encounters at each; the Islamic mi'raj tradition describes Muhammad's ascent through seven heavens to the divine throne; the Hindu tantric tradition maps the ascent of kundalini energy through the seven chakras; and the Tibetan Buddhist tradition describes passage through the bardo realms between death and rebirth. Each tradition provides a detailed map of the stages through which the practitioner must pass to reach the ultimate reality.
