---
title: "Evening Prayers"
scripture: "qolasta"
chapter: 4
---

**1** In the name of the Great Life. I stand at the end of the day and turn my face to the north.

**2** Great Life, the day is ending. The sun descends beneath the earth. Darkness comes upon the world.

**3** But your light does not set. Your radiance does not diminish. In the darkness of night, your light still shines in the hearts of the faithful.

**4** I give thanks for the day that has passed. For the food I have eaten, for the water I have drunk, for the work I have done, for the words I have spoken.

**5** Forgive me if I have strayed from your way. Forgive me if I have spoken falsely or acted unkindly. Purify my soul as I sleep.

**6** Guard me through the night, O Great Life. Let your Uthras stand watch around me. Let no evil spirit approach me.

**7** And when the morning comes, let me rise again in your light, refreshed and renewed, ready to serve you another day. In the name of the Great Life, I rest. Life is victorious.
