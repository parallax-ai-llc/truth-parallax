---
title: "Prayers for the Sabbath"
scripture: "qolasta"
chapter: 8
---

**1** In the name of the Great Life. The prayers for the holy Sunday, the day of rest.

**2** Great Life, you have given us this day of rest. You have set it apart from the other days. On this day we cease from our labors and turn our hearts to you.

**3** We gather at the mandi, the place of worship. We turn our faces to the north. We remember your commandments and your mercies.

**4** On this day you created the world of light. On this day you brought forth the Uthras. On this day the Great Life shone forth in His radiance.

**5** Let us rest as you have rested. Let us be at peace as you are at peace. Let us dwell in your light as you dwell in the Great Place of Light.

**6** Bless our families, Great Life. Bless our community. Bless our priests. Bless all who keep your commandments.

**7** And when the day of rest is ended, let us return to our labors strengthened and renewed, carrying your light into the world of darkness. In the name of the Great Life.
