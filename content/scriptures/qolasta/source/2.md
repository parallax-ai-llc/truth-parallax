---
title: "Prayers for the Dead"
scripture: "qolasta"
chapter: 2
---

**1** In the name of the Great Life. The prayer of the masiqta, the ascent of the soul.

**2** O Great Life, receive the soul of your servant who has departed from this world. Open the gates of the watch-houses. Let the guardians stand aside.

**3** The soul speaks: I am a child of the Great Life. I was baptized in the Living Water. I kept the commandments. I walked in the way of truth.

**4** Let the Uthras come to meet this soul. Let them guide it through the darkness. Let them bring it to the Great Place of Light.

**5** We prepare the pihta and the mambuha for the departed soul. We speak the sacred formulas. We call upon the Great Life to be merciful.

**6** O soul, do not be afraid. The Great Life is with you. His Uthras surround you. His light goes before you.

**7** Rise, O soul, rise to the world of light. Leave behind the body of clay. Leave behind the sorrows of the material world. Rise to eternal joy and peace.

**8** In the name of the Great Life, the masiqta is performed. The soul ascends. Life is victorious over death.
