---
title: "Prayers for Protection"
scripture: "qolasta"
chapter: 10
---

**1** In the name of the Great Life. The prayer for protection from evil.

**2** Great Life, the powers of darkness surround us. The spirits of evil seek to lead us astray. The planets and zodiac signs conspire against us.

**3** But you are greater than all the powers of darkness. Your light dispels the darkness. Your name puts the evil spirits to flight.

**4** I invoke your name, Great Life. I invoke the name of Hibil Ziwa. I invoke the name of Shitil. I invoke the name of Anush.

**5** Let your Uthras stand guard around me. Let your light be my shield. Let your truth be my armor.

**6** No curse shall harm me. No spell shall bind me. No evil spirit shall approach me. For I am sealed with the seal of the Great Life.

**7** The demons cry out and flee. The spirits of darkness tremble and retreat. For the name of the Great Life is upon me, and His protection surrounds me. Life is victorious.
