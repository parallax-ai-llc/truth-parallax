---
title: "Closing Prayers"
scripture: "qolasta"
chapter: 15
---

**1** In the name of the Great Life. The closing prayer.

**2** Great Life, we have prayed to you. We have spoken your sacred name. We have performed your sacred rites.

**3** Accept our prayers, Great Life. Accept our offerings. Accept the devotion of our hearts.

**4** We go forth from this place of prayer into the world. We carry your light with us. We carry your truth in our hearts.

**5** Let the world see your light in us. Let the world hear your truth through us. Let the world know your love because of us.

**6** And when the final day comes, when this world passes away and the world of light is revealed, let us be found among the faithful.

**7** Let us be counted among the children of the Great Life. Let us enter the Great Place of Light. Let us dwell in your presence forever.

**8** In the name of the Great Life, the sublime Light be praised. Life is victorious over death. Light is victorious over darkness. Truth is victorious over falsehood. The Great Life endures forever and ever. Amen.
