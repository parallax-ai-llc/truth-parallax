---
title: "Prayers of Baptism"
scripture: "qolasta"
chapter: 1
---

**1** In the name of the Great Life. The prayer before the baptism. I pray to you, Great Life, Lord of all worlds, King of Light.

**2** I stand before the Living Water and I seek purification. Wash away my transgressions. Cleanse me from my sins. Make me white as the garment of light.

**3** Great Life, I have come to your Living Water. I have left behind the ways of darkness. I seek your light, your truth, your mercy.

**4** As the water flows over me, let your light flow into my soul. As the water washes my body, let your truth wash my spirit.

**5** I am baptized in your name, Great Life. I am sealed with your seal. I am clothed in your radiance. No power of darkness shall prevail against me.

**6** Blessed is the priest who baptizes. Blessed is the soul that is baptized. Blessed is the Living Water that purifies. Blessed is the Great Life who has ordained the baptism.

**7** In the name of the Great Life, I arise from the water, renewed, purified, and sanctified. Life is victorious.
