---
title: "Prayers for the Sick"
scripture: "qolasta"
chapter: 11
---

**1** In the name of the Great Life. The prayer for healing.

**2** Great Life, your servant is afflicted. Sickness has come upon the body. Pain has entered the limbs.

**3** But you are the healer of all diseases. You are the physician of the soul. Stretch forth your hand and heal your servant.

**4** Let the Living Water flow upon the sick one. Let the light of life enter the body. Let the darkness of disease depart.

**5** We call upon the Uthras to bring healing. We call upon Hibil Ziwa to drive out the sickness. We call upon the Great Life to restore health.

**6** If it is your will, Great Life, heal this body. If it is your will, restore this life. But if the time has come, receive this soul into the world of light.

**7** In sickness and in health, we trust in you. In life and in death, we belong to you. Your will be done, Great Life. Life is victorious.
