---
title: "Hymns of Praise"
scripture: "qolasta"
chapter: 13
---

**1** In the name of the Great Life. A hymn of praise.

**2** Praise to the Great Life, the Lord of all worlds. Praise to the Light that shines in the darkness. Praise to the Truth that endures forever.

**3** The heavens declare your glory, Great Life. The waters sing your praises. The earth trembles before your majesty.

**4** You are before all things, and after all things, and in all things. You are the beginning and the end. You are the Alpha and the Omega of all existence.

**5** The Uthras bow before you. The celestial beings sing your praise. The souls of the righteous glorify your name.

**6** Who can measure your greatness? Who can fathom your wisdom? Who can comprehend your love? You are beyond all understanding.

**7** Yet you care for the smallest soul. You hear the faintest prayer. You see the hidden tear. Great Life, we praise you, we adore you, we give thanks to you. Life is victorious.
