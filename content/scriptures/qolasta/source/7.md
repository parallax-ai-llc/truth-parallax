---
title: "Blessing of the Sacred Meal"
scripture: "qolasta"
chapter: 7
---

**1** In the name of the Great Life. The blessing over the pihta and the mambuha.

**2** Blessed are you, Great Life, who brings forth bread from the earth. Blessed are you who gives us the pihta, the bread of life.

**3** As this bread nourishes the body, so let your truth nourish the soul. As this bread sustains our earthly life, so let your light sustain our eternal life.

**4** Blessed are you, Great Life, who creates the fruit of the vine. Blessed are you who gives us the mambuha, the drink of life.

**5** As this drink refreshes the body, so let your Living Water refresh the soul. As this drink quenches our thirst, so let your truth satisfy our longing.

**6** We eat and drink in the name of the Great Life. We eat and drink in remembrance of the world of light. We eat and drink in hope of our return to the Great Place of Light.

**7** Blessed is the sacred meal. Blessed are those who partake of it in purity and faith. Life is victorious.
