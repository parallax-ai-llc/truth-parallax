---
title: "Morning Prayers"
scripture: "qolasta"
chapter: 3
---

**1** In the name of the Great Life. I rise in the morning and turn my face to the north, to the Place of Light.

**2** Great Life, a new day has dawned. Your light shines upon the world. I give thanks for the breath of life which you have given me.

**3** Protect me this day from the powers of darkness. Guard my steps. Guide my words. Keep my thoughts pure.

**4** Let me walk in your way of truth. Let me speak only what is right. Let me do only what is good.

**5** As the sun rises and illumines the earth, so let your truth rise and illumine my soul.

**6** I remember your commandments. I remember your baptism. I remember your sacred name. In all my deeds this day, I shall seek to honor you.

**7** Great Life, be with me from morning to evening, from evening to morning. Let your Uthras watch over me. Let your light never depart from me.
