---
title: "Prayers of Confession"
scripture: "qolasta"
chapter: 6
---

**1** In the name of the Great Life. The prayer of confession.

**2** Great Life, I have sinned against you. I have fallen short of your commandments. I have strayed from the path of truth.

**3** My eyes have seen what they should not have seen. My ears have heard what they should not have heard. My tongue has spoken what it should not have spoken.

**4** My hands have done what they should not have done. My feet have walked where they should not have walked. My heart has desired what it should not have desired.

**5** But I turn back to you, Great Life. I confess my sins before you. I seek your mercy and your forgiveness.

**6** Wash me in the Living Water. Cleanse me from all impurity. Restore the light within my soul.

**7** I pledge to walk again in your way. I pledge to keep your commandments. I pledge to speak truth and shun lies. Great Life, have mercy upon me.
