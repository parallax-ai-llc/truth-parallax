---
title: "Prayers for Marriage"
scripture: "qolasta"
chapter: 9
---

**1** In the name of the Great Life. The prayer for the sacred marriage.

**2** Great Life, you have created man and woman. You have ordained that they should be joined together in holy union.

**3** Bless this man and this woman who come before you today. Unite them in your truth. Bind them with your love.

**4** As Adam and Hawwa were joined in the beginning, so let these two be joined. Let them walk together in the way of truth.

**5** Grant them children who will keep the commandments. Grant them a household of peace. Grant them years of faithfulness.

**6** Let the husband honor his wife, and let the wife honor her husband. Let them bear one another's burdens and share one another's joys.

**7** And when their time in this world is ended, let their souls ascend together to the world of light, where they shall dwell in your presence forever. In the name of the Great Life.
