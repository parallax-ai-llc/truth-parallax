---
title: "Prayers of the Priest"
scripture: "qolasta"
chapter: 5
---

**1** In the name of the Great Life. The prayer of the priest before performing the sacred rites.

**2** Great Life, you have called me to serve your people. You have placed the sacred knowledge in my keeping. You have given me the authority to baptize and to perform the masiqta.

**3** Grant me wisdom that I may serve faithfully. Grant me purity that I may handle the sacred things worthily.

**4** Let my hands be clean and my heart be pure. Let my words be true and my intentions righteous.

**5** I stand between the world of light and the world of darkness. I bring your light to the people. I bring the prayers of the people to you.

**6** Strengthen me, Great Life, for the task is great and my strength is small. Without your help, I can do nothing. With your help, all things are possible.

**7** And when my time of service is ended, receive me into the world of light, where I may serve you forever among the Uthras. In the name of the Great Life.
