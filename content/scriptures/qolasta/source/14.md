---
title: "Prayers for the New Year"
scripture: "qolasta"
chapter: 14
---

**1** In the name of the Great Life. The prayer for the beginning of the new year, the Dehba Rabba.

**2** Great Life, a new year begins. The old year has passed with its joys and sorrows. A new year lies before us with its hopes and fears.

**3** Bless us in this new year. Grant us health and prosperity. Grant us wisdom and understanding. Grant us faithfulness and devotion.

**4** Forgive the sins of the past year. Wash away the transgressions we have committed. Let us begin anew, clean and pure.

**5** We remember the dead of the past year. We perform the masiqta for their souls. We pray that they have reached the world of light.

**6** And we pray for the living. Strengthen the weak. Comfort the mourning. Guide the young. Honor the old.

**7** Great Life, be with us in this new year as you have been with us in years past. Let your light go before us. Let your truth guide us. Let your love sustain us. Life is victorious.
