---
title: "Prayers for Purification"
scripture: "qolasta"
chapter: 12
---

**1** In the name of the Great Life. The prayer of purification from contact with the dead.

**2** Great Life, I have been in the presence of death. I have touched that which is unclean. My purity has been compromised.

**3** Purify me with the Living Water. Cleanse me with your sacred name. Restore to me the garment of light.

**4** Death is the work of the lord of darkness. Death is the separation of the soul from the body. Death is the door through which the soul passes.

**5** But death has no power over the children of the Great Life. For the soul is immortal. The soul returns to the world of light.

**6** Wash away the impurity of death. Wash away the shadow of the grave. Let me stand again in the light, pure and clean.

**7** In the name of the Great Life, I am purified. The impurity departs. The light returns. I am restored to the community of the faithful. Life is victorious.
