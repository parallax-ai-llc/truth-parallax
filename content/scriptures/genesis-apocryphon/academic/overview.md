## Overview

Academic study of the Genesis Apocryphon employs paleographic analysis, textual criticism, and comparative methods to establish the text's date, provenance, and relationship to other Second Temple Jewish literature. Scholars have situated the text within the broader context of Qumran sectarianism and its implications for understanding the diversity of ancient Judaism.

## Interpretive Approach

Scholarly analysis focuses on textual reconstruction, historical contextualization, and the text's significance for understanding Second Temple Jewish theology, law, worship, and social organization.
