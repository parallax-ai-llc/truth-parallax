chapter: 7

**1** And the king of Egypt, Pharaoh Zoan, heard of the beauty of Sarai, and he sent to bring her. And when she came to him, he was pleased with her greatly.

**2** But God sent great plagues upon Pharaoh and upon his household, afflictions and a great spirit of pestilence.

**3** And he was not able to approach Sarai, nor did he know her. And this went on for two years.

**4** And at the end of two years the plagues grew more severe. And Pharaoh called all the wise men and all the physicians. But they could not heal him.

**5** Then Lot came to Pharaoh and said: These plagues are upon you because of Sarai, the wife of Abram. Return her to Abram, and the plagues will cease.

**6** And Pharaoh called Abram and returned Sarai to him, and gave him gifts, and sent him away. And the plagues ceased, and Abram departed from Egypt with his wife and all his possessions.
