**1** And I, Lamech, was afraid and came to Bitenosh, my wife, and I said to her: I adjure you by the Most High, the Lord of Greatness, the King of all Ages, tell me in truth and without lies, whether this conception is from you, or this seed from one of the Sons of Heaven.

**2** Tell me the truth and do not lie to me. Tell me in truth and not in falsehood. Speak to me and do not deceive me.

**3** Then Bitenosh my wife spoke to me very harshly, and she wept and said: Oh my lord and my brother, remember my pleasure. I swear to you by the Great Holy One, by the King of Heaven, that this seed is from you, this conception is from you, this fruit was planted by you, and not by any stranger, nor by any of the Watchers, nor by any of the Sons of Heaven.

**4** Why has the appearance of your countenance changed and been deformed, and why is your spirit so depressed?

**5** I speak truly to you.
