---
title: "Genesis Apocryphon — Column 5"
scripture: "genesis-apocryphon"
chapter: 5
---

**1** And the land was filled with violence and wickedness. And God said to Noah: The end of all flesh has come before me, for the earth is filled with violence through them. And behold, I will destroy them with the earth.

**2** And I, Noah, built an ark, as God commanded me. And I brought into the ark every living thing of all flesh, two of every kind, to keep them alive with me.

**3** And the waters of the flood came upon the earth, and the fountains of the great deep were broken up, and the windows of heaven were opened.

**4** And the rain was upon the earth forty days and forty nights. And all flesh that moved upon the earth perished.

**5** And God remembered Noah, and every living thing. And God made a wind to pass over the earth, and the waters subsided.

**6** And Noah went forth from the ark, and he built an altar and made offerings to the Most High.
