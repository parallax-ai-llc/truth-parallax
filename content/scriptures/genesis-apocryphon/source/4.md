**1** And behold, I thought in my heart that the conception was from the Watchers, or the conception was from the Holy Ones, or it belonged to the Giants. And my heart within me was troubled regarding this child.

**2** Then I, Lamech, was afraid and I went to Bitenosh my wife and said: Behold, I adjure you by the Most High.

**3** But she spoke the truth to me, and my heart was then at rest. And when the child grew, I called his name Noah, for he would comfort us in our labor and the toil of our hands.

**4** This is the story of Noah, and this is the account of his birth, which was revealed to Lamech his father, and to Methuselah his grandfather.

**5** And Noah grew and was righteous, and he walked with God.
