**1** Then I, Lamech, ran to my father Methuselah and told him everything, so that he might go to Enoch his father and learn everything with certainty from him, since he is beloved and favored by God.

**2** And Methuselah went to Enoch his father to learn everything with certainty. And he went to Parvaim and there he found Enoch.

**3** And he said to Enoch his father: Oh my father and my lord, to whom I have come. I say to you: do not be angry with me because I have come here to you, because there is fear among us concerning this matter.

**4** Listen to me: a child has been born to Lamech my son, whose form and appearance are not like the form and appearance of human beings, but his form is like the children of the angels of heaven.

**5** His nature is different, and he is not like us. And Lamech thinks that he is not from him, but from the angels of heaven.
