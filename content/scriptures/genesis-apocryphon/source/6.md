---
title: "Genesis Apocryphon — Column 6"
scripture: "genesis-apocryphon"
chapter: 6
---

**1** And I, Abram, dreamed a dream on the night of my entry into the land of Egypt. And I saw in my dream a cedar tree and a date-palm growing from a single root.

**2** And men came and sought to cut down and uproot the cedar, and to leave the date-palm by itself. But the date-palm cried out and said: Do not cut down the cedar, for we are both from one root.

**3** And the cedar was spared on account of the date-palm. And it was not cut down.

**4** And I awoke from my sleep that night and I said to Sarai my wife: I have had a dream and I am frightened by this dream.

**5** She said to me: Tell me your dream so that I may know it. And I told her the dream.

**6** And I said: This dream means that they will seek to kill me and to spare you. Therefore say to everyone: He is my brother. And then I shall live on your account, and my soul shall be spared because of you.
