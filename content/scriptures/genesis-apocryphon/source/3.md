scripture: "genesis-apocryphon"
chapter: 3
---

**1** And now I tell you, my son, and I make known to you that when I, Enoch, was alive, the Watchers revealed to me a vision according to the will of the Lord of Greatness, and the holy ones showed it to me, and I heard everything from their mouths.

**2** And I knew and read the heavenly tablets, and I saw what was written upon them, and I understood everything, and I read the book of all the deeds of humanity and of all the children of flesh upon the earth for all the generations of the world.

**3** And so now, my son Methuselah, I say and make known: this child is righteous and trustworthy, and he is not of the Watchers. This child of Lamech is truly his child.

**4** And he shall be called Noah, for he shall be a comfort to you and a rest, and through him the earth shall be renewed.
