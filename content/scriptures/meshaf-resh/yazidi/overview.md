## Overview

The Yazidi reading of the Meshaf Resh engages with this text from within the living tradition of Yazidi thought and practice. The foundational cosmogonic narrative, transmitted orally and through ritual practice. This perspective brings the accumulated wisdom of Yazidi interpretation to bear on the text, illuminating dimensions that may not be apparent from external scholarly perspectives alone.

Within the Yazidi tradition, the Meshaf Resh is understood not merely as a historical document but as a living source of spiritual guidance and theological reflection. Yazidi interpreters bring distinctive hermeneutical principles, doctrinal frameworks, and experiential knowledge to their engagement with this text, producing readings that are both deeply informed and spiritually engaged.
