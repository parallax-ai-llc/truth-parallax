Section 3 of the Meshaf Resh, addressing humanity, holds particular significance within the Yazidi tradition. The themes of Adam, Eve are central to Yazidi theology and practice, and this section has been a focus of traditional interpretation and spiritual reflection.

Within Yazidi tradition, this section's treatment of humanity is understood not merely as historical or theoretical but as a living source of guidance for faith and practice. The teachings on Adam, Eve inform Yazidi self-understanding and continue to shape communal identity and spiritual aspiration.
