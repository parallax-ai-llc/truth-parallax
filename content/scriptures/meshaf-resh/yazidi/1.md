Section 1 of the Meshaf Resh, addressing creation, holds particular significance within the Yazidi tradition. The themes of cosmogony, God are central to Yazidi theology and practice, and this section has been a focus of traditional interpretation and spiritual reflection.

Within Yazidi tradition, this section's treatment of creation is understood not merely as historical or theoretical but as a living source of guidance for faith and practice. The teachings on cosmogony, God inform Yazidi self-understanding and continue to shape communal identity and spiritual aspiration.
