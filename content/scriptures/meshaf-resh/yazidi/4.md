Section 4 of the Meshaf Resh, addressing laws, holds particular significance within the Yazidi tradition. The themes of taboo, dietary laws are central to Yazidi theology and practice, and this section has been a focus of traditional interpretation and spiritual reflection.

Within Yazidi tradition, this section's treatment of laws is understood not merely as historical or theoretical but as a living source of guidance for faith and practice. The teachings on taboo, dietary laws inform Yazidi self-understanding and continue to shape communal identity and spiritual aspiration.
