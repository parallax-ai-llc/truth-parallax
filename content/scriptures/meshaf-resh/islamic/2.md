Section 2 of the Meshaf Resh, with its focus on seven angels, can be examined in relation to Islamic theological and philosophical traditions. The themes of heptad, archangels resonate with discussions in the Quran, hadith literature, and Islamic philosophy.

Islamic scholars and theologians have engaged with these themes from multiple perspectives — kalamic, philosophical, mystical, and legal. The section's treatment of seven angels invites consideration of both convergences and divergences with mainstream Islamic thought, enriching understanding of the broader landscape of monotheistic theology.
