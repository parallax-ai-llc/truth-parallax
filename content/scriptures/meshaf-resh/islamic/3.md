Section 3 of the Meshaf Resh, with its focus on humanity, can be examined in relation to Islamic theological and philosophical traditions. The themes of Adam, Eve resonate with discussions in the Quran, hadith literature, and Islamic philosophy.

Islamic scholars and theologians have engaged with these themes from multiple perspectives — kalamic, philosophical, mystical, and legal. The section's treatment of humanity invites consideration of both convergences and divergences with mainstream Islamic thought, enriching understanding of the broader landscape of monotheistic theology.
