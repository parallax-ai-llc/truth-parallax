---
title: "Meshaf Resh - Chapter 5"
scripture: "meshaf-resh"
chapter: 5
---

**1** God has said: "I was, am now, and shall have no end. I exercise dominion over all creatures and over the affairs of all who are under the protection of my image."

**2** "I am ever present to help all who trust in me and call upon me in time of need. There is no place in the universe that knows not my presence."

**3** "I participate in all the affairs which those who are without call evil, because their nature is not such as they approve. Every age has its own manager, who directs affairs according to my decrees."

**4** "This office is changeable from generation to generation, that the ruler of this world and his chiefs may discharge the duties of the world, each in his turn."

**5** "I allow everyone to follow the dictates of his own nature, but he that opposes me will regret it sorely."

**6** "No god has a right to interfere in my affairs, and I have made it an imperative rule that everyone shall refrain from worshipping all gods."

**7** "All the books of those who are without are altered by them; and they have declined from them, although they were written by the prophets and the apostles. That there are interpolations is seen in the fact that each sect endeavors to prove that the others are wrong and to destroy their books."

**8** "To me truth and falsehood are known. When temptation comes, I give my covenant to him that trusts in me."

**9** "Moreover, I give counsel to the skilled directors, for I have appointed them for periods that are known to me. I remember necessary affairs and execute them in due time."

**10** "I teach and guide those who follow my instruction. If anyone obey me and conform to my commandments, he shall have joy, delight, and goodness."

**11** I, Melek Taus, send a sign to the nations: he who obeys not shall be cut off. I am king over all the kings of the earth. Let the people honor the sacred bond and worship the one light that has no beginning and no end.

**12** These words are the seal of the Black Book, the Meshaf Resh, given to the Yezidi people as their covenant and their inheritance forever.
