---
title: "Meshaf Resh - Chapter 1"
scripture: "meshaf-resh"
chapter: 1
---

**1** In the beginning God created the White Pearl from His most precious essence. He also created a bird named Anfar. And He placed the Pearl on the back of the bird, and dwelt on it for forty thousand years.

**2** On the first day, Sunday, God created Azazil, and he is Melek Taus, the chief of all the angels.

**3** On Monday He created the angel Darda'il, who is Sheikh Hasan.

**4** On Tuesday He created the angel Israfil, who is Sheikh Shams.

**5** On Wednesday He created the angel Mika'il, who is Sheikh Abu Bakr.

**6** On Thursday He created the angel Jibra'il, who is Sajadin.

**7** On Friday He created the angel Shimna'il, who is Nasirdin.

**8** On Saturday He created the angel Nura'il, who is Yadin, who is Fakhradin.

**9** And God made Melek Taus the chief over all the angels, and over the world. He placed him in authority.

**10** Then God cried out to the White Pearl with a mighty shout. And the Pearl was shattered into four pieces.

**11** From the first piece He made the heavens. From the second piece He made the earth. From the third piece He made the sun and the moon. From the fourth piece He made the air and the water.

**12** And the world was still, for there was no motion. Then God commanded His angels: "I shall create Adam and Eve, and shall make them human beings."

**13** And from Adam's essence shall proceed Shehr ibn Jaber; and from Shehr ibn Jaber shall proceed a separate community on the earth, the people of Melek Taus, the Yezidi people. These are the chosen ones.

**14** Then God sent Melek Taus to the earth, to set it in order and to establish government therein.
