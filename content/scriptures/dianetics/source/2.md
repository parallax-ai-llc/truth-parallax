**1** The Clear is the goal of Dianetic therapy. A Clear is an individual who has been freed of all engrams and their effects.

**2** The Clear has no psychoses, neuroses, compulsions, repressions, or aberrations. His intelligence is considerably higher than the current norm. His vigor, persistence, and tenacity of life are markedly improved.

**3** A Clear does not have any engrams which, when restimulated, can derange the correctness of computation by entering hidden and false data into it.

**4** The Clear has full recall of everything which has ever happened to him or anything he has ever studied. He can recall at will any perception stored in his memory.

**5** The Clear is not an "adjusted" person. He is not a person whose problems have been "suppressed" or whose tensions have been "relieved." He is a person who has overcome the reactive mind entirely.

**6** The abilities of the Clear are not extraordinary by any standard except that of present aberrated humanity. These are the abilities every human being should possess.

**7** The dianetic Clear is to current normal individuals as the current normal is to the severely insane.
