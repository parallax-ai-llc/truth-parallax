scripture: "dianetics"
chapter: 10
---

**1** The creation of Dianetics is a milestone for Man comparable to his discovery of fire and superior to his inventions of the wheel and the arch.

**2** Dianetics is the science of the mind. It contains a therapeutic technique with which can be treated all inorganic mental ills and all organic psychosomatic ills, with assurance of complete cure.

**3** The hidden source of all psychosomatic ills and human aberration has been discovered and skills have been developed for their invariable cure.

**4** Further, Dianetics, the science of the mind, opens wide new vistas of research and investigation into the nature of human thought.

**5** The optimum individual is called the Clear. One can test the state of Clear just as one can test a bridge for its degree of structural integrity.

**6** The Clear is the goal of Dianetic therapy, a goal which some patience and a little study and work can bring about.

**7** The reactive mind is the source of aberration. It thinks in identities. It is the single source of all neuroses, psychoses, compulsions, and repressions.

**8** The analytical mind, which is the conscious, aware mind that thinks and observes and remembers, is essentially perfect in its operation when freed from the reactive mind's content.
