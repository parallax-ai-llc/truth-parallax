**1** The time track is a complete record of all perceptions of the individual from the earliest moment of existence to the present. Every perception is recorded on this track.

**2** The analytical mind uses the standard memory banks, which contain all consciously recorded perceptions. These are accurate and useful.

**3** The reactive mind maintains its own bank: the engram bank. This bank contains only moments of pain and unconsciousness. Its contents are not available to the analytical mind for review.

**4** The prenatal area contains engrams recorded before birth. The child in the womb has a functioning reactive mind and records engrams from a very early period.

**5** Birth itself is an engram of great magnitude. The birth engram contains all the perceptions of the birth experience: the pressure, the pain, the sounds, the first breath.

**6** The earlier the engram on the time track, the more aberrative its effect, for earlier engrams serve as foundations upon which later engrams can key in.

**7** Dianetic therapy consists of locating engrams on the time track, returning the individual to them, and reducing their force by causing the analytical mind to examine them.
