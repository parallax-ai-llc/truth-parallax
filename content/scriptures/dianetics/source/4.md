**1** The basic personality of the individual is strong, hardy, and constructively good. The basic personality is the individual himself. His aberrations are imposed from without.

**2** No human being possesses an "evil" basic personality. When stripped of his engrams, the individual demonstrates only constructive purposes and high ethical standards.

**3** The dynamic principle of existence is: SURVIVE! This is the common denominator of all life. All behavior can be understood in terms of survival.

**4** The urge to survive can be expressed through four dynamics. The First Dynamic is the urge toward survival of the individual. The Second Dynamic is the urge toward survival through procreation and the family.

**5** The Third Dynamic is the urge toward survival of the group. The Fourth Dynamic is the urge toward survival of mankind as a species.

**6** The optimum solution to any problem is that which brings the greatest good to the greatest number of dynamics.

**7** Aberration occurs when engrams force the individual to act against his own survival and the survival of others along the dynamics. The reactive mind works against the individual's true interests.
