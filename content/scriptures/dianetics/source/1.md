title: "Dianetics Section 1"
scripture: "dianetics"
chapter: 1
---

**1** The creation of Dianetics is a milestone for Man comparable to his discovery of fire and superior to his inventions of the wheel and arch.

**2** Dianetics is an organized science of thought built on definite axioms: statements of natural laws on the order of those of the physical sciences.

**3** The goal of thought is the raising of the individual and, through the individual, of the whole of mankind to a higher level of survival.

**4** The single source of all human aberration is the reactive mind, a hitherto unknown and unsuspected part of the mind which records and retains physical pain and painful emotion and seeks to direct the organism solely on a stimulus-response basis.

**5** The reactive mind thinks in identities. It computes: A equals A equals A. A bruised knee equals the chair which bruised it equals the color of the chair equals the sound in the room at the time.

**6** The analytical mind, on the other hand, is the conscious, rational mind which thinks in differences and similarities. It is incapable of error when supplied with correct data.

**7** Dianetics sets forth that the analytical mind is the fundamental mind, the mind the individual is intended to use, and that the reactive mind is an interloper.
