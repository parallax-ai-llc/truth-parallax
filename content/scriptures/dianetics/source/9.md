**1** The tone scale measures the emotional state of the individual, from death at the bottom to enthusiasm at the top.

**2** At 0.0 on the tone scale is death. At 0.1 is apathy. At 1.0 is fear. At 1.5 is anger. At 2.0 is antagonism. At 2.5 is boredom. At 3.0 is conservatism. At 4.0 is enthusiasm.

**3** The chronic tone of an individual is determined by his engram bank. The more engrams in restimulation, the lower his chronic tone.

**4** As engrams are reduced in therapy, the preclear moves up the tone scale. His emotional state improves, his ability to deal with his environment increases, and his survival potential rises.

**5** The tone scale also predicts behavior. An individual at 1.5 (anger) will be destructive. An individual at 2.0 (antagonism) will be contentious. An individual at 3.0 will be constructive.

**6** A society's survival potential is determined by the aggregate tone level of its members. A society of Clears would be a rational, constructive, and ethical society.

**7** Dianetics offers mankind the tools to raise the tone level of individuals and, through them, of the whole civilization. The reactive mind is the enemy of survival, and Dianetics is the means of its defeat.
