title: "Dianetics Section 7"
scripture: "dianetics"
chapter: 7
---

**1** The auditor is the practitioner of Dianetic therapy. The auditor guides the preclear through the process of locating and reducing engrams.

**2** The preclear is the individual undergoing Dianetic therapy. He is called a preclear because he has not yet attained the state of Clear.

**3** The primary tool of the auditor is the technique of returning. The preclear is asked to return along his time track to earlier incidents. He relives these incidents, perceiving them again in full.

**4** The auditor uses the repeater technique to locate engrams. When the preclear uses a certain phrase repeatedly, it often leads to an engram containing that phrase.

**5** When an engram is contacted, the preclear is asked to go through it from beginning to end, recounting all perceptions. Each pass through the engram reduces its force.

**6** An engram which has been fully reduced ceases to have any command power over the individual. Its content is refiled as a standard memory, available to the analytical mind.

**7** The auditor must follow the Auditor's Code: never evaluate for the preclear, never invalidate his data, never use Dianetics on the preclear for personal gain.
