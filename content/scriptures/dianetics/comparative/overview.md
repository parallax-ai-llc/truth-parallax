## Overview

The Comparative reading of Dianetics engages with L. Ron Hubbard's foundational text from the distinctive vantage point of the Comparative tradition. This perspective brings particular attention to the text's model of the human mind, its theory of mental suffering, its therapeutic methodology, and its vision of human perfectibility. The Comparative interpretation illuminates both the internal logic of Hubbard's system and its points of contact and tension with broader intellectual and spiritual traditions.
