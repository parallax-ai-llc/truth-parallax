## Overview

The Academic study of 2 Enoch addresses the complex textual, historical, and literary questions surrounding this pseudepigraphon. Scholars investigate the relationship between the longer and shorter recensions, the question of a Greek or Hebrew/Aramaic Vorlage behind the Slavonic text, the date and provenance of the original composition, and the text's place within the broader Enochic tradition and Second Temple Jewish literature.
