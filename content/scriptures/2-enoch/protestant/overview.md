## Overview

The Protestant reading of 2 Enoch examines the text for the light it sheds on the conceptual world of the New Testament authors. The seven-heavens cosmology illuminates Paul's reference to being caught up to the 'third heaven' (2 Corinthians 12:2), while the Melchizedek appendix provides crucial background for the Christological use of Melchizedek in the Epistle to the Hebrews. Though non-canonical, 2 Enoch is valued as a window into the theological imagination of Second Temple Judaism.
