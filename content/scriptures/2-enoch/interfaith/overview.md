## Overview

The Interfaith reading of 2 Enoch explores the text as a meeting point for Jewish, Christian, and broader mystical traditions. The heavenly ascent motif is shared across Jewish Merkabah mysticism, Islamic mi'raj literature, and Christian visionary traditions. The Melchizedek figure bridges Jewish, Christian, and Gnostic traditions. This reading examines how 2 Enoch's themes of cosmic order, ethical instruction, and transformative encounter with the divine resonate across religious boundaries.
