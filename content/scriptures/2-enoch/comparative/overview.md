## Overview

The Comparative reading of 2 Enoch examines the text's heavenly ascent narrative and seven-heavens cosmology alongside parallel traditions in Jewish Merkabah mysticism, the Islamic mi'raj (Muhammad's Night Journey), Zoroastrian cosmological texts, and Gnostic ascent narratives. The Melchizedek appendix invites comparison with Qumran Melchizedek traditions and the Epistle to the Hebrews.
