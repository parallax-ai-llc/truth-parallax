---
title: "2 Enoch Chapter 9"
scripture: "2-enoch"
chapter: 9
---

**1** And those men took me thence and led me to the east to the gates of heaven, and I saw the treasury of the snow and the treasury of the hail, and the gloomy angels who carry out their authority, holding great clouds and the rock of anointing.

**2** And I looked over the whole earth, and I saw the two great rivers, and they came to the ocean, that flow to the east; and I saw the great sea itself, and the great darkness, and the great night, and the evening and the morning.
