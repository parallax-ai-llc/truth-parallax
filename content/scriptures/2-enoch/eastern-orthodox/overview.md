## Overview

The Eastern Orthodox reading of 2 Enoch is shaped by the text's unique preservation within the Slavonic manuscript tradition and its resonance with Orthodox theological themes. The text's vision of Enoch's transformation before the divine face resonates with the Orthodox doctrine of theosis (divinization), while its detailed heavenly cosmology reflects the Orthodox sense of the cosmos as liturgically ordered and suffused with angelic presence.
