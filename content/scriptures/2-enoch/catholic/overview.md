## Overview

The Catholic reading of 2 Enoch engages with the text's rich angelological and cosmological traditions as part of the broader witness of Second Temple Jewish literature. The text's detailed hierarchies of angels and its seven-heavens cosmology contribute to the Catholic understanding of the development of angelological doctrine, while the Melchizedek appendix enriches reflection on the typological significance of the Melchizedek priesthood in relation to the priesthood of Christ.
