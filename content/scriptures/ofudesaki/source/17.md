---
title: "Ofudesaki Section 17: The Final Urging"
scripture: "ofudesaki"
chapter: 17
---

I have written all that I wish to say through this writing brush, yet I know that words alone cannot convey the full depth of God the Parent's heart. The truth I have shared is but the surface of an ocean of divine love and wisdom that extends beyond what human language can express.

From the beginning of this writing to its end, there is one message that underlies everything: you are loved. You are loved by the God who created you, who sustains you in every moment, who guides you through illness and difficulty, and who waits with infinite patience for you to awaken to the truth. This love is not conditional upon your behavior, your belief, or your worthiness. It is the love of a Parent for a child, unchanging and unconditional.

I have spoken of dust and sweeping, of illness and healing, of the Service and the Kanrodai, of the Joyous Life and the world as one family. All of these teachings point to a single truth: that the purpose of human existence is joy, and that this joy is achieved through the alignment of the human mind with the divine intention.

The writing brush now rests, but the teaching does not end. God the Parent continues to speak through the events of your lives, through the guidance of illness and blessing, through the circumstances that shape your days. Learn to read these messages from God as you have learned to read the words of this text. Every moment is an opportunity to hear the divine voice and to respond with sincerity.

Go forth and build the Joyous Life. Begin with yourself, extend to your family, reach out to your community, and embrace the world. The path has been opened. The truth has been revealed. The rest is up to you, in partnership with God the Parent who never ceases to work for the salvation of all the children of this world. Sah, sah, the time has come. The time has truly come.
