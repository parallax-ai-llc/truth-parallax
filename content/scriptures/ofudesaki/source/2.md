---
title: "Ofudesaki Section 2: God the Parent's Desire"
scripture: "ofudesaki"
chapter: 2
---

All of you throughout the world are brothers and sisters. There is no one who is a stranger. Yet why do you quarrel and fight among yourselves? The heart of God the Parent aches to see the children who should live in harmony treating each other with hostility and contempt.

You build fences between nations, between villages, between families, and even within your own hearts. These separations have no foundation in truth, for God created all human beings from the same origin, with the same care and the same intention. The differences you perceive are merely dust upon the mind, obscuring the fundamental unity of all people.

Day after day, I desire only to save you, yet you do not understand My heart. I work through the body, showing you through illness and affliction that your minds have wandered from the path of truth. These bodily disorders are not punishment but guidance, signs pointing you toward the dust that must be swept away.

When you feel pain in the body, do not merely seek to remove the symptom. Look within your mind and ask what dust has accumulated there. The body is a thing lent by God, and the mind alone is your own. If you use the mind in accord with God's intention, the body shall respond with health and vitality. But if the mind fills with dust, the body shall surely reflect this disorder.

I do not force you. I only teach and guide. The path to the Joyous Life must be walked willingly, with a mind swept clean of dust and a heart open to the truth of God's parental love. Step by step, little by little, the world shall be rebuilt.
