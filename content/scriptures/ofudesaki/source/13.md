---
title: "Ofudesaki Section 13: The Urgency of the Times"
scripture: "ofudesaki"
chapter: 13
---

The time is pressing. I have been patient through many ages, watching and waiting as humanity slowly matured, but now the time has come for decisive action. The dust has accumulated to a dangerous degree, and if it is not swept away, the consequences for the world shall be severe.

Do not misunderstand. I do not threaten punishment. But the accumulated dust of the human mind creates its own consequences, just as a neglected field produces weeds rather than crops. The suffering of the world is the natural result of minds filled with miserliness, anger, arrogance, and greed. If these dusts continue to accumulate unchecked, the suffering shall deepen until it becomes unbearable.

This is why I have descended into the world at this time. This is why I have opened the path and revealed the truth of creation. The moment has arrived when humanity must make a choice: continue on the path of dust and suffering, or turn toward the Joyous Life that was always intended.

I am working in ways that are not visible to human eyes. Behind the events of the world, behind the apparent chaos and confusion, the hand of God the Parent is guiding, adjusting, preparing the way. What seems like misfortune may be the clearing of ground for new growth. What seems like loss may be the removal of obstacles to progress.

Trust in the providence of God even when circumstances seem dark. The night is darkest just before the dawn, and the dawn I am bringing shall illuminate the entire world. But you must do your part. Sweep the dust from your minds, perform the Service with sincerity, and spread the truth to all who will hear. The salvation of the world is a cooperative effort between God and humanity, and neither can accomplish it alone.
