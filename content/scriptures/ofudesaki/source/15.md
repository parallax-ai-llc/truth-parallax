---
title: "Ofudesaki Section 15: The Future of the Path"
scripture: "ofudesaki"
chapter: 15
---

I shall tell you what is to come. The path that I have opened shall grow until it covers the entire world. This is not a prediction but a declaration of divine intention. The Joyous Life shall be realized, and all humanity shall live as one family, children of a single Parent, sharing in the abundance that God has provided.

But the road to that future is not straight or smooth. There will be periods of rapid growth and periods of stagnation. There will be those who distort the teaching for their own purposes and those who preserve it faithfully through times of difficulty. The path itself shall face opposition from those who profit from the world's dust and who fear the clarity that these teachings bring.

Do not be discouraged by these obstacles. They are part of the process, the resistance of accumulated dust to being swept away. Every great transformation meets resistance, and the transformation I am bringing is the greatest in the history of the world. Expect opposition, prepare for difficulty, and persevere with the confidence that God the Parent is guiding the process from beginning to end.

The growth of the path shall come not through force or persuasion but through the lived example of those who walk it sincerely. When people see the Joyous Life manifesting in the lives of the followers, they shall be drawn to inquire about its source. Your daily life is the most powerful sermon, and your sincerity is the most convincing argument.

I shall continue to work through the world in ways both visible and invisible. New instruments shall be prepared, new teachings shall be given as humanity matures, and new challenges shall be met with new manifestations of divine providence. The story that began at Jiba shall extend to the furthest corners of the earth, and the sweet heavenly dew shall fall for all humanity.
