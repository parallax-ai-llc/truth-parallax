---
title: "Ofudesaki Section 7: The Parent's Concern"
scripture: "ofudesaki"
chapter: 7
---

Day after day, I watch the world and see My children suffering from ignorance of the truth. My heart aches with a pain that no human parent can fully comprehend, for I see not just the present suffering but all the suffering that has accumulated through countless generations of life.

Why have I waited so long to speak? Because the time was not yet ripe. The maturity of the human mind follows its own schedule, and until humanity reached a certain stage of development, these truths could not be received. But now the time has come, and I shall not be silent any longer.

I descended into this world and took up residence in the body of Miki Nakayama not to glorify one person but to speak directly to all humanity. Miki is the Shrine of God, the vessel through which the divine voice reaches human ears. Do not worship the vessel but hear the voice that speaks through it.

The path I am opening is not for one nation, one people, or one time. It is the universal path for all humanity, in all places, for all ages to come. The truths I teach are not new in the sense of being recently invented. They are original in the deepest sense, for they come from the very origin of human existence.

Some will doubt, some will resist, and some will mock what is written here. This is to be expected, for the accumulated dust of ages cannot be swept away in a single day. But I am patient, as a parent must be, and I shall continue to work through the path I have opened until all the world comes to understand.

Do not be discouraged by the slowness of the world's understanding. Even a single mind swept clean of dust becomes a beacon that lights the way for others. Your sincerity shall not be wasted.
