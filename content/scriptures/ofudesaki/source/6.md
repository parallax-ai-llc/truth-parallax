---
title: "Ofudesaki Section 6: Illness and the Mind"
scripture: "ofudesaki"
chapter: 6
---

You wonder why illness comes even to those who seem to live good lives. Listen carefully, for the truth of illness has never been properly understood. All illness comes from the mind. The body is borrowed from God, and if the mind is used in accord with God's intention, the body shall be free from disorder.

This does not mean that the sick are being punished. God uses illness as a guide, a loving signal that something in the mind needs attention. When a child stumbles, a parent does not blame the child but helps them find their footing. So it is with God and human illness.

The locations and types of illness correspond to specific dusts of the mind. Disorders of the eyes relate to how one sees others and the world. Disorders of the ears relate to how one listens and what one refuses to hear. Pain in the legs and feet relates to the path one is walking. Each bodily affliction is a message from God the Parent, pointing with precision to the mental dust that must be swept away.

When you understand this truth, you need not fear illness. Instead, receive it as guidance and look within your mind for the dust to which it points. Sweep that dust away with sincere reflection and gratitude for God's parental guidance, and the body shall respond to the cleared mind.

This is not to say that medicine and doctors are useless. God works through all things, including the knowledge and skill of healers. But the fundamental healing comes from clearing the mind, and without this inner transformation, external remedies can only address symptoms while the root cause remains.

I write these truths not from human understanding but from the knowledge of the Creator who designed both body and mind and knows their relationship completely.
