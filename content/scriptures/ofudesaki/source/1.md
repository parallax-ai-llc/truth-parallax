---
title: "Ofudesaki Section 1: The Beginning of Divine Instruction"
scripture: "ofudesaki"
chapter: 1
---

Looking all over the world and through all ages, I find no one who has understood My heart. So should it be, for I have never taught it to you. It is natural that you know nothing. At this time I, God, reveal Myself and teach the truth of all things in detail.

This world is the body of God. Ponder this in all matters. The whole universe is the body of the one true God, and all human beings are children of God. Yet no one is aware of this truth. What is it that God desires? Nothing but the Joyous Life for all humanity. If only the minds of all the world settle, I shall provide blessings beyond your imagination.

Listen! I do not say what is unreasonable. I ask only that you sweep away the dust from your minds. The dusts of the mind are miserliness, covetousness, self-love, greed, arrogance, grudge-bearing, anger, and desire for more than is needed. These dusts accumulate daily and cloud the original pure mind that God bestowed upon each person at creation.

This writing brush moves not by human will but by the will of God the Parent. What I write may seem incomprehensible at first, but as you ponder deeply, the meaning will gradually become clear. Do not think lightly of what is written here, for every word is the direct teaching of the Creator of this world.

The place where I first began human creation is Jiba, in Yamato. This is the Home of the Parent, and to this place all children of God shall return. I shall build the Kanrodai at this place, and when it is established, the sweet heavenly dew shall fall, and with it shall come the salvation of all the world.
