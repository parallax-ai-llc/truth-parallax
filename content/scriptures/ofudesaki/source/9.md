---
title: "Ofudesaki Section 9: The Hinagata and the Model Path"
scripture: "ofudesaki"
chapter: 9
---

Miki has walked the path of hardship so that others may follow after her. The fifty years of divine instruction that have passed through her body and mind have not been without suffering. She has endured poverty, ridicule, persecution, and the incomprehension of those closest to her. Yet she has persevered, and her perseverance has carved the path that others may now walk.

This is the model path, the hinagata. It is not that all must suffer as Miki has suffered, but that the spirit of her endurance, her unwavering trust in God the Parent despite all hardship, provides the pattern for how the path should be walked.

When obstacles arise on your path, do not interpret them as signs that you have chosen wrongly. The path of truth passes through difficulty because the world is thick with dust, and clearing that dust requires effort and courage. Look to the model of Miki and take heart. If she could endure what she endured, you can endure what is asked of you.

The followers who have gathered around this teaching bear a special responsibility. You are the first to hear these truths, and upon your shoulders rests the task of spreading them to all the world. Do not take this responsibility lightly, but do not let it burden you with anxiety. God the Parent shall provide the strength and the means. Your task is only to maintain a sincere mind and to share what you have received.

The instruments that God has prepared to carry this teaching forward are like the instruments of creation itself. Each has a role, and each is essential. Do not compete with one another for prominence, but work together as the instruments of creation work together, each contributing its unique function to the whole. In this cooperative spirit, the Joyous Life shall gradually manifest.
