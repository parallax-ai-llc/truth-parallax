## Tenrikyo Reading

Tenrikyo followers receive Part I of the Ofudesaki as the foundational declaration of God the Parent's identity and purpose. In these opening verses, Oyagami announces the divine intent to reveal the truth of creation and the path to the Joyous Life. The Tenrikyo community understands these verses as God's self-introduction to humanity — a Parent who has watched over human beings since creation and now, through the hand of Nakayama Miki, speaks directly to reveal the divine will.

The verses of Part I establish the urgency of the divine message: God has waited through the ages for the right moment to communicate the truth, and that moment has arrived. Tenrikyo practitioners study Part I as the key to understanding all subsequent parts, finding in its verses the theological foundation — the existence of a single creator God who loves humanity as a parent loves children — upon which the entire Ofudesaki rests.
