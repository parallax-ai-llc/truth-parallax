## Tenrikyo Reading

Part XI intensifies the sense of divine urgency found throughout the Ofudesaki. God the Parent expresses growing concern about humanity's failure to understand and respond to the divine message, warning that continued resistance to the truth will bring consequences not as punishment but as the natural result of deviation from the path of joy.

The Tenrikyo community reads Part XI with particular seriousness, understanding the divine warnings as expressions of parental love — a parent who warns a child of danger not from anger but from desperate concern. The verses call for immediate response, deeper sincerity, and unwavering trust in the divine Parent's guidance.
