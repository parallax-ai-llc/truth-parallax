## Tenrikyo Reading

Part VII introduces the teaching of *innen* — the principle of causality that connects past actions to present circumstances. God the Parent explains that human suffering is not arbitrary but arises from the accumulation of causes and conditions across generations. Understanding *innen* allows human beings to accept their circumstances with gratitude and to create positive causes for future joy.

The Tenrikyo reading of Part VII emphasizes that *innen* is not fatalism but empowerment: by understanding the causal structure of reality, human beings can consciously choose to plant seeds of kindness and sincerity that will bear fruit in the Joyous Life. The divine Parent reveals *innen* not to condemn but to illuminate the path of return to the original state of joy.
