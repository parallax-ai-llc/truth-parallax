## Tenrikyo Reading

Part XVI reveals the doctrine of God's free and unlimited workings (*jiyu jizai*) — the divine power that operates without constraint to accomplish salvation. God the Parent promises that those who align their minds with the divine will shall experience miraculous workings — healing, protection, and transformation — that transcend ordinary causality.

Tenrikyo practitioners understand Part XVI as the assurance that divine power is not limited by human understanding or natural law. The free and unlimited workings are not arbitrary miracles but the natural consequence of living in harmony with God's will. When the mind is purified and the heart is sincere, the divine Parent's power flows freely through human life.
