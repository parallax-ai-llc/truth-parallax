## Tenrikyo Reading

Part XVII, the final section of the Ofudesaki, presents God the Parent's ultimate will for humanity — the complete realization of the Joyous Life for all people. The verses bring the entire work to its consummation, reaffirming the divine intent declared in Part I and calling upon humanity to respond with faith, sincerity, and action.

The Tenrikyo community reads Part XVII as the culmination of the divine message — the final statement of God's love and God's expectation. The Joyous Life is presented not as a distant utopia but as an achievable reality, waiting only for humanity to understand and accept the truth of origin, sweep away the dusts of the mind, and perform the Service with single-hearted devotion.
