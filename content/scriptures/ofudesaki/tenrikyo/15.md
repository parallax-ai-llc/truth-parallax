## Tenrikyo Reading

Part XV presents the final phase of the Ofudesaki's teaching, addressing themes of legacy, transmission, and the continuation of the divine work beyond the foundress's lifetime. God the Parent speaks of the path extending into the future, emphasizing that the truth revealed through Nakayama Miki is not limited to her generation but is intended for all humanity across all time.

The Tenrikyo community receives Part XV as both farewell and commission — the divine Parent entrusting the community with the responsibility of carrying the teaching forward. The verses encourage faithfulness, unity, and trust in God's continuing guidance even after the foundress is no longer physically present.
