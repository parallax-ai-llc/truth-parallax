## Tenrikyo Reading

Part XIII signals the completion and maturation of the Ofudesaki's teaching. God the Parent announces that the essential truths have been revealed and that what remains is for humanity to understand, accept, and practice them. The verses take on a tone of summary and consolidation, drawing together the themes of earlier parts into a unified vision.

The Tenrikyo community reads Part XIII as marking a transition from revelation to implementation — the divine teaching is substantially complete, and the responsibility shifts to the community of followers to embody and transmit the truth. The verses encourage confidence in the sufficiency of the revealed teaching and diligence in its application.
