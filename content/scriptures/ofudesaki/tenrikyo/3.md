## Tenrikyo Reading

Part III addresses the quality of sincerity (*makoto*) that God the Parent desires from human beings. The verses describe the true heart — a mind purified of the 'dusts' of greed, selfishness, and anger — as the essential condition for receiving divine blessings and contributing to the realization of the Joyous Life.

Tenrikyo practitioners study Part III as a guide to spiritual self-examination. The text's emphasis on inner sincerity over outward observance resonates with the Tenrikyo understanding that God sees the heart directly and that no amount of ritual performance can substitute for genuine spiritual transformation. The path of sincerity described here is understood as the daily practice of aligning one's mind with the divine will.
