## Interfaith Reading

The interfaith reading of Part 13 highlights the Ofudesaki's teaching on the completion of the teaching as a resource for interreligious dialogue. The text's vision of divine parenthood and universal human kinship provides common ground with traditions worldwide that affirm the dignity, equality, and shared destiny of all human beings.

Interfaith practitioners find in Part 13 an invitation to consider how different religious traditions understand the divine-human relationship and the purpose of human life. The Ofudesaki's particular contribution — its combination of intimate parental theology, ethical earnestness, and prophetic urgency — adds a distinctive voice to the global conversation about ultimate meaning, social justice, and the possibility of a world transformed by spiritual awakening.
