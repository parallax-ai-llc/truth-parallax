title: "Spring and Autumn Annals - Chapter 1"
scripture: "spring-autumn-annals"
chapter: 1
---

**1** In the first year of Duke Yin, in the spring, in the king's first month, the Duke came to the throne.

**2** In the third month, the Duke and Yifu of Zhu made a covenant at Mie.

**3** In the summer, in the fifth month, the Earl of Zheng overcame Duan at Yan.

**4** In the autumn, in the seventh month, the Heavenly King sent his steward Zai Xuan to present funeral gifts for Duke Hui.

**5** In the ninth month, the people of Song took hold of the Duke of Zhu.

**6** In the winter, in the twelfth month, the Duke of Zhu returned to his own state, having been released.

**7** The gentleman says: The record of these events shows that order and disorder in the states arise from the conduct of their rulers.

**8** When the ruler is upright, the state is well governed. When the ruler is not upright, disorder prevails.

**9** The Spring and Autumn Annals records praise and blame, distinguishing right from wrong, so that future generations may learn from the past.

**10** The Way of Heaven rewards the virtuous and punishes the wicked. This principle is evident in all the events recorded herein.

**11** He who reads these annals with a discerning mind will understand the principles that govern the rise and fall of states.

**12** This is the chronicle of the State of Lu, beginning with Duke Yin, as transmitted by Confucius.
