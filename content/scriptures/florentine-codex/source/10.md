---
title: "Book X - The People"
scripture: "florentine-codex"
chapter: 10
---

**1** These are the descriptions of the different kinds of people: the good and the bad, the virtuous and the vicious, as the elders described them.

**2** The good father is careful of his household, a teacher and an advisor. He provides for his family and disciplines his children with love. He is the heart of the home.

**3** The bad father is negligent, wasteful, and irresponsible. He abandons his children and squanders his goods. He brings shame upon his family.

**4** The good physician knows the properties of herbs. She grinds them, boils them, applies them with skill. She sets broken bones and bleeds those who need bleeding. She cures with knowledge passed down from the ancients.

**5** The bad physician is a deceiver. She pretends to know what she does not know. She gives false remedies and takes payment for useless treatments. She brings death instead of healing.

**6** The good ruler is a shelter and a shade for his people. He speaks with wisdom and judges with fairness. He puts the welfare of his people above his own. The bad ruler is a wild beast who devours his own people.
