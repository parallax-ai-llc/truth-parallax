**2.** And Peter spoke to the multitude and said, "Brothers and sisters, hear me. The Savior said to us before he ascended: 'I have given you authority over all the powers of the Enemy, and over every evil thing. Go forth and preach the word of truth, and do not add any other rule or law beyond what I have appointed for you.'

"And he said to us, 'Behold, I send you as lambs among wolves. Be therefore wise as serpents and innocent as doves. For they will deliver you up to the councils, and before governors you will stand for my sake, as a testimony to them and to the nations.'

"But I say to you: do not fear those who kill the body. Fear rather the ignorance that kills the soul. For the body is a garment that you will one day put off—but the soul is the dwelling place of truth, and it shall endure.

"Let each of you therefore guard the light that has been placed within you. Do not let the darkness of the world extinguish it. Carry the word of the Savior into every place, and let the truth speak through your deeds as much as through your words."

And the people rejoiced at the instruction of Peter.
