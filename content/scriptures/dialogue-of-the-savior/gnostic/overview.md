## Overview

For Gnostic readers, the Dialogue of the Savior is a record of esoteric post-resurrection teaching in which the risen Jesus reveals to his closest disciples — Matthew, Judas Thomas, and Mary — the hidden knowledge necessary for salvation. Mary's prominence as one who 'understood completely' affirms the Gnostic principle that gnosis is available to all spiritual persons regardless of gender. The dialogue format mirrors the Gnostic pedagogical ideal: truth is not proclaimed but discovered through questioning, and the Savior's answers lead the disciples progressively deeper into the mystery of their own divine origin.
