## Overview

For Christian readers, the Dialogue of the Savior raises important questions about the diversity of early Christian views on salvation, women's roles, and the nature of Jesus's post-resurrection teaching. Mary's recognition as one who 'understood completely' contrasts with traditions that marginalize her, and the text's emphasis on knowledge over faith challenges the dominant soteriological framework. The dialogue format itself — Jesus answering questions about cosmic mysteries — presents a very different image of the risen Christ from the canonical resurrection appearances.
