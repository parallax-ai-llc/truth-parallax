## Overview

The Dialogue of the Savior participates in the universal genre of instruction dialogue — a teacher transmitting wisdom to prepared students through question and answer. This format parallels the Upanishadic dialogues between guru and student, the Buddhist suttas recording the Buddha's conversations with monks and laypersons, the Platonic dialogues, and the Hermetic Corpus. The content — the nature of the cosmos, the path to liberation, the dissolution of the material body — addresses concerns shared across contemplative traditions worldwide.
