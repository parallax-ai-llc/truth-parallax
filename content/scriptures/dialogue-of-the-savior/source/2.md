---
title: "Dialogue of the Savior — Section 2: The Nature of the Cosmos"
scripture: "dialogue-of-the-savior"
chapter: 2
---

**1** Judas said, "Lord, when I see the works that are fashioned and the places that are built in the cosmos — are they good or are they evil?"

**2** The Lord said, "Judas, look at the things of this world, and see how these temporary works will be destroyed. The works that endure are the works of the spirit."

**3** Mary asked, "Lord, teach us about the fullness and the deficiency."

**4** The Lord said, "What is born of the truth does not die. What is born of the female dies."

**5** Matthew said, "Lord, tell us how the dead die and how the living live."

**6** The Lord said, "You have asked me about a saying that eye has not seen, nor have I heard it except from you. But I say to you that when what invigorates a man is removed, he will be called dead. And when what is alive leaves what is dead, what is alive will be called upon."

**7** Judas asked, "Why, for the sake of truth, do they die and live?"

**8** The Lord said, "Whatever is born of truth does not die; whatever is born of the female dies."
