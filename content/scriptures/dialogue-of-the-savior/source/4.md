---
title: "Dialogue of the Savior — Section 4: The Way of Ascent"
scripture: "dialogue-of-the-savior"
chapter: 4
---

**1** Matthew said, "Lord, how shall we journey to the rest?"

**2** The Lord said, "If you have the mind to go, and the spirit to journey, and understanding, then you have everything you need for the way."

**3** Judas said, "Lord, I believe all these things that you have told us. But one thing I ask of you: Tell me how the elect will know the truth."

**4** The Lord said, "The lamp of the body is the mind. As long as the things inside you are set in order, that is, your bodies are light, so long as you are not dark, you will know the truth."

**5** Mary said, "Lord, is there then a place which is empty of truth?"

**6** The Lord said, "The place where I am not."

**7** Judas said, "Lord, you are everything to those who know you. Therefore have mercy upon us, and save us."

**8** The Lord said, "Come to me, for I am gentle and humble in heart, and you will find rest for your souls."

**9** "For my yoke is easy and my lordship is mild. And you will find rest for yourselves."

**10** The Dialogue of the Savior.
