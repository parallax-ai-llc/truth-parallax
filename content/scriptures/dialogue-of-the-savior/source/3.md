---
title: "Dialogue of the Savior — Section 3: The Garment of Light"
scripture: "dialogue-of-the-savior"
chapter: 3
---

**1** The Lord said, "Pray in the place where there is no woman."

**2** Matthew said, "He tells us, pray in the place where there is no woman, meaning destroy the works of femaleness, not because there is any other manner of birth, but because the works of femaleness will cease."

**3** Mary said, "They will never be obliterated."

**4** The Lord said, "Who knows that they will not dissolve and will not perish?"

**5** Judas said to Matthew, "The works of femaleness will dissolve in the presence of the heights."

**6** The Lord said, "When you cast off your garment of shame and take up the garment of light, and when the two become one, and the male with the female is neither male nor female — then you will enter the kingdom."

**7** Mary said, "Lord, when I see you I shall enter the kingdom."

**8** The Lord said, "When you make the two one, and when you make the inside like the outside, then you will enter the kingdom."
