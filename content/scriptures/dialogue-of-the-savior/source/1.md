---
title: "Dialogue of the Savior — Section 1: The Savior's Teaching on Dissolution"
scripture: "dialogue-of-the-savior"
chapter: 1
---

**1** The Savior said to his disciples, "Already the time has come, brothers, for us to abandon our labor and stand at rest. For whoever stands at rest will rest forever."

**2** "I say to you, always strive to free yourselves from bondage, and you will find it so."

**3** Judas said, "Tell us, Lord, what existed before heaven and earth came into being."

**4** The Lord said, "There was darkness and water, and spirit upon water. And I say to you, what you seek and inquire after, behold, it is within you."

**5** Matthew said, "Lord, no one can find the truth except through you. Therefore teach us the truth."

**6** The Lord said, "He who seeks will find, and he who knocks, to him it will be opened."

**7** Mary said, "Lord, all the things that I ask you, I ask for the sake of the truth."

**8** The Lord said to her, "Whoever seeks the truth from true wisdom will make wings for himself in order to fly, fleeing the lust that is in those who pull and drag them."

**9** Matthew said, "Lord, I want to see that place of life, the place where there is no wickedness, but rather, there is pure light!"

**10** The Lord said, "Brother Matthew, you will not be able to see it as long as you are carrying flesh around."
