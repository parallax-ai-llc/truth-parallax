Danel's childlessness and divine granting of a son parallels numerous traditions:
- **Abraham and Sarah** (Genesis 18): Childless parents receive divine promise of a son
- **Hannah** (1 Samuel 1): A barren woman's prayer answered with a son dedicated to God
- **Zachariah and Elizabeth** (Luke 1): An elderly couple granted a son through divine intervention
- **Dasharatha** (Ramayana): A king performs a fire sacrifice to obtain sons

The connection between Danel and the biblical Daniel (Ezekiel 14:14, 20) is widely accepted: Ezekiel names Danel alongside Noah and Job as paragons of righteousness, drawing on the Ugaritic tradition of Danel as the archetypal righteous judge.
