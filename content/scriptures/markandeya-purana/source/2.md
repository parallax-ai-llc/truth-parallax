---
title: "Markandeya Purana - Chapter 2"
scripture: "markandeya-purana"
chapter: 2
---

**1** Markandeya spoke: In the age called Svayambhuva, the first Manu, Svayambhu, ruled the earth. He was the mind-born son of Brahma and the first lawgiver.

**2** From Svayambhu Manu were descended the great royal dynasties of the Solar and Lunar races, which ruled the earth through the successive ages.

**3** The Solar dynasty began with Ikshvaku, son of Manu, and from him descended Raghu, Dasharatha, and Rama, the incarnation of Vishnu.

**4** The Lunar dynasty began with Pururavas, the son of Budha, and from him descended the Pandavas and the Kauravas, whose great war is told in the Mahabharata.

**5** In each age, the Supreme Being manifests himself for the protection of the righteous and the destruction of the wicked.

**6** Markandeya said: The duties of kings are to protect their subjects, to uphold dharma, to punish the wicked, and to support the Brahmanas and the holy sages.

**7** A king who rules with justice and compassion is like a father to his people. A king who rules with cruelty and greed brings ruin upon himself and his kingdom.

**8** The earth supports those who walk upon her with righteousness, but she trembles beneath the weight of the unrighteous.

**9** Therefore let every ruler and every person follow the path of dharma, for dharma alone is the foundation of prosperity, happiness, and liberation.

**10** This is the teaching of the ancient Rishis, preserved in the Puranas for the guidance of all beings in every age.
