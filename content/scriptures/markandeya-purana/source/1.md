---
title: "Markandeya Purana - Chapter 1"
scripture: "markandeya-purana"
chapter: 1
---

**1** Jaimini spoke: O thou most excellent of Munis, thou who art versed in all the Shastras, I desire to hear from thee the Purana which was formerly narrated by Markandeya.

**2** That great Muni Markandeya, who was endowed with long life, who had traversed the entire world, who knew the past, present, and future -- he it was who narrated this most ancient Purana.

**3** The birds spoke: Hear, O Muni, the Purana which was told of old by Markandeya. In it are contained the most excellent of all sacred narratives.

**4** In the beginning, before creation, there existed only the Supreme Brahman, without beginning, without end, without form, beyond the reach of thought and speech.

**5** From that Supreme Being arose Prakriti, the primordial nature, and from Prakriti arose Mahat, the great principle of intelligence.

**6** From Mahat arose Ahankara, the principle of individuation. And from Ahankara arose the five subtle elements, the five gross elements, the ten organs of sense and action, and the mind.

**7** Thus was the universe brought forth from the Supreme Being through the power of his own nature. He is both the material cause and the efficient cause of all that exists.

**8** The sage Markandeya, having obtained this knowledge through the grace of the Supreme Being, declared it for the benefit of all beings.

**9** This knowledge destroys all sins, confers merit, and leads to liberation. Hear it with faith and devotion.

**10** Markandeya spoke: I shall narrate to you the account of creation, the genealogies of the great kings, the duties of the four stages of life, and the glory of the Divine Mother.
