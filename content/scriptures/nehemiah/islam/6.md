## Tawakkul and Discernment

Islamic interpretation of Nehemiah 6 highlights *tawakkul* (trust in God) and *firasat al-mu'min* (the discernment of the believer). The Prophet Muhammad taught that the believer possesses intuitive discernment granted by God.

## Key Themes

Nehemiah's ability to see through deception models the spiritual discernment valued in Islamic tradition. His refusal to be distracted reflects the Islamic virtue of *istiqamah* (steadfastness on the straight path). The completion of the wall confirms divine support.

## Theological Significance

- **Tawakkul**: Trust in God enables perseverance
- **Discernment**: The believer recognizes deception through divine guidance
- **Istiqamah**: Steadfastness on the path of duty
- **Divine completion**: God brings His work to fulfillment
