## Building Together: Synergeia in Action

Orthodox interpretation of the builders list sees a model of *synergeia* (cooperation between divine grace and human effort). Each builder works on their section while participating in the larger divine project. The diversity of workers reflects the Orthodox understanding of the Church as a communion of persons with diverse gifts serving the one Body.

## Key Themes

The cooperation of priests, merchants, goldsmiths, and rulers illustrates the Orthodox conviction that every vocation participates in the transfiguration of the world. The refusal of the Tekoite nobles (3:5) serves as a warning against spiritual pride.

## Theological Significance

- **Synergeia**: Human effort cooperates with divine grace
- **Diverse gifts**: Every vocation serves the common work
- **Spiritual pride**: Refusing to serve reveals false nobility
- **Corporate building**: The Church is built by many hands
