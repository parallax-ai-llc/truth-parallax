## Sacred Memory and Continuity

Orthodox interpretation of the genealogical register emphasizes the importance of sacred memory (*anamnesis*) and the continuity of the worshipping community. The Orthodox Church values its liturgical and institutional continuity as a visible sign of the Spirit's ongoing presence.

## Key Themes

The register establishes legitimate community membership across generations. Orthodox Christianity similarly maintains careful records of episcopal consecration, parish membership, and sacramental participation, understanding these as the visible thread connecting the present Church to its apostolic origins.

## Theological Significance

- **Sacred memory**: The community's past shapes its present identity
- **Continuity**: Legitimate membership is maintained across generations
- **Administrative care**: Record-keeping serves the community's life
- **Apostolic connection**: The present is linked to the origins
