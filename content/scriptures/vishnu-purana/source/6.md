---
title: "Vishnu Purana - Chapter 6"
scripture: "vishnu-purana"
chapter: 6
---

**1** Parashara said: I shall now give the concluding teaching of the Vishnu Purana, the essence of all sacred knowledge.

**2** The Supreme Lord Vishnu is the one reality behind all appearances. He is the thread upon which the universe is strung, as pearls are strung upon a thread.

**3** He is the taste in water, the light in the sun and moon, the syllable Om in the Vedas, the sound in ether, the ability in man.

**4** He is the fragrance of the earth, the heat of fire, the life of all beings, and the austerity of the ascetics. He is the eternal seed of all creatures.

**5** All this universe is pervaded by Vishnu. He exists in all things and all things exist in Him. There is nothing that is not Vishnu.

**6** He is the beginning, the middle, and the end of all beings. He is the sacrifice and the prayer, the offering and the fire, the giver and the gift.

**7** He who sees Vishnu in all things and all things in Vishnu — he never turns away from the Lord, and the Lord never turns away from him.

**8** The wise, seeing the same Lord equally present everywhere, do not harm the Self by the self. They reach the supreme goal.

**9** This Vishnu Purana, when heard with devotion, destroys all sins and bestows supreme merit. He who recites it at the time of the shraddha satisfies his ancestors for eternity.

**10** May Lord Vishnu, the pervader of all, the sustainer of all, the liberator of all, grant His grace to all who seek Him. He who knows Vishnu knows all. He who has found Vishnu has found everything. Om Namo Narayanaya. Om, peace, peace, peace.
