The Second World, the Blue World or Ni'hodootl'izh, represents the next stage of emergence. After being expelled from the Black World due to conflict and moral failings, the beings entered a realm populated by blue-feathered beings including swallow people. This world introduced new forms of social interaction and conflict, as the newcomers had to negotiate their place among existing inhabitants.
