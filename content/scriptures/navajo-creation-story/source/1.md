---
title: "Navajo Creation Story - Chapter 1"
scripture: "navajo-creation-story"
chapter: 1
---

**1** In the beginning, there were four worlds below this one. The First World was called the Black World. It was small, like a floating island in a sea of mist. There was no sun, no moon, and no stars. Only the mist people and the insect beings lived there.

**2** In the Black World, there lived the Insect People: the beetles, the ants, the dragonflies, the locusts, and other beings. They were not people as we know them, but spirit beings in insect form.

**3** The Insect People quarreled with one another and did wicked things. They committed adultery and fought and made trouble. The chiefs of the four directions — White, Blue, Yellow, and Black — told them they must leave.

**4** "You must go," said the chiefs. "Your behavior is not good. You bring only trouble and unhappiness."

**5** So the Insect People flew upward through an opening in the sky of the Black World, and they entered the Second World.

**6** The Second World was called the Blue World. It was larger than the first, and in it lived the Blue Birds, the Blue Hawks, the Blue Jays, the Blue Herons, and all manner of blue feathered beings.

**7** The Swallow People, who were chiefs of the Blue World, said to the Insect People: "You may live among us, but you must not disturb the peace."

**8** For a time the Insect People lived peacefully in the Blue World. But again they quarreled and again they committed wrongdoings.

**9** The Swallow People said: "We welcomed you, but you have brought your bad ways with you. You must leave this world."

**10** And so the Insect People flew upward again, through an opening in the sky of the Blue World, and they entered the Third World.

**11** The Third World was called the Yellow World. It was larger still, and there were great rivers — two flowing north and south, and two flowing east and west.

**12** In the Yellow World, there were mountains and valleys, rivers and springs. And the people of the Yellow World — the Grasshopper People — received the Insect People and allowed them to dwell there.
