---
title: "Navajo Creation Story - Chapter 4"
scripture: "navajo-creation-story"
chapter: 4
---

**1** But from the lower worlds, the monsters that had been born during the separation of the men and women found their way into the Glittering World. And they preyed upon the people.

**2** The monsters were many and terrible: Ye'iitsoh, the Big Giant; the Horned Monsters; the Bird Monsters that nested upon Shiprock; the Tracking Bear; the Eyes That Kill; and many others.

**3** The people were being destroyed. They prayed for deliverance, and their prayers were heard.

**4** Changing Woman was born upon the summit of the sacred mountain. She was found as an infant on the mountain by First Man and First Woman, who raised her.

**5** Changing Woman grew rapidly. In four days she reached womanhood. She is the most beloved of the Holy People, and her ceremony — the Kinaalda — is still performed when a girl comes of age.

**6** Changing Woman went to the top of the mountain and lay upon a flat rock in the sun. The rays of the sun fell upon her, and she conceived. She also went to a waterfall, and the water fell upon her. From these two unions were born the Hero Twins.

**7** The first twin was called Monster Slayer — Naayee' Neizghání. The second was called Born for Water — Tóbájíshchíní.

**8** The twins grew rapidly, and when they were young men, they set out to find their father, the Sun. Their journey was long and perilous.

**9** They crossed the great desert and climbed the sky. They faced tests and trials — crushing rocks, cutting reeds, boiling sands, and killing winds.

**10** At last they reached the house of the Sun. The Sun tested them, for he did not believe they were his sons. He threw them against walls of obsidian. He locked them in rooms of ice and fire.

**11** But with the help of their protectors — Spider Woman, who gave them a sacred feather and a life charm — the twins survived every test.

**12** The Sun acknowledged them as his sons and gave them weapons of lightning — zigzag lightning, straight lightning, sunbeam arrows, and rainbow arrows — to slay the monsters.
