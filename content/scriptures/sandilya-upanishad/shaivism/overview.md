## Overview

Shaiva interpreters find in the Sandilya Upanishad teachings that resonate with the Shaiva understanding of consciousness as the ultimate reality. The text's themes of yoga, eight limbs, nadis are read through the lens of Shiva as the universal consciousness that pervades and constitutes all existence.

## Theological Emphases

The Sandilya Upanishad's teachings on yoga are understood within the Shaiva framework of recognition (*pratyabhijna*) — the progressive discovery that one's own awareness is identical with Shiva's unlimited consciousness. The text's practical instructions serve as means of facilitating this recognition.
