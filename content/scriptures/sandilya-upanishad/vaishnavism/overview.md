## Overview

Vaishnava commentators read the Sandilya Upanishad's teachings as pointing to Vishnu-Narayana as the supreme reality underlying all its philosophical content. The text's central themes — yoga, eight limbs, nadis — are understood within the framework of the soul's relationship to the personal God who is the source, sustainer, and goal of all existence.

## Theological Emphases

The Sandilya Upanishad's philosophical insights are valued by the Vaishnava tradition as means of deepening devotion and understanding of the Lord's nature. Its practical teachings serve as aids to the devotional life, preparing the mind and heart for the ultimate realization of Vishnu's presence in all things.
