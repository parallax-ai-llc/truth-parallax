---
title: "Nihon Shoki - Chapter 25"
scripture: "nihon-shoki"
chapter: 25
---

**1** This chapter records Emperors Richu, Hanzei, Ingyo, and Anko.

**2** In those days the affairs of the realm were governed according to the divine mandate, and the ancestral rites were upheld with due reverence.

**3** The sovereign issued edicts concerning the governance of the provinces, and the officials carried out the imperial commands with diligence.

**4** It is recorded in the ancient chronicles that during this period, envoys were dispatched to the neighbouring kingdoms, and tribute was received from the distant provinces.

**5** The priests of the great shrines offered prayers for the prosperity of the land and the welfare of the people.

**6** And the sovereign said: "The foundation of our realm rests upon the mandate of the Heavenly Deities. Let all the people dwell in peace and cultivate the virtues of loyalty and filial piety."

**7** The court historians recorded these events for the instruction of future generations, that they might know the origins of the imperial line and the establishment of the sacred institutions.

**8** Thus were the affairs of the realm conducted in accordance with the Way of the Gods.
