---
title: "Yajur Veda - Chapter 1"
scripture: "yajur-veda"
chapter: 1
---

**1** For food thee, for strength thee! Ye are winds, ye are approachers. Let the god Savitar impel you to the most righteous offering.

**2** O Agni, open wide the door of darkness. Guard the sacrifice, guard the lord of the sacrifice. Guard this performer of good deeds.

**3** O invoked one, carry this offering to the gods. O Agni, accept the fuel. Blaze and illumine the four quarters.

**4** Let the god Savitar prepare the sacrifice. Let Agni make the offering ready. Let the divine Soma be purified.

**5** Thee for the purpose of worship, thee for the furtherance of the sacrifice! O divine waters, come hither and purify us.

**6** By impulse of the god Savitar, with the arms of the Ashvins, with the hands of Pushan, I take thee. Thou art the spade; thou art the woman. I am the man.

**7** Thou art the altar. I prepare thee for the holding of the fire. I prepare thee for the bearing of the Agni. I prepare thee for the fulfilment of the great sacrifice.

**8** Fill the atmosphere with thy smoke, O Agni. Fill the sky with thy radiance. The earth trembles at thy coming. The heavens rejoice.

**9** O Agni, the sacrificial fire, I lay thee down on the altar. I surround thee with the sacred waters. I feed thee with the fuel of truth.

**10** May Agni, the divine fire, accept our offerings. May the bright flames carry them to the gods. May the smoke of our sacrifice rise to heaven.

**11** The ladle is raised, the oblation is poured. With the sacred formula, with the holy chant, we make this offering to the gods.

**12** O ye gods, come hither. Accept our sacrifice. Sit upon the sacred grass. Drink of the Soma. Grant us your blessings.
