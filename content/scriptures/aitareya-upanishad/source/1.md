---
title: "Aitareya Upanishad - Chapter 1"
scripture: "aitareya-upanishad"
chapter: 1
---

**1** In the beginning this was Self alone, in the shape of a person. He looking round saw nothing but his Self. He first said, "This is I"; therefore he became I by name.

**2** He thought, "Shall I send forth worlds?" He sent forth these worlds, Ambhas, the highest world, above heaven; Marîkas, the sky; Mara, the earth; and Apa, the world below the earth.

**3** He thought, "There are these worlds; shall I send forth guardians of the worlds?" He then formed the Purusha, taking him forth from the water.

**4** He brooded over him. When that person had thus been brooded over, a mouth burst forth like an egg. From the mouth proceeded speech, from speech Agni (fire).

**5** Nostrils burst forth. From the nostrils proceeded scent (breath), from scent Vâyu (air).

**6** Eyes burst forth. From the eyes proceeded sight, from sight Âditya (sun).

**7** Ears burst forth. From the ears proceeded hearing, from hearing the Dis (quarters of the world).

**8** Skin burst forth. From the skin proceeded hairs, from the hairs plants and trees.

**9** A heart burst forth. From the heart proceeded mind, from mind Kandramas (moon).

**10** The navel burst forth. From the navel proceeded the Apâna (the down-breathing), from Apâna death.

**11** The generative organ burst forth. From the organ proceeded seed, from seed water.
