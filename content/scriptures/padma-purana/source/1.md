---
title: "Padma Purana - Chapter 1"
scripture: "padma-purana"
chapter: 1
---

**1** Suta said: I shall relate to you the sacred Padma Purana, so called because it describes the creation that arose from the lotus that sprang from the navel of Lord Vishnu.

**2** In the beginning, before creation, the Supreme Lord Vishnu reclined upon the cosmic serpent Shesha on the waters of the primal ocean.

**3** From His navel arose a golden lotus of infinite beauty, and from that lotus was born Brahma, the creator of the universe.

**4** This lotus is the symbol of creation itself — arising from the depths of the infinite, blooming in the light of consciousness, bearing within it all the worlds and all beings.

**5** Brahma, seated upon the lotus, looked about and beheld the vast waters. In meditation he perceived the Lord and received from Him the power and knowledge to create.

**6** From the lotus of creation, Brahma fashioned the five elements, the three worlds, the gods and demons, the sages and men, the animals and plants.

**7** All creation is contained within that lotus. All beings have their origin in it. He who meditates upon this cosmic lotus meditates upon the very source of existence.

**8** The Lord said: "This creation is My sport, My divine play. I create it, sustain it, and dissolve it, yet I remain unchanged, as the ocean remains unchanged though waves arise and fall."

**9** "The wise see Me in the lotus and the lotus in Me. They see the universe as My body and all beings as My children."

**10** He who hears this teaching of the Padma Purana with faith and devotion is freed from the bondage of ignorance and attains the lotus feet of the Lord.
