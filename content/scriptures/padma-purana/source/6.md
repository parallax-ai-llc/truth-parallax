---
title: "Padma Purana - Chapter 6"
scripture: "padma-purana"
chapter: 6
---

**1** The Lord said: I shall now give the final teaching of the Padma Purana, the essence of all sacred knowledge.

**2** There is one Supreme Reality, called by the wise as Brahman, Vishnu, Shiva, Shakti, or Atman. Names differ, but the Reality is one.

**3** The universe is the manifestation of that Reality. Every particle of creation contains the whole. Every being is a mirror of the Absolute.

**4** The purpose of human life is to realize this truth — to know the Self as Brahman, to see the Lord in all things, to live in the awareness of the divine presence.

**5** The means of realization are devotion, knowledge, selfless action, and meditation. Each is a path to the same summit. The seeker should follow the path that suits his nature.

**6** In this age of Kali, the path of devotion is the most accessible. The chanting of the divine name, the hearing of sacred stories, the service of the saints — these are the means of grace.

**7** The Lord is not far away. He dwells in the heart of every being. He is nearer than the nearest, dearer than the dearest. To find Him, one need only turn within.

**8** He who has found the Lord within has found everything. He who has lost the Lord within has lost everything, even if he possesses the entire world.

**9** This Padma Purana is the lotus of divine knowledge. He who reads it, hears it, studies it, and lives by it — he swims in the ocean of bliss and reaches the far shore of liberation.

**10** May the Lord Vishnu, who reclines upon the lotus of creation, who is the source and sustenance of all, grant liberation to all who seek Him. Om, peace, peace, peace.
