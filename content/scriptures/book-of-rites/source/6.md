## Yue Ling (Proceedings of Government in the Different Months)

In the first month of spring, the sun is in the constellation Ying Shi. The star culminating at dusk is Shen, and that culminating at dawn is Wei. Its days are jia and yi. Its divine ruler is Tai Hao, and the attending spirit is Gou Mang. Its creatures are the scaly. Its musical note is jue, its pitch-pipe is Tai Cu.

The Son of Heaven occupies the apartment on the left of the Green Bright Hall. He rides in the carriage with the phoenix bells, drawn by the azure-dragon horses, and bearing the green flags. He wears the green robes, and the green jade ornaments. He eats wheat and mutton.

In this month the vapors of heaven descend, and those of the earth ascend. Heaven and earth are in harmonious cooperation. All plants bud and grow.

The Son of Heaven gives orders to his commanders of the forces to charge the people: "Let the land that has been plowed be sowed." The inspectors of the fields go round about. The boundaries and limits of the land must be correctly defined.

In this month no trees must be cut down. Nests should not be thrown down; unformed creatures should not be killed, nor those that are pregnant, nor the very young, nor birds that are fledging. The young of animals and eggs should not be taken.
