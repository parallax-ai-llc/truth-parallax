## Wang Zhi (Royal Regulations)

In accordance with the royal regulations: the territories of the feudal princes formed five orders. Those of the first order contained a hundred li square; those of the second, seventy; those of the third, fifty; those of the fourth, no more than fifty; those of the fifth, no more than fifty.

The Son of Heaven, every five years, made a tour of inspection through the kingdom. In the second month of the year, he visited the east, where he examined the seasons and months, corrected the days, made uniform the standard tubes, the measures of length and capacity, and the steelyards, reformed ceremonies and musical instruments, and the different articles of introduction at audiences.

He commanded the chief minister to present the emoluments and ranks of the different orders, and conferred on all what was appropriate for each. He commanded the minister of instruction to exhibit the courses of study and the lessons, and to make full examination into dutiful filial piety and brotherly deference, with gentleness and goodness, selecting the accomplished and worthy to give a stimulus to all.

No state was to encroach upon another. The large states were not to change the rules for governing their smaller neighbors. The Son of Heaven did not speak about the territory of the different states as if they formed a whole kingdom.

He who had the rank of baron received his land from the feudal princes.
