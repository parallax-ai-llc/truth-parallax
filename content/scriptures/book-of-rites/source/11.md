## Jiao Te Sheng (The Single Victim at the Border Sacrifices)

In the border sacrifice, there was one victim -- a single bullock. In the sacrifice of the She, there was one pig. The single bullock was used to show the highest honor; the single pig to show simplicity.

When the victim was led past the ancestral temple, those inside the temple were moved with feeling. When the victim was fastened in the court, those present trembled. A superior man, having seen an animal alive, could not bear to see it die; having heard its cry, he could not bear to eat its flesh. Therefore he kept the slaughter-house at a distance.

By the suburban sacrifice, all the spirits receive their offices. By the sacrifice at the She, all the things of the earth find their proper place. By the sacrifices in the ancestral temple, the feelings of humanity are given their fullest expression.

In the sacrifice at the border, the things used were simple, and the reverence was great. Therefore it is said: "He who understands the border sacrifice and the She sacrifice would find the governance of a kingdom as easy as to look into his palm."

The meaning of rites is deep indeed. He who tries to enter it with the consideration of hardness and whiteness, sameness and difference, will drown in it. The meaning of rites is great indeed. He who tries to enter it with rash boldness and mere strength of will, will stumble in it.
