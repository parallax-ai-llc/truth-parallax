## Ming Tang Wei (The Places in the Hall of Distinction)

The Hall of Distinction was the hall of the Son of Heaven, in which he gave audience to the feudal princes. It was so called because in it the distinctions of honor were made clear to all.

The Son of Heaven in the Hall of Distinction issued his mandates and regulations, promulgated his teachings, and received the feudal princes. In it he gave a clear illustration of filial piety, brotherly duty, benevolence, and righteousness.

The Son of Heaven lived in the Grand Temple in spring, in the Grand Miao in summer, in the Grand Chamber in autumn, and in the Dark Hall in winter.

The apartment on the left of the Grand Temple was called the Green Bright Hall, and that on the right was the Hall of the Grand Miao.

Above the Grand Temple was the Ling Tai, the Spirit Tower. It was there that the Son of Heaven observed the movements of the heavens and examined the seasons, that the calendar might be correctly adjusted.

The inner apartments were for retirement and rest. The outer hall was for deliberation and government.

When the Son of Heaven sacrificed in the Hall of Distinction, there was a completeness in the ceremony. When the feudal princes sacrificed at their She altars, there was a restriction of the ceremony.
