*Pages 1-6 are missing from the manuscript.*

**7:1** "What is matter? Will it last forever?"

**7:2** The Savior replied, "All natures, all formed things, all creatures exist in and with one another, and will again be resolved into their own roots."

**7:3** "For the nature of matter is dissolved into what belongs to its nature alone."

**7:4** "Whoever has ears to hear, let them hear."

**7:5** Peter said to him, "Since you have explained everything to us, tell us this also: What is the sin of the world?"

**7:6** The Savior said, "There is no sin, but it is you who make sin when you do the things that are like the nature of adultery, which is called sin."

**7:7** "That is why the Good came into your midst, to the essence of every nature in order to restore it to its root."

**7:8** Then he continued and said, "That is why you become sick and die, for you are deprived of the one who can heal you."

**7:9** "Whoever has a mind to understand, let them understand."

**8:1** "Matter gave birth to a passion that has no equal, which proceeded from something contrary to nature. Then there arises a disturbance in its whole body."

**8:2** "That is why I said to you, Be of good courage, and if you are discouraged be encouraged in the presence of the different forms of nature."

**8:3** "Whoever has ears to hear, let them hear."

**8:4** When the Blessed One had said this, he greeted them all, saying, "Peace be with you. Receive my peace unto yourselves."

**8:5** "Beware that no one lead you astray saying, Lo here or lo there! For the Son of Man is within you."

**8:6** "Follow after him! Those who seek him will find him."

**8:7** "Go then and preach the gospel of the Kingdom."

**8:8** "Do not lay down any rules beyond what I appointed you, and do not give a law like the lawgiver lest you be constrained by it."

**8:9** When he said this he departed.
