## Overview

The Interfaith reading of the Letter of Aristeas draws on its model of respectful cultural encounter between Jewish and Greek traditions. The philosophical banquet scenes, in which Jewish wisdom is presented in terms accessible to Greek thought, offer a paradigm for interfaith dialogue that maintains distinctive identity while finding common ground. The question of sacred translation remains vital for all traditions with multilingual scripture.
