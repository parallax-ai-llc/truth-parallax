---
title: "Letter of Aristeas — Section 3"
scripture: "letter-of-aristeas"
chapter: 3
---

**1** Then the king ordered a letter to be written to Eleazar the Jewish High Priest, requesting him to send elders from each of the twelve tribes of Israel, men who had led exemplary lives and were experts in the Law.

**2** And Eleazar selected the men and sent them: seventy-two elders, six from each tribe, bringing the skins on which the Law was inscribed in gold lettering in Jewish characters.

**3** The parchments were wonderful, and the joining was made so skillfully that no seam was visible. The sight produced a deep impression upon us.

**4** When the king saw the men, he asked about the books. And they showed him the parchments with the Law inscribed upon them.

**5** The writing was in gold, and the characters were clear. And the king marveled at the beauty of the parchments and the skill of the scribes.
