---
title: "Letter of Aristeas — Section 4"
scripture: "letter-of-aristeas"
chapter: 4
---

**1** The king set apart seven days for feasting the elders, and he posed philosophical questions to them, testing their wisdom.

**2** He asked the first: What is the highest form of beauty? And the elder replied: Piety, for it is the highest form of beauty, and its power lies in love, which is the gift of God.

**3** He asked another: What is the essence of kingship? And he answered: To rule oneself well and not to be led astray by wealth or fame.

**4** He asked a third: What is the teaching of wisdom? And the reply was: As you wish that no evil should befall you, but to be a partaker of all good things, so you should act on the same principle toward those who are your subjects.

**5** And all the answers of the elders pleased the king greatly, and he honored them with gifts.

**6** For their wisdom was deep, and their answers were prompt, and the king said that these men were worthy of all praise.
