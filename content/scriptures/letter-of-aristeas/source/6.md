---
title: "Letter of Aristeas — Section 6"
scripture: "letter-of-aristeas"
chapter: 6
---

**1** When the work was completed, Demetrius collected the elders and read the translation to the assembled company of the Jews.

**2** The translation met with a great reception, both from the elders who had produced it and from the Jewish community, because of the great benefits which it conferred.

**3** And the community gave their approval to the translation, saying that it had been made well and truly and in every respect accurately.

**4** The king was greatly pleased and ordered that great care should be taken of the books and that they should be sacredly guarded.

**5** He ordered that the translators be sent home with fitting presents, both for themselves and for Eleazar the High Priest.

**6** These are the facts of the matter, O Philocrates, and I know that all who read this account will marvel at the greatness of the work and at the wisdom of the men who accomplished it.
