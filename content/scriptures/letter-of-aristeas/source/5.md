---
title: "Letter of Aristeas — Section 5"
scripture: "letter-of-aristeas"
chapter: 5
---

**1** After the feasting was ended, Demetrius led the elders to the island of Pharos, which lies across the causeway, and there he settled them in a house near the shore.

**2** The place was quiet and fitting for their sacred work. And they set about their task, comparing their translations and making them agree.

**3** They worked each day until the ninth hour, after which they attended to the refreshment of their bodies. Everything they required was provided in abundance.

**4** And Demetrius took notes of all that was agreed upon, and each morning the parchments were presented to him.

**5** And the work of translation was completed in seventy-two days, as if this coincidence had been the result of some design.
