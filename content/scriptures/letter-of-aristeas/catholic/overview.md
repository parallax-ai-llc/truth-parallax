## Overview

The Catholic reading of the Letter of Aristeas values the text for its testimony to the authority of the Septuagint tradition. The Septuagint served as the primary Old Testament for the early Church and influenced the Vulgate translation that became the Catholic standard. The Letter's account of divinely guided translation resonates with Catholic understanding of the Holy Spirit's guidance in the transmission of Scripture.
