---
title: "Cathar Ritual Section 3"
scripture: "cathar-ritual"
chapter: 3
---

**1** Then shall the Elder say: "You must understand that this baptism, which we give by the imposition of hands, is the baptism of the Holy Spirit, of which Christ spoke when He said: 'My Father will send the Holy Spirit in my name.'

**2** John the Baptist said: 'I baptize you with water unto repentance, but He who comes after me will baptize you with the Holy Spirit and with fire.'

**3** This spiritual baptism, by the imposition of hands, has been handed down from the Apostles, from Good Men to Good Men, until this present time, and it shall continue until the end of the world.

**4** You must understand that power is given to the Church of God to bind and to loose, to forgive sins and to retain them, as Christ said: 'Whatsoever you shall bind on earth shall be bound in heaven, and whatsoever you shall loose on earth shall be loosed in heaven.'

**5** The Elder shall then place the Book of the Gospels upon the head of the believer.

**6** And all the Good Christians present shall place their right hands upon the believer, and the Elder shall say the Pater Noster, and shall read the opening of the Gospel of John: 'In the beginning was the Word, and the Word was with God, and the Word was God.'"
