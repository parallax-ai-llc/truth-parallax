---
title: "Cathar Ritual Section 1"
scripture: "cathar-ritual"
chapter: 1
---

**1** In the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Spirit. Here begins the rite of the Consolamentum, by which the Holy Spirit is bestowed upon the believer.

**2** The believer who wishes to receive the Consolamentum must first undergo a period of preparation, known as the abstinentia, during which he shall fast and pray and learn the ways of the Good Christians.

**3** He shall renounce all oaths, all lies, all killing, and all eating of flesh, eggs, and cheese, for these are the products of generation and corruption.

**4** He shall learn the Lord's Prayer, which is the only prayer commanded by Christ, and he shall understand that the words "Give us this day our supersubstantial bread" refer not to earthly bread but to the bread of the Word of God.

**5** The Elder shall instruct him that this world was not made by the Good God but by the evil principle, and that our souls are imprisoned angels who fell from the heavenly kingdom.

**6** The believer shall make the melioramentum, bowing three times before the Elder and saying: "Bless us, Lord; pray God for us, sinners, that He may make us good Christians and lead us to a good end."
