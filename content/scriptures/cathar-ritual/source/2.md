---
title: "Cathar Ritual Section 2"
scripture: "cathar-ritual"
chapter: 2
---

**1** When the believer is ready to receive the Consolamentum, the Elder and the Good Christians shall assemble. The table shall be prepared with a white cloth, and upon it shall be placed the Book of the Gospels.

**2** The Elder shall say: "Do you wish to receive the spiritual baptism by which the Holy Spirit is given in the Church of God, together with the holy prayer, the imposition of hands by the Good Men?"

**3** The believer shall answer: "Yes, I desire it. Pray God for me, a sinner, that He may grant me His grace."

**4** The Elder shall say: "Do you promise to give yourself to God and to the Gospel? Do you promise never to eat meat, nor eggs, nor cheese, nor any food except from water and wood? Do you promise never to lie, never to swear an oath, never to kill, and never to abandon your body to any luxury?"

**5** The believer shall answer: "Yes, I so promise, with the help of God."

**6** Then the Elder shall instruct him in the Pater Noster, explaining each petition, for this prayer is the foundation of all worship, and no other prayer but this was given by the Lord Christ.
