---
title: "Cathar Ritual Section 4"
scripture: "cathar-ritual"
chapter: 4
---

**1** After the reading of the Gospel of John, the Elder shall say: "Let us adore the Father, and the Son, and the Holy Spirit." And all shall say: "It is worthy and just."

**2** Then the Elder shall give the kiss of peace to the newly consoled, and each Good Christian present shall do likewise, saying: "The peace of the Lord be with you always."

**3** The newly consoled is now a Perfect, a Good Christian, and must live according to all the precepts of the faith. He shall not eat flesh, eggs, or cheese. He shall not lie, nor swear, nor kill any creature.

**4** He shall say the Pater Noster at each meal, before eating and after. He shall say the prayer at each of the appointed hours.

**5** If a Perfect should sin after the Consolamentum, and break any of these precepts, he must again receive the Consolamentum, for the Spirit has departed from him.

**6** And at the hour of death, if a believer has not received the Consolamentum, the Good Christians shall administer it, for without this spiritual baptism, the soul cannot return to the heavenly Father but must again enter into a body of flesh.

**7** May the Holy Spirit guide and protect all who walk in the way of truth and goodness. Benedicite. Parcite nobis. Amen.
