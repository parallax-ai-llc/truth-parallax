---
title: "Platform Sutra - Chapter 1"
scripture: "platform-sutra"
chapter: 1
---

**1** Once, when the Great Master arrived at Pao Lin, Prefect Wei of Shao Chou and other officials went there to ask him to come to the lecture hall of the Ta Fan Temple in the city, to speak the Dharma for the assembly. When the Master had taken his seat, the prefect and more than thirty officials, more than thirty Confucian scholars, and more than a thousand monks and nuns, Taoists, and lay people all at once made obeisance, wishing to hear the essentials of Dharma.

**2** The Great Master said to the assembly: "Good Knowing Advisors, the self-nature of Bodhi is originally clear and pure. Simply use that mind, and you will straightaway accomplish Buddhahood. Good Knowing Advisors, listen while I tell you about the actions and intentions by which Hui Neng obtained the Dharma."

**3** "My stern father was originally from Fan Yang. He was banished to Hsin Chou in Ling Nan, where he became a commoner. My father died early and my aging mother and I, her only child, moved to Nan Hai. We suffered extreme poverty and I sold firewood in the marketplace."

**4** "By chance a customer bought firewood and took me to an inn. When the customer had left, I saw another man who was reciting a Sutra. As soon as I heard the words of that Sutra, my mind opened to awakening."

**5** "I asked the man which Sutra he was reciting. He said it was the Diamond Sutra. I then asked from where he had come, and where he had obtained that Sutra. He replied: 'I come from Tung Chan Temple in Chi Chou, Huang Mei District. There the Fifth Patriarch, the Great Master Hung Jen, teaches more than a thousand students.'"
