---
title: "Platform Sutra - Chapter 3"
scripture: "platform-sutra"
chapter: 3
---

**1** One night, in the third watch, so that no one would see him, Shen Hsiu held a candle and wrote a verse on the wall of the south corridor, submitting the verse from his mind. The verse said: "The body is the Bodhi tree, The mind is like a clear mirror. At all times we must strive to polish it, And must not let the dust collect."

**2** After Shen Hsiu had written this verse, he returned to his room. No one had seen him. Shen Hsiu thought it over: "Tomorrow, when the Fifth Patriarch sees the verse, if he is pleased, it will mean that I have an affinity with the Dharma. If he says that it is not worthy, it will mean that I am confused."

**3** The Fifth Patriarch then called Shen Hsiu inside his room and asked, "Did you write this verse?" Shen Hsiu said, "Yes, in fact, Hsiu did. He does not dare seek the position of Patriarch, but hopes the Compassionate Master will see whether or not your disciple has a little bit of wisdom."

**4** The Fifth Patriarch said, "The verse you wrote shows that you have not yet seen your own nature. You have arrived at the front door but you have not yet entered it. Common people who seek Supreme Perfect Enlightenment must understand their own original nature at each instant of thought."

**5** "Go think it over for a day or two and compose another verse and submit it to me. If your verse shows that you have entered the door, I will transmit the robe and the Dharma to you." Shen Hsiu did obeisance and withdrew.
