---
title: "Platform Sutra - Chapter 2"
scripture: "platform-sutra"
chapter: 2
---

**1** The next day the Patriarch called the disciples together and said: "I have something to say to you: for people in this world, the matter of birth and death is a great one. All day long you seek fields of blessing only, and do not try to get out of the bitter sea of birth and death. Moreover, if your own nature is confused, how can blessings save you?"

**2** "Each of you go back and look into your own wisdom. Use the prajna-nature of your own original mind to compose a verse. Submit it to me so I can look at it. If you understand the great meaning, the robe and Dharma will be passed on to you, and you will become the Sixth Patriarch."

**3** "Hurry, hurry! Speed is essential! Deliberating and considering is of no use in this matter. A person who sees his own nature sees it in the instant of a single thought. If it were as you say, a person who does that should be able to do it even while wielding a sword in battle."

**4** The disciples received his instructions and withdrew, saying to each other: "We don't need to clear our minds and spend effort to compose a verse to submit to the Master. What use would it be? Shen Hsiu is our senior instructor. He is certainly the one who should obtain it. It would be improper for us to compose verses, and a waste of effort."

**5** Shen Hsiu thought it over: "The others are not submitting verses because I am their teaching master. I must compose a verse and submit it to the Patriarch. If I do not submit a verse, how will the Patriarch know whether the understanding in my mind is deep or shallow?"
