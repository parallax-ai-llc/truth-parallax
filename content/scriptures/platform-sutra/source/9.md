---
title: "Platform Sutra - Chapter 9"
scripture: "platform-sutra"
chapter: 9
---

**1** Good Knowing Advisors, each of you must observe and examine himself. Do not mistakenly use your own natures. Do not get confused about the threefold body. Do not seek externally. The three bodies are in your own nature.

**2** What is the clear, pure Dharma-body Buddha? The nature of people of this world is originally pure. The ten thousand dharmas are all in the self-nature. If you think about all the evil things, you practice evil deeds. If you think about all the good things, you practice good deeds.

**3** The clear, pure Dharma-body is your nature. The perfect, full Reward-body is your wisdom. The hundred thousand ten thousand Transformation-bodies are your practices. Good Knowing Advisors, apart from the self-nature there is no other Buddha.

**4** What is the perfect, full Reward-body? Just as one lamp can disperse the darkness of a thousand years, one thought of wisdom can destroy ten thousand years of ignorance. Do not think of the past; always think of the future. If the future thoughts are always good, you may be called the Reward-body Buddha.

**5** What are the hundred thousand ten thousand Transformation-bodies? If you are without the thought of the ten thousand dharmas, the nature is basically like emptiness. One thought of calculation is transformation. If you think of evil things, you transform into hell. If you think of good things, you transform into heaven. Viciousness transforms you into a dragon or a snake. Compassion transforms you into a Bodhisattva. Wisdom transforms you into the upper realms. Stupidity transforms you into the lower realms. The self-nature transforms itself through many transformations.
