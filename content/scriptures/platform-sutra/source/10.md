---
title: "Platform Sutra - Chapter 10"
scripture: "platform-sutra"
chapter: 10
---

**1** The Master, in the year of Guizhou, the eighth month, the third day, after eating at Xinzhou, addressed his followers: Please be seated in order. I am going to say farewell to you.

**2** Fahai asked: Master, what teaching will you leave behind so that deluded people can see the Buddha-nature?

**3** The Master said: If you know the original mind, seeing the Buddha-nature is not difficult. If you do not know it, then even in ten thousand kalpas it will be difficult to find the Buddha, even if you search for him.

**4** I will now teach you to know the sentient beings within your own minds, and to see the Buddha-nature within your own minds. If you wish to see the Buddha, simply know the sentient beings. It is because sentient beings are deluded that they do not realize they are Buddhas. It is not that sentient beings are different from Buddhas.

**5** If you wish to see the Buddha, simply do not be attached to the ten thousand dharmas, and you will achieve the Buddha-nature. If you separate from the ten thousand dharmas, the nature of Suchness will be pure in itself.

**6** Good friends, each of you purify your own minds and practice according to the teaching of the Dharma. Each of you go and practice for yourselves. I hope you will accomplish the Buddha-Way.

**7** In the seventh month of the following year, the Master told his disciples: Come close. In the eighth month I wish to leave this world. If you have doubts, ask about them now, and I will explain them for you and break through your delusions. Once I am gone, there will be no one to teach you.

**8** On the third day of the eighth month, after eating, the Master said: Please be seated. I am going to say farewell to you. After my passing, do not grieve and weep, and do not accept condolences and gifts, and do not wear mourning. If you do these things, it is not my teaching and you are not my disciples.

**9** Be the same as when I was alive. Sit together in meditation. If you are only still without moving, without producing and destroying, without coming and going, without right and wrong, without staying and departing, this is the Great Way.

**10** After I have gone, just practice according to the teaching, as if I were still here. Even if I were still in the world, if you went against the teaching, there would be no use in my having stayed.

**11** After speaking these words, the Master sat upright until the third watch, then suddenly said to his disciples: I am going. And in an instant he passed away. At that time a strange fragrance filled the room, a white rainbow joined earth and heaven, and the trees in the forest turned white. The birds and beasts cried out in sorrow. This was the eleventh month of the year Guizhou.
