---
title: "Platform Sutra - Chapter 8"
scripture: "platform-sutra"
chapter: 8
---

**1** Good Knowing Advisors, what is meant by sitting in meditation? In this teaching, there is no obstruction or hindrance. Externally, when the mind does not activate thoughts with respect to any good or evil state, that is called sitting. Internally, seeing the unmoving self-nature is called meditation.

**2** Good Knowing Advisors, what is meant by meditation and concentration? Externally, being apart from marks is meditation. Internally, being undisturbed is concentration. If externally you dwell on marks, internally your mind will be disturbed. If externally you are apart from marks, your mind will not be disturbed.

**3** The original nature is naturally pure, naturally in concentration. It is only because you perceive states and think about states that you become disturbed. If you see states and your mind is not disturbed, that is true concentration.

**4** Good Knowing Advisors, externally being apart from marks is meditation. Internally being undisturbed is concentration. External meditation and internal concentration: that is meditation and concentration.

**5** The Vimalakirti Sutra says: "At that time suddenly return and regain the original mind." The Bodhisattva Precepts says: "Originally our own nature is pure." Good Knowing Advisors, in every thought, see your own original pure nature. Cultivate yourself, practice yourself, and accomplish the Buddha Way yourself.
