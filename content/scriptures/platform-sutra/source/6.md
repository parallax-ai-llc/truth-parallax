---
title: "Platform Sutra - Chapter 6"
scripture: "platform-sutra"
chapter: 6
---

**1** Good Knowing Advisors, the Dharma of the Prajna Paramita is originally possessed by people of this world themselves. It is only because the mind is confused that they cannot realize enlightenment by themselves, and must seek a Great Knowing Advisor to show them the way to see their own nature.

**2** Good Knowing Advisors, when enlightened, a stupid person becomes wise; when confused, a wise person becomes stupid. Good Knowing Advisors, the Prajna wisdom is originally possessed by all common people. It is only because the mind is confused that they cannot enlighten themselves.

**3** Good Knowing Advisors, what is Prajna? Prajna is a Sanskrit word which means wisdom. In all places, in every thought, not being stupid: that is Prajna. If you give rise to a single stupid thought, then Prajna is cut off; if you give rise to a single wise thought, then Prajna is produced.

**4** Common people are confused and do not understand; they have Prajna in their mouths, but confusion in their minds. They always say, "I cultivate Prajna," and yet, in continuous thought, they speak of emptiness but do not know true emptiness. Prajna has no form or mark; the wisdom mind is just that.

**5** If you thus understand, this is called the wisdom of Prajna. What is Paramita? It is a Sanskrit word which means "arrived at the other shore." When you understand the meaning, you are apart from birth and death. When you cling to states, birth and death arise, as waves arise on the sea. That is this shore. When you are apart from states, like water that constantly flows, that is called the other shore. Therefore it is called Paramita.
