---
title: "Platform Sutra - Chapter 7"
scripture: "platform-sutra"
chapter: 7
---

**1** Good Knowing Advisors, in this teaching, from the outset, sitting in meditation has basically not been about the mind and has not been about purity, and we do not speak of being unmoving.

**2** If one speaks of contemplating the mind, the mind is basically false. Since it is like an illusion, there is nothing to contemplate. If one speaks of contemplating purity, the nature of a person is basically pure. It is because of false thoughts that True Suchness is covered over. Simply be without false thoughts, and the nature is pure of itself.

**3** If the mind dwells on purity, you are simply producing the falseness of purity. The false has no place to dwell. So you know that what you contemplate is false. Purity has no form. If you set up the mark of purity, you obstruct your own original nature and become bound by purity.

**4** If you cultivate non-movement, and at the time of seeing all people, you do not see the right and wrong, the good and evil, the merit and demerit of other people, that is the non-movement of the self-nature.

**5** Good Knowing Advisors, the confused person may not move physically, but the moment he opens his mouth he speaks of the right and wrong, the strengths and weaknesses, the good and bad of other people, and thereby turns against the Way. If you contemplate the mind or contemplate purity, this obstructs the Way.
