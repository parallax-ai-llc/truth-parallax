## Confucian Reading

The lower Tuan Zhuan continues the moral interpretation of the hexagram judgments, focusing particularly on the later hexagrams that address more complex and challenging situations. The Confucian reader finds in these commentaries practical wisdom for navigating the difficulties of public life — the management of conflict, the exercise of authority, the cultivation of relationships, and the acceptance of limitation. The moral seriousness of the Tuan Zhuan reflects the Confucian conviction that the *Yijing*'s ultimate subject is the human character.
