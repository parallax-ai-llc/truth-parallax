## Confucian Reading

The lower Xi Ci Zhuan deepens the philosophical vision with reflections on the nature of change, the function of symbolic language, and the sage's relationship to the cosmic order. The famous statement that 'the *Yijing* is the measure of Heaven and Earth' expresses the Confucian conviction that this text captures the fundamental patterns of reality. The Great Commentary's discussion of the *Yijing*'s creation by the ancient sages establishes the text's authority as the wisdom of the founders of civilization.
