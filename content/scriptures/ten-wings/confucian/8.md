## Confucian Reading

The Shuo Gua Zhuan explains the symbolism and correlative associations of the eight trigrams — the fundamental building blocks of the hexagram system. The Confucian reading finds in these correlations a systematic account of the cosmos as a web of meaningful relationships: each trigram is associated with a direction, a season, a family member, a body part, an animal, and a moral quality. This correlative cosmology expresses the Confucian conviction that the natural, social, and moral orders are intimately interconnected.
