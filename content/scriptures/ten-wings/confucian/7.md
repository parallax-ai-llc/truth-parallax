## Confucian Reading

The Wenyan Zhuan provides the most detailed Confucian commentary on any part of the *Yijing*, focusing exclusively on the first two hexagrams: *Qian* (Heaven/The Creative) and *Kun* (Earth/The Receptive). The Confucian reader finds in this commentary the fullest expression of the moral metaphysics implicit in the hexagram system: *Qian* represents the active, creative principle embodied in the *junzi*'s moral initiative, while *Kun* represents the receptive, nurturing principle embodied in faithful responsiveness to what is right.
