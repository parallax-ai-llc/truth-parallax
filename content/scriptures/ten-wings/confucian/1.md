## Confucian Reading

The Confucian reading of the upper Tuan Zhuan finds in the Commentary on the Judgments a systematic demonstration of how the hexagrams encode moral and political wisdom. Each hexagram judgment is interpreted as describing an ethical situation — a configuration of relationships, forces, and responsibilities — that the *junzi* must navigate with virtue and discernment. The Tuan Zhuan transforms cryptic divinatory formulas into clear moral guidance, establishing the principle that the *Yijing* speaks not to fortune-tellers but to those who seek to cultivate their character.
