## Confucian Reading

The Xu Gua Zhuan explains the sequence of the 64 hexagrams, providing a narrative logic for their traditional arrangement. The Confucian reader finds in this sequence a philosophy of history and personal development: the hexagrams move from the primordial creative forces through the stages of social formation, political organization, cultural achievement, and eventual dissolution and renewal. The sequence models the Confucian understanding of civilization as a dynamic process of creation, order, and transformation.
