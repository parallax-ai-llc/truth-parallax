## Neo-Confucian Reading

The Neo-Confucian reading of the Xu Gua Zhuan finds in the hexagram sequence a cosmogonic narrative that parallels the *Taijitu Shuo*'s account of creation. The progression from *Qian* and *Kun* through the subsequent hexagrams models the emergence of increasing complexity from primordial simplicity — a process governed at every stage by *li*. The sequence thus illustrates the Neo-Confucian principle that cosmic evolution is not random but follows an intelligible pattern discoverable through philosophical inquiry.
