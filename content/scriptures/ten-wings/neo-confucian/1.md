## Neo-Confucian Reading

The Neo-Confucian reading of the upper Tuan Zhuan, shaped by the interpretive tradition of Cheng Yi and Zhu Xi, finds in the Commentary on the Judgments evidence for the metaphysical principle (*li*) that structures all reality. Each hexagram judgment is read as expressing a particular configuration of *li* — a pattern of cosmic order that the sage perceives through moral intuition and intellectual inquiry. The Tuan Zhuan thus becomes a manual for metaphysical discernment as well as moral guidance.
