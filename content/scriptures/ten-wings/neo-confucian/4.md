## Neo-Confucian Reading

The lower Xiang Zhuan continues the Neo-Confucian project of reading natural symbolism as moral metaphysics. Zhang Zai's concept of *qi* (material force) as the medium through which *li* manifests provides the theoretical framework for understanding how natural images can yield moral instruction: the patterns visible in nature are the same patterns that should govern human conduct, because both nature and morality are expressions of a single cosmic principle.
