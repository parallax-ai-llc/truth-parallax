## Neo-Confucian Reading

The Neo-Confucian reading of the Wenyan Zhuan finds in its commentary on *Qian* and *Kun* the fullest expression of the relationship between cosmic creativity and moral cultivation. *Qian*'s four virtues — *yuan* (origination), *heng* (success), *li* (furthering), *zhen* (perseverance) — are identified by Cheng Yi with the four moral virtues of *ren* (humaneness), *li* (propriety), *yi* (righteousness), and *zhi* (wisdom), establishing the identity of cosmic and moral order.
