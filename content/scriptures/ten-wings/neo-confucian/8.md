## Neo-Confucian Reading

The Neo-Confucian reading of the Shuo Gua Zhuan finds in the trigram correlations a systematic cosmology that supports the *li-qi* metaphysics. The trigrams' associations with directions, seasons, and natural phenomena are understood as expressions of the way *li* organizes *qi* into the structured patterns of the natural world. Shao Yong's elaborate numerological systems, based on the trigram arrangements described in this Wing, represent the most systematic Neo-Confucian development of these correlations.
