## Neo-Confucian Reading

The upper Xi Ci Zhuan is the single most important source for Neo-Confucian metaphysics. Zhou Dunyi's *Taijitu Shuo* (Explanation of the Diagram of the Supreme Ultimate) — the foundational text of Neo-Confucian cosmology — is directly rooted in the Great Commentary's concept of *Taiji*. The Neo-Confucian reading finds in the Xi Ci Zhuan the basis for a complete metaphysical system: the *Taiji* generates yin and yang, yin and yang generate the five phases, and the five phases generate the myriad things.
