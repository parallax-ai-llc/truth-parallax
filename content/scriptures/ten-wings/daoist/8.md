## Daoist Reading

The Shuo Gua Zhuan's discussion of the trigrams provides the Daoist tradition with the symbolic vocabulary for practices ranging from feng shui to internal alchemy to martial arts. The eight trigrams' correlations with directions, seasons, body organs, and natural phenomena constitute a comprehensive map of correspondences that the Daoist practitioner uses to navigate the invisible forces that shape the visible world. The Earlier Heaven and Later Heaven trigram arrangements are central to Daoist ritual and meditation practice.
