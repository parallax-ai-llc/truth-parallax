## Daoist Reading

The Daoist reading of the Xu Gua Zhuan interprets the hexagram sequence as a description of the *Dao*'s natural rhythm of creation, growth, maturity, decline, and renewal. Rather than a moral narrative of civilization's development (as in the Confucian reading), the Daoist sees in the sequence the universal pattern of cyclic change — the endless turning of the wheel of transformation that the sage observes with equanimity. The sequence models the Daoist understanding that all things arise, flourish, and return to their source.
