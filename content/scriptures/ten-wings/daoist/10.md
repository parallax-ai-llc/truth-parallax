## Daoist Reading

The Daoist reading of the Za Gua Zhuan appreciates its terse, paradoxical pairings as expressions of the Daoist insight that every quality contains its opposite and every situation carries the seed of its own reversal. The paired hexagrams illustrate the Daoist principle of complementary opposition — the understanding that yin and yang are not enemies but partners in the dance of cosmic transformation. The Za Gua's economy of expression models the Daoist preference for suggestive brevity over elaborate explanation.
