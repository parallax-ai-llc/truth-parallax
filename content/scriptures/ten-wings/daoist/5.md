## Daoist Reading

The upper Xi Ci Zhuan is the most cosmologically significant Wing for the Daoist reading, as it articulates the concepts of yin-yang, *Taiji*, and cosmic transformation that are central to Daoist philosophy and practice. The Great Commentary's description of the *Dao* as 'that which lets now the yin, now the yang, predominate' provides the theoretical foundation for Daoist internal alchemy (*neidan*), in which the practitioner learns to harmonize the yin and yang forces within the body and mind.
