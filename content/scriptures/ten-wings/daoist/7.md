## Daoist Reading

The Daoist reading of the Wenyan Zhuan finds in its commentary on *Qian* and *Kun* the primordial yin-yang polarity that underlies all Daoist cosmology and practice. *Qian* as pure yang and *Kun* as pure yin represent the two fundamental forces whose interaction generates the myriad things. The Daoist practitioner of internal alchemy works with these forces within the body, seeking to achieve the primordial unity (*Taiji*) that precedes their differentiation.
