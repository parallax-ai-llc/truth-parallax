## Daoist Reading

The lower Tuan Zhuan continues the Daoist reading of the hexagram judgments as descriptions of natural process rather than moral prescription. The Daoist finds in these commentaries guidance for the practice of *wu-wei* — acting in harmony with the natural momentum of each situation rather than imposing predetermined plans or moral categories. The wisdom of the *Yijing*, in this reading, is not the wisdom of the moralist but the wisdom of the sage who perceives the *Dao* in every configuration of change.
