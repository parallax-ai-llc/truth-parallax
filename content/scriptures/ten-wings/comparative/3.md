## Comparative Reading

Comparative study of the upper Xiang Zhuan's method of deriving moral instruction from natural imagery connects with similar practices across cultures: the medieval European tradition of *exempla* drawn from nature, the Buddhist use of natural metaphors for dharmic teaching, and the Native American tradition of learning from animal and plant teachers. Each tradition assumes a meaningful correspondence between natural phenomena and human conduct.
