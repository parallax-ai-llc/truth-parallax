## Comparative Reading

Comparative analysis places the upper Tuan Zhuan alongside other traditions' commentarial practices on divinatory and sacred texts: the Jewish *Midrash* on Torah, Hindu commentaries on the *Brahma Sutras*, and Islamic *tafsir* on the Qur'an. Each tradition develops sophisticated interpretive methods for extracting philosophical and ethical meaning from texts that were originally composed for different purposes — a hermeneutical challenge that generates some of the most creative thinking in each tradition.
