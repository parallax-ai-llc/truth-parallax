## Comparative Reading

Comparative analysis of the Shuo Gua Zhuan's trigram correlations reveals parallels with other traditions' systems of cosmic correspondence: the Kabbalistic *sefirot* and their associations, the Hindu *chakra* system, the Islamic science of *huruf* (letters), and the medieval European system of the four elements and their qualities. Each system maps the cosmos through a network of meaningful correspondences that connect the macrocosm with the microcosm.
