## Comparative Reading

The Xu Gua Zhuan's hexagram sequence invites comparison with other traditions' accounts of cosmic development and historical process: the Jewish concept of sacred history from creation to redemption, Hegel's dialectical philosophy of history, the Hindu concept of the four *yugas*, and the Buddhist concept of the decline of the dharma. Each tradition proposes a meaningful order to the unfolding of events across time.
