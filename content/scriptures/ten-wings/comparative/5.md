## Comparative Reading

Comparative study of the upper Xi Ci Zhuan's cosmology places it alongside other traditions' accounts of cosmic origin and order: the Neoplatonic scheme of emanation from the One, the Hindu cosmogony of the *Nasadiya Sukta*, the Kabbalistic *seder hishtalshelut*, and the Buddhist concept of dependent origination. The Great Commentary's distinctive feature — its grounding of cosmology in the practice of divination and symbolic thinking — offers a unique perspective in this global philosophical conversation.
