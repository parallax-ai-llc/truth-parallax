## Comparative Reading

The lower Tuan Zhuan's interpretation of the hexagram judgments invites comparison with other traditions' approaches to the relationship between symbolic systems and moral knowledge. The claim that natural symbols can yield moral instruction connects with the medieval European tradition of reading nature as a 'Book of God,' the Islamic concept of *ayat* (signs of God in creation), and the Hindu reading of cosmic order (*rta*) as moral order (*dharma*).
