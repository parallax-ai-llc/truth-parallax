## Comparative Reading

Comparative study of the Za Gua Zhuan's paired descriptions connects with the broader cross-cultural phenomenon of thinking in opposites: the Pythagorean table of opposites, the Buddhist concept of dependent designation (things defined by their opposites), the Structuralist analysis of binary oppositions, and the Daoist insight that all polarities are mutually dependent. The Za Gua's terse pairings exemplify a mode of thinking that is both ancient and universal.
