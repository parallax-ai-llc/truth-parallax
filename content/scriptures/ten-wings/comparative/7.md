## Comparative Reading

Comparative study of the Wenyan Zhuan's commentary on *Qian* and *Kun* connects with other traditions' explorations of cosmic polarity: the Hindu *Shiva-Shakti*, the Kabbalistic *tiferet-malkhut*, the Zoroastrian *Ahura Mazda-Angra Mainyu*, and the Gnostic *syzygy*. Each tradition articulates a primal duality whose interaction generates the manifest world, though the *Yijing*'s yin-yang is distinctive in being complementary rather than oppositional.
