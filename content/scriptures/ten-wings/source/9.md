---
title: "Ten Wings - Chapter 9"
scripture: "ten-wings"
chapter: 9
---

**1** The movement of heaven is full of power. Thus the superior man makes himself strong and untiring.

**2** The earth's condition is receptive devotion. Thus the superior man who has breadth of character carries the outer world.

**3** Clouds and thunder represent difficulty at the beginning. Thus the superior man brings order out of confusion.

**4** A spring wells up at the foot of the mountain. Thus the superior man cultivates his character through thoroughness in all that he does.

**5** Clouds rise up to heaven. Thus the superior man eats and drinks and is joyous and of good cheer.

**6** Water over earth. Thus the ancient kings divided the lands and established the feudal princes.

**7** In the middle of the earth there is water. Thus the superior man organizes the multitudes and educates the people.

**8** Wind over lake. Thus the superior man encourages people to approach and be open.

**9** Fire within the mountain. Thus the superior man is clear-minded and cautious in imposing penalties.

**10** The judgments of the sages show us the eternal principles by which heaven and earth operate.
