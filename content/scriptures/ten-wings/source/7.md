---
title: "Ten Wings - Chapter 7"
scripture: "ten-wings"
chapter: 7
---

**1** "Sublimely creative is the primal power of the Creative, to which all beings owe their beginning. It brings about all things under heaven."

**2** The clouds pass and the rain does its work, and all individual beings flow into their forms. Vastly comprehending the connection between the end and the beginning, the six stages complete themselves in time.

**3** Each one in its appropriate time is borne by the six dragons as if riding to heaven. The way of the Creative works through change and transformation.

**4** Each thing receives its true nature and destiny, and thus its life is preserved in union with the great harmony. All this is beneficial and persevering.

**5** Rising up high above all things, the sovereign ruler brings peace to all the lands.

**6** The Receptive is the most devoted of all things. Through devotion it achieves its task. The Receptive receives the seed and gives to all things their completion.

**7** The way of the Receptive — how devoted it is! It receives heaven into itself and acts in its own time.

**8** The firm and the yielding undergo change. These two lines represent the Tao of heaven and earth.

**9** The superior man makes his character broad and solid, so that he can support all things. His magnanimity corresponds to that of the earth, which sustains all creatures.

**10** He must act in time, in accord with the proper moment. The superior man first acts, then speaks; he first practises, then teaches.
