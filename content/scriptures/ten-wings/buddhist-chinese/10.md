## Buddhist-Chinese Reading

The Buddhist-Chinese reading of the Za Gua Zhuan finds in its paired hexagrams an illustration of the Madhyamaka teaching that all opposites are ultimately empty of inherent existence. The contrasting descriptions of paired hexagrams — advance and retreat, fullness and emptiness, strength and weakness — model the Buddhist insight that all dualities are conventional rather than ultimate, and that wisdom consists in perceiving the emptiness at the heart of all apparent oppositions.
