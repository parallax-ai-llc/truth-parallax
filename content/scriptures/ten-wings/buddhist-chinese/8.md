## Buddhist-Chinese Reading

The Buddhist-Chinese reading of the Shuo Gua Zhuan approaches the trigram correlations through the Buddhist concept of *dharmadhatu* — the realm of reality in which all phenomena are interconnected through the web of dependent origination. The eight trigrams' network of correspondences illustrates the Huayan teaching that each phenomenon reflects and contains every other phenomenon. The trigram system provides Chinese Buddhist thinkers with a culturally familiar model for expressing the concept of universal interpenetration.
