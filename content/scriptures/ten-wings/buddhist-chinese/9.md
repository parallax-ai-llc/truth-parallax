## Buddhist-Chinese Reading

The Buddhist-Chinese reading of the Xu Gua Zhuan interprets the hexagram sequence through the Buddhist concept of *karma* and cyclic existence (*samsara*). The progression of hexagrams models the Buddhist understanding that all conditioned phenomena arise, develop, and pass away in dependence on causes and conditions. The sequence's movement from creation through development to dissolution and renewal illustrates the Buddhist teaching on the cyclic nature of conditioned existence.
