## Buddhist-Chinese Reading

The Buddhist-Chinese reading of the Wenyan Zhuan interprets the commentary on *Qian* and *Kun* through the Mahayana concepts of *prajna* (wisdom) and *karuna* (compassion). *Qian*'s creative power is associated with the active compassion that seeks to benefit all beings, while *Kun*'s receptive nature is associated with the wisdom that perceives the emptiness of all phenomena. This reading transforms the Confucian moral metaphysics of the Wenyan into a Buddhist spiritual teaching.
