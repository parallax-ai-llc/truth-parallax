## Academic Reading

Academic analysis of the Xu Gua Zhuan examines the logic of the hexagram sequence — whether it reflects a cosmogonic narrative, a political philosophy, a theory of natural process, or some combination of these. Scholars compare the received hexagram order with the alternative sequence found in the Mawangdui manuscripts, noting significant differences that raise questions about the authoritativeness of either arrangement. The Xu Gua's explanatory method — connecting each hexagram to its predecessor through causal or logical relationships — is analyzed as a distinctive hermeneutical strategy.
