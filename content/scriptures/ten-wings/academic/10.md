## Academic Reading

Scholars study the Za Gua Zhuan as the briefest and most enigmatic of the Ten Wings. Its terse, paired descriptions of the hexagrams are analyzed for their interpretive value and their relationship to the longer commentaries. Some scholars view the Za Gua as an independent interpretive tradition that preserves alternative readings of the hexagrams, while others regard it as a mnemonic summary of the main commentarial tradition. Its dating — possibly the latest of the Wings — remains debated.
