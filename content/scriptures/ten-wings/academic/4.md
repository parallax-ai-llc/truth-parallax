## Academic Reading

Scholars study the lower Xiang Zhuan's treatment of individual line statements, analyzing how the Commentary constructs moral meaning from often obscure divinatory formulas. The interpretive strategies used — allegorical reading, etymological analysis, structural symbolism — are examined as hermeneutical methods that would influence Chinese textual interpretation for millennia. The Xiang Zhuan's approach is compared with hermeneutical traditions in other cultures.
