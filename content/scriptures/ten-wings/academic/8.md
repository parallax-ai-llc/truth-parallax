## Academic Reading

Scholars analyze the Shuo Gua Zhuan's trigram correlations as a systematic cosmological schema rooted in the correlative thinking of the late Warring States and early Han periods. The two trigram arrangements described — Earlier Heaven (*xiantian*) and Later Heaven (*houtian*) — are examined for their origins, their relationship to each other, and their subsequent influence on Chinese cosmology, ritual, and practice. The text's connections to the Yin-Yang and Five Phases schools of thought are assessed.
