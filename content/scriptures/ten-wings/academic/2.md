## Academic Reading

Scholars analyze the lower Tuan Zhuan in relation to the upper section, noting differences in vocabulary, style, and interpretive method that may indicate different authorship or periods of composition. The Commentary's treatment of the later hexagrams is compared with alternative interpretive traditions preserved in the Mawangdui manuscripts and other early sources, revealing the diversity of approaches to *Yijing* interpretation in the pre-Han period.
