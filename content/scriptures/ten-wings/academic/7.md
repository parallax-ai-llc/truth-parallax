## Academic Reading

Academic analysis of the Wenyan Zhuan examines its intensive focus on the first two hexagrams as a hermeneutical strategy that uses *Qian* and *Kun* as paradigms for the entire system. Scholars note the Commentary's multiple interpretive approaches — moral, cosmological, political, and personal — applied to the same hexagram texts, revealing the polysemic richness that the commentarial tradition finds in the *Yijing*. The Wenyan's dating and authorship remain subjects of scholarly debate.
