## Academic Reading

Academic analysis of the upper Tuan Zhuan examines its composition history, dating, and relationship to the hexagram texts it interprets. Scholars note the Commentary's systematic use of trigram symbolism and yin-yang theory to explain the hexagram judgments — an interpretive method that reflects intellectual developments of the late Warring States period. The Tuan Zhuan's vocabulary and philosophical assumptions are compared with other texts of the period to establish its intellectual context.
