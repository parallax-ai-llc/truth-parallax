---
title: "Hidden Words - Chapter 11"
scripture: "hidden-words"
chapter: 11
---

**1** O Son of Dust! Verily I say unto thee: Of all men the most negligent is he that disputeth idly and seeketh to advance himself over his brother. Say, O brethren! Let deeds, not words, be your adorning.

**2** O Children of Adam! A dewdrop out of the ocean of My mercy I have shed upon the peoples of the world, yet found none turn thereunto, inasmuch as everyone hath turned away from the celestial wine of unity unto the foul water of impurity.

**3** O My Servant! The tongue is for mentioning what is good, defile it not with unseemly talk. God hath forgiven what is past. Henceforward everyone should utter that which is meet and seemly.

**4** O Son of Being! Say: O rich men! When My beauty passeth by, wherefore bow ye not? When My fragrance wafteth, wherefore breathe ye not? When the sun of My knowledge riseth, wherefore walk ye not in its light?

**5** O Brethren! Let not your doings belie your words, that ye may reflect the beauty of the divine in every act.

**6** O Children of the Divine! Be ye as fingers of one hand and as limbs of one body. Thus counselleth you the Pen of Revelation, if ye be of them that believe.

**7** O Son of Being! My eternity is My creation, I have created it for thee. Make it the garment of thy temple. My unity is My handiwork; I have wrought it for thee; clothe thyself therewith.

**8** O Offspring of Dust! Be not content with the ease of a passing day, and deprive not thyself of everlasting rest. Barter not the garden of eternal delight for the dust-heap of a mortal world.

**9** O My Friends! Call ye to mind that covenant ye have entered into with Me upon Mount Paran, situate within the hallowed precincts of Zaman.

**10** O Son of Earth! Know verily, the heart wherein the least remnant of envy yet lingers, shall never attain My everlasting dominion, nor inhale the sweet savors of the Holy Spirit breathed within the Ridvan of My Name, the Almighty.
