## Overview

The Islamic reading of the Hidden Words engages with Bahá'u'lláh's mystical aphorisms from the distinctive vantage point of the Islamic tradition. This perspective brings particular attention to the text's ethical teachings, its mystical imagery, its relationship to prior revelatory traditions, and its vision of the purified human heart as the dwelling place of the divine. The Islamic interpretation illuminates both the inner spiritual logic of the Hidden Words and its resonances with broader religious and philosophical traditions.
