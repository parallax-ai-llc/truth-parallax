---
title: "Apocalypse of Adam Section 2"
scripture: "apocalypse-of-adam"
chapter: 2
---

**1** Now then, my son Seth, I will reveal to you the things which those men whom I saw before me at first revealed to me.

**2** After I have completed the times of this generation and the years of the generation have been accomplished, then the servant shall come upon you in the flood.

**3** For when the God of the powers comes, fire and water and darkness will cover all who dwell upon the earth. And they will destroy all flesh from the earth, both men and beasts and birds and fish and every reptile that moves upon the face of the earth.

**4** And those who have not known the God of truth, who are in the wrong, together with the creations of those men, shall be destroyed, all of them.

**5** But the seed of the great Seth shall be saved through the flood.

**6** Noah and many other people from the immovable race shall enter a place where they shall dwell upon a high mountain, upon a rock of truth.

**7** And the rain and the flood, the darkness and the water shall come upon them, and they shall be covered with darkness and shadow.

**8** And 400,000 men of the great race shall be preserved from the flood, the rain and the wind, and they shall be overshadowed with a great cloud of light and a knowing perception.

**9** Then the angels and all the generations of the powers will use the name in error, asking: Where did the great eternal knowledge come from? It did not come from us, nor from the angels of the great powers, nor from the powers.

**10** But the seed of the great Seth will be saved, and it shall be taken by great angels to the place where the spirit of life dwells.

**11** And the whole multitude of the seed shall dwell upon the earth, having received the glory and the power and the immortality and knowledge.

**12** And after Noah, and his sons and their wives and their children, they shall serve in fear and slavery all the days of their lives. And the whole multitude of the seed of the great Seth was called by a name and was not found to be in error.
