---
title: "Apocalypse of Adam Section 3"
scripture: "apocalypse-of-adam"
chapter: 3
---

**1** Then the God of the aeons will give to them from those who serve him a fire which will consume all the powers and their works. And the wrath will bring upon them their error, and it will destroy them.

**2** And four hundred men of the immovable race will be taken up, and they will leave the land and dwell upon the heights of the great aeons.

**3** But the God of the powers will be disturbed, saying: What is the power of this man who is higher than we? Then he will raise a great wrath against that man.

**4** And the glory will withdraw and dwell in holy houses which it has chosen for itself. And the powers will not see it with their eyes, nor will they see the illuminator either.

**5** Then they will punish the flesh of the man upon whom the holy spirit came. Then the angels and all the generations of the powers will use the name in error.

**6** For a second time the illuminator of knowledge will pass by in great glory, in order to leave something of the seed of Noah and the sons of Ham and Japheth—to leave for himself fruit-bearing trees.

**7** And he will redeem their souls from the day of evil. For the whole creation that came from the dead earth will be under the authority of death. But those who reflect upon the knowledge of the eternal God in their heart will not perish.

**8** For they have not received spirit from this kingdom alone, but they have received it from one of the eternal angels. The illuminator will come upon them and will dwell with them upon the earth.

**9** And all things which the illuminator has requested will be granted to him. And the glory of God will draw him to itself, and the holy spirit will bring him to the great aeons, to the holy angels and the aeons, and the men will be like those angels.

**10** For they will trample upon death in the power of the great illuminator. And they will draw near to the light through the knowledge and the great commandments and the great teachings.
