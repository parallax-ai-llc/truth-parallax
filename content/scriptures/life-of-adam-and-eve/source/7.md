---
title: "Life of Adam and Eve - Chapter 7"
scripture: "life-of-adam-and-eve"
chapter: 7
---

**1** And when Seth and his mother had come to Adam, they told him how the beast, the serpent, had bitten Seth. And Adam said to Eve: "What hast thou done? A great plague hast thou brought upon us, transgression and sin for all our generations."

**2** "And this which thou hast done, tell thy children after my death, for those who arise from us shall toil and fail, but they shall be wanting and they will curse us and say, All evils have our parents brought upon us, who were from the beginning."

**3** When Eve heard these words, she began to weep and moan.

**4** And when the six days were fulfilled, as the angel Michael had told him, Adam perceived that he was going to die. And he said to all his sons: "Behold, I am nine hundred and thirty years old, and when I die, bury me towards the sunrising in the field of yonder dwelling."

**5** And it came to pass when he had finished all his discourse, he gave up his spirit.

**6** Then the sun and moon and stars were darkened, and the heaven opened, and the earth trembled, and there was a light from the east, and all the luminaries were dim.

**7** And Seth was still clinging to the hand of his father, and no one knew that he was dead, for they stood praying and waiting.

**8** And the angels came and gathered themselves together, as they had been commanded. And when all the angels had assembled in the sight of Adam, Michael and Gabriel were before the presence of God. And the seraphim and cherubim, and all the angels that were above, gave praise to the Most High, saying: "Holy, Holy, Holy, is the Lord of Hosts. The heaven and the earth are full of His glory."

**9** And when all the angels had assembled together before God in the height, and they had made supplication for Adam, then Michael came and brought sweet incense to God, and the Lord said: "Let the body of Adam be brought, and let it be anointed with the oil of the third heaven."

**10** And the three great angels came and wrapped him in a linen cloth, and oil was poured upon him; and the first body of Adam was laid in the grave.

**11** Then the Lord said: "Behold, the first man that I created upon the earth, in the sweat of his face he ate bread, and now he lies in the earth from which he was taken. I formed his body of the dust of the earth, and I return him unto his earth."

**12** And the Lord said: "As I have said, thou art earth and to earth shalt thou return. I promise thee the resurrection. I will raise thee on the last day in the resurrection with every man of thy seed."

**13** And after these words, God made a seal shaped like a triangle, and sealed the tomb of Adam, and said: "Let no man touch it for six days until his rib shall return to him."

**14** And the Lord and His angels returned to heaven. And Adam was buried according to the word of God in the place where God found the dust from which He formed Adam, in the region of paradise.
