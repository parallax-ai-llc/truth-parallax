## Overview

The Comparative reading of the Life of Adam and Eve sets the text alongside other traditions about the first human beings — the Qur'anic account of Iblis's refusal to prostrate before Adam (Surah 2:34, 7:11-18), Zoroastrian traditions about Mashya and Mashyana, and indigenous creation narratives from around the world. The universal themes of paradise lost, the origin of death, and the hope of restoration emerge as cross-cultural constants.
