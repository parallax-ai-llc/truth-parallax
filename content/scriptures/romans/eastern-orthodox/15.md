## Bearing One Another's Burdens

Romans 15's call to bear with the failings of the weak (15:1) aligns with the Orthodox emphasis on communal spiritual life. The monastic tradition stresses that bearing with one another's weaknesses is a spiritual practice, the crucible in which love is forged.

## The Unity of Jew and Gentile in Worship

Paul's Old Testament quotations (15:9-12) demonstrate that the worship of God by all nations was always the divine intention. Orthodox liturgical theology sees the fulfillment of this vision in the Divine Liturgy, where people of every nation are gathered into one Body.

## Paul's Missionary Vision

Paul's ambition to preach where Christ has not been named (15:20) reflects the missionary dimension of the apostolic Church. Orthodox mission history, from the evangelization of the Slavs to the Alaskan mission, takes Paul's strategy as a model.
