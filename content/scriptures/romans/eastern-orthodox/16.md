## The Community of the Faithful

Romans 16's greetings reveal the social reality of the early Christian community: a network of house churches united by faith and mutual love. Orthodox ecclesiology sees in this chapter the embryonic form of the parish. The names listed are commemorated as saints in the Orthodox calendar.

## Women in the Early Church

The prominence of women in Romans 16, including Phoebe the deaconess, Priscilla the co-worker, and Junia the apostle, is acknowledged in Orthodox tradition. The early Church had an order of deaconesses, and the Orthodox Church is discussing its restoration.

## The Final Doxology

The letter's concluding doxology (16:25-27) gathers the theological argument into an act of worship. The "mystery that was kept secret for long ages" is the revelation of God's plan to include all nations in salvation, the mystery at the heart of the liturgy.
