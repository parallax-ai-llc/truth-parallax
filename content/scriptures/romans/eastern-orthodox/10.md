## The Nearness of the Word

Paul's citation of Deuteronomy 30 (10:8) resonates with the Orthodox understanding that the Logos (Christ) is intimately present to every human being. The Incarnation brings the divine Word into the fabric of human existence. Faith is a response to a Presence already given.

## Confession and the Liturgy

The formula of 10:9 is echoed in the baptismal confession and every Eucharistic liturgy. The public confession of faith is not a private act but a communal, liturgical one expressing the faith of the whole Church.

## The Beautiful Feet of the Evangelists

Paul's quotation of Isaiah 52:7 (10:15) grounds the Orthodox theology of evangelism and mission. The apostolic preaching continues through the ordained ministry, the reading of Scripture in the liturgy, and the witness of the saints.
