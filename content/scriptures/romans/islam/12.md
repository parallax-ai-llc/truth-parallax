## Ethical Parallels

Romans 12's ethical instructions overlap significantly with Islamic moral teaching. Paul's call to genuine love (12:9), to "outdo one another in showing honor" (12:10), and to contribute to others' needs echo Quranic teachings on ihsan (excellence in conduct), ikram (honoring others), and infaq (charitable spending).

## Overcoming Evil with Good

Paul's instruction to "overcome evil with good" (12:21) aligns with the Quranic teaching: "Repel evil by that which is better" (41:34). The Prophet Muhammad also taught: "The strong man is not the one who can wrestle, but the one who controls himself in anger."

## The Body Metaphor and the Ummah

Paul's body comparison (12:4-5) parallels the Islamic concept of the ummah as a single body. The Prophet said: "The believers in their mutual kindness are like one body. When one organ ails, the whole body responds with sleeplessness and fever" (Sahih Muslim).
