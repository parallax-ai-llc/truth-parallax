## Christ the Servant of Revelation

Paul's description of Christ as a "servant to the circumcised" (15:8) was read as the Pleromatic Christ's mission within the Demiurge's covenant community. Christ came to reveal the truth the Demiurge concealed: the existence of the supreme God beyond the creator.

## The Gentiles and the Pleroma

The Old Testament quotations about Gentiles praising God (15:9-12) were read as prophecies of the pneumatic community's worship of the true God. The "fullness" (pleroma) of blessing Paul invokes (15:29) employs the Gnostic technical term, confirming Paul's esoteric knowledge.

## The Mystery Hidden for Ages

Paul's reference to the mystery kept secret (anticipating 16:25) was the ultimate proof text: Paul acknowledged his gospel contained a secret dimension, hidden from the psychic majority and revealed only to the pneumatic elite.
