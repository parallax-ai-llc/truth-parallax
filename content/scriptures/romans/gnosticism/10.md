## The Word Near You

Paul's quotation of Deuteronomy 30 (10:8) was read as describing the pneumatic seed already present within the elect. The "word" is not an external message but an inner reality to be recognized. Gnosis is awakening to what has always been present within.

## Confession as Recognition

"If you confess with your mouth that Jesus is Lord" (10:9) was interpreted as the pneumatic's acknowledgment of liberation from the archons. "Jesus is Lord" means the revealer from the Pleroma has overcome the rulers of this world.

## The Call from Beyond

Paul's chain of hearing, preaching, sending (10:14-15) was read as the transmission of gnosis from the Pleroma through successive teachers. The Valentinian claim to a tradition from Paul through Theudas was grounded in this understanding of apostolic succession as the transmission of esoteric knowledge.
