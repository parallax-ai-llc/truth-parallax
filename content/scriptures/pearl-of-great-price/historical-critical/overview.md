## Overview

Historical-Critical engagement with the Pearl of Great Price brings distinctive interpretive methods and concerns to this foundational text. This approach illuminates dimensions of the text that may be less visible from other perspectives.

## Key Interpretive Approaches

### Source Criticism
Historical-critical scholars examine the Pearl of Great Price's sources, identifying the literary, oral, and documentary materials that contributed to its composition.

### Redaction History
Analysis of the Pearl of Great Price's editorial history reveals how the text was shaped by successive editors, compilers, and tradents over time.

### Social-Historical Context
Historical-critical interpretation situates the Pearl of Great Price within its original social, economic, and political context, examining how these factors shaped the text's composition and reception.
