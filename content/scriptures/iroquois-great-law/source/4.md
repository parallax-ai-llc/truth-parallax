---
title: "Laws of Adoption"
scripture: "iroquois-great-law"
chapter: 4
---

**1** When any person or family belonging to the Five Nations desires to abandon their nation and join another of the Five Nations, they shall be received in full membership.

**2** Any member of the Five Nations who through esteem or other feelings wishes to adopt an individual, a family, or a number of families may offer them adoption.

**3** When the adoption has been confirmed by the lords of the nation, the adopted shall enjoy every right and privilege as though born of the nation.

**4** If any person of foreign birth who has been adopted into the Five Nations shall commit any crime against the laws of the Great Peace, they shall be punished in the same manner as any native-born person.

**5** Should a nation of the Five Nations threaten to dissolve the Confederacy, the other nations shall use persuasion and reason to restore it. The Confederacy shall not be dissolved while there are those who wish to maintain it.
