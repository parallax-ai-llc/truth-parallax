---
title: "The Clans and the Council Fire"
scripture: "iroquois-great-law"
chapter: 3
---

**1** The Council of the Mohawk shall be divided into three parties: Tortoise, Wolf, and Bear. Each clan shall choose its representatives.

**2** The women of every clan of the Five Nations shall be the owners of the land. The soil and all its resources belong to the women.

**3** In every clan, the women shall hold the office of title-holder, that is, the right to nominate the lords of the Confederacy. If a lord fails in his duty, the women of his clan shall warn him three times. If he does not amend, they shall remove him.

**4** The lineal descent of the people of the Five Nations shall run in the female line. Women shall be considered the progenitors of the nation. They shall own the land and the soil.

**5** The Council Fire of the Confederacy shall burn at Onondaga, the central nation. Adodarho shall be the fire-keeper.

**6** When matters of great importance come before the council, the Mohawk lords shall first deliberate, then the Seneca lords, then the matter shall be referred to the Oneida and Cayuga lords, and finally Adodarho and the Onondaga lords shall confirm or reject the decision.
