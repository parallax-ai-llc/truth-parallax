---
title: "Laws of the Nations"
scripture: "iroquois-great-law"
chapter: 11
---

**1** Each nation shall maintain its own council fire and its own customs, provided they do not conflict with the Great Peace.

**2** The internal affairs of each nation are the concern of that nation alone. No other nation shall interfere in the domestic affairs of another.

**3** But in matters that affect the whole Confederacy, the Confederate Council shall have jurisdiction. The decisions of the Council in such matters shall be binding upon all.

**4** If a dispute arises between two nations that they cannot settle among themselves, they shall submit the matter to the Confederate Council for resolution.

**5** The territory of each nation is sacred. No nation shall encroach upon the territory of another without consent.
