---
title: "The Perpetual Nature of the Great Peace"
scripture: "iroquois-great-law"
chapter: 12
---

**1** The Great Peace shall endure as long as the grass grows, the rivers flow, and the sun shines.

**2** If the Confederacy should ever be broken apart by external force or internal discord, those who remain faithful to the principles of the Great Peace shall gather together and re-establish it.

**3** The laws of the Great Peace are not to be altered except by the unanimous consent of the Confederate Council, including all fifty lords.

**4** These laws are given for all time. They are not the words of men alone, but of the Peacemaker, who was sent by the Creator to bring an end to war and bloodshed among the nations.

**5** Let the future generations know that the Five Nations came together in peace, put aside their weapons, and pledged to live as one family under the Great Tree of Peace.

**6** This is the Great Law of Peace, the Kaianerekowa, given by Dekanawida the Peacemaker, confirmed by the fifty lords of the Confederacy, and established for the welfare of all people for all time to come.
