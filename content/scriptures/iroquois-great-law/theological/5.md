This section establishes the organizational structure of the Haudenosaunee Confederacy. The Five Nations (Mohawk, Oneida, Onondaga, Cayuga, and Seneca) are arranged in a specific seating pattern at the Grand Council, with the Mohawk and Seneca as Elder Brothers, the Oneida and Cayuga as Younger Brothers, and the Onondaga as Firekeepers. The clan system and moiety structure provide the basis for representation and decision-making.
