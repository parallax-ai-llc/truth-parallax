The final section affirms the eternal and unalterable nature of the Great Law. It establishes that the principles of peace, righteousness, and power (the three pillars of the Great Law) shall endure as long as the earth exists. The section includes provisions for the renewal and transmission of the law across generations, ensuring that the Confederacy's foundational principles remain a living guide for the Haudenosaunee people.
