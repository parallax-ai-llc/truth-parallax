This section addresses the procedures for adopting individuals and entire nations into the Haudenosaunee Confederacy. Nations seeking shelter under the Tree of Peace may be admitted with full rights and representation. Individuals from other peoples may be adopted into clans. This section reflects the Confederacy's expansive vision of peace and its openness to incorporating others into its framework of governance.
