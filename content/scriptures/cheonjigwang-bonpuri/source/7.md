---
title: "Cheonjigwang Bonpuri Section 7"
scripture: "cheonjigwang-bonpuri"
chapter: 7
---

**1** And so the world was set in order. Sobyeol ruled the heavens above, governing the sun and moon and stars. Daebyeol ruled the earth below, governing the affairs of human beings.

**2** The boundaries between heaven and earth, between the living and the dead, between good and evil, were established and maintained.

**3** But Daebyeol knew that evil could not be destroyed entirely. He said: As long as human beings have desires and fears, evil spirits will find a way to enter the world.

**4** Therefore he established the mudang, the shamans, who could communicate with the spirit world. He gave them the power to drive out evil spirits and to heal the sick.

**5** He said: When evil spirits trouble the people, call upon the shamans. They will perform the rites and restore the balance between the worlds.

**6** And Daebyeol taught the shamans the sacred songs and dances, the rituals and prayers, the offerings and invocations. He gave them the knowledge of the spirit world.

**7** And then Daebyeol ascended to a place between heaven and earth, where he watches over the human world. When the people are in distress, they call upon him, and he sends aid. This is the origin of the Cheonjigwang, the King of Heaven and Earth, who guards the boundary between the worlds.
