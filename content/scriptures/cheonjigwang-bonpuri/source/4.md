---
title: "Cheonjigwang Bonpuri Section 4"
scripture: "cheonjigwang-bonpuri"
chapter: 4
---

**1** Daebyeol and Sobyeol set out to determine who would rule the heavens and who would rule the earth. They agreed to a contest.

**2** The first contest was the growing of flowers. Each brother planted a seed and tended it. Whoever grew the more beautiful flower would rule the heavens.

**3** Daebyeol planted his seed and a magnificent flower bloomed, with petals of gold and silver. Sobyeol planted his seed, but his flower was small and ordinary.

**4** But Sobyeol was cunning. While Daebyeol slept, he switched the flowers, placing his own small flower before Daebyeol and taking the magnificent flower for himself.

**5** When the judges came, they saw the magnificent flower before Sobyeol and declared him the winner. Sobyeol was given the right to rule the heavens.

**6** Daebyeol knew what had happened, but he accepted the result without complaint. He said: I will rule the earth. The earth needs a strong ruler, for it is full of evil spirits and wickedness.

**7** And so Sobyeol ascended to heaven and became the ruler of the celestial realm, while Daebyeol remained on earth to bring order to the human world.
