---
title: "Cheonjigwang Bonpuri Section 6"
scripture: "cheonjigwang-bonpuri"
chapter: 6
---

**1** But there remained in the world a great evil spirit, the most powerful of all. His name was Nokdisaengin, the Green Forest Spirit, and he had terrorized the people for generations.

**2** Nokdisaengin could change his shape at will. He could become a tiger, a serpent, or a beautiful woman. He deceived the people and led them to destruction.

**3** Daebyeol tracked Nokdisaengin to his lair deep in the mountains. There they fought a great battle that lasted seven days and seven nights.

**4** The mountains shook and the rivers changed their courses. The sky darkened and thunder rolled across the land.

**5** At last, Daebyeol prevailed. He bound Nokdisaengin with chains forged from heavenly iron and cast him into the deepest pit beneath the earth.

**6** And Daebyeol placed a great stone over the pit, and he inscribed upon it sacred words of binding, so that Nokdisaengin could never escape.

**7** With the great evil spirit defeated, peace came to the earth. The people lived without fear, and the crops grew abundantly.
