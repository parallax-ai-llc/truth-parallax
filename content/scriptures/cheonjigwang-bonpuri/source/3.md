---
title: "Cheonjigwang Bonpuri Section 3"
scripture: "cheonjigwang-bonpuri"
chapter: 3
---

**1** In those days, there lived a poor woman named Baekju. She had no husband and lived alone in a small hut at the edge of the village.

**2** One night, a heavenly being descended to her hut. He was clothed in radiance and his face shone like the sun.

**3** He said to her: Baekju, you have been chosen by heaven. You shall bear two sons who will become the rulers of heaven and earth.

**4** And in time, Baekju gave birth to two sons. The elder was named Daebyeol, the Great Star, and the younger was named Sobyeol, the Small Star.

**5** The two brothers grew up strong and wise. They could speak with animals and command the winds. They knew the secrets of heaven and earth.

**6** But the world was in chaos. Evil spirits roamed the earth freely. They tormented the people, stealing their food, destroying their homes, and leading them astray.

**7** And Baekju said to her sons: You were born for a purpose. Go forth and bring order to the world. Drive out the evil spirits and establish the rule of heaven on earth.
