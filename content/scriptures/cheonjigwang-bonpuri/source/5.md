---
title: "Cheonjigwang Bonpuri Section 5"
scripture: "cheonjigwang-bonpuri"
chapter: 5
---

**1** Daebyeol, the ruler of the earth, set about his task. He traveled to every corner of the land, seeking out the evil spirits.

**2** He found spirits that had taken the form of humans. They lived among the people, causing strife and discord. They whispered lies and stirred up hatred.

**3** Daebyeol confronted these spirits and drove them out. He sealed the entrances to the underworld so that the spirits could not return.

**4** He established laws for the people. He taught them to distinguish right from wrong, truth from falsehood, good from evil.

**5** He said: In this world, the living and the dead must be separated. The world of the living is here, above the ground. The world of the dead is below.

**6** Let the living care for the living, and let the dead rest in peace. Let offerings be made to the ancestors, but let the dead not trouble the living.

**7** And Daebyeol set guardians at the boundaries between the world of the living and the world of the dead, so that order might be maintained.
