---
title: "The Chinese Campaign"
scripture: "gesar-epic"
chapter: 9
---

**1** The emperor of China sent a great army against Ling, seeking to conquer the land and subjugate its people.

**2** Gesar rode to meet the Chinese army. Before the battle, he sent a message to the emperor: We seek not war but peace. Let us live as neighbors, each respecting the other's land.

**3** But the emperor would not listen. The Chinese army was vast, outnumbering Gesar's forces ten to one.

**4** Gesar used strategy and divine power. He caused fog to cover the battlefield, so that the Chinese soldiers could not see. He sent magical illusions that terrified the enemy horses.

**5** In the confusion, Gesar's warriors struck. They defeated the Chinese army decisively, but Gesar ordered that no prisoners be harmed and that the wounded be treated with compassion.

**6** The emperor made peace, and the two nations agreed to respect each other's borders. Gesar said: A true warrior fights only when necessary and shows mercy when the battle is won.
