---
title: "The Descent to the Underworld"
scripture: "gesar-epic"
chapter: 7
---

**1** Gesar learned that the souls of many righteous people had been trapped in the underworld by the King of the Dead, who had been corrupted by demonic influence.

**2** Gesar descended to the underworld. The journey was through caverns of darkness and rivers of fire. The spirits of the dead wailed around him.

**3** In the underworld, Gesar confronted the corrupted King of the Dead and defeated him. He restored the proper order of the afterlife, so that the righteous would be rewarded and the wicked punished.

**4** He freed the trapped souls and guided them to their proper destinations. Some were reborn in better circumstances. Some ascended to the heavenly realms.

**5** When Gesar returned from the underworld, he had been gone for many years, though it seemed to him like only days. The world above had changed in his absence.

**6** Gesar said: Even death must bow to justice. The Dharma extends to all realms, the heavenly, the earthly, and the infernal. No being, living or dead, is beyond the reach of righteousness.
