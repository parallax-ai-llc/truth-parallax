---
title: "The Departure of Gesar"
scripture: "gesar-epic"
chapter: 12
---

**1** Gesar called his warriors and his people together one last time. He said: My work on earth is nearly done. The demons have been defeated. The Dharma has been established. It is time for me to return to heaven.

**2** The people wept and begged him to stay. But Gesar said: I was sent to earth for a purpose, and that purpose is fulfilled. But I leave you the Dharma, which is greater than any king.

**3** He said: Remember my teachings. Protect the weak. Punish the wicked. Honor the Dharma. If you do these things, I will always be with you.

**4** He said: If ever the demons return and the world is again in darkness, I will come again. For the battle between good and evil is never truly over. It continues in every generation.

**5** Then Gesar mounted his magical horse and rode into the sky. The people watched as he ascended, growing smaller and smaller, until he vanished among the clouds.

**6** But the people of Tibet say that Gesar did not truly leave. He waits in Shambhala, the hidden kingdom, preparing for the day when he will return to save the world once more. And they sing his songs and tell his stories, keeping his memory alive until that day comes.
