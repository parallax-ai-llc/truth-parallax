---
title: "The War Against the Eastern Demons"
scripture: "gesar-epic"
chapter: 6
---

**1** The demon king of the east, Sadan, was the most cunning of all the demons. He did not fight with armies alone but with deception, poison, and dark magic.

**2** Sadan sent enchantresses to Ling to seduce Gesar's warriors and weaken their resolve. He spread rumors and lies to divide the people. He poisoned the wells and the crops.

**3** Gesar saw through every deception. He said: The most dangerous weapon of evil is not the sword but the lie. Against lies, the best defense is truth.

**4** Gesar marched east with his army. Sadan retreated before them, drawing them deeper and deeper into his territory, setting traps at every turn.

**5** At last, in a great battle at the Iron Fortress, Gesar confronted Sadan. The demon used every dark art at his command, but Gesar's divine power was greater.

**6** Sadan was destroyed, and his kingdom was liberated. The people of the east were freed from the tyranny of demons and restored to the practice of the Dharma.
