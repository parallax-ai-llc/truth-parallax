---
title: "The Wisdom of Gesar"
scripture: "gesar-epic"
chapter: 10
---

**1** In the times of peace, Gesar taught his people the ways of righteousness. He said: A kingdom is not measured by its wealth or its armies, but by the happiness of its people.

**2** He established laws that protected the weak and punished the strong who abused their power. He said: The law must be the same for all, from the king to the beggar.

**3** He promoted the Dharma throughout his kingdom. He built temples and monasteries, invited teachers and scholars, and encouraged his people to practice compassion and wisdom.

**4** He said: The sword conquers the enemy without. The Dharma conquers the enemy within. The enemy within is more dangerous than any demon, for it is our own ignorance, hatred, and greed.

**5** He taught his warriors: True courage is not the absence of fear. True courage is doing what is right despite your fear. The greatest warrior is the one who conquers himself.

**6** And the people of Ling prospered under Gesar's rule, and the land was at peace, and the Dharma flourished.
