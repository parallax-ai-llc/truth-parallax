---
title: "The War Against the Southern Demons"
scripture: "gesar-epic"
chapter: 5
---

**1** While Gesar was in the north, the demon king of the south, Shingti, invaded the land of Ling. He captured Drugmo, Gesar's queen, and carried her away to his fortress.

**2** When Gesar learned of this, his wrath was terrible. He rode south with a small band of his bravest warriors, leaving the main army behind.

**3** The journey to the south was through burning deserts and poisonous jungles. Many of Gesar's companions fell ill, but Gesar healed them with his divine power.

**4** Gesar used cunning as well as strength. He disguised himself as a wandering monk and entered Shingti's fortress. He found Drugmo imprisoned in a high tower.

**5** When Shingti discovered the deception, the battle was fierce. Gesar fought the demon king and his entire army single-handedly, for his companions were too few.

**6** At last Shingti was slain, and Drugmo was rescued. Gesar said: The demons threaten from every direction. As long as evil exists, the warrior of righteousness must never rest.
