---
title: "The Birth of Gesar"
scripture: "gesar-epic"
chapter: 2
---

**1** In the land of Ling, there lived a poor man named Singlen. He was despised by his people and driven to the edges of the settlement.

**2** His wife conceived and gave birth to a strange child. The child was ugly and wild, with tangled hair and dirty skin. The people of Ling laughed at him and called him Joru, the worthless one.

**3** But the child had extraordinary powers. He could speak from birth. He could command animals and make stones move. The demons trembled at his presence, though they did not know why.

**4** Joru grew up in poverty and hardship. His uncle, Khrothung, was his chief enemy. Khrothung was jealous and fearful of the boy, and he plotted to destroy him.

**5** But the gods watched over Joru. Every plot against him failed. Every trap set for him was turned against his enemies. The boy grew stronger with each passing year.

**6** When Joru was twelve years old, the time came for the great horse race that would determine the king of Ling. Whoever won the race would rule the kingdom and claim the beautiful Drugmo as his bride.
