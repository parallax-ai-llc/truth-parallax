---
title: "The War Against the Northern Demons"
scripture: "gesar-epic"
chapter: 4
---

**1** The demon king of the north, Lutzen, had conquered many lands. He enslaved the people and defiled the sacred places. His army of demons was vast and terrible.

**2** Gesar gathered his warriors and rode north. The journey was long and perilous. The mountains were high, the rivers were deep, and the demons set traps and ambushes along the way.

**3** Gesar used his divine powers to overcome every obstacle. He sang magical songs that shattered the demons' spells. He wielded his heavenly sword, which could cut through any armor.

**4** At last he reached the fortress of Lutzen. The demon king came forth to meet him in single combat. They fought for three days and three nights.

**5** Gesar struck the final blow, and Lutzen fell. But before the demon died, his soul tried to escape. Gesar caught it and bound it with sacred mantras, so that it could never be reborn as a demon again.

**6** The people of the north were freed. They praised Gesar and offered him their allegiance. But Gesar said: I do not seek your lands. I seek only to free you from tyranny. Govern yourselves in justice, and honor the Dharma.
