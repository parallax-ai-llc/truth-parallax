---
title: "The Betrayal"
scripture: "gesar-epic"
chapter: 8
---

**1** While Gesar was in the underworld, Khrothung, his treacherous uncle, had seized power in Ling. He had told the people that Gesar was dead and would never return.

**2** Khrothung ruled as a tyrant. He taxed the people heavily, ignored the Dharma, and allied himself with the remaining demons.

**3** When Gesar returned, Khrothung refused to yield power. He gathered his allies and prepared for war against his own nephew.

**4** But the people of Ling remembered Gesar's justice. They rallied to his side, and Khrothung's allies deserted him one by one.

**5** Gesar said to Khrothung: I will not kill you, for you are my uncle, and the blood of kinsmen must not be shed. But you must leave Ling forever, and never return.

**6** Khrothung departed in shame. And Gesar restored the Dharma in Ling and ruled with justice and compassion. But he knew that his work was not yet done, for demons still lurked in the corners of the world.
