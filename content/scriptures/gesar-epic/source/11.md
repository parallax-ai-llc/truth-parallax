---
title: "The Final Battles"
scripture: "gesar-epic"
chapter: 11
---

**1** In Gesar's old age, the demons made one last effort to destroy the world. They gathered from all directions, led by the most powerful demon lords who had survived Gesar's campaigns.

**2** The demon army was vast, greater than any force Gesar had faced before. They marched upon Ling from every direction, determined to wipe out Gesar and his people.

**3** Gesar, though old in body, was undiminished in spirit and divine power. He rallied his warriors one last time and rode forth to meet the enemy.

**4** The final battle lasted seven days and seven nights. The earth shook and the sky darkened. Mountains crumbled and rivers changed their courses.

**5** At last, with a great cry, Gesar struck down the last of the demon lords. The demon armies scattered and fled. The world was cleansed of their evil.

**6** But Gesar was weary. He had fought for a lifetime, and the burden of the world lay heavy upon his shoulders. He knew that his time on earth was nearing its end.
