---
title: "The Great Horse Race"
scripture: "gesar-epic"
chapter: 3
---

**1** The chiefs of Ling gathered for the horse race. Khrothung was confident of victory. He had the finest horse and the most experienced riders.

**2** But Joru appeared on a wild horse that no one had seen before. It was his heavenly horse, disguised as a common pony. The people laughed at the sight.

**3** When the race began, Joru's horse transformed. It grew wings of flame and ran like the wind. It leaped over mountains and flew across rivers.

**4** Joru won the race, and as he crossed the finish line, his form changed. The ugly, dirty boy became a magnificent warrior, shining with divine light. He was no longer Joru the worthless one. He was Gesar, the Lion King.

**5** The people of Ling fell to their knees in awe. Even Khrothung was forced to acknowledge him. Gesar was crowned king of Ling, and Drugmo became his queen.

**6** And Gesar said to his people: I have come from heaven to serve you. I am your king, but I am also your servant. My task is to destroy the demons that torment the world and to establish the reign of justice.
