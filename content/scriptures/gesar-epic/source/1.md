---
title: "The Heavenly Origins"
scripture: "gesar-epic"
chapter: 1
---

**1** In the heavenly realm of the gods, the great assembly gathered. The world below was in chaos. Demons and monsters tormented the people. The strong preyed upon the weak, and the Dharma was forgotten.

**2** The gods deliberated: Who shall descend to the world of humans to destroy the demons and restore the Dharma? Who among us is brave enough, strong enough, wise enough?

**3** The youngest son of the great god was chosen. He was called Thubpa Gawa in heaven. He would descend to earth, be born as a human child, and become the greatest warrior king the world had ever known.

**4** Before his descent, the gods gave him weapons and armor forged in heaven. They gave him a magical horse that could fly through the air and run upon the water. They gave him companions who would be born on earth as his warriors.

**5** And the young god said: I will descend. I will be born among the poorest of the poor. I will endure hardship and suffering before I claim my kingdom. For a king who has never known suffering cannot understand his people.
