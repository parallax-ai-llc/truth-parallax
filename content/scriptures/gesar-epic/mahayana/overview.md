## Overview

Mahayana Buddhist engagement with the Epic of King Gesar brings distinctive interpretive methods and concerns to this foundational text. This approach illuminates dimensions of the text that may be less visible from other perspectives.

## Key Interpretive Approaches

### Bodhisattva Ideal
Mahayana interpreters read the Epic of King Gesar through the lens of the bodhisattva path — the aspiration to attain enlightenment for the benefit of all sentient beings.

### Skillful Means (Upaya)
The Epic of King Gesar's narrative strategies are understood as examples of skillful means — the adaptation of teaching methods to the needs and capacities of different audiences.

### Compassion and Wisdom
Mahayana reading emphasizes the twin virtues of compassion (*karuna*) and wisdom (*prajna*) as they manifest in the Epic of King Gesar's characters and teachings.
