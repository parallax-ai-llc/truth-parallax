The Sunni reading of Sahih Muslim, Chapter 3 (Zakat, Fasting, and Hajj) engages with the obligatory acts of worship that structure the Muslim year and life cycle. Through the lens of legal and theological authority, this section's 26 traditions are examined with attention to jurisprudential derivation and creedal affirmation. These traditions cover the regulations, spiritual significance, and practical details of communal obligations that bind the Muslim community together across time and geography.

In this chapter, the interplay between specific prophetic directives and broader ethical principles provides rich material for orthodox Sunni scholarship.
