---
title: "Sahih Muslim - Chapter 6"
scripture: "hadith-muslim"
chapter: 6
---

**1** It is narrated on the authority of Abu Hurairah that the Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "Whoever performs Hajj and does not commit any obscenity or transgression will return like the day his mother bore him."

**2** It is narrated on the authority of Ibn Abbas that the Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "An Umrah in Ramadan is equivalent to a Hajj," or he said, "a Hajj with me."

**3** It is narrated on the authority of Aisha that she said: "O Messenger of Allah, we consider jihad as the best deed. Should we not then go for jihad?" He said: "The best jihad for you women is an accepted Hajj."

**4** It is narrated on the authority of Abu Hurairah that the Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, was asked: "Which deed is best?" He said: "Belief in Allah and His Messenger." He was asked: "Then what?" He said: "Jihad in the cause of Allah." He was asked: "Then what?" He said: "An accepted Hajj."

**5** It is narrated on the authority of Abu Hurairah that the Prophet, peace be upon him, said: "Whoever comes to this House and does not commit any obscenity or transgression will return as on the day his mother bore him."

**6** It is narrated on the authority of Jabir that the Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "The water of Zamzam is for whatever it is drunk for."

**7** It is narrated on the authority of Ibn Abbas that the Prophet, peace be upon him, said: "The Black Stone descended from Paradise, and it was whiter than milk, but the sins of the children of Adam turned it black."

**8** It is narrated on the authority of Aisha that she asked the Prophet, peace be upon him: "O Messenger of Allah, should we not build a structure over the Station of Ibrahim?" He said: "Were it not that your people are close to the pre-Islamic period of ignorance, I would have demolished the Ka'bah and rebuilt it with two doors, one through which people would enter and another through which they would exit."
