---
title: "Sahih Muslim - Chapter 2"
scripture: "hadith-muslim"
chapter: 2
---

**1** It is narrated on the authority of Abu Hurairah that the Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "Purity is half of faith. Alhamdulillah fills the scale. SubhanAllah and Alhamdulillah fill what is between the heavens and the earth."

**2** It is narrated on the authority of Uthman ibn Affan that the Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "Whoever performs ablution and does it well, his sins will depart from his body, even from beneath his nails."

**3** It is narrated on the authority of Abu Malik al-Ash'ari that the Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "Prayer is light, charity is proof, patience is illumination, and the Quran is an argument for or against you. Every person goes out in the morning and sells himself, either freeing himself or destroying himself."

**4** It is narrated on the authority of Abu Hurairah that the Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "When a Muslim servant performs ablution and washes his face, every sin he committed with his eyes is washed away with the water, or with the last drop of water. When he washes his hands, every sin committed by his hands is washed away. When he washes his feet, every sin toward which his feet walked is washed away, until he emerges cleansed of sins."

**5** It is narrated on the authority of Uqbah ibn Amir that the Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "No Muslim performs ablution and does it well, then stands and prays two units of prayer, giving them his full attention and devotion, except that Paradise becomes his due."

**6** It is narrated on the authority of Abu Hurairah that the Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "Shall I not tell you something by which Allah erases sins and raises ranks? Performing ablution thoroughly despite difficulty, taking many steps to the mosque, and waiting for the next prayer after a prayer. That is the ribat, that is the ribat."

**7** It is narrated on the authority of Jabir that the Prophet, peace be upon him, said: "The key to Paradise is prayer, and the key to prayer is purification."

**8** It is narrated on the authority of Abu Hurairah that the Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "When any one of you wakes from sleep, let him not dip his hand into the vessel until he has washed it three times, for he does not know where his hand has been during the night."
