---
title: "Acharanga Sutra - Chapter 16"
scripture: "acharanga-sutra"
chapter: 16
---

**1** Mahavira said: This is my final teaching. Let all who seek liberation hear it and take it to heart.

**2** The soul is your own. No one else can liberate it for you. No god, no guru, no scripture can do what you must do for yourself. You are your own friend and your own enemy.

**3** If you conquer yourself, you conquer the world. If you are conquered by yourself, you are conquered by the world. The battle is within.

**4** The weapons of this inner battle are right faith, right knowledge, and right conduct. With these three, no enemy can prevail against you.

**5** Be vigilant at all times. Watch your thoughts, your words, your deeds. Every moment is an opportunity to accumulate merit or to accumulate sin.

**6** Practise non-violence with your whole being. Let your thoughts be thoughts of compassion, your words be words of kindness, your deeds be deeds of service.

**7** Practise truthfulness with your whole being. Let your mind be free from deceit, your speech free from falsehood, your life free from hypocrisy.

**8** Practise non-attachment with your whole being. Let go of possessions, of desires, of the very sense of "I" and "mine." In letting go, you find everything.

**9** The path is difficult, but the goal is worth every sacrifice. The liberated soul enjoys a bliss that all the pleasures of all the worlds cannot equal.

**10** Thus spoke the venerable Mahavira, the last of the twenty-four Tirthankaras of this era. He who follows this teaching with sincerity and perseverance attains the supreme goal of liberation. Thus ends the Acharanga Sutra. Om, peace to all beings.
