## Overview

Madhva's Dvaita school reads the Subala Upanishad's teachings within the framework of the eternal distinction between God, souls, and the world. The text's themes of creation, dissolution, elements are understood as revealing aspects of Vishnu's supreme nature and the soul's dependent relationship to Him.

## Theological Emphases

For Dvaita, the Subala Upanishad's philosophical content supports the understanding that all Upanishadic teachings ultimately point to Vishnu's supremacy and the soul's need for divine grace. Its practical instructions serve as devotional disciplines that prepare the soul for receiving God's liberating grace.
