---
title: "Prashna Upanishad - Chapter 1"
scripture: "prashna-upanishad"
chapter: 1
---

**1** Sukesha Bharadvaja, and Shaibya Satyakama, and Sauryayani Gargya, and Kausalya Ashvalayana, and Bhargava Vaidarbhi, and Kabandhin Katyayana — these were devoted to Brahman, intent on Brahman, seeking the Highest Brahman. They came to the revered Pippalada with fuel in their hands, thinking that he would teach them all.

**2** That seer said to them: Dwell with me a year more, with austerity, chastity, and faith; then ask what questions you will. If I know, I will tell you all.

**3** Then Kabandhin Katyayana came near and asked: Master, from what indeed are these creatures born?

**4** To him he said: Prajapati, the Lord of creatures, was desirous of offspring. He performed austerity, and having performed austerity, he produced a pair: Matter and Life, thinking that they together would produce creatures for him in manifold ways.

**5** The sun is indeed Life; Matter is the moon. All this, the formed and the formless, is indeed Matter. Therefore form is indeed Matter. Now the sun, when it rises, enters the eastern quarter, and thereby embraces the living beings of the east in its rays. And when it illumines the south, the west, the north, the nadir, the zenith, and all the intermediate quarters, thereby it embraces all living beings in its rays.
