## Huang Bai (Yellow and White)

The arts of yellow and white refer to the transformation of base metals into gold and silver. These arts are closely related to the preparation of the golden elixir.

The principle underlying both is the same: transformation. Just as base metal can be transformed into gold through the application of the right reagents under the right conditions, so the base human body can be transformed into an immortal vehicle through the application of the right practices under the right conditions.

The preparation of gold: Take the proper minerals. Combine them in the correct proportions. Apply heat in the correct sequence. Maintain the correct temperature for the correct duration. If all conditions are met, the transformation occurs.

But the arts of yellow and white are not merely physical processes. They require the spiritual participation of the practitioner. The man whose mind is impure, whose intentions are selfish, whose practice is insincere -- he will not succeed, no matter how precisely he follows the physical procedures.

The ancient masters who achieved these transformations were men of great virtue and great patience. They did not seek wealth for its own sake but used the gold they produced to fund their further cultivation and to help others.

The Master says: Do not pursue the arts of yellow and white for profit. If your intention is profit, the arts will not work. The Way responds to sincerity, not to greed. If you approach these arts with a pure heart and a desire for genuine understanding, they may reveal their secrets to you.
