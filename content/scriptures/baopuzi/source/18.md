## Di Zhen (Terrestrial Perfected Beings)

The terrestrial perfected beings are those who have achieved a high degree of cultivation but remain in the world rather than ascending to heaven. They walk among ordinary people, often unrecognized.

Some of the terrestrial perfected serve as teachers, passing on the Way to sincere students. Some serve as healers, using their knowledge of qi and herbs to cure the sick. Some serve as protectors, using their spiritual power to ward off calamities.

The terrestrial perfected can be recognized by certain signs: They appear younger than their actual age. Their eyes are clear and bright. Their movements are graceful and effortless. They speak little but their words carry weight. They are calm in all circumstances, neither agitated by bad fortune nor elated by good.

But these signs can be imitated by clever charlatans. The surest way to identify a terrestrial perfected being is by his character: He is genuinely humble. He does not seek recognition. He does not accept payment for his teachings. He does not make extravagant claims.

The terrestrial perfected being lives simply. He eats little. He sleeps little. He requires little. His needs are few because his inner resources are vast.

The Master says: You may have met a perfected being without knowing it. They move through the world like fish through water -- present everywhere but visible only to those with eyes to see. If you cultivate sincerity and humility, they may reveal themselves to you.
