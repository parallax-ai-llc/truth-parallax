## Overview

The Academic study of the Testaments of the Twelve Patriarchs engages with the complex questions of date, provenance, and literary history that surround this work. The central scholarly debate concerns whether the Testaments are a Jewish composition with Christian interpolations or a Christian composition drawing on Jewish sources. Analysis of the Two Spirits doctrine and its parallels with the Dead Sea Scrolls, the eschatological passages, and the ethical framework has generated an extensive scholarly literature.
