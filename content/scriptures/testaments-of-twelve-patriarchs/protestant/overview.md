## Overview

The Protestant reading of the Testaments of the Twelve Patriarchs examines the work for its illumination of New Testament ethical teaching and eschatological expectation. The Two Spirits doctrine provides important background for Paul's language of the flesh and the Spirit (Galatians 5), while the virtue-and-vice catalogues parallel the ethical lists found in Paul's letters. The messianic expectations for figures from Levi and Judah illuminate early Christian Christological reflection.
