---

**1** The copy of the Testament of Levi, the third son of Jacob, concerning the priesthood and arrogance.

**2** And when he was gathered to his fathers, being a hundred and thirty-seven years old, he called his children together and began to recount to them all that had been revealed to him.

**3** Levi, your father, was born in Haran and came with his father to Shechem.

**4** And he was a youth of about twenty years when, with Simeon, he wrought vengeance on Hamor for their sister Dinah.

**5** And when he was feeding the flocks in Abel-Maul, the spirit of understanding of the Lord came upon him, and he saw a vision.

**6** And he saw a mountain, and the name of it was Aspis. And he was told: Levi, to thee have I given the blessings of the priesthood, until I come and sojourn in the midst of Israel.

**7** Then the angel brought him down to the earth, and gave him a shield and a sword, and said: Execute vengeance on Shechem because of Dinah, and I will be with thee, because the Lord hath sent me.

**8** And he destroyed the sons of Hamor at that time, as it is written in the tables of the fathers.

**9** And he said to his children: I have learned that at the end of the ages ye will transgress against the Lord, stretching out hands to wickedness against Him; and to all the Gentiles shall ye become a scorn.

**10** For our father Israel is pure from the transgressions of the chief priests who shall lay their hands upon the Saviour of the world.

**11** For as the heaven is purer in the Lord's sight than the earth, so also be ye, the lights of Israel, purer than all the Gentiles.

**12** But if ye be darkened through transgressions, what, therefore, will all the Gentiles do living in blindness?

**13** Yea, ye shall bring a curse upon our race, because the light of the law which was given for to lighten every man, this ye desire to destroy by teaching commandments contrary to the ordinances of God.

**14** The offerings of the Lord ye shall rob, and from His portion shall ye steal choice portions, eating them contemptuously with harlots.

**15** And out of covetousness ye shall teach the commandments of the Lord, wedded women shall ye pollute, and the virgins of Jerusalem shall ye defile; and with harlots and adulteresses shall ye be joined.

**16** The daughters of the Gentiles shall ye take to wife, purifying them with an unlawful purification; and your union shall be like unto Sodom and Gomorrah.
