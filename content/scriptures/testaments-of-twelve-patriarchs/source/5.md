**1** The copy of the Testament of Judah, the fourth son of Jacob, what things he spake to his sons before he died.

**2** They gathered themselves together, therefore, and came to him, and he said to them: Hearken, my children, to Judah your father.

**3** I was the fourth son born to my father Jacob; and Leah my mother named me Judah, saying, I give thanks to the Lord, because He hath given me a fourth son also.

**4** I was swift in my youth, and obedient to my father in everything.

**5** And I honoured my mother and my mother's sister.

**6** And it came to pass, when I became a man, that my father blessed me, saying, Thou shalt be a king, prospering in all things.

**7** And the Lord showed me favour in all my works both in the field and in the house.

**8** I know that the race of the kings shall be from me, and I feel shame for my deeds, because I have sinned against my father.

**9** For truly I say unto you, I was never a braggart in warlike deeds, but was a humble husbandman.

**10** And when I became a man, my father Jacob prayed for me, saying, Thou shalt be for a king, and shalt succeed in all things.

**11** And he showed me in visions a sceptre of my kingdom, and said unto me: Thou shalt reign, and thy seed shall increase, and the Lord shall give thee a kingdom.

**12** But keep thyself from harlotry and love of money; because for these things I was almost cut off from the priesthood.

**13** For I humbled myself before my father Jacob; and because of my shame for this sin, I mourned greatly.

**14** Beware, therefore, my children, of fornication and the love of money, and hearken to Judah your father.

**15** For these things withdraw you from the law of God, and blind the inclination of the soul, and teach arrogance, and suffer not a man to have compassion upon his neighbour.

**16** They rob his soul of all goodness, and oppress him with toils and troubles, and drive away sleep from him, and devour his flesh.

**17** And he hindereth the sacrifices of God; and he remembereth not the blessing of God, he hearkeneth not to a prophet when he speaketh, and resenteth the words of godliness.

**18** For he is a slave to two contrary passions, and cannot obey God, because they have blinded his soul, and he walketh in the day as in the night.
