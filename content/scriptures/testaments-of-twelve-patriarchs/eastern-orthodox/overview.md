## Overview

The Eastern Orthodox reading of the Testaments of the Twelve Patriarchs resonates deeply with the Orthodox ascetical tradition of spiritual combat against the passions (pathē). The text's analysis of specific vices — lust, envy, anger, hatred — and its prescription of opposing virtues parallels the teaching of the Desert Fathers and the Philokalia tradition. The Two Spirits doctrine connects to the Orthodox concept of nepsis (watchfulness) against demonic suggestion.
