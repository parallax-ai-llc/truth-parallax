## Overview

The Comparative reading of the Testaments of the Twelve Patriarchs examines the work's ethical dualism alongside parallel traditions. The Two Spirits doctrine invites comparison with Zoroastrian ethical dualism (Spenta Mainyu vs. Angra Mainyu), the yetzer ha-tov and yetzer ha-ra of rabbinic Judaism, and Buddhist teachings on wholesome and unwholesome mental factors. The virtue-and-vice structure parallels Greek philosophical ethics and later Christian catechetical tradition.
