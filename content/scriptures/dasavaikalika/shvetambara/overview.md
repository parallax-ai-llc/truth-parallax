## Overview

For the Shvetambara tradition, the Dasavaikalika is the foundational text of monastic training. As a Mula Sutra (root text), it is among the first texts studied by newly initiated monks and nuns. Its ten evening lectures provide a complete introduction to the monastic life in a format designed for systematic study.

## Key Interpretive Approaches

### Foundational Training

Shvetambara monastic institutions use the Dasavaikalika as the primary manual for novice training. Its clear, practical instructions cover every aspect of daily monastic life.

### Practical Guidance

The text's chapters on begging, walking, speaking, and accepting food provide specific, actionable guidance that monks and nuns apply in their daily practice.

### Spiritual Formation

Beyond rules, the Dasavaikalika is understood as a text of spiritual formation -- shaping not only the outward behavior but the inner disposition of the monastic practitioner.
