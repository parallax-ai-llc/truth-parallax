## The Noble Path

Comparative analysis of this teaching on the noble path of non-violence and self-restraint reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar discussions in Buddhist, Hindu, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Jain perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
