## The True Monk

Interfaith interpretation finds in this teaching on the qualities and conduct of the true monk resonances with the concerns of multiple religious traditions. The text addresses universal human questions about meaning, morality, and liberation that transcend sectarian boundaries and invite constructive dialogue.
