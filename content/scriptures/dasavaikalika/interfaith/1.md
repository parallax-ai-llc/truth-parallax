## The Noble Path

Interfaith interpretation finds in this teaching on the noble path of non-violence and self-restraint resonances with the concerns of multiple religious traditions. The text addresses universal human questions about meaning, morality, and liberation that transcend sectarian boundaries and invite constructive dialogue.
