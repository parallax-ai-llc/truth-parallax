## Carefulness in Walking

Interfaith interpretation finds in this teaching on mindful walking to avoid harming living beings resonances with the concerns of multiple religious traditions. The text addresses universal human questions about meaning, morality, and liberation that transcend sectarian boundaries and invite constructive dialogue.
