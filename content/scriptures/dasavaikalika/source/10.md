---
title: "Dasavaikalika - Chapter 10"
scripture: "dasavaikalika"
chapter: 10
---

**1** Thus ends the Dasavaikalika Sutra, the scripture of the ten chapters, spoken for the guidance of monks on the path of liberation.

**2** The essence of this teaching is simple: conquer yourself. Conquer the passions of anger, pride, deceit, and greed. Conquer the attachment to the body and the senses.

**3** Practise non-violence in thought, word, and deed. Extend your compassion to all beings, from the most complex to the most simple.

**4** Practise truthfulness in all your dealings. Let your words be true, kind, and beneficial. Let your life be an expression of truth.

**5** Practise non-attachment. Let go of possessions, of desires, of the sense of ownership. In letting go, you gain everything.

**6** Practise self-control. Master your senses, your speech, your mind. Be the ruler of your inner kingdom.

**7** Practise austerity. Endure hardship with equanimity. Use penance as a means of purifying the soul and shedding karma.

**8** Practise meditation. Withdraw from the world and contemplate the soul. In the silence of meditation, the truth reveals itself.

**9** The goal of all these practices is liberation — the state of infinite knowledge, infinite perception, infinite bliss, and infinite energy that is the soul's natural state.

**10** May all beings attain this supreme goal. May all beings be free from suffering. May all beings live in peace and harmony. Parasparopagraho Jivanam — souls render service to one another. Om, peace to all beings.
