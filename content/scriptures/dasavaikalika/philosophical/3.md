## Carefulness in Walking

Philosophical interpretation examines this teaching on mindful walking to avoid harming living beings as a contribution to systematic philosophy. The text raises fundamental questions about the nature of reality, knowledge, and ethics that remain relevant to contemporary philosophical discussion.
