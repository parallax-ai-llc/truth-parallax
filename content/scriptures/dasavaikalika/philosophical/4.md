## Carefulness in Speech

From a philosophical standpoint, this section on right speech, silence, and the avoidance of harmful words presents arguments and positions that engage with perennial questions in metaphysics, epistemology, and moral philosophy. The Jain approach offers distinctive solutions to problems debated across philosophical traditions.
