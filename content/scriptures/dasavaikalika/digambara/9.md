## The True Monk

The Digambara tradition reads this the qualities and conduct of the true monk with characteristic doctrinal strictness, emphasizing the necessity of complete renunciation and the precise application of Jain principles. Digambara interpreters stress that the qualities and conduct of the true monk must be understood within the framework of absolute non-attachment and rigorous ascetic practice that defines the Digambara path.
