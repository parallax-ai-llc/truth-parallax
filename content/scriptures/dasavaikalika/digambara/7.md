## The Three Controls

The Digambara tradition reads this controlling mind, speech, and body with characteristic doctrinal strictness, emphasizing the necessity of complete renunciation and the precise application of Jain principles. Digambara interpreters stress that controlling mind, speech, and body must be understood within the framework of absolute non-attachment and rigorous ascetic practice that defines the Digambara path.
