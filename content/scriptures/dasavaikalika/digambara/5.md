## Carefulness in Receiving

The Digambara tradition reads this proper acceptance of food and requisites with characteristic doctrinal strictness, emphasizing the necessity of complete renunciation and the precise application of Jain principles. Digambara interpreters stress that proper acceptance of food and requisites must be understood within the framework of absolute non-attachment and rigorous ascetic practice that defines the Digambara path.
