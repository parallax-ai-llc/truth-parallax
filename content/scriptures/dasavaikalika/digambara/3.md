## Carefulness in Walking

The Digambara tradition reads this mindful walking to avoid harming living beings with characteristic doctrinal strictness, emphasizing the necessity of complete renunciation and the precise application of Jain principles. Digambara interpreters stress that mindful walking to avoid harming living beings must be understood within the framework of absolute non-attachment and rigorous ascetic practice that defines the Digambara path.
