## The Noble Path

The Digambara tradition reads this the noble path of non-violence and self-restraint with characteristic doctrinal strictness, emphasizing the necessity of complete renunciation and the precise application of Jain principles. Digambara interpreters stress that the noble path of non-violence and self-restraint must be understood within the framework of absolute non-attachment and rigorous ascetic practice that defines the Digambara path.
