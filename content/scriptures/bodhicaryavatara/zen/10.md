Zen appreciation for Chapter 10's dedication of merit focuses on the selfless quality of the aspiration. The dedication of all accumulated merit for the benefit of all beings embodies the fundamental Zen teaching that practice is not for oneself. The chapter's sweeping vision — encompassing all beings in all realms — expresses in devotional language the same boundless compassion that Zen realizes through direct insight into the interconnectedness of all existence.

The chapter's closing aspiration represents a fitting culmination: having analyzed, practiced, and realized, the practitioner offers everything for the benefit of others, holding nothing back.
