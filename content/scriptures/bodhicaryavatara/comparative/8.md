Comparative study of Chapter 8's exchange of self and other places this radical practice alongside comparable teachings in other traditions. The Christian mystical tradition of kenosis (self-emptying), the Sufi practice of fana (annihilation of the ego in God), and Emmanuel Levinas's philosophy of radical responsibility for the Other all advocate a fundamental decentering of the self. Shantideva's formulation is distinctive in providing both a philosophical argument (the self-other distinction is merely conventional) and a practical meditation technique for realizing this truth.

The chapter represents one of the most powerful expressions of radical altruism in world religious literature.
