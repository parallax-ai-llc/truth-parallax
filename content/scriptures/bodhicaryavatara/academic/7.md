Scholarly study of Chapter 7 examines Shantideva's treatment of motivation and effort within the broader context of Buddhist soteriology. The chapter's analysis of the psychological obstacles to sustained practice has been compared with contemporary motivational psychology and self-regulation theory. Academic work by Barbra Clayton examines the chapter's three types of laziness as a sophisticated typology of motivational failure.

The chapter's use of military metaphors has been studied in the context of Indian literary conventions and compared with similar imagery in other religious traditions.
