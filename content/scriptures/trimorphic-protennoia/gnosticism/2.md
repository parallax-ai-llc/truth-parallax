The second descent of Protennoia as Speech represents a deeper and more articulate revelation than the first. Where the Voice was hidden and pervasive, Speech is directed and intelligible. Protennoia now reveals herself more clearly, speaking in recognizable forms and empowering the elect to begin their ascent from the material world. The archons become increasingly disturbed as they sense a power greater than their own operating within their domain.

In Sethian cosmology, this second descent corresponds to the giving of knowledge — not Torah, as in Jewish tradition, but the secret gnosis that reveals the true nature of the world and the divine origin of the soul. Protennoia as Speech makes the hidden truths available in a form that the spiritually mature can receive. She is the divine teacher who instructs the seeds of Seth in the mysteries of their origin and destiny.

The Gnostic community would have understood this chapter as a description of their own deepening understanding. The initial stirring of the Voice has become articulate knowledge. The Gnostic initiate now knows who they are, where they came from, and where they are going — the three fundamental questions of Gnostic self-knowledge as described by Clement of Alexandria.
