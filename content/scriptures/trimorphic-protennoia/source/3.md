---
title: "Trimorphic Protennoia — Third Descent: The Word of the First Thought"
scripture: "trimorphic-protennoia"
chapter: 3
---

**1** I am the Word who dwells in the ineffable Voice. I dwell in undefiled Light and a Thought revealed itself perceptibly through the great Speech of the Mother, although it is a male offspring that supports me as my foundation.

**2** And it exists from the beginning in the foundations of the All. But there is a Light that dwells hidden in Silence, and it was first to come forth. Whereas she alone exists as Silence, I alone am the Word, ineffable, unpolluted, immeasurable, inconceivable.

**3** It is a hidden Light, bearing a Fruit of life, pouring forth a living water from the invisible, unpolluted, immeasurable spring, that is, the unreproducible Voice of the glory of the Mother, the glory of the offspring of God.

**4** Now I have come the third time — I am the Word of the Father — I came in their likeness. And I wore everyone's garment, and I hid myself within them, and they did not know the one who empowers me.

**5** For I am ungraspable, together with my Seed. And it is I who sow in everyone the seed of the Voice, and I have put it on the Voice.

**6** It is I who put on Jesus. I bore him from the cursed wood, and established him in the dwelling places of his Father. And those who watch over their dwelling places did not recognize me.

**7** For I am unrestrainable, together with my seed. And my seed, which is mine, I shall place into the holy Light within the incomprehensible Silence.

**8** I am Protennoia, the Thought that dwells in the Light. I am the movement that dwells in the All, she in whom the All takes its stand.

**9** The First Thought, the Image of the Spirit, she who fills the All, dwelling in them all, striving in them all, breathing in them all, walking in them all, strengthening them all, giving them life.

**10** It is she who exists in everyone. Through her they all live. She is the comprehension of all.
