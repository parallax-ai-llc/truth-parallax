---
title: "Trimorphic Protennoia — Second Descent: The Speech of the First Thought"
scripture: "trimorphic-protennoia"
chapter: 2
---

**1** Now I have come the second time in the likeness of a female, and have spoken with them. And I shall tell them of the coming end of this age and teach them of the beginning of the coming age, the unalterable one.

**2** I am the Womb that gives shape to the All by giving birth to the Light that shines in splendor. I am the Aeon to come. I am the fulfillment of the All, that is, Meirothea, the glory of the Mother.

**3** I cast voiced Speech into the ears of those who know me. And I am inviting you into the exalted, perfect Light.

**4** Moreover when you enter into the Light, you will be glorified by those who give glory, and those who enthrone will enthrone you. You will accept robes from those who give robes, and the baptizers will baptize you, and you will become gloriously glorious, the way you first were when you were in the Light.

**5** I hid myself in everyone and revealed myself within them, and every mind seeking me longed for me, for it is I who gave shape to the All when it had no form. And I transformed their forms into other forms until the time when a form will be given to the All.

**6** It is through me that the Voice originated, and it is I who put the breath within my own. And I cast into them the eternally holy Spirit, and I ascended and entered my Light.

**7** I am their Father, and I shall tell you a mystery: it is I who bore them all. And I know the All, and I shall complete the All through my thought. For I am the knowledge of the Invisible One; I am the immeasurable, the irrefutable.

**8** I am the one who fashioned all these things. It is through me that the Voice originated, and it is I who put the breath within my own.

**9** And the eternal ones I fashioned, and I put in them three permanent powers: perception, knowledge, and understanding.
