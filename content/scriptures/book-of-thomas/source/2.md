---
title: "Book of Thomas Section 2"
scripture: "book-of-thomas"
chapter: 2
---

**1** The Savior answered and said: "If what is visible to you is obscure to you, how can you hear about what is not visible? If the deeds of the truth that are visible in the world are difficult for you to perform, how indeed, then, shall you perform those that pertain to the exalted height and to the pleroma, which are not visible?

**2** And how shall you be called 'doers'? In this respect you are apprentices, and have not yet received the height of perfection."

**3** Thomas answered and said to the Savior: "Tell us about these things that you say are not visible, but are hidden from us."

**4** The Savior said: "All bodies of men and of beasts are begotten by the intertwining of natures, being fashioned from fire and earth. And the fire within them moves and stirs, and the fire within the body consumes.

**5** Therefore the bodies change. But when the fire consumes the soul, it becomes changeable and is cast into this world and into that, through the agency of those who serve.

**6** Woe to you, for you are captives! Woe to you, for you are bound with fire! Woe to you, for you burn! Better is it for you to be servants than to be lords."
