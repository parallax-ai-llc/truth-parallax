---
title: "Book of Thomas Section 4"
scripture: "book-of-thomas"
chapter: 4
---

**1** The Savior said: "Blessed are you who have known the temptations and have fled from them. Blessed are you who are mocked and are not esteemed on account of the love that your Lord has for you.

**2** Blessed are you who weep and are oppressed by those who have no hope, for you will be released from every bondage.

**3** Watch and pray, for you possess the great light. The light shines upon you, those who do not persist in the shame and the passion and the wavering.

**4** Blessed will you be when you yourselves are persecuted. And you will find a place where you will not be persecuted.

**5** Blessed are you who were insulted, for you will be found in honor. Blessed are you who weep, for in the time to come you will laugh.

**6** Look, I have revealed to you, Thomas, those things that the blind cannot see, that are hidden from those unable to comprehend. Rejoice and be glad as sons of God. From this moment onward, meditate on the kingdom, for thus the Father wills."

**7** These are the secret words that the Savior spoke to Judas Thomas, which I, Mathaias, wrote down.
