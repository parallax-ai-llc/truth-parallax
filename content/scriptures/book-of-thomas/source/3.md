---
title: "Book of Thomas Section 3"
scripture: "book-of-thomas"
chapter: 3
---

**1** The Savior said: "Woe to you, godless ones, who have no hope, who rely on things that will not happen!

**2** Woe to you who hope in the flesh and in the prison that will perish! How long will you be oblivious? And how long will you suppose that the imperishables will also perish?

**3** Your hope is set upon the world, and your god is this life! You are corrupting your souls!

**4** Woe to you within the fire of the ruling archons! Woe to you within the grip of the turning fire! Woe to you for the burning! For the fire that is within you will consume you!

**5** Woe to you, for the wheel of the seven rulers turns within you! Woe to you, for the burning fire will consume you, and your enemies will not spare you!

**6** For you have abandoned the power of the knowledge and the whole perfection, and you have become a servant of the lack."

**7** Watch and pray that you do not come to be in the flesh, but rather that you come forth from the bondage of the passions and the beasts of this nature.
