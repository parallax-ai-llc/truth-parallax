---
title: "Messianic Rule - Chapter 1"
scripture: "messianic-rule"
chapter: 1
---

**1** This is the rule for all the congregation of Israel in the last days, when they shall join the community to walk according to the law of the sons of Zadok the priests and of the men of their covenant.

**2** They who have turned aside from the way of the people: these are the men of His council who keep His covenant in the midst of iniquity, who shall atone for the land.

**3** When they come, they shall assemble all the people together, women and children, and they shall read into their ears all the precepts of the covenant.

**4** And they shall instruct them in all their ordinances, that they may no longer stray in their errors.

**5** And this is the rule for all the hosts of the congregation, for every native-born in Israel:

**6** From his youth they shall educate him in the Book of Meditation. According to his age he shall be instructed in the precepts of the covenant, and he shall receive his education in their statutes.

**7** For ten years, from the time of his entry into the children's group, he shall be instructed in the foundations of the community.

**8** At the age of twenty years, he shall be enrolled among those who are mustered for the lot, to take his place in the midst of his family, and shall be joined to the holy congregation.

**9** He shall not approach a woman to know her by lying with her before he is fully twenty years old, when he shall know good and evil.

**10** And at the age of twenty-five years, he may take his place among the foundations of the holy congregation to work in the service of the congregation.

**11** And at the age of thirty years, he may approach to participate in lawsuits and judgments, and may take his place among the chiefs of the thousands of Israel, the chiefs of the hundreds, the chiefs of the fifties, and the chiefs of the tens.

**12** These are the judges and the officers according to their divisions, which are under the authority of the sons of Aaron the priests.

**13** And every head of a family in the congregation who is chosen to hold office, to go out and come in before the congregation, shall strengthen himself according to his understanding and the perfection of his way.

**14** And according to his strength, he shall gird his loins. If the lot falls to him to exercise authority, he shall walk before the people according to all these rules.

**15** And if he grows old, according to his strength they shall assign him service in the service of the congregation.
