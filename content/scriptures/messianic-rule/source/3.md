---
title: "Messianic Rule - Chapter 3"
scripture: "messianic-rule"
chapter: 3
---

**1** This is the rule of the congregation of Israel in the last days, a gathering of the community to walk according to the ordinance of the sons of Zadok:

**2** The congregation shall be governed by the rule of the Many. They shall eat together, bless together, and take counsel together.

**3** In every place where there are ten men of the council of the community, there shall not be absent from among them a priest. And they shall sit before him according to their rank, and they shall be asked for their counsel in all things.

**4** And when the table is prepared for eating, or the new wine for drinking, the priest shall stretch out his hand first to bless the first-fruits of the bread and of the new wine.

**5** And the man who is learned in the law shall study it day and night, continually, concerning the proper conduct of a man with his companion.

**6** And the Many shall keep watch together for a third of every night of the year, to read the Book and to study the law and to pray together.

**7** This is the rule for a session of the Many: each man shall sit in his place. The priests shall sit first, and the elders second, and all the rest of the people shall sit, each in his place.

**8** And they shall be asked for judgment, or for any counsel or matter coming before the Many, each in order of his registration.

**9** No man shall interrupt the speech of his fellow before his brother has finished speaking. And no man shall speak before the one who is registered before him.

**10** The man who is asked shall speak in his turn. And in the session of the Many, no man shall say anything which is not according to the pleasure of the Many.

**11** And if the overseer of the Many, or any man who has something to say to the Many but is not in the position of one who asks the counsel of the congregation —

**12** He shall stand on his feet and say: "I have something to say to the Many." If they bid him speak, he shall speak.

**13** And every man of Israel who volunteers to join the council of the community shall be examined by the overseer who is at the head of the Many concerning his understanding and his deeds.

**14** If he comprehends the teaching, he shall bring him into the covenant to turn to the truth and to turn away from all perversity. And he shall instruct him in all the precepts of the community.

**15** And afterwards, when he enters to stand before the Many, they shall all be questioned concerning his affairs. And as the lot determines according to the counsel of the Many, he shall approach or depart.

**16** These are the statutes by which the men of perfect holiness shall walk, each with his fellow, every one who enters the holy congregation, walking in perfect holiness as He commanded. And all who deviate from the way shall be punished according to their deeds.
