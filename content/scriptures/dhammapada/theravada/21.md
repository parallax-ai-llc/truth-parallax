The Pakinnakavagga's 16 miscellaneous verses gather teachings on diverse topics. The Theravada tradition values these for their practical wisdom on happiness, friendship, and daily conduct.

Theravada commentators provide individual narrative backgrounds for each verse, connecting them to events in the Buddha's life. The chapter's diversity illustrates the breadth of the Buddha's teaching across every dimension of human experience.
