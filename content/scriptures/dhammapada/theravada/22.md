The Nirayavagga's 14 verses on hell describe the consequences of unwholesome action. The Theravada tradition maintains a cosmology including multiple hell realms. The chapter warns against false speech, hypocrisy, and harmful conduct.

Theravada interpretation understands these descriptions as both literal realities and metaphors for psychological suffering from unwholesome mental states. The warnings motivate ethical conduct and mindful awareness of consequences.
