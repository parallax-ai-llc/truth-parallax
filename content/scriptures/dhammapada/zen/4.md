Zen approaches these 16 verses with emphasis on direct experience rather than conceptual analysis. The Flowers chapter's teachings are pointers toward a reality that must be personally verified through meditation and mindful engagement with daily life.

Zen masters might use these verses as starting points for dharma talks or informal koans, inviting students beyond the words to the living truth they indicate. The practical wisdom aligns with Zen's insistence that awakening is realized precisely in everyday experience.
