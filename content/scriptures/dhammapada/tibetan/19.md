Tibetan Buddhism situates these 17 verses within its comprehensive system of study and practice. The The Just chapter addresses specific stages of the graduated path (lamrim) and is integrated into systematic monastic training.

Tibetan commentators bring Madhyamaka and Abhidharma philosophy to bear on these verses, examining implications for karma, mental factors, and the path's stages. The practical teachings complement advanced philosophical and tantric practices.
