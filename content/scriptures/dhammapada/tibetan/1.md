Tibetan Buddhism reads these 20 Twin Verses through the graduated path (lamrim) framework. The opening declaration on the primacy of mind aligns with the Tibetan emphasis on motivation (kun slong) as the determining factor in moral quality.

Tibetan commentators such as Tsongkhapa situate these teachings within the initial scope of the lamrim, where understanding karma is essential. The chapter's clear presentation of mental cause and effect provides the foundation for advanced Madhyamaka analysis and tantric transformation.
