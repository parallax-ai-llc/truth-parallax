Tibetan Buddhist philosophy engages deeply with these 11 verses. The Madhyamaka analysis reveals that while mind is the source of both suffering and liberation, mind itself is empty of inherent existence.

In Tibetan tantric practice, teachings on mind gain additional significance through the concept of clear light mind (od gsal), the subtlest consciousness accessed in advanced meditation. The instruction to train the mind applies to progressively subtler levels of consciousness.
