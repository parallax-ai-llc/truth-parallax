**King Wen**

**1** King Wen is on high; he shines in heaven. Although Zhou was an old country, the appointment it has received from Heaven is new.

**2** Is not the dignity of Zhou illustrious? Is not the appointment of God timely? King Wen ascends and descends, on the left and the right of God.

**3** Full of earnestness was King Wen, and his fame is without end. The gifts of God to Zhou extend to the descendants of King Wen.

**The People Labor**

**4** The people indeed are heavily burdened, but perhaps a little ease may be got for them. Let us cherish this center of the kingdom, to secure the repose of the four quarters of it.

**5** We should not be too indulgent to the deceitful. We should repress the robbers and oppressors, who have no fear of the clear will of Heaven.

**6** We should show compassion to those who are far off, and help those who are near. We should secure the throne of our king.
