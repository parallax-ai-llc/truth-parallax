**Section III: Death in the Underworld**

**1** Naked and bowed low, Inanna entered the throne room.
**2** Ereshkigal rose from her throne.
**3** Inanna started toward the throne.

**4** The Anunnaki, the judges of the underworld, surrounded her.
**5** They passed judgment against her.

**6** Then Ereshkigal fastened on Inanna the eye of death.
**7** She spoke against her the word of wrath.
**8** She uttered against her the cry of guilt.

**9** She struck her.

**10** Inanna was turned into a corpse,
**11** a piece of rotting meat,
**12** and was hung from a hook on the wall.

**13** When, after three days and three nights, Inanna had not returned,
**14** Ninshubur, the faithful servant of Inanna,
**15** set up a lament for her by the ruins.

**16** She beat the drum for her in the assembly places.
**17** She circled the houses of the gods.
**18** She tore at her eyes, at her mouth, at her thighs.
**19** She dressed herself in a single garment like a beggar.

**20** Alone, she set out for Nippur and the temple of Enlil.
**21** When she entered the holy shrine,
**22** she cried out:

**23** "O Father Enlil, do not let your daughter
**24** be put to death in the underworld.
**25** Do not let your good metal
**26** be covered with the dust of the underworld.

**27** Do not let your good lapis lazuli
**28** be broken up into the stone of the stoneworker.
**29** Do not let your boxwood
**30** be cut up into the wood of the woodworker.

**31** Do not let the holy priestess of heaven
**32** be put to death in the underworld."

**33** Father Enlil answered angrily:
**34** "My daughter craved the Great Below.
**35** Inanna craved the Great Below.
**36** She who receives the me of the underworld does not return.
**37** She who goes to the Dark City stays there."

**38** Father Enlil would not help.

**39** Ninshubur went to Ur and the temple of Nanna.
**40** When she entered the holy shrine,
**41** she cried out:

**42** "O Father Nanna, do not let your daughter
**43** be put to death in the underworld."

**44** Father Nanna answered angrily:
**45** "My daughter craved the Great Below.
**46** Inanna craved the Great Below.
**47** She who receives the me of the underworld does not return.
**48** She who goes to the Dark City stays there."

**49** Father Nanna would not help.

**50** Ninshubur went to Eridu and the temple of Enki.
**51** When she entered the holy shrine,
**52** she cried out:

**53** "O Father Enki, do not let your daughter
**54** be put to death in the underworld.
**55** Do not let your good metal
**56** be covered with the dust of the underworld."

**57** Father Enki said:
**58** "What has happened?
**59** What has my daughter done!
**60** Inanna! Queen of All the Lands! Holy Priestess of Heaven!
**61** What has happened?"

**62** Father Enki was troubled.
**63** He was grieved.
