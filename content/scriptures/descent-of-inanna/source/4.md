**Section IV: The Rescue**

**1** From under his fingernail Father Enki brought forth dirt.
**2** He fashioned the dirt into a kurgarra, a creature neither male nor female.
**3** From under his other fingernail he brought forth dirt.
**4** He fashioned the dirt into a galatur, a creature neither male nor female.

**5** To the kurgarra he gave the food of life.
**6** To the galatur he gave the water of life.

**7** Enki spoke to the kurgarra and galatur, saying:
**8** "Go to the underworld,
**9** enter the door like flies.

**10** Ereshkigal, the Queen of the Underworld, is moaning
**11** with the cries of a woman about to give birth.
**12** No linen is spread over her body.
**13** Her breasts are uncovered.
**14** Her hair swirls about her head like leeks.

**15** When she cries, 'Oh! Oh! My inside!'
**16** cry out, 'Oh! Oh! Your inside!'
**17** When she cries, 'Oh! Oh! My outside!'
**18** cry out, 'Oh! Oh! Your outside!'

**19** The queen will be pleased.
**20** She will offer you a gift.
**21** Ask her only for the corpse that hangs from the hook on the wall.
**22** One of you will sprinkle the food of life on it.
**23** The other will sprinkle the water of life.
**24** Inanna will arise."

**25** The kurgarra and the galatur heeded Enki's words.
**26** They set out for the underworld.
**27** Like flies, they slipped through the cracks of the gates.

**28** They entered the throne room of the Queen of the Underworld.
**29** No linen was spread over her body.
**30** Her breasts were uncovered.
**31** Her hair swirled about her head like leeks.

**32** Ereshkigal was moaning:
**33** "Oh! Oh! My inside!"
**34** They moaned:
**35** "Oh! Oh! Your inside!"

**36** She moaned:
**37** "Oh! Oh! My outside!"
**38** They moaned:
**39** "Oh! Oh! Your outside!"

**40** She groaned:
**41** "Oh! Oh! My belly!"
**42** They groaned:
**43** "Oh! Oh! Your belly!"

**44** She groaned:
**45** "Oh! Oh! My back!"
**46** They groaned:
**47** "Oh! Oh! Your back!"

**48** She sighed:
**49** "Ah! Ah! My heart!"
**50** They sighed:
**51** "Ah! Ah! Your heart!"

**52** Ereshkigal stopped.
**53** She looked at them.
**54** She asked:
**55** "Who are you,
**56** moaning—Loss—Loss—with me?
**57** Groaning—groaning—with me?

**58** If you are gods, I will bless you.
**59** If you are mortals, I will give you a gift.
**60** I will give you the water-gift, the river in its fullness."

**61** The kurgarra and galatur answered:
**62** "We do not wish it."

**63** Ereshkigal said:
**64** "I will give you the grain-gift, the fields in their fullness."

**65** The kurgarra and galatur answered:
**66** "We do not wish it."

**67** "What, then, do you wish?"

**68** "We wish only the corpse that hangs from the hook on the wall."

**69** "The corpse belongs to Inanna."

**70** "Whether it belongs to our queen,
**71** whether it belongs to our king,
**72** give it to us."

**73** The corpse was given to them.

**74** The kurgarra sprinkled the food of life on the corpse.
**75** The galatur sprinkled the water of life on the corpse.
**76** Inanna arose.
