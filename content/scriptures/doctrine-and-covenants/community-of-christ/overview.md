## Overview

Community of Christ engagement with the Doctrine and Covenants brings distinctive interpretive methods and concerns to this sacred text. This tradition reads the text as authoritative scripture within its community of faith.

## Key Interpretive Approaches

### Continuing Revelation
Community of Christ interpretation emphasizes that God continues to speak to the church through its prophet-president, and that earlier revelations must be understood in light of ongoing divine guidance.

### Peace and Justice
The Doctrine and Covenants is read through the Community of Christ's emphasis on peace, justice, and the worth of all persons, with these values serving as interpretive lenses.

### Responsible Interpretation
Community of Christ scholars emphasize historical context, scholarly methods, and the community's discernment process in interpreting the Doctrine and Covenants.
