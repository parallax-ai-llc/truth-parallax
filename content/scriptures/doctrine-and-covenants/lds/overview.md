## Overview

Latter-day Saint Tradition engagement with the Doctrine and Covenants brings distinctive interpretive methods and concerns to this sacred text. This tradition reads the text as authoritative scripture within its community of faith.

## Key Interpretive Approaches

### Prophetic Authority
Latter-day Saint interpretation emphasizes the Doctrine and Covenants as divinely authorized scripture, received through prophetic channels and confirmed by the witness of the Holy Ghost.

### Restoration Framework
The Doctrine and Covenants is understood within the broader context of the Restoration — the re-establishment of Christ's original church, priesthood, and ordinances in the latter days.

### Living Scripture
LDS interpretation treats the Doctrine and Covenants as a living text, applicable to contemporary life and illuminated by continuing revelation through living prophets and apostles.
