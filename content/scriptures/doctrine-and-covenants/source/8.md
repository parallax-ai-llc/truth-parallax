---
title: "Section 19 - The Suffering of Christ"
scripture: "doctrine-and-covenants"
chapter: 8
---

**19:15** Therefore I command you to repent—repent, lest I smite you by the rod of my mouth, and by my wrath, and by my anger, and your sufferings be sore—how sore you know not, how exquisite you know not, yea, how hard to bear you know not.

**19:16** For behold, I, God, have suffered these things for all, that they might not suffer if they would repent.

**19:18** Which suffering caused myself, even God, the greatest of all, to tremble because of pain, and to bleed at every pore, and to suffer both body and spirit—and would that I might not drink the bitter cup, and shrink.

**19:23** Learn of me, and listen to my words; walk in the meekness of my Spirit, and you shall have peace in me.
