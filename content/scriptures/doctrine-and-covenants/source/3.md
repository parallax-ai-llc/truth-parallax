---
title: "Section 6 - Revelation to Oliver Cowdery"
scripture: "doctrine-and-covenants"
chapter: 3
---

**6:2** Behold, I am God; give heed to my word, which is quick and powerful, sharper than a two-edged sword, to the dividing asunder of both joints and marrow; therefore give heed unto my word.

**6:7** Seek not for riches but for wisdom, and behold, the mysteries of God shall be unfolded unto you, and then shall you be made rich. Behold, he that hath eternal life is rich.

**6:33** Fear not to do good, my sons, for whatsoever ye sow, that shall ye also reap; therefore, if ye sow good ye shall also reap good for your reward.

**6:36** Look unto me in every thought; doubt not, fear not.
