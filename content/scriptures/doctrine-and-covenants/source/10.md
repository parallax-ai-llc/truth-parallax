---
title: "Section 25 - Revelation to Emma Smith"
scripture: "doctrine-and-covenants"
chapter: 10
---

**25:3** Behold, thy sins are forgiven thee, and thou art an elect lady, whom I have called.

**25:5** And the office of thy calling shall be for a comfort unto my servant, Joseph Smith, Jun., thy husband, in his afflictions, with consoling words, in the spirit of meekness.

**25:10** And verily I say unto thee that thou shalt lay aside the things of this world, and seek for the things of a better.

**25:12** For my soul delighteth in the song of the heart; yea, the song of the righteous is a prayer unto me, and it shall be answered with a blessing upon their heads.
