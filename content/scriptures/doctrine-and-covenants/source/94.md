---
title: "Doctrine and Covenants Section 94"
scripture: "doctrine-and-covenants"
chapter: 94
---

**1** And again, verily I say unto you, it is my will that the city of the stake of Zion, and all the stake's round about in the land of Kirtland, should be built up and strengthened.

**2** Verily I say unto you, let the first lot on the south be consecrated unto me for the building of a house for the presidency, for the work of the presidency, in obtaining revelations; and for the work of the ministry of the presidency, in all things pertaining to the church and kingdom.

**3** Verily I say unto you, that it shall be built fifty-five by sixty-five feet in the width thereof and in the length thereof, in the inner court.

**4** And there shall be a lower court and a higher court, according to the pattern which shall be given unto you hereafter.

**5** And it shall be dedicated unto the Lord from the foundation thereof, according to the order of the priesthood, according to the pattern which shall be given unto you hereafter.

**6** And it shall be wholly dedicated unto the Lord for the work of the presidency.
