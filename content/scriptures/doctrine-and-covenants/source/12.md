---
title: "Section 42 - The Law of the Church"
scripture: "doctrine-and-covenants"
chapter: 12
---

**42:11** Again I say unto you, that it shall not be given to any one to go forth to preach my gospel, or to build up my church, except he be ordained by some one who has authority.

**42:22** Thou shalt love thy wife with all thy heart, and shalt cleave unto her and none else.

**42:29** If thou lovest me thou shalt serve me and keep all my commandments.

**42:30** And behold, thou wilt remember the poor, and consecrate of thy properties for their support.
