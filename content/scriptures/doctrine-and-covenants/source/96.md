---
title: "Doctrine and Covenants Section 96"
scripture: "doctrine-and-covenants"
chapter: 96
---

**1** For verily I say unto you, it is expedient in me that the first lot on the south be consecrated unto me for the building of a house of worship.

**2** And again, there are many things which I will make known unto you, inasmuch as you are faithful and seek me with all your hearts.

**3** And, verily I say unto you, my servant Newel K. Whitney should be appointed to keep the Lord's storehouse. And he shall receive a consecration of properties from the church in order to be the bishop of the church. And in this he shall do a great good, receiving consecrations and distributing them according to the commandments.
