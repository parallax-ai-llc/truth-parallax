---
title: "Section 29 - The Second Coming"
scripture: "doctrine-and-covenants"
chapter: 11
---

**29:7** And ye are called to bring to pass the gathering of mine elect; for mine elect hear my voice and harden not their hearts.

**29:9** For the hour is nigh and the day soon at hand when the earth is ripe; and all the proud and they that do wickedly shall be as stubble; and I will burn them up, saith the Lord of Hosts.

**29:11** For I will reveal myself from heaven with power and great glory, with all the hosts thereof, and dwell in righteousness with men on earth a thousand years, and the wicked shall not stand.
