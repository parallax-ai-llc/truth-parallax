---
title: "Section 93 - Intelligence and Light"
scripture: "doctrine-and-covenants"
chapter: 18
---

**93:2** And the day shall come when you shall comprehend even God, being quickened in him and by him.

**93:24** And truth is knowledge of things as they are, and as they were, and as they are to come.

**93:29** Man was also in the beginning with God. Intelligence, or the light of truth, was not created or made, neither indeed can be.

**93:33** For man is spirit. The elements are eternal, and spirit and element, inseparably connected, receive a fulness of joy.

**93:36** The glory of God is intelligence, or, in other words, light and truth.

**93:40** But I have commanded you to bring up your children in light and truth.
