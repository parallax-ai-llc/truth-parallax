---
title: "Section 14 - A Great and Marvelous Work"
scripture: "doctrine-and-covenants"
chapter: 6
---

**14:7** And, if you keep my commandments and endure to the end you shall have eternal life, which gift is the greatest of all the gifts of God.

**14:8** And it shall come to pass, that if you shall ask the Father in my name, in faith believing, you shall receive the Holy Ghost, which giveth utterance, that you may stand as a witness of the things of which you shall both hear and see.
