---
title: "Section 76 - The Vision of the Three Degrees of Glory"
scripture: "doctrine-and-covenants"
chapter: 14
---

**76:22** And now, after the many testimonies which have been given of him, this is the testimony, last of all, which we give of him: That he lives!

**76:23** For we saw him, even on the right hand of God; and we heard the voice bearing record that he is the Only Begotten of the Father.

**76:50** And again we bear record—for we saw and heard, and this is the testimony of the gospel of Christ concerning them who shall come forth in the resurrection of the just.

**76:89** And thus we saw, in the heavenly vision, the glory of the telestial, which surpasses all understanding.

**76:92** And thus we saw the glory of the terrestrial which excels in all things the glory of the telestial.

**76:96** And the glory of the celestial is one, even as the glory of the sun is one.
