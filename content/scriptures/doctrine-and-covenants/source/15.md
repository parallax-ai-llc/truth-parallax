---
title: "Section 84 - The Oath and Covenant of the Priesthood"
scripture: "doctrine-and-covenants"
chapter: 15
---

**84:33** For whoso is faithful unto the obtaining these two priesthoods of which I have spoken, and the magnifying their calling, are sanctified by the Spirit unto the renewing of their bodies.

**84:38** And he that receiveth my Father receiveth my Father's kingdom; therefore all that my Father hath shall be given unto him.

**84:85** Neither take ye thought beforehand what ye shall say; but treasure up in your minds continually the words of life, and it shall be given you in the very hour that portion that shall be meted unto every man.

**84:88** And whoso receiveth you, there I will be also, for I will go before your face. I will be on your right hand and on your left, and my Spirit shall be in your hearts, and mine angels round about you, to bear you up.
