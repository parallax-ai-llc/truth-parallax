**Hymn 25: To the Muses and Apollo**

**1** With the Muses let me begin, and with Apollo, and with Zeus. For it is through the Muses and far-shooting Apollo that there are singers upon the earth and players upon the lyre; but kings are from Zeus. Happy is he whom the Muses love: sweet flows speech from his lips.

**2** Hail, children of Zeus! Honour my song! And now I will remember you and another song also.
