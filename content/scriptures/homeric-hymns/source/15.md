**Hymn 15: To Heracles**

**1** I will sing of Heracles, the son of Zeus and mightiest of mortals on earth. Alcmena bare him in Thebes, the city of lovely dances, when the dark-clouded Son of Cronos had lain with her.

**2** He used to wander over unmeasured tracts of land and sea at the bidding of King Eurystheus, and himself did many deeds of violence and endured many; but now he lives happily in the glorious home of snowy Olympus, and has neat-ankled Hebe for his wife.

**3** Hail, lord, son of Zeus! Give me success and prosperity.
