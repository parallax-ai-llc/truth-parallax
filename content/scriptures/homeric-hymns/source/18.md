**Hymn 18: To Hermes II**

**1** I sing of Hermes of Cyllene, Slayer of Argus, lord of Cyllene and Arcadia rich in flocks, luck-bringing messenger of the deathless gods. He was born of Maia, the daughter of Atlas, when she had made with Zeus a secret union.

**2** Even so hail to you, Son of Zeus and Maia; with you I have begun: now I will turn to another song!
