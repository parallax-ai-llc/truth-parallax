**Hymn 20: To Hephaestus**

**1** Sing, clear-voiced Muse, of Hephaestus famed for inventions. With bright-eyed Athene he taught men glorious crafts throughout the world—men who before used to dwell in caves in the mountains like wild beasts.

**2** But now that they have learned crafts through Hephaestus the famed worker, easily they live a peaceful life in their own houses the whole year round.

**3** Be gracious, Hephaestus, and grant me success and prosperity!
