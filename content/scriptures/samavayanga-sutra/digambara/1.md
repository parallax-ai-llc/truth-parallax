## Cosmological Foundations

The Digambara tradition reads this cosmological structures and time cycles with characteristic doctrinal strictness, emphasizing the necessity of complete renunciation and the precise application of Jain principles. Digambara interpreters stress that cosmological structures and time cycles must be understood within the framework of absolute non-attachment and rigorous ascetic practice that defines the Digambara path.
