## Ethical Classifications

The Digambara tradition reads this ethical classifications and moral gradations with characteristic doctrinal strictness, emphasizing the necessity of complete renunciation and the precise application of Jain principles. Digambara interpreters stress that ethical classifications and moral gradations must be understood within the framework of absolute non-attachment and rigorous ascetic practice that defines the Digambara path.
