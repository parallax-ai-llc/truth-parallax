## Overview

The Digambara tradition values the Samavayanga Sutra's systematic treatment of how multiple doctrinal factors converge and interact. While working from their own canonical sources, Digambara scholars appreciate the text's cosmological and mathematical precision.

## Key Interpretive Approaches

### Cosmological Exactness

Digambara interpreters emphasize the precise cosmological measurements and classifications found in the text, using them to support their detailed understanding of the Jain universe's structure.

### Karmic Convergence

The text's analysis of how different types of karma combine and interact is read through the Digambara lens of strict karmic theory, where every aspect of bondage and liberation is precisely calculable.

### Doctrinal Completeness

Digambara scholars value the Samavayanga Sutra's comprehensive scope as evidence that the Jain doctrinal system accounts for every aspect of reality through its categories and their combinations.
