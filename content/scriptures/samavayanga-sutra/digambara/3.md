## Karmic Combinations

The Digambara tradition reads this karmic convergence and interaction with characteristic doctrinal strictness, emphasizing the necessity of complete renunciation and the precise application of Jain principles. Digambara interpreters stress that karmic convergence and interaction must be understood within the framework of absolute non-attachment and rigorous ascetic practice that defines the Digambara path.
