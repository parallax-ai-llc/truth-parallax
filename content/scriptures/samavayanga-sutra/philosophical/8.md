## Comprehensive Summaries

From a philosophical standpoint, this section on comprehensive summaries and final convergences presents arguments and positions that engage with perennial questions in metaphysics, epistemology, and moral philosophy. The Jain approach offers distinctive solutions to problems debated across philosophical traditions.
