## Doctrinal Enumerations

From a philosophical standpoint, this section on doctrinal enumerations and their combinations presents arguments and positions that engage with perennial questions in metaphysics, epistemology, and moral philosophy. The Jain approach offers distinctive solutions to problems debated across philosophical traditions.
