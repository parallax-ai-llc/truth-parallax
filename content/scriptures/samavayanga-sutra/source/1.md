---
title: "Samavayanga Sutra - Chapter 1"
scripture: "samavayanga-sutra"
chapter: 1
---

**1** Thus have I heard. The venerable Mahavira declared the enumeration of all things that exist in the universe.

**2** One is the soul. The soul is one in essence, though it manifests in countless forms. The soul is eternal, uncreated, and indestructible.

**3** One is the path to liberation. That path is the threefold path of right faith, right knowledge, and right conduct.

**4** One is the universe. The universe is self-existent. It was not created by any external agency.

**5** One is the truth proclaimed by the Jinas: non-violence is the supreme religion.

**6** One is the essence of all beings: consciousness. All beings, from the smallest to the greatest, possess consciousness.

**7** One is the law that governs all: the law of karma. Every action produces its corresponding result.

**8** One is the goal of all spiritual endeavour: liberation from the cycle of birth and death.

**9** One is the fundamental quality of the soul: knowledge. In its pure state, the soul knows all things.

**10** One is the teaching that encompasses all: the teaching of the Tirthankaras, the ford-makers, who have crossed the ocean of existence and shown the way to others.
