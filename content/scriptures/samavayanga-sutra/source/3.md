---
title: "Samavayanga Sutra - Chapter 3"
scripture: "samavayanga-sutra"
chapter: 3
---

**1** Three are the jewels of the spiritual path: right faith, right knowledge, and right conduct. These three together constitute the path to liberation.

**2** Three are the worlds: the lower world, the middle world, and the upper world. Beings transmigrate through all three worlds according to their karma.

**3** Three are the bodies that encage the soul: the gross body, the transformative body, and the fiery body. The gross body is shed at death. The others accompany the soul.

**4** Three are the controls: control of mind, control of speech, and control of body. He who masters all three is truly disciplined.

**5** Three are the channels of activity: mental activity, verbal activity, and physical activity. All karma is generated through these three channels.

**6** Three are the qualities of matter: sattva, rajas, and tamas. Sattva is light and pure. Rajas is active and passionate. Tamas is heavy and dark.

**7** Three are the times: past, present, and future. The omniscient ones know all three times simultaneously.

**8** Three are the aspects of every substance: origination, destruction, and permanence. In every moment, a substance undergoes all three.

**9** Three are the causes of bondage: wrong faith, non-renunciation, and carelessness. He who avoids all three progresses on the path.

**10** Three are the kinds of penance: penance of body, penance of speech, and penance of mind. The highest penance is the penance of the mind.
