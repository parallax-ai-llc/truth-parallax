---
title: "Samavayanga Sutra - Chapter 2"
scripture: "samavayanga-sutra"
chapter: 2
---

**1** Two are the categories of reality: the living and the non-living. All that exists falls into one of these two categories.

**2** Two are the kinds of knowledge: right knowledge and wrong knowledge. Right knowledge sees things as they are. Wrong knowledge sees things as they are not.

**3** Two are the kinds of faith: right faith and wrong faith. Right faith leads to liberation. Wrong faith leads to bondage.

**4** Two are the kinds of conduct: right conduct and wrong conduct. Right conduct leads to the shedding of karma. Wrong conduct leads to the accumulation of karma.

**5** Two are the states of the soul: the bound state and the liberated state. In the bound state, the soul is associated with karmic matter. In the liberated state, it is free from all karmic matter.

**6** Two are the kinds of substances: those that have form and those that are formless. Matter has form. The soul, space, time, and the principles of motion and rest are formless.

**7** Two are the kinds of bodies: the gross body and the subtle body. The gross body is visible. The subtle body is invisible.

**8** Two are the extremes to be avoided: extreme indulgence and extreme austerity. The middle path is the path of wisdom.

**9** Two are the kinds of purity: external purity and internal purity. External purity is the cleanliness of the body. Internal purity is the cleanliness of the mind.

**10** Two are the aspects of every truth: what it is and what it is not. The wise man sees both aspects.
