---
title: "Samavayanga Sutra - Chapter 8"
scripture: "samavayanga-sutra"
chapter: 8
---

**1** Eight are the types of karma: knowledge-obscuring, perception-obscuring, feeling-producing, deluding, life-span-determining, body-determining, status-determining, and obstructive.

**2** Eight are the limbs of Yoga as taught in the Jain tradition: non-violence, truthfulness, non-stealing, celibacy, non-possessiveness, purity, contentment, and self-study.

**3** Eight are the qualities of the right believer: freedom from doubt, freedom from expectation of worldly gain, freedom from disgust at the body, freedom from admiration of the wrong path, praising the virtuous, encouraging the virtuous, being steadfast in the right path, and spreading the right teaching.

**4** Eight are the types of pride: pride of knowledge, pride of worship, pride of family, pride of caste, pride of power, pride of wealth, pride of body, and pride of austerity.

**5** Eight are the matrix of karmic manifestations that must be understood: the nature of karma, its duration, its fruition, its bondage, its influx, its stoppage, its shedding, and its complete destruction.

**6** Eight are the auspicious symbols: the svastika, the srivatsa, the nandyavarta, the vardhamanaka, the bhadrasana, the kalasha, the minayugala, and the darpana.

**7** Eight are the powers of the omniscient: supreme knowledge, supreme perception, supreme bliss, supreme energy, supreme subtlety, supreme immovability, supreme formlessness, and supreme equality.

**8** Eight are the factors that support the spiritual life: proper food, proper dwelling, proper company, proper study, proper meditation, proper austerity, proper service, and proper worship.

**9** Eight are the types of living beings with eight corners of existence: those in the four main directions and those in the four intermediate directions.

**10** This is the eightfold enumeration as taught by the venerable Mahavira. He who understands these categories understands the nature of reality and moves toward liberation.
