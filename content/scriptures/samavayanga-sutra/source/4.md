---
title: "Samavayanga Sutra - Chapter 4"
scripture: "samavayanga-sutra"
chapter: 4
---

**1** Four are the passions that bind the soul: anger, pride, deceit, and greed. These four are the root causes of all bondage.

**2** Four are the forms of existence: as a celestial being, as a human being, as an animal, and as a hellish being. The soul transmigrates through all four forms.

**3** Four are the types of giving: giving food, giving shelter, giving medicine, and giving knowledge. Of these, giving knowledge is the highest.

**4** Four are the infinite qualities of the liberated soul: infinite knowledge, infinite perception, infinite bliss, and infinite energy.

**5** Four are the elements to which one-sensed beings are attached: earth, water, fire, and air. These beings have only the sense of touch.

**6** Four are the stages of a monk's spiritual practice: equanimity, praise of the Tirthankaras, reverence to teachers, and repentance for transgressions.

**7** Four are the objects of reflection: the nature of the soul, the nature of karma, the nature of the universe, and the means of liberation.

**8** Four are the types of non-violence: non-violence in thought, non-violence in speech, non-violence in action, and non-approval of violence by others.

**9** Four are the aspects of right conduct: equanimity toward all beings, compassion for all beings, joy in the welfare of the virtuous, and tolerance toward all.

**10** Four are the great vows beyond the fifth: these are the foundations upon which the spiritual life is built.
