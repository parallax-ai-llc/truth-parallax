## Doctrinal Enumerations

From an academic perspective, this teaching on doctrinal enumerations and their combinations is examined for its historical context, compositional layers, and relationship to parallel discussions in Buddhist and Hindu literature. Scholars note both the distinctive Jain contributions and the shared intellectual culture of ancient Indian philosophical debate.
