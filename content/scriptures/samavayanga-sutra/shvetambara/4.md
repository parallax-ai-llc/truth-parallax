## Doctrinal Enumerations

Shvetambara scholars read this section on doctrinal enumerations and their combinations as authoritative canonical teaching that guides both monastic and lay practice. The tradition's inclusive approach ensures that these teachings remain relevant and applicable to the entire Jain community.
