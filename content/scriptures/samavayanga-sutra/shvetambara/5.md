## Ethical Classifications

The Shvetambara tradition approaches this teaching on ethical classifications and moral gradations within the context of its broader Agamic canon, reading it as part of the comprehensive teaching transmitted from Mahavira through his disciples. Shvetambara interpreters emphasize both the rigor of the teaching and its accessibility to sincere practitioners at all levels of commitment.
