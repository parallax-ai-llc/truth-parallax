## Comprehensive Summaries

Shvetambara scholars read this section on comprehensive summaries and final convergences as authoritative canonical teaching that guides both monastic and lay practice. The tradition's inclusive approach ensures that these teachings remain relevant and applicable to the entire Jain community.
