Shaiva interpretation of the Agneya fire hymns connects them to Shiva's identity as the lord of fire and the source of all transformative energy. The sacrificial fire is understood as a manifestation of Shiva's *tapas* (creative heat), the cosmic energy through which Shiva creates, sustains, and dissolves the universe. The samans' melodies activate this divine fire-energy within the practitioner's consciousness.

Kashmir Shaivism interprets the fire of the Agneya chants as *chiti-shakti* — the power of consciousness that burns away ignorance and reveals the self's identity with Paramashiva. The musical rendering of these hymns is understood as a form of *mantra-yoga* that harmonizes the practitioner's inner vibration with the cosmic vibration (*spanda*) of Shiva's consciousness.
