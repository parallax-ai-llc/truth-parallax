---
title: "Sama Veda - Chapter 2"
scripture: "sama-veda"
chapter: 2
---

**1** Sing to Agni, the domestic priest, the divine minister of sacrifice, the invoker, best bestower of treasure.

**2** Praised by the singers, honoured by the sages, Agni shines forth when morning comes, the carrier of our offerings to the gods.

**3** Agni, God, is the friend of man. The tribes of men approach him daily, bringing their prayers to him who shines with brightness.

**4** Thou, Agni, art a guardian close to man. Thou art a father to thy worshippers. Thy flames bring blessing to the homes of men.

**5** Bring to us, O Agni, strength and noble offspring. With thy flames destroy the evil-doers, the rivals who would harm us.

**6** O Soma, thou art lord of all, the strength of gods and men. Being purified, settle in the pitcher. Come forth in sweetness for the gods.

**7** Soma, flow forth, the conqueror, bringing food and wealth to him who worships thee. Drive away the foes.

**8** Purified by the sacred prayer, the Soma juice streams through the filter. It is the drink of Indra, it is the drink of the gods.

**9** The Soma drops, most sweet, are pressed for Indra. They flow, bright-coloured, to the vat of the worshipper.

**10** Indra, lord of might, drink the Soma that is pressed for thee. Fill thyself with its strength, and be the protector of the world.

**11** Drink, Indra, drink the pressed juice. It is thine own portion. It gives thee strength to conquer all thy foes.

**12** O Indra, come to our sacrifice. The Soma is pressed, the hymns are sung, the chants are raised. We invite thee with all our hearts.
