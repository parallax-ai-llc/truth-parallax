---
title: "Sama Veda - Chapter 3"
scripture: "sama-veda"
chapter: 3
---

**1** We have drunk the Soma; we have become immortal; we have gone to the light; we have found the gods. What can hostility do to us now? What, O Immortal, the deceit of mortal man?

**2** Be gracious to our heart when drunk, O Soma, as a father to his son, as friend to friend. O thou far-famed Soma, extend our term of life that we may live.

**3** These glorious drops, that give me freedom, I have drunk. They knit my joints as straps bind a chariot. May these drops guard my foot from slipping on the way, and from disease defend my body.

**4** Make me shine bright like fire produced by friction. Illumine us, make us ever richer. For in thy rapture, O Soma, I consider myself rich. Enter within us for our well-being.

**5** Of that sweet food I partake, the sweet, with gladsome mind, which Mitra and Varuna and the Wind have fed upon, the one who is called the true and glorious.

**6** O Soma, king, prolong thou our existence as the sun causes the shining days to grow longer. King Soma, favour us, and make us prosperous. We are thy devotees — of this be mindful.

**7** With spirit do I call to thee, O Soma, the all-pervading lord of joy. Be gracious unto us, for great is thy dominion.

**8** The fathers have drunk thee, the gods have drunk thee. The sages have drunk thee, O Soma. Guard and protect us; guide us on the path of righteousness.

**9** Soma, these pressing-stones have called aloud to thee. O Golden One, come to the worshippers, come to the pious ones who seek thy grace.

**10** Through all the regions of the sky thy glory spreads, O Soma. The chants ascend to thee like eagles in their flight. May thy strength be our refuge.

**11** May the divine Soma grant us immortality. May the sacred juice cleanse us of all impurity. May the divine drops bring us to the realms of light.

**12** From the immortal root was Soma born. To the immortal light the worshipper ascends. Through the drinking of Soma, the barriers of death are broken. The singer reaches the eternal abode.
