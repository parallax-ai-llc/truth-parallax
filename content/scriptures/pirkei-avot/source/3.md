---
title: "Pirkei Avot - Chapter 3"
scripture: "pirkei-avot"
chapter: 3
---

**1** Akavia ben Mahalalel said: Contemplate three things, and you will not come to the hands of transgression: Know from where you came, where you are going, and before whom you are destined to give a judgment and accounting. From where you came — from a putrefying drop; where you are going — to a place of dust, worms and maggots; and before whom you are destined to give a judgment and accounting — before the supreme King of Kings, the Holy One, blessed be He.

**2** Rabbi Chanina, the deputy of the priests, said: Pray for the welfare of the government, for were it not for the fear of it, men would swallow each other alive.

**3** Rabbi Chanina ben Tradion said: If two sit together and there are no words of Torah between them, then this is a session of scorners. But if two sit together and words of Torah pass between them, then the Divine Presence rests between them.

**4** Rabbi Shimon said: Three who ate at one table and did not speak words of Torah there, it is as if they ate of sacrifices to the dead. But three who ate at one table and spoke words of Torah there, it is as if they ate at the table of God.

**5** Rabbi Nechunia ben Hakanah said: Whoever takes upon himself the yoke of Torah, from him the yoke of government and the yoke of worldly care will be removed; but whoever casts off the yoke of Torah, upon him the yoke of government and the yoke of worldly care will be placed.
