---
title: "Yasna 33 – The Prophet's Prayer"
chapter: 6
---

**1.** According as it is with the laws that belong to the present life, so shall the Judge act with most just deed towards the man of the Lie and the man of the Right, and him whose false things and good things balance.

**2.** He that worketh ill for the Liar by word or thought or hands, or converts his dependent unto good — such men meet the will of Ahura Mazda to his satisfaction.

**3.** He that is most good to the righteous man, be he a noble or a member of the community or of the brotherhood, O Ahura — or with diligence cares for the cattle — he shall be hereafter in the pasture of Right and Good Thought.

**4.** I who by my prayer will keep from Thee, O Mazda, disobedience and ill thought, revolt from the family, and the nearest Lie from the community, and scoffing from the pasture of the Ox, and the very worst counsellor from the council of the Good —

**5.** I who would invoke Thee as the greatest of all at the consummation, of those that seek Right, we who share thine aid — may we come into the presence of the Dominion of Good Thought.

**6.** I who am a worshipper of Mazda and an enemy of the Lie, let me be permitted to pray to Thee from the region of the Greatest Height, and let me fulfil my desire for the Good through the will of Good Thought, O Thou who art the Right.

**7.** Come hither to me, O ye Best Ones, hither, O Mazda, in thine own person and visibly, O thou the Right and Good Thought, that I may be heard beyond the limits of the earth. Let your sovereign grants appear clearly to us all.

**8.** For my need I pray to Ahura: grant me my desire. May I reach that which is in conformity with your will, when through Righteousness I am mighty and powerful.

**9.** Thus fulfil to me my desire through Righteousness, O Mazda; the desire of Zarathushtra, of Frashaostra, and of all others who serve you well. May your blessings come from the Dominion of Good Thought.

**10.** Those satisfactions which thou hast appointed, O Mazda, give them through Righteousness. Grant me the blessings of Good Thought. O Piety, strengthen Vishtaspa and me.

**11.** O Thou most beneficent Spirit Mazda, maker of the material world, thou Righteous One — to that in Thy kingdom which is best shall the souls of the righteous go, the beneficent immortals; and through long ages shall they mourn who have followed the counsels of the wicked.

**12.** Mazda Ahura, the King, the Ruler over all, made known his Religion, made known that he is the Creator of all things, made known that he is the Lord of all creation.

**13.** In immortality shall the soul of the righteous be joyful, in perpetuity shall be the torments of the Liars. All this doth Mazda Ahura appoint by his Dominion.

**14.** So I strive to win for you, O Mazda, the mightiest strength of Right. With the fervent prayer I make the Lie powerless and drive it from us. I arm myself against the workers of the Lie.
