---
title: "Yasna 51 – Vohu Khshathra Gatha: The Good Dominion"
chapter: 16
---

**1.** The Good Dominion is to be chosen. It is the best lot. It is that of Right. A blessing it is, a blessing shall it be unto the man who is righteous. Through the holiest Spirit, through the deeds we do with Good Thought and with the truth of Mazda Ahura.

**2.** This man it is who by his action, by his word, by his conscience, fulfils the ordinance of Ahura Mazda. He it is whose thought and mind are in accord with Good Thought. He is the truest friend of the Right.

**3.** He, O Mazda, is the reformer, the prophet, the lord that is mighty, strong in devotion, who through the Right holds the land in peace. He, through whose ruling the Ox shall prosper, the man who furthers the welfare of all that live.

**4.** O Mazda, where art Thou? Where is Thy protection? Where is the help of Right? Where is Good Thought? Where is Thy Dominion? Where is Piety? Where art Thou ready with Thy reward?

**5.** All this I ask, how the herdsman rich in cattle may prosper through Right. He is a man of understanding who has an upright mind. Whoso in his dominion is compassionate unto all that live — upon him shall Good Thought descend.

**6.** What help through Right hast thou for Zarathushtra who calls upon thee? What help through Good Thought for me who will praise thee with my prayer? What help by thy Dominion, O Mazda, for him who strives for the best?

**7.** Tell me that I may know: what is the award for the deed and the word? Through the Right, through Good Thought, teach us, O Mazda. Reveal unto us the hidden signs of the future consummation.

**8.** Their souls and consciences shall torment them when they come to the Bridge of the Separator, those who through their evil doctrine have turned men from the best action. For all time they shall be guests of the House of the Lie.

**9.** He that gives to the righteous man, be he a noble or a peasant of the brotherhood — and makes gift to the cattle of their desire — he shall be in the pasture of Right and Good Thought.

**10.** He, O Mazda, is the man that has good vision, who undertakes by word and deed to promote thy Good Thought and the Right. In thy Dominion, O Ahura, Thou art the mightiest of all.

**11.** Together shall they strive after the Dominion of Good Thought, O Mazda. Together shall Right and Piety grow strong, to the honor and glory of Ahura. Let all men learn the law of the Prophet.

**12.** Who through devotion is known as virtuous in his actions, he hath right to speak publicly of his belief. Through the understanding of Truth and the Right, O Mazda Ahura, wise and holy art Thou with thy tongue.

**13.** He that advances Devotion and Righteousness, he, the Prophet of the Lord, is the friend of Mazda Ahura. Him will I name for you; and he through Good Thought shall teach the way to the abode where Mazda dwells.

**14.** Both Frashaoshtra of the Hvogvas and all others for whom Mazda hath designed the prizes — with them I will worship and strive for the Right, with devotion of mind and heart.

**15.** Kavi Vishtaspa, who through the Dominion of the fellowship of the Good Mind and the paths that the Prophet of Mazda hath taught — through Right and Piety — hath resolved to walk according to the Religion;

**16.** The Frashaoshtra Hvogva hath dedicated himself with his being. May Mazda Ahura who created the Ox, give him and those with him the peace and the pasture of Good Thought through the Right.

**17.** The most blessed Armaiti hath chosen for herself. May she be ours for great endeavor. Through Good Thought may she come, and through the Right. Power and authority are his, who gives happiness to the greatest number.

**18.** He who opposes the evil by thought, by word, and with his hands — he who instructs others to advance the Good Religion — he walks according to the will of Ahura Mazda; he is his faithful friend.

**19.** He is the man of understanding who strives for the Right, who in his thought and deed proclaims the Good Religion. Through Thy Dominion, O Mazda, through the strength of Good Thought — this faithful man walks in the ways of Thy will.

**20.** What reward Thou hast for giving, what reward for receiving — by the Fire of Ordeal, through the molten metal, allotted to the two parties — this, O Ahura, make plain to our souls, that we may know it.

**21.** Ahura Mazda, knowing the best, through His ordinance — brought forth these precepts: "Welfare and Immortality shall be for him who hears and fulfils the word of the Right." Through the Dominion of Good Thought he shall attain the Best Existence.

**22.** He who thinketh of Righteousness, and who fulfils its behests by word and deed — he is thy most welcome worshipper, O Mazda Ahura. Unto him shall the blessings of Good Thought come; unto him the promises of thy Dominion.
