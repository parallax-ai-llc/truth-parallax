---
title: "Yasna 32 – Against the Wicked"
chapter: 5
---

**1.** To his land came the call of Thy Will, O Mazda: one that had known it and one that had not. Among both, of the two spirits the holy will choose the right.

**2.** But into this the Daevas and their servants have set themselves, the workers of the Lie. The Daeva-worshippers wilfully deceive themselves, and teach to act for the worse.

**3.** But Thou, O Mazda, through the Fire of the Ordeal wilt give the mark of woe to the Lie, and the mark of bliss to the Right. Through the Spirit and the Fire cometh the award to each according to his works.

**4.** He who works ill for the Liar and whoso works ill for the righteous, each shall receive his reward: so sayest Thou, O Mazda.

**5.** But he who makes the wicked man better by word or thought or hands, such a one serves the will of Ahura Mazda and pleases him well.

**6.** He that is most good to the righteous man, be he a noble, or a peasant, or a pastor — he who with devoted zeal gives himself to the care of cattle — he shall dwell in the pasture of Right and Good Thought.

**7.** I will keep far from thy mind, O Mazda, disobedience and ill thought, from the family arrogance, from the Lie that is very near to mislead, and from the tongue's false speech, from the body that does evil — from all these I will keep guard.

**8.** From the righteous one keep far the deceiver and the desolater. Let the workers of evil be confounded. He that does most to forward the Lie, let the Lie be given over into his hands.

**9.** Grehma and the Kavis: they were not pleased by God and by the pastures worked through righteousness. They overpower the Ox-Soul, making it weep and wail.

**10.** Rather they have attacked the Ox, and the farmer, to afflict them; these whom Grehma with the Karapans has set over the earth. They have destroyed life by their false teaching, they have wailed for the Daevas.

**11.** But the Kavis have given Dominion and Lordship to the followers of the Lie. Through the actions of evil purpose they have destroyed life. Their own soul and their own self shall torment them when they come where the Bridge of the Separator is, to be for all time guests of the House of the Lie.

**12.** When among the sons and grandsons of the Turanian, Fryana, the righteous ones shall arise to further the dwellings of those who serve the Right — through Good Thought they gather the cattle that were in fear and dread.

**13.** He that destroys the Lie with good shall win fame. So hast Thou ordained, O Mazda, through the Spirit, and through the Fire. Promise the reward for the two parties; for this teach us, O Ahura!

**14.** Zarathushtra makes the Lie-followers and the Truth-followers known. Of all Thy teachings, O Mazda, this shall I accomplish by deed and word.

**15.** The Karapans will not obey the laws and precepts of the tillage. For the torments they bring on the cattle and the farmer, they shall in the end go to the House of the Lie.

**16.** Zarathushtra himself, O Ahura, chooses that very Spirit of thine which is the holiest of all, O Mazda. May the Right be embodied, full of life and strength! May Piety abide in the Dominion where the sun shines! May Good Thought give the due reward of their deeds to men!
