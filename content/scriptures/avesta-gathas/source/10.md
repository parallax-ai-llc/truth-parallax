---
title: "Yasna 45 – The Prophet's Proclamation"
chapter: 10
---

**1.** I will speak forth: hear and hearken now, ye who from near and ye who from afar have come seeking knowledge. Now ponder clearly all that is of Him. Not for a second time shall the false teacher destroy life, the Liar with his tongue perverted in his faith.

**2.** I will speak of the Spirits twain at the first beginning of the world, of whom the holier spoke thus to the enemy: "Neither our thoughts, nor our commands, nor our understandings, nor our beliefs, nor our deeds, nor our inner selves, nor our souls, are at one."

**3.** I will speak of that which Mazda Ahura, the all-knowing, revealed to me first in this earthly life. Those of you that do not practice this word as I think and utter it, to them shall be woe at the end of life.

**4.** I will speak of what is best for the life. Through the Right I have learnt to know, O Mazda, who created it — the Father, through Good Thought, of all good works. And his daughter is the good-working Armaiti. Not to be deceived is the all-seeing Lord.

**5.** I will speak of that which the Most Bountiful told me, that word which is the best to be heeded by mortals: "They who for me give ear and heed unto Him, shall attain unto Welfare and Immortality, by the deeds of Good Spirit." So Mazda Ahura spoke.

**6.** I will speak of Him who is the Greatest of all, praising him, O Right, who is bounteous to all that live. By the holy Spirit let Mazda Ahura hearken, in whose homage Good Thought was given to instruct me. By his wisdom let him teach me what is best.

**7.** He whose blessings the living shall receive as well as those who have been and shall be — in immortality shall the soul of the Righteous be ever in joy, but in torment the soul of the Liar. These things hath Mazda Ahura appointed through His Dominion.

**8.** Him will I seek to turn to us by praises of reverence, for now indeed I have seen Him with mine eyes, knowing him in thought as the Lord of the Good Spirit, the Lord of Good Word and Good Deed — even Ahura Mazda. Let us offer Him homage in the House of Song.

**9.** Him will I seek to please for us with Good Thought, Him who at His Will can give us weal or woe. May Mazda Ahura through His Dominion make the cattle and the men of the farm prosper, through the innermost power of Right and Good Thought.

**10.** Him will I seek to magnify for us with prayers of Piety, Him who in vision is known as Mazda Ahura, and who through Good Thought hath promised that the Right and Good Devotion in His Dominion shall have an eternal Home, the first in the House of Song.

**11.** Now therefore let him that knows seek to win the favor of Him who is the Lord of Wisdom, who is the true friend of the Right. For he that is Ahura Mazda hath promised: Welfare and Immortality shall be in His Dominion, the gain of Good Thought, unto whosoever desireth the Right.
