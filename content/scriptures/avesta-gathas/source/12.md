---
title: "Yasna 47 – Spenta Mainyu Gatha: Of the Holy Spirit"
chapter: 12
---

**1.** For the holy Spirit, the Best, doth Mazda Ahura clothe with the hardest stones of heaven those who are in Good Thought and in deed and confession are united with the Right.

**2.** By this most holy Spirit, O Mazda Ahura, and by the Fire, Thou givest the two-fold promise to the Righteous among men: by Right and by Good Thought the blessings of Welfare and Immortality.

**3.** By this most holy Spirit, Mazda Ahura, and by the Best Thought, and by the Right working in deed and in word — to him shall Welfare and Immortality belong, through Devotion and Dominion.

**4.** Of this most holy Spirit, the father is Mazda Ahura, and Good Thought is his daughter. Armaiti, the worker of good, is his offspring, and the all-seeing Ahura is not to be deceived.

**5.** By this most holy Spirit, O Mazda Ahura, and by thy Fire — the sacred gift — has the faithful man his reward. Grant us the blessing of Right, O Good Thought, that it may reach us unto all time.

**6.** By this most holy Spirit, O Mazda Ahura, hast Thou declared, through the divine Wisdom, those things that are best. Thou who knowest Right, in the fullness of time — through this knowledge, through the Dominion of Good Thought — dost ordain the destiny of all things.
