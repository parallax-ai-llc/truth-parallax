---
title: "Yasna 53 – Vahishtoishti Gatha: The Wedding Hymn"
chapter: 17
---

**1.** The best prayer of Zarathushtra Spitama is known: Mazda Ahura through the Right will give him the blessings of Good Thought, and also the Dominion, the holy Piety whose works are done in joyfulness — and Welfare and Immortality.

**2.** He who is most obedient to Mazda through worship, to him the Lord himself shall give the way of Good Thought. He is the lord of Right and Good Thought, the blessed one. He shall come through Piety, strong, acting through the Dominion.

**3.** Now to thee, O maiden Pouruchista, youngest daughter of Zarathushtra — thee hath he given a husband who walks in Good Thought and Right and Mazda. So take counsel with thine own understanding, practice deeds of good devotion of the mind.

**4.** She speaks: "This is my wish; what he may desire, will I give him — the devotion of my life. Through Good Thought, O Mazda Ahura, and through the Right — Armaiti, the blessed, shall guide me."

**5.** So I say unto you brides, and to you bridegrooms: take these words to heart. Apprehend them with understanding. Practice ye the law of devotion and Right. Let each of you strive to excel the other in the Right. So shall it be a happy life for you.

**6.** Thus it is in truth, O ye men and women: the teachings of the Lie lead to sorrows. Those who give power to the wicked, and those who take away the livelihood of the tiller of the soil — their place is the House of the Lie.

**7.** But for you, if ye follow these commands, there shall be reward: the joyful life of Good Thought. But for those who give themselves to the Lie, the evil existence shall be their reward.

**8.** There shall be for the Lie-followers woe and destruction. The evil doer shall be punished. But for the servants of the Right there shall be the best reward — such is the law which Mazda hath ordained through his Dominion.

**9.** So strive ye, each and all, to please the Lord by deeds of Right. Armaiti shall come to your aid and Good Thought. Through the Dominion of the Right shall the faithful man prevail, and from the House of the Lie shall the truth-speaker be saved. So hath Ahura Mazda ordained it.
