---
title: "Yasna 34 – Communal Prayer"
chapter: 7
---

**1.** The action, the word, and the worship for which Thou, O Mazda, shalt bestow immortality and Right and the Dominion of blessed Welfare — these we offer most joyfully unto Thee.

**2.** And all the deeds of the Good Spirit, accomplished with the hands of the holy man who acts in accord with Good Thought by the ordinance of Mazda — all these are dedicated unto Thee, O Ahura.

**3.** He, O Mazda, is the most helpful in deed, who fulfils the ordinance of the Good Mind for the living and the dead, and knows the reward of Ahura by the Right.

**4.** All that is of the Good Spirit I will place in Thy keeping, O Mazda. The offerings of the holy man's well-doing and of Good Thought — with all praise of Thy Dominion through Thy Right.

**5.** For this I have looked to Thee, O Mazda, for Thy teaching through Right and through Good Thought. From Thy Spirit shall the Dominion grow according to thy Will, giving reward to the living and the dead.

**6.** With Thy Spirit and Best Thought, O Mazda, come Thou to us, that at the promptings of Thy Will the Wise One may give us counsel. Make our understanding keen, and give unto us the doctrine of Right.

**7.** Hear with your ears what is best, understand with clear thought the two choices of decision, each man for himself — before the great trial comes — bethinking you that it be well done to our pleasure.

**8.** Turn not from this faith, O Mazda, the faith of him who gives ear to Thy teaching. Increase for us the sweetness of Good Thought, that through it we may enjoy the bliss of Right.

**9.** Grant me, O Mazda, through Thy Grace, the blessing of Right and the reward of Good Thought, whereby Piety and Righteousness shall grant content to their minds.

**10.** What Thou appointest for those who know the law, and what Thou dost for those who forget it — according to Thy command through Fire and Right at the last turning point of life — make us wise.

**11.** The Liars shall be given into the hands of the Righteous at the renovation of the world. Do Thou teach me, O Mazda, through Thy mouth, through the spirit of Thy Word, that I may proclaim to all the living Thy ordinances.

**12.** Grant us, O Mazda, through the fullness of Right and Good Thought, the Dominion for ever. May we serve Thee as faithful workers through our lives. For this we pray, with hands outstretched, at the council of the wise.

**13.** Declare to us, O thou Righteous One: what are the rewards which shall be given through the Good Religion? If these things be so, then proclaim them, O Mazda, through thy divine word, that there be fulfilment for those who seek the Right.

**14.** What is the reward that Mazda will give at the last, to the righteous and the wicked? This I ask Thee, O Ahura, what shall happen unto them.

**15.** I ask Thee, O Mazda, what vengeance cometh to him who furthers the power of the Liar, of the evil-doer who knows no other life than by doing violence to the good man and the husbandman.
