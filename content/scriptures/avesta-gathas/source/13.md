---
title: "Yasna 48 – Expectation of the Renovation"
chapter: 13
---

**1.** If through the Right the Lie is to be vanquished, and that which was long the Liars' stronghold shall be fulfilled by the promise made to the faithful in the House of Song — then shall the faithful have power to overcome the Liar.

**2.** Tell me then, O Mazda, what the prophet knows: shall the righteous smite the Liar before the retributions which Thou hast conceived shall come to pass? For that would be indeed the salvation of the world.

**3.** Whoever attains to right wisdom through the instruction of the living, through Good Thought — that man, O Ahura, is a helper of the Right. His tongue shall be with truth; he shall speak the word that springs from his heart in righteousness.

**4.** He that fashions the Lie by tongue or mind, he that promotes it — they who by their deeds destroy the life that should grow: to them the law of Mazda is not dear, not the religion of Good Thought.

**5.** The better portion doth the righteous man prepare for the wise, not for the liar. The maker of evil, the man of bad understanding, cannot bring forth that which is good. But through Good Thought the faithful man gains Thy fellowship, O Mazda.

**6.** This is the counsel that Mazda Ahura, the all-wise, gave me through the Right: "He that strives with ill speech to lure the innocent — he shall be the companion of the Lie."

**7.** Whom hast Thou appointed as guardian for me, O Mazda, if the Liar shall dare to persecute me? Whom but Thy Fire and Thy Good Thought, through whose workings I have upheld the Right? Reveal therefore this thy Faith as the Religion.

**8.** He that schemes to destroy my possessions — from his actions nothing is to be feared by me. But round him shall come the consequences that oppose his wishes, keeping him far from Good Thought, not from ill, O Mazda.

**9.** Who is thy faithful man, O Mazda, that first received from thee Thy teaching? He is the righteous, the wise, through Good Thought engaged in promoting the well-being of cattle and of men. Through Thy Dominion, O Ahura, shall he flourish.

**10.** The wicked rulers and wicked priests and wicked warriors — their souls shall greet them with ill food when they come to the Bridge of the Separator: for all time they shall dwell in the House of the Lie.

**11.** Since, O Mazda, from the beginning Thou didst create for us thinking beings and teachings and wills — since Thou didst give our bodies life — since Thou didst create works and words that man might freely make his choice —

**12.** Therefore now the truthful speaker lifts his voice, as does the liar: the wise man and the fool, each according to his own belief. But Armaiti, counselling with Thy Spirit, shall examine the hearts of those that hesitate.
