## Overview

The Mabinogion is central to academic Celtic studies and medieval Welsh literary scholarship. Scholars examine the tales through philological analysis of Middle Welsh prose, source criticism of the manuscript tradition, and comparative study with Irish and continental Celtic traditions. Academic research focuses on dating the tales, identifying mythological substrata beneath the medieval literary surface, and understanding the cultural context of their composition and transmission in medieval Wales.
