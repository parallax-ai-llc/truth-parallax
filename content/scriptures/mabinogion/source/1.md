---
title: "The First Branch - Pwyll Prince of Dyfed"
scripture: "mabinogion"
chapter: 1
---

**1** Pwyll Prince of Dyfed was lord of the seven cantrefs of Dyfed. And one day he was at Arberth, a chief court of his, and it came into his head and heart to go hunting.

**2** The part of his realm he wished to hunt was Glyn Cuch. And that night he set out from Arberth, and came as far as Pen Llwyn Diarwya, and there he was that night.

**3** And on the morrow at the young of the day he rose up and came to Glyn Cuch, to loose his dogs into the wood. And he sounded his horn and began the hunt.

**4** As he followed the dogs, he lost sight of his companions, and while he listened to the cry of the pack, he could hear the cry of another pack, a different cry, coming toward him.

**5** He saw a clearing in the wood, and as his pack reached the edge of the clearing, he could see a stag before the other pack. And toward the middle of the clearing, the pack that was pursuing overtook the stag and brought it down.

**6** He looked at the color of the pack, without troubling to look at the stag; and of all the hounds he had seen in the world he had never seen dogs the color of these. The color that was on them was a brilliant shining white, and their ears were red.

**7** And as the whiteness of the dogs glittered, so did the redness of their ears. He came to the dogs and drove off the pack that had killed the stag, and fed his own pack on the stag.

**8** And as he was feeding his dogs, he saw a horseman coming after the pack on a large dapple-grey horse, with a hunting horn round his neck, and wearing a garment of grey woollen stuff about him as hunting gear. This was Arawn, King of Annwn, the Otherworld.
