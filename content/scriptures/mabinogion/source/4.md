---
title: "The Fourth Branch - Math Son of Mathonwy"
scripture: "mabinogion"
chapter: 4
---

**1** Math son of Mathonwy was lord over Gwynedd in those days, and Pryderi son of Pwyll was lord over one and twenty cantrefs in the south.

**2** Math could not live unless his feet were in the lap of a maiden, except when the turmoil of war prevented him. The maiden who was with him was Goewin daughter of Pebin of Dol Pebin in Arfon.

**3** Gilfaethwy son of Don set his heart on Goewin, and his brother Gwydion son of Don devised a plan to start a war between Math and Pryderi, so that Math would leave Goewin.

**4** Gwydion used his magic to steal the pigs of Pryderi, which had been given by Arawn King of Annwn. This led to war, and in the battle, Gwydion slew Pryderi by enchantment and strength.

**5** While Math was at war, Gilfaethwy violated Goewin. When Math learned of this, he married Goewin to restore her honor, and punished his nephews by turning them into animals for three years.

**6** Then Math needed a new foot-holder, and Gwydion suggested his sister Arianrhod. But when tested, she gave birth to two sons. The first was Dylan, who took to the sea. The second was raised by Gwydion.

**7** Arianrhod swore that the boy would never have a name, arms, or a wife unless she gave them. Gwydion used trickery to gain all three, naming the boy Lleu Llaw Gyffes.

**8** Math and Gwydion made Blodeuwedd from flowers as a wife for Lleu. But she betrayed him with Gronw Pebr. Through her treachery, Lleu was struck and transformed into an eagle, but Gwydion found him and restored him. Lleu slew Gronw, and Blodeuwedd was turned into an owl.
