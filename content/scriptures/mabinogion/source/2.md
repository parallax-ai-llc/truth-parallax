---
title: "The Second Branch - Branwen Daughter of Llyr"
scripture: "mabinogion"
chapter: 2
---

**1** Bendigeidfran son of Llyr was the crowned king of this Island, and exalted with the crown of London. One afternoon he was at Harlech in Ardudwy, at a court of his.

**2** He was seated on the rock of Harlech above the sea, and with him were his brother Manawydan son of Llyr, and his brothers on his mother's side, Nisien and Efnisien, and besides these, nobles of his court.

**3** As they were sitting there, they could see thirteen ships coming from the south of Ireland. They came swiftly toward them, the wind behind them, and they neared them quickly.

**4** This was Matholwch king of Ireland, and he came to ask for Branwen daughter of Llyr, the fairest maiden in the world, as his wife, to make an alliance between the Island of the Mighty and Ireland.

**5** Bendigeidfran agreed to the match, and a feast was prepared. But Efnisien, who had not been consulted about the marriage, was angered, and he mutilated the horses of Matholwch.

**6** To appease the king of Ireland, Bendigeidfran gave him the Cauldron of Rebirth, which could restore dead warriors to life, though they could not speak after their restoration.

**7** Branwen went to Ireland with Matholwch. But after a time, the insult of Efnisien was not forgotten, and they punished Branwen. She was driven from the king's chamber and forced to work in the kitchen, and a butcher came every day and struck her on the ear.

**8** Branwen trained a starling and sent it across the sea with a letter to her brother. Bendigeidfran raised a mighty host and crossed the sea to Ireland to rescue his sister.
