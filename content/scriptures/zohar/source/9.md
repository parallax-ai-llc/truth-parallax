---
title: "The Mystery of Death"
scripture: "zohar"
chapter: 9
---

**1** Rabbi Shimon said: When a person's time comes to depart from this world, the Angel of Death appears to him. But to the righteous, it is not an angel of death but an angel of life.

**2** The righteous person sees a great light at the moment of death. The Shekhinah comes to receive the soul, and the soul departs in joy.

**3** The wicked person sees darkness at the moment of death. The forces of the Other Side come to claim the soul, and the soul departs in terror.

**4** After death, the soul passes through seven chambers of purification. Each chamber removes a layer of impurity, until the soul is clean enough to enter the presence of the Holy One.

**5** The souls of the perfectly righteous go directly to the Garden of Eden, where they study Torah in the academy of heaven. They see the face of the Ancient of Days and are filled with eternal bliss.
