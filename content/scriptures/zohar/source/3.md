---
title: "The Letters of Creation"
scripture: "zohar"
chapter: 3
---

**1** Rabbi Shimon said: When the Holy One was about to create the world, all the letters of the alphabet came before Him, each asking to be the instrument of creation.

**2** The letter Tav came first and said: Master of the World, may it please You to create the world through me, for I am the final letter of Emet, Truth. The Holy One replied: You are worthy, but you are also the final letter of Mavet, Death. You shall not be the instrument of creation.

**3** Each letter came in turn, and each was rejected for a reason. The letter Bet came and said: Master of the World, may it please You to create the world through me, for I am the first letter of Bracha, Blessing.

**4** The Holy One said: Indeed, through you I shall create the world. Therefore the Torah begins with the letter Bet: "Bereshit" — In the beginning.

**5** But the letter Aleph stood silent and did not come forward. The Holy One said: Aleph, why do you not come? Aleph replied: Because all the other letters came and were rejected. The Holy One said: Through you I shall give the Torah at Sinai, for the first word of the Commandments begins with Aleph: "Anokhi" — I am.
