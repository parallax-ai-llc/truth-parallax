---
title: "The Rose Among the Thorns"
scripture: "zohar"
chapter: 2
---

**1** Rabbi Chizkiyah opened: "As a rose among thorns, so is my love among the maidens." What is the rose? It is the Community of Israel.

**2** Just as the rose among the thorns has the colors red and white, so the Community of Israel has judgment and mercy. Just as the rose has thirteen petals, so the Community of Israel has thirteen attributes of mercy.

**3** Between the first mention of Elohim and the second mention of Elohim in Genesis, there are thirteen words. These correspond to the thirteen petals of the rose.

**4** The rose grows among thorns. The thorns are the forces of the Other Side, the Sitra Achra. But the rose is protected by the thorns even as it is threatened by them.

**5** So Israel dwells among the nations. The nations may oppress Israel, but they also protect it, for through suffering comes purification, and through purification comes redemption.
