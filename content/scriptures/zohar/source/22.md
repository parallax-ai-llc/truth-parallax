---
title: "The Future World"
scripture: "zohar"
chapter: 22
---

**1** Rabbi Shimon said: In the World to Come, there will be no eating or drinking, no business or trade, no envy or hatred or competition.

**2** The righteous will sit with crowns on their heads, enjoying the radiance of the Shekhinah. This radiance is greater than any pleasure this world can offer.

**3** In the World to Come, all the mysteries of the Torah will be revealed. What is hidden now will be made plain. What is obscure now will be made clear.

**4** There will be no more death, no more suffering, no more separation. The soul will dwell in the presence of the Infinite, experiencing unbounded joy.

**5** But the World to Come is not only in the future. Every moment of true connection with the divine is a taste of the World to Come. Every act of kindness, every prayer from the heart, every moment of Torah study opens a window to eternity.
