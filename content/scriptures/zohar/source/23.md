---
title: "The Final Redemption"
scripture: "zohar"
chapter: 23
---

**1** Rabbi Shimon said: In the end of days, the Holy One will remove the spirit of impurity from the earth. The dead will be resurrected, and the world will be renewed.

**2** The righteous of all generations will arise. They will be clothed in bodies of light, free from sickness, free from death, free from all limitation.

**3** The Holy One will judge the nations. Those who oppressed Israel will be called to account. Those who showed mercy will be shown mercy.

**4** The Temple will be rebuilt, not by human hands but by the Holy One Himself. It will descend from heaven, a building of fire and light, eternal and indestructible.

**5** And the Holy One will wipe away every tear from every eye. There will be no more mourning, no more crying, no more pain. The old order will pass away, and all things will be made new.

**6** This is the hope of Israel and the promise of the Zohar: the light that is hidden will be revealed, the exile will end, and the world will be filled with the knowledge of God as the waters cover the sea. Amen.
