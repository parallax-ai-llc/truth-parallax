---
title: "The Sifra di-Tzniuta - The Book of Concealment"
scripture: "zohar"
chapter: 12
---

**1** The Book of Concealment is the most ancient section of the Zohar. It speaks of the primordial state before creation, when all was hidden within the Infinite.

**2** Before the Ancient of Days established His throne, before the crowns of crowns were fashioned, a spark of darkness struck against the void.

**3** From this spark, the forms began to emerge. But they had no stability. They collapsed and reformed, collapsed and reformed, until the Ancient of Days established His balance.

**4** The balance is the equilibrium between mercy and judgment, between male and female, between giving and receiving. Without this balance, creation cannot endure.

**5** The Book of Concealment reveals that all of creation exists in a state of dynamic tension, held in balance by the will of the Infinite. If the balance shifts, the world trembles.
