---
title: "Prayer"
scripture: "zohar"
chapter: 18
---

**1** Rabbi Shimon said: When a person prays with true devotion, the prayer ascends through the heavens and comes before the throne of glory.

**2** Prayer is more powerful than sacrifice. Sacrifice requires an animal, a priest, and a Temple. Prayer requires only a sincere heart.

**3** The prayer of the community is always accepted. Even if a single individual's prayer is flawed, when it is joined with the prayers of the community, it is perfected.

**4** The words of prayer are vessels. The devotion of the heart is the content. Without devotion, the words are empty vessels. With devotion, even simple words become channels of divine light.

**5** The highest form of prayer is silence. In silence, the soul communes directly with the Infinite, without the mediation of words. This is the prayer of the righteous who have transcended language.
