---
title: "Reincarnation"
scripture: "zohar"
chapter: 15
---

**1** Rabbi Shimon said: The soul that has not fulfilled its purpose in this world must return in another body to complete its mission.

**2** This is the secret of gilgul, the rolling of the soul from body to body. Some souls return once, some twice, some three times. Some return many times.

**3** A soul may return to correct a specific sin, to fulfill a specific commandment, or to complete a specific relationship.

**4** The soul does not remember its previous lives, for if it did, the test of this life would have no meaning. But traces of previous lives remain in the character and inclinations of the person.

**5** When a soul has completed all its tasks, it is freed from the cycle of reincarnation and ascends to its place in the Garden of Eden. This is the true rest of the soul.
