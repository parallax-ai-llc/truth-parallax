---
title: "The Palaces of Heaven"
scripture: "zohar"
chapter: 14
---

**1** There are seven palaces in the upper Garden of Eden. Each palace corresponds to one of the seven lower Sefirot.

**2** The first palace is the Palace of Sapphire, where the righteous who suffered in this world find rest and healing.

**3** The second palace is the Palace of the Firmament, where those who repented and returned to God are received.

**4** The third palace is the Palace of Brightness, where the patriarchs Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob dwell.

**5** The fourth is the Palace of Merit, the fifth the Palace of Love, the sixth the Palace of Desire, and the seventh the Palace of the Holy of Holies, where the Shekhinah dwells.

**6** Each soul ascends through these palaces according to its merit. The highest souls reach the seventh palace and dwell in the presence of the Shekhinah forever.
