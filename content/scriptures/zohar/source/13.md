---
title: "The Mystery of the Torah"
scripture: "zohar"
chapter: 13
---

**1** Rabbi Shimon said: The Torah that we have is the Torah of this world. In the World to Come, the Torah will be read differently, and its deeper meanings will be revealed.

**2** Each word of the Torah has seventy faces, seventy interpretations. Each interpretation is true, and all together they form the complete truth.

**3** The white spaces between the letters of the Torah are also Torah. They represent the light that is too pure to be contained in letters. The future Torah will be read from these white spaces.

**4** The Torah was given in fire and water. The black letters are fire; the white spaces are water. Together, fire and water form the world.

**5** When a person studies Torah for its own sake, the Torah becomes a tree of life for them. It heals the soul and illuminates the mind. It connects the student to the source of all wisdom.
