---
title: "The Concealed of the Concealed"
scripture: "zohar"
chapter: 4
---

**1** Before the Ancient of Ancients, the Concealed of the Concealed, established anything, He created and destroyed worlds.

**2** When He wished to create, He began by projecting a single point of supernal light. This light was En Sof, the Infinite, beyond all comprehension.

**3** Within this point, a fountain sprang forth, from which flowed all the emanations. From one came two, from two came three, and from three emerged all of creation.

**4** Ten emanations, the Sefirot, emerged from the Infinite: Keter (Crown), Chochmah (Wisdom), Binah (Understanding), Chesed (Lovingkindness), Gevurah (Strength), Tiferet (Beauty), Netzach (Eternity), Hod (Splendor), Yesod (Foundation), and Malkhut (Kingdom).

**5** These ten Sefirot are not separate from God. They are like flames bound to a coal. They are the garments through which the Infinite manifests in the world.
