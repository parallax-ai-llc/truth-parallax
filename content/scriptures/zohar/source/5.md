---
title: "The Souls of the Righteous"
scripture: "zohar"
chapter: 5
---

**1** Rabbi Shimon said: When the Holy One created the world, He created all the souls that would ever inhabit it. All souls were present before Him from the beginning.

**2** Each soul has its origin in one of the Sefirot. The soul of the righteous person descends from the highest realms and returns to them after completing its mission in the world.

**3** Before a soul descends into a body, it is shown the Garden of Eden and Gehinnom. It sees the reward of the righteous and the punishment of the wicked. Then it descends.

**4** The soul in this world is like a stranger in a foreign land. It longs to return to its home. This longing is the source of all spiritual striving.

**5** When the righteous person dies, the soul ascends through the seven heavens, passing through the palaces of light, until it reaches the throne of glory, where it dwells in the presence of the Holy One.
