---
title: "The Torah and Its Secrets"
scripture: "zohar"
chapter: 6
---

**1** Rabbi Shimon said: Woe to the person who says that the Torah merely tells stories and speaks of worldly matters. If this were so, even we could compose a Torah of worldly matters, and one even better.

**2** But in truth, all the words of the Torah are supernal secrets. The stories of the Torah are the garments of the Torah. Some people look only at the garments, but the wise look at the body beneath the garments, and the truly wise look at the soul of the Torah.

**3** The Torah has a body: the commandments. The Torah has a soul: the deeper meaning. The Torah has a soul of the soul: the mystery that transcends all understanding.

**4** In the future world, the soul of the soul of the Torah will be revealed. Then all the mysteries will be understood, and the world will be filled with the knowledge of God as the waters cover the sea.
