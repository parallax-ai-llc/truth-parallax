---
title: "The Idra Rabba - The Great Assembly"
scripture: "zohar"
chapter: 10
---

**1** Rabbi Shimon gathered his companions and said: The time has come to reveal the deepest secrets of the Torah. Let those who are worthy come forward.

**2** Nine companions gathered: Rabbi Elazar, Rabbi Abba, Rabbi Yehuda, Rabbi Yosi bar Yaakov, Rabbi Yitzchak, Rabbi Chizkiyah, Rabbi Chiya, Rabbi Yosi, and Rabbi Yisa.

**3** Rabbi Shimon said: We shall speak of the configuration of the Ancient One, the Most Hidden of all Hidden. He whose form cannot be grasped, whose essence cannot be comprehended.

**4** The skull of the Ancient One is white, pure white. No redness, no blackness, no other color mingles in it. It is pure compassion, pure mercy, pure light.

**5** From the skull flow thirteen streams of pure balsam. These are the thirteen attributes of mercy that sustain the world. Without them, the world would be destroyed in an instant.
