---
title: "The Messiah"
scripture: "zohar"
chapter: 16
---

**1** Rabbi Shimon said: In the Garden of Eden there is a palace called the Palace of the Messiah. There the Messiah sits, waiting for the time of redemption.

**2** The Messiah knows all the suffering of Israel. He bears it in his own body. For every wound that Israel suffers, the Messiah suffers a corresponding wound.

**3** When the appointed time comes, the Messiah will arise and go forth. He will gather the scattered of Israel and restore the kingdom. He will rebuild the Temple and bring peace to the world.

**4** The Messiah will not come through war and conquest but through righteousness and truth. His weapon is the Torah, and his army is the righteous of all generations.

**5** The coming of the Messiah depends on the repentance of Israel. If Israel repents fully, the Messiah will come immediately. If not, he will come at the appointed time.
