---
title: "The Other Side"
scripture: "zohar"
chapter: 17
---

**1** Rabbi Shimon said: The Sitra Achra, the Other Side, was created as a counterpart to holiness. Without the Other Side, there would be no free will, and without free will, there would be no merit.

**2** The Other Side draws its power from the leftovers of holiness. It feeds on the sins of human beings. When people sin, they strengthen the Other Side.

**3** The primary weapons of the Other Side are temptation and illusion. It presents falsehood as truth, evil as good, and darkness as light. Only the wise can see through its deceptions.

**4** The ultimate fate of the Other Side is to be swallowed by holiness. In the end of days, the Holy One will destroy the Angel of Death and the Other Side, and only pure good will remain.

**5** Until that time, the struggle between holiness and the Other Side continues. Every righteous act weakens the Other Side, and every sin strengthens it.
