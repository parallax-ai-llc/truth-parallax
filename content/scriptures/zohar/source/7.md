---
title: "Male and Female"
scripture: "zohar"
chapter: 7
---

**1** Rabbi Shimon said: When the Holy One created Adam, He created him male and female joined together, back to back. Then He separated them and presented the woman to the man face to face.

**2** This teaches that wholeness requires both male and female. The Shekhinah, the divine feminine presence, dwells where male and female are united in holiness.

**3** When the Temple was destroyed, the Shekhinah went into exile with Israel. She wanders through the world, weeping for her children.

**4** When Israel is redeemed, the Shekhinah will return to her place. The Holy One and His Shekhinah will be reunited, and the world will know complete joy.

**5** Until that time, every holy marriage between man and woman reflects the union above. When husband and wife dwell together in love and holiness, the Shekhinah dwells between them.
