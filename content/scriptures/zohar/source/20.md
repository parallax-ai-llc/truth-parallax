---
title: "The Land of Israel"
scripture: "zohar"
chapter: 20
---

**1** Rabbi Shimon said: The Holy Land is the center of the world, and Jerusalem is the center of the Holy Land, and the Temple is the center of Jerusalem.

**2** The Shekhinah dwells in the Holy Land with special intensity. The air of the Land of Israel makes one wise. The very stones of the Land are holy.

**3** When Israel is in the Land, the channels of blessing flow freely from heaven to earth. When Israel is in exile, the channels are blocked, and the blessings are diminished.

**4** The Holy Land is connected to the upper worlds through the Foundation Stone, which lies beneath the Temple Mount. This stone is the point from which creation expanded.

**5** In the future, the Holy Land will expand to encompass the entire world. All the earth will become holy, and the Shekhinah will dwell everywhere.
