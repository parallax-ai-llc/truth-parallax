---
title: "The Sabbath Bride"
scripture: "zohar"
chapter: 19
---

**1** Come, let us go to meet the Sabbath, for she is the source of blessing. From the very beginning she was honored; last in creation but first in thought.

**2** Rabbi Shimon said: When the Sabbath enters, all harsh judgments are removed from the world. All fires of Gehinnom are extinguished. Even the wicked are given rest.

**3** The Sabbath is the bride of the Holy One. She is the Shekhinah, adorned in her finest garments, radiant with light.

**4** When the family gathers around the Sabbath table, the Shekhinah is present. When the Sabbath candles are lit, the light of the World to Come shines in the home.

**5** The Sabbath is the secret of unity. On the Sabbath, all divisions are healed, all separations are bridged, and the world is restored to its original wholeness.
