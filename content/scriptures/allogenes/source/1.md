**1.** I am Allogenes. I was dwelling in rest, and I was hearing and receiving revelations. When the great Illuminator appeared to me, he said, "Allogenes, behold, I shall teach you about the Triple-Powered One who exists within the Invisible Spirit, about the First One who is unknowable to all of them—the Unknown God who is above the Aeons."

And he said to me, "Write down the things that I shall tell you, and of which I shall remind you, for the sake of those who will be worthy after you. And you will leave this writing on a mountain, and you will adjure the guardian: Come, Dreadful One."

Then I prayed and I received power over the Luminaries, and the knowledge of the Universal Ones was granted to me. And I withdrew to the holy place of rest. I heard a voice saying to me, "Allogenes, Allogenes, Allogenes!"

And I said, "Who is it that calls my name? And what is the origin of this voice?"

He said, "I am Youel, she who belongs to the majesties. I shall teach you about the Triple-Powered One and about the Invisible Spirit. It is he who possesses that First One—the Unbegotten One, who is above the Aeons."
