**2.** And Youel, the angel of knowledge, said to me, "The Triple-Powered One exists as an invisible, incorporeal being, transcending all those that exist in substance. He is the head of all Aeons, and he gives them power in his goodness.

"For he directed his will from the First Power that was in him, from the Blessedness of the Invisible Spirit and from the Self-begotten Knowledge. He directed his will to know himself by means of the Invisible Spirit, the Single One.

"And there came into being from this a Vitality and a Mentality and a Blessedness and a Life. And all these came to exist in silence and in the spirit of the One who is eternally unknowable, the Invisible, the incomprehensible.

"It is he who knows himself within himself. He is the Unknowable. It is he who is the first principle of the Universe, the Immeasurable, the Invisible, the Eternal, the Unnameable, the Uncontainable, the Unfathomable."

And I was afraid, and I turned toward myself. And Youel said to me, "Do not be afraid, Allogenes, for the Unknown God is gentle—he stands in need of nothing."
