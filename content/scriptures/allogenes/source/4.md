**4.** Then I stood upon my knowledge, and I inclined my rational soul toward the knowledge of the Unknown God. Then the Invisible Spirit blessed me, and I was given a primary revelation.

And I saw a great Light, and Luminaries before me. And the Triple-Powered One appeared to me, the One who truly exists. And I saw the Holy One—the Aeon of Barbelo. And I saw the Invisible, the Triple-Powered Invisible Spirit. And the first power was Vitality, and the second was Blessedness, and the third was Existence.

And Youel said to me, "Concerning the generation of those in the Aeon of Barbelo: the Invisible Spirit exists over them all. He stands in need of nothing, for he was complete in himself. It was not something deficient that he received his completion; rather, at every moment he is complete and immeasurable and imperishable.

"There is no one who blessed him; he is the one who blesses himself. There is no one who praises him; he is the one who praises himself. This is so because he has no need. He himself is his own truth and his own blessedness and his own understanding."
