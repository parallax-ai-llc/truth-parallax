**3.** Then the Luminaries of the Aeon of Barbelo said to me, "Now, concerning the Triple-Powered Invisible Spirit: hear! He exists as an Invisible One who is incomprehensible to them all. He contains them all within himself, for they all exist because of him. He is perfect, and he is greater than perfect. He is blessed.

"He is always One, and he exists in them all, being ineffable, unnameable, being One who exists through them all—he whom, should one know him, one would not know him; he whom, should one see him, one would not see him; he who is the measureless deep.

"He is the one over whom no one rules. He is the God who is above the universe. He is that one who cannot be known, who does not exist. He is something else that is better than all the Aeons. He is the non-being existence.

"He is the life of his own life. He is mind of mind. He is knowledge of knowledge. He is eternity of eternity. He is an eye of the blessed who sees in silence."

I received a primary revelation: the knowledge of the Unknown God, the Triple-Powered One, was disclosed to me.
