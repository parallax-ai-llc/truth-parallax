**5.** And after a hundred years the revelation came to me again. And I wrote three tablets of knowledge, and I left them as knowledge for those who come after me—the elect, the holy, the worthy.

Allogenes, guard these things in silence. Do not reveal them to anyone except those who are worthy—those who are able to receive them with gentleness and with the knowledge of the Great Invisible Spirit and the Triple-Powered One.

Write these things which I have told you, and deposit them securely. I adjure you who hear: do not reveal these words, and do not speak them to the unworthy. Guard the revelation of Allogenes.

These are the things that were disclosed to me, Allogenes, as I dwelt in the place of rest above. I heard all these things from the Luminaries who appeared before me. And I received baptism in the waters of the Invisible, and I was perfected in the Great Light.

And I became divine, and I stood in the rest of the Unknown God, the one over whom no one rules. He gave me power, and I found myself standing in the presence of the eternal knowledge. And I wrote this book.

Allogenes. The Writing of the Stranger.
