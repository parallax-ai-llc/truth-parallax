## Overview

The Academic study of the Didache examines the text's composition, dating, and relationship to other early Christian and Jewish sources. Scholars analyze the Two Ways section's dependence on Jewish ethical tradition (paralleled in the Epistle of Barnabas and the Doctrina Apostolorum), the liturgical instructions' relationship to New Testament eucharistic accounts, and the text's evidence for the transition from itinerant to settled church leadership.
