## Overview

The Protestant engagement with the Didache values the text as a window into Christian practice before the development of later hierarchical and sacramental structures. The simplicity of its baptismal and eucharistic instructions, the prominence of prophetic ministry alongside emerging episcopal office, and the ethical emphasis of its catechesis resonate with Protestant convictions about the primitive simplicity of apostolic Christianity.
