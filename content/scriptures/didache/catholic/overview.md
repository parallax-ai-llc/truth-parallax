## Overview

The Catholic reading of the Didache values the text as the earliest witness to Christian sacramental practice in continuity with later Catholic tradition. The trinitarian baptismal formula, the eucharistic prayers, the Sunday gathering with confession, and the appointment of bishops and deacons all anticipate the developed sacramental and hierarchical structure of the Catholic Church. The text demonstrates that liturgy and order were present from the earliest period.
