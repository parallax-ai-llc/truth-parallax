---
title: "Didache Section 3"
scripture: "didache"
chapter: 3
---

**1** My child, flee from every evil thing and from every likeness of it.

**2** Be not angry, for anger leads to murder; be not jealous nor contentious nor quarrelsome, for from all these murders are engendered.

**3** My child, be not lustful, for lust leads to fornication; be not a filthy talker nor of lofty eye, for from all these adulteries are engendered.

**4** My child, be not an observer of omens, since it leads to idolatry; be not an enchanter nor an astrologer nor a purifier, and wish not to look at these things, for from all these is idolatry engendered.

**5** My child, be not a liar, since a lie leads to theft; be not a lover of money nor a lover of vainglory, for from all these thefts are engendered.

**6** My child, be not a grumbler, since it leads to blasphemy; be not self-willed nor evil-minded, for from all these blasphemies are engendered.

**7** But be meek, since the meek shall inherit the earth. Be long-suffering and pitiful and guileless and gentle and good, and tremble always at the words which thou hast heard.
