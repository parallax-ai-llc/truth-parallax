---
title: "Didache - Chapter 15"
scripture: "didache"
chapter: 15
---

**1** Appoint, therefore, for yourselves, bishops and deacons worthy of the Lord, men meek, and not lovers of money, and truthful and proved; for they also render to you the service of prophets and teachers.

**2** Despise them not therefore, for they are your honoured ones, together with the prophets and teachers.

**3** And reprove one another, not in anger, but in peace, as ye have it in the Gospel; and to every one that acts amiss against another, let no one speak, nor let him hear anything from you until he repent.

**4** But your prayers and alms and all your deeds so do, as ye have it in the Gospel of our Lord.
