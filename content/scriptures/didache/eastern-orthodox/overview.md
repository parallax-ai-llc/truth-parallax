## Overview

The Eastern Orthodox encounter with the Didache recognizes in it the roots of Orthodox liturgical and ascetical practice. The eucharistic prayers echo themes found in the Divine Liturgy, the fasting instructions prefigure Orthodox fasting tradition, and the eschatological watchfulness resonates with the liturgical expectation of Christ's return that pervades Orthodox worship. The text witnesses to the apostolic origins of traditions preserved in Orthodoxy.
