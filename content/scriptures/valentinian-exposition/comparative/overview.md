## Overview

The Valentinian Exposition's integration of systematic theology with liturgical practice mirrors a pattern found across religious traditions. The Hindu temple puja integrates cosmological symbolism with ritual action; the Buddhist tantric initiation combines doctrinal instruction with empowerment ritual; the Jewish seder combines theological narrative with ritual eating. The Valentinian combination of pleroma theology with anointing, baptism, and eucharist represents the ancient world's most sophisticated example of a Gnostic liturgical system.
