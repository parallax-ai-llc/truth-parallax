## The Emanation of the Aeons — Academic Analysis

Scholarly analysis of this section situates it within the broader context of Gnostic literary production and the specific theological tradition to which Valentinian Exposition belongs. The Valentinian Exposition is critically important for reconstructing the Valentinian system in its own terms, rather than relying solely on the hostile reports of heresiologists.
