## Overview

The Valentinian Exposition demonstrates a truth recognized across all religious traditions: that theological understanding and ritual practice belong together. Every living religion integrates belief and practice, doctrine and worship, understanding and enactment. The text's combination of cosmic theology with sacramental liturgy reflects the universal recognition that spiritual truth must be not only understood but embodied, not only believed but practiced.
