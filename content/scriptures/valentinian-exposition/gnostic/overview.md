## Overview

For Gnostic readers, the Valentinian Exposition with its appended liturgical supplements represents the most complete surviving presentation of Valentinian theology and practice in a single document. The theological section provides the cosmological and soteriological framework — the emanation of the Pleroma, Sophia's fall, and the redemption of the spiritual seed — while the liturgical supplements provide the ritual means by which this salvation is enacted: anointing, baptism, and eucharist. Together they demonstrate that Valentinian Gnosticism was not merely a philosophical school but a full religious system with both doctrine and worship.
