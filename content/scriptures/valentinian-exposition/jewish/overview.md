## Overview

Jewish readers find in the Valentinian Exposition's concept of the Pleroma a possible precursor or parallel to the Kabbalistic concept of the sefirot — the ten emanations through which the infinite God (Ein Sof) manifests and interacts with the created world. The paired masculine and feminine aeons (syzygies) parallel the Kabbalistic pairing of the sefirot and the concept of the Shekhinah as the feminine aspect of the divine. Scholars continue to debate whether these parallels reflect genetic influence or parallel development from shared Platonic sources.
