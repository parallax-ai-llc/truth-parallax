---
title: "Valentinian Exposition Section 3"
scripture: "valentinian-exposition"
chapter: 3
---

**1** And the formless offspring of Sophia, which is also called Achamoth, the lower Sophia, wandered in the void, suffering grief and fear and confusion.

**2** And she was separated from the light of the Pleroma, and she did not know the Father. She was like a child lost in the darkness, seeking its mother.

**3** And from her passions, the elements of the material world were formed. From her grief came the water. From her fear came the darkness. From her confusion came the matter. And from her turning back toward the light came the psychic substance.

**4** And the Christ, together with the Holy Spirit, was sent forth from the Pleroma to give form to the formless offspring of Sophia. And they gave her the formation according to gnosis.

**5** And Achamoth, having received the formation, brought forth the Demiurge from the psychic substance. And the Demiurge fashioned the material world, not knowing the Pleroma.

**6** And the Demiurge created the heavens and the earth, and all the things that are in them. And he created the human being, and breathed into the human being the psychic breath.

**7** But Achamoth secretly placed within the human being the spiritual seed, without the knowledge of the Demiurge. And this seed is the spark of the divine, the portion of the Pleroma that is hidden in the world of matter.

**8** And the spiritual seed lies dormant within the human being, waiting to be awakened. It is like a seed planted in the ground, which lies in the darkness until the rain and the sun call it forth.

**9** And the rain is the word of truth, and the sun is the light of gnosis. And when the seed receives these, it sprouts and grows, and it reaches upward toward the Pleroma.

**10** And this is the mystery of the creation: that the Father has placed his own seed within the world of the Demiurge, so that through the seed, the deficiency might be healed and the fullness restored.
