---
title: "Ginza Rabba Section 1"
scripture: "ginza-rabba"
chapter: 1
---

**1:1** In the name of the Great Life, the sublime Light be praised.

**1:2** This is the great first book of the Ginza Rabba, the Great Treasure, which is the book of Adam, the first man.

**1:4** The Great Life spoke and said: I created the worlds and made them perfect. I created the firmaments and established them. I created the luminaries and set them in their places.

**1:7** I created the earth and spread it out. I created the waters and poured them forth. I created the plants and made them grow. I created the mountains and raised them up.

**1:10** I am the Great Life who dwells in the Great Place of Light. I am the Life who exists before all things, who has always been, who shall endure forever.

**1:13** From the Great Life emanated the Second Life, and from the Second Life emanated the Third, and they became many.

**1:16** And the Great Life said: I am the Light that shines in the darkness. I am the Voice that calls in the wilderness. I am the Life that brings forth all living things.

**1:19** Before the firmaments existed, before the earth was spread out, before the waters were poured forth, I was. I am the Wellspring from which all things flow.

**1:22** And the Great Life said to the Uthra: Go, create for yourself a world, as the Great Place of Light was created. Establish firmaments, create luminaries, bring forth creatures.

**1:25** And the Uthra descended and created the physical world. But the physical world was not like the world of light, for in it there was mixture and darkness.

**1:28** And the Great Life placed a soul, a spark of light, within the body of Adam. And He said: This spark shall remember its home, the Great Place of Light, and shall long to return.

**1:31** And the Great Life commanded the Uthras to watch over the souls of the righteous, to guide them on the path of truth, and to help them return to the world of light.
