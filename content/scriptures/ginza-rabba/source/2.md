---
title: "Ginza Rabba Section 2"
scripture: "ginza-rabba"
chapter: 2
---

**2:1** In the name of the Great Life. This is the book of the souls, the passage of the soul from the body.

**2:4** When the soul departs from the body, it rises up and passes through the watch-houses. At each watch-house, the guardians question the soul.

**2:7** And the soul says: I am a soul of the Great Life. I have kept the commandments. I have performed the rites of immersion. I have given alms to the poor. I have spoken truth and shunned lies.

**2:10** I am clothed in the garment of radiance which the Great Life gave me. I carry the sign of the Living Water with which I was baptized.

**2:13** And when the soul passes the final watch-house, it enters the realm of the Uthras, the world of light, where there is no darkness, no death, no deficiency.

**2:16** But the soul that has not kept the commandments, that has not been baptized in the Living Water, that has not walked in the way of truth, shall be detained at the watch-houses.

**2:19** And it shall be purified through many stages until it is fit to enter the Great Place of Light. For no impurity can enter the world of light.

**2:22** The Great Life said: I have sent my messengers to the world. I have sent Hibil, Shitil, and Anosh to teach the children of Adam the way of truth.

**2:25** They taught them the rites of baptism in the Living Water. They taught them the prayers and the sacred meals. They taught them to turn their faces toward the north, toward the Place of Light, when they pray.

**2:28** And they said: Guard the Sunday, for it is the day of the Great Life. Perform the masiqta, the raising of the soul, for the dead, that their souls may ascend to the world of light.

**2:31** And the Great Life said: Blessed are those who hear my voice and keep my commandments. They shall dwell in the Great Place of Light forever, and the luminaries shall shine upon them, and they shall know neither death nor darkness.

**2:34** In the name of the Great Life, the sublime Light be praised. Life is victorious.
