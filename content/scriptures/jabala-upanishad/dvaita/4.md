Madhva reads Avimukta as the place of God's faithful presence — Vishnu's commitment to never abandon His devotees. Whether understood as the sacred city of Varanasi or the heart of the devotee, Avimukta testifies to the Lord's steadfast love. The devotee who knows Avimukta knows that God is always near.

The teaching that liberation comes through knowledge of Avimukta is read by Dvaita as confirming that the awareness of God's unfailing presence is itself liberating. The devotee does not need to earn God's presence — it is already given and can never be withdrawn.
