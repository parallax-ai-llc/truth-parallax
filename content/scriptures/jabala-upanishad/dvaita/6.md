Madhva reads the final teaching as confirming that the fruit of renunciation is eternal bliss in the presence of Vishnu. The sannyasi who has dedicated everything to God receives the ultimate reward: liberation from the cycle of birth and death and permanent residence in the Lord's transcendent abode.

For Dvaita, liberation is not the dissolution of the individual self but its perfection. The liberated soul retains its individuality and enjoys an eternal relationship of love and service with the Supreme Lord.
