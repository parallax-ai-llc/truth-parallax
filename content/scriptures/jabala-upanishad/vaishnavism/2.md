Section 2 provides the sacred mantras spoken at the moment of renunciation. Vaishnava interpreters understand these as formulas of surrender (*prapatti*) — the verbal expression of the soul's complete offering of itself to the Lord. The most significant mantra is the declaration of abandonment of all worldly ties, which Vaishnavas read as the verbal seal on the internal act of surrender to Vishnu.

The ritualization of renunciation through mantras demonstrates that sannyasa is not merely a personal decision but a sacred act witnessed by the divine. The renunciant is not simply walking away from society but entering a new relationship with God.
