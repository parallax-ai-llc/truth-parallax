Academic analysis of Songs of the Sabbath Sacrifice section 12, "Twelfth Sabbath Song — The Divine Glory," examines the text's paleography, language, compositional history, and theological significance. Scholars debate questions of authorship, dating, and the relationship between this text and other Dead Sea Scrolls and Second Temple Jewish literature.

This section has been studied for its contributions to understanding the Qumran community's theological beliefs, liturgical practices, and social organization. Comparative analysis with biblical texts, other Second Temple Jewish compositions, and early Christian literature has illuminated both the distinctive features of the Qumran worldview and its connections to broader currents of Jewish thought in the Second Temple period.
