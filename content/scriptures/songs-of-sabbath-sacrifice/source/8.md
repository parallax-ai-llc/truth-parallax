---
title: "Songs of the Sabbath Sacrifice - Chapter 8"
scripture: "songs-of-sabbath-sacrifice"
chapter: 8
---

**1** For the Instructor. Song of the sacrifice of the eighth Sabbath.

**2** Praise the God of gods, O you angels of the holy of holies. Let the splendour of His glory be praised above all.

**3** The heavens of His holy kingdom, and the firmaments of His glory, He praises above the godlike beings of knowledge.

**4** For the splendour of the luminaries is the glory of His kingdom. And the praises of all the elohim together with the splendour of all His kingdom.

**5** The vestibule of the angels of the inner sanctum, and the structure of the most holy, in the inner chambers of the King.

**6** The designs of the figures of the godlike beings are engraved around the glorious dwelling places.

**7** The figures of the luminous spirits are fashioned in the innermost chambers where the King dwells in glory.

**8** Seven wonderful territories are governed by the princes of the angels, in the holy of holies.

**9** The tongue of the first shall be strengthened seven times with the tongue of the second to him. The tongue of the second shall be strengthened seven times with the tongue of the third.

**10** All the angels of knowledge bless the God of gods, and all the spirits of righteousness praise His truth. They accept the knowledge of His mouth and make confession.
