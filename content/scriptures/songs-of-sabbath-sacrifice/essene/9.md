Essene interpretation of Songs of the Sabbath Sacrifice section 9, "Ninth Sabbath Song — The Divine Throne," reflects the community's conviction that they were living in the last days and that their faithful observance of the covenant would be vindicated at the final judgment. The Qumran community read this text not as historical or speculative but as a description of their own reality — a community set apart by divine election, practicing the true worship of God while the rest of Israel had gone astray.

The passage's significance for the Essenes lay in its confirmation of their distinctive practices and beliefs. Every detail was understood as divinely ordained instruction that distinguished the Sons of Light from the Sons of Darkness and prepared the community for its role in the eschatological drama.
