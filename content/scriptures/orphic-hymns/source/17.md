---
title: "To Poseidon"
section: 17
---

Hear, Poseidon, ruler of the sea profound,
Whose liquid grasp begirts the solid ground.
Thy dread command directs the spacious deep
With all its monsters, while the billows leap.
Dark-haired, strong-handed, trident-bearing king,
Whose deeds the very gods with wonder sing.
Come, shaker of the earth, with gentle might,
And guard our vessels through the stormy night.
