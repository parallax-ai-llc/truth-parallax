---
title: "To Zeus Keraunos (Zeus of Thunder)"
section: 19
---

I call the mighty Thunderer, great in wrath,
Whose power divine makes earth beneath it quake.
O Zeus, hurling lightning through the sky,
Shake heaven and earth, and hear thy suppliant's cry.
With sacred rites and mystic vows I call,
Great king of gods, the ruler over all.
