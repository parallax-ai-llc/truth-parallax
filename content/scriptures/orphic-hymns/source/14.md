---
title: "To Rhea"
section: 14
---

Daughter of great Protogonus, divine,
Illustrious Rhea, to my prayer incline.
Who drives thy sacred car with speed along,
Drawn by fierce lions, mother strong.
Drum-beating, honored, saving, raging, brave,
Of mortals the beginning and the grave.
Come, blessed Goddess, with propitious mind,
And peace and health and needful stores provide.
