---
title: "To Aether"
section: 5
---

O ever untamed Aether, raised on high
In Zeus's wide and brilliant realms of sky;
Great portion of the stars and lunar light,
And of the sun, with dazzling lustre bright;
All-taming power, ethereal shining fire,
The world's best element, light of the entire;
Brightest of compounds, and life-giving flame,
Star of the morn that wakes the world to fame.
Come, blessed power, whom aether bright surrounds,
With eternal, flowery, ever-verdant bounds;
Propitious shine upon us from above,
With all the blessings of thy care and love.
