---
title: "To Protogonos (First-Born)"
section: 6
---

O mighty First-begotten, hear my prayer,
Twofold, egg-born, and wandering through the air;
Bull-roarer, glorying in thy golden wings,
From whom the race of gods and mortals springs.
Erikepaios, celebrated power,
Ineffable, occult, all-shining flower.
From thy eyes the gloom of night away thou drivest
And all the clouds of sorrow thou survivest.
'Tis thine to regulate with judgement true,
All Nature's law, from its first state renew.
Come, blessed power, but to these rites incline,
And grant our mystic offering may be thine.
With holy, joyous, favouring mind attend,
And crown the feast with pleasures to the end.
