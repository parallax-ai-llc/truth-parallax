---
title: "To the Nereids"
section: 24
---

O lovely Nereids of the watery plain,
Who dance within the deep upon the main,
Fair-haired, bright-eyed, divine, of lucid frame,
Whose race from Nereus, great in praise, became.
Swift-moving nymphs upon the ocean's breast,
Protect our ships and grant the sailor rest.
