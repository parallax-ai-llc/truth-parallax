---
title: "To Pluto (Hades)"
section: 18
---

Pluto, magnanimous, whose realms profound
Are fixed beneath the firm and solid ground,
In the Tartarean plains remote from sight,
And wrapped forever in the depths of night.
Zeus of the dead, whose scepter stern and strong,
The fate of mortals dooms, severe and long.
Come, blessed power, accept our sacrifice,
And to the dead restore the light of life.
