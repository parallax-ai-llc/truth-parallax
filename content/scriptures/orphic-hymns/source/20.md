---
title: "To Astrapaios Zeus (Zeus of Lightning)"
section: 20
---

O Zeus of Lightning, bright ethereal beam,
Whose flashing splendor lights the midnight gleam.
Flaming, ethereal, starry, shining light,
Empowering the stormy gloom of night.
Holy, unmatched, descending from above,
Great sire of lightning and the king thereof.
