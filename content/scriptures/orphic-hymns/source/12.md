---
title: "To Heracles"
section: 12
---

Hear me, illustrious, mighty Heracles,
Strong-handed, tireless, valiant, dread disease,
Whose weapon is the club, of race divine,
Father of Time, eternal and benign.
Ineffable, much-lauded, many-formed,
Great leader of the host, by whom is stormed
Each wall of strength. Come, blessed hero, come,
And bring to us thy medicine from the gloom.
