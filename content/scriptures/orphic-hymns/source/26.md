---
title: "To Earth (Gaia)"
section: 26
---

O Goddess, Earth, of gods and mortals source,
Rich in thy fruits, sustaining nature's course.
All-parent, bountiful, all-nourishing dame,
Blessed are thy meadows, ever green thy fame.
Mother of all, thy children's nurse divine,
Whose fruitful harvests crown each field and vine.
