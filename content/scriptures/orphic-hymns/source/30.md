---
title: "To Dionysos"
section: 30
---

Come, blessed Dionysos, bull-faced god,
Born of two mothers, secret, bearing rod.
Bacchic king, pure, of various form and face,
Blessed offspring of the gods, of mortal race.
Ivy-crowned, bull-horned, guardian of the vine,
Raise high the torch and bless this rite divine.
