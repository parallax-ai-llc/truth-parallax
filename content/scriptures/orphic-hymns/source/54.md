---
title: "To Silenos, Satyros, and the Bacchai"
section: 54
---

I call upon thee, Silenos, Satyros, and the Bacchai, with reverent voice,
Attend our rites and in our prayers rejoice.
Divine in nature, honored and revered,
By mortals worshipped and by gods endeared.
Come with thy blessings to this sacred ground,
And let thy gracious presence here be found.
Accept our incense and our offerings given,
And guide our souls upon the path to heaven.
