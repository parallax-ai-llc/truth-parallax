## Overview

Gnostic engagement with 2 Chronicles 3 applies the characteristic hermeneutical inversion to the narrative of building the temple. While no surviving Gnostic text directly comments on this chapter, the principles established in Gnostic treatments of the Hebrew Bible — the Demiurge, the divine spark, archontic powers, and the primacy of *gnosis* — provide an interpretive framework.

## Gnostic Reading

### The Holy of Holies as Sealed Chamber

The construction of the Most Holy Place — sealed, dark, and entered only once per year — represents the Gnostic concept of the hidden divine. The Demiurge conceals the true light behind veils and restrictions, ensuring that only the privileged few access it. Gnostic interpretation inverts this: the veil must be torn for *gnosis* to be universal. The *Gospel of Philip* explicitly connects the tearing of the Temple veil with the revelation of hidden mysteries.

### The Cherubim as Archons

The golden cherubim guarding the Holy of Holies can be read as archontic guardians — spiritual powers that prevent unauthorized access to the divine realm. In the *Apocryphon of John*, archons guard the boundaries between cosmic spheres, restricting the soul's ascent. The cherubim's wings spanning the entire width of the inner room create a barrier that only the high priest — the Demiurge's chief administrator — may pass.

### Mount Moriah and the Threshing Floor

The Temple's location on Mount Moriah, at the threshing floor of Ornan, carries Gnostic significance. The threshing floor is a place where wheat is separated from chaff — an image that resonates with the Gnostic distinction between the pneumatic and the hylic. Yet the Demiurge claims this site of discernment for his own purposes, building a material structure over a place of spiritual separation.

## Theological Significance

- **The hidden divine**: The Demiurge conceals what Gnostic teaching reveals
- **Archontic guardians**: Cherubim restrict access to the spiritual realm
- **Material splendor as distraction**: Gold overlay dazzles the eye while obscuring inner truth
- **The veil**: Every material barrier represents the archontic system's control of knowledge
