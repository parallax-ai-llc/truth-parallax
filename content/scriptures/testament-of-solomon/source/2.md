---
title: "Testament of Solomon - Chapter 2"
scripture: "testament-of-solomon"
chapter: 2
---

**1** And I, Solomon, was overjoyed and praised and glorified the God of heaven and earth. And on the morrow I called the boy and gave him the ring.

**2** And I said: "Take this and at the hour in which the demon shall come unto thee, throw this ring at the chest of the demon, and say to him: 'In the name of God, King Solomon calls thee hither.' "

**3** So the child took the ring and went off. And, behold, at the customary hour, the demon Ornias came like a burning fire to take the wage from the child.

**4** But the child, according to the instructions received from the king, threw the ring at the chest of the demon and said: "King Solomon calls thee hither."

**5** And then the child went off to the king, and the demon followed him, and cried out: "What have I done to thee, man? Take the ring off me and I will give thee all the gold of the earth."

**6** But the child said: "As the Lord God of Israel liveth, I will not brook thee. Come hither."

**7** And the child came running to the king and said: "Behold, O king, I have brought the demon to thee as thou didst command me. And behold, he stands before the gate of the court, crying out and supplicating with a loud voice."

**8** When I, Solomon, heard this, I rose up from my throne and saw the demon trembling with fear.

**9** I said to him: "Who art thou? What is thy name?" He answered: "I am called Ornias, and I am one of the demons who sit in the heavenly places."

**10** I said to him: "Tell me, in what sign of the zodiac dost thou reside?" He answered: "In Aquarius; and I strangle those who reside in Aquarius because of their passion for women. I also cause them to be sleepless."
