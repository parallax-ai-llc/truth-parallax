---
title: "Testament of Solomon - Chapter 3"
scripture: "testament-of-solomon"
chapter: 3
---

**1** Then I, Solomon, sealed the demon Ornias with my ring and commanded him to cut stones for the building of the Temple.

**2** And I commanded Ornias to bring me the prince of all demons. And Ornias went off and brought me Beelzeboul, who is the ruler of the demons.

**3** When he was brought before me, I said to him: "Who art thou?" He answered: "I am Beelzeboul, the ruler of the demons. And all the demons have their seat with me."

**4** I said to him: "Dost thou not fear the Lord God?" He answered: "King Solomon, I am not afraid, for I am the highest among the angels who fell from heaven."

**5** I said to him: "Tell me, by what angel art thou thwarted?" He answered: "The Almighty God, and I am destroyed by the holy archangel."

**6** And I, Solomon, glorified God and commanded the demon Beelzeboul to cut Theban marble for the construction of the Temple.

**7** Then I commanded all the demons to come before me, one by one, and I questioned each one concerning his nature and his activity.

**8** And each one told me his name, his power, and what angel thwarted him. And I wrote down all their names and the names of the angels that opposed them.

**9** By the power of the ring given to me by the Lord, I mastered all the demons and set them to work on the Temple.

**10** And the Temple of God was built in great magnificence, and every stone was set in place by the labour of the demons, who obeyed the power of the seal of God.
