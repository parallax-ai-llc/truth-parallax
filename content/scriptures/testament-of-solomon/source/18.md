---
title: "Testament of Solomon - Chapter 18"
scripture: "testament-of-solomon"
chapter: 18
---

**1** Then I, Solomon, summoned the next demon and he stood before me trembling.

**2** I said to him: 'Who art thou? And what is thy name?' And he answered me and told his name.

**3** And I questioned him concerning his works and his powers, and by what angel he was thwarted.

**4** He said: 'I am thwarted by the angel of the Lord, whose power is greater than mine.'

**5** And I sealed him with the ring of God and set him to labour upon the Temple.

**6** For the wisdom which God gave me was greater than the power of all the demons together.

**7** By the seal of the living God were all the demons brought into subjection and made to serve.

**8** And the work of the Temple progressed, stone upon stone, beam upon beam, until its glory was manifest.

**9** The angels of heaven looked down upon the work and were pleased, for it was done to the glory of God.

**10** And I, Solomon, recorded all these things for the instruction of those who would come after me.
