## Overview

The Interfaith reading of the Testament of Solomon explores the figure of Solomon as a point of convergence for Jewish, Christian, and Islamic traditions. All three Abrahamic religions revere Solomon as a wise king with authority over the spirit world. The Islamic tradition of Sulayman and the jinn, the Jewish Solomonic magical tradition, and the Christian Testament of Solomon all reflect a shared conviction that divine wisdom confers authority over the demonic realm.
