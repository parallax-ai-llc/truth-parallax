## Overview

The Eastern Orthodox reading of the Testament of Solomon resonates with the Orthodox tradition's robust engagement with the reality of demonic forces and the power of divine authority to overcome them. The Orthodox baptismal liturgy includes exorcism prayers, and the sign of the cross is understood as a powerful weapon against demonic influence. The text's narrative of Solomon's fall through spiritual pride also resonates with Orthodox ascetical teaching on the dangers of prelest (spiritual delusion).
