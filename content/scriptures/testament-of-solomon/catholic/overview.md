## Overview

The Catholic reading of the Testament of Solomon engages with the text's demonological and exorcistic traditions in light of the Catholic Church's long tradition of exorcism. The text's insistence that demons are subject to divine authority and can be bound by sacred names and angelic invocations resonates with Catholic sacramental theology and the Rite of Exorcism. The text also provides important background for understanding the development of Catholic angelological doctrine.
