## Overview

The Protestant reading of the Testament of Solomon examines the text for its illumination of the New Testament world's demonological assumptions. Jesus's exorcisms in the Gospels, Paul's references to 'principalities and powers,' and the spiritual warfare language of Ephesians all presuppose a worldview in which demons are real, named, hierarchically organized, and subject to divine authority — precisely the worldview elaborated in the Testament.
