## Overview

The Interfaith reading of the Ascension of Isaiah draws on its dual nature as a text that bridges Jewish and Christian traditions. The Martyrdom section reflects shared Jewish-Christian-Muslim reverence for prophets who suffer for their faithfulness. The heavenly journey provides common ground with Islamic traditions of prophetic ascent and mystical traditions across many faiths.
