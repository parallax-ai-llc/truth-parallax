## Overview

The Catholic reading of the Ascension of Isaiah values both its martyrological and its liturgical dimensions. Isaiah's faithful witness unto death exemplifies the theology of martyrdom central to Catholic hagiography. The heavenly worship scenes in the Vision section resonate with Catholic understanding of the Mass as a participation in the eternal liturgy of heaven.
