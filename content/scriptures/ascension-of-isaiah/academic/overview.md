## Overview

The Academic study of the Ascension of Isaiah examines the composite nature of the text, distinguishing the Jewish Martyrdom (chs. 1-5) from the Christian Vision (chs. 6-11) and analyzing the redactional process that joined them. Scholars investigate the text's angelomorphic Christology, its relationship to other heavenly ascent traditions, and the evidence it provides for the diversity of early Christian theological expression.
