---
title: "Ascension of Isaiah Chapter 11"
scripture: "ascension-of-isaiah"
chapter: 11
---

**1** And I saw the Lord, and he was glorious, very greatly glorious. And all the righteous whom I had seen, and the angels whom I had seen, drew near to him.

**2** And Adam and Abel and Seth and all the righteous drew near first and worshipped him, and they all praised him with one voice.

**3** And the angel said to me, This is the Lord of all the glory of which I have spoken to you.

**4** And he showed me books, and he opened them, and the books were written, but not as books of this world. And he gave them to me and I read them, and the deeds of the children of Israel were recorded in them.

**5** And I saw that he would descend into the world, and would be born of a woman, and would live among men. And he would be persecuted, and be crucified on a tree. And he would be buried in a sepulchre.

**6** And the angel who is in the realm of the dead would be afraid of him. And he would rise again on the third day. And he would remain in that world for five hundred and forty-five days.

**7** And then many of the righteous would ascend with him, and the Holy Spirit would come upon the remnant. And the angel said to me: Understand, Isaiah, for this vision was granted to you.
