---
title: "Ascension of Isaiah Chapter 9"
scripture: "ascension-of-isaiah"
chapter: 9
---

**1** And he raised me up into the second heaven, and the height of that heaven was like the first.

**2** And I saw there, as in the first heaven, angels on the right and on the left, and a throne in the midst, and the praise of the angels in the second heaven.

**3** And he who sat on the throne in the second heaven was more glorious than all the rest.

**4** And I was raised up into the third heaven likewise. And the praise and the glory was even greater.

**5** And so likewise into the fourth, and the fifth, each more glorious than the last. And the praise increased, and the glory was greater.
