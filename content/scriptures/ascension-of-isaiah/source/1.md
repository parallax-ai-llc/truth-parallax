---
title: "Ascension of Isaiah Chapter 1"
scripture: "ascension-of-isaiah"
chapter: 1
---

**1** And it came to pass in the twenty-sixth year of the reign of Hezekiah, king of Judah, that he called Manasseh his son. Now he was his only son.

**2** And he called him into the presence of Isaiah the son of Amoz the prophet, and into the presence of Josab the son of Isaiah.

**3** For he wished to deliver to him the words of righteousness which the king himself had seen, and the words about the eternal judgments, and the torments of Gehenna, and the prince of this world.

**4** And about his angels and his powers and authorities, and about the words of the faith of the Beloved which he himself had seen in the fifteenth year of his reign.

**5** And he delivered to him the written words which Samnas the scribe had written out.
