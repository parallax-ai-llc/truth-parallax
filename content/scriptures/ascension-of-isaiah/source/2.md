---
title: "Ascension of Isaiah Chapter 2"
scripture: "ascension-of-isaiah"
chapter: 2
---

**1** And it came to pass that when Hezekiah died, Manasseh his son reigned in his stead. And he did not remember the commands of Hezekiah his father, but forgot them.

**2** And Manasseh turned aside his heart to serve Beliar; for the angel of lawlessness, who is the ruler of this world, is Beliar, whose name is Matanbuchus.

**3** And he delighted in Jerusalem because of Manasseh, and he made him strong in apostatizing Israel, and in the lawlessness which was spread abroad in Jerusalem.

**4** And witchcraft and magic increased, and divination and augury and fornication and the persecution of the righteous at the hands of Manasseh.

**5** And the word of prophecy was taken away from Isaiah, and he withdrew to Bethlehem.
