---
title: "Ascension of Isaiah Chapter 5"
scripture: "ascension-of-isaiah"
chapter: 5
---

**1** And when Isaiah was being sawn asunder, he neither cried aloud nor wept, but his lips spoke with the Holy Spirit until he was sawn in two.

**2** And this Beliar did to Isaiah through Manasseh, for Sammael dwelt in Manasseh and worked through him.

**3** For the prince of this world was angry with Isaiah, because of the vision, and because of the exposure of Sammael through Isaiah.

**4** For he had seen the coming of the Beloved from the seventh heaven, and he had seen the transformation and the descent and the likeness into which he would be transformed.

**5** And the angel of the Holy Spirit and Michael, the chief of the holy angels, on the third day opened the sepulchre of Isaiah.
