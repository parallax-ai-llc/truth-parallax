---
title: "Ascension of Isaiah Chapter 7"
scripture: "ascension-of-isaiah"
chapter: 7
---

**1** And in the twentieth year of the reign of Hezekiah, Isaiah saw a wonderful vision. And the vision which he saw was not of this world, but of the world which is hidden from all flesh.

**2** And after Isaiah had beheld this vision, he communicated it to Hezekiah and to Josab his son.

**3** And he saw a glorious angel, not like the angels which he used to see, but one having great glory and a position such that he could not describe the glory of this angel.

**4** And he took him by the hand and led him up on high. And he said to him, Who are you, and what is your name, and where are you taking me?

**5** For power was given to him to speak with his lips in this vision.
