---
title: "Ascension of Isaiah Chapter 10"
scripture: "ascension-of-isaiah"
chapter: 10
---

**1** And he raised me up into the sixth heaven. And I saw a great glory there which I had not seen in the five heavens.

**2** For I saw there angels in great glory. And the praise there was holy and wonderful.

**3** And I said to the angel who led me, What is this that I see, my lord? And he said to me, This is the dwelling place of the holy ones.

**4** And he raised me up into the seventh heaven, and I saw there a wonderful light, and angels innumerable.

**5** And there I saw all the righteous from the time of Adam. And I saw there the holy Abel and all the righteous.

**6** And there I saw Enoch and all who were with him, stripped of the garments of the flesh; and I saw them in their garments of the upper world, and they were like the angels who stand there in great glory.
