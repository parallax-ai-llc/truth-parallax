---
title: "Ascension of Isaiah Chapter 3"
scripture: "ascension-of-isaiah"
chapter: 3
---

**1** And the vision which Isaiah had seen, he narrated to Hezekiah and to Josab his son and to the rest of the prophets who had come.

**2** The leaders and the eunuchs and the officers of the king were standing there. And there were also present there forty prophets and sons of the prophets.

**3** And they had come from the surrounding countryside and from the mountains and the plains, when they had heard that Isaiah had come to Bethlehem.

**4** And they came to him and he prophesied to them. And they rejoiced greatly in the words of the prophet.

**5** But Belkira, of the family of Zedekiah, accused Isaiah and the prophets before Manasseh.
