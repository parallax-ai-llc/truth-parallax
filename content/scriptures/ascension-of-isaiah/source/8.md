---
title: "Ascension of Isaiah Chapter 8"
scripture: "ascension-of-isaiah"
chapter: 8
---

**1** And he took me up into the firmament, and there I saw a great war between Sammael and his hosts, and the angels of the heavens were resisting them.

**2** And I saw that in the firmament there was great envy and warfare, and from every direction.

**3** And the angel who led me said to me, This is the war of the adversary. And this war will not cease until the coming of him whom you are about to see.

**4** And he raised me up to that which is above the firmament, which is the first heaven.

**5** And I saw there a throne in the midst, and on the right and on the left of it there were angels.
