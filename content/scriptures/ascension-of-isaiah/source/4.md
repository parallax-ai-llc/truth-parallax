---
title: "Ascension of Isaiah Chapter 4"
scripture: "ascension-of-isaiah"
chapter: 4
---

**1** And in the twentieth year of the reign of Manasseh, Isaiah was sawn asunder with a wooden saw.

**2** And while Isaiah was being sawn asunder, Belkira stood accusing him, and all the false prophets stood laughing and rejoicing because of Isaiah.

**3** And Belkira, through the instigation of Mechirah, stood before Isaiah, mocking him.

**4** And Isaiah was in a vision of the Lord, and though his eyes were open, he did not see them.

**5** And Belkira spoke to Isaiah, Say, I have lied in everything I have spoken, and the ways of Manasseh are right and good.
