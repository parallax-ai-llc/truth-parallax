---
title: "Ascension of Isaiah Chapter 6"
scripture: "ascension-of-isaiah"
chapter: 6
---

**1** In the year in which Uzziah king of Judah died, I saw the Lord sitting upon a throne high and lifted up, and the train of his robe filled the temple.

**2** Above him stood the seraphim; each had six wings: with two he covered his face, and with two he covered his feet, and with two he flew.

**3** And one called to another and said, Holy, holy, holy is the Lord of hosts; the whole earth is full of his glory.

**4** And the foundations of the thresholds shook at the voice of him who called, and the house was filled with smoke.

**5** And I said, Woe is me, for I am lost; for I am a man of unclean lips, and I dwell in the midst of a people of unclean lips; for my eyes have seen the King, the Lord of hosts.
