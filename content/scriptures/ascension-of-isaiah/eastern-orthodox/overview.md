## Overview

The Eastern Orthodox encounter with the Ascension of Isaiah finds resonance in its depiction of heavenly hierarchy, liturgical worship, and the progressive revelation of divine glory. The text's vision of ascending through levels of increasing luminosity toward the Great Glory connects with Orthodox theology of theosis and the liturgical experience of entering into divine mystery.
