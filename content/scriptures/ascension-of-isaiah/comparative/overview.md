## Overview

The Comparative reading of the Ascension of Isaiah situates its heavenly journey within the global tradition of cosmic ascent narratives — from Mesopotamian mythic journeys to Merkabah mysticism, from the Prophet Muhammad's mi'raj to shamanic traditions of celestial travel. The Beloved's descent through disguise resonates with myths of divine figures crossing cosmic boundaries incognito.
