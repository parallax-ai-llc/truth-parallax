---
title: "Shiva Purana - Chapter 7"
scripture: "shiva-purana"
chapter: 7
---

**1** Suta said: I shall now give the final teaching of the Shiva Purana, the essence of Shaiva wisdom.

**2** Lord Shiva is the Supreme Being, identical with Brahman, the Absolute Reality. He is worshipped in His personal form by devotees and known in His impersonal nature by the wise.

**3** The path to Shiva is the path of devotion combined with knowledge. Devotion purifies the heart; knowledge illumines the mind. Together they lead to liberation.

**4** The five sacred acts of Shiva are: creation, preservation, dissolution, concealment, and grace. Through concealment He binds the soul; through grace He liberates it.

**5** The three impurities that bind the soul are: Anava Mala, the impurity of limitation; Karma Mala, the impurity of action; and Maya Mala, the impurity of illusion. By Shiva's grace, these are removed.

**6** The Pashupata vow, the supreme vow of Shiva's devotees, consists of bathing in sacred ash, meditating on the five-syllabled mantra, worshipping the Linga, and surrendering all to Shiva.

**7** The devotee who has attained Shiva's grace sees Shiva in all things and all things in Shiva. For him, the entire universe is the body of Shiva, and every action is worship.

**8** Shiva says: "I am the Self of all beings. He who harms any being harms Me. He who serves any being serves Me. He who loves all beings loves Me."

**9** "Abandon all other supports and take refuge in Me alone. I shall free you from all sins and grant you eternal liberation. This is My promise."

**10** Thus ends the Shiva Purana. May Lord Shiva, the auspicious one, the destroyer of sorrow, the bestower of bliss, grant liberation to all who seek Him. Om Namah Shivaya. Om, peace, peace, peace.
