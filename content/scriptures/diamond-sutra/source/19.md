---
title: "Diamond Sutra - Chapter 19"
scripture: "diamond-sutra"
chapter: 19
---

**1** "Subhuti, what do you think? If anyone filled three thousand galaxies of worlds with the seven treasures and gave all away in gifts of alms, would he on the strength of that gain great merit?"

**2** "Yes indeed, World-honoured One, he would gain great merit!"

**3** "Subhuti, if such merit were real, the Tathagata would not have declared it to be great, but because it is without a foundation the Tathagata characterised it as great."
