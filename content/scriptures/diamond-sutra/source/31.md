---
title: "Diamond Sutra - Chapter 31"
scripture: "diamond-sutra"
chapter: 31
---

**1** "Subhuti, if anyone were to say that Buddha declares any conception of the ego, do you consider he would understand my teaching aright?"

**2** "No, World-honoured One, such a one would not have any sound understanding of the Tathagata's teaching. Wherefore? Because when the Tathagata speaks of concepts of egoity, they are not really what they seem; they are only called concepts of egoity."

**3** "Subhuti, it is the same concerning all things. Those who aspire to the Consummation of Incomparable Enlightenment should recognise and understand all varieties of things and phenomena, but should not believe in or be attached to any idea of their reality. The reason is that the idea of things is merely a figure of speech."
