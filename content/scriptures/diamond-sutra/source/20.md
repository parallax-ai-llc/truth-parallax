---
title: "Diamond Sutra - Chapter 20"
scripture: "diamond-sutra"
chapter: 20
---

**1** "Subhuti, what do you think? Can the Buddha be perceived by His perfectly-formed body?"

**2** "No, World-honoured One, the Tathagata cannot be perceived by His perfectly-formed body. Wherefore? Because the Tathagata teaches that a perfectly-formed body is not really such; it is merely called a perfectly-formed body."

**3** "Subhuti, what do you think? Can the Tathagata be perceived by means of any physical characteristic?"

**4** "No, World-honoured One, the Tathagata may not be perceived by any physical characteristic. Wherefore? Because the Tathagata has explained that physical characteristics are not really such; they are merely called physical characteristics."
