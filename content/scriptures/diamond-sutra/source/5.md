---
title: "Diamond Sutra - Chapter 5"
scripture: "diamond-sutra"
chapter: 5
---

**1** "Subhuti, what do you think? Can the Tathagata be perceived by His bodily form?"

**2** "No, World-honoured One, the Tathagata cannot be perceived by His bodily form. Wherefore? Because the Tathagata has said that bodily form is not really such; it is only called bodily form."

**3** Buddha said to Subhuti: "All that has form is illusive and unreal. When you see that all forms are illusory and unreal, then you will begin to perceive your true Buddha nature."
