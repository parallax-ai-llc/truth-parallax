---
title: "Diamond Sutra - Chapter 23"
scripture: "diamond-sutra"
chapter: 23
---

**1** "Furthermore, Subhuti, the Dharma is equally everywhere, without high or low, and it is called the Consummation of Incomparable Enlightenment. It is attained through the practice of all good actions, without attachment to self, to person, to being, or to individuality; these good actions are equally called not-good-actions; hence they are called good actions."
