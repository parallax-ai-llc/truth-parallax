---
title: "Diamond Sutra - Chapter 12"
scripture: "diamond-sutra"
chapter: 12
---

**1** "Furthermore, Subhuti, you should know that wherever this Discourse is proclaimed, by even so little as four lines, that place should be regarded as a sacred site to which countless devas and asuras make obeisance, as if it were a Buddha-Shrine. How much more is this so in the case of one who is able to receive, retain, study, and recite the whole Discourse!

**2** "Subhuti, you should know that such a one attains the highest and most wonderful truth. Wheresoever this sacred Discourse may be found, there should you comport yourself as though in the presence of Buddha and disciples worthy of honour."
