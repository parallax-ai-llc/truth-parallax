Tibetan scholars analyze Section 12, "Veneration of the True Teaching," through Madhyamaka and Pramana categories. The teachings express the Prasangika view that all phenomena lack inherent existence while maintaining conventional functionality.

Tibetan commentators connect these teachings to the progressive stages of the bodhisattva path, identifying how the insights correspond to specific levels of realization. The dialectical method of negation is understood as skillful means leading beyond conceptual understanding to non-dual insight.
