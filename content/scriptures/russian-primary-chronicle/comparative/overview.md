## Overview

The Russian Primary Chronicle offers rich material for comparative study of Christianization processes, medieval historiography, and the encounter between indigenous and missionary religions. Vladimir's testing of the faiths — his evaluation of Islam, Judaism, Western Christianity, and Eastern Orthodoxy — provides a unique comparative theology from a medieval Slavic perspective. The Chronicle's account of conversion can be compared with similar narratives from Anglo-Saxon England, Scandinavia, the Baltic, and other regions that underwent Christianization in the medieval period.
