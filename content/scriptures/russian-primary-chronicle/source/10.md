---
title: "The Feuding of the Princes"
scripture: "russian-primary-chronicle"
chapter: 10
---

**1** After the death of Yaroslav, his sons divided the Rus land among them. He had commanded them to live in peace, each content with his portion.

**2** But they did not heed their father's counsel. The brothers quarreled, and war broke out among them. Prince fought against prince, and the people suffered.

**3** The chronicler wrote: The devil rejoices at the discord of brothers. When brothers love one another, God dwells among them. When they quarrel, the devil enters in.

**4** In the year 6576, the Cumans first invaded the Rus land. Izyaslav, Sviatoslav, and Vsevolod marched against them but were defeated. This was the first of many Cuman raids.

**5** The Rus princes weakened themselves with their feuding, and their enemies took advantage. The chronicler lamented: O Rus land, you are already over the hill! Feuding has brought ruin upon you.

**6** And yet, in times of common danger, the princes could still unite. They gathered at councils and agreed to put aside their quarrels. But the peace never lasted long.
