---
title: "The Founding of Kiev"
scripture: "russian-primary-chronicle"
chapter: 2
---

**1** Among the Polyanians there were three brothers: Kii, Shchek, and Khoriv, and their sister was Lybid. Kii lived on the hill where the Borichev trail now is, and Shchek lived on the hill now called Shchekavitsa, and Khoriv on the hill called Khorevitsa.

**2** And they built a town and named it Kiev, after their eldest brother Kii. Around the town was forest and a great wood, and they used to catch wild animals there.

**3** Kii was a wise and prudent man. He went to Constantinople and received great honor from the emperor. On his return he settled on the Danube and built a small town called Kiyets, but the local tribes drove him away.

**4** After the deaths of the three brothers, the Polyanians were oppressed by the Drevlyanians and other neighbors. They were a mild and quiet people, and shame came upon them.
