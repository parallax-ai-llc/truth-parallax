---
title: "The Division of the Earth"
scripture: "russian-primary-chronicle"
chapter: 1
---

**1** After the Flood, the three sons of Noah divided the earth among them. To Shem fell the east: Persia, Bactria, and even unto India. To Ham fell the south: Egypt, Ethiopia, and Libya. To Japheth fell the north and west: Media, Armenia, and the lands of the Slavs.

**2** From the line of Japheth came the peoples who settled along the Danube. They were called Slavs. And the Slavs spread far and wide, and each took the name of the place where they settled.

**3** Those who settled along the Morava were called Moravians. Those along the Vistula were called Poles. The Slavs who settled on the Dnieper were called Polyanians; those in the forests, Drevlyanians; those between the Pripet and Dvina, Dregovichians.

**4** Thus the Slavic people spread across the earth, and they took their languages from the Slavic tongue. And their writing is called Slavic.
