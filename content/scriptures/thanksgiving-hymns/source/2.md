---
title: "Thanksgiving Hymns - Chapter 2"
scripture: "thanksgiving-hymns"
chapter: 2
---

**1** I thank Thee, O Lord, for Thou hast illumined my face by Thy Covenant, and from the dawning I seek Thee; and as sure dawn Thou hast appeared to me with perfect light.

**2** But they, Thy people, have been led astray; they stagger about in their folly. They flatter themselves with words and the diviner of lies deceives them, for they speak without understanding.

**3** They have become unstable, without wisdom, for their works are in foolishness. For I am despised by them and they have no esteem for me when Thou dost manifest Thy might within me.

**4** For they drive me from my land like a bird from its nest; all my friends and brethren are driven far from me and hold me for a broken vessel.

**5** And they, teachers of lies and seers of falsehood, have schemed against me a devilish scheme, to exchange the Law engraved on my heart by Thee for the smooth things which they speak to Thy people.

**6** And they withhold from the thirsty the drink of knowledge, and assuage their thirst with vinegar, that they may gaze on their straying, on their folly concerning their feast-days, on their fall into their snares.

**7** But Thou, O God, dost despise all Belial's designs; it is Thy counsel that shall stand, and the design of Thy heart that shall endure forever.

**8** As for them, they dissemble, they plan devilish schemes, they seek Thee with a double heart and are not confirmed in Thy truth.

**9** A root bearing poisoned and bitter fruit is in their designs; they walk in stubbornness of heart and seek Thee among idols.

**10** And they have set before them the stumbling block of their sin, and they come to inquire of Thee from the mouth of lying prophets deceived by error.

**11** And then, with mocking lips and alien tongue, they speak to Thy people that they may craftily turn all their works to folly.

**12** For they have not chosen the way of Thy heart, nor have they given ear to Thy word; but they said of the vision of knowledge, It is not sure, and of the way of Thy heart, It is not that.

**13** But Thou, O God, wilt reply to them, chastising them in Thy might because of their idols and the multitude of their sins.

**14** That they who have turned aside from Thy Covenant may be cut off by the judgment of Thy mouth. And all who devour them shall be found guilty, for they have dealt faithlessly with Thee.

**15** And I, the Master, have known Thee, O my God, by the spirit which Thou hast given to me, and by Thy Holy Spirit I have faithfully hearkened to Thy marvellous counsel.

**16** In the mystery of Thy wisdom Thou hast opened knowledge to me, and in Thy mercies Thou hast unlocked for me the fountain of Thy might.
