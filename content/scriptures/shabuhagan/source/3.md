---
title: "Shabuhagan - Chapter 3"
scripture: "shabuhagan"
chapter: 3
---

**1** The Living Spirit, together with the Mother of the Living, fashioned the world from the bodies of the defeated archons of darkness.

**2** From their skins he made the heavens. From their bones he made the mountains. From their flesh and excrement he made the earth.

**3** The ten heavens were spread out, and the eight earths were laid below them. And over all, the sun and the moon were placed as great ships to carry the liberated light back to its home.

**4** The sun collects the light by day, and the moon collects it by night. Thus, drop by drop, the imprisoned light is freed from the dark matter.

**5** The Third Messenger was called forth. He set the heavenly wheels in motion, drawing up the light through the pillar of glory — the Milky Way — which is the path of ascending light.

**6** The demons of darkness, seeing the beauty of the Third Messenger, were overcome with desire. In their agitation, they released some of the light they had swallowed.

**7** From the light that fell upon the earth, plants and trees sprang forth. In every living thing, a portion of the divine light is trapped, waiting to be freed.

**8** This is why the Elect treat all living things with gentleness — for in harming any creature, one harms the imprisoned light within it.

**9** The purpose of all creation is the liberation of light. Every soul, every particle of light, yearns to return to the Kingdom of the Father.

**10** The great mechanism of salvation — the sun, the moon, the stars, the plants, the souls of men — all serve this one purpose: the gathering and purification of light.
