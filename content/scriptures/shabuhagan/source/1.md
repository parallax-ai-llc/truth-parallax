---
title: "Shabuhagan - Chapter 1"
scripture: "shabuhagan"
chapter: 1
---

**1** In the name of the Father of Greatness, and of His Beloved Son, and of the Holy Spirit: I, Mani, apostle of Jesus Christ, by the will of God, the Father of Truth.

**2** From whom I come — and from whom truth comes — truth which was before the creation of the world and after its end shall continue.

**3** All that has been and all that shall be stands by His power. I give thanks and glorify the Father of Greatness.

**4** Before the existence of heaven and earth and all that is in them, there were Two Natures: the one Good and the other Evil. They were separate one from the other.

**5** The Good Principle dwells in the region of Light. It is called the Father of Greatness. Outside of Him are His five Shekinahs: Mind, Knowledge, Thought, Deliberation, and Resolution.

**6** The Evil Principle dwells in the region of Darkness. Its five worlds are: Smoke, Fire, Wind, Water, and Darkness.

**7** These two principles have existed from all eternity. They had no beginning and they shall have no end in their essential natures.

**8** The Kingdom of Light is above and on the north and west and east. The Kingdom of Darkness is below and on the south. They were formerly entirely separate.

**9** The Father of Greatness dwelt in His Light in perfect peace, wisdom, and goodness. His kingdom was without boundary and without defect.

**10** But the Prince of Darkness stirred within his realm, and beholding the light from afar, desired it, and rose up to make war against the Kingdom of Light.
