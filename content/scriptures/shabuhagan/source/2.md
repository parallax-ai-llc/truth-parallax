---
title: "Shabuhagan - Chapter 2"
scripture: "shabuhagan"
chapter: 2
---

**1** When the King of Darkness rose up against the Kingdom of Light, the Father of Greatness perceived the assault and called forth the Mother of the Living.

**2** The Mother of the Living called forth the First Man, who is called Ohrmizd. The First Man put on five garments of light: Air, Wind, Light, Water, and Fire.

**3** With these five garments as his armour, the First Man descended to do battle with the forces of Darkness.

**4** But the powers of Darkness overwhelmed the First Man. They devoured his Light, and the First Man fell unconscious in the abyss.

**5** When the First Man awoke, he called out seven times to the Father of Greatness. The Father heard his cry and sent forth the Friend of Light, the Great Builder, and the Living Spirit.

**6** The Living Spirit descended into the abyss and extended his right hand to the First Man. The First Man grasped the right hand of the Living Spirit and was raised up.

**7** This is the mystery of the handclasp — the greeting of the righteous — by which the soul is raised from darkness into light.

**8** But the light that had been swallowed by the darkness could not yet be freed. It remained mixed with the dark matter, imprisoned in the bodies of the archons.

**9** Therefore the Father of Greatness devised a plan for the liberation of the Light. The entire creation was fashioned for this purpose — to separate the light from the darkness.

**10** The sun and the moon were set in the heavens as vessels to receive the purified light and carry it back to the Kingdom of Light.
