---
title: "Shabuhagan - Chapter 5"
scripture: "shabuhagan"
chapter: 5
---

**1** And Mani spoke further concerning the apostles of light: Hear, O children of light, the mystery that has been revealed to me.

**2** The Father of Greatness, in His mercy, has sent messengers of light in every age to guide the souls of men back to the Kingdom of Light.

**3** Adam was the first to receive the message. Seth carried it forward. Enosh preserved it. And from generation to generation, the truth was transmitted.

**4** But in every age, the forces of darkness sought to corrupt the message and lead men astray through false teachings and worldly desires.

**5** Therefore the Father sent His greatest messengers: the Buddha in the lands of the East, Zoroaster in Persia, and Jesus in the lands of the West.

**6** Each taught the same truth in different words, according to the understanding of their people: that the soul is a particle of divine light, trapped in the body of darkness, seeking to return home.

**7** I, Mani, am the Seal of the Prophets, sent to bring together all the teachings of the messengers of light into one complete and final revelation.

**8** The way of salvation is through knowledge, through purity, and through the liberation of the light within. He who knows the truth and lives by it shall be saved.

**9** He who does not know the truth, or knowing it, does not live by it, shall remain in the cycle of darkness, passing from body to body until the light within is finally freed.

**10** This is the teaching of the Father of Greatness, revealed to His servant Mani, for the salvation of all who have ears to hear.
