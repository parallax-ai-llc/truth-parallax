## Overview

The Christian reading of the Shabuhagan engages with this text from within the living tradition of Christian thought and practice. Shows how Manichaean theology related to and diverged from Christian eschatology. This perspective brings the accumulated wisdom of Christian interpretation to bear on the text, illuminating dimensions that may not be apparent from external scholarly perspectives alone.

Within the Christian tradition, the Shabuhagan is understood not merely as a historical document but as a living source of spiritual guidance and theological reflection. Christian interpreters bring distinctive hermeneutical principles, doctrinal frameworks, and experiential knowledge to their engagement with this text, producing readings that are both deeply informed and spiritually engaged.
