---
title: "Pistis Sophia Section 6"
scripture: "pistis-sophia"
chapter: 6
---

1. Jesus descended into the chaos and confronted the powers that held Pistis Sophia captive.

2. He sent forth a great light-power that struck the lion-faced emanation of the Self-Willed One, and it was driven back into the lower chaos.

3. He sent forth another light-power that struck the serpent-faced emanation, and it was cast down.

4. He sent forth a third light-power that struck the bull-faced emanation, and it was scattered.

5. He sent forth a fourth light-power that struck the eagle-faced emanation, and it fled into the abyss.

6. All the emanations of the Self-Willed One were driven from Pistis Sophia, and the chaos around her was illuminated by the light of Jesus.

7. Jesus took Pistis Sophia by the hand and led her upward, out of the chaos, step by step, through the regions of darkness.

8. As she ascended, the light that had been taken from her was restored. Particle by particle, her power returned.

9. At each stage of the ascent, Pistis Sophia sang a hymn of praise, giving thanks for her deliverance.

10. "I will praise you, O Light, for you have delivered me. I was in the chaos, and you brought me out. The emanations oppressed me, and you scattered them."

11. "My light was taken, and you restored it. I was in darkness, and you illuminated me. I was material, and you made me spiritual again."

12. When she reached the region below the thirteenth Aeon, Jesus set her in that place and said: "Wait here until I have subdued all your enemies."

13. Then Jesus went forth and bound the Self-Willed One and stripped him of his unauthorized power. The lion-faced power was sealed in the chaos.

14. Jesus returned to Pistis Sophia and restored her fully to the thirteenth Aeon, and she took her rightful place among the emanations of the Invisible One.

15. And Jesus said to his disciples: "This is the story of Pistis Sophia, and it is the story of every soul that falls through deception and is restored through repentance and the mercy of the Light."

16. "Let those who have ears to hear, hear. The mystery of Pistis Sophia is the mystery of your own salvation. You were in the chaos, and the Light has come to lead you out."

17. Mary Magdalene stood and said: "Lord, I understand this mystery. Pistis Sophia is the soul of every person who has fallen into the world of matter and cries out for the Light."

18. Jesus said to her: "Blessed are you, Mary, for you have understood the mystery. You are the most spiritual of all my disciples, and your heart is turned toward the Kingdom of Light more than all the rest."
