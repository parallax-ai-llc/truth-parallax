---
title: "Pistis Sophia Section 5"
scripture: "pistis-sophia"
chapter: 5
---

1. Pistis Sophia continued her repentances, and at her tenth cry, the First Mystery heard her and began to act.

2. Her tenth repentance: "O Light, you have heard my voice in the chaos. You have turned your face toward me. Do not turn away again."

3. "Save me quickly, for the emanations that pursue me are strong and many, and I am alone and without help."

4. Her eleventh repentance: "I cry to you, O Light, from the lowest regions. Let my prayer come before you. Attend to my supplication."

5. "If you keep a record of all transgressions, who shall stand? But with you there is forgiveness, and therefore you are revered."

6. Her twelfth repentance: "O Light, remember my suffering. Remember the Light that I once possessed. Remember my faithfulness before I fell."

7. "I did not fall through wickedness but through the deception of the Self-Willed One. Restore to me what was taken by fraud."

8. Her thirteenth and final repentance: "O Light of lights, hear me in my distress. Open the gate of mercy to me."

9. "I have completed my repentance. Let the full number be accomplished. Hear me now and deliver me from the chaos."

10. When Pistis Sophia had completed her thirteen repentances, the First Mystery looked down from the height and had compassion on her.

11. The First Mystery sent Jesus, the Light of the Light, to descend into the chaos and rescue Pistis Sophia.

12. Jesus descended with a great power of light, and the emanations of the Self-Willed One fled before him, and the lion-faced power cowered.

13. Jesus spoke to Pistis Sophia: "Take courage, Sophia. Your repentance has been heard. The First Mystery has accepted your petition."

14. "I am sent to bring you out of the chaos and to restore you to the thirteenth Aeon, your rightful place."

15. And Pistis Sophia saw the light of Jesus and was filled with hope, and she sang a hymn of thanksgiving to the Light of lights.
