---
title: "Pistis Sophia Section 3"
scripture: "pistis-sophia"
chapter: 3
---

1. Pistis Sophia, trapped in the chaos, sent forth her first repentance to the Light of lights:

2. "O Light of lights, in whom I have had faith from the beginning, hear my repentance. Deliver me from the Darkness into which I have fallen."

3. "I have transgressed, for I left my own region and descended into the chaos. Now the emanations of the Self-Willed One press upon me from every side."

4. "They have taken my light from me, and my power is weakened. I am like a peculiar demon that dwells in no place—neither in the height nor in the depth."

5. "I have become like a heavy-laden spirit among the powers of matter. My light has been stripped from me, and I am material."

6. "I have become like a fallen angel, cast from the height into the chaos. The rulers who dwell in the Aeons mock me."

7. "Save me, O Light, for you are my savior. Deliver me from this chaos and from the grip of the Self-Willed One."

8. She sent forth her second repentance: "O Light, be my helper. Let not the chaos swallow me. Let not the emanations of the Self-Willed One take all my light."

9. "For they have set a snare for me, and they have laid a trap in my path. They have spread a net of darkness, and I have fallen into it."

10. She sent forth her third repentance: "O Light of my salvation, I cry to you in the darkness. Let your light come down to me. Let your image guide me out of this chaos."

11. And the emanations of the Self-Willed One continued to press upon her, the lion-faced power stealing her light, and she grew weaker with each passing moment.

12. She sent forth her fourth repentance: "O Light, why have you tarried? Come quickly to my aid. I am sinking in the chaos like a ship without a helmsman."

13. And still the Light did not come, for the time of her full repentance had not yet arrived, and the mystery of her salvation required the completion of all thirteen repentances.
