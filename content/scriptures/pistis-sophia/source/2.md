---
title: "Pistis Sophia Section 2"
scripture: "pistis-sophia"
chapter: 2
---

1. Jesus spoke to his disciples concerning the fall of Pistis Sophia: "I will tell you all that befell Pistis Sophia, for her story is the story of every soul."

2. "Pistis Sophia was one of the twenty-four emanations of the great Invisible One. She dwelt in the thirteenth Aeon, above the twelve, near to the Pleroma."

3. "She looked upward and saw the light of the veil of the Treasury of Light, and she longed to ascend to that place."

4. "But she did not perform the mystery of her own Aeon. Instead, she ceased singing the hymn of the thirteenth Aeon and sang the hymn of the height."

5. "The rulers of the twelve Aeons below her were angry, for she had looked upward and desired the light of the height rather than performing the mystery of her own station."

6. "The Self-Willed One, the great Archon, was the most wrathful. He had not received the emanation of the First Mystery, and he hated Pistis Sophia for her desire to ascend."

7. "The Self-Willed One projected from himself a great lion-faced power and sent it against Pistis Sophia."

8. "He also caused a false light to appear below Pistis Sophia, so that she thought she saw the Light of the Treasury beneath her."

9. "Pistis Sophia looked down and beheld the false light, and she mistook it for the true Light, and she descended toward it."

10. "When she descended, the rulers of the twelve Aeons pursued her, and the lion-faced power of the Self-Willed One surrounded her."

11. "She found herself in the chaos, in a region of thick darkness, trapped among hostile powers. The false light was revealed to be a trap."

12. "She cried out in anguish, for her own light was being taken from her by the emanations of the Self-Willed One, and she was being dragged ever deeper into the chaos."

13. "And she turned to the Light and began her first repentance, the first of thirteen cries for help that she would send forth from the depths of the chaos."
