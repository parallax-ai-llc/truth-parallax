---
title: "Pistis Sophia Section 4"
scripture: "pistis-sophia"
chapter: 4
---

1. Pistis Sophia continued her repentances, crying out from the depths of the chaos:

2. Her fifth repentance: "O Light, I have trusted in you. Do not let me remain in the chaos. Incline your ear to me and save me."

3. "The rulers of the Aeons and the emanations of the Self-Willed One say to me: 'The Light has forsaken her. Seize her and take all her light.'"

4. "But I will not cease to have faith in you, O Light. Though all my power is taken, still I will trust."

5. Her sixth repentance: "O Light, I have sinned against you, but unknowingly. I was deceived by the false light that the Self-Willed One projected."

6. "I did not sin willfully but was led astray by my desire for the heights. Have mercy on me according to the mystery of your name."

7. Her seventh repentance: "O Light, save me for the sake of your goodness. I know that you are compassionate and merciful."

8. "Do not leave me in the chaos in this terrible hour. Do not let all my afflictions bring me to nothing."

9. "For the emanations of the Self-Willed One and the lion-faced power have taken all my light and all my power. I am weak and I cry to you."

10. Her eighth repentance: "O Light, do not be angry with me, for I have transgressed through ignorance, not through malice."

11. "I am consumed by fear in the midst of the chaos. The darkness is thick about me, and I cannot see the way."

12. Her ninth repentance was the most desperate: "O Light, if you do not save me, I shall perish entirely. My light shall be dissolved in the chaos, and I shall cease to be."

13. "The emanations of the Self-Willed One say: 'Where now is her faith? Where now is her light?' They taunt me ceaselessly."

14. "But I will not abandon my faith. Even in the deepest darkness, I will call upon the Light, for the Light is my life and my salvation."
