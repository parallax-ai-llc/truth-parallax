## Overview

An interfaith reading of the Kiteba Cilwe seeks to build bridges of understanding between Yazidi Faith and other religious communities, fostering mutual respect and appreciation through engagement with sacred texts. A vital text for interreligious understanding of Yazidi faith. This approach emphasizes shared values, complementary insights, and the potential for enriching dialogue.

Interfaith engagement with the Kiteba Cilwe addresses common misconceptions, highlights the depth and sophistication of Yazidi Faith theological thought, and identifies points of convergence with other traditions' scriptures and teachings. Such readings contribute to a more inclusive and informed understanding of humanity's diverse spiritual heritage.
