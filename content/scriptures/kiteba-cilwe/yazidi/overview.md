## Overview

The Yazidi reading of the Kiteba Cilwe engages with this text from within the living tradition of Yazidi thought and practice. A direct divine revelation defining Yazidi theology and identity. This perspective brings the accumulated wisdom of Yazidi interpretation to bear on the text, illuminating dimensions that may not be apparent from external scholarly perspectives alone.

Within the Yazidi tradition, the Kiteba Cilwe is understood not merely as a historical document but as a living source of spiritual guidance and theological reflection. Yazidi interpreters bring distinctive hermeneutical principles, doctrinal frameworks, and experiential knowledge to their engagement with this text, producing readings that are both deeply informed and spiritually engaged.
