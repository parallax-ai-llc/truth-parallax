Section 4 of the Kiteba Cilwe, addressing eschatology, holds particular significance within the Yazidi tradition. The themes of end times, judgment are central to Yazidi theology and practice, and this section has been a focus of traditional interpretation and spiritual reflection.

Within Yazidi tradition, this section's treatment of eschatology is understood not merely as historical or theoretical but as a living source of guidance for faith and practice. The teachings on end times, judgment inform Yazidi self-understanding and continue to shape communal identity and spiritual aspiration.
