## Overview

A comparative approach to the Kiteba Cilwe situates this text within the broader context of world religious literature, identifying parallels, contrasts, and mutual influences across traditions. Compared with other first-person divine revelations across religious traditions. This comparative perspective enriches understanding both of the Kiteba Cilwe itself and of the religious traditions with which it is brought into dialogue.

Key areas of comparative inquiry include cosmological narratives, theological concepts, ethical frameworks, ritual practices, and soteriological visions. The comparative study of the Kiteba Cilwe contributes to a deeper appreciation of both the universal themes in human religious experience and the distinctive contributions of Yazidi Faith to the world's spiritual heritage.
