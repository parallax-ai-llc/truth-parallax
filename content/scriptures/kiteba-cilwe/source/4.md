---
title: "Kiteba Cilwe - Chapter 4"
scripture: "kiteba-cilwe"
chapter: 4
---

**1** These are the final teachings of the Book of Illumination, concerning the end of days and the renewal of the world.

**2** Melek Taus said: "There shall come a time when the world grows dark, when the faithful are persecuted and driven from their homes, when the sacred shrine of Lalish is threatened."

**3** "In that time, do not lose faith. For I am with you always, even in the darkest hour. My light shall not be extinguished, and my people shall not perish."

**4** "Hold fast to the teachings that I have given you through Sheikh Adi. Keep the sacred observances. Maintain the order of the castes. Guard the mysteries of the faith."

**5** "For in the end, the darkness shall be overcome. The light of God shall shine forth again, and the faithful shall be vindicated. Those who persecuted you shall be brought to shame, and those who remained true shall be rewarded."

**6** "I, Melek Taus, shall descend again to the earth. I shall gather my people from the four corners of the world. I shall lead them to the valley of Lalish, and there shall be a great celebration."

**7** "The springs shall flow with pure water, and the trees shall bear abundant fruit. The earth shall bring forth its bounty, and there shall be no more hunger or thirst among the faithful."

**8** "On that day, the seven angels shall stand before the throne of God, and they shall give account of their stewardship. And God shall judge all peoples according to their deeds."

**9** "The righteous shall enter the garden of eternal light, where there is no sorrow and no pain, where the soul is at peace forever."

**10** "But until that day, the faithful must endure. They must keep the faith, generation after generation, father to son, mother to daughter, until the promise is fulfilled."

**11** "The Yazidi people are the people of Melek Taus. They are set apart from all other nations. Their religion is the oldest religion, the first light that God kindled upon the earth."

**12** "Therefore, O my people, be proud of your heritage. Do not be ashamed of who you are. Do not hide your faith. Stand firm in the truth that has been given to you."

**13** "For God created you for a purpose, and that purpose shall be fulfilled. You are the keepers of the light, the guardians of the ancient wisdom, the children of Melek Taus."

**14** "And I say unto you: he who keeps these teachings shall live forever in the light of God. He who abandons them shall wander in darkness. Choose therefore the light, and live."

**15** Thus ends the Book of Illumination, the sacred teaching of Melek Taus, given through his servant Sheikh Adi, preserved by the faithful, and guarded through all the ages. Praise be to God, the Lord of all worlds.
