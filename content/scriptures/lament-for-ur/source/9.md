**Section IX: The Fallen Greatness**

**1** The mighty, where is the mighty?
**2** The mighty of the city—where is the mighty?
**3** The mighty of the land—where is the mighty?

**4** Its queen weeps bitter tears.
**5** "My city which existed no more—my city which existed no more!
**6** I am not its queen any more.
**7** O Nanna, Ur which existed no more—I am not its queen any more."

**8** The ox in its stable has not been tended;
**9** its herdsman is gone.
**10** The sheep in its fold has not been tended;
**11** its shepherd is gone.

**12** In the rivers of the city, where the boats were launched,
**13** weeds grow long.
**14** On the highways of the city, where the carriages were driven,
**15** the mourning reeds grow.

**16** The crouching ones, the dead ones,
**17** no new ones walk among them.
**18** The haunting ones, the haunted ones,
**19** no new ones sit among them.

**20** Ur, the city of lordship and kingship,
**21** has been set on its side.
**22** Its lordship and its kingship have been removed from it.

**23** Ur, the city of abundance,
**24** where the granaries once stood full—
**25** its granaries have been emptied.

**26** Ur, the city of splendid feasts—
**27** its feasts have been ended.
**28** Ur, the city that knew fine music—
**29** its music has been silenced.

**30** The house of the land which was resplendent—
**31** ashes have replaced it.
**32** The temple to which the congregation used to come—
**33** the congregation comes no more.
