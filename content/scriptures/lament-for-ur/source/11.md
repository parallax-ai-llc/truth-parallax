**Section XI: The Final Appeal**

**1** O bitter storm, retreat from the city!
**2** Storm, retreat from the city, retreat from Ur!
**3** Storm that has destroyed the loyal land, retreat!

**4** O An, may your city be rebuilt!
**5** O An, may your people return!
**6** O Nanna, may your house be restored!

**7** The people in their misfortune cry out to you.
**8** May the cry of your city reach you!
**9** May the song of Ur reach your ears!

**10** O Nanna, let the people who have experienced your wrath
**11** bring their tears before you.
**12** Let the city that has been destroyed by the storm
**13** be returned in your name.

**14** Let the house that has been destroyed
**15** be firmly re-established.
**16** Let the stalls be filled with cattle once more.
**17** Let the sheepfolds be filled with sheep.

**18** O Nanna, may the people who fell to the ground rise again.
**19** May the people who were driven out return.
**20** May the mother find her child.
**21** May the father find his son.

**22** May the people of Ur find their families.
**23** May the widows find their husbands.
**24** May the orphans find their fathers.

**25** O Nanna, in your restored city,
**26** may praise be sung unto you.
**27** O Nanna, may your city Ur
**28** be restored to its place before you.

**29** By the life of the city, may it be restored before you.
**30** By the life of the people of Ur, may they pray to you.

**31** O Nanna, your faithful people,
**32** may they bow before you.
**33** O city, your bitter lament has been placed before Nanna.
**34** Your tears are shed before him.

**35** May An return the city to its place for you!
**36** May he make it resplendent once more!
**37** O Nanna, may your restored city shine before you!

**38** [Antiphon: It is a lament of Ur.]
