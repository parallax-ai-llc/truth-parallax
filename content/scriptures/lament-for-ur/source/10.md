**Section X: Prayers for Mercy**

**1** O Nanna, O your city! O your house! O your people!
**2** To the city may you show compassion. Return to it.
**3** O Nanna, to your city may you show compassion. Return to it.

**4** Let not the faithful shepherd be destroyed.
**5** Let not the faithful shepherd of Ur be destroyed.
**6** Let not the faithful shepherd of the Ekishnugal be destroyed.

**7** To the city which has been made into a ruin heap,
**8** may you show compassion.
**9** O Nanna, to the city which has been made into a ruin heap,
**10** may you show compassion.

**11** O Nanna, may the city which has been dealt its fate
**12** be restored to you.
**13** May the city whose people have been killed
**14** have prayers said for it.

**15** O Nanna, your destroyed city—when will it be rebuilt?
**16** Your ruined house—when will it be restored?
**17** Will the brickwork shine once more?
**18** Will your temple be raised once more?

**19** Will the festivals be celebrated once more?
**20** Will the rites be performed once more?
**21** Will the songs of rejoicing once more be heard?

**22** Will the righteous who were killed come to life?
**23** Will the mothers who were destroyed cradle their children?
**24** Will the fathers who were destroyed hold their sons?

**25** O Nanna, your city which has been returned to the clay—
**26** let it be given back to you!
**27** Your house which lies in rubble—
**28** let it be given back to you!

**29** May An restore it for you.
**30** May Enlil give you a favourable sign.
**31** May the mother of the land show you mercy.
**32** May Nanna show mercy to his city.
