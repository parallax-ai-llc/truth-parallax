**Section I: The Abandonment Decreed**

**1** He has abandoned his stable, his sheepfold has been delivered to the wind.
**2** The Wild Bull has abandoned his stable, his sheepfold has been delivered to the wind.
**3** The lord of all the lands has abandoned his stable, his sheepfold has been delivered to the wind.

**4** Enlil has abandoned Nippur, his sheepfold has been delivered to the wind.
**5** Ninlil has abandoned her stable, her sheepfold has been delivered to the wind.
**6** Ninlil has abandoned the Kiur, her sheepfold has been delivered to the wind.

**7** The queen of Kesh has abandoned her stable, her sheepfold has been delivered to the wind.
**8** Ninmah has abandoned Kesh, her sheepfold has been delivered to the wind.

**9** The mistress of Isin has abandoned her stable, her sheepfold has been delivered to the wind.
**10** Ninisinna has abandoned the shrine of Egal-mah, her sheepfold has been delivered to the wind.

**11** The queen of Erech has abandoned her stable, her sheepfold has been delivered to the wind.
**12** Inanna has abandoned Erech, her sheepfold has been delivered to the wind.

**13** Nanna has abandoned Ur, his sheepfold has been delivered to the wind.
**14** Sin has abandoned the Ekishnugal, his sheepfold has been delivered to the wind.

**15** His wife Ningal has abandoned her stable, her sheepfold has been delivered to the wind.
**16** Ningal has abandoned the Agrunna, her sheepfold has been delivered to the wind.

**17** The Wild Bull has abandoned his stable, his sheepfold has been delivered to the wind.
**18** The queen has abandoned her stable, her sheepfold has been delivered to the wind.

**19** The city of Ur has been given to the wind.
