**Section VII: The People Scattered**

**1** The people of Sumer and Akkad have been stricken.
**2** The weakened city—its appearance has been destroyed.
**3** Its people are scattered; they have been driven away.

**4** Its possessions have been scattered in all directions.
**5** Its precious metals have been cut off from the city.
**6** Its precious stones have been carried away.
**7** Its silver has been scattered in the outlying regions.

**8** Ur—its fat and its cream have been carried off from the stable.
**9** Its cattle have been driven off;
**10** its fat sheep have been dispersed.

**11** The mother before the eyes of her child,
**12** the father before the eyes of his child—
**13** they have been put to death.

**14** In the city, he who said "Let me eat" has found no food.
**15** He who said "Let me drink" has found no water.
**16** He who said "Let me rest" has found no resting place.

**17** The mother left her daughter;
**18** the father turned away from his son.
**19** In the city, the faithful wife was abandoned.
**20** The young child was abandoned.
**21** The suckling babe was abandoned.

**22** She who had a husband, she who had a child—
**23** they were scattered like reeds.
**24** The city was given to a strange people.

**25** In the land, the crops have been destroyed.
**26** In the fields, the grain withered upon its stalk.
**27** The orchards and gardens withered.

**28** The cattle groan in their stalls.
**29** The sheep bleat in their folds.
**30** The herdsman has put his stick to the ground.
**31** The shepherd has put his crook to the ground.

**32** The rivers of the city were filled with blood.
**33** The rivers that had run with sweet water—
**34** now bitter water runs through them.
