The destruction of temples and sacred precincts has generated some of the most powerful religious literature across traditions. The Lament for Ur's description of desecrated temples parallels the biblical Psalms of communal lament (Psalm 74, 79) and the book of Lamentations. The Roman destruction of the Second Temple (70 CE) and the destruction of Buddhist monasteries by invading forces have generated analogous literatures of sacred loss.
