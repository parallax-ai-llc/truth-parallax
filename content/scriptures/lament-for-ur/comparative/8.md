The extension of destruction from city to countryside parallels similar expansions in biblical lamentation. Jeremiah 4:23-26 describes creation itself reverting to chaos — 'I looked at the earth, and it was formless and empty.' The Egyptian Admonitions of Ipuwer similarly describe the breakdown of agricultural order. In the Baal Cycle, Baal's absence causes cosmic drought. Across traditions, the failure of the land is understood as a sign of cosmic disorder, not merely economic hardship.
