---
title: "Teachings of Silvanus - Chapter 6"
scripture: "teachings-of-silvanus"
chapter: 6
---

**1** Remember that Sodom was once a land of plenty, but because of the foolishness of its people, it became an uninhabited wasteland.

**2** Do not be like those who are proud and boast of their possessions, for their possessions will be taken from them, and they will be left with nothing.

**3** Do not be like the wealthy man who stored up treasures for himself but was not rich toward God. For in the night his soul was required of him.

**4** My son, do not cling to the things of this world, for they are passing away. But cling to the things that are eternal, and you will live forever.

**5** The teachings I give you are a treasure. Guard them in your heart, and they will bring you life.

**6** For wisdom is more precious than gold, and understanding is better than silver. He who finds wisdom finds life.

**7** Cast away from you all evil thoughts, and fill your mind with good. For as a man thinks in his heart, so is he.

**8** Guard your heart above all things, for out of it are the issues of life. And guard your tongue, for life and death are in the power of the tongue.

**9** Walk in the light of Christ, and you will not stumble. For he is the light of the world, and whoever follows him will not walk in darkness.

**10** These are the teachings of Silvanus, written for those who seek the truth. May the grace of Christ be with you. Amen.
