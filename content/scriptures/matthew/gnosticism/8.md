## Healing as Liberation from Archonic Power

Gnostic interpreters read Jesus' healing miracles in Matthew 8 as acts of liberation from the archons' power over the material body. Disease and disability are understood not merely as physical conditions but as manifestations of the archons' control over matter. Jesus' healings demonstrate the superiority of the Pleromatic power over the Demiurge's domain — the divine light dispelling material darkness.

## The Centurion and Spiritual Authority

The centurion's recognition of Jesus' authority (8:5-13) is read in Gnostic terms as a recognition of spiritual hierarchy. Just as the centurion commands soldiers by delegated authority, so Jesus commands the archonic forces by the authority of the true Father. The centurion's faith, as a Gentile and outsider, may represent the *pneumatikoi* — those who recognize divine truth regardless of their ethnic or religious background.

## The Storm and Cosmic Chaos

The stilling of the storm (8:23-27) is interpreted as Jesus demonstrating power over the chaotic elements of the material world — the primordial waters and winds that the Demiurge fashioned but cannot fully control. The disciples' fear represents the condition of the unregenerate soul, still enslaved to the material world's terrors. Jesus' calm mastery reveals the Pleromatic Christ's transcendence over all created powers.
