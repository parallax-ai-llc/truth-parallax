---
title: "Second Treatise of the Great Seth - Chapter 4"
scripture: "second-treatise-of-great-seth"
chapter: 4
---

**1** Now I have taught you, my brothers, about the plan of the archons and the error of their teaching, and the mystery of the economy of salvation.

**2** He who has been sent from above has revealed to us what is from the beginning, and what is from the end, and what the rest will be.

**3** We know him. We have believed that he came from the Unbegotten One, the Father of the All, the true God, who exists from the beginning.

**4** He it is who was called by the great name, the only one in the Father. He came from the Imperishable, who is not known to the powers.

**5** And they did not recognize him. They did not know who he was. But now, through the revelation, all this has been made known.

**6** And the thought of the Father was not void, but His Son came forth from Him. From the thought of the Father, the Christ was born.

**7** He it is who gives strength to those who seek truth, who gives light to those who dwell in darkness, who gives life to those who are dead in the world.

**8** From henceforth, O house of Jacob, come and let us walk in the light of the Lord, for it is His grace and His goodness that we have received.

**9** And his purpose is set forth: that we should know the truth, and the truth shall make us free, free from the error of the archons and from the blindness of the world.

**10** This is the Second Treatise of the Great Seth. Written in peace. Holy, holy, holy. Amen.
