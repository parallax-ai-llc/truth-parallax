---
title: "Second Treatise of the Great Seth - Chapter 1"
scripture: "second-treatise-of-great-seth"
chapter: 1
---

**1** The perfect Greatness is at rest in the ineffable Light, in the truth of the Mother of all these, and all of you that attain to me, to me alone who am perfect, because of the Word.

**2** For I exist with all the greatness of the Spirit, which is a friend to us and our kindred alike, since I brought forth a word to the glory of our Father, through His goodness.

**3** He had a thought and it became a work, and it appeared, giving praise and glory. For he is self-begotten, and he is God. He it is whom I praised.

**4** For he exists in the greatness and the fullness that encompasses everything, and praise and glory and power are his from all eternity.

**5** I visited a bodily dwelling. I cast out the one who was in it first, and I went in. And the whole multitude of the archons became troubled.

**6** All the matter of the archons was shaken, as were all the principalities and powers of the earth. For the whole world was disturbed and frightened.

**7** The rulers did not know who I was. They thought their creation was supreme, but they did not know the Father who is above all.

**8** I am Christ, the Son of Man, who is above the heavens. I am he who was called by the great name.

**9** I did not succumb to them as they had planned. And I did not suffer at all. Those who were there punished me, yet I did not die in reality but in appearance.

**10** For my death which they think happened, happened to them in their error and blindness, since they nailed their man unto their death.
