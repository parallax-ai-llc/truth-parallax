## Overview

For Gnostic readers, the Prayer of the Apostle Paul reveals the devotional heart of Valentinian Christianity. Inscribed on the front flyleaf of Codex I, this short prayer may have served as a protective invocation for the entire collection. The prayer addresses Christ as 'my Redeemer' and 'my Psychagogue' (guide of souls) — titles that reflect the Valentinian understanding of Christ as the one who leads the soul from the material world back to the Pleroma. The prayer's intimacy and urgency demonstrate that Valentinian theology was not merely intellectual speculation but the foundation of a living devotional practice.
