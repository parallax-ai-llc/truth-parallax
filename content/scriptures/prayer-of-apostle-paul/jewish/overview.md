## Overview

Jewish readers find in the Prayer of the Apostle Paul parallels to Jewish prayer forms, particularly the use of divine names and attributes as the basis for petition. The prayer's invocation of Christ through multiple titles parallels the Jewish practice of invoking God through multiple names (Adonai, El Shaddai, YHWH Tzevaot) and attributes. The tradition of placing a prayer or sacred text at the beginning of a collection parallels the Jewish practice of beginning sacred study with a berakhah (blessing).
