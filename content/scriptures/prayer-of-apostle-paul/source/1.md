---
title: "Prayer of Apostle Paul - Chapter 1"
scripture: "prayer-of-apostle-paul"
chapter: 1
---

**1** My Redeemer, redeem me, for I am Thine: from Thee have I come forth. Thou art my mind; bring me forth! Thou art my treasure house; open for me! Thou art my fullness; take me to Thee!

**2** Thou art my repose; give me the perfect thing that cannot be grasped! I invoke Thee, the one who is and who pre-existed in the name which is exalted above every name, through Jesus Christ, the Lord of Lords, the King of the Ages.

**3** Give me Thy gifts, on which Thou dost not repent, through the Son of Man, the Spirit, the Paraclete of Truth. Give me authority when I ask Thee; give healing for my body when I ask Thee through the Evangelist, and redeem my eternal light soul and my spirit.

**4** And the First-born of the Pleroma of grace — reveal him to my mind! Grant what no angel eye has seen and no archon ear has heard, and what has not entered into the human heart which came to be angelic and came to be after the image of the psychic God when it was formed.

**5** I have faith and hope. And place upon me Thy beloved, elect, and blessed greatness, the First-born, the First-begotten, and the wonderful mystery of Thy house; for Thine is the power and the glory and the praise and the greatness for ever and ever. Amen.
