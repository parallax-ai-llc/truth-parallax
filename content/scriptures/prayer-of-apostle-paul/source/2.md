---
title: "Prayer of Apostle Paul Section 2"
scripture: "prayer-of-apostle-paul"
chapter: 2
---

**1** Grant what no angel eye has seen and no archon ear has heard, and what has not entered into the human heart — the angelic beings who came to be after the image of the psychic God when it was formed.

**2** I have believed in Thee through Thy Son Jesus Christ, who was born of the Virgin Mary by the will of the Father of Truth. I call upon Thee, who art and who pre-existed, in the name of Jesus Christ.

**3** Thou art the one who was, who is, and who shall be, the unchangeable one, the immovable one, who is established in the true Fatherhood.

**4** In the name of the Father, by the power of the Son, and through the blessing of the Spirit, I lift my prayer to the heights above every name and every power.

**5** I ask not for the things of this world which perish, but for the imperishable treasures of the Pleroma, that Thy fullness might descend upon me.

**6** For Thine is the power and the glory, the blessing and the greatness, now and in all the Aeons of Aeons. Amen.

**7** The Prayer of the Apostle Paul. In peace. Christ is holy.
