Comparative interpreters note parallels between the Sutrakritanga's refutation of materialism and similar critiques in Buddhist texts (rejection of annihilationism), Hindu philosophy (critique of Charvaka), and Western philosophy (Platonic critique of sophistry). The universal pattern of religious traditions defining themselves against materialist alternatives is clearly illustrated.
