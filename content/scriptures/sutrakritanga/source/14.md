---
title: "Sutrakritanga - Chapter 14"
scripture: "sutrakritanga"
chapter: 14
---

**1** Liberation is the highest goal of human life. All other goals are secondary.

**2** Liberation is not a place to which one goes. It is a state that one attains. It is the natural state of the soul.

**3** In liberation, the soul possesses infinite knowledge. It knows everything — past, present, and future — simultaneously.

**4** In liberation, the soul possesses infinite perception. It perceives everything directly, without the mediation of the senses.

**5** In liberation, the soul possesses infinite bliss. This bliss is not dependent on any external object. It is the soul's own nature.

**6** In liberation, the soul possesses infinite energy. Nothing can obstruct it. Nothing can limit it.

**7** The liberated soul is free from birth, death, disease, old age, and all forms of suffering.

**8** The liberated soul does not return to the cycle of birth and death. Its liberation is permanent.

**9** The path to liberation is the path of right faith, right knowledge, and right conduct.

**10** He who walks this path with sincerity and devotion will surely reach the goal, whether in this life or in future lives.

**11** The Jinas have shown the way. They have walked the path. They have reached the goal. They beckon to all beings to follow.

**12** May all beings attain liberation. May all beings be free from suffering. This is the teaching of the Jinas.
