---
title: "Sutrakritanga - Chapter 6"
scripture: "sutrakritanga"
chapter: 6
---

**1** One should know the distinction between the soul and the body. The body is material, the soul is conscious.

**2** The body is born and dies. The soul is eternal. It was never born and will never die.

**3** The body is subject to disease, old age, and decay. The soul is free from all these.

**4** The body is the house in which the soul dwells. When the house collapses, the soul moves to another house.

**5** The wise man identifies himself with the soul, not with the body. The ignorant man identifies himself with the body.

**6** He who identifies with the body experiences pleasure and pain. He who identifies with the soul is beyond pleasure and pain.

**7** The soul is pure consciousness. It is self-luminous. It does not need any external means to know itself.

**8** Just as a lamp illumines itself and other objects, so the soul illumines itself and the body it inhabits.

**9** The soul is the knower, the experiencer, and the doer. It is responsible for its own happiness and suffering.

**10** No one else can liberate the soul. The soul must liberate itself through its own efforts.

**11** Right faith, right knowledge, and right conduct are the means of self-liberation.

**12** He who practises these three attains liberation. He becomes free from all suffering forever.
