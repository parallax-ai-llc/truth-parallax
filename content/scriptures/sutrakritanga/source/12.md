---
title: "Sutrakritanga - Chapter 12"
scripture: "sutrakritanga"
chapter: 12
---

**1** The truth taught by the Jinas is this: the soul is eternal, karma is real, and liberation is possible.

**2** Those who deny the existence of the soul are wrong. The soul is self-evident. It is the knower, the experiencer, the doer.

**3** Those who deny the reality of karma are wrong. The law of karma is universal. Every action has its consequence.

**4** Those who deny the possibility of liberation are wrong. Liberation has been attained by countless souls. It can be attained by all.

**5** The truth is many-sided. No single viewpoint can capture the whole truth. This is the doctrine of many-sidedness.

**6** From one perspective, a thing exists. From another perspective, it does not exist. From a third perspective, it both exists and does not exist. From a fourth perspective, it is indescribable.

**7** The wise man sees all perspectives. He does not cling to any one viewpoint. He sees the truth in its fullness.

**8** The foolish man clings to one viewpoint. He denies all other viewpoints. He sees only a part of the truth.

**9** Therefore, cultivate the vision of many-sidedness. See things from all perspectives. Understand things in their fullness.

**10** This is the way to right knowledge. This is the way to right faith. This is the way to liberation.

**11** The Jinas have seen the truth from all perspectives. They have attained omniscience. They teach the complete truth.

**12** He who follows their teaching attains liberation. This is certain.
