---
title: "Sutrakritanga - Chapter 16"
scripture: "sutrakritanga"
chapter: 16
---

**1** This is the summary of the teaching: Non-violence is the supreme religion. Truth is the supreme virtue. Self-restraint is the supreme discipline.

**2** He who practises non-violence in thought, word, and deed is a true follower of the Jinas.

**3** He who speaks the truth at all times, regardless of consequences, is a true follower of the Jinas.

**4** He who controls his mind, his speech, and his body is a true follower of the Jinas.

**5** He who is free from anger, pride, deceit, and greed is a true follower of the Jinas.

**6** He who practises the five vows — non-violence, truth, non-stealing, chastity, and non-attachment — is a true follower of the Jinas.

**7** He who practises austerity and meditation, who studies the scriptures and serves the teachers, is a true follower of the Jinas.

**8** He who sees all beings as equal, who does not discriminate between high and low, rich and poor, is a true follower of the Jinas.

**9** He who has faith in the teachings of the Jinas, who has knowledge of the truth, and who conducts himself rightly, is on the path to liberation.

**10** The path is long but the goal is certain. The journey is difficult but the destination is glorious.

**11** Many have walked this path before. Many have reached the goal. The Jinas are the proof that liberation is possible.

**12** May all beings find the path. May all beings walk the path. May all beings reach the goal. This is the final teaching of the Sutrakritanga.
