---
title: "Sutrakritanga - Chapter 2"
scripture: "sutrakritanga"
chapter: 2
---

**1** There are some who, on account of their wrong belief, contend that the world is eternal, and that there is no such thing as liberation.

**2** There are others who contend that the world was created by God, and that liberation is attainable only through His grace.

**3** There are others who say that everything is determined by fate, and that there is no use of human effort.

**4** There are others who say that there is no soul, no future life, and that death is the end.

**5** All these views are wrong. The truth is this: the soul exists, karma exists, and liberation exists.

**6** The soul is bound by its own karma. It is freed by the destruction of karma.

**7** Neither God nor fate determines one's destiny. One's own actions determine one's destiny.

**8** He who does good deeds reaps good results. He who does evil deeds reaps evil results. This is the law of karma.

**9** The wise man, knowing this, practises non-violence, truth, non-stealing, chastity, and non-attachment.

**10** He who practises these five vows is freed from all bondage. He attains liberation.

**11** This is the teaching of the Jinas, the omniscient ones. It is the truth.

**12** He who follows this teaching crosses the ocean of suffering and reaches the shore of liberation.
