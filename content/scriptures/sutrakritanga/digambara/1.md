The Digambara tradition reads the opening chapter's refutation of hedonism and materialism as establishing the absolute necessity of the Jain path. The systematic dismantling of rival doctrines demonstrates that only the teaching of the Tirthankaras leads to truth. Digambara commentators emphasize the chapter's logical rigor, connecting its arguments to the later Digambara tradition of philosophical debate represented by Akalanka and Vidyananda.
