For the Shvetambara tradition, the Sutrakritanga's opening chapter establishes the framework for distinguishing true dharma from false teaching. The refutation of materialism and hedonism protects the community from doctrinal error and affirms the authority of Mahavira's teaching as transmitted through the Agamic tradition.
