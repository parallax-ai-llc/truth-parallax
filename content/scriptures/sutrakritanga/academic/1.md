Academic scholars analyze the first chapter's polemical content to identify the rival philosophical schools active during the text's composition. References to materialists (Lokayata) and hedonists provide evidence for the intellectual landscape of ancient India. Philological study of the Ardhamagadhi text reveals layers of composition.
