---
title: "Gospel of Judas — Section 3: The Cosmology"
scripture: "gospel-of-judas"
chapter: 3
---

**1** Judas said, "Master, as you have listened to all of them, now also listen to me. For I have seen a great vision." When Jesus heard this, he laughed and said to him, "You thirteenth spirit, why do you try so hard? But speak up, and I shall bear with you."

**2** Judas said to him, "In the vision I saw myself as the twelve disciples were stoning me and persecuting me severely. And I also came to the place where I saw you. I saw a house there, and my eyes could not comprehend its size. Great people were surrounding it, and that house had a roof of greenery, and in the middle of the house was a crowd."

**3** "Master, take me in along with these people." Jesus answered and said, "Judas, your star has led you astray. No person of mortal birth is worthy to enter the house you have seen, for that place is reserved for the holy."

**4** "Neither the sun nor the moon will rule there, nor the day, but the holy will abide there always, in the eternal realm with the holy angels."

**5** "Look, I have explained to you the mysteries of the kingdom and I have taught you about the error of the stars; and I have sent it on the twelve aeons."

**6** Jesus said, "Come, that I may teach you about secrets no person has ever seen. For there exists a great and boundless realm, whose extent no generation of angels has seen, in which there is a great invisible Spirit, which no eye of an angel has ever seen, no thought of the heart has ever comprehended, and it was never called by any name."

**7** "And a luminous cloud appeared there. He said, 'Let an angel come into being as my attendant.' A great angel, the enlightened divine Self-Generated, emerged from the cloud."

**8** "And the Self-Generated God created twelve angels to attend him, and he said to them, 'Let twelve aeons come into being to rule over chaos and the underworld.' And the twelve aeons were created."
