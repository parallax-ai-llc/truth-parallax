---
title: "Gospel of Judas — Section 4: The Betrayal and Conclusion"
scripture: "gospel-of-judas"
chapter: 4
---

**1** Jesus said, "Truly I say to you, for all of them the stars bring matters to completion. When Saklas completes the span of time assigned for him, their first star will appear with the generations, and they will finish what they said they would do."

**2** "Then they will fornicate in my name and slay their children, and they will do all sorts of things in my name. And your star will rule over the thirteenth aeon."

**3** And after that Jesus laughed. Judas said, "Master, why are you laughing at us?" Jesus answered and said, "I am not laughing at you but at the error of the stars, because these six stars wander about with these five combatants, and they all will be destroyed along with their creatures."

**4** Judas said to Jesus, "Look, what will those who have been baptized in your name do?" Jesus said, "Truly I say to you, this baptism in my name will destroy the whole generation of the faithless. Truly I say to you, Judas, those who offer sacrifices to Saklas — everything that is evil."

**5** "But you will exceed all of them. For you will sacrifice the man that clothes me."

**6** "Already your horn has been raised, your wrath has been kindled, your star has shown brightly, and your heart has become strong."

**7** "Truly I say to you, your last days are coming. Lift up your eyes and look at the cloud and the light within it and the stars surrounding it. The star that leads the way is your star."

**8** Judas lifted up his eyes and saw the luminous cloud, and he entered it. Those standing on the ground heard a voice coming from the cloud.

**9** Their high priests murmured because he had gone into the guest room for his prayer. But some scribes were there watching carefully in order to arrest him during the prayer, for they were afraid of the people, since he was regarded by all as a prophet.

**10** They approached Judas and said to him, "What are you doing here? You are Jesus' disciple." Judas answered them as they wished. And he received some money and handed him over to them.

**11** The Gospel of Judas.
