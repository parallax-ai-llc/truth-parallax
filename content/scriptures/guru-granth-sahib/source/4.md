---
title: "Rehras Sahib - Evening Prayer"
scripture: "guru-granth-sahib"
chapter: 4
---

**1** Rehras Sahib is the evening prayer, recited at sunset. It is a collection of hymns from several Gurus.

**2** O my body, the Lord infused His Light into you, and then you came into the world. The Lord infused His Light into you and then you were given birth.

**3** The Lord Himself is immersed in all. From Him, all creatures have come forth. Those who are blessed by His grace find the True Guru.

**4** You are the river of all. I do not know anyone else. O Lord, You create and You behold. What other may I address?

**5** O Lord, I have come to Your sanctuary. Teach me, O Lord, that I may serve You. My body and mind are all Yours.

**6** O Lord, all creatures are Yours, and You are for all creatures. Whom shall I blame, when all belongs to You?

**7** How sweet is Your Name! Through Your Name, I have obtained total peace. In the Saints' company, I sing the Glorious Praises of the Lord. Nanak, the Lord is my Protector.
