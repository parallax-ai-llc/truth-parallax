---
title: "Sukhmani - The Psalm of Peace"
scripture: "guru-granth-sahib"
chapter: 2
---

**1** Sukhmani, composed by Guru Arjan Dev, the fifth Guru, is the Psalm of Peace. It brings tranquility to the mind and liberation to the soul.

**2** O God, the Merciful, the Compassionate, the Almighty, I seek Your shelter. I do not know Your limit. You are the highest of the high.

**3** Remembering God, one does not have to enter the womb again. Remembering God, the fear of death is dispelled. Remembering God, death itself is vanquished.

**4** Remembering God, enemies are repelled. Remembering God, no obstacles are met. Remembering God, one is awake day and night. Remembering God, fear does not trouble.

**5** Remembering God, sorrow does not afflict. The remembrance of God takes place in the company of the saints.

**6** All treasures, the eighteen supernatural powers, are held in the palm of the Lord's hand. Whatever He wishes, comes to pass. No one can change His decree.

**7** He is the ruler of the universe, the Lord of the world. O Nanak, meditate on Him, the Creator.
