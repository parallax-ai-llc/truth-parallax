---
title: "Kirtan Sohila - Bedtime Prayer"
scripture: "guru-granth-sahib"
chapter: 5
---

**1** Kirtan Sohila, the Song of Praise, is recited at bedtime and at funeral ceremonies. It speaks of the soul's departure from the world.

**2** In the house in which the Lord's praise is sung and the Lord is contemplated, in that house sing the songs of praise and remember the Creator.

**3** Sing the songs of praise of my fearless Lord. I am a sacrifice to that song of praise which brings everlasting peace.

**4** Day after day, He cares for His beings. The Giver of blessings thinks of us all. Your gifts cannot be appraised; how can the Giver be appraised?

**5** The day of my wedding is set. Come, friends, and pour the oil of blessing on my threshold. Bless me, my friends, that I may merge with the Lord.

**6** In each and every home, this summons is sent. Each day, the call is issued. Remember the One who summons you. O Nanak, that day is drawing near.

**7** Six houses, six masters, six teachings. But the Master of masters is one, though His appearances are many. O Nanak, that home in which the Master is praised, that home is beautiful.
