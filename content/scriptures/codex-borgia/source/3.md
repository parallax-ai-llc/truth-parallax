title: "Codex Borgia Section 3"
scripture: "codex-borgia"
chapter: 3
---

**1** The great cosmological pages of the codex depict the four directions and their associated deities, colors, trees, and birds. Each direction is a pillar of the universe.

**2** To the East stands the red Tezcatlipoca, associated with the color red, the ceiba tree, and the quetzal bird. The East is the direction of dawn, of new beginnings, and of fertility.

**3** To the North stands the black Tezcatlipoca, associated with the color black, the mesquite tree, and the eagle. The North is the direction of the dead, of cold, and of sacrifice.

**4** To the West stands Quetzalcoatl, associated with the color white, the willow tree, and the humming bird. The West is the direction of sunset, of the feminine, and of rain.

**5** To the South stands Huitzilopochtli, associated with the color blue, the thorn tree, and the parrot. The South is the direction of warmth, of growth, and of war.

**6** At the center stands the Lord and Lady of Duality, Ometeotl, the supreme principle from which all the gods emanate, the force that holds the four directions in balance.
