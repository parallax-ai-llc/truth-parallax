**1** The pages depicting the body and its associations show the twenty day signs mapped onto parts of the human body, for the body is a microcosm of the universe.

**2** Cipactli governs the liver. Ehecatl governs the lungs. Calli governs the temples. Cuetzpalin governs the ribs. Coatl governs the genitals.

**3** Miquiztli governs the skull. Mazatl governs the right foot. Tochtli governs the left ear. Atl governs the breast. Itzcuintli governs the nose.

**4** Ozomatli governs the left arm. Malinalli governs the intestines. Acatl governs the right arm. Ocelotl governs the left foot. Cuauhtli governs the right ear.

**5** Cozcacuauhtli governs the eyes. Ollin governs the tongue. Tecpatl governs the teeth. Quiahuitl governs the brain. Xochitl governs the heart.

**6** The healers and diviners use these correspondences to diagnose illness and prescribe cures, for a disease that strikes a particular body part reveals which day sign is afflicted.
