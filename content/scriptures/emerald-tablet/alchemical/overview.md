## Overview

The Alchemical reading of the Emerald Tablet approaches the text as the foundational charter of the alchemical art. Alchemists from Jabir ibn Hayyan to Isaac Newton have regarded the Tablet as encoding, in symbolic language, the practical operations of the Great Work — the purification, separation, and recombination of matter in pursuit of the philosopher's stone. The axiom 'As above, so below' becomes, in this reading, a statement about the correspondence between laboratory operations and cosmic processes.
