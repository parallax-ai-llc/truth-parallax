## Overview

The Hermetic reading of the Emerald Tablet approaches this text as the supreme condensation of Hermetic wisdom, attributed directly to Hermes Trismegistus. Within the Hermetic tradition, the Tablet is not merely a philosophical statement but a practical guide to the Great Work — the transmutation of the self and the cosmos. The axiom 'As above, so below' encapsulates the Hermetic doctrine of correspondence, linking the practitioner's inner transformation to the cosmic process of creation and return.
