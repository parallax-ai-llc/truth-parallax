## Overview

The Comparative reading of the Emerald Tablet examines the text's themes of correspondence, transmutation, and cosmic unity alongside parallel teachings in other traditions. The principle of 'As above, so below' finds resonance in the Taoist doctrine of correspondence between heaven and earth, in Kabbalistic teachings on the sefirot, and in Indian tantric notions of the body as microcosm. This approach illuminates the Tablet as an expression of a perennial philosophical insight shared across cultures.
