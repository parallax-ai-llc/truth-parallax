## Overview

The Interfaith reading of the Emerald Tablet approaches the text as a point of convergence for multiple religious and philosophical traditions. The Tablet's themes of cosmic unity, correspondence, and transmutation have been embraced by Islamic alchemists, Christian mystics, Jewish Kabbalists, and modern esotericists alike. This reading explores the Tablet as a bridge text — a document whose compressed wisdom has facilitated dialogue across traditions and centuries.
