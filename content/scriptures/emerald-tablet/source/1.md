---
title: "Emerald Tablet - Chapter 1"
scripture: "emerald-tablet"
chapter: 1
---

**1** True it is, without falsehood, certain and most true.

**2** That which is above is like to that which is below, and that which is below is like to that which is above, to accomplish the miracles of one thing.

**3** And as all things were from one, by the mediation of one, so all things arose from this one thing by adaptation.

**4** The father of it is the Sun, the mother of it is the Moon; the Wind carried it in its belly; the nurse thereof is the Earth.

**5** This is the father of all perfection, or consummation of the whole world.

**6** The power of it is integral, if it be turned into earth.

**7** Thou shalt separate the earth from the fire, the subtle from the gross, gently, with much sagacity.

**8** It ascends from the earth to the heaven, and again descends to the earth, and receives the power of the superiors and of the inferiors.

**9** So thou hast the glory of the whole world; therefore let all obscurity flee before thee.

**10** This is the strong fortitude of all fortitude, overcoming every subtle and penetrating every solid thing.

**11** So the world was created.

**12** Hence were all wonderful adaptations, of which this is the manner.

**13** Therefore am I called Hermes Trismegistus, having the three parts of the philosophy of the whole world.

**14** What I have said of the operation of the Sun is complete.
