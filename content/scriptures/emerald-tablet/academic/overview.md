## Overview

The Academic study of the Emerald Tablet situates this enigmatic text within the history of alchemy, Hermeticism, and the transmission of knowledge between the Islamic world and medieval Europe. Scholars trace the Tablet's origins to Arabic alchemical literature of the sixth to eighth centuries CE, examining its attribution to Balinas (Apollonius of Tyana), its incorporation into the Hermetic corpus, and its enormous influence on Western natural philosophy and esotericism.
