The divine promise of progeny to a childless figure resonates profoundly across Abrahamic traditions:
- **Judaism**: God's covenant with Abraham — 'Look toward heaven and count the stars... so shall your descendants be' (Genesis 15:5)
- **Christianity**: The Annunciation — Gabriel's promise to Mary of a son who will reign forever (Luke 1:31-33)
- **Islam**: The promise of a son to Ibrahim and Sarah (Quran 11:69-73); the promise to Zakariya (Quran 19:7)

The motif of the unfulfilled vow adds a cautionary dimension found across traditions: religious obligations, once undertaken, must be honored. The consequences of broken vows are a theme in Jewish, Christian, Islamic, and Hindu ethical teaching.
