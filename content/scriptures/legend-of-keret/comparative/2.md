The march to obtain a bride and the subsequent divine blessing of progeny parallel several biblical and Near Eastern narratives:
- **Abraham's journey**: A divinely directed journey that leads to covenant promises of progeny
- **Jacob's journey to Laban**: A patriarch traveling to obtain a wife from a distant land
- **The siege of Troy**: A military expedition motivated by the desire to obtain (or recover) a woman

Keret's unfulfilled vow to Athirat parallels Jephthah's vow (Judges 11) and introduces the dangerous motif of the rash vow — a promise made to a deity in a moment of crisis that later exacts an unexpected price.
