## Overview

The Prose Edda's systematic presentation of Norse mythology makes it exceptionally valuable for comparative study. Snorri's account of creation from the void of Ginnungagap, the world tree Yggdrasil, the three-tiered cosmos, and the final battle of Ragnarök provides well-structured mythological narratives that can be compared with creation myths, cosmic geography, and eschatological visions from traditions worldwide — from Vedic and Greek to Mesopotamian and Mesoamerican.
