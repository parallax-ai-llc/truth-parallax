## Overview

The Prose Edda is a remarkable document of interfaith engagement — a Christian author's sympathetic yet critical preservation of a pre-Christian religious tradition. Snorri's approach offers a model for how adherents of one faith can engage respectfully with the sacred narratives of another. The text also raises important questions about the relationship between mythology and theology, the nature of religious truth, and the possibility of finding spiritual wisdom in traditions outside one's own.
