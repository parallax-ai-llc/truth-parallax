---
title: "Taiping Jing - Chapter 7"
scripture: "taiping-jing"
chapter: 7
---

**1** The Master of Great Peace continued speaking on the nourishment of life: Heaven and Earth operate through the harmony of yin and yang.

**2** When yin and yang are in balance, all things prosper. When they are out of balance, all things decline.

**3** The sage understands this principle and governs accordingly. He does not favour one over the other but maintains the balance.

**4** The five elements — wood, fire, earth, metal, and water — are the manifestations of this balance. Each has its proper place and season.

**5** When the five elements are in harmony, the seasons follow their proper course, the rain falls at the right time, and the harvest is abundant.

**6** The body of man is a microcosm of heaven and earth. The same principles that govern the universe govern the body.

**7** He who cultivates his body according to the Tao will live long. He who violates the Tao will die young.

**8** The cultivation of virtue is the foundation of long life. The practice of benevolence, righteousness, propriety, wisdom, and faithfulness brings harmony to the body and peace to the mind.

**9** The Master said: "He who attains Great Peace within himself attains Great Peace in the world. The inner and the outer are one."

**10** "Therefore, cultivate yourself first. Then your family will be harmonious. Then your state will be well governed. Then the whole world will be at peace."
