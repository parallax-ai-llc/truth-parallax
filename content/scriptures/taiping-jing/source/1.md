---
title: "Taiping Jing - Chapter 1"
scripture: "taiping-jing"
chapter: 1
---

**1** The Master of Great Peace spoke: The Way of Heaven and Earth is the root of the ten thousand things. From it all things proceed and to it all things return.

**2** When Heaven and Earth are in harmony, the myriad creatures flourish. When Heaven and Earth are in discord, calamity befalls all beings.

**3** The great Tao gives birth to all things, nourishes them, brings them to maturity, protects them, gives them peace, feeds them, and shelters them.

**4** It gives birth to them without possessing them; it acts without expecting gratitude; it guides without controlling. This is called the Mysterious Virtue.

**5** The way to achieve Great Peace is through the harmony of the three forces: Heaven, Earth, and Humanity. When these three are in accord, the world is at peace.

**6** Heaven has its seasons; Earth has its resources; Humanity has its duties. When each fulfils its proper function, Great Peace prevails.

**7** The sage ruler governs by the Tao. He does not impose his will upon the people but allows each to follow his own nature.

**8** When the ruler is virtuous, the ministers are loyal. When the ministers are loyal, the people are content. When the people are content, the land is at peace.

**9** The root of disorder is the violation of the Tao. When the ruler is unjust, the ministers are treacherous. When the ministers are treacherous, the people are rebellious.

**10** Therefore the sage says: Return to the Tao, and Great Peace will be achieved. Abandon the Tao, and Great Disorder will follow.
