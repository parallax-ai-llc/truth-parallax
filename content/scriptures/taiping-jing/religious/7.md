Religious Taoism receives Section 7, 'Communication Between Heaven and Earth,' as revealed scripture with direct implications for ritual practice, community organization, and eschatological expectation. The teachings contained in this section have shaped Taoist liturgy, social vision, and spiritual practice from the second century CE to the present, contributing to the distinctive character of communal Taoism as distinct from the individual cultivation emphasized in earlier philosophical texts.

The religious tradition reads this section's teachings as divinely revealed instructions for bringing about the Great Peace (taiping) that is both the text's central promise and the ultimate goal of Taoist practice. The section's specific prescriptions — for ritual, meditation, healing, social organization, or cosmic alignment — are received as practical guidance for practitioners working toward this transformative vision.
