## On Cosmology

The Digambara tradition reads this the structure of the cosmos with characteristic doctrinal strictness, emphasizing the necessity of complete renunciation and the precise application of Jain principles. Digambara interpreters stress that the structure of the cosmos must be understood within the framework of absolute non-attachment and rigorous ascetic practice that defines the Digambara path.
