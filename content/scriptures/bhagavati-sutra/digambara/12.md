## On the Tirthankaras

From the Digambara perspective, this teaching on the lives and teachings of the Tirthankaras reinforces the tradition's emphasis on uncompromising spiritual discipline. The Digambara commentarial tradition elaborates these teachings with philosophical precision, connecting them to the broader framework of strict karma theory and the path of total renunciation.
