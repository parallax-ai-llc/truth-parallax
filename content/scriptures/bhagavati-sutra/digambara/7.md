## On Ethics

The Digambara tradition reads this ethical conduct and its consequences with characteristic doctrinal strictness, emphasizing the necessity of complete renunciation and the precise application of Jain principles. Digambara interpreters stress that ethical conduct and its consequences must be understood within the framework of absolute non-attachment and rigorous ascetic practice that defines the Digambara path.
