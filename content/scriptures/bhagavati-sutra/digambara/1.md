## On the Soul

The Digambara tradition reads this the nature and states of the soul with characteristic doctrinal strictness, emphasizing the necessity of complete renunciation and the precise application of Jain principles. Digambara interpreters stress that the nature and states of the soul must be understood within the framework of absolute non-attachment and rigorous ascetic practice that defines the Digambara path.
