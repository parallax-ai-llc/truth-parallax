## On Knowledge

The Digambara tradition reads this types of knowledge and perception with characteristic doctrinal strictness, emphasizing the necessity of complete renunciation and the precise application of Jain principles. Digambara interpreters stress that types of knowledge and perception must be understood within the framework of absolute non-attachment and rigorous ascetic practice that defines the Digambara path.
