---
title: "Gospel of the Egyptians (Gnostic) Section 1"
scripture: "gospel-of-the-egyptians-gnostic"
chapter: 1
---

**1** The holy book of the Egyptians about the great invisible Spirit, the Father whose name cannot be uttered, he who came forth from the heights of the perfection, the light of the light of the aeons of light, the light of the silence of the providence and the Father of silence, the light of the word and the truth, the light of the incorruptions.

**2** The infinite light, the radiance from the aeons of light of the unmanifest, unmarked, ageless, unproclaimable Father, the aeon of the aeons, self-begotten, self-conceived, foreign, the truly immeasurable aeon.

**3** Three powers came forth from him; they are the Father, the Mother, and the Son, from the living silence, what came forth from the incorruptible Father. These came forth from the silence of the unknown Father.

**4** And from that place, Domedon Doxomedon came forth, the aeon of the aeons and the light of each one of their powers. And thus the Son came forth fourth; the Mother came forth fifth; the Father came forth sixth.

**5** He was unproclaimable, unmarked, inaccessible, having all the greatnesses, having all the majesties, and he was proclaimed by the power which is the Mother of all things.

**6** The Father of the light of everything, who came forth from the silence, rested in the silence, he whose name is in an invisible symbol. A hidden, invisible mystery came forth.

**7** And the silence of the great invisible Spirit was established: the three great powers, the incorruptible Father, the Mother, and the Son.

**8** From the great, invisible, virginal Spirit and the Barbelo—the male virgin Barbelo, the glory, the three-powered one, the three-named, the three-begotten, the aeon that does not grow old—there came forth the great Doxomedon.

**9** And the throne of his glory was established in it, and his unrevealable name is inscribed on the tablet, the word of the Father, the aeon of the aeons.

**10** The three males, the three male children, the three male races are established in the incorruptible one, the holy seed of the great Seth.
