---
title: "Gospel of the Egyptians (Gnostic) Section 3"
scripture: "gospel-of-the-egyptians-gnostic"
chapter: 3
---

**1** Then the great Seth was sent by the four lights, by the will of the Autogenes and the whole pleroma, through the gift and the good pleasure of the great invisible Spirit, and through the five seals.

**2** He passed through the three parousias. The first parousia was the flood. The second parousia was the conflagration. And the third parousia is the coming of the great Seth himself, the incorruptible seed.

**3** In the first parousia, the world of the archon of this aeon was overwhelmed. And a great cloud overshadowed the earth, and the race of Seth was protected.

**4** For the great angel Hormos came to prepare, through the virgins of the corrupt generation of this great aeon, a dwelling place worthy of him, through the holy spirit, the seed of the great Seth.

**5** Then the great Seth came and brought his seed. And it was sown in the aeons which had been brought forth, their number being the amount of the sojourn of the great Seth.

**6** Phoitale came and prepared for them a holy dwelling and a holy baptism. And the great Seth was baptized through the logogenous one, the one who was begotten by the word, through the five seals.

**7** And through the incorruptible man Poimael, for the sake of the five seals and the whole pleroma, he established the holy baptism which surpasses every power.

**8** Then the great Seth rejoiced, for he saw the race of the perfect. He saw the holy seed from the incorruptible seed, established forever, through the great invisible Spirit and the five seals.

**9** And the protector of the great Seth is the great angel who stands in the presence of the Autogenes and the four luminaries.

**10** And the race of Seth shall endure until the consummation of the age, when the garments of light are given to the children of the great invisible Spirit.
