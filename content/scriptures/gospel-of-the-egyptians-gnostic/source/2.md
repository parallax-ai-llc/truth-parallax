---
title: "Gospel of the Egyptians (Gnostic) Section 2"
scripture: "gospel-of-the-egyptians-gnostic"
chapter: 2
---

**1** Then the great self-begotten living God, the Adamas of light, the one whom the great invisible Spirit and the male virgin Barbelo and the great Protennoia approved, whom the great invisible Spirit anointed—the Adamas stood before him.

**2** And from the great Adamas came forth the great incorruptible Seth, the son of the incorruptible man Adamas.

**3** And thus was established the race of the great Seth, by the four great lights: Harmozel, Oroiael, Daveithai, and Eleleth.

**4** The great Seth was set over the first aeon with the first light Harmozel. And Gamaliel is the great angel who is set over that aeon.

**5** Over the second aeon was placed Oroiael, and with him Gabriel, the great angel. And Seth himself received his race from the second aeon.

**6** Over the third aeon was placed Daveithai, and with him Samblo, the great angel. And the souls of the saints were set in the third aeon.

**7** Over the fourth aeon was placed Eleleth, and with him Abrasax, the great angel. And those who were not known were placed in the fourth aeon, for they did not know the great invisible Spirit.

**8** Then came forth the great cloud of light, the living power, the mother of the holy, incorruptible ones, the great power, Mirothoe.

**9** And she gave birth to the one whose name I utter: EA EA EA, three times. For this one, Seth Emmacha Seth, is the one who appeared as the living Jesus, and who nailed the powers of the thirteen aeons.

**10** And he established those who are brought forth by him in faith, through the baptism and the five seals: the baptism of water, the baptism of fire, the baptism of the holy spirit, and the baptism of the light of the luminaries, and the seal of the cross.
