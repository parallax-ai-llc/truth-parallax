---
title: "Tao Te Ching - Chapter 43"
scripture: "tao-te-ching"
chapter: 43
---

**1** The softest thing in the world dashes against and overcomes the hardest; that which has no substantial existence enters where there is no crevice.

**2** I know hereby what advantage belongs to doing nothing with a purpose.

**3** There are few in the world who attain to the teaching without words, and the advantage arising from non-action.
