---
title: "Tao Te Ching - Chapter 33"
scripture: "tao-te-ching"
chapter: 33
---

**1** He who knows other men is discerning; he who knows himself is intelligent. He who overcomes others is strong; he who overcomes himself is mighty.

**2** He who is satisfied with his lot is rich; he who goes on acting with energy has a firm will.

**3** He who does not fail in the requirements of his position, continues long; he who dies and yet does not perish, has longevity.
