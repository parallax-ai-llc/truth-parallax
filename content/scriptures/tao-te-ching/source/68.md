---
title: "Tao Te Ching - Chapter 68"
scripture: "tao-te-ching"
chapter: 68
---

**1** He who in Tao's wars has skill assumes no martial port; he who fights with most good will to rage makes no resort. He who vanquishes yet still keeps himself from strife; and he whose hests men most fulfil yet humbly plies his life.

**2** Thus we say, 'He ne'er contends, and therein is his might.' Thus we say, 'Men's wills he bends, that so he may use their light.' Thus we say, 'He Heaven's compeer is found, for such has been the rule from of old, and such to him shall crown be given.'
