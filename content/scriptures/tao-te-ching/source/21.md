---
title: "Tao Te Ching - Chapter 21"
scripture: "tao-te-ching"
chapter: 21
---

**1** The grandest forms of active force from Tao come, their only source. Who can of Tao the nature tell? Our sight it flies, our touch as well. Eluding sight, eluding touch, the forms of things all in it crouch; eluding touch, eluding sight, there are their semblances, all right.

**2** Profound it is, dark and obscure; things' essences all there endure. Those essences the truth enfold of what, when verified, is told.

**3** Now it is so; 'twas so of old. Its name, what passes not away; so, in their beautiful array, things form and never know decay.

**4** How know I that it is so with all the beauties of existing things? By this nature of the Tao.
