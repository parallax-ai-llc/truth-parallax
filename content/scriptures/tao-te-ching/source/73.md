---
title: "Tao Te Ching - Chapter 73"
scripture: "tao-te-ching"
chapter: 73
---

**1** He whose boldness appears in his daring to do wrong, in being put to death; he whose boldness appears in his daring not to do wrong, in living on. Of these two cases the one is advantageous, and the other is injurious. But even He dislikes the things which Heaven dislikes; who would presume to know the reason why?

**2** The Way of Heaven does not strive, and yet it skilfully overcomes; does not speak, and yet it is skilful in obtaining a reply; does not call, and yet men come to it of themselves. Its demonstrations are quiet, and yet its plans are skilful and effective. The meshes of the net of Heaven are large; far apart, but letting nothing escape.
