## Overview

The Poetic Edda is the cornerstone of academic Old Norse studies. Scholars examine the poems through philological analysis of Old Norse language and meter, source criticism of the Codex Regius manuscript tradition, and comparative Indo-European mythology. Academic study focuses on dating individual poems, identifying layers of composition, reconstructing pre-Christian beliefs from post-conversion literary sources, and situating the texts within the broader context of medieval Scandinavian literary culture.
