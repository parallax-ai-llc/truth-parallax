## Overview

For the Essene community, the 4QMMT was a living document that expressed their deepest convictions about their role in the divine plan. As the self-identified "Sons of Light," the Qumran community understood this text as divinely revealed instruction that set them apart from the corruption they perceived in the Jerusalem establishment.

## Interpretive Approach

The Essene community's strict separation from mainstream Judaism and their expectation of an imminent eschatological consummation shaped every aspect of their reading. Their interpretation was not academic but existential — these texts defined who they were and what they hoped for.
