**B:39** And concerning lepers: we say that they should not enter any place with the holy purity. They shall dwell alone outside the camp.

**B:42** And it is written that from the time he shaves and washes, he shall dwell outside his tent for seven days.

**B:44** But at present, while they are still impure, the lepers come into the house of the holy purity. And you know that anyone who sins inadvertently, who transgresses a commandment and is forgiven for it — he brings a sin-offering.

**B:48** And concerning him who acts intentionally: it is written that he is a despiser and a blasphemer.

**B:49** And furthermore: while they are impure, they are not to eat the sacred food. The impure have no right to eat from the purity until the sun has set.

**B:52** And concerning the impurity of the corpse of a human being: we say that every bone, whether or not it still has flesh on it, should be treated according to the law pertaining to the dead or the slain.

**B:55** And concerning the practices of fornication carried out among the people: they are sons of holiness, as it is written: "Israel is holy." And concerning his garment it is written: it shall not be of mixed stuff; and one should not sow his field or vineyard with mixed seed.
