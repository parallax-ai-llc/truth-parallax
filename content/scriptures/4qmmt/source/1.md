title: "4QMMT Section 1"
scripture: "4qmmt"
chapter: 1
---

**A:1** These are some of our words concerning the Law of God; that is, some of the works that we reckon as justifying you. All of them concern the purity laws and related matters.

**A:3** And concerning the calendar: We have determined that the year consists of three hundred and sixty-four days.

**A:5** And concerning the first month: On the fourth day of the week is its beginning. And concerning the Sabbaths: On the seventh day is the Sabbath, and the first day of the first month falls on the fourth day.

**A:8** And the festival of weeks falls on the fifteenth day of the third month. And the Day of Memorial falls on the first day of the seventh month.

**A:11** And the Day of Atonement falls on the tenth day of the seventh month. And the festival of Booths falls on the fifteenth day of the seventh month.

**A:14** And on the twenty-second day of the seventh month is the solemn assembly. These are the appointed times.

**A:17** We have calculated all the appointed seasons according to the reckoning, and we have found that the Sabbaths and the festivals must be observed according to our reckoning, not according to the reckoning used by those who have not entered the Community.
