title: "4QMMT Section 3"
scripture: "4qmmt"
chapter: 3
---

**B:21** And concerning the deaf who have not heard the law and the judgment and the purity rules, and have not heard the statutes of Israel: he who has not seen should not come near the purity of the Temple.

**B:24** And concerning the blind who cannot see so as to beware of every impure mixture: they shall not see the purity of the Temple.

**B:27** And concerning the pouring of liquids: we say that they contain no purity. Even the pouring of liquids cannot separate the unclean from the clean, for the moisture of the pouring of liquids and that of the receiving vessel are alike — one and the same moisture.

**B:30** And one should not let dogs enter the holy camp, for they may eat some of the bones from the Temple while the flesh is still on them.

**B:32** For Jerusalem is the camp of holiness, and is the place which He has chosen from among all the tribes of Israel. For Jerusalem is the chief of the camps of Israel.

**B:36** And concerning the planting of fruit trees planted in the land of Israel: they are to be treated as firstfruits belonging to the Priests. And the tithe of the cattle and sheep belongs to the Priests.
