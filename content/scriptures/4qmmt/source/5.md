**B:58** And concerning marriages: they should not have relations, for the people are holy, as it is written: "Israel is holy." And concerning the garments: the garments are holy.

**B:62** One should not yoke together an ox and a donkey; one should not wear together wool and linen. No one shall bring an abomination into his house.

**B:64** And you know that some of the Priests and the people mingle with one another and they unite with one another and pollute the holy seed as well as their own seed with women whom they are forbidden to marry.

**B:66** For the sons of Aaron should take care about these matters, so as not to cause the people to bear guilt.

**B:69** And concerning the practice which is done regarding the tithes of corn, wine, and oil: we say that the Priests should not eat the tithe except in the holy place.

**B:72** And concerning the practice done regarding the tenth of the tithe given to the Levites: the Levites should not eat it in any place where there is unclean food, for the tithe to the Levites is like an offering.

**B:75** And you know that this is so; and if it is not so, then tell us. We have sent this to you because we have studied the Book of Moses and the Books of the Prophets and the writings of David.
