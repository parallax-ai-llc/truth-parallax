---
title: "4QMMT Section 2"
scripture: "4qmmt"
chapter: 2
---

**B:1** And concerning the offering of grain of the Gentiles: they should not accept it, because it is contaminated.

**B:3** And concerning the sin-offering: one should not cook it in any unclean vessel. One should not eat it before sunset.

**B:5** And concerning the purity of the heifer of the sin-offering: he who slaughters it and he who burns it and he who gathers its ashes and he who sprinkles the water of purification — all of these should be pure at sunset so that the one who is pure sprinkles upon the one who is impure.

**B:8** And concerning the hides of cattle and sheep: one should not bring their hides into the holy place, for they are impure.

**B:11** And concerning the bones and hides: one should not make from the bones and from the hide of a carcass any utensil. And one should not bring any hide or bone of an unclean animal into the holy place.

**B:14** And concerning the offering of wood and frankincense: let it be burnt only on the appointed day.

**B:17** These are the rules of purity concerning the offerings for the altar. Let them not be profaned by bringing impurity near them.
