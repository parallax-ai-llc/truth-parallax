## Overview

Eastern Orthodox engagement with the 4QMMT is guided by the patristic principle that the Old Covenant progressively reveals the mysteries that find their fullness in Christ and the Church. Orthodox scholars read the Dead Sea Scrolls as witnesses to the spiritual intensity of Second Temple Judaism — an intensity that, in the Orthodox view, was providentially preparing the ground for the Incarnation.

## Interpretive Approach

Orthodox interpretation emphasizes the liturgical and mystical dimensions of the Dead Sea Scrolls, finding in texts like this one anticipations of the Church's own liturgical and contemplative tradition.
