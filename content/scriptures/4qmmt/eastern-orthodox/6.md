Eastern Orthodox reading of 4QMMT section 6, "The Concluding Exhortation," approaches the text through the lens of patristic theology and the Orthodox understanding of salvation history. The spiritual realities described here — heavenly worship, angelic communion, covenant fidelity, eschatological hope — find their fulfillment in the liturgical and mystical tradition of the Orthodox Church.

Orthodox scholars are attentive to the text's witness to the Jewish roots of Christian worship and the anticipations of sacramental theology found in the Dead Sea Scrolls. The Qumran community's sense of participating in heavenly worship through earthly liturgy resonates with the Orthodox understanding of the Divine Liturgy as the intersection of heaven and earth.
