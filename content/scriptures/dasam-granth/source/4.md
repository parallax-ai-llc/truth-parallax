---
title: "Zafarnama - The Epistle of Victory"
scripture: "dasam-granth"
chapter: 4
---

**1** This is the letter written by Guru Gobind Singh to the Emperor Aurangzeb, after the battles of Chamkaur and Anandpur.

**2** O Aurangzeb, what though my four sons were killed, the Khalsa remains like a coiled snake. What avails it to put out a few sparks when the fire still rages?

**3** When all other means have failed, it is righteous to draw the sword. I had no choice but to fight, for you broke every promise, every treaty, every oath.

**4** You swore on the Quran that you would let us pass in peace. But no sooner had we left the fort than your armies attacked. What faith can be placed in your word?

**5** I am the worshipper of the one God. I fight only for truth and justice. I have no enmity against any man. I fight against tyranny and oppression.

**6** Know this: the Eternal God is the master of all. He who trusts in Him shall never be confounded. The truth shall prevail, and injustice shall be destroyed.
