---
title: "Jaap Sahib"
scripture: "dasam-granth"
chapter: 1
---

**1** O Lord, Thou art without mark, without caste, without lineage. Thou art without form, without color, without contour.

**2** Without enmity, without bodily form, beyond birth, beyond the power of any to describe Thee.

**3** Neither of the earth nor of the sky, neither of the world nor of the void. Thou art the ultimate warrior, Thou art self-illuminated.

**4** Thou art the acme of places, Thou art the king of kings. The sovereign of the three worlds, the master of the universe.

**5** Infinite are Thy forms, Thy virtues are beyond counting. Beyond numbering are the ways to praise Thee. Beyond description is Thy true nature.

**6** I bow to Thee, O Lord of Might. I bow to Thee, O Giver of Sustenance. I bow to Thee, O Wonderful Lord. I bow to Thee, O Formless One.

**7** Salutations to Thee, O Destroyer of All. Salutations to Thee, O Creator of All. Salutations to Thee, O Remover of Darkness. Salutations to Thee, O Lord of Light.
