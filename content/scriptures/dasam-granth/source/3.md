---
title: "Bachitar Natak - The Wondrous Drama"
scripture: "dasam-granth"
chapter: 3
---

**1** Now I tell my own story, how God brought me into this world and sent me to spread the truth.

**2** I was absorbed in deep meditation on the mountain called Hem Kunt. There I worshipped the Almighty with single-minded devotion.

**3** The Lord said to me: I have cherished thee as my son. I have created thee to spread the Dharma. Go into the world and teach the path of righteousness.

**4** I was born in the city of Patna, in the family of Guru Tegh Bahadur. My father gave his head for the faith, and I was given the task of continuing his work.

**5** I created the Khalsa, the pure. I gave them the five articles of faith. I gave them the sword to fight injustice and the scripture to guide their souls.

**6** The Lord said: Those who worship me shall be protected. Those who serve the truth shall triumph. The Khalsa shall endure until the end of time.
