---
title: "Chaupai Sahib - The Prayer of Protection"
scripture: "dasam-granth"
chapter: 5
---

**1** Grant me this boon, O Lord of the Universe: may I never refrain from righteous acts.

**2** May I fight without fear against all foes in life's battles, and may I emerge victorious through Your grace.

**3** May I teach my mind to sing Your praises, and when the end of life comes, may I die fighting on the field of battle.

**4** O Lord, protect me with Your hand. Destroy my enemies. May my household flourish, and may the power of the Khalsa prevail.

**5** Those who meditate on You, O Lord, are freed from the cycle of birth and death. Those who serve You find eternal peace.

**6** O Lord of the Sword, O Lord of the Arrow, O Lord of the Shield, O Lord of the Iron Gauntlet, protect us. Your servant calls upon You. Destroy the enemies of truth and righteousness.
