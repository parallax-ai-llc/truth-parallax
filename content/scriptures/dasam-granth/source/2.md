---
title: "Akal Ustat - In Praise of the Timeless"
scripture: "dasam-granth"
chapter: 2
---

**1** I bow to Him who is beyond time, beyond death, beyond form. I bow to Him who is the steel, the source of all strength.

**2** He is without color, without form, without mark. He is the primal power, the ultimate reality. All creation springs from Him, and all creation returns to Him.

**3** The Hindu calls Him Ram, the Muslim calls Him Rahim. But He is the same God, the One, without a second.

**4** The temple and the mosque are the same. The Hindu worship and the Muslim prayer are the same. All human beings are one. It is the same Lord who created all.

**5** Why do you argue among yourselves? There is only one God, one creation, one human family. Know this truth, and you shall be free.

**6** The devotees of many faiths worship the one God. They call Him by different names, but He is the same. O Nanak, if you know this truth, you have understood everything.
