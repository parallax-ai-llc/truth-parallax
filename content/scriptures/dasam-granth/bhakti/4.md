## Bhakti Reading

The Bhakti reading of the Chandi Charitar situates the text within the broader tradition of devotional literature celebrating the divine feminine in Hindu and Indic spirituality. The composition's vivid portrayal of Chandi's power and grace resonates with the Shakta bhakti tradition of goddess worship, while its Sikh reinterpretation redirects this devotional energy toward the formless God.

Bhakti scholars note the text's masterful use of the *bir ras* (heroic aesthetic sentiment) — a mode of devotional expression that celebrates courage, strength, and righteous anger as forms of divine worship. The Chandi Charitar demonstrates that bhakti devotion encompasses not only gentle love and tender surrender but also fierce, passionate commitment to the cause of divine justice.
