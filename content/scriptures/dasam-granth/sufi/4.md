## Sufi Reading

The Sufi reading of the Chandi Charitar engages with the text's treatment of cosmic warfare as a spiritual metaphor. While the surface narrative describes the goddess Chandi's battles against demonic forces, Sufi readers find parallels with the Islamic concept of the eternal struggle between truth and falsehood, light and darkness — themes central to Sufi cosmology and psychology.

Sufi scholars note that the Chandi Charitar's celebration of divine power overcoming evil resonates with Quranic narratives of prophets battling against corrupt powers and the Sufi understanding that the spiritual path requires courage, persistence, and willingness to confront the inner demons of the *nafs* (ego-self).
