## Academic Reading

Academic study of the Bustan's epilogue examines the conventions of literary closure in medieval Persian poetry. Scholars analyze Sa'di's authorial persona in the closing passage, noting how the poet positions himself as both the humble servant of his subject and the conscious artisan of his medium. Manuscript colophons and reception history reveal how different communities of readers have received and transmitted the Bustan's concluding message over the centuries.
