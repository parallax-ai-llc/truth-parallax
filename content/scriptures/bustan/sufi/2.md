## Sufi Reading

For the Sufi, benevolence flows naturally from the realization that all beings share a single divine source. Sa'di's stories of generous souls illustrate the Sufi station of *ihsan* — excellence in conduct that arises from the awareness of God's perpetual gaze. True generosity in the Sufi reading is not calculated charity but the spontaneous overflow of a heart filled with divine love. The one who has tasted the wine of divine nearness cannot help but pour for others, for the nature of light is to illuminate everything it touches.
