## Sufi Reading

The Sufi reading of gratitude (*shukr*) transforms it from social courtesy into a fundamental spiritual practice. To be grateful is to recognize that every breath, every heartbeat, every moment of awareness is a gift from the Beloved. Sa'di's poetic rendering of gratitude as a form of worship aligns with the Sufi teaching that *shukr* is the recognition of the divine source of all blessing — a perpetual state of wonder at the generosity of the Real (*al-Haqq*). The ungrateful soul, in this reading, is spiritually asleep, unable to perceive the constant shower of divine grace.
