## Sufi Reading

Repentance (*tawba*) in the Sufi reading is not merely turning from sin but turning toward God — a fundamental reorientation of the soul from the periphery of existence to its divine center. Sa'di's stories of transformation through sincere repentance illustrate the Sufi teaching that no soul is beyond redemption, for the divine mercy is infinite and the door of return is always open. The Sufi understands repentance as a continuous process, not a single event — each moment offers the opportunity to turn more fully toward the Beloved.
