## Sufi Reading

Sa'di's praise of contentment resonates with the Sufi station of *rida* — radical acceptance of divine decree. The contented soul has passed beyond the oscillation of desire and aversion to rest in the unchanging peace of divine presence. Sa'di's stories of those who found freedom through renunciation of excess illustrate the Sufi teaching that the heart has room for either God or the world, but not both. Contentment is thus not passive resignation but the most active spiritual posture: the deliberate turning of the heart away from the transient toward the Eternal.
