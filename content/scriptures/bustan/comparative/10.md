## Comparative Reading

Sa'di's epilogue connects with the universal literary convention of the closing blessing or benediction found across cultures: the biblical benediction, the Chinese literary postface, the Sanskrit *phala-shruti* (fruit of recitation), and the Islamic *khatima*. Comparative study reveals how different traditions frame the relationship between the literary work and the spiritual benefit it is believed to confer, illuminating the cross-cultural conviction that sacred literature possesses transformative power beyond its informational content.
