## Shia Reading

The Shia reading of Sa'di's chapter on education is informed by the doctrine that the Imam is the supreme teacher of humanity, possessing divinely granted knowledge that transcends ordinary learning. Sa'di's stories of the transformative power of good teachers and the limitations of education for unreceptive students find theological resonance in the Shia understanding that true knowledge (*ma'rifa*) comes through the chain of spiritual transmission from the Prophet through the Imams.
