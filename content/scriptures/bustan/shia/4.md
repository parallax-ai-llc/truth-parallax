## Shia Reading

The Shia reading of humility is profoundly shaped by the example of Imam Husayn, whose supreme sacrifice at Karbala represents the ultimate act of spiritual lowliness before God. Sa'di's stories of the humble exalted find their deepest resonance in the Karbala narrative, where the apparent defeat of Husayn's small band became the spiritual victory that sustains Shia faith. The Shia reader understands humility not as mere self-deprecation but as the courageous willingness to place oneself entirely in God's hands.
