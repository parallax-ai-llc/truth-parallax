## Shia Reading

The Shia reader finds in Sa'di's chapter on gratitude an echo of the profound thankfulness (*shukr*) that characterizes the devotional life modeled by the Imams. The prayers attributed to Imam Zayn al-Abidin in the Sahifa Sajjadiyya represent perhaps the most elaborate expression of gratitude in Islamic devotional literature, and Sa'di's poetic treatment of thankfulness resonates with this Shia tradition of prayer as perpetual acknowledgment of divine grace.
