## Shia Reading

The Shia reading grounds Sa'di's praise of benevolence in the exemplary generosity of the Ahl al-Bayt. The Qur'anic verse 'they give food, despite their love for it, to the needy, the orphan, and the captive' (76:8) is understood in Shia tradition as referring specifically to Imam Ali, Fatima, Hasan, and Husayn. Sa'di's stories of selfless giving echo this paradigm, reinforcing the Shia conviction that true benevolence flows from the spiritual authority (*walaya*) of the Prophet's household.
