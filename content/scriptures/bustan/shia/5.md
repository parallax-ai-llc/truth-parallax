## Shia Reading

Sa'di's celebration of contentment is read through the Shia concept of *rida* — serene acceptance of divine will exemplified by the Imams in their sufferings. Imam Ali's contentment with a life of simplicity despite his right to political power, and Imam Husayn's acceptance of martyrdom rather than compromise with injustice, provide the spiritual context for the Shia appreciation of Sa'di's tales. Contentment in this reading is not passivity but active trust in God's ultimate justice.
