## Shia Reading

The Shia tradition reads Sa'di's love poetry through the lens of *irfan* — the mystical gnosis transmitted through the Imams. Love (*ishq*) in the Shia mystical tradition is directed toward the divine light (*nur*) manifest in the Ahl al-Bayt, and the beloved of Sa'di's verses is understood as a reflection of this sacred beauty. The grief of separation evokes the Shia community's longing for the Hidden Imam, while the ecstasy of union anticipates the eschatological fulfillment when the Mahdi will establish justice on earth.
