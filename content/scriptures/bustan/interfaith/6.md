## Interfaith Reading

Gratitude as a shared spiritual practice offers one of the most accessible entry points for interfaith encounter. People of all faiths — and none — can relate to the experience of thankfulness and its transformative effect on the one who practices it. Sa'di's poetic celebration of gratitude provides material for interfaith services, shared readings, and contemplative practices that honor diversity while celebrating what is held in common.
