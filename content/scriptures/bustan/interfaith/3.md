## Interfaith Reading

Sa'di's love poetry opens interfaith conversation about the role of love in the spiritual life. Every major tradition recognizes love as a — or the — fundamental spiritual force: Sufi *ishq*, Christian *agape*, Jewish *ahavah*, Hindu *bhakti*, Buddhist *metta*. Sa'di's characteristic ambiguity between human and divine love provides a starting point for exploring how different traditions negotiate the relationship between earthly affection and transcendent aspiration.
