## Interfaith Reading

Sa'di's reflections on education open interfaith dialogue about how different traditions transmit wisdom and form character across generations. In a multicultural world, the question of how to educate children in their own tradition while fostering respect for others is one of the most pressing interfaith challenges. Sa'di's balanced view — valuing education while acknowledging its limits — offers wisdom for navigating these complex waters.
