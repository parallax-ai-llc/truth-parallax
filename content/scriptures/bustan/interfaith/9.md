## Interfaith Reading

Sa'di's prayer chapter creates space for interfaith exploration of devotional practice. Shared prayer and meditation — whether conducted in silence or in carefully choreographed multi-faith services — constitute one of the most powerful forms of interfaith encounter. Sa'di's poetry, with its combination of specific Islamic devotion and universal spiritual longing, serves as a bridge between traditions in the shared practice of turning toward the Transcendent.
