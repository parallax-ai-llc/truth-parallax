## Interfaith Reading

Repentance and transformation provide powerful themes for interfaith encounter, as every major tradition affirms the possibility of moral renewal. Sa'di's stories of sinners who find redemption through sincere turning resonate with the Jewish High Holy Days, the Christian season of Lent, Buddhist vows of renewal, and similar practices across traditions. The shared conviction that human beings are capable of change provides a foundation for interfaith hope.
