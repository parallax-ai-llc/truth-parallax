## Interfaith Reading

Sa'di's praise of contentment resonates with the growing interfaith movement for ecological sustainability and economic justice. The shared wisdom of all major traditions — that material sufficiency, rather than unlimited accumulation, opens the path to genuine flourishing — provides a powerful resource for addressing the environmental and social crises of the contemporary world. Interfaith groups find in Sa'di a compelling poetic voice for the values they hold in common.
