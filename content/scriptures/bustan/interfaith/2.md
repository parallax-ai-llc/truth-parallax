## Interfaith Reading

Sa'di's stories of benevolence create interfaith common ground by illustrating a virtue universally affirmed yet differently nuanced across traditions. Interfaith service projects — from Habitat for Humanity to interfaith food banks — embody the practical expression of the generosity Sa'di celebrates. His insistence that genuine benevolence transcends boundaries of religion, ethnicity, and social class provides a powerful resource for interfaith social action.
