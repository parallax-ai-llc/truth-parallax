## Interfaith Reading

Sa'di's closing benediction invites interfaith readers to conclude their shared journey through the Bustan's orchard with a common gesture of blessing and hope. The universal human impulse to end significant shared experiences with words of peace and goodwill finds expression across all traditions, and Sa'di's final verses provide a beautiful model for interfaith closure — rooted in a specific tradition yet open to all who seek beauty, wisdom, and the grace of the Divine.
