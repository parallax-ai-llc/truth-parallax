## Interfaith Reading

Interfaith dialogue on humility reveals it as a shared virtue that each tradition grounds in its own theological or philosophical framework. The universal recognition that egoism is the primary obstacle to spiritual growth, and that some form of self-transcendence is the remedy, provides common ground for practitioners of all faiths. Sa'di's practical illustrations of humility in action offer concrete starting points for interfaith conversation.
