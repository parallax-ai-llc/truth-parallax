## Sunni Reading

Sa'di's chapter on prayer resonates deeply with the Sunni emphasis on *salat* (the five daily prayers) as the pillar of faith and the believer's most direct connection with God. Sa'di's verses on the transformative power of prayer echo the hadith that 'prayer is the mi'raj (ascension) of the believer.' The Sunni reading emphasizes both the discipline of regular worship and the aspiration for *khushu'* (reverent concentration) that transforms mechanical ritual into genuine spiritual communion.
