## Sunni Reading

Sa'di's praise of contentment finds its Sunni theological foundation in the Qur'anic assurance that God determines each soul's provision (*rizq*). The Prophet's teaching that 'richness is not in having many possessions but richness is the richness of the soul' provides the framework for reading Sa'di's tales of those who found peace through simplicity. The Sunni tradition of *wasatiyya* (moderation) values contentment not as extreme asceticism but as the middle path between miserliness and extravagance.
