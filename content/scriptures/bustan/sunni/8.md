## Sunni Reading

Repentance (*tawba*) is a foundational concept in Sunni theology, rooted in the Qur'anic assurance that God loves those who turn to Him in sincere repentance. Sa'di's stories of transformed sinners illustrate the Prophetic teaching that 'the one who repents from sin is like one who has no sin.' The Sunni reader finds in this chapter a powerful affirmation of divine mercy and human moral agency — the conviction that however far one has strayed, the path of return is always open to those who sincerely seek it.
