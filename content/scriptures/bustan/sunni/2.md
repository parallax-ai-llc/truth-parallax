## Sunni Reading

Sa'di's celebration of benevolence aligns with the Qur'anic emphasis on *infaq* (spending in the way of God) and the Prophetic model of extraordinary generosity. The Sunni reader finds in these stories illustrations of the hadith that 'the upper hand is better than the lower hand' — that it is nobler to give than to receive. Sa'di's poetic rendering makes abstract ethical principles vivid and memorable, reinforcing the Sunni teaching that generosity is not optional but an obligation flowing from faith in God's abundant provision.
