## Overview

The Academic study of 4 Ezra focuses on the text's composition, original language, textual transmission through multiple versions, and its significance as a witness to Jewish theological reflection after 70 CE. Scholars analyze the dialogical structure as a sophisticated literary strategy for exploring the limits of human comprehension of divine justice, and debate the relationship between the three dialogue sections and the four vision sections.
