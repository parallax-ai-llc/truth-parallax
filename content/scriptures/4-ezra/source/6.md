---
title: "4 Ezra Chapter 6"
scripture: "4-ezra"
chapter: 6
---

**1** And I beheld, and lo, an eagle came up from the sea, and had twelve feathered wings and three heads.

**2** And I saw, and behold, she spread her wings over all the earth, and all the winds of heaven blew on her, and were gathered together.

**3** And I beheld, and out of her feathers there grew other contrary feathers; and they became little feathers and small.

**4** But her heads were at rest: the head in the midst was greater than the other, but it rested with the others.

**5** Moreover I beheld, and lo, the eagle flew with her feathers, and reigned upon earth, and over them that dwelt therein.
