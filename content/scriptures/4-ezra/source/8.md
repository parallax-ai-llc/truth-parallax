title: "4 Ezra Chapter 8"
scripture: "4-ezra"
chapter: 8
---

**1** And he answered me, saying, The Most High made this world for the sake of many, but the future world for the sake of only a few.

**2** But I tell you a parable, Ezra. Just as, if you ask the earth, it will tell you that it produces a large amount of clay from which pottery is made, but only a little dust from which gold comes, so it is with the present world.

**3** Many have been created, but only a few will be saved.

**4** Hear me, and I will instruct you, and I will admonish you yet again.

**5** For this reason the Most High has made not one world but two.
