---
title: "4 Ezra Chapter 15"
scripture: "4-ezra"
chapter: 15
---

**1** Behold, speak in the ears of my people the words of prophecy that I will put in your mouth, says the Lord.

**2** And cause them to be written on paper, for they are trustworthy and true.

**3** Do not fear the plots against you; do not let the unbelief of those who oppose you trouble you.

**4** For every unbeliever shall die in his unbelief.

**5** Behold, says the Lord, I bring evils upon the whole earth: sword and famine, and death and destruction.
