---
title: "4 Ezra Chapter 16"
scripture: "4-ezra"
chapter: 16
---

**1** Woe to you, Babylon and Asia! Woe to you, Egypt and Syria!

**2** Gird yourselves with sackcloth and garments of mourning, and bewail your children, and lament for them; for your destruction is at hand.

**3** A sword has been sent upon you, and who is there to turn it aside?

**4** A fire has been sent upon you, and who is there to quench it?

**5** Calamities have been sent upon you, and who is there to drive them away?

**6** The Lord God has not forgotten you, and he shall deliver those who trust in him. The end of the book of 4 Ezra.
