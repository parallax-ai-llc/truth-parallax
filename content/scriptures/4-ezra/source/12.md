---
title: "4 Ezra Chapter 12"
scripture: "4-ezra"
chapter: 12
---

**1** And it came to pass that when the thirty years were accomplished, the Most High spoke to me, saying: Come, Ezra, and hear the words that I have come to speak to you.

**2** Behold, the days come when I shall draw near to visit the inhabitants of the earth.

**3** And when I shall require from the doers of iniquity the penalty of their iniquity, and when the humiliation of Zion shall be complete.

**4** And when the seal shall be set upon the age that is about to pass away, then I will show these signs.

**5** The books shall be opened before the face of the firmament, and all shall see together.
