---
title: "4 Ezra Chapter 11"
scripture: "4-ezra"
chapter: 11
---

**1** And he said to me: Just as no one can explore or know what is in the depths of the sea, so no one on earth can see my Son or those who are with him, except in the time of his day.

**2** This is the interpretation of the dream that you saw. And you alone have been enlightened about these things.

**3** Because you have forsaken your own ways and have applied yourself to mine, and have searched out my law.

**4** You have devoted your life to wisdom, and called understanding your mother.

**5** Therefore I have shown you these things, for there is a reward laid up with the Most High.
