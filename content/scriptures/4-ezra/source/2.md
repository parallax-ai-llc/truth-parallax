---
title: "4 Ezra Chapter 2"
scripture: "4-ezra"
chapter: 2
---

**1** And it came to pass after this, that I wept again, and fasted seven days in like manner, that I might complete the three weeks which had been told me.

**2** And in the eighth night was my heart vexed within me again, and I began to speak before the Most High.

**3** For my spirit was greatly set on fire, and my soul was in distress.

**4** And I said, O Lord, thou spakest from the beginning of the creation, even the first day, and saidst thus: Let heaven and earth be made; and thy word was a perfect work.

**5** And then was the spirit, and darkness and silence were on every side; the sound of a human voice was not yet formed.

**6** Then commandedst thou a fair light to come forth of thy treasures, that thy work might appear.
