---
title: "4 Ezra Chapter 13"
scripture: "4-ezra"
chapter: 13
---

**1** Behold, the days come when the Most High is about to deliver those who are on the earth.

**2** And astonishment of mind shall come over those who dwell on the earth.

**3** And they shall plan to wage war one against another, city against city, place against place, people against people, and kingdom against kingdom.

**4** And it shall come to pass, when these things come to pass, and the signs occur which I showed you before, then my Son shall be revealed, whom you saw as a man ascending.

**5** And he shall stand on the top of Mount Zion.
