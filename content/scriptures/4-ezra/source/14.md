title: "4 Ezra Chapter 14"
scripture: "4-ezra"
chapter: 14
---

**1** And on the third day, as I sat under an oak, behold, a voice came from a bush opposite me and said, Ezra, Ezra!

**2** And I said, Here I am, Lord. And I rose to my feet.

**3** And he said to me, I revealed myself in the bush and spoke to Moses when my people were in bondage in Egypt.

**4** And I sent him and led my people out of Egypt, and I brought them to Mount Sinai, and I kept him with me many days.

**5** And I told him many wondrous things, and showed him the secrets of the times, and declared to him the end of the seasons.
