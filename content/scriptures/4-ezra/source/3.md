---
title: "4 Ezra Chapter 3"
scripture: "4-ezra"
chapter: 3
---

**1** And it came to pass after seven days that I dreamed a dream by night.

**2** And lo, there arose a wind from the sea, that it moved all the waves thereof. And I beheld, and lo, that wind brought up from the midst of the sea as it were the likeness of a man.

**3** And I beheld, and lo, that man flew with the clouds of heaven. And when he turned his countenance to look, all things trembled that were seen under him.

**4** And whensoever the voice went out of his mouth, all they burned that heard his voice, like as the wax melteth when it feeleth the fire.

**5** And after this I beheld, and lo, there was gathered together a multitude of men, out of number, from the four winds of heaven, to make war against the man that came out of the sea.

**6** But I beheld, and lo, he had graved himself a great mountain, and flew up upon it.
