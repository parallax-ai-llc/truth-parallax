---
title: "4 Ezra Chapter 9"
scripture: "4-ezra"
chapter: 9
---

**1** He answered and said to me: Measure carefully in your mind, and when you see that a certain part of the predicted signs have passed, then you will know that it is the very time.

**2** For the age is divided into twelve parts, and nine of its parts have already passed, as well as half of the tenth part.

**3** Two parts remain, besides half of the tenth part.

**4** Now therefore put your house in order, reprove your people, comfort the lowly among them, and instruct those that are wise.

**5** And now renounce the life that is corruptible, and dismiss from you mortal thoughts; cast away from you the burdens of mankind, and divest yourself now of your weak nature.
