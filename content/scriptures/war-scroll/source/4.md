---
title: "War Scroll Section 4"
scripture: "war-scroll"
chapter: 4
---

**4:1** On the shields of the towers they shall write: on the first, "Michael," on the second, "Gabriel," on the third, "Sariel," on the fourth, "Raphael."

**4:2** "Michael" and "Gabriel" on the right, and "Sariel" and "Raphael" on the left. They shall set an ambush to... four gates.

**4:7** The horsemen shall be stationed on both flanks of the formation, on this side and on that, to cause dread in the enemy ranks; with them the horses shall be nimble and made firm, and their riders shall be men of valor.

**4:9** They shall be between forty and fifty years of age. The riders of the rank shall be from forty to fifty years of age, and they and their mounts shall wear breastplates, helmets, and greaves.

**4:11** They shall carry in their hands round shields and a spear eight cubits long, a bow, arrows, and a battle-sword. All these shall be prepared in order and shall be equipped for the purpose that is planned for them.

**4:14** And the slingers shall stand behind the men of war, and their number shall be seven battle formations: to each formation shall belong seven slingers.

**4:16** The men of the formation shall be from thirty to forty-five years of age, and the inspectors of the camps from fifty to sixty.

**4:17** Their battle formation shall advance, and they shall stand in columns. In the first column there shall be a thousand men; a line of seven formations; each formation shall consist of one hundred and forty-three men.
