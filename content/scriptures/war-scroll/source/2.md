---
title: "War Scroll Section 2"
scripture: "war-scroll"
chapter: 2
---

**2:1** The Rule for the ordering of the battle divisions for the war. When the army is complete, the men shall select from all the tribes of Israel twelve chief Priests to serve before God continually.

**2:3** Twenty-six chiefs of the divisions shall serve in their positions. After them the chiefs of the Levites shall serve continually, twelve, one per tribe.

**2:5** The chiefs of their divisions shall serve each in his position. The chiefs of the tribes and the fathers of the congregation shall support them, taking their stand continually at the gates of the sanctuary.

**2:7** The chiefs of their course, from the age of fifty upward, shall take their stand at the holocausts and the sacrifices, to arrange the incense of sweet savor for the will of God.

**2:9** These shall dispose themselves in ordered ranks at the feast of the New Moon and on the Sabbath days and on all the days of the year, and their age shall be from fifty years upward.

**2:11** These shall dispose themselves at the holocausts and the sacrifices, to prepare incense of sweet savor for the will of God, to atone for all His congregation, and to satisfy themselves perpetually before Him at the table of glory.

**2:14** All these they shall arrange at the season of the year of Release. During the remaining thirty-three years of the war the men of renown, those summoned to the Assembly, together with all the heads of family of the congregation, shall choose for themselves men of war for all the lands of the nations.
