---
title: "War Scroll Section 5"
scripture: "war-scroll"
chapter: 5
---

**10:1** And when the battle lines are arrayed against the enemy, battle line against battle line, there shall go forth from the middle opening into the gap between the battle lines seven Priests of the sons of Aaron dressed in fine white linen garments.

**10:3** And they shall carry in their hands the trumpets of slaughter; and the seven Levites accompanying them shall carry in their hands seven ram's horns. And these shall take up their stand in the center of the Chief Priest and the Priests.

**10:8** And the Priest shall go forth and stand before the formation and shall strengthen their hands in battle. He shall speak and shall say:

**10:9** "Hear O Israel! You are about to go into battle this day against your enemies. Do not fear! Do not let your hearts faint! Do not be terrified! Do not be in dread of them!

**10:10** For your God goes with you to fight for you against your enemies, and to give you victory." And the officers shall speak to all those prepared for battle, those of steadfast heart, saying:

**12:7** "For Thine is the battle! And by the strength of Thy hand their corpses were scattered without burial. Goliath of Gath, a mighty warrior, Thou didst deliver into the hands of Thy servant David."

**12:9** "For he trusted in Thy great Name and not in sword and spear. For the battle is Thine! The Philistines Thou didst humble many times by Thy holy Name."

**12:10** "And also through our kings Thou didst save us many times, because of Thy mercy, and not according to our works by which we have done evil, nor for our rebellious deeds."

**13:1** "For Thine is the battle, and from Thee comes the power, and it is not ours. It is not our strength nor the power of our hands that does valiantly, but it is through Thy strength and through the power of Thy great valor."

**13:14** "Rise up, O Hero! Lead off Thy captives, O Glorious One! Gather up Thy spoils, O Author of mighty deeds! Lay Thy hand on the neck of Thine enemies and Thy feet on the pile of the slain! Strike the nations, Thine adversaries, and devour flesh with Thy sword!"
