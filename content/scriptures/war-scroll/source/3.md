---
title: "War Scroll Section 3"
scripture: "war-scroll"
chapter: 3
---

**3:1** On the trumpets of assembly of the congregation they shall write "The Called of God." On the trumpets of assembly of the chiefs they shall write "The Princes of God."

**3:3** On the trumpets of enrollment they shall write "The Rule of God, the order of battle, and the trumpets of pursuit when the enemy is defeated."

**3:5** On the trumpets of ambush they shall write "The Mysteries of God for the destruction of wickedness." On the trumpets of pursuit they shall write "God has struck all the sons of darkness. He shall not abate His fury until they are utterly consumed."

**3:7** And on the standard of the whole congregation they shall write "The People of God" together with the names of Israel and Aaron, and the names of the twelve tribes of Israel according to their order of birth.

**3:9** And on the standard of the camp of the three tribes they shall write the name of the chief of the camp, together with the names of the commander of the thousands and the commanders of the hundreds.

**3:13** On the standard of Merari they shall write "The Offering of God" with the name of the chief of Merari and the names of the commanders of its thousands. On the standard of the thousand they shall write "The Wrath of God is kindled against Belial and against the men of his company, leaving no remnant."

**3:15** And when they go to battle they shall write on their standards: "The Truth of God," "The Righteousness of God," "The Glory of God," "The Justice of God."

**3:16** And after these the whole order of the description of their names, with their ranks, their call-up, their standards, and their trumpets and their formation.
