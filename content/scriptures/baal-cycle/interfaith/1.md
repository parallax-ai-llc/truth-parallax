The challenge of chaos (Yamm/Sea) to divine order speaks to a universal religious theme: the ongoing struggle between order and chaos, cosmos and primordial disorder. In Judaism, God's creation of order from *tohu va-bohu* (Genesis 1:2) echoes the Chaoskampf. In Christianity, Christ's calming of the sea (Mark 4:39) demonstrates divine sovereignty over chaos. In Hinduism, Indra's defeat of Vritra releases the waters. In Buddhism, the Buddha's defeat of Mara establishes the dharma.

The first tablet of the Baal Cycle presents the foundational version of this universal mythological pattern in the Canaanite tradition.
