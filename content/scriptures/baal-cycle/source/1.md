**Section I: Baal and Yam (The Sea)**

**1** [The tablets begin fragmentarily. El, the supreme god, holds court on his mountain.]

**2** El, the father of the gods, sat in his court.
**3** He proclaimed to the assembly of the gods:
**4** "The name of my son shall be Yam,
**5** Prince Sea, Judge River.

**6** Let him be given dominion."

**7** Yam sent his messengers to the mountain of El,
**8** to the assembly of the gods:
**9** "Message of Yam, your master,
**10** your lord, Judge River:

**11** Give up, O gods, the one you obey,
**12** the one you obey, O multitudes.
**13** Give up Baal and his retainers,
**14** the son of Dagon, that I may possess his gold!"

**15** The messengers of Yam arrived.
**16** They did not bow before El.
**17** They did not prostrate before the assembly.

**18** Standing, they spoke their piece,
**19** they recited their message.
**20** Fire, burning fire, appeared;
**21** their eyes were a whetted sword.

**22** They said to Bull El, his father:
**23** "Message of Yam, your master,
**24** your lord, Judge River:
**25** Give up, O gods, the one you obey,
**26** the one you obey, O multitudes.
**27** Give up Baal and his retainers,
**28** the son of Dagon, that I may possess his gold!"

**29** And Bull El, his father, replied:
**30** "Baal is your servant, O Yam.
**31** Baal is your servant, O Sea.
**32** The son of Dagon is your prisoner.

**33** He shall bring you tribute.
**34** Like the gods he shall bring you gifts,
**35** like the holy ones your presents."

**36** Prince Baal was furious.
**37** He seized a knife in his hand,
**38** a butcher's-knife in his right hand.
**39** He would have struck the messengers.

**40** But Anat seized his right hand,
**41** Astarte seized his left:
**42** "How can you strike the messengers of Yam,
**43** the emissaries of Judge River?

**44** A messenger bears on his shoulder
**45** the word of his lord.
**46** A messenger—"

**47** Prince Baal answered:
**48** "I reply to the messengers of Yam,
**49** to the emissaries of Judge River:

**50** I will not accept servitude to Yam!
**51** I will not be a servant of Judge River!

**52** Let Yam come forth!
**53** Let me humble him!
**54** Let me drive him from his throne,
**55** from his royal seat!"

**56** [Gap in text]

**57** Then Kothar-wa-Khasis, the craftsman of the gods, spoke:
**58** "Did I not tell you, O Prince Baal?
**59** Did I not say, O Rider of the Clouds?
**60** Your foe, Baal, your foe you shall strike!
**61** Your enemy you shall destroy!

**62** You shall take your eternal kingdom,
**63** your dominion everlasting."

**64** Kothar fashioned two clubs and pronounced their names:
**65** "Your name, yours, is Yagarrish—Drive-Out!
**66** Drive out Yam, drive Yam from his throne,
**67** River from the seat of his dominion!

**68** You shall swoop from the hand of Baal,
**69** like an eagle from his fingers.
**70** Strike the shoulders of Prince Yam,
**71** between the hands of Judge River!"

**72** The club swooped from the hand of Baal.
**73** It struck the shoulders of Prince Yam,
**74** between the hands of Judge River.
**75** Yam was strong; he did not falter.

**76** His joints did not fail,
**77** his frame did not collapse.

**78** Kothar fashioned the second club and pronounced its name:
**79** "Your name, yours, is Ayyamarri—Expeller!
**80** Expel Yam, expel Yam from his throne,
**81** River from the seat of his dominion!

**82** You shall swoop from the hand of Baal,
**83** like an eagle from his fingers.
**84** Strike the skull of Prince Yam,
**85** between the eyes of Judge River!

**86** Let Yam collapse and fall to the earth!"

**87** The club swooped from the hand of Baal.
**88** It struck the skull of Prince Yam,
**89** between the eyes of Judge River.
**90** Yam collapsed; he fell to the earth.

**91** His joints failed; his frame collapsed.
**92** Baal dragged Yam and laid him down;
**93** he finished off Judge River.

**94** Astarte rebuked him:
**95** "Scatter him, O Mighty Baal!
**96** Scatter him, O Rider of the Clouds!
**97** For Prince Yam is our captive,
**98** for Judge River is our captive!"

**99** And Baal scattered him.
**100** Mighty Baal became king.
