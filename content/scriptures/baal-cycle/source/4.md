**Section IV: Baal and Mot (Death)**

**1** Gapn and Ugar, the messengers of Baal,
**2** departed for the underworld.
**3** They descended to the city of Mot,
**4** to his city below the earth.

**5** They entered the throne room of Mot, son of El.
**6** At his feet they bowed and fell,
**7** prostrated themselves and honoured him.

**8** They repeated the message of Mighty Baal:
**9** "Message of Mighty Baal,
**10** word of the Mightiest of Heroes:

**11** My house I have built of silver,
**12** my palace of gold.
**13** I invite you to feast in my palace!"

**14** Mot, the beloved son of El, replied:
**15** "Was I pleased when Mighty Baal vanquished the sea?
**16** Was I pleased when he destroyed Judge River?

**17** Will he now invite me to eat bread?
**18** Will he invite me to drink wine?
**19** Does he not know that I swallow the mighty?

**20** My appetite is like the appetite of the lion in the waste,
**21** or the desire of the dolphin in the sea.
**22** Or I advance to the pool and eat;
**23** truly my hands eat seven portions,
**24** and my cup mixes a whole river.

**25** Invite me, Baal, with my brothers!
**26** Call me, Hadd, with my kinsmen!
**27** To eat bread with my brothers,
**28** to drink wine with my kinsmen.

**29** Have you forgotten, Baal, that I can pierce you?
**30** Though you struck Lotan, the fleeing serpent,
**31** destroyed the twisting serpent,
**32** the close-coiling one with seven heads—

**33** the heavens will burn dry,
**34** I will crush you—
**35** I will eat you: flanks, limbs, head, and all.
**36** You will descend into the throat of Mot, son of El."

**37** The messengers returned to Baal.
**38** They repeated the words of Mot.

**39** Baal was afraid.
**40** The Rider of the Clouds was terrified.

**41** Baal spoke: "Return, tell Mot, son of El:
**42** 'Message of Mighty Baal:
**43** Hail, O Mot, son of El!
**44** I am your servant, I am yours forever.'"

**45** The messengers departed and returned to Mot.
**46** They repeated the message of Baal.

**47** "Hail, Mot, son of El!
**48** Baal is your servant, Baal is yours forever."

**49** Mot, the beloved son of El, rejoiced.

**50** [Gap in text]

**51** "And you, take your clouds,
**52** your wind, your bolts, your rains;
**53** take with you your seven lads,
**54** your eight boars.

**55** Take with you Pidray, Girl of Light,
**56** take with you Tallay, Girl of Rain.
**57** Then set face toward Mount Kankaniya.

**58** Lift the mountain on your hands,
**59** the hill on your palms.
**60** Descend to the house of the dead in the earth,
**61** be counted among those who go down into the earth.

**62** And you shall know, O god, that you are dead."

**63** Mighty Baal obeyed.
**64** He loved a heifer in the pasture,
**65** a cow in the fields of Shlmmt.
**66** He lay with her seventy-seven times,
**67** she let him mount eighty-eight.
**68** And she conceived and bore his likeness.

**69** Baal dressed his likeness in his robes,
**70** as a gift to Mot, son of El.

**71** Then Mighty Baal descended.
**72** Mot, son of El, swallowed him.
**73** He perished—Mighty Baal.
**74** He died—the Prince, Lord of the Earth.
