**Section III: The Building of Baal's Palace**

**1** Baal sent word to Kothar-wa-Khasis:
**2** "Hasten! Build a house!
**3** Hasten! Erect a palace!
**4** In the midst of the heights of Zaphon
**5** let a house be built, let a palace be erected!

**6** A thousand acres the house shall encompass,
**7** ten thousand hectares the palace!"

**8** Kothar-wa-Khasis replied:
**9** "Listen, O Mighty Baal:
**10** attend, O Rider of the Clouds.
**11** Shall I not put a window in the house,
**12** a casement in the midst of the palace?"

**13** Mighty Baal replied:
**14** "Do not put a window in the house,
**15** a casement in the midst of the palace!

**16** Let not Pidray, Girl of Light, be seen,
**17** let not Tallay, Girl of Rain, be observed,
**18** by Prince Yam, Judge River."

**19** [Baal fears that if a window is placed, Yam (or Mot) may enter.]

**20** Kothar-wa-Khasis replied:
**21** "You will come back to my word, Baal."

**22** His house he built,
**23** his palace he erected.
**24** He sent to Lebanon and its trees,
**25** to Sirion and its precious cedars.

**26** Lebanon and its trees,
**27** Sirion and its precious cedars—
**28** fire was set in the house,
**29** flame in the palace.

**30** One day and a second, the fire burned in the house,
**31** the flame in the palace.
**32** A third, a fourth day, the fire burned in the house,
**33** the flame in the palace.

**34** A fifth, a sixth day, the fire burned in the house,
**35** the flame in the palace.
**36** Then on the seventh day,
**37** the fire departed from the house,
**38** the flame from the palace.

**39** The silver had turned to blocks,
**40** the gold had been turned to bricks.

**41** Mighty Baal rejoiced:
**42** "I have built my house of silver,
**43** my palace of gold!"

**44** Baal prepared his house,
**45** Hadd prepared his palace.
**46** He slaughtered both oxen and sheep.
**47** He felled bulls and fatling rams,
**48** yearling calves, lambs, and kids.

**49** He invited his brothers into his house,
**50** his kinsmen into his palace.
**51** He invited the seventy sons of Athirat.

**52** He gave the ram-gods wine to drink,
**53** he gave the ewe-goddesses wine to drink.
**54** He gave the bull-gods wine to drink,
**55** he gave the cow-goddesses wine to drink.

**56** He gave the chair-gods wine to drink,
**57** he gave the throne-goddesses wine to drink.
**58** He gave them jar upon jar of wine,
**59** barrels and barrels to drink.

**60** [Gap in text]

**61** Then Baal returned to his word:
**62** "I will install a window in the house,
**63** a casement in the midst of the palace!"

**64** Baal opened a rift in the clouds.
**65** Baal sounded his holy voice.
**66** Baal repeated the utterance of his voice.
**67** His holy voice covered the earth.

**68** The mountains quaked at his voice.
**69** The ancient mountains trembled.
**70** The enemies of Baal took to the forests,
**71** the haters of Hadd to the mountain recesses.

**72** Mighty Baal spoke:
**73** "O enemies of Baal, why are you afraid?
**74** Why are you afraid, O weapons of the Valiant One?"

**75** Baal's eye sought out his foes.
**76** His hand struck the cedars.
**77** His right hand struck the mighty ones.

**78** Then Baal sat enthroned in his house:
**79** "No king or commoner shall establish dominion
**80** over the earth that I rule.

**81** I alone am he who shall reign over the gods.
**82** I alone shall fatten gods and men.
**83** I alone shall satisfy the multitudes of the earth."

**84** Baal called aloud to his pages:
**85** "Look, Gapn and Ugar,
**86** [the messengers of Baal]:

**87** Set face toward the mountain of Targuzziza,
**88** toward the mountain of Tharumagi,
**89** toward the two mounds at the edge of the earth.

**90** Lift the mountain on your hands,
**91** the hill on your palms,
**92** and descend to the house of the dead in the earth,
**93** be counted among those who go down into the earth.

**94** Then set face toward his city Hamray,
**95** the throne where he sits,
**96** the land of his heritage.

**97** And say to Mot, son of El:
**98** 'Message of Mighty Baal,
**99** word of the Mightiest of Heroes:

**100** My house I have built of silver,
**101** my palace of gold.'"
