**Section II: The Palace of Baal**

**1** Baal had no house like the other gods,
**2** no court like the sons of Athirat.
**3** The dwelling of El was the shelter of his son;
**4** the dwelling of Lady Athirat of the Sea was the shelter of the beautiful goddesses.

**5** Mighty Baal spoke:
**6** "I have no house like the other gods,
**7** no court like the sons of Athirat.
**8** Am I a servant, an attendant of Athirat?

**9** Am I a servant who works at the millstone?
**10** Am I a maid who makes bricks?"

**11** Let a house be built for Baal like the gods',
**12** a court like the sons of Athirat's.

**13** The virgin Anat lifted her voice:
**14** "Bull El, my father, will respond to me;
**15** he will respond to me and I will make him respond!

**16** I will drag him like a lamb to the ground!
**17** I will make his grey hair run with blood,
**18** the grey of his beard with gore,
**19** unless he gives Baal a house like the gods',
**20** a court like the sons of Athirat."

**21** She stamped her foot and the earth shook.
**22** Then she set face
**23** toward El at the source of the rivers,
**24** amid the channels of the two deeps.

**25** She penetrated the domain of El and entered
**26** the pavilion of the King, Father of Years.
**27** At El's feet she bowed and fell,
**28** prostrated herself and honoured him.

**29** She spoke to Bull El, her father:
**30** "Let a house be built for Baal like the gods',
**31** a court like the sons of Athirat!"

**32** [Gap in text]

**33** Bull El, the Kindly One, replied:
**34** "Am I a servant, an attendant of Athirat?
**35** Am I a servant who molds bricks?
**36** Let a house be built for Baal like the gods',

**37** a court like the sons of Athirat!
**38** But first, gain the favour of Lady Athirat of the Sea."

**39** [Gap in text]

**40** The virgin Anat and mighty Baal set out.
**41** They approached Lady Athirat of the Sea.
**42** They brought gifts: silver, they brought gold,
**43** they brought precious jewels.

**44** Kothar-wa-Khasis fashioned a gift:
**45** a fine table covered with silver,
**46** plated with a layer of gold;
**47** a fine throne overlaid with electrum;

**48** a fine footstool plated with silver,
**49** overlaid with a layer of gold;
**50** a fine bed, covered with gold;
**51** a fine goblet for her hand.

**52** Lady Athirat of the Sea saw them approach.
**53** She saw the coming of the virgin Anat,
**54** the advance of Baal's Sister.

**55** Her feet stumbled,
**56** her back muscles snapped,
**57** her face above began to sweat,
**58** the joints of her backbone quivered,
**59** her vertebrae became weak.

**60** She raised her voice and cried:
**61** "Why has the virgin Anat come?
**62** Why has Baal's Sister come?
**63** Is it to strike my sons,
**64** to destroy the horde of my kindred?"

**65** But when she saw the gifts,
**66** when she saw the silver, the gold—
**67** Lady Athirat of the Sea rejoiced.

**68** She called to her servant:
**69** "See the gift! The silver, the burnished gold!
**70** Saddle an ass, harness a donkey.
**71** Put silver reins on the ass,
**72** golden trappings on the donkey."

**73** Her servant saddled an ass,
**74** harnessed a donkey.
**75** He lifted Lady Athirat, placed her on the back of the ass.

**76** She set face toward El at the source of the rivers,
**77** amid the channels of the two deeps.

**78** She penetrated the domain of El and entered
**79** the pavilion of the King, Father of Years.
**80** At El's feet she bowed and fell,
**81** prostrated herself and honoured him.

**82** "Great is the wisdom of El! Wise you are, eternal!
**83** Your grey beard instructs you!

**84** Now, mighty Baal has no house like the gods,
**85** no court like the sons of Athirat.
**86** Let a house be built for Baal like the gods',
**87** a court like the sons of Athirat!"

**88** And Bull El, the Kindly One, replied:
**89** "Shall I act as a servant of Athirat?
**90** Shall I act as one who molds bricks?
**91** Let a house be built for Baal like the gods',
**92** a court like the sons of Athirat!"

**93** Lady Athirat of the Sea replied:
**94** "Great indeed is El! Wise you are!
**95** Your grey beard instructs you!
**96** Now Baal shall have rain in his season,
**97** the season of the chariot in the snow.

**98** He shall sound his voice in the clouds,
**99** flash lightning to the earth.
**100** Let Kothar-wa-Khasis build the house,
**101** let him erect the palace!"

**102** El gave his decree: the house was to be built.
