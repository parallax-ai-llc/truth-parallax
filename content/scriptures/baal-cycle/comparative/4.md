The palace-building theme connects the Baal Cycle to a widespread ancient Near Eastern pattern:
- **Babylonian**: After defeating Tiamat, Marduk builds his palace Esagila (Enuma Elish V-VI)
- **Biblical**: After establishing his kingship, Solomon builds the Temple (1 Kings 5-8)
- **Sumerian**: Ningirsu receives his temple after Gudea's divine dream

The pattern — cosmic victory followed by palace/temple construction — reveals a fundamental theological principle: legitimate sovereignty is demonstrated through building. The king who can build a house is the king who truly rules.
