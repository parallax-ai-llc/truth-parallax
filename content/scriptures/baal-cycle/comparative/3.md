Anat's warrior exploits parallel those of other divine warrior women in world mythology:
- **Hindu**: Durga's battle against Mahishasura, wading through demon blood
- **Greek**: Athena as warrior goddess, though more restrained than Anat
- **Irish**: The Morrigan as battle goddess
- **Mesopotamian**: Ishtar's warrior aspect

The graphic violence of Anat's battle scene is striking even by ancient standards. Her knee-deep wading through blood and her wearing of severed hands and heads as ornaments find their closest parallel in Hindu iconography of Kali.
