Baal's battle with Yamm, using divinely crafted weapons, closely parallels several traditions:
- **Babylonian**: Marduk defeats Tiamat with winds and a net (Enuma Elish IV)
- **Greek**: Zeus defeats Typhon with thunderbolts
- **Hindu**: Indra defeats Vritra with the vajra (thunderbolt) crafted by Tvastr
- **Norse**: Thor battles the Midgard Serpent with Mjolnir

The pattern of the storm god receiving specially crafted weapons from a divine artisan before battle is a shared motif that suggests deep cultural connections. The Hebrew Bible's adaptation is distinctive: YHWH needs no weapons or artisan — he defeats the sea by his own word and power.
