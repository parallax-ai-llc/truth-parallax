---
title: "Sentences of Sextus - Chapter 2"
scripture: "sentences-of-sextus"
chapter: 2
---

**1** You cannot receive anything from God if your soul is not in accord with God.

**2** God does not hear the prayer of one who does not hear the cry of the poor.

**3** A man who loves God is known to God.

**4** Know who God is, and know who thinks within you. A good man is the good work of God.

**5** The body must be treated as the temple of God.

**6** He who directs his mind toward God attains God.

**7** The thought of a wise man is always with God.

**8** The man who says that he knows God, but does not do what is worthy of God, is a liar.

**9** Say with your mind that the body is the garment of your soul. Keep it therefore pure, since it is innocent.

**10** Whatever sin the body does, is from the soul. For if it were not for the soul, the body would do nothing.

**11** The faithful soul is a soul which has God.

**12** Choose for yourself that which is most profitable: a holy way of life.

**13** There is no possession on earth comparable to a good understanding.

**14** A wise man is a benefactor after God.

**15** Let your life confirm your word before those who hear.

**16** What the mind does not accept, faith does not practice.

**17** If a man has no evil desire, he is protected by God.

**18** A pure heart is the best temple for God.

**19** The beginning of sin is desire; the beginning of salvation is discipline.

**20** That which God will not do, He does not wish.
