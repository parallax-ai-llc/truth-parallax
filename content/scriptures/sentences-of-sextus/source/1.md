---
title: "Sentences of Sextus - Chapter 1"
scripture: "sentences-of-sextus"
chapter: 1
---

**1** A faithful man is an elect man of God.

**2** An elect man of God is a man who is worthy of God.

**3** A man who is worthy of God is one who does nothing unworthy of God.

**4** Strive earnestly to make yourself worthy of God.

**5** A wise man who shows forth wisdom in his life is a blessing to men.

**6** The wise man who does wisdom with his tongue alone is a burden.

**7** Esteem nothing so precious that it can be compared to the truth.

**8** It is better to cast a stone in vain than a word.

**9** Do not say anything that you have not first weighed in your mind.

**10** What it is not right to do, do not even consider doing.

**11** What you do not wish to happen to you, do not do it yourself either.

**12** What it is shameful to do is also shameful to speak about.

**13** Let not your tongue outrun your thought.

**14** Guard yourself from lying; there is one who deceives and there is one who is deceived.

**15** Before all men, guard your tongue.

**16** Accept the counsel of the wise man who fears God.

**17** A wise tongue reveals a wise heart.

**18** A lying tongue is a disease of the soul.

**19** When you have something to say, first consider it in your heart, and then speak.

**20** Choose rather to be a listener than a speaker.
