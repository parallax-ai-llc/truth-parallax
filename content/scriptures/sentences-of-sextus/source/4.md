---
title: "Sentences of Sextus - Chapter 4"
scripture: "sentences-of-sextus"
chapter: 4
---

**1** Be as one who is a stranger in the world: for a stranger you are.

**2** Every gift of God is good, and there is nothing to be rejected if it is received with thanksgiving.

**3** Trust your possessions to God, and you will be a true steward.

**4** A man who boasts of his gift is ungrateful to the giver.

**5** Every good gift comes from God.

**6** The true fast is to fast from every evil deed.

**7** If you fast from bread, fast also from evil speech.

**8** Better is a man who fasts in secret from evil than one who fasts openly from food.

**9** The man who loves men serves God.

**10** The man who serves men worthily serves God.

**11** After God, honor a wise man; for he is the servant of God.

**12** The soul of a wise man is always with God.

**13** A wicked man is his own worst enemy, for he works evil against his own soul.

**14** Do not seek to become wealthy through injustice; for what is unjustly gained perishes.

**15** Let your work be profitable to your neighbor. And you will have profit from God.

**16** No one lives rightly who lives for himself alone.

**17** He lives well who benefits his neighbor.

**18** When you eat, remember the poor.

**19** Blessed is he who gives to the needy from what he has.

**20** For with whatever measure you give, God will measure out to you.
