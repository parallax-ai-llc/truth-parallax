The birth of Teshub from Kumarbi's body parallels several traditions of extraordinary divine birth:
- **Greek**: Athena born from Zeus's head; Dionysus from Zeus's thigh
- **Hindu**: The gods and demons churned from the cosmic ocean
- **Norse**: The world created from the body of the giant Ymir

The storm god's birth as the culmination of a succession narrative connects to the broader pattern of the young storm god achieving supremacy: Marduk in the Enuma Elish, Baal in the Baal Cycle, Zeus in the Theogony. In each tradition, the storm god represents the establishment of a new, more just cosmic order.
