The birth of a new divine king speaks to the universal religious theme of renewal and new beginning. Across traditions, the emergence of the divine into the world takes the form of a birth:
- **Christianity**: The Incarnation — God born as a human child
- **Buddhism**: The birth of the Buddha as the beginning of a new dharma age
- **Hinduism**: The birth of Krishna and Rama as *avatars* of Vishnu
- **Zoroastrianism**: The birth of the Saoshyant (savior) at the end of time

The Song of Kumarbi presents this theme in its most mythologically radical form: the new god born from the body of the old god, carrying within him the power of all who came before.
