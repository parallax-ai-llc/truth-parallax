---
title: "Song of Kumarbi - Chapter 4"
scripture: "song-of-kumarbi"
chapter: 4
---

**1** Kumarbi, in his anger, conceived a plan against the Storm God. He went to the Sea, to the great waters.

**2** And the Sea bore unto Kumarbi a mighty son, a creature of stone, whose name was Ullikummi.

**3** Ullikummi was placed upon the shoulder of Upelluri, the giant who holds up the world. And Ullikummi grew.

**4** He grew one acre in a month. In fifteen days, he grew a full fathom. The sea reached only to his waist, and he grew still further.

**5** When the Sun God saw Ullikummi, he was filled with dismay. He went to the Storm God and reported what he had seen.

**6** The Storm God assembled the gods for battle. Ishtar sang before Ullikummi, but the creature of stone had no ears to hear and no eyes to see.

**7** The Storm God attacked with all his might, but Ullikummi could not be moved. The Storm God was defeated and cast down from his chariot.

**8** The gods were filled with despair. Who could defeat this creature born of Kumarbi's vengeance?

**9** Then Ea, the wise god of the deep waters, spoke: "There is a way. In the old times, when heaven and earth were separated from each other, the ancient copper cutting tool was used."

**10** "Let us use that tool to cut Ullikummi from the shoulder of Upelluri, and his power shall be broken."
