---
title: "Song of Kumarbi - Chapter 5"
scripture: "song-of-kumarbi"
chapter: 5
---

**1** They brought the ancient copper cutting tool, the instrument with which heaven and earth had been separated in the beginning of time.

**2** They cut the feet of Ullikummi from the shoulder of Upelluri, the giant who supports the world.

**3** When Ullikummi was cut from his base, his power diminished. He who had been like a mountain became weak and began to totter.

**4** The Storm God mounted his chariot once more, and the gods rallied to his side. With renewed strength they attacked Ullikummi.

**5** The earth shook, the heavens trembled, and the sea was stirred to its depths as the gods made war upon the creature of stone.

**6** And Ullikummi fell. The mighty champion of Kumarbi was overthrown, and the threat to the gods was ended.

**7** The Storm God returned to his throne in heaven, and the gods praised him. Order was restored to the universe, and peace returned to the divine assembly.

**8** Kumarbi's plot had failed. For though he was cunning, the wisdom of Ea and the might of the Storm God were greater.

**9** And so the kingship in heaven was confirmed upon the Storm God, and he ruled over gods and men with justice and power.

**10** This is the Song of Kumarbi, which tells of the succession of the gods, the rise of the Storm God, and the defeat of Ullikummi. Let those who hear it remember: the old gives way to the new, and the purposes of the gods cannot be thwarted.
