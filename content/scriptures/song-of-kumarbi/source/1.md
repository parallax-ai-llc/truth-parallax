---
title: "Song of Kumarbi - Chapter 1"
scripture: "song-of-kumarbi"
chapter: 1
---

**1** Let there listen the gods, let there listen the thousand gods, the great gods of Hatti. Let Nara, Napshara, Minki, and Ammunki listen.

**2** Let Ammezzadu listen, and the ancient gods, the fathers and mothers of the gods, let them listen.

**3** In former days, in ancient days, in olden days, Alalu was king in heaven. Alalu was seated on the throne, and mighty Anu, the first among the gods, stood before him.

**4** He bowed down at the feet of Alalu and placed the drinking cup in his hand. For nine counted years, Alalu was king in heaven.

**5** In the ninth year, Anu gave battle against Alalu. He defeated Alalu, and Alalu fled before him. He went down to the dark earth.

**6** Down to the dark earth he went, and Anu seated himself on the throne. Anu was seated on the throne, and mighty Kumarbi served him his food.

**7** He bowed down at the feet of Anu and placed the drinking cup in his hand.

**8** For nine counted years, Anu was king in heaven. In the ninth year, Anu gave battle against Kumarbi.

**9** Kumarbi, the offspring of Alalu, gave battle against Anu. Anu could no longer withstand the eyes of Kumarbi. He slipped away from Kumarbi's hands and fled.

**10** He fled like a bird into the sky. Kumarbi rushed after him, seized Anu by his feet, and dragged him down from the sky.
