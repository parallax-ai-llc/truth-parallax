---
title: "Tripartite Tractate Section 6"
scripture: "tripartite-tractate"
chapter: 6
---

**1** And the Son descended from the fullness, passing through the aeons, and he entered the realm of the Demiurge. He clothed himself in the garments of the three orders, so that he might be present to all.

**2** To the spiritual, he appeared as spirit. To the psychic, he appeared as soul. And to the material, he appeared as flesh. For the Son adapted himself to the nature of those he came to save.

**3** And the Son came into the world of the Demiurge, and the Demiurge did not know him. For the Demiurge perceived only his outer garment, the garment of flesh.

**4** And the Son spoke the words of truth to humanity. And those who had the spiritual seed within them recognized his words immediately, for the seed responded to the voice of the Son as a child responds to its parent.

**5** And those who were psychic heard his words and were moved by them, though they did not fully understand. They received the teaching with faith, even where they lacked full comprehension.

**6** But those who were material did not hear. They perceived only the outward form, and they were offended, for the truth was contrary to their nature.

**7** And the archons perceived that their authority was being undermined by the words of the Son. They rose up against him, and they handed him over to death.

**8** But the death of the Son was the means by which the spiritual seeds were fully awakened. For in his death, the power of the fullness was released into the world of the Demiurge.

**9** And in his resurrection, the Son demonstrated the victory of the spiritual over the material. He showed that death has no power over the one who possesses the spiritual seed.

**10** And the Demiurge himself was moved by these events. He began to perceive that there was a power above him, and he began to turn toward the truth. For the psychic nature has the capacity for conversion.
