---
title: "Tripartite Tractate Section 8"
scripture: "tripartite-tractate"
chapter: 8
---

**1** And at the consummation of the age, all things shall be restored. The Logos shall be reunited with his fullness, and the deficiency shall be abolished.

**2** For the deficiency came into being because of ignorance, and when gnosis is complete, the deficiency shall disappear. As a dream dissolves when the sleeper awakens, so shall the deficiency dissolve when the truth is fully known.

**3** And the spiritual seeds shall be gathered from every place where they have been sown, and they shall be united with the aeons in the fullness. They shall put off the garments of the material world and clothe themselves in the light of the Father.

**4** And the psychic nature shall be elevated. Those who turned toward the truth in faith shall receive the reward of their faith. They shall dwell in the place that is prepared for them, in the realm of the Demiurge who has himself been converted.

**5** For the Demiurge, having recognized the Father, shall become the servant of the fullness. He shall govern his realm in accordance with the will of the Father, and his realm shall become a place of rest for the psychic nature.

**6** But the material nature, which has no capacity for the divine, shall be dissolved. It shall return to the nothingness from which it came. For the material nature was never real; it was only a shadow cast by the deficiency.

**7** And when the material nature is dissolved, the fullness shall be complete. The Father shall be all in all. The Son shall present the Totalities to the Father, and the Father shall receive them, and the unity shall be restored.

**8** And the aeons shall rejoice, and the Church shall sing praise, and the harmony of the fullness shall be perfect. For the deficiency was a temporary condition, not an eternal one.

**9** And the struggle between ignorance and gnosis shall come to an end. For gnosis shall prevail, as light always prevails over darkness. And the darkness shall be no more, for it shall be revealed as nothing.

**10** And the Father shall look upon all that has been restored, and he shall be pleased. For his will is that all things should return to the unity from which they came.
