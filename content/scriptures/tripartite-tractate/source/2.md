---
title: "Tripartite Tractate Section 2"
scripture: "tripartite-tractate"
chapter: 2
---

**1** The Father brought forth the Totalities as an emanation, not as a division of himself, but as a manifestation. He produced a Son, who is the First, the Only-begotten of the Father.

**2** The Son is in the bosom of the Father from the beginning. He possesses the mind of the Father within him, being the thought and the wisdom and the grace and the power of the Father.

**3** And the Father sent forth the Son as a revelation of himself to the Totalities. And the Son is the name of the Father, for it is through the Son that the Father is known.

**4** Before anything was visible among the things that exist, the Father and the Son existed. And the Father is the head and the Son is the body, and together they form the fullness.

**5** And the Son, in whom the Father is pleased, emanated the Totalities according to the will of the Father. And the Totalities are the aeons, the eternal beings who dwell in the fullness.

**6** Each aeon has its own thought and its own will and its own grace, but all are united in the Son, and through the Son in the Father.

**7** And the aeons love the Father through the Son, and they know the Father through the Son, and they glorify the Father through the Son. For the Son is the face of the Father turned toward the Totalities.

**8** And the Church is the assembly of the aeons, the gathering of all that has emanated from the Father through the Son. And the Church praises the Father in unity.

**9** For the Father, the Son, and the Church are the three that constitute the fullness. And the fullness is the dwelling place of the divine, where all things are in harmony and all things are at rest.

**10** And the aeons in the fullness know no deficiency, for they dwell in the abundance of the Father. They know no ignorance, for the light of the Son illumines them. They know no separation, for the unity of the Church binds them together.
