---
title: "Tripartite Tractate Section 4"
scripture: "tripartite-tractate"
chapter: 4
---

**1** Now the Logos brought forth the Demiurge, the craftsman of the material world. The Demiurge was psychic in nature, not spiritual, for he came from the turning back of the Logos.

**2** And the Demiurge did not know the fullness, nor did he know the Father. He thought himself to be the only God, and he fashioned the world according to his own understanding.

**3** But unknowingly he worked according to the plan of the Logos, who guided him from above. For the Logos used the Demiurge as an instrument to bring order to the material realm.

**4** And the Demiurge fashioned the heavens and the earth, and the stars and the elements. And he created living beings of every kind, and he set them in their places.

**5** And he was pleased with his work, and he said: I am God, and there is no other beside me. But he spoke in ignorance, for the Father of the Totalities is above him, and the Son is above him, and the aeons of the fullness are above him.

**6** And the Logos placed within the creation of the Demiurge the seeds of the three orders: the spiritual, the psychic, and the material. These seeds were hidden, and the Demiurge did not perceive them.

**7** And the spiritual seeds were placed in the human beings, as sparks of the divine light, waiting to be awakened. And the psychic seeds were placed in the souls, giving them the capacity for turning toward the light or away from it.

**8** And the material nature was given form by the Demiurge, but it has no capacity for the divine. It is destined to be dissolved when the consummation comes.

**9** And the human beings were fashioned by the Demiurge in his own image, but they carried within them the image of the Father, placed there by the Logos. And this image is the spiritual seed.

**10** And the Demiurge breathed into the human beings the breath of life, which is the psychic nature. But the spiritual seed he did not know, for it was hidden from him by the Logos.
