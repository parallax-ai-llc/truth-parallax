---
title: "Tripartite Tractate Section 7"
scripture: "tripartite-tractate"
chapter: 7
---

**1** After the Son ascended back to the fullness, he sent the Spirit into the world, to continue the work of awakening the spiritual seeds.

**2** And the Spirit works invisibly within the hearts of human beings, stirring the seeds and calling them to remember their origin in the Father.

**3** And the Church in the world is the gathering of those who have been awakened. It is the image of the Church in the fullness, the assembly of the aeons.

**4** But the Church in the world is not yet perfect, for it contains both the spiritual and the psychic. And the two are mingled together until the consummation, when they shall be separated.

**5** The spiritual members of the Church know the Father directly, through the seed within them. They do not need faith, for they have gnosis. They perceive the truth as it is, without a veil.

**6** The psychic members of the Church know the Father through faith. They believe the truth, even where they do not fully comprehend it. And their faith is acceptable, for the Father does not reject those who turn toward him.

**7** And the material members of the world cannot enter the Church, for they have no capacity for the divine. They remain in the world of the Demiurge, bound by the material nature.

**8** But the Father is patient, and the Son is merciful, and the Spirit continues to work. And even those who seem to be material may discover within themselves a seed that they did not know was there.

**9** For the ways of the Father are beyond our comprehension. He has placed his seeds in places where no one would think to look. And the harvest shall be greater than any have imagined.

**10** And the Spirit teaches the Church the mysteries of the fullness, so that the awakened ones may know their origin and their destination. For the Church is the body of the Son, and the Son is the face of the Father.
