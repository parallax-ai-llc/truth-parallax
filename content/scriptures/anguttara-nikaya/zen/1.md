## Book of Ones

Zen Buddhism finds in this teaching on fundamental singular teachings on the nature of mind and basic Buddhist principles a directness and practicality that resonates with Zen values. While Zen emphasizes experience beyond textual study, these teachings provide foundations for the mindful awareness and direct insight that are central to Zen practice.
