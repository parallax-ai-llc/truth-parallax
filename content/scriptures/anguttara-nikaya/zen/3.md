## Book of Threes

Zen Buddhism finds in this teaching on triadic teachings including the three characteristics, three trainings, and three types of suffering a directness and practicality that resonates with Zen values. While Zen emphasizes experience beyond textual study, these teachings provide foundations for the mindful awareness and direct insight that are central to Zen practice.
