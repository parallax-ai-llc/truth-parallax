## Book of Nines

Zen Buddhism finds in this teaching on nine-fold teachings on the progressive abodes of beings and sequential attainments a directness and practicality that resonates with Zen values. While Zen emphasizes experience beyond textual study, these teachings provide foundations for the mindful awareness and direct insight that are central to Zen practice.
