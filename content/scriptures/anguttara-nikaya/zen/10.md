## Book of Tens

Zen practitioners appreciate this section on ten-fold teachings on the fetters, perfections, and powers of a Tathagata for its emphasis on direct experience and practical application. The teaching's focus on immediate awareness and the transformation of consciousness aligns with the Zen aim of awakening to one's true nature in this very moment.
