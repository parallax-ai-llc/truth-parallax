## Book of Eights

Zen practitioners appreciate this section on eight-fold teachings including the Noble Eightfold Path and the eight worldly conditions for its emphasis on direct experience and practical application. The teaching's focus on immediate awareness and the transformation of consciousness aligns with the Zen aim of awakening to one's true nature in this very moment.
