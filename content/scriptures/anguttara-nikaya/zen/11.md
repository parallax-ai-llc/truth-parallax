## Book of Elevens

Zen Buddhism finds in this teaching on eleven-fold groupings as the culmination of the numerical system a directness and practicality that resonates with Zen values. While Zen emphasizes experience beyond textual study, these teachings provide foundations for the mindful awareness and direct insight that are central to Zen practice.
