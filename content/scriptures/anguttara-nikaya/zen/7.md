## Book of Sevens

Zen Buddhism finds in this teaching on seven-fold teachings including the factors of awakening and the seven treasures of a noble one a directness and practicality that resonates with Zen values. While Zen emphasizes experience beyond textual study, these teachings provide foundations for the mindful awareness and direct insight that are central to Zen practice.
