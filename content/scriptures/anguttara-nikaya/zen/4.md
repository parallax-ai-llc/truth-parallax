## Book of Fours

Zen practitioners appreciate this section on four-fold teachings including the four noble truths, four bases of power, and four divine abidings for its emphasis on direct experience and practical application. The teaching's focus on immediate awareness and the transformation of consciousness aligns with the Zen aim of awakening to one's true nature in this very moment.
