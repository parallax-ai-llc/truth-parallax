## Overview

Zen Buddhism appreciates the Anguttara Nikaya's practical, direct approach to Buddhist teaching. The collection's emphasis on actionable ethical guidance and its avoidance of excessive metaphysical speculation align with Zen values.

## Zen Reading

Zen practitioners value the Anguttara Nikaya's short, pithy teachings that can be applied directly to daily life. The collection's emphasis on the quality of mind in every activity resonates with the Zen practice of bringing mindfulness to every moment.

## Relevance to Practice

The Anguttara Nikaya's teachings on mindfulness, ethical conduct, and the gradual cultivation of wisdom complement the specifically Zen practices of zazen and koan study.
