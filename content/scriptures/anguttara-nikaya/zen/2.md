## Book of Twos

Zen practitioners appreciate this section on paired teachings on contrasting qualities, wholesome and unwholesome states for its emphasis on direct experience and practical application. The teaching's focus on immediate awareness and the transformation of consciousness aligns with the Zen aim of awakening to one's true nature in this very moment.
