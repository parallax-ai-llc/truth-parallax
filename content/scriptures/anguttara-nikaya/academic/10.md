## Book of Tens

From an academic perspective, this teaching on ten-fold teachings on the fetters, perfections, and powers of a Tathagata is examined for its compositional history, its relationship to parallel versions in other Buddhist canons, and its significance for reconstructing the teachings of the historical Buddha.
