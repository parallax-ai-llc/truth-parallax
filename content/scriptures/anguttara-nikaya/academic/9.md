## Book of Nines

Academic scholars analyze this section on nine-fold teachings on the progressive abodes of beings and sequential attainments using the methods of textual criticism, historical contextualization, and comparative study. The text provides evidence for understanding the development of early Buddhist thought and its relationship to the broader intellectual culture of ancient India.
