## Book of Twos

From an academic perspective, this teaching on paired teachings on contrasting qualities, wholesome and unwholesome states is examined for its compositional history, its relationship to parallel versions in other Buddhist canons, and its significance for reconstructing the teachings of the historical Buddha.
