## Book of Ones

Academic scholars analyze this section on fundamental singular teachings on the nature of mind and basic Buddhist principles using the methods of textual criticism, historical contextualization, and comparative study. The text provides evidence for understanding the development of early Buddhist thought and its relationship to the broader intellectual culture of ancient India.
