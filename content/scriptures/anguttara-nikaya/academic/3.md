## Book of Threes

Academic scholars analyze this section on triadic teachings including the three characteristics, three trainings, and three types of suffering using the methods of textual criticism, historical contextualization, and comparative study. The text provides evidence for understanding the development of early Buddhist thought and its relationship to the broader intellectual culture of ancient India.
