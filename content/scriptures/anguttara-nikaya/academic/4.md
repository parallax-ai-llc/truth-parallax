## Book of Fours

From an academic perspective, this teaching on four-fold teachings including the four noble truths, four bases of power, and four divine abidings is examined for its compositional history, its relationship to parallel versions in other Buddhist canons, and its significance for reconstructing the teachings of the historical Buddha.
