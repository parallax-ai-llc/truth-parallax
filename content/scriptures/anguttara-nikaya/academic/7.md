## Book of Sevens

Academic scholars analyze this section on seven-fold teachings including the factors of awakening and the seven treasures of a noble one using the methods of textual criticism, historical contextualization, and comparative study. The text provides evidence for understanding the development of early Buddhist thought and its relationship to the broader intellectual culture of ancient India.
