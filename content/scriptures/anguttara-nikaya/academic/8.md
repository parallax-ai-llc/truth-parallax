## Book of Eights

From an academic perspective, this teaching on eight-fold teachings including the Noble Eightfold Path and the eight worldly conditions is examined for its compositional history, its relationship to parallel versions in other Buddhist canons, and its significance for reconstructing the teachings of the historical Buddha.
