## Book of Elevens

Academic scholars analyze this section on eleven-fold groupings as the culmination of the numerical system using the methods of textual criticism, historical contextualization, and comparative study. The text provides evidence for understanding the development of early Buddhist thought and its relationship to the broader intellectual culture of ancient India.
