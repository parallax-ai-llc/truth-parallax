## Book of Sixes

From an academic perspective, this teaching on six-fold teachings on the sense bases, recollections, and types of harmony is examined for its compositional history, its relationship to parallel versions in other Buddhist canons, and its significance for reconstructing the teachings of the historical Buddha.
