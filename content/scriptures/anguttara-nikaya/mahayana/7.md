## Book of Sevens

Mahayana Buddhism engages with this teaching on seven-fold teachings including the factors of awakening and the seven treasures of a noble one through its parallel Agama traditions and recognizes it as foundational material upon which later Mahayana developments build. The emphasis on compassion, wisdom, and the welfare of all beings connects these early teachings to the bodhisattva ideal.
