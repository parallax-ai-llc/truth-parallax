## Book of Twos

From the Mahayana perspective, this section on paired teachings on contrasting qualities, wholesome and unwholesome states preserves important foundational teachings that the Mahayana tradition inherits and develops. Mahayana scholars identify in these early discourses the seeds of later philosophical developments in Madhyamaka and Yogachara thought.
