## Book of Elevens

Mahayana Buddhism engages with this teaching on eleven-fold groupings as the culmination of the numerical system through its parallel Agama traditions and recognizes it as foundational material upon which later Mahayana developments build. The emphasis on compassion, wisdom, and the welfare of all beings connects these early teachings to the bodhisattva ideal.
