## Overview

Mahayana Buddhism engages with the Anguttara Nikaya through its Chinese parallel, the Ekottara Agama, which contains additional material with distinctly Mahayana features. The collection's practical ethical teachings provide foundations for the Mahayana emphasis on compassion and skillful engagement with the world.

## Key Themes

Mahayana scholars note that the Ekottara Agama version contains passages that emphasize the bodhisattva ideal, suggesting early Mahayana influences. The Anguttara Nikaya's teachings on generosity, compassion, and ethical conduct align with Mahayana values.

## Relevance to Mahayana

The collection's teachings on the cultivation of wholesome mental states, the practice of generosity, and the development of wisdom provide practical foundations shared across all Buddhist traditions.
