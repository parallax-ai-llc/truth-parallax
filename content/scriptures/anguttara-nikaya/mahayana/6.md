## Book of Sixes

From the Mahayana perspective, this section on six-fold teachings on the sense bases, recollections, and types of harmony preserves important foundational teachings that the Mahayana tradition inherits and develops. Mahayana scholars identify in these early discourses the seeds of later philosophical developments in Madhyamaka and Yogachara thought.
