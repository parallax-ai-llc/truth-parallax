## Book of Tens

From the Mahayana perspective, this section on ten-fold teachings on the fetters, perfections, and powers of a Tathagata preserves important foundational teachings that the Mahayana tradition inherits and develops. Mahayana scholars identify in these early discourses the seeds of later philosophical developments in Madhyamaka and Yogachara thought.
