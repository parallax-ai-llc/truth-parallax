## Book of Threes

Mahayana Buddhism engages with this teaching on triadic teachings including the three characteristics, three trainings, and three types of suffering through its parallel Agama traditions and recognizes it as foundational material upon which later Mahayana developments build. The emphasis on compassion, wisdom, and the welfare of all beings connects these early teachings to the bodhisattva ideal.
