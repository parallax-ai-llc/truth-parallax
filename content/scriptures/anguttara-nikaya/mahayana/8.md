## Book of Eights

From the Mahayana perspective, this section on eight-fold teachings including the Noble Eightfold Path and the eight worldly conditions preserves important foundational teachings that the Mahayana tradition inherits and develops. Mahayana scholars identify in these early discourses the seeds of later philosophical developments in Madhyamaka and Yogachara thought.
