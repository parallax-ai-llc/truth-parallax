## Book of Ones

Mahayana Buddhism engages with this teaching on fundamental singular teachings on the nature of mind and basic Buddhist principles through its parallel Agama traditions and recognizes it as foundational material upon which later Mahayana developments build. The emphasis on compassion, wisdom, and the welfare of all beings connects these early teachings to the bodhisattva ideal.
