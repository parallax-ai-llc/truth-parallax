## Book of Fours

From the Mahayana perspective, this section on four-fold teachings including the four noble truths, four bases of power, and four divine abidings preserves important foundational teachings that the Mahayana tradition inherits and develops. Mahayana scholars identify in these early discourses the seeds of later philosophical developments in Madhyamaka and Yogachara thought.
