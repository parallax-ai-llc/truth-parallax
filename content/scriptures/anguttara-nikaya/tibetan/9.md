## Book of Nines

Tibetan Buddhism engages with this teaching on nine-fold teachings on the progressive abodes of beings and sequential attainments as part of the foundational sutra vehicle upon which the complete Tibetan Buddhist path is constructed. These teachings inform the analytical meditation practices and the stages-of-the-path (lam-rim) literature that structures Tibetan Buddhist education.
