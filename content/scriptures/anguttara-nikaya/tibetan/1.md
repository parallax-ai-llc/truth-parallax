## Book of Ones

Tibetan Buddhism engages with this teaching on fundamental singular teachings on the nature of mind and basic Buddhist principles as part of the foundational sutra vehicle upon which the complete Tibetan Buddhist path is constructed. These teachings inform the analytical meditation practices and the stages-of-the-path (lam-rim) literature that structures Tibetan Buddhist education.
