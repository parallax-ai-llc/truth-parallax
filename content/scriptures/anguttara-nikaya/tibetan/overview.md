## Overview

Tibetan Buddhism values the Anguttara Nikaya's systematic enumeration of Buddhist teachings as a reference for the comprehensive doctrinal education provided in Tibetan monastic institutions. The numerical format facilitates the memorization and debate that are central to Tibetan scholastic training.

## Key Themes

Tibetan scholars appreciate the collection's systematic treatment of the hindrances, the factors of awakening, and the graduated stages of ethical and meditative development, which inform the lam-rim (stages of the path) literature.

## Relevance to Tibetan Study

The Anguttara Nikaya's numbered teachings provide ready reference points for the topical discussions that form the basis of Tibetan monastic debate.
