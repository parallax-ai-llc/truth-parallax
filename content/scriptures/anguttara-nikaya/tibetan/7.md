## Book of Sevens

Tibetan Buddhism engages with this teaching on seven-fold teachings including the factors of awakening and the seven treasures of a noble one as part of the foundational sutra vehicle upon which the complete Tibetan Buddhist path is constructed. These teachings inform the analytical meditation practices and the stages-of-the-path (lam-rim) literature that structures Tibetan Buddhist education.
