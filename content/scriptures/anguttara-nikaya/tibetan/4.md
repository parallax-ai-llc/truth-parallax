## Book of Fours

In the Tibetan scholastic tradition, this section on four-fold teachings including the four noble truths, four bases of power, and four divine abidings provides essential doctrinal foundations studied in the monastic curriculum. Tibetan scholars connect these teachings to the broader framework of Buddhist philosophy, from Abhidharma analysis through Madhyamaka and Yogachara to tantric practice.
