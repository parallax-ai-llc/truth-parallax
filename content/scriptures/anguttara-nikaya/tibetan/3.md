## Book of Threes

Tibetan Buddhism engages with this teaching on triadic teachings including the three characteristics, three trainings, and three types of suffering as part of the foundational sutra vehicle upon which the complete Tibetan Buddhist path is constructed. These teachings inform the analytical meditation practices and the stages-of-the-path (lam-rim) literature that structures Tibetan Buddhist education.
