## Book of Eights

In the Tibetan scholastic tradition, this section on eight-fold teachings including the Noble Eightfold Path and the eight worldly conditions provides essential doctrinal foundations studied in the monastic curriculum. Tibetan scholars connect these teachings to the broader framework of Buddhist philosophy, from Abhidharma analysis through Madhyamaka and Yogachara to tantric practice.
