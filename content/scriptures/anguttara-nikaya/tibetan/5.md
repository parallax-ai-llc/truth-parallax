## Book of Fives

Tibetan Buddhism engages with this teaching on five-fold teachings on the aggregates, hindrances, faculties, and powers as part of the foundational sutra vehicle upon which the complete Tibetan Buddhist path is constructed. These teachings inform the analytical meditation practices and the stages-of-the-path (lam-rim) literature that structures Tibetan Buddhist education.
