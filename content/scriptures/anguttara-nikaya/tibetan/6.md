## Book of Sixes

In the Tibetan scholastic tradition, this section on six-fold teachings on the sense bases, recollections, and types of harmony provides essential doctrinal foundations studied in the monastic curriculum. Tibetan scholars connect these teachings to the broader framework of Buddhist philosophy, from Abhidharma analysis through Madhyamaka and Yogachara to tantric practice.
