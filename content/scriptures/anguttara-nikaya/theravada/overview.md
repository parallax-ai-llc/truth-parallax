## Overview

The Anguttara Nikaya holds a special place in the Theravada tradition for its practical ethical teachings and its accessibility to both monastic and lay audiences. Its numerical organization makes it an invaluable reference for the entire range of Buddhist teaching.

## Key Themes

Theravada interpreters value the Anguttara Nikaya for its extensive teachings addressed to laypeople on topics such as friendship, livelihood, family relations, and community life. The collection also contains important doctrinal discussions on the hindrances, the factors of awakening, and the stages of the path.

## Practice and Study

The Anguttara Nikaya's numbered format makes it particularly useful for memorization and recitation. Its practical teachings are frequently cited in Dhamma talks addressed to lay audiences.
