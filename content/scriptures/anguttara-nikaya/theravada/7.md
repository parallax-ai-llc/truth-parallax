## Book of Sevens

The Theravada tradition regards this section on seven-fold teachings including the factors of awakening and the seven treasures of a noble one as essential canonical teaching. Theravada interpreters emphasize its direct relevance to meditation practice and the cultivation of wisdom, reading it as part of the complete path from morality through concentration to liberating insight.
