## Book of Elevens

The Theravada tradition regards this section on eleven-fold groupings as the culmination of the numerical system as essential canonical teaching. Theravada interpreters emphasize its direct relevance to meditation practice and the cultivation of wisdom, reading it as part of the complete path from morality through concentration to liberating insight.
