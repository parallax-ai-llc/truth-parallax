## Book of Eights

In the Theravada tradition, this teaching on eight-fold teachings including the Noble Eightfold Path and the eight worldly conditions holds a position of central importance for both doctrinal understanding and practical application. Monks and scholars study this section as part of the comprehensive training in the Buddha's teaching, connecting its insights to the daily practice of mindfulness and ethical conduct.
