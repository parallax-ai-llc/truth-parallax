## Book of Ones

The Theravada tradition regards this section on fundamental singular teachings on the nature of mind and basic Buddhist principles as essential canonical teaching. Theravada interpreters emphasize its direct relevance to meditation practice and the cultivation of wisdom, reading it as part of the complete path from morality through concentration to liberating insight.
