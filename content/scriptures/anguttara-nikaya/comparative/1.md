## Book of Ones

Comparative interpretation of this teaching on fundamental singular teachings on the nature of mind and basic Buddhist principles reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar teachings in Hindu, Jain, Christian, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Buddhist perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
