## Book of Sixes

This section on six-fold teachings on the sense bases, recollections, and types of harmony provides rich material for cross-traditional analysis. Comparative scholars examine how the Buddhist approach to these questions relates to and differs from the approaches of other major religious and philosophical traditions.
