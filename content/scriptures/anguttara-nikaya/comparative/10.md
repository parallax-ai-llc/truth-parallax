## Book of Tens

This section on ten-fold teachings on the fetters, perfections, and powers of a Tathagata provides rich material for cross-traditional analysis. Comparative scholars examine how the Buddhist approach to these questions relates to and differs from the approaches of other major religious and philosophical traditions.
