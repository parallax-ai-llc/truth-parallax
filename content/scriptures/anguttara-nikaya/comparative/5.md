## Book of Fives

Comparative interpretation of this teaching on five-fold teachings on the aggregates, hindrances, faculties, and powers reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar teachings in Hindu, Jain, Christian, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Buddhist perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
