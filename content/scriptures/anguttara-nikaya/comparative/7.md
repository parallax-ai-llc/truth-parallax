## Book of Sevens

Comparative interpretation of this teaching on seven-fold teachings including the factors of awakening and the seven treasures of a noble one reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar teachings in Hindu, Jain, Christian, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Buddhist perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
