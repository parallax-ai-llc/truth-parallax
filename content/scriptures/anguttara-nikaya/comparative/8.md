## Book of Eights

This section on eight-fold teachings including the Noble Eightfold Path and the eight worldly conditions provides rich material for cross-traditional analysis. Comparative scholars examine how the Buddhist approach to these questions relates to and differs from the approaches of other major religious and philosophical traditions.
