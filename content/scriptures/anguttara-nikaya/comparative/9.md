## Book of Nines

Comparative interpretation of this teaching on nine-fold teachings on the progressive abodes of beings and sequential attainments reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar teachings in Hindu, Jain, Christian, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Buddhist perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
