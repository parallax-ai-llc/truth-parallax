## Book of Elevens

Comparative interpretation of this teaching on eleven-fold groupings as the culmination of the numerical system reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar teachings in Hindu, Jain, Christian, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Buddhist perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
