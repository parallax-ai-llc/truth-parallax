## Book of Fours

This section on four-fold teachings including the four noble truths, four bases of power, and four divine abidings provides rich material for cross-traditional analysis. Comparative scholars examine how the Buddhist approach to these questions relates to and differs from the approaches of other major religious and philosophical traditions.
