## Book of Twos

This section on paired teachings on contrasting qualities, wholesome and unwholesome states provides rich material for cross-traditional analysis. Comparative scholars examine how the Buddhist approach to these questions relates to and differs from the approaches of other major religious and philosophical traditions.
