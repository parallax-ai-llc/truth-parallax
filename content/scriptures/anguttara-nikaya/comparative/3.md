## Book of Threes

Comparative interpretation of this teaching on triadic teachings including the three characteristics, three trainings, and three types of suffering reveals illuminating parallels and contrasts with similar teachings in Hindu, Jain, Christian, and other philosophical traditions. The distinctive Buddhist perspective enriches the global conversation about these fundamental questions.
