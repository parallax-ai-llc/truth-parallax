---
title: "The Saints and Martyrs"
scripture: "kebra-nagast"
chapter: 13
---

**1** Ethiopia has produced many saints and martyrs who gave their lives for the faith. The nine saints who came from Syria brought monasticism to Ethiopia.

**2** They translated the scriptures into Ge'ez, the ancient language of Ethiopia. They established monasteries in the mountains and on the islands of Lake Tana.

**3** King Lalibela carved eleven churches from solid rock, inspired by a vision of the heavenly Jerusalem. These rock-hewn churches stand to this day as a testament to the faith of Ethiopia.

**4** Through centuries of isolation, the monks and priests of Ethiopia preserved the ancient texts, the ancient chants, and the ancient ceremonies.

**5** The saints of Ethiopia are a cloud of witnesses, testifying to the endurance of the faith. Through persecution and suffering, they kept the light burning.
