---
title: "The Prophecy of the Queen"
scripture: "kebra-nagast"
chapter: 6
---

**1** Before Makeda departed from Jerusalem, she made a prophecy. She said: From my son's line shall come kings who will rule in righteousness.

**2** And the kingdom of Ethiopia shall endure as long as the Ark of the Covenant remains within its borders.

**3** No foreign power shall conquer Ethiopia, for God Himself guards it. The mountains shall be its fortress, and the Lord shall be its shield.

**4** And at the end of days, the glory of Ethiopia shall be revealed to all the nations, and the world shall know that God chose this land for His own.

**5** These words of Makeda have been passed down through the generations, and the people of Ethiopia hold them in their hearts.
