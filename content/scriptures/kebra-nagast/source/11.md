---
title: "The Glory of Zion"
scripture: "kebra-nagast"
chapter: 11
---

**1** Zion is the Ark of the Covenant, the throne of God on earth. Where Zion dwells, there is the glory of God.

**2** Zion dwelt in Jerusalem in the time of Solomon. But when the time came, God moved Zion to Ethiopia, to the city of Aksum.

**3** The glory that was Jerusalem's is now Ethiopia's. The blessing that was Israel's is now Ethiopia's. God chose Ethiopia to be the guardian of His covenant.

**4** And the Kebra Nagast says: Ethiopia shall stretch out her hands unto God. This prophecy is fulfilled, for Ethiopia has received the Ark and the glory of the Lord.

**5** Let all nations know: the God of Israel is the God of Ethiopia. The covenant made with Abraham is fulfilled in the kings of Ethiopia, who are of the seed of Solomon.
