---
title: "The Faith of Ethiopia"
scripture: "kebra-nagast"
chapter: 10
---

**1** The faith of Ethiopia is the faith of the fathers: Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob. It is the faith of Moses and David and Solomon.

**2** When Christ came, Ethiopia received Him gladly. The Ethiopian church is one of the oldest Christian churches in the world.

**3** The Ethiopian church preserves many ancient practices: the observance of the Sabbath as well as Sunday, the dietary laws of Leviticus, and the veneration of the Ark of the Covenant.

**4** The Book of Enoch, rejected by other churches, is preserved in the Ethiopian canon. The wisdom of the ancients is treasured in Ethiopia as nowhere else.

**5** The faith of Ethiopia has endured through persecution and isolation. Cut off from other Christian nations for centuries, the Ethiopian church maintained its traditions pure and intact.
