---
title: "The Birth of Menelik"
scripture: "kebra-nagast"
chapter: 3
---

**1** Makeda returned to her kingdom and gave birth to a son. She named him Menelik, which means "son of the wise man."

**2** When Menelik grew to manhood, he desired to see his father. He traveled to Jerusalem with a great company.

**3** Solomon received his son with joy. He recognized him immediately, for Menelik resembled his father in every way.

**4** Solomon wished Menelik to remain in Jerusalem and inherit his throne. But Menelik said: My mother's kingdom awaits me. I must return to Ethiopia.

**5** Solomon said: If you will not stay, then take with you the firstborn sons of my nobles, that they may serve you as their fathers serve me.

**6** And Solomon anointed Menelik as king of Ethiopia, and the firstborn sons of the nobles of Israel went with him.
