---
title: "The Glory of Kings"
scripture: "kebra-nagast"
chapter: 1
---

**1** This is the book of the Glory of Kings of Ethiopia, the Kebra Nagast. It tells how the glory of the kingdom of Solomon was transferred to Ethiopia through Menelik, son of Solomon and the Queen of Sheba.

**2** The glory of a kingdom is established by the will of God. He raises up whom He will and casts down whom He will. No kingdom endures except by the decree of the Almighty.

**3** In the beginning, God created Adam and gave him dominion over the earth. From Adam came the patriarchs, and from the patriarchs came the kings.

**4** The greatest of the kings was Solomon, son of David, who built the Temple in Jerusalem and whose wisdom was renowned throughout the world.

**5** But the glory of Solomon was destined to pass to another land, to Ethiopia, through the Queen of Sheba, whose name was Makeda.
