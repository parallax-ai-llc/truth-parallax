---
title: "The Ethiopian Church"
scripture: "kebra-nagast"
chapter: 5
---

**1** When the Ark came to Ethiopia, the people turned from the worship of the sun and the moon and the stars, and they worshipped the God of Israel.

**2** They learned the laws of Moses and the psalms of David. They kept the Sabbath and observed the feasts of Israel.

**3** And when Christ came into the world, Ethiopia was among the first nations to receive the gospel. The Ethiopian eunuch, whom Philip baptized, brought the faith back to his homeland.

**4** The Ethiopian church preserved the ancient traditions: the Sabbath, the dietary laws, the Ark of the Covenant. They kept the faith pure and undefiled.

**5** The kings of Ethiopia claimed descent from Solomon and Makeda, and they bore the title "Conquering Lion of the Tribe of Judah, Elect of God, King of Kings."

**6** This is the glory of Ethiopia, established by God and confirmed by the Ark of His Covenant.
