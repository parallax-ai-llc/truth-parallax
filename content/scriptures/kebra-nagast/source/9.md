---
title: "The Laws of the Kingdom"
scripture: "kebra-nagast"
chapter: 9
---

**1** The laws of Ethiopia were established by Menelik according to the laws of Moses and the wisdom of Solomon.

**2** The king was the supreme judge, but he was subject to the law of God. No king could rule against the commandments of the Almighty.

**3** Justice was dispensed at the gate. The elders of each community judged disputes according to the ancient law. Difficult cases were brought before the king.

**4** The tithe was collected for the support of the priests and the poor. One-tenth of all produce and livestock was given to the service of God.

**5** Murder, theft, adultery, and false witness were punished severely. The law was firm but just, and the people lived in security.
