---
title: "Solomon's Wisdom"
scripture: "kebra-nagast"
chapter: 7
---

**1** Solomon spoke three thousand proverbs, and his songs were a thousand and five. He spoke of trees, from the cedar of Lebanon to the hyssop that grows out of the wall.

**2** He spoke of beasts and of birds, of creeping things and of fish. People came from all nations to hear the wisdom of Solomon.

**3** But Solomon's greatest wisdom was this: The fear of the Lord is the beginning of wisdom, and the knowledge of the Holy One is understanding.

**4** And Solomon said to Menelik: My son, remember this above all: a wise king serves his people, and a righteous king serves God. Without righteousness, power is a curse.

**5** Walk in the ways of David your grandfather. Keep the commandments of the Lord. Build your kingdom on justice and mercy, and it shall endure forever.
