---
title: "The Dynasty of Menelik"
scripture: "kebra-nagast"
chapter: 8
---

**1** Menelik returned to Ethiopia and established his kingdom. He ruled with wisdom and justice, following the example of Solomon his father.

**2** He built temples and palaces. He established laws and appointed judges. He organized his kingdom according to the model he had seen in Jerusalem.

**3** The Ark of the Covenant was placed in the holy of holies at Aksum, and priests of the line of Azariah guarded it day and night.

**4** From Menelik descended a long line of kings, each anointed in the tradition of Solomon, each bearing the title "King of Kings, Conquering Lion of the Tribe of Judah."

**5** The dynasty of Menelik is the oldest royal dynasty in the world, stretching from Solomon to the present day.
