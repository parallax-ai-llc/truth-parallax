---
title: "The Coming of Christ"
scripture: "kebra-nagast"
chapter: 12
---

**1** The Kebra Nagast speaks of Christ, who was prophesied by the prophets of Israel and by the wise men of Ethiopia.

**2** The Queen of Sheba, when she visited Solomon, was shown a vision of the Messiah who would come from the line of David.

**3** And she said: Blessed is the womb that shall bear Him. Blessed is the generation that shall see Him. For He shall bring salvation to all the nations.

**4** When Christ was born in Bethlehem, the wise men came from the East to worship Him. Some traditions say that among them was a king of Ethiopia.

**5** Christ is the fulfillment of the covenant. In Him, the promises made to Abraham, David, and Solomon are completed. Through Him, all nations are blessed.
