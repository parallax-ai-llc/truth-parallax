---
title: "The Ark of the Covenant"
scripture: "kebra-nagast"
chapter: 4
---

**1** Before Menelik departed, the sons of the nobles of Israel, led by Azariah, son of the high priest Zadok, took the Ark of the Covenant from the Temple.

**2** They replaced it with a replica, and they carried the true Ark with them to Ethiopia. The Lord allowed this, for He had decreed that His glory should pass to Ethiopia.

**3** When Solomon discovered the theft, he was grieved. He sent messengers to bring back the Ark, but they could not overtake Menelik's company, for the Ark carried them with supernatural speed.

**4** The Ark crossed the Red Sea, as it had done in the time of Moses. It came to Ethiopia and was placed in the city of Aksum, where it remains to this day.

**5** And the glory of Israel passed to Ethiopia. The Ark of the Covenant, the throne of David, and the blessing of God were transferred to the kingdom of Menelik.

**6** From that time, the kings of Ethiopia have been of the line of Solomon and Makeda, the Lion of the Tribe of Judah.
