---
title: "The Enduring Glory"
scripture: "kebra-nagast"
chapter: 14
---

**1** The Kebra Nagast concludes with a promise: the glory of Ethiopia shall endure until the end of time.

**2** As long as the Ark of the Covenant remains at Aksum, the kingdom shall stand. As long as the kings are of the line of Solomon, the blessing shall continue.

**3** No foreign power shall permanently conquer Ethiopia. Though enemies may come, they shall be driven back. The mountains of Ethiopia are the fortress of God.

**4** And at the end of days, when Christ returns, Ethiopia shall be among the first to greet Him. For Ethiopia has kept the faith, guarded the Ark, and honored the covenant.

**5** This is the Kebra Nagast, the Glory of Kings. It is the national epic of Ethiopia, the foundation of its royal house, and the testimony of its ancient faith. May its glory endure forever.
