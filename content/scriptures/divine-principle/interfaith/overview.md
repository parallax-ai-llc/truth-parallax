## Overview

The Interfaith reading of the Divine Principle engages with Sun Myung Moon's systematic theology from the distinctive vantage point of the Interfaith tradition. This perspective brings particular attention to questions of creation theology, the nature of the Fall, the mechanics of restoration, and the eschatological expectations that animate the Unification movement. The Interfaith interpretation illuminates both the internal coherence of the Divine Principle's theological system and its points of contact and tension with broader religious and intellectual traditions.
