---
title: "Divine Principle Section 1"
scripture: "divine-principle"
chapter: 1
---

**1** Everyone, without exception, is struggling to gain happiness, but no one has been able to attain it. All human endeavors are directed toward the purpose of realizing the ideal of happiness.

**2** From the standpoint of the original ideal of creation, we were not meant to fall into ignorance. God, the Creator, did not create human beings to live in darkness and suffering.

**3** We are meant to know two kinds of truth: internal truth, which comes through religion, and external truth, which comes through science. Religion and science each pursue their respective truths in order to overcome the two aspects of human ignorance and attain full knowledge.

**4** Throughout history, religion and science have seemed to be in conflict. But this conflict is not fundamental. A new expression of truth must emerge which can reconcile religion and science, internal truth and external truth.

**5** The purpose of the new truth is to bring about the complete salvation of humanity. It must be able to resolve the fundamental questions of life, clarify the purpose of creation, and reveal God's plan for the restoration of fallen humankind.

**6** The Divine Principle is the teaching that reveals this new truth. It explains the principles by which God created the universe and by which He intends to restore fallen humanity to its original ideal.
