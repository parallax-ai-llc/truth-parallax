---
title: "Divine Principle Section 2"
scripture: "divine-principle"
chapter: 2
---

**1** God is the Creator of the universe and the source of all truth, beauty, and goodness. God created all things through the Principle, and the Principle is the expression of God's nature.

**2** God has dual characteristics: internal nature and external form, and masculinity and femininity. These dual characteristics exist in harmony within God and are reflected throughout all creation.

**3** Every created being exists through the reciprocal relationship of dual characteristics. The mind and body of a human being, the stamen and pistil of a flower, the proton and electron of an atom — all reflect the dual characteristics of God.

**4** God created human beings as His children, to be His objects of love. The purpose of creation is to establish a world in which God and human beings can share love and joy.

**5** The family is the fundamental unit through which God's love is expressed. A man and a woman, united as husband and wife in God's blessing, form a four-position foundation with God and their children.

**6** The ideal of creation is a world in which all human beings live as one family under God, loving one another and living in harmony with all creation. This is the Kingdom of Heaven on earth.
