---
title: "Divine Principle Section 9"
scripture: "divine-principle"
chapter: 9
---

**1** The Second Coming of the Lord is the central hope of Christianity. But the manner of his coming must be correctly understood.

**2** Jesus said he would come "on the clouds of heaven." But clouds in scripture often symbolize the masses of faithful believers, or the purified state of rebirth.

**3** Just as the first coming was as a man born on earth, the Second Coming will also be as a man born on earth. He will not come literally on the clouds.

**4** The Lord of the Second Advent will be born in a nation that has been prepared by God. This nation must fulfill certain conditions: it must be at the front line of the struggle between God's side and Satan's side.

**5** Korea fulfills these conditions. Korea has endured great suffering, is divided between North and South — representing Cain and Abel — and has a deep tradition of religious seeking.

**6** The Lord of the Second Advent will come as the Third Adam, completing the mission of the First Adam and the Second Adam (Jesus). He will establish the true family and engraft all humanity into God's lineage.

**7** Through the True Parents — the Lord of the Second Advent and his Bride — the original sin will be removed, and the Kingdom of Heaven on earth will be established. This is the completion of God's providence of restoration.
