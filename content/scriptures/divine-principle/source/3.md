---
title: "Divine Principle Section 3"
scripture: "divine-principle"
chapter: 3
---

**1** The Human Fall was not an act of eating literal fruit. The fruit of the tree of knowledge of good and evil represents the love of Eve. The serpent represents the archangel Lucifer.

**2** The archangel, who was supposed to serve as a guardian to Adam and Eve, instead seduced Eve in an illicit spiritual relationship. This was the spiritual fall.

**3** Eve, having fallen spiritually, then entered into an immature physical relationship with Adam. This was the physical fall.

**4** Through this fallen act, human beings inherited the fallen nature of the archangel. The original sin is not simply disobedience; it is the corruption of love through an illicit relationship.

**5** As a result of the Fall, Satan — the fallen archangel — became the false lord of this world. Human beings, who were meant to be children of God, became children of Satan's lineage.

**6** The four fallen natures inherited from the archangel are: failure to take God's standpoint, leaving one's proper position, reversal of dominion, and multiplication of evil.

**7** All the suffering and evil in human history stem from the Fall. The purpose of God's providence of restoration is to reverse the effects of the Fall and restore humanity to the original ideal.
