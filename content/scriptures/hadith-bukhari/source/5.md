---
title: "Sahih al-Bukhari - Chapter 5"
scripture: "hadith-bukhari"
chapter: 5
---

**1** Narrated Abu Hurairah: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "Were it not that I would place a hardship on my followers, I would have commanded them to use the tooth-stick before every prayer."

**2** Narrated Aisha: The Prophet, peace be upon him, said: "The tooth-stick is a purification for the mouth and pleasing to the Lord."

**3** Narrated Anas: The Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him, said: "I have told you repeatedly about the tooth-stick." He emphasized its use greatly.

**4** Narrated Hudhaifah: Whenever the Prophet, peace be upon him, got up at night for Tahajjud prayer, he used to clean his mouth with the tooth-stick.

**5** Narrated Abu Musa al-Ash'ari: I came to the Prophet, peace be upon him, and found him using the tooth-stick, with the end of the tooth-stick on his tongue, and he was saying "Ugh, ugh" as if he was retching.

**6** Narrated Ibn Umar: The Prophet, peace be upon him, said: "I saw myself in a dream using a tooth-stick, and two men came to me, one older than the other. I gave the tooth-stick to the younger one, but was told: 'Give it to the older one first.' So I gave it to the older one."

**7** Narrated Aisha: Abdur-Rahman ibn Abu Bakr came in, holding a green tooth-stick with which he was cleaning his teeth. The Messenger of Allah looked at it. I took the tooth-stick, chewed it to soften it, and gave it to the Prophet, who cleaned his teeth with it. This was in his final illness.

**8** Narrated Abu Hurairah: The Prophet, peace be upon him, said: "Five things are a part of natural disposition: circumcision, shaving the pubic hair, trimming the mustache, cutting the nails, and removing the hair of the armpits."
