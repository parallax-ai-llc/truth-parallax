## Overview

The Comparative reading of the Chaldean Oracles examines the text's theurgical cosmology alongside parallel systems in other traditions. The Oracles' doctrine of emanation from a transcendent Fire finds resonance in Hindu conceptions of Agni and tapas, in Kabbalistic sefirot, and in Gnostic pleroma theology. The figure of Hecate as cosmic mediator invites comparison with Hindu Shakti, the Gnostic Sophia, and the Kabbalistic Shekhinah.
