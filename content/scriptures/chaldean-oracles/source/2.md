---
title: "Chaldean Oracles — Fragment 2"
scripture: "chaldean-oracles"
chapter: 2
---

**1** The Mind of the Father whirred, thinking with vigorous counsel, and all things burst forth from the one source. For the will and the end were already with the Father when the Paternal Mind sowed the bond of fire.

**2** For the Father brought forth all things perfect, and delivered them to the Second Mind, whom all nations of men call First.

**3** All things were begotten from one fire. For the Father accomplished all things and delivered them to mind, which mind the nations of mortals call First.

**4** The Dyad sits beside Him, for she has a double function: to hold in her hands all things intellectual and to bring the sense of light to all worlds.

**5** From both of these there flows the body of the bond: the connecting spirit which runs through all things, making the cosmos one.
