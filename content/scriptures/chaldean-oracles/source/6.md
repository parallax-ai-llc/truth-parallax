---
title: "Chaldean Oracles — Fragment 6"
scripture: "chaldean-oracles"
chapter: 6
---

**1** A human soul will press God close to itself, possessing nothing mortal. It is wholly intoxicated by the Father. For the soul of mortals will clasp God to itself.

**2** Do not stoop down, for a precipice lies below the earth, drawing through the ladder which has seven steps, beneath which is the throne of Necessity.

**3** Do not look upon Nature, for her name is Fate. Do not plunge yourself into the ocean of the world. For there is a portion of the soul which always remains in the Intelligible.

**4** Guard the channel of the soul. Let it be protected by the symbols and the works of fire, and the unspeakable words, and the mystical signs.

**5** The soul that has fallen into generation must be regenerated through fire.
