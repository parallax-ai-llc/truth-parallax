---
title: "Chaldean Oracles — Fragment 3"
scripture: "chaldean-oracles"
chapter: 3
---

**1** Do not invoke the self-visible image of Nature, for you must not look upon these before your body has been purified by the sacred rites.

**2** For those who are uninitiated, approaching the sacred mysteries in ignorance, are struck from on high by the thunderbolt.

**3** The First Transcendent Fire does not enclose its power in matter, but through Mind. For the Maker of the Fiery Cosmos is the Mind of Mind.

**4** He who first sprang from Mind, clothing the one Fire with the other Fire, binding them together that he might mingle the source-giving craters, while preserving unsullied the brilliance of His own Fire.

**5** For from the Paternal Source nothing imperfect whirls forth.
