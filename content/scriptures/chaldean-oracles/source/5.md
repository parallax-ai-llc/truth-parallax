---
title: "Chaldean Oracles — Fragment 5"
scripture: "chaldean-oracles"
chapter: 5
---

**1** The Teletarchs are grouped together with the Connectors and the Maintainers and Foundations and the Fountains and Veils and Supports.

**2** And Hecate, whom the Father placed in the center, bears all things. For three roads are within her. She controls all things: the Empyrean world, the Ethereal world, and the Material world.

**3** Between the Fathers and the Material realm there extends the Soul-membrane; the fiery Soul that stretches through all.

**4** The Connectors minister to the Paternal Depth. The Maintainers guard the whirling of the fiery realm. The Teletarchs perform the perfecting rites.

**5** All these are servants of the fiery cosmos which the Father created first.
