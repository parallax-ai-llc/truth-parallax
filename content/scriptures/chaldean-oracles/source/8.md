---
title: "Chaldean Oracles — Fragment 8"
scripture: "chaldean-oracles"
chapter: 8
---

**1** If you call upon Me often, you shall perceive everything in lion-form. For then neither the vault of heaven appears, nor the stars shine; the light of the moon is hidden, and the earth stands not still; but all things are seen in lightning.

**2** To you I give the vision of the All-Father. Believe, and you shall know. Knowledge is not opinion, but faith of the heart.

**3** The theurgist who has worked these rites can be released from Fate. The Father has sown the bond of love within the fiery cosmos, the love which unites all things.

**4** Believe that you have seen the Father. Believing is a sacred deed. And the way of the soul leads upward through the fire.

**5** These are the oracles of the gods, delivered through fire, and they contain the wisdom of the Chaldeans, the sacred philosophy of the divine fire.
