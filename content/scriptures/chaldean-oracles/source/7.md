---
title: "Chaldean Oracles — Fragment 7"
scripture: "chaldean-oracles"
chapter: 7
---

**1** Direct not your mind to the vast surfaces of the earth, for the Plant of Truth grows not upon the ground. Nor measure the motions of the sun, collecting rules, for it is carried by the eternal Will of the Father, and not for your sake.

**2** Dismiss the swift course of the moon, for she always runs by the work of Necessity. The progression of the stars was not generated for your sake.

**3** For these things are beneath the realm of the soul. Look upward toward the Intelligible and toward the Father.

**4** Let the deep mind of the Paternal Intellect guide you. When you perceive the shape of the supernal Fire blazing bright and formless through the depths of the whole world, then give ear to the Voice of Fire.

**5** You must not exchange the barbarous Names. For there are certain divine Names in every nation given by God, which have an unspeakable power in sacred rites.
