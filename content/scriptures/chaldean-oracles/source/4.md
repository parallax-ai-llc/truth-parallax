---
title: "Chaldean Oracles — Fragment 4"
scripture: "chaldean-oracles"
chapter: 4
---

**1** Let the immortal depth of the soul lead you, and lift your eyes upward intensely. For the intelligible is nourished by intelligence.

**2** You should hasten toward the Light and toward the rays of the Father, from whence was sent to you a soul clothed in much mind.

**3** All things are served by the lightning bolts of the thrice-transcendent God. But all things are sprung from one Fire.

**4** The Soul, being a radiant fire, by the power of the Father, remains immortal and is mistress of life, and holds the fullness of the many-bosomed cosmos.

**5** For there is nothing in the whole cosmos that is without soul.
