## Overview

The Theological reading of the Chaldean Oracles examines the text's systematic theology of a transcendent Paternal Fire, an intelligible triad of Being-Life-Intellect, and a mediating World-Soul identified with Hecate. The Oracles present a sophisticated doctrine of divine emanation and return, in which the soul's salvation depends not merely on knowledge but on participation in theurgical rites that activate the divine symbols (sunthemata) sown throughout creation.
