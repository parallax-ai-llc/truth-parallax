**Utterances 625-658: Serpent Spells**

**1** A face has fallen against a face; a face has seen a face. The mottled knife, black and green, has gone forth against it. It has swallowed that which it tasted.

**2** Hail, you serpent! Go away, cobra! Get back! Turn around, get lost, be blind!

**3** O Rerek serpent, do not raise yourself! O dweller in the earth, lie down! Your mouth is closed by the god who is in Buto. He who would see the King, his face falls!

**4** Get back, hidden serpent! Do not show yourself to the King!
