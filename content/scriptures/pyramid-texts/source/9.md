**Utterances 302-336: The Ferryman of the Sky**

**1** O Mahaf, Ferryman of the Winding Waterway! Bring me this! Bring me the ferryboat!

**2** The King wishes to cross to the great god, Lord of the Horizon, for the King is one who serves the great god.

**3** If you fail to ferry the King, he will leap and sit on the wing of Thoth. Then it is he who will ferry the King to yonder side.

**4** The Winding Waterway is flooded. The Field of Rushes is filled with water. The King is ferried thereover to yonder eastern side of the sky, to the place where the gods fashioned him.

**5** He was born there, he was made young again there.
