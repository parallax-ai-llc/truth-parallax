**Utterances 436-468: The Imperishable Stars**

**1** O King, you are the great star, the companion of Orion, who traverses the sky with Orion, who navigates the Duat with Osiris.

**2** You ascend from the east of the sky, being renewed in your due season, being rejuvenated in your due time.

**3** The sky has borne you with Orion. The dawn-light bears you with Orion. He who lives, lives by the command of the gods, and you live.

**4** You will regularly ascend with Orion from the eastern region of the sky, you will regularly descend with Orion into the western region of the sky.

**5** Your third is Sothis, pure of thrones, who guides you on the beautiful roads which are in the sky, in the Field of Rushes.
