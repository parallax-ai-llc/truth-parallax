**Utterances 569-600: The Reed Floats**

**1** The reed-floats of the sky are set down for the King, that the King may cross to the horizon, to Ra, to the place where the gods were born.

**2** The reed-floats of the sky are set down for the King by the Day-bark, that the King may cross thereon to the horizon, to Ra.

**3** The reed-floats of the sky are set down for the King by the Night-bark, that the King may cross thereon to the horizon, to Horus of the Netherworld.

**4** The reed-floats of the sky are set down for the King by Shezmu, that the King may go up on them to the sky, to Ra.
