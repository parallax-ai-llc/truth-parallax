**Utterances 717-741: The Ladder to Heaven**

**1** Hail to you, O Ladder of the god! Hail to you, O Ladder of Set! Stand up, O Ladder of the god! Stand up, O Ladder of Set! Stand up, O Ladder of Horus, which was made for Osiris that he might ascend on it to the sky.

**2** Ra has set up his ladder. Horus has set up his ladder. He gives the King his arm on the stairway to the sky, to the place where the Imperishable Stars dwell.

**3** "Everyone who has a ka shall use the Ladder," says Ra. The face of the King is that of the Falcon-god. His wings are those of the god who crosses the sky.
