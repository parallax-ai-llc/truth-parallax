**Utterances 601-624: The Two Enneads**

**1** O you Great Ennead which is in Heliopolis: Atum, Shu, Tefnut, Geb, Nut, Osiris, Isis, Set, Nephthys! Atum's children! Extend his heart to his child, in your name of Nine Bows.

**2** O you Lesser Ennead which is in Heliopolis: bring the King! Make him endure! Cause the King to endure!

**3** If you live, the King will live. If you are sound, the King will be sound. For the King is the great one among you.
