**Utterances 375-405: Osiris Risen**

**1** O Osiris the King, arise! Horus comes. He claims you from the gods. Horus has loved you. He has filled you with his Eye.

**2** Horus has opened your eye for you that you may see with it. The gods have bound your face to you, and they love you.

**3** Isis and Nephthys have healed you. Horus is not far from you; you are his ka.

**4** Your face is that of a jackal. Your flesh is restored to your bones. You hear with your ears. You see with your eyes. You speak with your mouth.

**5** O Osiris the King! Rise up! You are alive! You are young! Your ka lives for you.
