**Utterances 147-212: The King Among the Stars**

**1** O King, you are this great star, the companion of Orion, who traverses the sky with Orion, who sails the Duat with Osiris.

**2** You ascend from the East of the sky, being renewed at your due season and rejuvenated at your due time.

**3** The sky has borne you with Orion. The year has put a fillet on you with Osiris. Raise yourself, O King!

**4** Your bones are those of the divine falcons who are in the sky. May you be beside the god. May you leave your house to your son of your begetting.

**5** The sky speaks, the earth quakes, for fear of you, O Osiris, when you ascend. O you milch-cows here, O you herdsmen here, O you who tend!

**6** This King comes to you. He shall not be destroyed. He is an imperishable star, the son of the sky-goddess.
