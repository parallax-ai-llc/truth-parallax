**Utterances 841-872: The King as Osiris**

**1** This King is Osiris in a dust-devil. The earth is his abhorrence. The King enters not into Geb, that he may be destroyed.

**2** The King sleeps every day in the northern part of the sky. He rises as a star. The King is at the fore of the celestial spirits.

**3** O you who stand, O you who sit, O you who dwell in the dust! Rise up for this King! He is your brother. He is the likeness of the god.
