**Utterances 58-93: Spells of Resurrection**

**1** O Atum! Raise this King up to you. Enclose him within your embrace, for he is your son of your body for ever.

**2** O Atum! Set this King upon you, upon your shoulder, that he may raise himself up. He will not die; his ka will not perish. He is an imperishable star.

**3** O King, you have not departed dead, you have departed alive. Sit upon the throne of Osiris, your sceptre in your hand, that you may give orders to the living.

**4** Rise up, O King! You have your water, you have your inundation, you have your milk, which is from the breasts of the goddess Isis.

**5** Your mother Nut has spread her wings over you. She has caused you to be a god. She has caused you to be without an enemy.

**6** O King, live! You shall not die! Geb has given you your two eyes that you may be content.
