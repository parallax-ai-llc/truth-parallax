**Utterances 94-146: The Ascent to the Sky**

**1** A stairway to the sky is set up for me that I may ascend on it to the sky, and I ascend on the smoke of the great censing.

**2** I fly up as a bird, I alight as a beetle. I fly up as a bird, I alight as a beetle on the empty throne which is in your bark, O Ra!

**3** Stand up, remove yourself, O you who do not know the Reed-floats, that the King may sit in your place and row over the sky in your bark, O Ra!

**4** The King ascends to the sky! The King ascends to the sky on the wind, on the wind!

**5** He is not barred. There is no one who bars him. He ascends on the thigh of Isis, he climbs upon the thigh of Nephthys.

**6** O King, the sky thunders, the earth quakes before you, for you are a god. You walk on your feet, you swing your arms, you fly as a goose, you hover as a hawk, you alight as a heron.

**7** The doors of heaven are opened for you, the gates of the cool firmament are thrown open for you.
