**Utterances 771-806: The King's Ka**

**1** O King, your ka stands among the gods. Your awe is on their hearts. O King, mount upon your throne!

**2** Command those who do things! Your ka is the ka of the living. You are the ka of all the gods.

**3** O King, you are a spirit. Your ka comes to you among the spirits. O King, you are an akh. Your ka comes to you among the akhs.

**4** Your face is washed. Your tears are wiped away. Your body is clothed. You are a god. You stand among the gods.
