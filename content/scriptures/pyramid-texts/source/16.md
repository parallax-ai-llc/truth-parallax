**Utterances 536-568: The King as Horus**

**1** I am Horus, O Osiris the King, I am your son. I have come that I may greet you, O Osiris, that I may cleanse you and revivify you.

**2** I have reassembled you. I have gathered together your limbs. I have put your heart into your body.

**3** O Osiris the King, I have set in order for you the two portions of your body. I have set your back for you. O God, be aware! O God, awake! Rise from your side!

**4** I have done for you what Geb commanded. The thrones of the Two Lands are yours. Their nourishment is yours. The Ennead serves you.

**5** You have power over the gods and their ka's. You have power over the imperishable stars.
