**Utterances 247-272: Spells for the Pyramid**

**1** O Atum, set your arms about this King, about this construction, about this pyramid, as the arms of a ka-symbol, that the King's ka may be in it, enduring for ever and ever.

**2** O Great Ennead which is in Heliopolis: Atum, Shu, Tefnut, Geb, Nut, Osiris, Isis, Set, Nephthys! O children of Atum, extend his heart to his child, in your name of Nine Bows.

**3** Let not his back be turned from you, in your name of Pillar of the Sky. O Horus, this King is Osiris, this Pyramid of the King is Osiris, this construction of his is Osiris.

**4** Betake yourself to it. Do not be far from it in its name of Pyramid. Be complete and great in your name of 'The Mansion of the Ka of Horus-and-Set.'

**5** The face of this King is opened. His stride is wide upon the stairway to the sky before Ra.
