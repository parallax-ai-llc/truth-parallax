**Utterances 1-23: The Ritual of Offering**

**1** O King, I have come to you. I bring to you the Eye of Horus that you may equip your face with it. O King, receive this your bread-offering.

**2** O Osiris the King, take the Eye of Horus; provide yourself with it. Take your thousand of bread, your thousand of beer, your thousand of oxen, your thousand of fowl.

**3** Libation is poured. O Osiris the King, take the water which is in the Eye of Horus. Do not leave it, do not leave it!

**4** O King, the water of the Eye of Horus is before you. I have come to you that I may purify you, that I may cleanse you, that I may revive you.

**5** O King, take to yourself the Eye of Horus, the perfume of which comes to you. The incense comes, the fragrance comes. The fragrance of the Eye of Horus is upon you.

**6** O King, I bring to you the Eye of Horus. I have placed it before you. The bread of offering is in it, that you may taste thereof.
