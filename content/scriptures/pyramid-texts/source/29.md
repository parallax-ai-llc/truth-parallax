**Utterances 941-965: The Journey Through the Duat**

**1** The doors of the sky are opened for you. The doors of the firmament are thrown open for you. Geb has opened your eyes for you.

**2** You traverse the Lake of Jackals. You pass through the Field of Rushes. You walk through the beautiful ways which are in the sky.

**3** The sky-goddess stretches out her hand to you. She carries you to the horizon. She places you among the Imperishable Stars.

**4** O King, you have not gone away dead. You have gone away alive to sit on the throne of Osiris.
