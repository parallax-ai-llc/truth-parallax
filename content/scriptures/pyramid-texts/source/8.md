**Utterances 273-301: Spells of Protection**

**1** O you snake, get back! Get back! A flame is in the earth. The flame has come forth against you.

**2** Get back, you rebel! The Eye of Horus stands against you. The great Eye of Horus is upon you. It will destroy you. It will burn you.

**3** O you who would bite! O you who would sting! Get back from this King. He is protected by the Great God.

**4** O you crocodile of the west, who lives on the never-setting stars! O you crocodile of the east! O you crocodile of the south! O you crocodile of the north! Get back! Your mouth is closed.

**5** The King passes you in safety. He is protected. He travels in peace.
