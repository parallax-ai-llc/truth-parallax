**Utterances 966-end: The Final Spells**

**1** The sky is clear, Sothis lives, for the King is a living one, the son of Sothis. The Two Enneads have purified themselves for him as the Great Bear, the imperishable.

**2** The house of the King in the sky will not perish, the throne of the King on earth will not be destroyed.

**3** Men shall hide, the gods shall fly up, when the King ascends to the sky among the gods who are in the sky.

**4** The King stands among them, for the Moon is the King's brother, the Morning Star is the King's offspring.

**5** O King, you shall not depart from the earth lifeless; you shall depart from the earth as one who is alive. You sit upon the throne of Osiris, your sceptre in your hand, that you may give orders to the living.

**6** Your arm is Atum, your shoulders are Atum, your belly is Atum, your back is Atum, your rear is Atum, your legs are Atum, your face is Anubis.

**7** The servitors of Horus serve you. The servitors of Set serve you. The King is a great one. The King has appeared. The King has appeared forever and ever.
