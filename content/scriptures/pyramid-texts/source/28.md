**Utterances 906-940: The King and the Stars**

**1** You shall reach the sky as Orion. Your soul shall be as effective as Sothis. You shall have power. You shall have force. You shall have spirit.

**2** You are a spirit among the spirits. Sothis is your sister. The Morning Star is your offspring.

**3** You sit upon your throne of iron. You judge the gods. You stand among the spirits. You command among the gods.

**4** O King, you are among the Imperishable Stars. Your name is 'Living One.' You sit on the throne of Osiris, your sceptre in your hand.
