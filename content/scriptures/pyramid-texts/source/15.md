**Utterances 507-535: Prayers to Geb and Nut**

**1** O Geb, Bull of Nut, the King is Horus, the heir of his father. The King is he who went and came back, the fourth of these four gods who brought the water, who made the purification, who rejoiced over the strength of their fathers.

**2** He desires that he be justified through that which he has done. Tefnut and Maat have judged the King. Shu is a witness.

**3** Tefnut and Maat have judged that the thrones of Geb shall revert to the King, that he shall raise himself to that which he desired.

**4** His members which were in hidden places are reassembled. He has joined those who dwell in the Abyss.
