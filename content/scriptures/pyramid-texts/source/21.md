**Utterances 692-716: Opening of the Mouth**

**1** My mouth is opened by Ptah. The bonds on my mouth are loosened by my city-god. Thoth has come fully equipped with charms.

**2** He loosens the bonds of Set from my mouth. Atum has hurled back those who would restrain him with their arms.

**3** My mouth is opened. My mouth is split open by Shu with that iron harpoon of his with which he split open the mouths of the gods.

**4** I am the great Sekhet, who sits in the great Place, among the great souls of Heliopolis.
