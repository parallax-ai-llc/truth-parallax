**Utterances 873-905: Protection Spells**

**1** Back, you evil dead! Do not come against this King. Your face is the face of a dog. Your heart is the heart of a baboon.

**2** The arm of the King shall not be seized by men; the arm of the King shall not be seized by gods; the arm of the King shall not be seized by spirits; the arm of the King shall not be seized by the dead.

**3** Whoever shall seize the arm of the King—when the sky falls to the earth, when the Ennead falls to the earth—his bones shall be broken, his joints shall be loosened.
