**Utterances 807-840: Hymn to the Sun**

**1** Hail to you, Ra, in your beauty, in your places, in your diadems! The King comes to you. He is a soul.

**2** Ra purifies himself. A stairway to the sky is set up for the King among the gods. The King stands on it, and he is far from wrongdoing.

**3** The King appears as the Eye of Ra. The King shines as the Eye of Ra. The King is a flame moved before the wind to the ends of the sky and to the ends of the earth.

**4** O Ra, make the King sound. Make the King sound for the duration of eternity.
