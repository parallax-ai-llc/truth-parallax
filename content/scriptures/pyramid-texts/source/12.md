**Utterances 406-435: The King's Power**

**1** The sky trembles, the earth quakes before the King, for the King is a great power. The King is a bull of heaven who charges and conquers.

**2** The King lives on the gods. He eats their magic. He swallows their spirits. Their great ones are for his morning meal. Their middle-sized ones are for his evening meal. Their little ones are for his night meal.

**3** The old gods and the old goddesses become fuel for his furnace. The King takes their magic, he takes their glory.

**4** He has encompassed the two heavens. He has circled the two banks. The King is the great power that overpowers the powers.
