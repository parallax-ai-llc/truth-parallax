**Utterances 469-506: Hymn to Nut**

**1** O great strider who sows emeralds, malachite, and turquoise of the stars! If you are green, the King will be green, even as a living reed is green.

**2** O Nut, you have spread yourself over your son the King, that you may conceal him from Set. Protect him, O Nut!

**3** O Nut, you became a spirit, you waxed mighty in the belly of your mother Tefnut even before you were born.

**4** O Nut, your mother has said to you in your name of Nut: You are the daughter, mighty in her mother. You are crowned as a queen of Upper Egypt.

**5** O Nut, fall upon the earth! O Nut, spread yourself over your son, that you may conceal him from Set.
