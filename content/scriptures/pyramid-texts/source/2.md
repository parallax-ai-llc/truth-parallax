**Utterances 24-57: Spells of Purification**

**1** This King is purified with the Eye of Horus. The evil which was on him has been removed by the two Enneads of the gods.

**2** O waters, appear! O flood, come forth! O you who are great, arise! O you of the Nile, come! Purify this King.

**3** Purified is he who is purified in the Field of Rushes. Ra is purified in the Field of Rushes. Purified is he who is purified in the Field of Rushes. This King is purified in the Field of Rushes.

**4** O Ra-Atum! This King comes to you, an imperishable spirit, lord of the affairs of the place of the four pillars. Your son comes to you. This King comes to you.

**5** May you two traverse the sky, united in the darkness. May you rise in the horizon, in the place where it is well with you.

**6** The face of every god is washed. The King washes his face. His face is the face of the great Falcon.
