**Utterances 742-770: The Field of Offerings**

**1** The Field of Offerings is given to the King. He ploughs therein. He reaps therein. He eats therein. He drinks therein.

**2** He shall plow the barley and reap the emmer. He makes his provision from it. He is satisfied with it for ever and ever.

**3** How beautiful to see! How satisfying to behold! says Isis. How beautiful to see! How satisfying to behold! says Nephthys. When they see the King approaching in peace.

**4** The two doors of the sky stand open for you. The two doors of Qebhu stand open for you.
