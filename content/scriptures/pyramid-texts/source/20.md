**Utterances 659-691: The King's Ascension**

**1** The sky speaks, the earth quakes, Geb quivers, the two domains of the god roar, the earth is hacked up for the King, and the offering is presented before him.

**2** He has appeared upon the throne of the Lord of All. He traverses the sky. He cleaves its iron.

**3** He is led along the ways of the imperishable stars. He follows in the retinue of Ra. He steers a course to the Field of Rushes.

**4** The King's lifetime is eternity. His limit is everlastingness.
